## Summary

SEBI has issued a completion order for Recovery Certificate No. 8714 of 2025 against Natvarbhai Vegda. This order relates to enforcement proceedings concerning trading activities in the scrip of Kavit Industries Limited. The completion order indicates the conclusion of recovery proceedings against the individual.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8714 of 2025 issued against Natvarbhai Vegda
- Related to trading activities in Kavit Industries Limited scrip
- Completion order marking the conclusion of recovery proceedings
- Individual enforcement action

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement completion order.

## Compliance Requirements

This is a completion order indicating concluded proceedings. No new compliance requirements for market participants.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: October 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact on broader market operations. This is an individual enforcement matter specific to trading activities in Kavit Industries Limited. The completion order indicates concluded recovery proceedings with no direct implications for other market participants.
