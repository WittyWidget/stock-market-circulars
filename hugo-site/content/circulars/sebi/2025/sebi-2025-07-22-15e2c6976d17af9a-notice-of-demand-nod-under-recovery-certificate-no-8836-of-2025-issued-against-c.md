## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand (NoD) under Recovery Certificate No. 8836 of 2025 against Chetan Yashwant Shukla for providing unregistered investment advisory services. This enforcement action is part of SEBI's ongoing efforts to curb unauthorized investment advisory activities.

## Key Points

- Notice of Demand issued under Recovery Certificate No. 8836 of 2025
- Action taken against Chetan Yashwant Shukla for unregistered investment advisory services
- Part of SEBI's enforcement proceedings for recovery of penalties
- Demonstrates regulatory action against unauthorized investment advisory activities

## Regulatory Changes

This document does not introduce new regulatory changes but represents enforcement of existing regulations regarding investment advisory registration requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

Investment advisory service providers must ensure they are properly registered with SEBI before providing any investment advisory services to clients.

## Important Dates

- Notice of Demand date: July 22, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action reinforces SEBI's commitment to protecting investors from unregistered investment advisors. It serves as a warning to other entities providing investment advisory services without proper registration. The action has limited direct market impact but strengthens regulatory oversight of the investment advisory sector.
