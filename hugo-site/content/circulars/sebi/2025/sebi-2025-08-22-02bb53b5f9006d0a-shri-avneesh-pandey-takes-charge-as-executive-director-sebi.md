## Summary

Shri Avneesh Pandey has been appointed as Executive Director at the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) and will be responsible for the Information Technology Department (ITD).

## Key Points

- Shri Avneesh Pandey took charge as Executive Director, SEBI in Mumbai
- He will handle the Information Technology Department (ITD)
- Previously served as Chief General Manager in SEBI
- Has three decades of securities market regulatory experience
- Expertise covers IT initiatives, business process innovation, big data analytics, SupTech, cyber security, IT audits and inspections
- Describes himself as technology strategist, process innovator and cyber security evangelist
- Holds MPhil in Technology Policy from University of Cambridge, UK
- Former ADB-Japan Scholar at Asian Institute of Management, Manila, Philippines

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced - this is a personnel appointment.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements introduced.

## Important Dates

- August 22, 2025: Date Shri Avneesh Pandey took charge as Executive Director

## Impact Assessment

This is an administrative appointment with no direct impact on market operations or trading. The appointment strengthens SEBI's technology leadership given Pandey's extensive IT and cyber security experience.
