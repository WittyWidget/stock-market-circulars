## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. 8846/2025 dated 01.08.2025 against Pandoo Prabhakar Naig (PAN: ACNPN2800J) in connection with Onelife Capital Advisors Limited. This certificate authorizes recovery proceedings for outstanding dues/penalties.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: 8846/2025
- Date of Issue: 01.08.2025
- Target Individual: Pandoo Prabhakar Naig (PAN: ACNPN2800J)
- Related Entity: Onelife Capital Advisors Limited
- Legal basis for recovery proceedings established

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- The named individual must comply with the recovery certificate
- Outstanding dues/penalties must be paid as per the certificate
- Non-compliance may lead to further enforcement measures

## Important Dates

- Certificate Issue Date: 01.08.2025
- Recovery proceedings can commence immediately

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact - affects specific individual and entity. No broader market implications. Part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement and recovery activities to ensure compliance with securities regulations.
