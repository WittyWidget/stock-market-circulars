## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. 8924 of 2025 dated October 15, 2025 against Yuvika Tradewing LLP (PAN No. AABFY4620R) in connection with the matter of Cressanda Solutions Limited. This recovery certificate represents SEBI's formal enforcement action to recover outstanding amounts through the recovery certificate mechanism.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8924 of 2025 issued on October 15, 2025
- Entity: Yuvika Tradewing LLP
- PAN: AABFY4620R
- Related matter: Cressanda Solutions Limited
- Enforcement action initiated by SEBI for recovery of dues

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Yuvika Tradewing LLP must comply with the recovery certificate demand
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement measures including attachment of bank accounts, property, and other assets
- The recovery officer may initiate coercive measures for recovery as per applicable laws

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Issue Date: October 15, 2025
- Recovery Certificate Number: 8924 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Direct Impact:**
- High impact on Yuvika Tradewing LLP as recovery proceedings have been initiated
- Potential attachment of assets and bank accounts if demand is not met
- Credit implications and restrictions on business operations

**Market Impact:**
- Signals SEBI's continued focus on enforcement and recovery of dues
- Demonstrates consequences for non-compliance in matters related to listed entities
- Related to Cressanda Solutions Limited, indicating ongoing regulatory matters involving the company

**Operational Impact:**
- Yuvika Tradewing LLP may face restrictions on business operations
