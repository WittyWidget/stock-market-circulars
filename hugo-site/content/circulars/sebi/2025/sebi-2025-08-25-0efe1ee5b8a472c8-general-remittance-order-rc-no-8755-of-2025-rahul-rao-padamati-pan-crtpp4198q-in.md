## Summary

SEBI has issued General Remittance Order RC No. 8755 of 2025 against Rahul Rao Padamati (PAN: CRTPP4198Q) in connection with unregistered investment advisory activities conducted through 'Baap of Chart'.

## Key Points

- SEBI enforcement action against individual for unregistered investment advisory services
- Subject identified as Rahul Rao Padamati with PAN CRTPP4198Q
- Activities conducted under the name 'Baap of Chart'
- Formal General Remittance Order issued as enforcement measure

## Regulatory Changes

No specific regulatory changes mentioned in this enforcement action.

## Compliance Requirements

- All investment advisers must be registered with SEBI before providing advisory services
- Individuals and entities must comply with SEBI Investment Adviser Regulations
- Violation of registration requirements subject to enforcement action

## Important Dates

- Order Date: August 2025
- Reference Number: RC No. 8755 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action serves as a deterrent against unregistered investment advisory activities. It reinforces SEBI's commitment to ensuring compliance with registration requirements and protecting investors from unauthorized advisory services.
