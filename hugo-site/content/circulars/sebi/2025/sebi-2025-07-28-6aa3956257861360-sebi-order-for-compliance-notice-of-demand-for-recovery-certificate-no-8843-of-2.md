## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand for Recovery Certificate No. 8843 of 2025 against Umesh Balkrishan Tibrewala HUF in connection with matters relating to Pressure Sensitive (India) Limited. This represents enforcement action for compliance violations.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8843 of 2025 issued
- Target entity: Umesh Balkrishan Tibrewala HUF
- Related company: Pressure Sensitive (India) Limited
- Notice of demand for recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced in this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Umesh Balkrishan Tibrewala HUF must respond to the recovery notice
- Payment of demanded amounts as specified in the notice
- Compliance with recovery certificate proceedings

## Important Dates

- Notice issued: July 28, 2025
- Response deadlines as specified in the notice document

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action against Umesh Balkrishan Tibrewala HUF may impact their business operations and financial standing. Stakeholders of Pressure Sensitive (India) Limited should monitor developments in this recovery proceeding.
