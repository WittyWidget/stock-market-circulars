## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated 29.08.2025 under RC No. 8881 of 2025 against Thousand Keys and its proprietor Tapas Das (PAN: ANKPD3883D) for operating unregistered investment advisory services in violation of securities regulations.

## Key Points

- Enforcement action taken against Thousand Keys for providing unregistered investment advisory services
- Notice issued to proprietor Tapas Das with PAN: ANKPD3883D
- Violation relates to operating investment advisory services without proper SEBI registration
- Demand notice issued under regulatory case number RC No. 8881 of 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing investment advisory regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investment advisors must obtain proper SEBI registration before providing advisory services
- Entities providing investment advice must comply with SEBI (Investment Advisers) Regulations
- Unregistered entities must cease providing investment advisory services immediately

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 29.08.2025
- Regulatory Case: RC No. 8881 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action reinforces SEBI's commitment to regulating investment advisory services. While specific to one entity, it serves as a warning to other unregistered advisors operating in the market. The action helps protect investors from potentially unqualified or unregulated advisory services.
