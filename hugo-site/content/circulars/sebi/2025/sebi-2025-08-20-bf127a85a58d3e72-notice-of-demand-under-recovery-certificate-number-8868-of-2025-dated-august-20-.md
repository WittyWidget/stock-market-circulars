## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate number 8868 of 2025 dated August 20, 2025, to Anju Devi, Proprietor of Shree Balaji Enterprises (PAN: BHGPD3597M) in connection with investigation into trading activities in Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited scrip.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8868 of 2025 issued against Anju Devi
- Related to Recovery Certificate No. 8794 of 2025
- Investigation concerns trading activities in Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited
- Defaulter identified as Proprietor of Shree Balaji Enterprises
- PAN: BHGPD3597M

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Anju Devi must comply with the demand notice
- Payment of outstanding amounts as per recovery certificate
- Compliance with investigation requirements

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: August 20, 2025
- Recovery Certificate Date: 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a targeted enforcement action against a specific entity identified as a defaulter in the Continental Seeds investigation. Limited market impact as it affects individual defaulter rather than broader market participants.
