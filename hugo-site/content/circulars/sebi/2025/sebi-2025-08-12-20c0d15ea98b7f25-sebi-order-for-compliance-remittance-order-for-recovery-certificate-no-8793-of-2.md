SEBI has issued a compliance order regarding Recovery Certificate No. 8793 of 2025 against Mudrikaben Pareshkumar Shah in the matter of illiquid stocks options trading at BSE. This order pertains to enforcement and recovery proceedings.

- Recovery Certificate No. 8793 of 2025 issued against Mudrikaben Pareshkumar Shah
- Matter relates to illiquid stocks options trading at BSE
- Remittance order for compliance enforcement
- Part of SEBI's regulatory enforcement actions

No specific regulatory changes announced in this compliance order.

- Remittance of specified amounts as per the order
- Adherence to enforcement directions issued by SEBI

## Important Dates

- Order Date: August 12, 2025
- Recovery Certificate No. 8793 of 2025

This is an individual enforcement action with limited broader market impact. It demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement of regulations related to illiquid stocks options trading violations.
