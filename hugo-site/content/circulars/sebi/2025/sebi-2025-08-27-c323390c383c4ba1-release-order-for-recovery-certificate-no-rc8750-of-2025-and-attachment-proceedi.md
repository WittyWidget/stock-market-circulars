## Summary

SEBI has issued Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. RC8750 of 2025 and attachment proceeding Nos. 14746 & 14747 of 2025 against Pankaj Kumar Agarwal (PAN: AGKPA4563A) in relation to illiquid stock options matter.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC8750 of 2025 issued against Pankaj Kumar Agarwal
- Attachment proceeding Nos. 14746 & 14747 of 2025 initiated
- Individual PAN: AGKPA4563A
- Matter relates to illiquid stock options

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes specified.

## Compliance Requirements

Pankaj Kumar Agarwal must comply with the recovery certificate and attachment proceedings as per SEBI directives.

## Important Dates

- Release Order Date: August 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is an enforcement action against an individual regarding illiquid stock options. The impact is limited to the specific individual and does not affect broader market operations or trading activities.
