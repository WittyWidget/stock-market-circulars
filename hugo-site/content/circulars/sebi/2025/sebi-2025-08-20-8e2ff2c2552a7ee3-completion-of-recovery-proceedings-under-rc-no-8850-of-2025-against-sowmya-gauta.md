## Summary

SEBI has completed recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. 8850 of 2025 against Sowmya Gautam Deshpande, Gautam Mohan Deshpande, Amit Satishchand Tyagi and Pandoo Prabhakar Naig in the matter of Family Care Hospitals Limited.

## Key Points

- Recovery proceedings under RC No. 8850 of 2025 have been completed
- Four individuals named: Sowmya Gautam Deshpande, Gautam Mohan Deshpande, Amit Satishchand Tyagi and Pandoo Prabhakar Naig
- Related to Family Care Hospitals Limited matter
- Formal completion notice issued by SEBI

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced - this is a completion notice for existing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No additional compliance requirements for market participants as this relates to completion of specific recovery proceedings.

## Important Dates

- August 20, 2025: Recovery proceedings completion notice issued

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this relates to completion of recovery proceedings against specific individuals. May affect Family Care Hospitals Limited if still operating, but primarily represents closure of enforcement action rather than new regulatory burden.
