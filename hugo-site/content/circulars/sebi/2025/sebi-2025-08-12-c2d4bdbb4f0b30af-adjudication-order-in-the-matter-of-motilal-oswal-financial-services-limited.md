## Summary

SEBI has issued an adjudication order against Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited (SEBI Registration No. INZ000158836) for violations of securities laws identified during a joint inspection conducted in January 2024 covering the period from April 1, 2022 to October 31, 2023. The order addresses multiple regulatory non-compliance issues.

## Key Points

- Joint inspection by SEBI and Exchanges conducted in January 2024 for inspection period April 1, 2022 to October 31, 2023
- Adjudication proceedings initiated under Section 15-I of SEBI Act, 1992
- Show Cause Notice issued on September 27, 2024
- Adjudicating Officer appointed on August 22, 2024
- Multiple violations identified across SEBI regulations and NSE circulars

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action for existing regulatory violations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stock brokers must ensure compliance with SEBI Master Circular provisions
- Adherence to Code of Conduct under SEBI (Stock Brokers) Regulations, 1992
- Compliance with NSE circular requirements for trading members
- Proper maintenance of regulatory compliance during operations

## Important Dates

- Inspection Period: April 1, 2022 to October 31, 2023
- Joint Inspection Conducted: January 2024
- Inspection Findings Communicated: April 26, 2024
- Broker Reply Submitted: May 20, 2024
- Adjudicating Officer Appointed: August 22, 2024
- Show Cause Notice Issued: September 27, 2024

## Impact Assessment

This adjudication order demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement of compliance standards for stock brokers. The action against a major financial services company like Motilal Oswal signals regulatory vigilance and may prompt other brokers to review their compliance frameworks. The specific violations and penalties will serve as precedent for similar cases in the industry.
