## Summary

SEBI has launched a comprehensive investor awareness campaign titled 'जागरूक निवेशक सुरक्षित निवेशक' (Alert Investor Safe Investor) on All India Radio to combat trading frauds occurring on social media platforms. The campaign aims to protect investors from fraudsters operating on platforms like YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, X (Twitter), Telegram, and app stores.

## Key Points

- SEBI has observed significant investor losses through stock trading fraud on various social media platforms
- Campaign specifically targets frauds related to securities market taking place through social media platforms
- All India Radio chosen for its PAN India reach to maximize awareness
- SEBI Chairman Shri Tuhin Kanta Pandey will inaugurate the campaign on August 15, 2025
- Campaign will be broadcast across 276 stations nationwide

## Regulatory Changes

No specific regulatory changes announced. This is a public awareness initiative.

## Compliance Requirements

No direct compliance requirements. Initiative encourages investors to:
- Be cautious of trading advice on social media platforms
- Protect themselves from fraudulent schemes
- Tune into the awareness sessions on All India Radio

## Important Dates

- **August 15, 2025**: Inaugural session by SEBI Chairman
  - AIR Primary Channel/Local Radio Stations: 10:45 am - 11:00 am
  - FM Rainbow & FM Gold Stations: 10:45 am - 11:00 am
  - Vividh Bharati Network: 07:45 pm - 08:00 pm

## Impact Assessment

This campaign represents a significant public outreach effort to address the growing problem of social media-based investment frauds. The use of All India Radio's extensive network (201 Primary/Local stations, 29 FM Rainbow stations, 4 FM Gold stations, and 42 Vividh Bharati stations) ensures wide coverage across urban and rural areas. The initiative is expected to enhance investor awareness and potentially reduce fraud victims, contributing to overall market integrity and investor confidence.
