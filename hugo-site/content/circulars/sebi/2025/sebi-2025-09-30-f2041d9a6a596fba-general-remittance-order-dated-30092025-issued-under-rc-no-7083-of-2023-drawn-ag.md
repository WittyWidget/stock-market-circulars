## Summary

SEBI has issued a General Remittance Order dated 30.09.2025 under Recovery Certificate No. 7083 of 2023 against M/s Gainer Street (Proprietor: Mr. Anoop Singh Tomar). This order pertains to the recovery of dues/amounts payable to SEBI pursuant to enforcement proceedings.

## Key Points

- Order issued under Recovery Certificate No. 7083 of 2023
- Entity: M/s Gainer Street (Proprietorship firm)
- Proprietor: Mr. Anoop Singh Tomar
- Order type: General Remittance Order for recovery of dues
- Issuance date: 30th September 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement/recovery action under existing SEBI regulations and recovery procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- The entity/proprietor is required to remit the specified amount to SEBI as per the recovery certificate
- Payment must be made within the stipulated timeline mentioned in the order
- Non-compliance may lead to further enforcement actions including attachment of assets
- The entity must follow the payment instructions and banking details specified in the order

## Important Dates

- **Order Date:** 30th September 2025
- **Recovery Certificate Reference:** RC No. 7083 of 2023 (original certificate from 2023)
- Payment deadline and other specific dates are mentioned in the detailed order document

## Impact Assessment

**Entity-Specific Impact:** High - The named entity (M/s Gainer Street and its proprietor Mr. Anoop Singh Tomar) faces enforcement action for recovery of dues. Failure to comply may result in asset attachment and further legal proceedings.

**Market Impact:** Low - This is an enforcement action against a specific entity and does not have broader market implications. No impact on listed securities or general market operations.

**Compliance Impact:** This order demonstrates SEBI's continued focus on recovery of dues from entities subject to enforcement proceedings and serves as a reminder for entities to comply with SEBI orders and pay dues in a timely manner.
