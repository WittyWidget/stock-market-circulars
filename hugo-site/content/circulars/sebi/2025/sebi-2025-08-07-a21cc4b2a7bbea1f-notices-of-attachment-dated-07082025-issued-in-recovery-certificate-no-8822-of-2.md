## Summary

SEBI has issued an attachment notice dated 07.08.2025 in Recovery Certificate No. 8822 of 2025 against Mohandass Sangaiyah, who operates as the proprietor of Smart Gain Advisory Services (PAN: DDKPM6432C). This enforcement action is related to the provision of unregistered investment advisory services.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8822 of 2025 issued against Mohandass Sangaiyah
- Entity: Smart Gain Advisory Services (Proprietorship)
- PAN: DDKPM6432C
- Violation: Operating unregistered investment advisory services
- Notice Date: 07.08.2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI regulations regarding investment advisory services registration.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investment advisors must be registered with SEBI before providing advisory services
- Unregistered entities must cease investment advisory activities immediately
- Recovery proceedings initiated for penalties and dues

## Important Dates

- Attachment Notice Date: 07.08.2025
- Recovery Certificate No: 8822 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action demonstrates SEBI's continued vigilance against unregistered investment advisors. The attachment notice indicates serious compliance violations and serves as a warning to other unregistered entities. Impact is primarily on Smart Gain Advisory Services and serves as a deterrent for similar violations in the market.
