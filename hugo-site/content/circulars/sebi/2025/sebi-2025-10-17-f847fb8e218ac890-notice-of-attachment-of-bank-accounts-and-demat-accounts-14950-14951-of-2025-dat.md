## Summary

SEBI has issued Notice of Attachment for bank accounts and demat accounts (Notice Nos. 14950 & 14951 of 2025 dated October 17, 2025) against SHRAWAN (PAN: KVSPS2783B), who is a defaulter in the matter of Superior Finlease Ltd. This action is being taken under Recovery Certificate No. 8333 of 2024 to recover outstanding dues.

## Key Points

- Attachment notices issued against SHRAWAN (PAN: KVSPS2783B)
- Notice Numbers: 14950 & 14951 of 2025
- Date of Notice: October 17, 2025
- Recovery Certificate: No. 8333 of 2024
- Entity involved: Superior Finlease Ltd
- Action covers both bank accounts and demat accounts

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and depository participants are required to comply with the attachment notices
- The defaulter's accounts will be frozen/attached as per SEBI's directions
- Financial institutions must prevent any transactions in the attached accounts

## Important Dates

- **October 17, 2025**: Date of attachment notices
- Recovery proceedings initiated under Certificate No. 8333 of 2024

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Negligible - This is an individual enforcement action with no broader market implications.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to the specific defaulter and financial institutions holding their accounts. Banks and depositories must comply with attachment orders.

**Investor Impact**: None for general investors. This affects only the named defaulter (SHRAWAN) who has outstanding dues in the Superior Finlease Ltd matter.
