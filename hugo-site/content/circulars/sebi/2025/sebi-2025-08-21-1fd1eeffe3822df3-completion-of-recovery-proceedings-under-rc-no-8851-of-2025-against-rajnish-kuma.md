## Summary

SEBI has completed recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. 8851 of 2025 against Rajnish Kumar Pandey in the matter of Family Care Hospitals Limited. This represents the conclusion of enforcement action to recover dues from the individual in connection with regulatory violations.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8851 of 2025 proceedings completed
- Action taken against Rajnish Kumar Pandey
- Related to Family Care Hospitals Limited matter
- Enforcement proceedings concluded

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a completion notice for existing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No additional compliance requirements for market participants. This notice serves as confirmation of completed enforcement action.

## Important Dates

- August 21, 2025: Recovery proceedings completion announced

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this represents completion of individual enforcement action. Demonstrates SEBI's continued focus on recovering dues from regulatory violations and completing enforcement proceedings.
