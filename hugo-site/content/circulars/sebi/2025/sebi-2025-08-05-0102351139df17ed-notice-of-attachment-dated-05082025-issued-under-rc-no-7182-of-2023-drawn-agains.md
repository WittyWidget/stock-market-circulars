## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment dated August 5, 2025, under Recovery Case No. 7182 of 2023 against Sun Earth Ceramics Limited for failure to redress investor grievances. This enforcement action represents SEBI's continued efforts to protect investor interests and ensure companies comply with their obligations to address investor complaints.

## Key Points

- Notice of Attachment issued on August 5, 2025
- Recovery Case No. 7182 of 2023 initiated against Sun Earth Ceramics Limited
- Action taken due to company's failure to redress investor grievances
- Part of SEBI's enforcement mechanism to recover dues and ensure compliance

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice represents application of existing SEBI recovery and enforcement provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Sun Earth Ceramics Limited must comply with the attachment order
- Company required to address pending investor grievances
- Full compliance with SEBI's recovery proceedings under RC No. 7182 of 2023

## Important Dates

- **August 5, 2025**: Date of Notice of Attachment issuance
- **2023**: Original recovery case RC No. 7182 initiated

## Impact Assessment

This attachment notice has significant implications for Sun Earth Ceramics Limited, potentially affecting the company's assets and operations. The enforcement action highlights SEBI's commitment to investor protection and may impact investor confidence in the company. Other listed entities should take note of this action as it demonstrates SEBI's willingness to use attachment powers for non-compliance with investor grievance redressal obligations.
