## Summary

SEBI issued a final order under Section 12(3) of SEBI Act, 1992 and Regulation 27 of SEBI (Intermediaries) Regulations, 2008 against Raghunandan Capital Private Limited (SEBI Registration No. INZ000307234). The order follows a thematic inspection conducted jointly with stock exchanges (NSE, BSE, MCX, NCDEX) from November 7-8, 2023, covering the period April 1, 2022 to September 30, 2023.

## Key Points

- Thematic inspection revealed violations of Stock Brokers Regulations and applicable SEBI/Stock Exchange circulars
- Show cause notice issued on December 4, 2024 following post-enquiry proceedings
- Designated Authority conducted enquiry proceedings under Chapter V of Intermediaries Regulations
- Personal hearing granted on November 6, 2024, with additional submissions filed on November 16, 2024
- Enquiry Report submitted on November 27, 2024

## Regulatory Changes

No specific regulatory changes announced in this enforcement order.

## Compliance Requirements

Stock brokers must ensure compliance with:
- SEBI (Stock Brokers) Regulations, 1992
- SEBI (Intermediaries) Regulations, 2008
- Applicable SEBI circulars
- Stock exchange circulars

## Important Dates

- Inspection Period: April 1, 2022 to September 30, 2023
- Inspection Conducted: November 7-8, 2023
- Findings Communicated: January 3, 2024
- Noticee Response: April 29, 2024
- Designated Authority Appointed: July 29, 2024
- Pre-Enquiry SCN: August 19, 2024
- Reply Filed: October 24, 2024
- Personal Hearing: November 6, 2024
- Additional Submissions: November 16, 2024
- Enquiry Report: November 27, 2024
- Final SCN: December 4, 2024

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action demonstrates SEBI's continued focus on ensuring intermediary compliance through thematic inspections. The order serves as a reminder to all stock brokers to maintain strict adherence to regulatory requirements and circulars issued by SEBI and stock exchanges.
