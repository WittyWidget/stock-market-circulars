## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated October 9, 2025, under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 8918 of 2025. This notice is drawn against Laxman Dhananjay Gunde in the matter of Generic Engineering Construction and Projects Limited. The issuance of a Recovery Certificate indicates that SEBI is taking formal recovery proceedings to collect outstanding dues or penalties from the named individual in connection with the company.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC No. 8918 of 2025
- Date of Notice: October 9, 2025
- Individual Named: Laxman Dhananjay Gunde
- Company Involved: Generic Engineering Construction and Projects Limited
- Nature: Notice of Demand under recovery proceedings
- Issued By: Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI)

## Regulatory Changes

This document represents an enforcement action rather than a regulatory change. SEBI has initiated Recovery Certificate proceedings under its powers to recover dues, which typically follows:

- Previous enforcement orders or penalties
- Non-compliance with payment of dues
- Formal demand for payment
- Recovery proceedings initiated when voluntary payment is not made

## Compliance Requirements

- The individual named (Laxman Dhananjay Gunde) is required to respond to the Notice of Demand
- Payment of the demanded amount as specified in the Recovery Certificate
- Failure to comply may result in:
  - Attachment of bank accounts
  - Attachment of demat accounts
  - Attachment of other assets
  - Further enforcement proceedings
  - Potential arrest warrant in case of willful default
