## Summary

SEBI has completed recovery proceedings under RC No. 8848 of 2025 against Family Care Hospitals Limited. This circular serves as formal notification of the conclusion of enforcement action taken against the entity.

## Key Points

- Recovery proceedings under RC No. 8848 of 2025 have been completed
- Proceedings were against Family Care Hospitals Limited
- SEBI has formally concluded the enforcement action
- Circular serves as official notification of completion

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a notification of completed enforcement proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements specified in this completion notice.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: August 20, 2025
- Recovery proceedings completion: As notified in this circular

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this represents the conclusion of existing enforcement proceedings rather than initiation of new action. The completion suggests resolution of the matter against Family Care Hospitals Limited.
