## Summary

SEBI has announced the completion of recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. RC7968 of 2024 against Mani Marketing and Holdings Pvt. Ltd (PAN: AABCM7440C). The recovery action was initiated in connection with matters related to illiquid stock options.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC7968 of 2024
- Entity: Mani Marketing and Holdings Pvt. Ltd
- PAN: AABCM7440C
- Matter: Illiquid Stock Options
- Status: Recovery proceedings completed
- Date of completion notice: October 9, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a completion notice for an existing recovery proceeding.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. The recovery proceedings against the named entity have been completed.

## Important Dates

- **October 9, 2025**: Notice of completion of recovery proceedings published

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to the completion of recovery proceedings against a specific private limited company. The completion indicates that SEBI has successfully concluded its enforcement action related to illiquid stock options matter. This serves as a record of regulatory enforcement completion and demonstrates SEBI's ongoing efforts to recover dues in cases of securities market violations.
