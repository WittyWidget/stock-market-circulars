## Summary

SEBI has issued an adjudication order against Dadabhoy Bothe (PAN: ABXPB9117B) for violating disclosure norms under SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011. The violation pertains to delayed disclosure of sale of 90,000 shares (2.12% stake) in Hypersoft Technologies Limited by 21 days. The proceedings were initiated following SEBI's examination of a Draft Letter of Offer for an open offer by acquirers.

## Key Points

- Dadabhoy Bothe failed to disclose sale of 90,000 shares (2.12%) within prescribed timeline
- Disclosure was delayed by 21 days, violating Regulation 29(2) read with Regulation 29(3) of SAST Regulations
- Adjudication proceedings initiated under Section 15-I of SEBI Act, 1992
- Show Cause Notice issued on May 21, 2025 for the alleged violations
- BSE provided confirmation of the violation through multiple communications from September 2024 to January 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This order reinforces existing disclosure requirements under SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shareholders must comply with timely disclosure requirements when selling substantial stakes
- Disclosure must be made within prescribed timelines under Regulation 29(2) and 29(3) of SAST Regulations
- Failure to comply attracts penalties under Section 15A(b) of SEBI Act, 1992

## Important Dates

- April 15, 2025: Adjudicating Officer appointed
- May 21, 2025: Show Cause Notice issued
- May 23, 2025: Notice served to the noticee
- September 2024 - January 2025: BSE communications confirming violation

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action demonstrates SEBI's continued focus on ensuring compliance with disclosure norms in takeover situations. The penalty serves as a deterrent for delayed disclosures that can impact market transparency and investor decision-making. The case highlights the importance of timely compliance with substantial acquisition disclosure requirements.
