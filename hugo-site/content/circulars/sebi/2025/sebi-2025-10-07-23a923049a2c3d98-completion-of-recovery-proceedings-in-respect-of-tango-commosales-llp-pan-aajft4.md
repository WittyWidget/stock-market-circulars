## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice regarding the completion of recovery proceedings against Tango Commosales LLP (PAN: AAJFT4529J) under Recovery Certificate No. 8672 of 2025. This matter is related to Optiemus Infracom Limited and represents the conclusion of enforcement and recovery actions initiated by the regulator.

## Key Points

- Recovery proceedings completed against Tango Commosales LLP
- Entity PAN: AAJFT4529J
- Recovery Certificate Number: 8672 of 2025
- Related to Optiemus Infracom Limited matter
- Administrative closure of recovery process

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a procedural notice regarding completion of existing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This notice confirms the conclusion of recovery proceedings against the named entity.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 7, 2025
- Recovery proceedings concluded as of the notice date

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market impact as it pertains to the administrative conclusion of recovery proceedings against a specific entity. The impact is primarily confined to Tango Commosales LLP and parties involved in the Optiemus Infracom Limited matter. No broader market or regulatory implications are evident from this completion notice.
