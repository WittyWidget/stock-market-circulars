## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand (NoD) under Recovery Certificate No. 8836 of 2025 against Chetan Yashwant Shukla for operating unregistered investment advisory services. This enforcement action is part of SEBI's ongoing efforts to curb unauthorized financial service providers in the securities market.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8836 of 2025 issued against Chetan Yashwant Shukla
- Violation relates to providing unregistered investment advisory services
- Action taken under SEBI's recovery proceedings framework
- Part of regulatory enforcement against unauthorized market participants

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI regulations governing investment advisory services registration requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investment advisors must register with SEBI before providing advisory services
- Unauthorized investment advisory services are prohibited
- Market participants should verify registration status of investment advisors before engaging services
- Penalty recovery proceedings can be initiated against violators

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate issued: 2025
- Notice publication date: July 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action reinforces SEBI's commitment to protecting investors from unregistered investment advisors. While targeting an individual practitioner, it sends a broader market message about compliance requirements. Limited direct market impact but contributes to regulatory deterrence against unauthorized financial service providers. Investors are reminded to verify advisor registration status before seeking investment advice.
