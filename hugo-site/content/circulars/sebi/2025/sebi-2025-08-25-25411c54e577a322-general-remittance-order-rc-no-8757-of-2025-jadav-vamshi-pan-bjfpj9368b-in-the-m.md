## Summary

SEBI has issued General Remittance Order RC No. 8757 of 2025 against Jadav Vamshi (PAN: BJFPJ9368B), who was operating under the name 'Baap of Chart', for conducting unregistered investment advisory activities in violation of SEBI regulations.

## Key Points

- Enforcement action against individual conducting unregistered investment advisory services
- Subject was operating under the brand name 'Baap of Chart'
- Recovery certificate issued for monetary penalties/disgorgement
- Part of SEBI's ongoing crackdown on unregistered investment advisors

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced - this is an enforcement action under existing SEBI (Investment Advisers) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- All investment advisory activities must be conducted only by SEBI-registered Investment Advisers
- Individuals providing investment advice for consideration must obtain proper SEBI registration
- Unregistered entities must cease advisory activities immediately

## Important Dates

- Order Date: August 25, 2025
- Recovery proceedings initiated as per the remittance order

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action reinforces SEBI's commitment to regulating the investment advisory space and protecting investors from unregistered advisors. While specific to one individual, it serves as a warning to others operating without proper registration. The action has limited direct market impact but strengthens the regulatory framework for investor protection.
