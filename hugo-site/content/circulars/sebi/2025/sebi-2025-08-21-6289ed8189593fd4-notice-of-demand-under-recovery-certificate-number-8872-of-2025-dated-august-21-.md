## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. 8872 of 2025 dated August 21, 2025, against Hari Om Singh (PAN: BGBPS4181A) as a defaulter in connection with investigation into trading activities in the scrip of Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8872 of 2025 issued against Hari Om Singh
- Defaulter PAN: BGBPS4181A
- Related to investigation in Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited trading activities
- Notice of demand issued under recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No specific regulatory changes announced in this recovery certificate notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Hari Om Singh must comply with the recovery certificate demands
- Payment obligations as specified in the recovery certificate
- Adherence to SEBI recovery proceedings

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate dated: August 21, 2025
- Specific payment deadlines would be detailed in the full recovery certificate

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact on broader market operations as this is an individual enforcement action. May serve as deterrent for similar trading violations in Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited or other securities.
