## Summary

Shri Amit Pradhan has taken charge as Executive Director at the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) in Mumbai. He will oversee the Legal Affairs Department (Vertical 1) and Prosecution & Settlement Department (PSD).

## Key Points

- Pradhan brings 28 years of experience in legal & regulatory affairs, enforcement, and policy development
- Previously served as Chief General Manager and Regional Director at SEBI's Northern Regional Office in New Delhi
- Has extensive experience across SEBI, IBBI, and CCI
- Previously led Legal Affairs Department at SEBI Head Office, providing legal guidance on policy matters
- Handled quasi-judicial work as Designated Authority & Adjudicating Officer
- Science graduate with law degree from Rohilkhand University (1989)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced - this is a personnel appointment.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements specified.

## Important Dates

- August 22, 2025: Date Shri Amit Pradhan took charge as Executive Director

## Impact Assessment

This is an internal organizational change with no direct impact on market operations or trading activities. The appointment ensures continuity in SEBI's legal affairs and enforcement functions under experienced leadership.
