SEBI has announced the completion of recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. RC8355 of 2024 dated November 19, 2024 against Sanjay Shastri HUF (PAN: AAYHS3088Q) in the matter of Illiquid Stock Options.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC8355 of 2024
- Original Issue Date: November 19, 2024
- Entity: Sanjay Shastri HUF
- PAN: AAYHS3088Q
- Matter: Illiquid Stock Options
- Status: Recovery proceedings completed

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a completion notice for an existing recovery action.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. This notice confirms that the recovery proceedings have been completed for the named entity.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Issue Date: November 19, 2024
- Completion Notice Date: October 16, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact as it pertains to the completion of recovery proceedings against a specific HUF entity. It serves as an informational disclosure regarding the conclusion of SEBI's enforcement action in the matter of illiquid stock options.
