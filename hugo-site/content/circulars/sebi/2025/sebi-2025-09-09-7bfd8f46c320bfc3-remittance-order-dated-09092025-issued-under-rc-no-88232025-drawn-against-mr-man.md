## Summary

SEBI has issued a remittance order dated 09.09.2025 under Recovery Certificate No. 8823/2025 against Mr. Manish Bhardwaj (PAN: CEUPB0805G) in connection with providing unregistered investment advisory services.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8823/2025 issued against Mr. Manish Bhardwaj
- Individual PAN: CEUPB0805G
- Related to unregistered investment advisory services
- Remittance order dated September 9, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

This order reinforces SEBI's stance on unregistered investment advisory activities and demonstrates ongoing enforcement actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investment advisors must be registered with SEBI before providing advisory services
- Individuals providing investment advice without registration face recovery proceedings
- Market participants should verify registration status of investment advisors

## Important Dates

- **Remittance Order Date:** September 9, 2025
- **Recovery Certificate:** RC No. 8823/2025

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action serves as a reminder to market participants about the importance of using only SEBI-registered investment advisors. It demonstrates SEBI's continued efforts to protect investors from unregistered advisory services and maintain market integrity.
