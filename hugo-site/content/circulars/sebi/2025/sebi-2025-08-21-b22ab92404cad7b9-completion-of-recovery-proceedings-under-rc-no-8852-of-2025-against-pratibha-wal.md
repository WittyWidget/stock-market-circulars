## Summary

SEBI has completed recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. 8852 of 2025 against Pratibha Walinjkar in connection with the matter of Family Care Hospitals Limited. This represents the conclusion of enforcement action taken by the regulator.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8852 of 2025 proceedings have been completed
- Individual involved: Pratibha Walinjkar
- Connected to Family Care Hospitals Limited matter
- Enforcement action has been concluded

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a completion notice for existing enforcement proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. The recovery proceedings have been completed as per existing regulatory framework.

## Important Dates

- August 21, 2025: Completion of recovery proceedings announced

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this relates to completion of individual enforcement proceedings. The conclusion of recovery proceedings provides closure to the regulatory action against the individual in connection with Family Care Hospitals Limited.
