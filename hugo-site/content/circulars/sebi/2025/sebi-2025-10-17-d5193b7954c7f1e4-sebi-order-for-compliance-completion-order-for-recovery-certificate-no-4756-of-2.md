## Summary

SEBI has issued a Completion Order for Recovery Certificate No. 4756 of 2022 against Siddharth J Chaturvedi in the matter of Brijlaxmi Leasing and Finance Limited. This order marks the conclusion of recovery proceedings initiated under SEBI's enforcement and recovery framework.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4756 of 2022 has been completed
- Order pertains to Siddharth J Chaturvedi
- Related to the matter of Brijlaxmi Leasing and Finance Limited
- Completion order signifies conclusion of recovery proceedings
- Document published on October 17, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action completion notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- The completion order concludes the recovery proceedings against Siddharth J Chaturvedi
- No further compliance actions required as the recovery certificate has been completed
- This serves as formal closure of Recovery Certificate No. 4756 of 2022

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Number: 4756 of 2022
- Completion Order Date: October 17, 2025
- Publication Date: October 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This completion order has minimal market impact as it is specific to an individual recovery case. The order indicates that SEBI has concluded its recovery proceedings in this matter. The impact is limited to the parties directly involved in the Brijlaxmi Leasing and Finance Limited case and does not affect broader market operations or other market participants.
