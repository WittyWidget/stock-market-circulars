## Summary

SEBI has issued a compliance completion order for Recovery Certificate No. 4758 of 2022 against Mr. Ankur J. Chaturvedi in connection with Brijlaxmi Leasing and Finance Limited. This order confirms the completion of compliance proceedings related to the recovery certificate.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4758 of 2022
- Individual involved: Mr. Ankur J. Chaturvedi
- Company matter: Brijlaxmi Leasing and Finance Limited
- Order type: Compliance Completion Order
- Indicates closure of enforcement proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a completion order confirming compliance with existing recovery certificate proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

The compliance requirements under Recovery Certificate No. 4758 of 2022 have been completed as per this order. No further compliance actions are specified.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: October 27, 2025
- Recovery Certificate issued: 2022

## Impact Assessment

This order has limited market impact as it relates to the completion of compliance proceedings against a specific individual in relation to a recovery certificate. The completion order indicates that the matter has been resolved and no further action is required under this recovery certificate.
