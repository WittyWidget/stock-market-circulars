## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated 07.08.2025 under RC no. 8856 of 2025 against Lucy Maqbul Massey [PAN: AMBPM4077E] in connection with matters related to Family Care Hospitals Limited. This represents an enforcement action by SEBI requiring specific compliance or payment from the named individual.

## Key Points

- Notice issued under Registration Certificate number 8856 of 2025
- Directed against Lucy Maqbul Massey (PAN: AMBPM4077E)
- Related to Family Care Hospitals Limited
- Formal demand notice indicating regulatory enforcement action
- Part of SEBI's ongoing regulatory oversight and compliance enforcement

## Regulatory Changes

This notice represents enforcement of existing regulations rather than introduction of new regulatory changes. It demonstrates SEBI's active monitoring and enforcement of compliance requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Lucy Maqbul Massey must respond to the demand notice as specified
- Compliance with the terms and conditions outlined in the notice
- Payment or other remedial action as demanded by SEBI
- Adherence to timelines specified in the notice

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 07.08.2025
- Response deadlines and payment schedules would be specified in the detailed notice document

This enforcement action has limited direct market impact as it targets a specific individual in relation to a particular company matter.
