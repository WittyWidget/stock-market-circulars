## Summary

SEBI has issued Recovery Certificate No. 8879 of 2025 dated 26.08.2025 against Satish Agrawal HUF (PAN: AAJHS3607N) in connection with trading violations in illiquid stock options at BSE. This is an enforcement action for recovery of dues.

## Key Points

- Recovery certificate issued against Satish Agrawal HUF
- PAN: AAJHS3607N
- Violation relates to trading in illiquid stock options at BSE
- Certificate number: 8879 of 2025
- Date of issue: 26.08.2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- The entity named in the recovery certificate must comply with the demand
- Payment of dues as specified in the recovery certificate
- Compliance with any additional conditions specified in the notice

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Date: 26.08.2025
- Payment deadline: As specified in the detailed notice

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact on broader market operations. This is a specific enforcement action against one entity for trading violations in illiquid stock options. Market participants should ensure compliance with trading regulations, particularly regarding illiquid securities.
