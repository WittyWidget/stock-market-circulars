## Summary

SEBI has issued Certificate No. RC8922 of 2025 containing a Notice of Demand against Smart Infraproperties Pvt. Ltd. (PAN: AAQCS6937F) in connection with the matter of Cressanda Solutions Limited. This is a recovery proceeding initiated by SEBI to collect outstanding amounts.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC8922 of 2025
- Entity Against Whom Demand Issued: Smart Infraproperties Pvt. Ltd.
- PAN of Entity: AAQCS6937F
- Related Matter: Cressanda Solutions Limited
- Nature: Notice of Demand under SEBI recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Smart Infraproperties Pvt. Ltd. is required to:
- Pay the demanded amount as specified in the notice
- Comply with the payment timeline provided in the recovery certificate
- Respond to SEBI's recovery proceedings as per applicable regulations

## Important Dates

- Certificate Issue Date: October 14, 2025
- Payment deadline and other specific dates are contained in the detailed notice document

## Impact Assessment

**Entity-Specific Impact:** High impact on Smart Infraproperties Pvt. Ltd., which faces enforcement action and mandatory payment obligations.

**Market Impact:** Limited broader market impact as this is an entity-specific recovery proceeding.

**Compliance Implications:** Demonstrates SEBI's active enforcement of recovery proceedings against entities involved in regulatory violations related to Cressanda Solutions Limited matter.
