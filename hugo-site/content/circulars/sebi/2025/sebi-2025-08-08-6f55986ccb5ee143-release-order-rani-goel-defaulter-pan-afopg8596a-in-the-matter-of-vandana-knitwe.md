## Summary

SEBI has issued a release order for Rani Goel (PAN: AFOPG8596A), who was designated as a defaulter in recovery proceedings related to Vandana Knitwear Limited under Certificate No. 2255 of 2019.

## Key Points

- Release order issued for defaulter Rani Goel (PAN: AFOPG8596A)
- Related to recovery proceedings in the matter of Vandana Knitwear Limited
- Certificate No. 2255 of 2019 referenced
- Individual enforcement action completed

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action completion notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants. This relates to completion of individual enforcement proceedings.

## Important Dates

- Release order date: August 8, 2025
- Original certificate date: 2019

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this relates to individual defaulter proceedings completion. No direct impact on trading operations or market participants beyond the specific individual mentioned.
