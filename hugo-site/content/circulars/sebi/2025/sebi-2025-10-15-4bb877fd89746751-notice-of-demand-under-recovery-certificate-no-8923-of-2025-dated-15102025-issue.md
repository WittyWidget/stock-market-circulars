## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 8923 of 2025 dated October 15, 2025, against Nilesh Malshi Savla (PAN: AAEPS0827P) and Meena Nilesh Savla (PAN: AKUPS3442E) in the matter of RKD Agri & Retail Limited. This represents an enforcement action for recovery of outstanding amounts.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8923 of 2025 issued on October 15, 2025
- Notice directed against two individuals: Nilesh Malshi Savla and Meena Nilesh Savla
- Related to the matter of RKD Agri & Retail Limited
- Formal demand notice issued by SEBI for recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Named individuals (Nilesh Malshi Savla and Meena Nilesh Savla) are required to comply with the demand notice
- Payment of outstanding amounts as specified in the recovery certificate
- Non-compliance may lead to further enforcement actions including attachment of assets

## Important Dates

- **Recovery Certificate Date:** October 15, 2025
- **Notice Date:** October 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Severity:** High - Recovery certificate issuance is a serious enforcement action indicating unpaid dues or penalties

**Impact on Individuals:** High - Direct financial liability on named individuals with potential for asset attachment if demand is not met

**Market Impact:** Medium - May affect investor confidence in RKD Agri & Retail Limited and raise concerns about compliance practices

**Precedent:** Recovery proceedings demonstrate SEBI's commitment to enforcement and collection of dues from non-compliant entities and individuals
