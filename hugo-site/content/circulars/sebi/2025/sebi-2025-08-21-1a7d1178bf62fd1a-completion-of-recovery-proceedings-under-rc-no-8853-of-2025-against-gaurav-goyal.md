## Summary

SEBI has completed recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. 8853 of 2025 against Gaurav Goyal in the matter of Family Care Hospitals Limited. This represents the conclusion of enforcement action taken by the securities regulator.

## Key Points

- Recovery proceedings under RC No. 8853 of 2025 have been completed
- Proceedings were against Gaurav Goyal
- Matter relates to Family Care Hospitals Limited
- Notice dated August 21, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a completion notice for existing recovery proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. The recovery proceedings have been completed as per existing regulatory framework.

## Important Dates

- August 21, 2025: Completion of recovery proceedings announced

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a completion notice for recovery proceedings. The matter appears to be concluded with no ongoing implications for market participants or Family Care Hospitals Limited operations.
