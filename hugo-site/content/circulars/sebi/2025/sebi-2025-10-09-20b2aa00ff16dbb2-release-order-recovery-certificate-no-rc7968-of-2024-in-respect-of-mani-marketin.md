- Entity: Mani Marketing and Holdings Pvt. Ltd
- PAN: AABCM7440C
- Matter: Illiquid Stock Options

Compliance requirements are specific to Mani Marketing and Holdings Pvt. Ltd as detailed in the recovery certificate proceedings.
