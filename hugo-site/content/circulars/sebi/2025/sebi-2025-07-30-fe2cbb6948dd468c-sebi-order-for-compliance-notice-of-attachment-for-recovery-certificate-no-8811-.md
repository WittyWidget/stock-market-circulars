## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment for Recovery Certificate No. 8811 of 2025 against BIR Finance Private Limited in connection with illiquid stocks options on BSE. This enforcement action indicates serious compliance violations requiring immediate regulatory intervention.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8811 of 2025 issued against BIR Finance Private Limited
- Matter relates to illiquid stocks options on Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE)
- Notice of attachment indicates asset attachment for recovery purposes
- Enforcement action demonstrates severity of compliance breach

## Regulatory Changes

This order represents enforcement of existing regulations rather than new regulatory changes. It demonstrates SEBI's continued focus on market integrity and compliance with options trading regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- BIR Finance Private Limited must comply with the recovery certificate terms
- Asset attachment may be implemented for recovery purposes
- Company must address underlying compliance violations that led to this action
- Full compliance with SEBI regulations regarding options trading required

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Date: 2025 (Certificate No. 8811)
- Order Publication: July 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

High impact enforcement action with significant implications for BIR Finance Private Limited. Asset attachment notices indicate serious financial consequences and potential operational restrictions. Market participants should note SEBI's strict enforcement approach regarding options trading compliance violations.
