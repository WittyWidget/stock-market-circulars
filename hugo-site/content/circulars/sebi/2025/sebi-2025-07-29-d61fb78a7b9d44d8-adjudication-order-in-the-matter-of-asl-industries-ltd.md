## Summary

SEBI has issued an adjudication order against ASL Enterprises Limited (PAN: AACCA5111D), a promoter of ASL Industries Limited, for failing to submit mandatory disclosures under Regulation 29(2) of SAST Regulations on two occasions during the examination of an open offer made by Skybridge Incap Advisors LLP.

## Key Points

- ASL Enterprises Limited failed to comply with disclosure requirements under SAST Regulation 29(2)
- Violations occurred during SEBI's examination of Draft Letter of Offer (DLOO) for open offer to ASL Industries Limited shareholders
- Adjudication proceedings initiated under Section 15-I of SEBI Act, 1992
- Show Cause Notice issued on April 29, 2025
- Acquirer in the matter is Skybridge Incap Advisors LLP
- Manager to the Offer is Swaraj Shares and Securities Private Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action for existing SAST compliance requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Promoters must submit timely disclosures under Regulation 29(2) of SAST Regulations during takeover proceedings
- Proper compliance with disclosure obligations during open offer processes
- Adherence to all provisions under Chapter V of SEBI SAST Regulations, 2011

## Important Dates

- March 10, 2025: Adjudicating Officer appointed
- March 18, 2025: Appointment communicated to AO
- April 29, 2025: Show Cause Notice issued
- April 30, 2024: SCN served via digitally signed email and Speed Post

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action reinforces SEBI's commitment to ensuring compliance with takeover regulations and disclosure requirements. While specific to ASL Enterprises Limited, it serves as a reminder to all promoters and entities involved in takeover transactions about the importance of timely regulatory disclosures.
