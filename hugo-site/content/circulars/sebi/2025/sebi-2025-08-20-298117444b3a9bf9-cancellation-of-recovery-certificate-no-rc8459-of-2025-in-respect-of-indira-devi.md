## Summary

SEBI has issued a cancellation order for Recovery Certificate No. RC8459 of 2025 that was issued against Indira Devi Jain (PAN: ACPPJ2461H) in relation to a matter concerning illiquid stock options.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC8459 of 2025 has been cancelled
- Certificate was issued against Indira Devi Jain (PAN: ACPPJ2461H)
- Matter relates to illiquid stock options
- Administrative action by SEBI

## Regulatory Changes

No broader regulatory changes. This is a specific administrative action cancelling a recovery certificate.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants. This is an individual-specific administrative action.

## Important Dates

- Order date: August 20, 2025
- Recovery Certificate originally issued: 2025 (specific date not provided)

## Impact Assessment

This cancellation has minimal market impact as it relates to a specific individual case regarding illiquid stock options. The action is administrative in nature and does not affect broader market operations or regulatory framework.
