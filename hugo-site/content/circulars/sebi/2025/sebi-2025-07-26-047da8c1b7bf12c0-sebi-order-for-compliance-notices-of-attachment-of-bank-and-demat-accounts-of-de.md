## Summary

SEBI has issued an enforcement order for the attachment of bank and demat accounts of In House Productions Limited LLP (PAN: AAACV1685K) under Recovery Case No. 4833 of 2022. This action is part of recovery proceedings against the entity for non-compliance with regulatory requirements.

## Key Points

- Entity: In House Productions Limited LLP (PAN: AAACV1685K)
- Recovery Case Number: RC No. 4833 of 2022
- Action: Attachment of bank and demat accounts
- Nature: Enforcement action for recovery of dues/penalties
- Status: Defaulter classification under SEBI proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

This order represents enforcement of existing SEBI regulations related to recovery proceedings and compliance obligations. No new regulatory framework introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- All banks and depositories must comply with the attachment order
- Freezing of specified bank accounts and demat accounts
- Restriction on transactions until compliance or settlement
- Banks required to provide account details and balances to SEBI

## Important Dates

- Order Date: July 25, 2025
- Effective immediately upon issuance
- Recovery proceedings under case RC No. 4833 of 2022 ongoing

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited direct market impact as this affects a specific LLP entity rather than broader market participants.

**Operational Impact**: High impact on the defaulting entity with complete freezing of financial accounts, effectively halting business operations until compliance.

**Precedential Value**: Demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement efforts in recovery proceedings and serves as deterrent for other potential defaulters.
