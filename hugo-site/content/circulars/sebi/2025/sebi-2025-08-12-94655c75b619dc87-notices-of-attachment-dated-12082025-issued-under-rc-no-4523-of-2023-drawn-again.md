## Summary

SEBI has issued attachment notices dated August 12, 2025, under Recovery Certificate No. 4523 of 2023 against Nimbus Industries Limited for non-redressal of investor grievances. This represents enforcement action to recover dues from the company.

## Key Points

- Attachment notices issued on August 12, 2025
- Related to Recovery Certificate No. 4523 of 2023
- Target company: Nimbus Industries Limited
- Cause: Non-redressal of investor grievances
- Document is a scanned PDF containing 4 pages

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced - this is an enforcement action under existing investor protection framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must maintain proper investor grievance redressal mechanisms
- Timely resolution of investor complaints is mandatory
- Non-compliance can lead to recovery proceedings and asset attachment

## Important Dates

- **August 12, 2025**: Date of attachment notices
- **2023**: Year of original Recovery Certificate No. 4523

## Impact Assessment

**High Impact**: Attachment notices represent serious enforcement action that can freeze company assets and operations. This affects Nimbus Industries Limited's ability to conduct normal business and indicates significant regulatory non-compliance regarding investor protection obligations.
