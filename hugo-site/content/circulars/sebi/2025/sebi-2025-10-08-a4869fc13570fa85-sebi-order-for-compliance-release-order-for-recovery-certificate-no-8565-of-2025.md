## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. 8565 of 2025 against Ajaykumar Pujara and Natu Vegda in the matter of Manaksia Coated Metals and Industries Limited. This order releases the recovery proceedings against the named individuals.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8565 of 2025 has been released
- Individuals involved: Ajaykumar Pujara and Natu Vegda
- Matter relates to Manaksia Coated Metals and Industries Limited
- This is a compliance release order issued by SEBI

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a case-specific compliance order.

## Compliance Requirements

No ongoing compliance requirements as this is a release order indicating completion of recovery proceedings.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: October 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The release order has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to specific individuals in a concluded recovery proceeding. The order indicates resolution of the recovery certificate proceedings against the named parties in relation to Manaksia Coated Metals and Industries Limited.
