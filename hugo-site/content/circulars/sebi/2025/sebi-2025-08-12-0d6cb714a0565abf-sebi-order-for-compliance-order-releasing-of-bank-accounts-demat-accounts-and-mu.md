## Summary

SEBI has issued a compliance order for the release of bank accounts, demat accounts and mutual fund folios of Mr. Nemichand Kasturchand Jain (PAN: ABBPJ2880M) in connection with Recovery Certificate No. 8395 of 2024.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8395 of 2024 proceedings have been completed
- Release order covers bank accounts, demat accounts and mutual fund folios
- Individual enforcement action against Mr. Nemichand Kasturchand Jain
- PAN number: ABBPJ2880M

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks, depositories and mutual funds to comply with the release order
- Lifting of restrictions on specified accounts and folios
- Implementation of SEBI's compliance directives

## Important Dates

- Order date: August 12, 2025
- Recovery Certificate reference: RC No. 8395 of 2024

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement action with no broader market implications. The order represents the conclusion of recovery proceedings against a specific individual and the subsequent release of frozen assets.
