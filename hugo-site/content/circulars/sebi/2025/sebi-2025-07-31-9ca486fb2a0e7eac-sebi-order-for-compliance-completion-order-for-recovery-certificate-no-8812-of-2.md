## Summary

SEBI has issued a completion order for Recovery Certificate No. 8812 of 2025 against Mr. Priyank Dineshbhai Shah for conducting unregistered investment advisory activities. This enforcement action demonstrates SEBI's continued efforts to regulate investment advisory services and ensure compliance with registration requirements.

## Key Points

- Recovery certificate issued against Mr. Priyank Dineshbhai Shah
- Violation relates to unregistered investment advisory activities
- Certificate number: 8812 of 2025
- Order represents completion of enforcement proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This order enforces existing requirements for investment advisor registration under SEBI regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- All investment advisors must be registered with SEBI before providing advisory services
- Unregistered advisory activities are subject to enforcement action
- Recovery proceedings may be initiated for violations

## Important Dates

- Order date: July 31, 2025
- Recovery certificate completion order issued

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action serves as a reminder to market participants about the importance of proper registration before providing investment advisory services. It reinforces SEBI's regulatory oversight and enforcement capabilities in the investment advisory space.
