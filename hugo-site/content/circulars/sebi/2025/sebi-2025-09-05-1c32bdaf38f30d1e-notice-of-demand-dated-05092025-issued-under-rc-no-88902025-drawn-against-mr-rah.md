## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated September 5, 2025, under Recovery Certificate No. 8890/2025 against Mr. Rahul Goswami (PAN: AXUPG5665L) for providing unregistered investment advisory services in violation of SEBI regulations.

## Key Points

- Demand notice issued against Mr. Rahul Goswami for unregistered investment advisory activities
- Recovery Certificate No. 8890/2025 has been drawn for enforcement action
- Individual found to be providing investment advisory services without proper SEBI registration
- Notice serves as formal demand for compliance and potential penalties

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI regulations regarding investment advisory services registration requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investment advisors must obtain proper SEBI registration before offering advisory services
- Individuals providing investment advice must comply with SEBI's Investment Advisers Regulations
- Unregistered advisory services are subject to enforcement action and penalties
- Market participants should ensure all advisory services are provided by registered entities

## Important Dates

- **September 5, 2025**: Date of demand notice issuance
- Payment and compliance deadlines would be specified in the full notice document

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is an individual enforcement action with no direct impact on market operations or trading activities.

**Regulatory Impact**: Medium - Reinforces SEBI's commitment to enforcing registration requirements for investment advisory services and serves as a deterrent for unregistered operators.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Primarily affects the individual named in the notice, but serves as a warning to investors to verify advisor registration status before engaging services.
