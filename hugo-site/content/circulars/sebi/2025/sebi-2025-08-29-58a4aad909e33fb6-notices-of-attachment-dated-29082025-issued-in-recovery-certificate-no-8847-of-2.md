## Summary

SEBI has issued notices of attachment dated 29.08.2025 under Recovery Certificate No. 8847 of 2025 against Sanjay Jethalal Soni, Krupa Sanjay Soni, and J M Soni Consultancy in connection with proceedings related to Parichay Investment Limited. This enforcement action is part of SEBI's debt recovery process.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8847 of 2025 issued against multiple individuals and entities
- Attachment notices dated 29.08.2025
- Involves Sanjay Jethalal Soni, Krupa Sanjay Soni, and J M Soni Consultancy
- Connected to Parichay Investment Limited matter
- Part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes announced. This is an enforcement action under existing recovery procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Named individuals and entities must comply with attachment notices
- Assets specified in the notices are subject to attachment
- Non-compliance may result in further enforcement action
- Parties may need to engage with recovery officers as specified

## Important Dates

- **Attachment Notice Date**: 29.08.2025
- **Recovery Certificate**: No. 8847 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

This represents significant enforcement action that could result in asset attachment and financial recovery. The high-impact nature stems from the serious implications for the named parties and potential market participants who may face similar recovery proceedings for non-compliance with SEBI regulations. This action demonstrates SEBI's commitment to enforcing compliance and recovering dues.
