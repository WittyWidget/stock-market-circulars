## Summary

SEBI issued Settlement Order No. SO/AN/SM/2025-26/8339 against Mr. Ankit Jain for suspected insider trading in AVG Logistics Limited. Mr. Jain, a designated person of AVG, executed contra trades resulting in unlawful profit of Rs. 1,39,361.35, violating Clause 10 of the Minimum Standards for Code of Conduct under SEBI (PIT) Regulations, 2015.

## Key Points

- Settlement Application No. 8339/2025 filed by Mr. Ankit Jain (PAN: AFNPJ5441E)
- Show Cause Notice issued on January 10, 2025
- Internal Committee meeting held on April 3, 2025
- Applicant transferred unlawful gains as part of settlement
- Settlement processed under SEBI (Settlement Proceedings) Regulations, 2018

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Settlement reinforces existing insider trading prohibitions under SEBI (PIT) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Designated persons must comply with Code of Conduct provisions
- Prohibition on contra trades by designated persons
- Proper disclosure and reporting of trades by insiders
- Transfer of unlawful gains in settlement proceedings

## Important Dates

- January 6, 2025: Adjudicating Officer appointed
- January 10, 2025: Show Cause Notice issued
- January 28, 2025: Settlement application filed
- April 3, 2025: Internal Committee meeting conducted

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact case involving individual violation with modest financial implications. Reinforces SEBI's continued enforcement of insider trading regulations and availability of settlement mechanism for designated person violations.
