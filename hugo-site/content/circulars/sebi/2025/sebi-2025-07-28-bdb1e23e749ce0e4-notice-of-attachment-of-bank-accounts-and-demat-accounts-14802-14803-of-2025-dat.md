## Summary

SEBI has issued notices 14802 & 14803 of 2025 for attachment of bank accounts and demat accounts belonging to Columbia Sales (PAN: DAIPS9917R) as part of recovery proceedings related to the IPO of Eco Friendly Food Processing Park Limited under Recovery Certificate No. 8027 of 2024.

## Key Points

- Entity: Columbia Sales (Defaulter)
- PAN: DAIPS9917R
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8027 of 2024
- Related IPO: Eco Friendly Food Processing Park Limited
- Notice Numbers: 14802 & 14803 of 2025
- Notice Date: July 28, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing recovery procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and depositories must comply with attachment orders
- Frozen accounts cannot be operated until further orders
- Third parties dealing with the defaulter should exercise caution

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 28, 2025
- Recovery Certificate: 8027 of 2024

## Impact Assessment

Direct impact on Columbia Sales' financial operations through account freezing. Serves as enforcement precedent for IPO-related recovery actions. Limited broader market impact but demonstrates SEBI's active pursuit of defaulters in IPO matters.
