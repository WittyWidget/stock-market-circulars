- Connected to Pressure Sensitive (India) Limited

- Adherence to enforcement proceedings timeline

- Notice issued: July 28, 2025

This enforcement action affects the specific HUF entity and may have implications for Pressure Sensitive (India) Limited. The recovery certificate indicates SEBI's pursuit of outstanding financial obligations through formal enforcement mechanisms.
