## Summary

SEBI's Appellate Authority dismissed Appeal No. 6468 of 2025 filed by Nitin Goradia regarding an RTI application seeking information about a SEBI employee's supervisor. The appeal was filed after the appellant was dissatisfied with the CPIO's response, claiming incomplete or false information was provided.

## Key Points

- RTI application filed on June 12, 2025 seeking supervisor information for alleged harassment by SEBI employee
- CPIO responded on July 02, 2025 stating no employee exists by the mentioned name but provided information on similar named employee
- Appeal filed on July 02, 2025 claiming incomplete/misleading information
- Appellate Authority found CPIO's response adequate and dismissed the appeal
- Appellant has filed 7 previous RTI applications on same subject matter, all dismissed

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative decision on RTI appeal process.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Reaffirms that RTI appeals cannot expand scope of original query and must use proper grievance channels like SCORES portal.

## Important Dates

- June 12, 2025: Original RTI application filed
- July 02, 2025: CPIO response and appeal filed
- July 29, 2025: Appeal dismissed

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact - administrative matter with no effect on market operations, trading, or regulatory framework. Demonstrates SEBI's RTI appeal process and proper use of grievance redressal mechanisms.
