## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. RC8414 of 2024 in respect of Om Prakash Dalmia (PAN: ADKPD7701J) in connection with the Illiquid Stock Options matter.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. RC8414 of 2024 has been released
- Concerns Om Prakash Dalmia (PAN: ADKPD7701J)
- Related to Illiquid Stock Options enforcement action
- Release order indicates resolution of the recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an individual enforcement resolution.

## Compliance Requirements

No additional compliance requirements for market participants. This concerns resolution of individual enforcement matter.

## Important Dates

- Release Order Date: August 26, 2025
- Original Recovery Certificate: RC8414 of 2024

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact on broader market operations. This represents closure of individual enforcement proceedings related to illiquid stock options violations. No systemic implications for market participants.
