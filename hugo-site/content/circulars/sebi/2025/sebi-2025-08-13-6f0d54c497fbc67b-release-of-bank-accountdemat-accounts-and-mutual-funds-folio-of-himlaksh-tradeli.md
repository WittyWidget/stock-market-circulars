## Summary

SEBI has released the attachment on bank accounts, demat accounts and mutual fund folios of Himlaksh Tradelinks Private Limited, Mr. Karan Singh Shekhawat and Ms. Meena Shekhavat in connection with the PACL Limited matter. This action is part of ongoing recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Release of attached financial accounts and investments
- Involves Himlaksh Tradelinks Private Limited and two individuals
- Connected to PACL Limited enforcement case
- Administrative action in recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced - this is an administrative action releasing previously attached assets.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is a case-specific release order.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: August 13, 2025
- Effective immediately upon issuance

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is an administrative release of attachments in a specific enforcement case. The release suggests progress in the resolution of recovery proceedings related to PACL Limited matter.
