## Summary

SEBI has issued attachment orders 14800 & 14801 of 2025 dated July 28, 2025, against Ram Prakash (PAN: AXFPR4439L) and his proprietorship firm Khan Enterprise in connection with Recovery Certificate No. 7073 of 2023 related to Esteem Bio Organic Food Processing Ltd.

## Key Points

- Attachment orders issued against Ram Prakash and Khan Enterprise
- Recovery Certificate No. 7073 of 2023 in matter of Esteem Bio Organic Food Processing Ltd.
- Both bank accounts and demat accounts subject to attachment
- Orders dated July 28, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is an enforcement action under existing recovery procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and depositories to comply with attachment orders
- Frozen accounts cannot be operated until further orders
- Third parties dealing with attached accounts may face legal consequences

## Important Dates

- Order Date: July 28, 2025
- Recovery Certificate: No. 7073 of 2023

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact on broader market operations. Specific enforcement action against identified defaulter. Banks and depositories must ensure compliance with attachment orders to avoid regulatory issues.
