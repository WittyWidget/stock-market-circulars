## Summary

SEBI has issued a release order for Recovery Certificate No. 8223 of 2024 against Arpita Ilesh Patel in connection with trading violations involving the scrip of Econo Trade (India) Ltd. This compliance order releases the recovery certificate, indicating completion of recovery proceedings or other resolution.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8223 of 2024 has been released
- Order pertains to Arpita Ilesh Patel
- Connected to trading activities in Econo Trade (India) Ltd. scrip
- Release order indicates closure or resolution of recovery proceedings
- Part of SEBI's enforcement and compliance activities

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action completion notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Completion of recovery proceedings against the specified individual
- Resolution of trading violation matter
- Closure of enforcement action

## Important Dates

- Order Date: August 1, 2025
- Recovery Certificate No: 8223 of 2024

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this pertains to a specific individual entity and represents the completion of enforcement proceedings. No broader market implications or new compliance requirements for other market participants.
