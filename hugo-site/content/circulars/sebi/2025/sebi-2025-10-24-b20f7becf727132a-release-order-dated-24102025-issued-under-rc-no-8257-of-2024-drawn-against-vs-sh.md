## Summary

SEBI has issued a Release Order dated 24.10.2025 under Registration Certificate No. 8257 of 2024 in the matter of irregularities in Stock Options (ISO) at the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) drawn against VS Shah.

## Key Points

- Release Order issued on October 24, 2025
- Case Reference: RC No. 8257 of 2024
- Entity: VS Shah
- Matter: Irregularities in Stock Options (ISO) at BSE
- Regulatory action concluded

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an enforcement order related to specific case proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

Compliance requirements would be specific to the entity named in the order (VS Shah). Market participants should review the full order for any broader implications on stock options trading practices.

## Important Dates

- **Release Order Date**: October 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The release order pertains to specific enforcement proceedings against VS Shah in relation to irregularities in stock options trading at BSE. The impact is primarily entity-specific. Market participants engaged in stock options trading should review the order to understand the nature of irregularities identified and ensure their practices comply with SEBI regulations.
