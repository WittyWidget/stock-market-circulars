## Summary

SEBI has issued Release Order for Recovery Certificate No. RC7126 of 2025 concerning Ideal Plywood Traders Pvt Ltd (PAN: AACCI5171P) in relation to illiquid stock options matter. This represents an enforcement action by SEBI for recovery of dues.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate Number: RC7126 of 2025
- Company: Ideal Plywood Traders Pvt Ltd
- PAN: AACCI5171P
- Matter relates to illiquid stock options
- Document issued on August 1, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

This is an enforcement document rather than a regulatory change. It represents SEBI's recovery action against the specified entity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company must comply with recovery certificate requirements
- Payment of dues as specified in the recovery certificate
- Adherence to SEBI regulations regarding stock options

## Important Dates

- Release Order Date: August 1, 2025
- Recovery Certificate: RC7126 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action is specific to Ideal Plywood Traders Pvt Ltd and relates to recovery of dues in connection with illiquid stock options. The impact is primarily on the named entity with limited broader market implications.
