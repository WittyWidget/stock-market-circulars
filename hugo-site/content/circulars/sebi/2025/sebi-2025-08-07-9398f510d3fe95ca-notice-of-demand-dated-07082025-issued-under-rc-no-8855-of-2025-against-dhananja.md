## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand dated 07.08.2025 under Recovery Certificate no. 8855 of 2025 against Dhananjay Chandrakant Parikh (PAN: ACTPP2402L) in connection with matters related to Family Care Hospitals Limited. This represents enforcement action by SEBI to recover outstanding amounts from the individual.

## Key Points

- Notice issued against Dhananjay Chandrakant Parikh with PAN: ACTPP2402L
- Recovery Certificate no. 8855 of 2025
- Related to Family Care Hospitals Limited matter
- Date of notice: 07.08.2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- The individual named must respond to the demand notice as per SEBI regulations
- Compliance with recovery proceedings as specified in the notice

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 07.08.2025
- Recovery Certificate: 8855 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action indicates ongoing regulatory proceedings against an individual in connection with Family Care Hospitals Limited. The impact is primarily on the named individual and may have implications for the company's regulatory standing.
