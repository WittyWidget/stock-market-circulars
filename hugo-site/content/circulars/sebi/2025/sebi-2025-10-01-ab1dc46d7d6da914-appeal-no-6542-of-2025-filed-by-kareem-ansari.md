## Summary

The Appellate Authority disposed of Appeal No. 6542 of 2025 filed by Kareem Ansari as infructuous. The appellant claimed non-receipt of response to his RTI application dated July 04, 2025. However, records showed that CPIO responded on August 05, 2025 within the statutory timeframe, and the response was delivered via registered post on August 12, 2025 and also uploaded on the RTI portal. The appellant retains the right to file a fresh appeal if dissatisfied with the response content.

## Key Points

- RTI application filed by Kareem Ansari on July 04, 2025 (received by SEBI on July 08, 2025)
- Appeal filed on August 29, 2025 claiming non-receipt of response
- CPIO issued response on August 05, 2025 within statutory timeframe under RTI Act
- Response delivered via registered post on August 12, 2025 and uploaded on RTI portal
- Appeal disposed as infructuous since response was provided timely
- Appellant can file fresh appeal if dissatisfied with response content
- Order passed by Ruchi Chojer, Appellate Authority under RTI Act
