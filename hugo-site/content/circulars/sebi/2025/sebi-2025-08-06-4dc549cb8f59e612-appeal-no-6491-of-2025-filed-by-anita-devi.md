## Summary

SEBI's Appellate Authority dismissed RTI Appeal No. 6491 of 2025 filed by Anita Devi seeking information about PACL land allotment records. The appeal was filed after SEBI's CPIO informed that the requested information about land allotment dates, addresses, documents, and registry details was not available with SEBI.

## Key Points

- Appellant sought details about PACL land allotment including dates, addresses, plot numbers, and supporting documents
- SEBI informed that the requested information is not available with the organization
- Appeal was filed claiming incomplete or misleading information was provided
- Appellate Authority confirmed SEBI's response was appropriate as the information simply does not exist with SEBI

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this order.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements specified. Order notes that disposal of PACL properties and investor repayment is handled by the Justice R.M. Lodha Committee as per Supreme Court orders.

## Important Dates

- July 08, 2025: Original RTI application filed
- July 10, 2025: SEBI's response provided
- July 19, 2025: Appeal filed (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/25/00185)
- August 06, 2025: Appeal dismissed

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact as this is an administrative RTI matter with no implications for market operations, trading, or regulatory compliance. The order confirms existing arrangements for PACL matter resolution through the Supreme Court-appointed committee.
