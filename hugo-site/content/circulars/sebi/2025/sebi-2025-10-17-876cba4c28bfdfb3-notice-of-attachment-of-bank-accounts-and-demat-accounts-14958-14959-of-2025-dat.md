## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Attachment of Bank Accounts and Demat Accounts (Notice Nos. 14958 & 14959 of 2025) dated October 17, 2025, against JITNEY INVESTMENTS PVT. LTD (PAN: AADCJ0092M). This enforcement action is taken under Recovery Certificate No. 8331 of 2024 in the matter of Superior Finlease Ltd, indicating the entity has been identified as a defaulter in recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Enforcement action against JITNEY INVESTMENTS PVT. LTD (PAN: AADCJ0092M)
- Attachment notices issued: 14958 & 14959 of 2025
- Notice date: October 17, 2025
- Related to Recovery Certificate No. 8331 of 2024
- Connected to the matter of Superior Finlease Ltd
- Entity classified as defaulter
- Both bank accounts and demat accounts are subject to attachment

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery and attachment provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- JITNEY INVESTMENTS PVT. LTD must comply with the attachment orders
- Banks and depository participants holding accounts of the entity must freeze the attached accounts
- The entity cannot operate the attached bank accounts and demat accounts until further orders
- Any persons or entities having dealings with JITNEY INVESTMENTS PVT. LTD should be aware of the attachment status

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 17, 2025
- **Recovery Certificate**: No. 8331 of 2024

## Impact Assessment

**Entity-Specific Impact**: High impact on JITNEY INVESTMENTS PVT. LTD as their bank and demat accounts are now attached, severely restricting their ability to conduct financial transactions and securities trading.

**Market Impact**: Low. This is an entity-specific enforcement action and does not affect broader market operations or other market participants. However, it demonstrates SEBI's continued vigilance in recovery proceedings against defaulters.

**Investor Impact**: Minimal for general investors. Investors or entities who have dealings with JITNEY INVESTMENTS PVT. LTD should exercise caution given the attachment proceedings and defaulter status.

**Precedent Value**: Part of SEBI's ongoing enforcement mechanism to recover dues through attachment of assets of defaulting entities in matters involving securities market violations or dues.
