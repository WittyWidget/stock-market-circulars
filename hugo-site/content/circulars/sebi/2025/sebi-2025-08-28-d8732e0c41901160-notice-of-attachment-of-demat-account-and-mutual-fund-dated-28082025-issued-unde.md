## Summary

SEBI has issued a notice of attachment of demat accounts and mutual fund units for Pragya Dairies and Agro Limited and related individuals under Recovery Certificate No. 4800/2022. The attachment covers multiple entities including company promoters and directors.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4800/2022 issued against Pragya Dairies and Agro Limited
- Multiple individuals affected: Punam Chand Patidar, Nana Lal Patidar, Pooja Patidar, Durga Patidar, and Ansar Ahmed
- Attachment covers both demat accounts and mutual fund units
- Enforcement action relates to outstanding dues to SEBI

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

- Affected entities must comply with attachment orders
- Financial institutions holding attached assets must freeze the accounts
- No transactions permitted on attached demat accounts and mutual fund units

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: August 28, 2025
- Attachment effective immediately upon notice

## Impact Assessment

High impact on the company and named individuals as their investment assets are frozen. This enforcement action may signal serious regulatory violations and could affect the company's operations and financial standing.
