## Summary

SEBI has completed recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. 8395 of 2024 dated 29.11.2024 against Mr. Nemichand Kasturchand Jain in connection with trading violations involving illiquid stock options at BSE.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8395 of 2024 dated November 29, 2024 has been completed
- Target: Mr. Nemichand Kasturchand Jain
- Violation: Trading in illiquid stock options at BSE
- Status: Recovery proceedings completed

## Regulatory Changes

No specific regulatory changes announced - this is a completion notice for existing enforcement action.

## Compliance Requirements

This notice serves as confirmation that the recovery proceedings have been completed. No additional compliance actions required from market participants.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Date: November 29, 2024
- Completion Notice Date: August 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this relates to completion of individual enforcement proceedings. Demonstrates SEBI's continued focus on monitoring trading activities in illiquid instruments and ensuring compliance with market regulations.
