## Summary

SEBI has issued a Completion Order for Recovery Certificate No. 8813 of 2025 against Mr. Priyank Dineshbhai Shah in connection with unregistered investment advisory activities. This order marks the completion of compliance proceedings in the matter.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8813 of 2025 issued against Mr. Priyank Dineshbhai Shah
- Matter relates to unregistered investment advisory activities
- Completion order indicates conclusion of compliance proceedings
- Enforcement action taken by SEBI against unauthorized investment advisory services

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI regulations regarding registration requirements for investment advisors.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investment advisors must be registered with SEBI before providing advisory services
- Unregistered entities/individuals are prohibited from offering investment advisory services
- Non-compliance results in recovery proceedings and enforcement actions

## Important Dates

- Order Date: October 14, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement action with no direct market-wide impact. The order serves as a reminder that investment advisory activities require proper SEBI registration. The completion order indicates that compliance proceedings for this particular recovery certificate have been concluded.
