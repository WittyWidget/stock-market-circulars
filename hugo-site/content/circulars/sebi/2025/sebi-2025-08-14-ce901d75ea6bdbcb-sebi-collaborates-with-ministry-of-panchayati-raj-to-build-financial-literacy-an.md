## Summary

SEBI has partnered with the Ministry of Panchayati Raj (MoPR) to launch a nationwide training initiative targeting Block Level Panchayat representatives. The program aims to build financial literacy and investor awareness capacities at the grassroots level, empowering rural community leaders to educate their constituencies about financial planning, investments, and securities market awareness.

## Key Points

- First training program held on August 09-10, 2025 at YASHADA in Pune, Maharashtra
- Over 100 Sarpanch and Block-level representatives from various Maharashtra districts participated
- Focus on financial planning, savings, investments, and protection against fraudulent schemes
- Training covers securities market ecosystem and regulated investment avenues
- Initiative reflects shared vision to strengthen Panchayats as centers of informed decision-making

## Regulatory Changes

No specific regulatory changes introduced. This is a collaborative capacity-building initiative between SEBI and MoPR.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an educational outreach program.

## Important Dates

- August 09-10, 2025: First training program conducted in Pune
- August 14, 2025: Press release date

## Impact Assessment

This initiative has potential medium-term positive impact on investor awareness in rural India. By training Panchayat representatives, SEBI aims to create a multiplier effect where trained leaders can educate their communities about financial literacy and safe investment practices. This could contribute to broader financial inclusion and protection of rural investors from fraudulent schemes over time.
