## Summary

SEBI has completed recovery proceedings under Certificate No. 4558 of 2022 dated 16.03.2022 issued against Mr. Nirmal Jain in connection with violations related to Mega Corporation Limited.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4558 of 2022 has been completed
- Original certificate dated 16.03.2022
- Proceedings against Mr. Nirmal Jain
- Related to Mega Corporation Limited matter
- Administrative closure of enforcement action

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced - this is completion of existing enforcement proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements - completion notice for concluded proceedings.

## Important Dates

- Original Recovery Certificate Date: 16.03.2022
- Completion Date: 29.08.2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this represents administrative completion of individual enforcement proceedings. No broader regulatory or market operational changes involved.
