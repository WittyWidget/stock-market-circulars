## Summary

SEBI has issued a General Remittance Order dated 29.08.2025 under Recovery Certificate No. 8817 of 2025 against Krishnamurthy G. (PAN: BSFPK6683P) in connection with unregistered investment advisor activities.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8817 of 2025 issued against Krishnamurthy G.
- Individual's PAN: BSFPK6683P
- Matter relates to unregistered investment advisor activities
- General Remittance Order dated 29.08.2025
- Enforcement action under SEBI's recovery framework

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This represents enforcement of existing regulations regarding unregistered investment advisors.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investment advisors must be registered with SEBI before providing advisory services
- Individuals providing investment advice without registration face recovery actions
- Compliance with SEBI's investment advisor regulations mandatory

## Important Dates

- Order Date: 29.08.2025
- Recovery Certificate: No. 8817 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is an individual enforcement action. Reinforces SEBI's commitment to regulating investment advisory services and taking action against unregistered advisors. Serves as a deterrent for others operating without proper registration.
