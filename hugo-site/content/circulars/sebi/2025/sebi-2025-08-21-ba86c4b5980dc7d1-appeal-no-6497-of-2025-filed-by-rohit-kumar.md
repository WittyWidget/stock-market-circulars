## Summary

SEBI's Appellate Authority dismissed Appeal No. 6497 of 2025 filed by Rohit Kumar as time-barred. The appellant sought information under RTI Act regarding BSE's technical glitch on July 12, 2024, but filed the appeal 109 days after the statutory 30-day deadline.

## Key Points

- Appeal filed on July 25, 2025 against CPIO response dated April 7, 2025
- Appeal was time-barred as it exceeded the 30-day statutory limit under RTI Act Section 19(1)
- No request for condoning delay or valid reasons provided by appellant
- Information sought related to BSE's compliance with SEBI's technical glitch reporting procedures

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this order.

## Compliance Requirements

This order reaffirms the importance of adhering to statutory timelines under the RTI Act for filing appeals.

## Important Dates

- March 22, 2025: Original RTI application filed
- April 7, 2025: CPIO response date
- May 7, 2025: Last date for filing appeal (30 days from response)
- July 25, 2025: Actual appeal filing date (time-barred)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact as this is a procedural dismissal of an RTI appeal. The order does not affect market operations or regulatory compliance requirements for market participants.
