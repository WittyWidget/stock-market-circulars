## Summary

SEBI has issued an adjudication order against Options King Research Analyst (SEBI Reg No: INH000003325), proprietor Sunil Suklal Pawar, for violations of qualification and certification requirements under the SEBI (Research Analysts) Regulations, 2014. The order follows a thematic inspection conducted for the period April 1, 2023 to September 30, 2024.

## Key Points

- SEBI conducted thematic inspection of Options King Research Analyst from April 2023 to September 2024
- Violations found under Regulation 7(2) of SEBI (Research Analysts) Regulations, 2014
- Show Cause Notice issued on February 25, 2025
- Adjudication proceedings initiated under Section 15-I of SEBI Act, 1992
- Specific violation relates to qualification and certification requirements for Mr. Sunil Pawar

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action for existing compliance requirements under Research Analysts Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Research Analysts must meet qualification and certification requirements as per Regulation 7(2)
- Proper compliance with SEBI (Research Analysts) Regulations, 2014 is mandatory
- Research Analysts must maintain valid certifications throughout their registration period

## Important Dates

- Inspection Period: April 1, 2023 to September 30, 2024
- SEBI inspection findings communicated: December 16, 2024
- Noticee reply submitted: December 20, 2024
- Adjudicating Officer appointed: February 7, 2025
- Show Cause Notice issued: February 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action reinforces SEBI's commitment to ensuring research analysts maintain proper qualifications and certifications. It serves as a warning to other research analysts to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements. The action may impact Options King Research Analyst's operations and reputation in the market.
