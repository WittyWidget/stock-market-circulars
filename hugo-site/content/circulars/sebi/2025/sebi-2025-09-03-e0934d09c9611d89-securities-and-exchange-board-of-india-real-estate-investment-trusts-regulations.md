## Summary

SEBI has published the consolidated Securities and Exchange Board of India (Real Estate Investment Trusts) Regulations, 2014, as last amended on September 3, 2025. These regulations establish a comprehensive framework for Real Estate Investment Trusts, covering registration requirements, regulatory compliance, governance structures, and operational guidelines for REITs in India.

## Key Points

- Establishes complete regulatory framework for Real Estate Investment Trusts in India
- Defines comprehensive terminology including 'associate', 'change in control', 'bonus issue', and other key concepts
- Sets out registration certificate requirements for REITs
- Includes multiple amendments from 2016 and 2023 incorporating evolved regulatory requirements
- Provides detailed definitions of associates including controlled entities, promoters, and related parties
- Specifies governance and compliance standards for REIT operations

## Regulatory Changes

- Updated definition of 'associate' to align with Companies Act, 2013 and applicable accounting standards
- Revised 'change in control' definition as per 2023 amendment effective April 1, 2023
- Removed certain sub-clauses from associate definition through 2016 amendments
- Streamlined regulatory definitions to reduce ambiguity and improve compliance clarity

## Compliance Requirements

- REITs must obtain registration certificate under these regulations
- Compliance with associate and change in control definitions for related party transactions
- Adherence to bonus issue procedures and unit holder record date requirements
- Implementation of governance frameworks as specified in the regulations
- Regular compliance with amended provisions effective from respective dates

## Important Dates

- Original regulations effective: September 26, 2014
- 2016 amendments effective: November 30, 2016
- 2023 amendments effective: April 1, 2023
- Latest consolidation date: September 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

- High impact on existing and prospective REITs requiring compliance with updated framework
- Establishes clear regulatory pathway for real estate investment through REIT structure
- Provides investor protection through comprehensive governance and disclosure requirements
- Enables growth of real estate investment market through structured regulatory approach
- Streamlined definitions reduce regulatory uncertainty and improve compliance efficiency
