## Summary

SEBI has issued a remittance advice against Komal (PAN: DSSPK2912L) as part of recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. 8468 of 2024. This action relates to trading activities in the scrip of Vikas Proppant and Granite Limited that violated securities regulations.

## Key Points

- Individual defaulter: Komal (PAN: DSSPK2912L)
- Recovery proceedings under Certificate No. 8468 of 2024
- Violations related to trading in Vikas Proppant and Granite Limited
- Part of SEBI's enforcement action against unauthorized trading activities

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This represents enforcement of existing securities regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Recovery proceedings initiated against the defaulter
- Payment of outstanding amounts as determined by SEBI
- Cessation of unauthorized trading activities

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 28, 2025
- Recovery Certificate: No. 8468 of 2024

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this involves enforcement action against a specific individual defaulter. The action demonstrates SEBI's commitment to pursuing recovery against violators of securities regulations, particularly in cases involving unauthorized trading activities.
