## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate number 8825 of 2025 dated August 13, 2025, against 13 defaulters in the matter of Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited. The notice targets multiple individuals and proprietors of various enterprises for outstanding dues.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate number 8825 of 2025 issued on August 13, 2025
- 13 defaulters identified including individuals and enterprise proprietors
- Case relates to Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited
- Enforcement action initiated for recovery of outstanding amounts
- Multiple entities including Tirupati Enterprises, Jahan Enterprises, Shiv Enterprises, and others involved

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced; this is an enforcement action under existing recovery procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Defaulters must respond to the demand notice as per recovery certificate provisions
- Outstanding dues must be settled as specified in the notice
- Non-compliance may result in further enforcement measures including asset attachment

## Important Dates

- August 13, 2025: Date of Notice of Demand
- Recovery Certificate number 8825 of 2025 issued

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action demonstrates SEBI's continued efforts to recover outstanding dues from market participants. The action affects 13 specific entities and their associated businesses. Market participants should note SEBI's active pursuit of recovery in enforcement cases.
