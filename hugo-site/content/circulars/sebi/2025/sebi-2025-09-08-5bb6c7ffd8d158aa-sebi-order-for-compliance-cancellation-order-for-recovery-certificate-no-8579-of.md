## Summary

SEBI has issued a compliance order cancelling Recovery Certificate No. 8579 of 2025 against Mohd Faisal in connection with a matter involving Biocon Limited. This represents the resolution of an enforcement proceeding through the cancellation of the recovery certificate.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8579 of 2025 has been cancelled
- Order pertains to Mohd Faisal in relation to Biocon Limited
- Document issued on September 8, 2025
- Represents closure of enforcement action through certificate cancellation

## Regulatory Changes

No broader regulatory changes are indicated by this individual compliance order.

## Compliance Requirements

The cancellation of the recovery certificate resolves the specific compliance obligation that was previously outstanding against the named individual.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: September 8, 2025
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8579 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual compliance matter with minimal market impact. The cancellation of a recovery certificate typically indicates resolution of outstanding obligations or procedural closure of enforcement proceedings. Impact on Biocon Limited's operations or stock performance is expected to be negligible.
