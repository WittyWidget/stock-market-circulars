## Summary

SEBI has announced the completion of recovery proceedings against Himlaksh Tradelinks Pvt Ltd., Mr. Karan Singh Shekhawat and Ms. Meena Shekhavat in connection with the PACL Ltd. matter. This notice signifies the conclusion of enforcement actions against these entities.

## Key Points

- Recovery proceedings completed against Himlaksh Tradelinks Pvt Ltd.
- Proceedings also concluded against Mr. Karan Singh Shekhawat and Ms. Meena Shekhavat
- Related to the broader PACL Ltd. enforcement matter
- Represents closure of regulatory action against these specific entities

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a completion notice for existing enforcement proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

No additional compliance requirements. The notice confirms completion of recovery proceedings.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: August 13, 2025
- Recovery proceedings: Completed

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a completion notice rather than initiation of new proceedings. Provides regulatory closure on enforcement actions against the specified entities in the PACL matter.
