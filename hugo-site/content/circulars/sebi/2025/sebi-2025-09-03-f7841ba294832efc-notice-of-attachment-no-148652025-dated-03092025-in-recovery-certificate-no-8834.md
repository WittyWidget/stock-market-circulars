## Summary

SEBI has issued Notice of Attachment No. 14865/2025 dated September 3, 2025, in Recovery Certificate No. 8834 of 2025 against Nikhil Dayanand Baljekar (PAN: ABYPB7880N), a research analyst. This notice is part of SEBI's enforcement proceedings to recover dues from the individual.

## Key Points

- Notice issued under Recovery Certificate No. 8834 of 2025
- Target: Nikhil Dayanand Baljekar (PAN: ABYPB7880N)
- Individual is registered as a Research Analyst
- Notice number: 14865/2025
- Date of notice: September 3, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Research analysts must ensure compliance with SEBI regulations
- Non-compliance may result in enforcement actions and recovery proceedings
- Penalties and dues must be paid as per SEBI directives

## Important Dates

- Notice date: September 3, 2025
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8834 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action is specific to an individual research analyst and does not have direct market-wide implications. However, it demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement activities against non-compliant market participants. Research analysts and other market intermediaries should ensure full compliance with regulatory requirements to avoid similar enforcement actions.
