## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 8867 of 2025 dated August 20, 2025, directed at Vivek Kumar Varshney (PAN: AWTPV7908B) identified as a defaulter in the matter involving Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8867 of 2025 issued on August 20, 2025
- Defaulter: Vivek Kumar Varshney (PAN: AWTPV7908B)
- Matter relates to Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited
- Formal demand notice issued by SEBI for recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing recovery proceedings framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Vivek Kumar Varshney must comply with the demand notice
- Payment of outstanding amounts as specified in the recovery certificate
- Failure to comply may result in further enforcement actions

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: August 20, 2025
- Recovery Certificate Date: August 20, 2025
- Certificate Number: 8867 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action is specific to Continental Seeds and Chemicals Limited and the identified defaulter. Impact is primarily compliance-focused with potential implications for the company's regulatory standing. Market impact is expected to be limited given the specific nature of the proceedings.
