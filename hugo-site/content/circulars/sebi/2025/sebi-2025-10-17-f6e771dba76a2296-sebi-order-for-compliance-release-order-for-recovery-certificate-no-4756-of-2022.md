## Summary

SEBI has issued a release order for Recovery Certificate No. 4756 of 2022 against Siddharth J Chaturvedi in the matter of Brijlaxmi Leasing and Finance Limited. This order pertains to enforcement and recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4756 of 2022
- Noticee: Siddharth J Chaturvedi
- Matter relates to Brijlaxmi Leasing and Finance Limited
- Release order issued by SEBI

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action related to existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

This is a release order relating to recovery proceedings against the named individual. No new compliance requirements for market participants.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: October 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This order has limited market impact as it relates to a specific enforcement and recovery matter against an individual in connection with Brijlaxmi Leasing and Finance Limited. The release order suggests resolution or conclusion of the recovery proceedings for this particular certificate.
