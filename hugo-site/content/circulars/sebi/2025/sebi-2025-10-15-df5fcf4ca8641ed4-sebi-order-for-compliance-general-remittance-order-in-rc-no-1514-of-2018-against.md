SEBI has issued a general remittance order for compliance in Recovery Certificate No. 1514 of 2018 against Bankesh Bhavsar (PAN: AFAPB3386D). This order pertains to enforcement and recovery proceedings initiated by SEBI.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No.: RC No. 1514 of 2018
- Person/Entity: Bankesh Bhavsar
- PAN: AFAPB3386D
- Order Type: General Remittance Order for Compliance
- Issuing Authority: Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI)

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing SEBI regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

The named individual, Bankesh Bhavsar, is required to comply with the terms specified in the general remittance order issued under Recovery Certificate No. 1514 of 2018. Specific compliance obligations and payment terms would be detailed in the full order document.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: October 15, 2025
- Recovery Case: RC No. 1514 of 2018

This order has limited market impact as it is specific to enforcement proceedings against an individual.
