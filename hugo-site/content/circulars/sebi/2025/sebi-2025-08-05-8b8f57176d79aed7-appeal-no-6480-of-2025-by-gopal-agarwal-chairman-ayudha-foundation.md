## Summary

SEBI's Appellate Authority under the RTI Act disposed of Appeal No. 6480 of 2025 filed by Gopal Agarwal, Chairman of Ayudha Foundation, after determining that the CPIO had provided a timely response to the original RTI application within the statutory timeframe.

## Key Points

- Appeal filed on July 9, 2025 claiming non-receipt of response to RTI application dated May 30, 2025
- Records showed CPIO replied on June 26, 2025 through RTI MIS Portal within statutory timeframe
- Appellate Authority found no grounds for intervention as proper response was provided
- Appeal disposed of with option for fresh appeal if appellant remains unsatisfied

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this order.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Standard RTI Act procedures followed.

## Important Dates

- Original RTI Application: May 30, 2025
- CPIO Response: June 26, 2025
- Appeal Filed: July 9, 2025
- Order Date: August 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact - this is a procedural RTI matter with no implications for market operations, trading, or regulatory compliance for market participants. The order demonstrates SEBI's RTI processes are functioning within statutory timeframes.
