## Summary

SEBI has issued a compliance completion order for Recovery Certificate No. 7354 of 2023 against Manisha Rajendra Modi in connection with an investigation into trading activities in the scrip of Dhyana Finstock Ltd. This order marks the completion of compliance requirements under the recovery certificate.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 7354 of 2023 compliance completed
- Individual involved: Manisha Rajendra Modi
- Related to investigation into trading activities in Dhyana Finstock Ltd
- Enforcement action has reached completion stage

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a completion order for existing enforcement action.

## Compliance Requirements

Compliance requirements under Recovery Certificate No. 7354 of 2023 have been fulfilled by the concerned individual.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: August 1, 2025
- Recovery Certificate No: 7354 of 2023

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this represents the completion of an enforcement action rather than initiation of new proceedings. The order indicates successful resolution of compliance requirements under the recovery certificate mechanism.
