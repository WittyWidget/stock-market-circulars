## Summary

SEBI has issued a General Remittance Order (RC No. 8759 of 2025) against Jadav Vamshi (PAN – BJFPJ9368B) for conducting unregistered investment advisory activities through the platform 'Baap of Chart'. This enforcement action addresses violations of investment advisory regulations.

## Key Points

- Individual: Jadav Vamshi (PAN – BJFPJ9368B)
- Platform involved: 'Baap of Chart'
- Violation: Conducting investment advisory activities without proper registration
- Order type: General Remittance Order
- Order number: RC No. 8759 of 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This order enforces existing investment advisory regulations under SEBI guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investment advisors must obtain proper registration before providing advisory services
- Platforms and individuals providing investment advice must comply with SEBI registration requirements
- Unregistered advisory activities are subject to enforcement action

## Important Dates

- Order date: August 25, 2025
- Effective immediately upon issuance

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action reinforces SEBI's commitment to regulating investment advisory services and protecting investors from unregistered advisors. While the direct market impact is limited, it serves as a deterrent for others considering providing unregistered investment advice and helps maintain market integrity.
