## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate number 8833 of 2025 dated August 29, 2025 to Manish Kumar Kanodia HUF (PAN: AANHM2862D) in connection with dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8833 of 2025 issued
- Defaulter: Manish Kumar Kanodia HUF
- PAN: AANHM2862D
- Matter relates to dealing in illiquid stock options at BSE
- Notice dated August 29, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is an enforcement action under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

The named defaulter must comply with the recovery demand as specified in the certificate. This serves as a public notice of the enforcement action.

## Important Dates

- August 29, 2025: Recovery Certificate issued

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is an individual enforcement action. Serves as a deterrent for violations related to trading in illiquid stock options.
