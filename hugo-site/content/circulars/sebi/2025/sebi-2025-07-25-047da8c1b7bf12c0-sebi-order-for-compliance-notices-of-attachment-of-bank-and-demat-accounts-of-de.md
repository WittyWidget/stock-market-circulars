## Summary

SEBI has issued an order for compliance regarding notices of attachment of bank and demat accounts of In House Productions Limited LLP (PAN: AAACV1685K) under Recovery Certificate No. 4833 of 2022. This enforcement action is part of SEBI's recovery proceedings against the defaulter.

## Key Points

- Enforcement action against In House Productions Limited LLP
- Recovery Certificate No. 4833 of 2022
- Attachment notices for bank and demat accounts
- PAN: AAACV1685K

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing recovery proceedings framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and depository participants must comply with attachment notices
- Freezing of specified bank and demat accounts
- Cooperation required from financial institutions

## Important Dates

- Order date: July 25, 2025
- Immediate compliance required upon receipt of notices

## Impact Assessment

Limited direct market impact as this is an enforcement action against a specific entity. Banks and depositories holding accounts for the defaulter must comply with attachment orders. This demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement of recovery proceedings against non-compliant entities.
