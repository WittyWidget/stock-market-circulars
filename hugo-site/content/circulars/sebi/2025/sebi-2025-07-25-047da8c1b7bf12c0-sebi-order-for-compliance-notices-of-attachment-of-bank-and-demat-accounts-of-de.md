## Summary

SEBI has issued an order for compliance regarding notices of attachment of bank and demat accounts of In House Productions Limited LLP (PAN: AAACV1685K) under Recovery Certificate No. 4833 of 2022. This enforcement action is part of SEBI's recovery proceedings against the defaulting entity.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4833 of 2022 issued against In House Productions Limited LLP
- Entity PAN: AAACV1685K
- Notices of attachment issued for both bank accounts and demat accounts
- Part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings
- Compliance order dated July 25, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action under existing recovery provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and depositories must comply with the attachment notices
- In House Productions Limited LLP accounts to be frozen/attached as per the order
- Financial institutions must cooperate with SEBI's recovery proceedings

## Important Dates

- Order date: July 25, 2025
- Recovery Certificate reference: RC No. 4833 of 2022

## Impact Assessment

This enforcement action is specific to In House Productions Limited LLP and does not have broader market implications. The attachment of bank and demat accounts is a standard recovery mechanism used by SEBI to recover dues from defaulting entities. Banks and depositories holding accounts of the defaulter are required to comply with the attachment notices to facilitate the recovery process.
