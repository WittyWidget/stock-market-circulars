## Summary

SEBI has issued a Notice of Demand under Recovery Certificate No. 8859 of 2025 dated August 12, 2025, against M Ashok Mootha (PAN: AAFPM1898R) for non-payment of turnover fees and interest levied.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8859 of 2025 issued on August 12, 2025
- Target: M Ashok Mootha (PAN: AAFPM1898R)
- Outstanding dues: Turnover fees and associated interest
- Enforcement action for fee recovery

## Regulatory Changes

No specific regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing provisions for recovery of outstanding fees.

## Compliance Requirements

- M Ashok Mootha must pay the outstanding turnover fees and interest as demanded
- Failure to comply may result in further recovery proceedings
- Market participants should ensure timely payment of all regulatory fees to avoid similar action

## Important Dates

- August 12, 2025: Date of Recovery Certificate issuance
- Payment deadline and recovery timeline to be specified in the detailed notice

## Impact Assessment

This is an individual enforcement action with limited market impact. It demonstrates SEBI's continued efforts to recover outstanding fees and maintain regulatory compliance among market participants.
