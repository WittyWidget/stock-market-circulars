## Summary

SEBI has issued a release order for Recovery Certificate No. 4757 of 2022 against Jaykishor Chaturvedi in the matter of Brijlaxmi Leasing and Finance Limited. This order signifies the conclusion of recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 4757 of 2022 has been released
- Individual concerned: Jaykishor Chaturvedi
- Related entity: Brijlaxmi Leasing and Finance Limited
- Order type: Release Order indicating completion of recovery process
- Order date: October 17, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an enforcement action completion notice.

## Compliance Requirements

No ongoing compliance requirements. The release order indicates that the recovery proceedings under this certificate have been concluded.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: October 17, 2025
- Recovery Certificate Year: 2022

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact on broader market operations. This is a specific enforcement matter relating to debt recovery proceedings against an individual in connection with Brijlaxmi Leasing and Finance Limited. The release order suggests that obligations under the recovery certificate have been satisfied or the proceedings have been concluded.
