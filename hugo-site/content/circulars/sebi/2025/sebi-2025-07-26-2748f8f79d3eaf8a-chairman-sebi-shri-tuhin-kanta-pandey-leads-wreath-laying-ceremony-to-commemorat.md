## Summary

SEBI Chairman Shri Tuhin Kanta Pandey led a wreath laying ceremony at SEBI Head office in BKC, Mumbai to commemorate the 26th Kargil Vijay Diwas on July 26, 2025. The ceremony honored the brave soldiers who sacrificed their lives during the 1999 Kargil War, with participation from SEBI's whole time members, executive directors, and staff.

## Key Points

- Chairman led wreath laying ceremony at SEBI Head office in BKC, Bandra
- Ceremony marked the 26th anniversary of Kargil Vijay Diwas
- SEBI staff including whole time members and executive directors participated
- Chairman interacted with Army veterans who served during Kargil conflict
- Veterans are currently employed as Administrative and Technical Staff (ATS) at SEBI on contractual basis
- Veterans shared personal battlefield experiences and memories
