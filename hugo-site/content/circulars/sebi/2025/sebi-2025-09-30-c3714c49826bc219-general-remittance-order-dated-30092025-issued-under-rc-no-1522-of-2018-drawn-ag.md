## Summary

SEBI has issued a General Remittance Order dated 30.09.2025 under Recovery Certificate (RC) No. 1522 of 2018 drawn against Vijay H Shah in the matter of Oasis Media Matrix Limited. This order is part of SEBI's enforcement and recovery proceedings.

## Key Points

- Order issued under RC No. 1522 of 2018
- Directed against Vijay H Shah
- Related to Oasis Media Matrix Limited matter
- Issued on 30 September 2025
- Part of SEBI's recovery proceedings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is an enforcement action under existing recovery framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Vijay H Shah is required to comply with the remittance order as per the terms specified in the detailed order document.

## Important Dates

- **Order Date**: 30 September 2025
- **Recovery Certificate**: RC No. 1522 of 2018

## Impact Assessment

This is a specific enforcement action against an individual in relation to Oasis Media Matrix Limited. The impact is limited to the parties involved in the recovery proceedings. Market participants should note SEBI's continued enforcement actions to recover dues under existing recovery certificates.
