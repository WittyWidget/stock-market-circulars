## Summary

SEBI has issued a remittance order under Recovery Certificate No. 8765 of 2025 against Manish Mishra in connection with the matter of Pressure Sensitive Systems (India) Limited. This order directs compliance with the recovery proceedings initiated by SEBI for enforcement of dues.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8765 of 2025 issued against Manish Mishra
- Matter relates to Pressure Sensitive Systems (India) Limited
- Order directs remittance/payment compliance under SEBI recovery proceedings
- Part of SEBI's enforcement action for recovery of dues

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an individual enforcement order under existing SEBI recovery provisions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Manish Mishra is required to comply with the remittance order
- Payment must be made as per the recovery certificate directives
- Non-compliance may result in further enforcement action including attachment of assets

## Important Dates

- Order Date: October 9, 2025
- Recovery Certificate No: 8765 of 2025
- Specific payment deadlines would be mentioned in the detailed order

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is an individual recovery action and does not affect broader market operations or trading.

**Entity Impact:** High impact on Manish Mishra who must comply with the recovery order. Failure to comply may lead to asset attachment and other enforcement measures by SEBI.
