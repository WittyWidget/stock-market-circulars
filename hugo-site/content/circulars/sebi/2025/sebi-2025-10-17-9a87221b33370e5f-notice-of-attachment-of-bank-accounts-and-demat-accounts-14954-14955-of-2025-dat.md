## Summary

SEBI has issued attachment notices (No. 14954 & 14955 of 2025 dated October 17, 2025) for bank accounts and demat accounts belonging to Ashworth Constructions Pvt Ltd (PAN: AALCA3008J) in connection with recovery proceedings under Recovery Certificate No. 8337 of 2024 in the matter of Superior Finlease Ltd. This enforcement action enables SEBI to secure assets for recovery of dues.

## Key Points

- Entity: Ashworth Constructions Pvt Ltd (PAN: AALCA3008J)
- Notice Numbers: 14954 & 14955 of 2025
- Recovery Certificate No. 8337 of 2024
- Related Matter: Superior Finlease Ltd
- Action: Attachment of bank accounts and demat accounts
- Legal Authority: SEBI recovery and enforcement provisions

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This notice represents enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery framework for defaulters.

## Compliance Requirements

- Banks and depositories must comply with the attachment order and freeze specified accounts
- The defaulter entity (Ashworth Constructions Pvt Ltd) cannot operate the attached accounts until further orders
- Financial institutions must report account balances and holdings to SEBI
- All transactions in attached accounts are prohibited except as directed by SEBI

## Important Dates

- **October 17, 2025**: Date of attachment notice issuance
- Attachment effective immediately upon receipt by banks/depositories

## Impact Assessment

**Entity Impact**: Severe impact on Ashworth Constructions Pvt Ltd with complete freeze on bank and demat accounts affecting business operations and asset liquidity.

**Market Impact**: Minimal direct market impact as this affects a private limited company. However, it demonstrates SEBI's continued enforcement against defaulters in recovery matters.

**Investor Impact**: No direct impact on general investors. This action is specific to the defaulter entity and related recovery proceedings.

**Precedent Value**: Reinforces SEBI's authority and willingness to use attachment powers for recovery of dues from defaulting entities.
