## Summary

SEBI has cancelled Recovery Certificate No. 5285/2022 dated 31.07.2025 that was issued against Rajendra Pathak (PAN: AQBPP0893P) in connection with the matter of K Lifestyle and Industries Limited.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 5285/2022 has been cancelled
- Certificate was issued against Rajendra Pathak (PAN: AQBPP0893P)
- Matter relates to K Lifestyle and Industries Limited
- Cancellation effective from 31.07.2025

## Regulatory Changes

Cancellation of the recovery certificate removes the enforcement action previously initiated against the individual.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements arising from this cancellation order.

## Important Dates

- Recovery Certificate Cancellation Date: 31.07.2025

## Impact Assessment

The cancellation appears to be an administrative action with minimal direct market impact, primarily affecting the individual named in the original recovery proceedings.
