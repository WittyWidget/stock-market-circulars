## Summary

SEBI has issued a General Remittance Order dated 21.08.2025 under Recovery Certificate No. 8810 of 2025 drawn against Ms. Silamkoti Abilash in relation to proceedings involving Bodhtree Consulting Limited.

## Key Points

- Recovery Certificate No. 8810 of 2025 issued against Ms. Silamkoti Abilash
- Proceedings related to Bodhtree Consulting Limited
- General Remittance Order mechanism being utilized for recovery
- Order dated 21.08.2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced - this is an enforcement action under existing SEBI recovery framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Ms. Silamkoti Abilash must comply with the recovery order requirements as specified in the document. Non-compliance may lead to further enforcement measures.

## Important Dates

- Order Date: 21.08.2025
- Recovery Certificate No.: 8810 of 2025

## Impact Assessment

This represents ongoing enforcement action by SEBI against an individual in connection with Bodhtree Consulting Limited. The use of general remittance order indicates formal recovery proceedings are underway for outstanding dues or penalties.
