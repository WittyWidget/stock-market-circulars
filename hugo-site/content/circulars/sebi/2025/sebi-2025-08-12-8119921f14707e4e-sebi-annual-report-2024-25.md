## Summary

SEBI has released its Annual Report for FY 2024-25, a statutory document submitted to the Government of India and tabled in Parliament as mandated under the SEBI Act, 1992. The report covers SEBI's policies, programmes, activities and market trends for the period April 2024 to March 2025.

## Key Points

- Statutory annual report as required under SEBI Act, 1992
- Prepared according to SEBI (Annual Report) Rules, 2021
- Covers April 2024 to March 2025 period
- Available in both Hindi and English versions
- Published in Reports/Publications section of SEBI website

## Regulatory Changes

No specific regulatory changes announced in this circular - this is a publication announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements specified - this is an informational release about report availability.

## Important Dates

- Report covers period: April 2024 - March 2025
- Publication date: August 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This annual report provides transparency and accountability regarding SEBI's regulatory activities and market performance. It serves as an important reference document for market participants, researchers, and stakeholders to understand SEBI's policy direction and market developments during FY 2024-25.
