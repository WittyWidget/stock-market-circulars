## Summary

SEBI has issued a remittance advice against Kanta Devi (PAN: BAMPD7042J) under Recovery Certificate No. 8474 of 2024. This relates to trading activities violations in the scrip of Vikas Proppant and Granite Limited.

## Key Points

- Individual defaulter: Kanta Devi (PAN: BAMPD7042J)
- Recovery Certificate No. 8474 of 2024
- Related to trading activities in Vikas Proppant and Granite Limited
- SEBI enforcement action for recovery of dues

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is an enforcement action against a specific individual.

## Compliance Requirements

- Recovery proceedings initiated against the defaulter
- Payment of outstanding dues as per the recovery certificate
- Compliance with SEBI directives on trading activities

## Important Dates

Recovery Certificate No. 8474 of 2024 - specific dates would be mentioned in the detailed document.

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is targeted enforcement action against an individual defaulter. Reinforces SEBI's commitment to recovery proceedings against violators of trading regulations.
