## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of Fedbank Financial Services Limited issued on private placement basis. Two tranches totaling Rs. 15 crores (3000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) are now available for trading on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Two commercial paper tranches listed: FFSL-21-11-25-CP (2000 units) and FFSL-24-11-25-CP (1000 units)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,785 per unit
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded under specified ISIN numbers

## Important Dates

- First tranche allotment: August 22, 2025
- First tranche redemption: November 21, 2025
- Second tranche allotment: August 25, 2025
- Second tranche redemption: November 24, 2025
- Trading effective: August 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt investment options for institutional investors in the 90-91 day maturity range.
