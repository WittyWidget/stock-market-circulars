## Summary

Patel Retail Limited (Scrip Code: 544487) shares will be transferred from Trade for Trade segment (T Group) to Rolling segment (B Group) effective September 10, 2025. This change follows Exchange notice 20250825-56 dated August 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Patel Retail Limited
- Scrip Code: 544487
- Current segment: Trade for Trade (T Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (B Group)
- Effective date: September 10, 2025
- Contact: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022 2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

Transfer from restrictive Trade for Trade segment to more liquid Rolling segment, allowing normal intraday trading and settlement cycles.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the segment change and adjust their trading systems accordingly. No specific compliance actions required from investors.

## Important Dates

- Notice date: August 26, 2025
- Effective date: Wednesday, September 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Positive impact on liquidity as shares move from restrictive T Group to normal B Group trading. This allows for standard rolling settlement and removes trade-for-trade restrictions, potentially improving market participation and price discovery.
