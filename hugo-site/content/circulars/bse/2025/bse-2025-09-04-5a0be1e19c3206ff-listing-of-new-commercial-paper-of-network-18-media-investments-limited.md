## Summary

BSE announces the listing of Network 18 Media & Investments Limited's commercial paper worth Rs. 30 crores (6000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on the debt segment, effective September 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper issued on private placement basis
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,727.50 per unit
- Total quantity: 6,000 units
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- ISIN: INE870H14VW6
- Scrip code: 730145

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Ltd acts as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: September 4, 2025
- Redemption date: December 4, 2025
- Trading effective: September 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The 3-month maturity period indicates standard corporate financing operations.
