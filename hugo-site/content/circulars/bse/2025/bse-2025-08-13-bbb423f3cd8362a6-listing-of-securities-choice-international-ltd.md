## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 6 million new equity shares of Choice International Ltd (Scrip Code: 531358) issued through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will commence trading on August 14, 2025, with a lock-in period until February 14, 2026.

## Key Points

- 6 million equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at Rs. 290/- premium
- Total issue price: Rs. 300/- per share
- Issued to non-promoters through warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinguished numbers: 199655501 to 205655500
- Allotment date: June 4, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. Standard listing procedure for warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability
- Lock-in compliance required until February 14, 2026
- ISIN code: INE102B01014 to be used for trading

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: June 4, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 14, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: February 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Significant equity dilution for Choice International Ltd with 6 million new shares entering the market. The preferential allotment to non-promoters may affect promoter shareholding percentage. Lock-in period provides temporary supply constraint until February 2026.
