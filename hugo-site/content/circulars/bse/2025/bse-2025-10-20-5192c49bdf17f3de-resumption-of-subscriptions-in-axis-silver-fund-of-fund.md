## Summary

Axis Asset Management Company Limited has withdrawn the temporary suspension of subscriptions to units in Axis Silver Fund of Fund. All applications for lumpsum and switch-ins under the scheme will be accepted effective October 20, 2025. This follows Notice no. 71 dated October 13, 2025, which had imposed the temporary suspension.

## Key Points

- Temporary suspension of subscriptions to Axis Silver Fund of Fund is being withdrawn
- Lumpsum and switch-in applications will be resumed from October 20, 2025
- All other terms and conditions of the Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM) remain unchanged
- Notice issued by Axis Asset Management Company Limited (CIN: U65991MH2009PLC189558)
- Issued by Gop Kumar Bhaskaran, Managing Director & Chief Executive Officer

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notice reversing a previously imposed temporary subscription suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors can now submit subscription applications for Axis Silver Fund of Fund
- All applications for lumpsum and switch-ins will be processed as per normal scheme terms
- This notice cum addendum forms an integral part of the SID & KIM of the scheme

## Important Dates

- **October 13, 2025**: Date of Notice no. 71 imposing temporary suspension
- **October 17, 2025**: Date of current notice (Notice no. 74/2025-26)
- **October 20, 2025**: Effective date for resumption of subscriptions (lumpsum and switch-ins)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This affects only investors interested in Axis Silver Fund of Fund, a niche commodity-focused fund of fund scheme.

**Operational Impact**: Positive for potential investors who were waiting to invest in this scheme during the suspension period. Normal subscription operations resume.

**Investor Impact**: Investors can now make fresh investments or switch into this silver-themed fund of fund scheme, providing access to silver as an asset class through mutual fund route.
