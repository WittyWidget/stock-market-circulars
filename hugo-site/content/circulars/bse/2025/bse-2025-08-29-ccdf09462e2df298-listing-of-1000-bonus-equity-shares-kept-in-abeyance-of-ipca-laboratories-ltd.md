## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 1000 bonus equity shares of Ipca Laboratories Ltd (Scrip Code: 524494) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares will be available for trading from September 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- 1000 bonus equity shares of Rs. 1/- each being listed
- Shares were previously kept in abeyance
- ISIN: INE571A01038
- Distinctive numbers: 1171453 to 1172452
- Trading permitted from Monday, September 1, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - standard listing procedure for bonus shares released from abeyance.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 1, 2025 (Monday)
- **Notice Date**: August 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a small number of shares (1000) for one company. The release from abeyance is a routine administrative action that makes previously allocated bonus shares available for trading.
