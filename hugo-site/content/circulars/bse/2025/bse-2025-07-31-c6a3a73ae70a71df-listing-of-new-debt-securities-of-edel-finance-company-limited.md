## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Edel Finance Company Limited on private placement basis, effective July 31, 2025. Two series of securities totaling ₹400 crores are now available for trading on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Two new debt security series listed: EFCL30725 and EFCL300725
- Total issue size: ₹400 crores (₹100 crores + ₹300 crores)
- Securities trade in dematerialized form only
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Credit rating: CRISIL A+/STABLE for both series
- Face value: ₹1,00,000 per security

## Security Details

**Series 1 (EFCL30725):**
- Quantity: 1,000 securities
- ISIN: INE836K07304
- Issue price: ₹96,488
- Scrip code: 976967

**Series 2 (EFCL300725):**
- Quantity: 3,000 securities  
- ISIN: INE836K07312
- Issue price: ₹95,669
- Scrip code: 976968

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: July 30, 2025
- Trading commencement: July 31, 2025
- Redemption date: October 30, 2028

## Trading Information

- Market lot: 1 security each
- Interest payment: As per Information Memorandum
- Put/Call options: As per Information Memorandum (Series 1), NA (Series 2)
- Further details available in Placement Memorandum on BSE website

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt listing with minimal market impact. Provides additional investment options for institutional investors in the debt segment.
