## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for KOLTE-PATIL DEVELOPERS LTD. The takeover process will run from August 22, 2025 to September 8, 2025, with settlement number 2025073.

## Key Points

- Takeover settlement number: 2025073
- Bid period: August 22, 2025 to September 8, 2025
- Obligation download date: September 19, 2025
- Settlement date: September 22, 2025
- Demat shares must be delivered through Early Pay-in mechanism only
- Both CDSL and NSDL depositories support this process

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Process follows existing Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange as per Notice No.20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "(Takeover)" and settlement No. 2025073 in depository systems
- Early pay-in of securities must be completed sufficiently in advance of BSE OTB bidding window closure
- Securities will be automatically earmarked/blocked in client accounts
- No EPN file upload required by Members & Custodians
- Members must refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges

## Important Dates

- **August 22, 2025**: Bid start date
- **September 8, 2025**: Bid end date
- **September 19, 2025**: Obligation download date
- **September 22, 2025**: Settlement date

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact limited to KOLTE-PATIL DEVELOPERS LTD shareholders and market participants involved in the takeover process. Standard settlement procedures apply with specific timeline requirements for early pay-in mechanisms.
