## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 400 crore issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis. The securities will trade in dematerialized form with a face value of Rs. 5 lakh each and mature on September 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- 8,000 Commercial Paper units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 400 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,97,885.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 729718, ISIN: INE01C314CE3
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Allotment date: August 28, 2025
- Maturity date: September 22, 2025
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise
- Market lot size of 1 unit

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 28, 2025
- Listing Date: August 29, 2025
- Redemption Date: September 22, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides additional liquidity options for institutional investors in the debt segment with high credit rating (A1+) ensuring low credit risk.
