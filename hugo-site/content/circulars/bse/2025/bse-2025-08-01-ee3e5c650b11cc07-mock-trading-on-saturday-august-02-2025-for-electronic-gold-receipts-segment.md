## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, August 2, 2025. This session is designed for testing and familiarization purposes, allowing trading members to test their systems and applications without any financial obligations.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for EGR segment on Saturday, August 2, 2025
- Trading timings: Login 10:15 AM, Pre-open 11:00 AM, Continuous trading 11:15 AM - 3:30 PM
- No new BOLT TWS release for this session
- Trades will not create any margin obligations or settlement obligations
- Members can test third-party platforms and in-house systems via IML/ETI APIs
- Feedback required by 4:30 PM on the same day

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine testing session in accordance with existing SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members may participate in either mock trading session or UAT environment
- Members using third-party platforms should utilize this opportunity for testing
- Active participation encouraged for system testing and familiarization
- Feedback submission required by 4:30 PM on August 2, 2025

## Important Dates

- **August 2, 2025**: Mock trading session
  - Login: 10:15 AM - 11:00 AM
  - Pre-Open: 11:00 AM - 11:08 AM
  - Matching: 11:08 AM - 11:15 AM
  - Continuous Trading: 11:15 AM - 3:30 PM
  - Block Deal Window: 12:00 PM - 12:15 PM
  - Closing: 3:30 PM - 3:40 PM
  - Post-closing: 3:40 PM - 3:50 PM
- **Feedback Deadline**: 4:30 PM on August 2, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a mock trading session with no financial obligations. The session serves operational purposes for system testing and member familiarization with EGR segment trading. No actual trades or settlements will occur, making this a low-impact administrative notice.
