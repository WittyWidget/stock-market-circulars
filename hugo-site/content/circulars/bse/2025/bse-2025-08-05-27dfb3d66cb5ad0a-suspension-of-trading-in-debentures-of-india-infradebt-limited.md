## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debentures of INDIA INFRADEBT LIMITED (Code: INE537P07513) effective August 8, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date of August 10, 2025 for redemption of debentures and payment of interest.

## Key Points

- Company: INDIA INFRADEBT LIMITED
- Debenture Code: IIL-8.35%-25-8-25-PVT (INE537P07513, 959312)
- Record Date: August 10, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: August 8, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Reference: DR-691/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard trading suspension procedure for corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from August 8, 2025
- All exchange trading members are advised to take note of this suspension
- Compliance with record date procedures for debenture redemption and interest payment

## Important Dates

- **August 5, 2025**: Notice issued
- **August 8, 2025**: Trading suspension begins (no dealings permitted)
- **August 10, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this affects only holders of specific INDIA INFRADEBT LIMITED debentures. Standard corporate action procedure ensuring proper settlement of redemption and interest payments to eligible debenture holders as per record date.
