## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 1,25,00,000 equity shares of Leading Leasing Finance and Investment Co. Limited (Scrip Code: 540360) effective from August 14, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium to non-promoters through warrant conversion.

## Key Points

- 1.25 crore equity shares of Rs. 1/- each listed
- Issued at premium of Rs. 6.40/- (total issue price Rs. 7.40/-)
- Allotted to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 524565501 to 537065500
- ISIN: INE715Q01029

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure for warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability
- Lock-in period compliance required until February 27, 2026
- All 12.5 million shares subject to lock-in restrictions

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: June 25, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 14, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: February 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine listing of preferential allotment shares. The lock-in period until February 2026 ensures these shares cannot be immediately traded, limiting any immediate supply pressure on the stock.
