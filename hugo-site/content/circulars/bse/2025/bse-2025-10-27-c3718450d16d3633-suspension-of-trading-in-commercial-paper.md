## Summary

BSE has announced suspension of trading in commercial papers of 14 companies due to scheduled redemptions. Trading members are advised not to deal in these commercial papers from the specified dates. The suspensions are effective from October 29, 2025 through November 4, 2025 based on respective record dates.

## Key Points

- 14 companies have fixed record dates for commercial paper redemption
- Trading suspension applies to debt segment only
- No dealings permitted from the specified suspension dates
- Record dates range from October 29 to November 3, 2025
- All redemptions are scheduled commercial paper maturities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine corporate action notification for commercial paper redemptions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial papers from the suspension dates
- Members must ensure their systems reflect the trading suspension for affected securities
- No new positions should be created in these instruments from the suspension date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Limited to debt segment participants holding or trading these specific commercial papers. No impact on equity trading.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must update their systems to prevent inadvertent trading in suspended instruments. Investors holding these commercial papers will receive redemption proceeds as per the terms.

**Investor Impact:** Holders of these commercial papers will have their investments redeemed on schedule. No liquidity available for exit before redemption date due to trading suspension.

**Scope:** Affects 14 companies across diverse sectors including financials, manufacturing, energy, and real estate development.
