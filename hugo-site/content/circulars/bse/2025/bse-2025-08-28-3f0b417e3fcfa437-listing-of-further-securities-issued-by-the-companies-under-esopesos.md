## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of further securities issued by 13 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be listed and admitted for trading effective Friday, August 29, 2025. All listed securities have no lock-in period restrictions.

## Key Points

- 13 companies are issuing additional securities under ESOP/ESOS
- Total of 5,285,535 new shares across all companies
- Securities will be available for trading from August 29, 2025
- No lock-in periods apply to any of the listed securities
- Face values range from Re. 1 to Rs. 10 per share

## Companies and Securities Details

**Major Issuances:**
- Vishal Mega Mart Limited: 7,933,333 shares (Rs. 10 face value)
- Motilal Oswal Financial Services: 899,165 shares (Re. 1 face value)
- Aptus Value Housing Finance: 222,439 shares (Rs. 2 face value)
- Fedbank Financial Services: 168,500 shares (Rs. 10 face value)

**Other Notable Companies:**
- ICICI Prudential Life Insurance, SBI Life Insurance, UTI Asset Management, Wipro, Marico, Hindustan Construction, Indegene, IndoStar Capital Finance, and Poonawalla Fincorp

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for ESOP/ESOS securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new listings
- Securities are immediately available for trading without restrictions
- Standard trading rules and procedures apply

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 28, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date**: August 29, 2025
- **Lock-in Period**: None applicable (NA for all securities)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine ESOP issuances with relatively small quantities compared to existing share capital. The absence of lock-in periods allows immediate liquidity for these securities.
