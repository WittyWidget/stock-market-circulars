## Summary

Continental Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 538868) is listing 15,00,000 new equity shares of Rs. 2/- each on BSE effective Monday, August 11, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 19/- (total issue price Rs. 21/-) to promoters and non-promoters on preferential basis following warrant conversion.

## Key Points

- 15,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each being listed
- Issue price: Rs. 21/- per share (Rs. 2/- face value + Rs. 19/- premium)
- Shares issued to both promoters and non-promoters on preferential basis
- Securities rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE183Q01020
- Distribution numbers: 26253001 to 27753000

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard equity listing procedure following preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities listing
- Lock-in provisions applicable as per SEBI regulations
- Securities permitted for trading from August 11, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Dates**: May 7, 12, and 16, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 11, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: 
  - 5,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 26253001-26753000): March 25, 2027
  - 5,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 26753001-27253000): May 31, 2026
  - 5,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 27253001-27753000): May 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. Routine equity listing with standard lock-in provisions. The preferential allotment increases Continental Securities' equity base by 15 lakh shares, providing additional liquidity once lock-in periods expire.
