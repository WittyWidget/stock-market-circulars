## Summary

QUANT MUTUAL FUND will launch a New Fund Offer (NFO) for QSIF EQUITY EX-TOP 100 LONG-SHORT FUND, a Specialized Investment Fund (SIF), on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The NFO period runs from October 24, 2025, to November 7, 2025, with a tentative allotment date of November 12, 2025. The fund offers six plan variants across Regular and Direct plans with IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, and Growth options.

## Key Points

- NFO period: October 24, 2025 to November 7, 2025
- Six scheme variants offered: Regular Plan (IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, Growth) and Direct Plan (IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, Growth)
- All schemes categorized under Equity with unique ISINs assigned
- Tentative allotment date: November 12, 2025
- Extended trading available until midnight on November 7, 2025 for lumpsum purchases only
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on November 12, 2025
- Cheques and OTM payments acceptable during NFO period subject to clear funds receipt by the deadline

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard NFO launch within existing BSE StAR MF Platform framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client-initiated fund payments by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid transaction cancellation
- Cancelled transactions will be reported to RTA/AMC with CC and N flag
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on November 12, 2025 for allotment
- Pending transactions remain in system until 3:00 PM on last day of NFO funds payout or T+5, whichever is earlier

## Important Dates

- **October 24, 2025**: NFO opening date
- **November 7, 2025**: NFO closing date (extended until midnight for lumpsum purchases)
- **November 12, 2025**: Tentative allotment date; clear funds deadline at 6:00 PM in ICCL Account
- **T+1 day**: Client must initiate funds by 9:00 AM to avoid cancellation
- **T+5 or last NFO payout day**: Pending transaction deadline at 3:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine NFO launch that expands investment options but does not affect existing market operations or trading mechanisms.

**Operational Impact**: Low to Medium for BSE StAR MF Platform members - Requires standard NFO processing procedures including transaction monitoring, fund collection, and timely settlement to meet the November 12 allotment deadline.

**Investor Impact**: Provides new investment opportunity in a long-short equity strategy focusing on stocks outside the top 100, offering portfolio diversification for investors seeking specialized investment vehicles.
