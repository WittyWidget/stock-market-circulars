## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Fabtech Technologies Limited (Scrip Code: 544558) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (T Group) to the Rolling segment (B Group) effective October 23, 2025. This change follows the exchange's earlier notice dated October 6, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Fabtech Technologies Limited
- Scrip Code: 544558
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade (T Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (B Group)
- Effective Date: October 23, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251007-16
- Continuation of earlier notice: 20251006-64 dated October 6, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for Fabtech Technologies Limited shares will change from Trade for Trade settlement to Rolling settlement, allowing for normal market operations with T+2 settlement cycle instead of full upfront payment and delivery requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the segment change for operational adjustments
- Contact person for queries: Mr. Parag Jain, Tel: 022 2272 8685
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new group classification

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 7, 2025
- Previous Notice: October 6, 2025 (Notice No. 20251006-64)
- Effective Date of Transfer: October 23, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This change represents a positive development for Fabtech Technologies Limited as the transfer from T Group to B Group indicates improved compliance and operational status. The shift to Rolling segment will:

- Enhance liquidity by allowing intraday trading
- Enable margin trading and derivatives participation (subject to eligibility)
- Remove the restrictions associated with Trade for Trade settlement
- Improve accessibility for retail and institutional investors
- Potentially increase trading volumes and price discovery

The move suggests the company has met BSE's criteria for graduation from the restrictive T Group segment.
