## Summary

Wallet Circle Technologies Limited has announced an open offer to acquire up to 12,72,133 equity shares (25.89% of total voting share capital) of Alchemist Corporation Limited at ₹16 per share. This offer is made pursuant to SEBI (SAST) Regulations 2011 for substantial acquisition accompanied with change in control.

## Key Points

- Acquirer: Wallet Circle Technologies Limited (CIN: U93000DL2013PLC250233)
- Target Company: Alchemist Corporation Limited (CIN: L74899DL1993PLC055768)
- Offer Price: ₹16 per equity share (face value ₹10)
- Offer Size: 12,72,133 equity shares representing 25.89% of total voting share capital
- Offer restricted to shares held by public shareholders only
- Open offer triggered under Regulations 3(1) and 4 of SEBI (SAST) Regulations 2011
- Not conditional upon minimum acceptance level
- No competing offers as of the date of Letter of Offer

## Regulatory Changes

None specified in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public shareholders must respond to the open offer through proper tendering process
- Acquirer must comply with SEBI (SAST) Regulations 2011
- Any statutory approvals required before closure of tendering period must be obtained
- Marketable lot is 1 share

## Important Dates

- Offer Size calculation basis: 10th working day from closure of tendering period
- Last date for competing offers has expired
- Specific tendering period dates not mentioned in the excerpt

## Impact Assessment

High impact takeover involving change in control of Alchemist Corporation Limited. Public shareholders holding 25.89% stake will have opportunity to exit at ₹16 per share. The acquisition represents substantial ownership change and may affect future operations and strategic direction of the target company.
