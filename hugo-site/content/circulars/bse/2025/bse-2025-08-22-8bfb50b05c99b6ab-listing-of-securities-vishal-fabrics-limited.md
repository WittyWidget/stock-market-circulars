## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 7,500,000 equity shares of Vishal Fabrics Limited (Scrip Code: 538598) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares will be available for trading from Monday, August 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- 7.5 million equity shares of Rs. 5 each issued at premium of Rs. 25.60
- Issue price: Rs. 30.60 per share
- Shares issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 217610004 to 225110003
- Date of allotment: July 15, 2025
- ISIN: INE755Q01025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed about the new securities listing
- Lock-in period applies to all issued shares until March 14, 2026

## Important Dates

- **August 25, 2025**: Trading commencement date
- **July 15, 2025**: Date of allotment
- **March 14, 2026**: Lock-in expiry date

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact expected as this is a routine preferential issue listing for a smaller company. The lock-in period until March 2026 means these shares cannot be freely traded immediately, reducing immediate supply pressure on the stock.
