## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for Currency Derivatives segment on Saturday, August 23, 2025. This session is designed for trading members to test their trading applications, including third-party platforms and in-house systems using ETI APIs. No new BOLT TWS release is planned.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for August 23, 2025
- Available for Currency Derivatives and Cross Currency Derivatives segments
- No new BOLT TWS release
- Testing opportunity for third-party platforms and in-house systems
- Trades will not create any obligations or liabilities
- Active participation encouraged with feedback requested

## Trading Schedule

- **Log-in**: 10:15 AM to 11:00 AM
- **Continuous Trading (Currency Derivatives)**: 11:00 AM to 2:00 PM
- **Continuous Trading (Cross Currency Derivatives)**: 11:00 AM to 2:00 PM
- **Trade Modification (Currency)**: 2:10 PM
- **Trade Modification (Cross Currency)**: 2:10 PM

## Important Notes

- Mock trades will not attract margin obligations
- No pay-in and pay-out obligations
- No rights and liabilities created from mock trading
- Feedback required by 3:00 PM on trading day

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a testing session only. Benefits include system validation and familiarization for trading members before actual trading sessions.
