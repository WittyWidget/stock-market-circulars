## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on its debt segment effective August 20, 2025. The securities are market-linked instruments issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1 lakh each and mature on January 22, 2029.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 660 securities listed
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 96,888 per security
- ISIN: INE918K07PX1 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Scrip Code: 976954
- Credit Rating: CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Refer to Placement Memorandum for complete details

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 18, 2025
- Listing Date: August 20, 2025
- Interest Payment Date: January 22, 2029 (cumulative)
- Redemption Date: January 22, 2029

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing for a specific financial institution. The securities are market-linked instruments with a 4-year tenure, providing investors with exposure to Nuvama Wealth Finance's credit profile.
