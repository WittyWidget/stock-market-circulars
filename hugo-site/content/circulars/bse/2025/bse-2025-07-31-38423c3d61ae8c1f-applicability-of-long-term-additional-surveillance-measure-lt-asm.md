## Summary

BSE has announced the applicability of Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective August 1, 2025. The circular lists securities being added to and removed from the LT-ASM framework, along with a consolidated list of all securities currently under various ASM stages.

## Key Points

- 5 securities newly shortlisted for Long Term ASM Framework
- 1 security (Remi Edelstahl Tubulars Ltd) moving out of LT-ASM due to inclusion in ESM Framework
- No securities moving between ASM stages
- Consolidated list shows securities across different ASM stages (I, II, III, IV)
- Framework includes special markings for T+0 scrips and various compliance categories

## Securities Added to LT-ASM

- Advait Energy Transitions Ltd (543230)
- Arman Holdings Ltd (538556)
- Garuda Construction and Engineering Ltd (544271)
- Hilltone Software and Gases Ltd (544308)
- Hind Rectifiers Ltd (504036)

## Securities Removed from LT-ASM

- Remi Edelstahl Tubulars Ltd (513043) - moved to ESM Framework

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 1, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Securities under LT-ASM framework face enhanced surveillance measures including stricter position limits, margin requirements, and monitoring. The addition of 5 new securities expands regulatory oversight while the removal of 1 security indicates successful compliance or transfer to other regulatory frameworks. This affects trading strategies and risk management for market participants dealing with these securities.
