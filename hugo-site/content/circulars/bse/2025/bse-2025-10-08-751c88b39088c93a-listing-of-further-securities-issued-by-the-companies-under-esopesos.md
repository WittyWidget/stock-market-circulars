## Summary

BSE Limited has announced the listing and admission for trading of additional securities issued by six companies under their Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Scheme (ESOS). A total of 952,001 new shares will be available for trading with effect from Thursday, October 9, 2025. None of the securities are subject to lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 6 companies issuing additional shares under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Total of 952,001 shares being listed
- Effective trading date: October 9, 2025
- No lock-in period applicable to any of the listed securities
- Notice number: 20251008-29 dated October 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - The total number of shares being listed (952,001) represents a relatively small addition to the existing share base of these companies. ICICI Bank accounts for the largest portion with 519,475 shares, followed by Shriram Properties with 301,485 shares.

**Liquidity Impact:** Minimal - The additional shares may marginally increase the free float of these securities, but the impact on overall liquidity is expected to be negligible given the scale.

**Dilution Impact:** Minimal - ESOP/ESOS issuances are routine corporate actions and typically represent a small percentage of total outstanding shares.

**Operational Impact:** None - This is a standard listing process with no lock-in restrictions, allowing immediate trading of these securities.
