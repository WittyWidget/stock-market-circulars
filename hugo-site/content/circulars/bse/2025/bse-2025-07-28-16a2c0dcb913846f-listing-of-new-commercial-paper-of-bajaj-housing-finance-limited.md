## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Bajaj Housing Finance Limited worth Rs. 250 crores (5,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on private placement basis. The commercial paper carries CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ ratings with a tenure of approximately 3 months.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 5,000 units (Total value: Rs. 250 crores)
- Scrip Code: 729906
- ISIN: INE377Y14BM0
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,788 per unit
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Central Bank of India acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 25, 2025
- Listing Date: July 28, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 24, 2025
- Tenure: Approximately 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding option for Bajaj Housing Finance Limited and investment avenue for institutional investors in the debt segment.
