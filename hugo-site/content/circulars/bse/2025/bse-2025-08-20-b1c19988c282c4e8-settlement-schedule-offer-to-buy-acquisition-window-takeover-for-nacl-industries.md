## Summary

BSE announces settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy - Acquisition Window (Takeover) for NACL Industries Limited, with bidding period from August 22 to September 8, 2025, and settlement on September 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- Settlement No: 2025074 for NACL Industries takeover offer
- Bidding window: August 22, 2025 to September 8, 2025
- Obligation download: September 19, 2025
- Final settlement: September 22, 2025
- Demat shares delivery only through Early Pay-in mechanism
- Market type "(Takeover)" to be used in depository system

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Process follows existing Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange as per Notice No.20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "(Takeover)" and settlement No.2025074 in depository system
- Early pay-in of securities must be completed sufficiently in advance before BSE OTB bidding window closure
- CDSL & NSDL securities will be automatically earmarked/blocked in client accounts
- No EPN file upload required for early pay-in
- Members to refer MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges

## Important Dates

- **August 22, 2025**: Bid start date
- **September 8, 2025**: Bid end date
- **September 19, 2025**: Obligation download
- **September 22, 2025**: Settlement date

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact limited to NACL Industries shareholders and participating members. Establishes clear timeline for takeover settlement process. Early pay-in mechanism ensures smooth settlement execution. Contact available through ICCL Help Desk (022-69158590/45720490/45720690) for clarifications.
