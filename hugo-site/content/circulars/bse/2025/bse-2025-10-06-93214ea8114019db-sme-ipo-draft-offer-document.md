## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of JAIN INTERNATIONAL POWER LIMITED filed on September 30, 2025 on its SME website for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will be available for at least 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by JAIN INTERNATIONAL POWER LIMITED on September 30, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments solicited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and public can review the Draft Offer Document on BSE SME website
- Comments, if any, should be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within 21 days from October 06, 2025
- Company must host document for minimum 21-day public comment period as per ICDR Regulations

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filed:** September 30, 2025
- **Notice Date:** October 06, 2025
- **Public Comment Deadline:** October 27, 2025 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains to a single SME company's IPO process. The notice is primarily informational for potential investors and stakeholders interested in JAIN INTERNATIONAL POWER LIMITED's public offering. The public comment period is a standard regulatory requirement under ICDR Regulations for SME IPOs.
