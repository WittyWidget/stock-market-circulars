## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement schedule for TANLA PLATFORMS LIMITED's buyback offer through acquisition window. The buyback will be conducted from July 29, 2025 to August 4, 2025 with settlement number 2025064.

## Key Points

- Buyback period: July 29, 2025 to August 4, 2025
- Settlement number: 2025064
- Obligation download date: August 8, 2025
- Final settlement date: August 11, 2025
- Demat shares must be delivered through Early Pay-in mechanism only
- Market type "Buyback" to be used in depository systems

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Process follows existing guidelines as per Notice No.20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020 regarding revised guidelines for share acquisition through stock exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians must use Market Type "Buyback" and settlement No. 2025064 in depository system
- Early pay-in of securities must be completed sufficiently in advance before BSE OTB bidding window closure
- CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities will be automatically earmarked/blocked in client accounts
- No EPN file upload required
- Members should refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges

## Important Dates

- **July 29, 2025**: Bid start date
- **August 4, 2025**: Bid end date
- **August 8, 2025**: Obligation download date
- **August 11, 2025**: Settlement date

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact limited to TANLA PLATFORMS shareholders and trading members participating in the buyback. Standard settlement procedures apply with emphasis on early pay-in mechanism to ensure smooth processing. Members and custodians need to follow specific procedural requirements for successful participation.
