## Summary

Bombay Stock Exchange has published the demat auction settlement No. 695 dated August 18, 2025, listing securities and their respective quantities that are part of the auction settlement process.

## Key Points

- Settlement number: 695
- Settlement date: August 18, 2025
- Total securities listed: Over 80 different scrips
- Quantities range from 1 share to 679,456 shares (QUADRANT TEL)
- Notable high-quantity securities include PRADHIN LIMI (277,567 shares), QUADRANT TEL (679,456 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes specified in this settlement notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must note the securities and quantities listed for settlement
- Relevant parties must ensure proper settlement of the listed securities
- Standard demat auction settlement procedures apply

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: August 18, 2025
- Settlement Number: 695

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on affected securities and their shareholders. The auction settlement process will facilitate proper delivery and settlement of the listed securities. Notable quantities in major stocks like RELIANCE IND (253 shares), ITC LTD (849 shares), and STATE BANK O (394 shares) indicate routine settlement activities. The large quantity of QUADRANT TEL (679,456 shares) and PRADHIN LIMI (277,567 shares) may indicate significant settlement requirements for these specific securities.
