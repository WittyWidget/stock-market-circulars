## Summary

Sihora Industries Limited has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) dated October 16, 2025. The filing discloses the distribution of 5,327,400 total equity shares among 252 shareholders, with promoter and promoter group holding 69.97% and public shareholders holding 30.03%. All shares are fully paid up with no partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, locked-in shares, pledged shares, or encumbrances.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 5,327,400 shares held by 252 shareholders
- Promoter & Promoter Group: 7 shareholders holding 3,727,400 shares (69.97%)
- Public shareholders: 245 shareholders holding 1,600,000 shares (30.03%)
- All shares are fully paid up and in dematerialized form
- No partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No shares are locked-in, pledged, or encumbered
- No depository receipts issued against shares
- No equity shares with differential voting rights
- No significant beneficial owners identified

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard shareholding pattern disclosure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Filing submitted under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Declaration confirms no partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, outstanding ESOPs, depository receipts, locked-in shares, pledged shares, non-disposal undertakings, or other encumbrances
- All shareholding data provided as per SCRR, 1957 requirements

## Important Dates

- Filing Date: October 16, 2025
- Pattern as on: October 16, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine shareholding pattern disclosure for Sihora Industries Limited following its equity listing. The shareholding structure shows standard promoter dominance at 69.97% with adequate public float at 30.03%, meeting minimum public shareholding requirements. The absence of encumbrances, pledges, or convertible securities indicates clean capital structure. No immediate market impact expected as this is an informational filing for a newly listed company.
