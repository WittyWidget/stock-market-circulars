## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Bajaj Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis. The instrument consists of 5,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 25 crores, with a 91-day maturity period from July 25, 2025 to October 24, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper worth Rs. 25 crores (5,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,788 per unit (discount to face value of Rs. 5 lakh)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+
- ISIN: INE377Y14BM0
- Scrip Code: 729906
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Central Bank of India acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 25, 2025
- Listing Date: July 28, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 24, 2025
- Maturity Period: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding for Bajaj Housing Finance Limited and offers investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
