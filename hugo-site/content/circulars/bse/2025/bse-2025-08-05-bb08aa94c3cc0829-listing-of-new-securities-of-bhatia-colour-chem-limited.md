## Summary

BSE has listed new securities of Bhatia Colour Chem Limited totaling 356,266 shares across 19 different allotments with varying lock-in periods ending between February 28, 2026 and March 1, 2026.

## Key Points

- Total shares allotted: 356,266
- 19 separate allotments with distinctive number ranges
- Lock-in periods expire on February 28, 2026 and March 1, 2026
- Distinctive numbers range from 13740950 to 14097215
- Largest single allotment: 70,400 shares
- Smallest single allotment: 2,667 shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes specified in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Shares are subject to lock-in restrictions until the specified dates. Trading of these securities will be restricted during the lock-in period.

## Important Dates

- Lock-in expiry dates: February 28, 2026 and March 1, 2026
- Securities can be freely traded after lock-in period expires

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine listing of new securities. The lock-in period ensures orderly market participation once restrictions are lifted.
