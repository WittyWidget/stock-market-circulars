## Summary

ICICI Home Finance Company Limited has listed new Commercial Paper securities on BSE's Debt segment effective August 18, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a total value of Rs. 200 crores.

## Key Points

- 4,000 Commercial Paper securities of Rs. 5,00,000 each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 4,96,873 per security
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- ISIN: INE071G14HA8 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Scrip Code: 730026
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Limited serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 18, 2025
- Listing Date: August 18, 2025
- Redemption Date: September 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides additional short-term investment options for institutional investors in the debt market.
