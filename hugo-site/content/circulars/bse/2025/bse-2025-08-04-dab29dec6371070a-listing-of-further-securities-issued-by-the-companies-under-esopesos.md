## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of additional securities from 11 companies issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be listed and admitted for trading effective Tuesday, August 5, 2025. All listed shares have no lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 11 companies are issuing additional securities under ESOP/ESOS programs
- Total of 1,276,081 new shares across all companies
- All securities will be available for trading from August 5, 2025
- No lock-in period applies to any of the listed securities
- Face values range from ₹1 to ₹10 per share

## Company Details

**Major Issuances:**
- SBFC Finance Limited: 406,657 shares (10 face value)
- IDFC FIRST BANK LIMITED: 411,445 shares (₹10 face value)
- PNB Housing Finance Limited: 318,367 shares (₹10 face value)
- POONAWALLA FINCORP LIMITED: 202,068 shares (₹2 face value)
- Quess Corp Limited: 135,491 shares (₹10 face value)

**Other Companies:**
- CreditAccess Grameen Ltd: 3,050 shares
- Eicher Motors Ltd: 25,750 shares
- LTIMindtree Limited: 67,252 shares
- Mahindra Lifespace Developers Ltd: 12,195 shares
- NACL Industries Limited: 21,500 shares
- NIIT Ltd: 78,304 shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing process for employee stock option exercises.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the new securities for trading purposes. No additional compliance requirements specified.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Trading Date**: Tuesday, August 5, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine ESOP share listings. The additional shares represent employee option exercises and will increase the free float of the respective companies. No price impact anticipated due to the relatively small quantities compared to existing share bases.
