## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Om Freight Forwarders Limited (Scrip Code: 544564) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (T Group) to the Rolling segment (B Group) effective Friday, October 24, 2025. This follows the exchange's previous notice dated October 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 544564 (Om Freight Forwarders Limited)
- Transfer from T Group (Trade for Trade) to B Group (Rolling segment)
- Change continuation of Notice No. 20251007-53 dated October 7, 2025
- Contact: Mr. Parag Jain (Tel: 022 2272 8685) for further details

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will move from Trade for Trade settlement mechanism to normal Rolling settlement, allowing for standard T+1 settlement cycles instead of compulsory delivery-based trading.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the segment change and update their systems accordingly to reflect the new trading group classification for the scrip.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 8, 2025
- **Effective Date:** October 24, 2025 (Friday)
- **Previous Notice:** October 7, 2025 (Notice No. 20251007-53)

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from T Group to B Group is generally positive as it removes trade-for-trade restrictions, potentially improving liquidity and allowing intraday trading. This indicates improved compliance or surveillance parameters for the company, enabling normal market operations for the scrip.
