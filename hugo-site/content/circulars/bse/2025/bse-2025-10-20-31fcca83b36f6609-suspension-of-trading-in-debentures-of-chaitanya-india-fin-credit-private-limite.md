## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debentures of CHAITANYA INDIA FIN CREDIT PRIVATE LIMITED (ISIN: INE140R08106, Code: 975194) effective October 24, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing October 26, 2025 as the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest on its 9.75% debentures maturing November 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading members advised not to deal in the specified debentures from October 24, 2025
- Record date set for October 26, 2025
- Debenture: CIFCPL-9.75%-10-11-25-PVT (INE140R08106)
- Security code: 975194
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice reference: DR-742/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational suspension related to corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the specified debentures from October 24, 2025
- Trading members must ensure their systems reflect the trading suspension
- No transactions should be executed in the affected security during the suspension period

## Important Dates

- **October 20, 2025**: Circular issued
- **October 24, 2025**: No dealings from this date (trading suspension begins)
- **October 26, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and redemption
- **November 10, 2025**: Debenture maturity date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and traders of this specific debenture issue. The suspension is procedural in nature to facilitate the redemption and interest payment process.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to prevent trades in this security from October 24, 2025. Debenture holders will receive their interest payment and redemption proceeds based on the October 26, 2025 record date.

**Investor Impact**: Current debenture holders will be identified as of the record date for receiving interest and principal redemption. New investors cannot acquire this security during the suspension period.
