## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by SIGNATUREGLOBAL (INDIA) LIMITED on private placement basis, effective October 17, 2025. The securities are 11% quarterly interest paying debentures with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, maturing on January 15, 2029. The securities are rated CARE A+/Stable and will trade in dematerialized form only on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977218, Scrip ID: 11SGIL29
- ISIN: INE903U07087
- Quantity: 87,500 units
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Date of Allotment: October 16, 2025
- Interest Rate: 11% per annum (paid quarterly)
- Credit Rating: CARE A+/Stable
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- No Put/Call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE903U07087
- Trading members must use the specified tick size of 1 paise
- Trading members can refer to the Placement Memorandum at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 17, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: January 15, 2026
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Quarterly from January 15, 2026 to January 15, 2029
- **Redemption Date**: January 15, 2029

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market impact as it pertains to a routine debt security listing on private placement basis. The impact is limited to:

- Debt segment trading members who will now have access to trade these securities
- Existing and potential investors in SIGNATUREGLOBAL debt instruments
- The securities offer 11% quarterly interest with CARE A+/Stable rating, providing a fixed income investment option
- No impact on equity markets or broader market operations
