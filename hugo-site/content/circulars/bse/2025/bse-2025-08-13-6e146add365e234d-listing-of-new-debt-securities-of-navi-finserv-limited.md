## Summary

BSE has listed two new debt securities of Navi Finserv Limited issued on private placement basis, effective August 13, 2025. The securities carry interest rates of 10.75% and 10.30% per annum with monthly interest payments.

## Key Points

- Two new debt securities listed on BSE Debt segment
- Securities issued on private placement basis
- Both securities allotted on August 11, 2025
- Trading commenced on August 13, 2025
- Securities traded only in dematerialized form
- Tick size is 1 paise

## Security Details

**Security 1:**
- Scrip Code: 976808 (NFL-10.75%-19-8-28-PVT)
- ISIN: INE342T07601
- Face Value: Rs. 10,000
- Interest Rate: 10.75% p.a. (monthly payments)
- Redemption Date: August 19, 2028
- Quantity: 100,000 units
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A/STABLE

**Security 2:**
- Scrip Code: 977017 (NFL-10.30%-30-09-27-PVT)
- ISIN: INE342T07635
- Face Value: Rs. 10,000
- Interest Rate: 10.30% p.a. (monthly payments)
- Redemption Date: September 30, 2027
- Quantity: 50,000 units
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A/STABLE

## Trading Requirements

- Securities must be traded in dematerialized form only
- Market lot size: 1 unit for both securities
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call options available

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** August 11, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** August 13, 2025
- **Redemption Dates:** August 19, 2028 and September 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides additional investment options in the corporate debt market and demonstrates Navi Finserv's continued access to capital markets through private placements.
