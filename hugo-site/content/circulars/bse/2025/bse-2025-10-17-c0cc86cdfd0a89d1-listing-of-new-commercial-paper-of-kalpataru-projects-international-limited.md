## Summary

BSE has listed Commercial Paper issued by KALPATARU PROJECTS INTERNATIONAL LIMITED on private placement basis. The securities consist of 4,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 200 crores) with a maturity period of 71 days. The instruments carry an IND A1+ credit rating and will be traded in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment effective October 17, 2025.

## Key Points

- 4,000 Commercial Paper units listed with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total issue size: Rs. 200 crores
- Issue price: Rs. 494,155.50 per unit
- Credit rating: IND A1+ 
- ISIN: INE220B14BN8
- Scrip Code: 730413
- Scrip ID: KPIL161025
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Ltd
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing announcement for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE220B14BN8
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- All trades subject to BSE Debt segment rules and regulations

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: October 16, 2025
- Listing Date: October 17, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 26, 2025
- Maturity Period: 71 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing by an established infrastructure company. The commercial paper has a short maturity of 71 days and carries a strong A1+ rating, indicating low credit risk. The Rs. 200 crore size is standard for corporate commercial paper programs. Trading will be limited to institutional investors given the Rs. 5 lakh minimum denomination. No significant operational or market-wide implications expected.
