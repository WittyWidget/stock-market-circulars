## Summary

Sara Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6348) will voluntarily cease trading operations in equity derivative segments effective August 1, 2025. This is a business closure decision by the trading member.

## Key Points

- Sara Securities Pvt. Ltd. is a BSE trading member (Member No. 6348)
- Voluntary closure of business operations
- Cessation applies to equity derivative segments only
- Contact: membership.ops@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a member business decision.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the closure for their records and operational adjustments.

## Important Dates

- **August 1, 2025**: Effective date for cessation of trading operations

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only one trading member's voluntary business closure. Other market participants can continue normal operations.
