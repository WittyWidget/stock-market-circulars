## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 100 crore issued by Balrampur Chini Mills Ltd. on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective July 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- denomination each
- Total quantity: 2000 units (Rs. 100 crore)
- Scrip Code: 729924, ISIN: INE119A14864
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,95,631/- per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 29, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: July 29, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: September 22, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding for Balrampur Chini Mills Ltd. with a maturity period of approximately 55 days.
