## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of 360 ONE WAM LIMITED on its debt segment effective August 14, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a total value of Rs. 75 crores (1500 securities of Rs. 5 lakhs each).

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each listed on BSE debt segment
- Total quantity: 1500 securities (Rs. 75 crores total value)
- Issue price: Rs. 491,604.50 per security
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 13, 2025
- Listing effective date: August 14, 2025
- Redemption date: November 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding option for 360 ONE WAM LIMITED and investment opportunity for debt market participants.
