## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in two series of bonds issued by REC LIMITED effective October 20, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of bonds and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified bonds from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Two bond series of REC LIMITED suspended from trading
- REC-6.89%-5-11-25-S-1A-NCD (ISIN: INE020B07JP4, Code: 935538)
- REC-7.14%-5-11-25-S-1B-NCD (ISIN: INE020B07JQ2, Code: 935540)
- Notice Number: 20251016-37
- Reference: DR-739/2025-2026
- Action mandated for all trading members of the exchange

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action procedure for bond redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified REC LIMITED bonds from October 20, 2025
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- No trading activity permitted in these securities from the no-dealings date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Record Date**: October 20, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: October 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of the two specific REC LIMITED bond series. The suspension is temporary and procedural in nature, related to scheduled redemption and interest payment.

**Investor Impact**: Bondholders will receive redemption proceeds and final interest payment. No trading is possible from October 20, 2025, which prevents any last-minute position changes.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to prevent trading in these two bond ISINs from the specified date to ensure compliance with BSE directive.
