## Summary

Dev Labtech Venture Limited (Scrip Code: 543848) has listed 5,60,000 new equity shares on BSE effective August 4, 2025. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis through conversion of warrants at Rs. 62 per share.

## Key Points

- 5,60,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 52/-
- Issued to promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 11303140 to 11863139
- Date of allotment: April 30, 2025
- Issue price: Rs. 62.00 per share
- ISIN: INE0NIJ01017

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading from the specified date.

## Important Dates

- **Trading Start Date**: August 4, 2025 (Monday)
- **Allotment Date**: April 30, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: February 28, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a limited number of shares (5,60,000) issued to promoters with a lock-in period until February 2027. The shares are subject to lock-in restrictions, preventing immediate trading by promoters.
