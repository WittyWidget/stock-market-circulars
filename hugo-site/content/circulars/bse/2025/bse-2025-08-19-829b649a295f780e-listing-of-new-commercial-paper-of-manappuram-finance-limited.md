## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 200 crores (4000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued by Manappuram Finance Limited on private placement basis. The securities will trade on BSE Debt segment with effect from August 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper quantity: 4000 units of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Total issue size: Rs. 200 crores
- Issue price: Rs. 464,644.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Scrip code: 730043
- ISIN: INE522D14OP1
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Axis Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 18, 2025
- Listing date: August 19, 2025
- Redemption date: August 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides additional short-term funding option for Manappuram Finance Limited and investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt market.
