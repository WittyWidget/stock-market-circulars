## Summary

J.K. CEMENT LTD has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 50 crores (1000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE Debt segment effective August 7, 2025. The commercial paper is issued on private placement basis with CARE A1+ rating and matures on September 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 1000 units (Total value: Rs. 50 crores)
- Scrip Code: 729976
- ISIN: INE823G14AT7
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,96,071/- per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- IDBI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 7, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 7, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: September 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding option for J.K. CEMENT LTD and investment opportunity for debt market participants. The 49-day maturity period indicates short-term liquidity management by the company.
