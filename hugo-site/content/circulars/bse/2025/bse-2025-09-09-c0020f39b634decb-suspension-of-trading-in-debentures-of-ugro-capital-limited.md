## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of UGRO Capital Limited effective September 11, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date for interest payment and redemption purposes.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to UGRO Capital Limited debentures (Code: UCL-10.50%-26-9-25-NCD)
- ISIN: INE583D07315 (BSE Code: 938149)
- Suspension effective from September 11, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Trading members advised not to deal in these debentures

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. Standard procedure for debenture corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified UGRO Capital debentures from the suspension date
- All trading members required to take note of the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: September 11, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: September 11, 2025
- **Notice Date**: September 9, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this affects only specific debenture series of UGRO Capital Limited. Routine corporate action procedure with temporary trading halt for administrative purposes. No broader market implications expected.
