## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities worth Rs. 30 crores issued by Sharekhan Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective August 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- 6,000 Commercial Paper units of Rs. 5,00,000 each listed
- Total issue size: Rs. 30 crores
- Scrip Code: 729991, Scrip ID: SL70825
- ISIN: INE211H14AD7
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: August 8, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: August 7, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 6, 2025
- **Tenure**: 3 months (91 days)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by Sharekhan Limited. The instrument provides short-term funding for the company and offers investment options for debt segment participants.
