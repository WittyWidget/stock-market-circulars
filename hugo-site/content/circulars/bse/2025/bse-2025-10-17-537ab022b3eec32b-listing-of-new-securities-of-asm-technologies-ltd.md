## Summary

BSE has listed 5,49,698 equity shares of ASM Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 526433) effective from Monday, October 20, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 460/- per share (issue price Rs. 470.70/-) to promoters and non-promoters on a preferential basis following conversion of warrants. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- **Total Shares Listed**: 5,49,698 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue Type**: Preferential allotment pursuant to warrant conversion
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 470.70/- per share (Rs. 460/- premium)
- **Allottees**: Promoter and Non-Promoter categories
- **Distinctive Numbers**: 14064693 to 14614390
- **Date of Allotment**: August 22, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 20, 2025
- **ISIN**: INE867C01010
- **Ranking**: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following SEBI preferential allotment regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities are available for trading from October 20, 2025
- Lock-in provisions apply as per SEBI (ICDR) Regulations

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 22, 2025
- **Trading Start Date**: October 20, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: 
  - 3,96,698 shares (Dist. Nos. 14064693-14461390): April 18, 2027
  - 1,53,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 14461391-14614390): April 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The listing represents a small dilution for ASM Technologies Ltd, with shares subject to lock-in periods that restrict immediate selling pressure.

**Liquidity Impact**: Limited immediate impact on free float as majority of shares (3.97 lakh) are locked until April 2027, while remaining 1.53 lakh shares are locked until April 2026.

**Investor Consideration**: Investors should note the preferential allotment nature and substantial lock-in periods indicating promoter/investor confidence.
