## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Adani Enterprises Ltd on private placement basis. The issue consists of 700 securities of ₹5 lakh face value each, totaling ₹350 crore, with redemption date of 26 March 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities: 700 commercial paper units of ₹5,00,000 each
- Total issue size: ₹350 crore
- Issue price: ₹4,77,465 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 729977, ISIN: INE423A14XX3
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: ₹5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of ₹5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: 6 August 2025
- Trading commencement: 7 August 2025
- Redemption date: 26 March 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. Provides additional short-term funding option for Adani Enterprises with no direct impact on equity trading or member operations.
