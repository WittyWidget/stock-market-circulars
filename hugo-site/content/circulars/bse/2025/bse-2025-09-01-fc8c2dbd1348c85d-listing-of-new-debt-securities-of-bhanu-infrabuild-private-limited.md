## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Bhanu Infrabuild Private Limited on its debt segment effective September 1, 2025. The securities were issued through private placement with a face value of Rs. 1 lakh each, totaling Rs. 19 crore.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 1,900 securities with market lot of 1
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Interest Rate: 16.90% per annum
- Credit Rating: IVR BB+ Stable
- Scrip Code: 977067
- ISIN: INE247407013
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard debt listing procedures followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities to be traded under specified ISIN number
- Reference to Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for detailed terms

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 28, 2025
- Listing Date: September 1, 2025
- Interest Payment: Monthly from September 1, 2025 to August 31, 2029
- Redemption Date: August 31, 2029

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves private placement debt securities of a private company. The listing provides additional debt instrument options for institutional investors in the debt segment.
