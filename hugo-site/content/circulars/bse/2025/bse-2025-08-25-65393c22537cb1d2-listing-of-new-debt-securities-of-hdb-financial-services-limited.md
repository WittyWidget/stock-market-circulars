## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of new debt securities issued by HDB Financial Services Limited on private placement basis. The securities worth Rs. 51 crore are listed on BSE Debt segment effective August 25, 2025, with an interest rate of 8.33% per annum and maturity date of August 6, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 51,000 securities with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 975729, ISIN: INE756I07FA8
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,02,061.40 per security
- Date of Allotment: August 22, 2025
- Interest Rate: 8.33% per annum (paid annually)
- Credit Rating: CARE AAA/Stable, CRISIL AAA/Stable
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities will be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details
- Contact debt department for clarifications on specified phone numbers

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 22, 2025
- Listing Date: August 25, 2025
- Interest Payment: Annually from August 6, 2024 to August 6, 2027
- Redemption Date: August 6, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing of debt securities. The high credit rating (AAA) indicates strong creditworthiness of HDB Financial Services. The listing expands the debt market offerings but has limited direct impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
