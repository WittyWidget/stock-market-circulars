## Summary

LIOTECH INDUSTRIES LIMITED has filed its Draft Offer Document on August 12, 2025, for its SME IPO on BSE. The document is available for public review and comments for a period of 21 days as per ICDR Regulations Schedule VI requirements.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by LIOTECH INDUSTRIES LIMITED on August 12, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for minimum 21-day public comment period
- Public comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned. This follows existing ICDR Regulations Schedule VI requirements for SME IPO draft offer documents.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public to submit comments within 21 days from notice date (by September 3, 2025)
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document hosted on www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- Draft filing date: August 12, 2025
- Notice date: August 13, 2025
- Public comment deadline: September 3, 2025 (21 days from notice)

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact for SME segment participants and potential investors. Standard regulatory process for SME IPOs with mandatory public consultation period before final approval.
