## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in three Treasury Bills effective August 5, 2025, as they reach their maturity/redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in these specific T-Bills from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills will mature on August 5, 2025
- Trading suspension becomes effective August 5, 2025
- Notice applies to all trading members of the exchange
- Suspension is due to reaching redemption date

## Treasury Bills Affected

| Scrip Code | ISIN | Particulars |
|------------|------|-------------|
| 805023 | IN002024Y431 | 182TB70825 |
| 804952 | IN002024Z198 | 364TB70825 |
| 805061 | IN002025X067 | 91TB070825 |

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified T-Bills from August 5, 2025
- All exchange trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- No new positions should be created in these instruments

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 4, 2025
- **Effective Date**: August 5, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: August 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine market operation with limited impact. The suspension affects only the three specified T-Bills and is standard procedure when debt instruments reach maturity. Trading members need to ensure compliance to avoid regulatory issues.
