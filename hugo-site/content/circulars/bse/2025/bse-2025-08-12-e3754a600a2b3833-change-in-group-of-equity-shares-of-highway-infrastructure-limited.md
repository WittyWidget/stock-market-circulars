## Summary

BSE has announced that Highway Infrastructure Limited (Scrip Code: 544477) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (T Group) to the Rolling segment under B Group, effective August 28, 2025. This change follows a previous exchange notice dated August 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- Highway Infrastructure Limited shares will move from T Group to B Group
- Transfer effective from Thursday, August 28, 2025
- Scrip Code: 544477
- This is a continuation of notice 20250811-30 dated August 11, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for Highway Infrastructure Limited shares will change from Trade for Trade settlement to Rolling settlement, allowing for more liquid trading conditions.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the segment change and adjust their trading systems accordingly. For queries, members can contact Mr. Parag Jain at 022 2272 8685.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Thursday, August 28, 2025
- **Notice Date**: Tuesday, August 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from T Group to B Group typically indicates improved trading conditions for the stock, moving from a restrictive trade-for-trade basis to normal rolling settlement, which may enhance liquidity and trading volumes.
