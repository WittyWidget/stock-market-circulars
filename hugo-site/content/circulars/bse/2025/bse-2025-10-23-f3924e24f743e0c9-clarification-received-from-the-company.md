## Summary

BSE has observed significant price and volume movements in the scrip of Hindustan Appliances Ltd (Security Code: 531918) and has sought clarification from the company. The exchange has taken this action to ensure trading members and investors have access to the latest relevant information. The company's response is awaited.

## Key Points

- BSE has identified unusual price/volume activity in Hindustan Appliances Ltd shares
- Exchange has written to the company requesting clarification
- Reply from the company is pending
- Action taken under surveillance measures to protect investor interests
- Security Code: 531918

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Hindustan Appliances Ltd is required to provide clarification to BSE regarding the observed price/volume movements
- Trading members and investors should await the company's response for informed decision-making

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 23, 2025
- Clarification response: Awaited

## Impact Assessment

This surveillance action indicates heightened scrutiny on Hindustan Appliances Ltd's stock due to abnormal trading patterns. Investors should exercise caution and await the company's clarification before making trading decisions. The request for clarification is a standard surveillance measure to ensure market integrity and prevent information asymmetry.
