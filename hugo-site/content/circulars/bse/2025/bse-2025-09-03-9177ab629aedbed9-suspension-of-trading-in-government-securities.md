## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in two government securities that are approaching their redemption date. The affected securities are 823GUJ2025 (Gujarat) and 823MH2025 (Maharashtra), both scheduled to mature on their redemption date.

## Key Points

- Two government securities will be suspended from trading
- Securities: 823GUJ2025 (ISIN: IN1520150047) and 823MH2025 (ISIN: IN2220150089)
- Both securities are maturing on their respective redemption dates
- Trading suspension is a standard procedure for securities approaching maturity

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a standard operational procedure for securities approaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified government securities from the effective date
- Members should ensure no new positions are created in these securities
- Existing positions should be managed appropriately before suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: September 3, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: September 4, 2025
- **Reference**: DR-708/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine suspension of government securities approaching maturity. Trading members holding positions in these securities should have been aware of the maturity timeline and planned accordingly. The suspension prevents any confusion or irregular trading as the securities approach their redemption date.
