## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of additional equity shares issued by 10 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS). These securities will be admitted for trading on the exchange effective Wednesday, September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- 10 companies have issued additional shares under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Total of 907,750 new shares across all companies
- All shares are admitted for trading from September 10, 2025
- No lock-in period applicable for any of the listed shares
- Face values range from Re 1 to Rs 10 per share

## Companies and Share Details

**Major Issuances:**
- Kalyan Jewellers India Limited: 657,446 shares (Rs 10 face value)
- Lancor Holdings Ltd: 555,000 shares (Rs 2 face value)
- Windlas Biotech Limited: 117,928 shares (Rs 5 face value)
- Zensar Technologies Ltd: 73,529 shares (Rs 2 face value)
- CG Power and Industrial Solutions: 71,980 shares (Rs 2 face value)

**Other Companies:**
- ICICI Bank Ltd: 30,305 shares (Rs 2 face value)
- IndoStar Capital Finance: 26,250 shares (Rs 10 face value)
- Infosys Ltd: 21,924 shares (Rs 5 face value)
- Carborundum Universal Ltd: 6,388 shares (Re 1 face value)
- Religare Enterprises Ltd: 3,000 shares (Rs 10 face value)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for ESOP shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading
- Standard trading procedures apply to these newly listed shares
- No additional compliance requirements specified

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** September 9, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** September 10, 2025
- **Lock-in Period:** None applicable

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine ESOP share listings. The additional shares represent employee stock option exercises and will increase the free float of the respective companies marginally. No significant trading volume or price impact anticipated.
