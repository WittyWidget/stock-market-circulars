## Summary

BSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework effective August 8, 2025. Five new securities are being added to ESM, one security is being moved to a higher ESM stage, and no securities are exiting the framework. The circular provides comprehensive lists of all securities currently under various ESM stages.

## Key Points

- 5 new securities shortlisted for ESM framework
- 1 security moved to higher ESM stage (Gayatri Sugars Ltd)
- No securities moving to lower ESM stages
- No securities exiting ESM framework
- Consolidated list shows securities across ESM Stage I and Stage II
- Changes effective from August 8, 2025

## Securities Entering ESM Framework

**New ESM Securities (5):**
- GG Automotive Gears Ltd (531399)
- Innovana Thinklabs Ltd (544302) 
- Ortin Global Ltd (539287)
- Sainik Finance & Industries Ltd (530265)
- Shree Pacetronix Ltd (527005)

## Stage Changes

**Moving to Higher ESM Stage:**
- Gayatri Sugars Ltd (532183) - moved to higher stage

**Moving to Lower ESM Stage:**
- NIL

## Securities Exiting ESM

- NIL - No securities are moving out of ESM framework

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** August 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Securities under ESM framework face enhanced trading restrictions and monitoring. The addition of 5 new securities and upgrade of 1 existing security indicates continued regulatory vigilance. Trading in these securities will be subject to stricter surveillance measures including price bands, position limits, and additional margin requirements.
