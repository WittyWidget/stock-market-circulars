## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Keertana Finserv Limited (Scrip Code: 977044, Scrip ID: 111KFL27) on the BSE Debt segment effective October 14, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 10,000 per unit, carrying 11.10% annual interest rate and maturing on August 19, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity listed: 100,000 units
- Scrip Code: 977044, Scrip ID: 111KFL27
- ISIN: INE0NES07261 (further listings under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 9,973 per unit
- Credit Rating: IND BBB+/Stable
- Interest Rate: 11.10% per annum (monthly payment)
- Date of Allotment: October 10, 2025
- Maturity Date: August 19, 2027
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- No Put/Call options

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notice for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0NES07261
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 14, 2025
- **Interest Payment Period**: Monthly from September 19, 2025 to August 19, 2027
- **Redemption Date**: August 19, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities from a non-banking finance company. The listing adds to the debt market inventory but has minimal impact on broader market operations.

**Investor Impact**: Low to Medium - Relevant only for investors in debt securities and those tracking Keertana Finserv Limited. The BBB+ credit rating indicates investment-grade quality with moderate credit risk. The 11.10% annual interest rate reflects current market conditions for similar rated instruments.

**Operational Impact**: None - Standard listing procedure with no special trading or settlement requirements beyond dematerialization mandate.
