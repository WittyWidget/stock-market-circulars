## Summary

KEC International Limited has successfully listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 75 crores (1,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE's Debt segment. The commercial paper was issued on private placement basis and is now available for trading in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper quantity: 1,500 units of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Total issue size: Rs. 75 crores
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,391/- per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+
- Scrip Code: 730165, Scrip ID: KIL090925
- ISIN: INE389H14LH9
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Axis Bank Limited serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 09, 2025
- Trading Start Date: September 09, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 05, 2025
- Maturity Period: ~3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Limited to debt segment trading with no impact on equity markets.
