## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth ₹75 crore issued by Capri Global Capital Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to dealings on BSE Debt segment effective August 21, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of ₹5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 1,500 units (₹75 crore)
- Issue price: ₹4,81,045.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+
- ISIN: INE180C14573
- Scrip Code: 730054

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of ₹5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applies
- Yes Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 21, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 17, 2026
- Trading Commencement: August 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. Provides short-term funding option for Capri Global Capital Limited with tenure of approximately 6 months.
