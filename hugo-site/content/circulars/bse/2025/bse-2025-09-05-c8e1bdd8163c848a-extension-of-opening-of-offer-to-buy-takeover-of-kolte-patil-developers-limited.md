## Summary

BSE has extended the opening period for the Offer to Buy (Takeover) of KOLTE-PATIL DEVELOPERS LIMITED. The revised schedule runs from Friday, August 22, 2025, to Tuesday, September 9, 2025, as informed by the Manager to Issue JM Financial Ltd.

## Key Points

- Extension of open offer period for KOLTE-PATIL DEVELOPERS LIMITED takeover
- Revised schedule communicated by JM Financial Ltd (Manager to Issue)
- Acquisition window for bidding closes on Monday, September 8, 2025
- Early closure due to SEBI Holiday on September 9, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a procedural schedule extension for an existing takeover offer.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Custodians must note the revised schedule
- All bidding must be completed by Monday, September 8, 2025
- Contact BSE officials for further clarification if needed

## Important Dates

- **Open Offer Period**: August 22, 2025 to September 9, 2025
- **Acquisition Window Closure**: Monday, September 8, 2025
- **SEBI Holiday**: Tuesday, September 9, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on shareholders of KOLTE-PATIL DEVELOPERS LIMITED who wish to participate in the takeover offer. The extension provides additional time for decision-making but requires attention to the earlier closure date due to the SEBI holiday.
