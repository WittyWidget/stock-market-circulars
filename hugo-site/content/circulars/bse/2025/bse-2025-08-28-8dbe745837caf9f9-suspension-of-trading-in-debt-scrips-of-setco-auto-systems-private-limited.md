## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debt securities of Setco Auto Systems Private Limited effective August 29, 2025, due to the maturity of the securities on their scheduled maturity date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to SCRIP CODE 973453 (ISIN: INE0I0R07037)
- Securities identified as SASPL-5%-1-9-25-PVT
- Suspension effective from August 29, 2025
- Action taken due to securities reaching maturity date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced - this is a routine operational notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members advised not to deal in the specified debt securities
- Trading members must take note of the suspension
- Compliance with notice DR-704/2025-2026

## Important Dates

- **August 29, 2025**: Trading suspension becomes effective
- **September 1, 2025**: Securities maturity date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only one private company's debt securities reaching natural maturity. Standard operational procedure for maturing debt instruments with no broader market implications.
