## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of RNIT AI Solutions Limited (formerly known as Autopal Industries Limited, Scrip Code: 517286) will be moved to the 'X' group effective Monday, November 10, 2025. This represents a change in the trading classification group for the company's shares.

## Key Points

- Company: RNIT AI Solutions Limited (formerly Autopal Industries Limited)
- Scrip Code: 517286
- New Group: 'X'
- Effective Date: Monday, November 10, 2025
- Notice issued by: Kinnar Mehta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be permitted to trade under the 'X' group starting November 10, 2025. Group 'X' typically includes companies that do not meet the listing requirements or have compliance issues. This classification affects trading mechanics and investor eligibility.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:
- Update their systems to reflect the new group classification
- Ensure compliance with 'X' group trading rules and restrictions
- Inform clients about the group change and its implications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 10, 2025 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

Movement to the 'X' group typically indicates:
- Increased trading restrictions or surveillance measures
- Potential liquidity impact due to classification change
- May reflect compliance or listing requirement issues
- Investors should exercise additional caution when trading in 'X' group securities
