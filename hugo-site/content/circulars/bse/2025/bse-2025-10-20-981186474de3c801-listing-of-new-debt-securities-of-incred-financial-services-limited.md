## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of InCred Financial Services Limited on its Debt segment effective October 20, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a total quantity of 2,500 units at face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit. The securities are rated CRISIL PP-MLD AA-/Stable and have a maturity date of October 13, 2028.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977230
- Scrip ID: IFSL151025
- Detail Name: IFSL-GOLDFUTURES-13-10-28-PVT
- ISIN: INE321N07582
- Quantity: 2,500 units
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 25 crore
- Market Lot: 1
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000
- Credit Rating: CRISIL PP-MLD AA-/Stable
- Date of Allotment: October 15, 2025
- Trading: Only in dematerialized form
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE321N07582
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: October 15, 2025
- Listing Date: October 20, 2025
- Date of Redemption: October 13, 2028
- Date of Payment of Interest: Not Applicable (NA)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification for privately placed debt securities with minimal market-wide impact. The listing provides InCred Financial Services Limited with additional debt capital and offers institutional investors access to AA- rated debt instruments. The private placement nature limits broader market participation. No put/call options are attached to these securities.
