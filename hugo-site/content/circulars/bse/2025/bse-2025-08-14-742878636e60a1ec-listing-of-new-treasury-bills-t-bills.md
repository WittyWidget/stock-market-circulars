## Summary

BSE announces the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) in the G Group - Debt Instruments category, effective August 18, 2025. The T-Bills include 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day instruments with specific ISIN codes and trading symbols.

## Key Points

- Three new Treasury Bills to be listed on August 18, 2025
- All T-Bills classified under G Group - Debt Instruments
- Market lot size of 1 for all three instruments
- Standard trading suspension applies two working days before maturity

## New T-Bills Details

- **91TB131125**: ISIN IN002025X208, matures November 13, 2025
- **182TB12226**: ISIN IN002025Y206, matures February 12, 2026  
- **364TB13826**: ISIN IN002025Z203, matures August 13, 2026

## Trading Restrictions

- Securities will not be available for trading two working days prior to maturity date
- Restriction applies to Maturity Date (T) minus two trading days (excluding bank holidays)

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: August 18, 2025
- **Maturity Dates**: November 13, 2025 | February 12, 2026 | August 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Routine addition of government securities to BSE's debt instrument segment with minimal market impact. Provides additional fixed-income investment options for institutional and retail investors.
