## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Jana Small Finance Bank Limited on a private placement basis, effective October 20, 2025. The securities carry a 10.55% p.a. interest rate with annual interest payments and mature on October 16, 2035. The issue consists of 250 securities with a face value of Rs. 10,00,00,000 each.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977229
- Scrip ID: 1055JSFB35
- ISIN: INE953L08337
- Quantity: 250 securities
- Face Value: Rs. 10,00,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 10,00,00,000
- Interest Rate: 10.55% per annum
- Credit Rating: IND A/Stable
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only
- Put/Call Options: As per Key Information Document (KID)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt security listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE953L08337
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: October 16, 2025
- Listing Date: October 20, 2025
- Interest Payment Dates: Annually from October 16, 2026 to October 16, 2035
- Redemption Date: October 16, 2035

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with limited market impact. The listing expands Jana Small Finance Bank Limited's debt funding sources through privately placed securities. The securities are rated IND A/Stable, indicating adequate credit quality. The relatively high interest rate of 10.55% reflects the risk profile of small finance bank debt instruments. Impact is limited to institutional investors participating in the private placement and does not affect retail equity markets.
