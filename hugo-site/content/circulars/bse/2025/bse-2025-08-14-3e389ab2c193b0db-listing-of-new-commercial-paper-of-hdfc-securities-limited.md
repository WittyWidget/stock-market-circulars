## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by HDFC Securities Limited on private placement basis worth Rs. 250 crores (5,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on the BSE Debt segment, effective August 14, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Quantity: 5,000 units (Total value: Rs. 250 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730031
- ISIN: INE700G14PH0
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,219.50 per unit
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE700G14PH0
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Ltd

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 14, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 13, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 14, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding for HDFC Securities Limited with a 3-month maturity period.
