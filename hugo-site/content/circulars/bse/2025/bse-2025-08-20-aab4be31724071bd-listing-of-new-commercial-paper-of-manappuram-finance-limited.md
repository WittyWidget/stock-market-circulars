## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Manappuram Finance Limited worth Rs. 35 crores (7,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on private placement basis. The commercial paper carries CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ credit ratings and will mature on November 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 7,000 units (Rs. 35 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,576 per unit
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Scrip code: 730051, ISIN: INE522D14OQ9
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- AXIS BANK LTD acts as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 20, 2025
- Redemption date: November 18, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding option for Manappuram Finance Limited and investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
