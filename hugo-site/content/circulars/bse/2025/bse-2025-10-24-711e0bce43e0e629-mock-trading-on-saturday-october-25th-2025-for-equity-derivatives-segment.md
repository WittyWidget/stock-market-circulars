## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity Derivatives segment on Saturday, October 25, 2025. This session is designed for trading members using third-party trading platforms, empaneled vendor systems, or in-house developed systems through ETI APIs to test their trading applications. The mock session will allow testing of various functionalities including exceptional market conditions like risk-reduction mode and trading halts. There will be no new BOLT TWS Release.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, October 25, 2025
- Available for Equity Derivatives segment only
- Session is for testing and familiarization purposes
- No margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out obligations, or rights and liabilities will be created
- No new BOLT TWS Release
- Feedback requested from all participants by 6:00 PM on the session day
- Active participation from all members is requested

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine mock trading session for system testing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to actively participate in the mock trading session
- Members must provide feedback on the mock trading session by 6:00 PM on October 25, 2025
- Feedback should be submitted via the contact numbers or email IDs provided
- Members can contact their Relationship Manager or BSE Helpdesk/Tech Support for queries

## Important Dates

- **October 25, 2025**: Mock Trading Session
  - Log-in: 1:15 PM to 2:15 PM
  - Continuous Trading: 2:15 PM to 5:00 PM
  - Trade Modification: 5:00 PM to 5:15 PM
- **October 25, 2025, 6:00 PM**: Deadline for feedback submission

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: None. This is a mock trading session with no real market impact.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members have the opportunity to test their systems and applications in a simulated environment. The session allows testing of various functionalities including exceptional market conditions, which can help members prepare for actual market scenarios.

**Member Impact**: Beneficial but optional. Members using third-party platforms or in-house systems can utilize this session to ensure their systems are functioning correctly and are familiar with various trading conditions.
