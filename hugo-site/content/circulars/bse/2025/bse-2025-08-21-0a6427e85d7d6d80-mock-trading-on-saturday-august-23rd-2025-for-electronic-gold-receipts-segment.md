## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, August 23rd, 2025. This session is designed for trading members to test their trading applications and familiarize themselves with various market conditions and functionalities.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for August 23rd, 2025
- Available for members using third-party trading platforms or in-house systems through ETI APIs
- Testing opportunity for various functionalities including risk-reduction mode and trading halt
- No new BOLT TWS release
- Trades will not create any real obligations or liabilities

## Trading Schedule

- **Log-in**: 10:15 am - 11:00 am
- **Pre-Open Order Entry**: 11:00 am - 11:08 am
- **Matching Period**: 11:08 am - 11:15 am
- **Continuous Trading**: 11:15 am - 02:00 pm
- **Block Deal Window**: 12:00 pm - 12:15 pm
- **Closing**: 02:00 pm - 02:10 pm
- **Post-closing**: 02:10 pm - 02:20 pm

## Important Dates

- **Mock Trading Date**: August 23rd, 2025
- **Feedback Deadline**: 3:00 pm on trading day

- **BSE Tech Support**: 022-22728053, bse.tech@bseindia.com
- **Help Desk**: 022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500, bsehelp@bseindia.com

## Impact Assessment

This is a testing session with no real market impact. Trades executed during mock trading will not attract margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out requirements, or create any legal rights and liabilities. The session serves purely for system testing and member familiarization purposes.
