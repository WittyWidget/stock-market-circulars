## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in two series of non-convertible debentures of FINQUEST FINANCIAL SOLUTIONS PRIVATE LIMITED effective September 10, 2025, due to early redemption. The suspension affects debentures with ISIN codes INE712W08037 and INE712W08029.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension effective from September 10, 2025
- Two debenture series affected: FFSPL-ZC-24-3-27-PVT and FFSPL-ZC-26-9-29-PVT
- Reason: Early redemption of non-convertible debentures
- Trading members advised not to deal in specified debentures

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. Standard procedure for debenture early redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from September 10, 2025
- No trading permitted in debentures with codes 955968 and 959019
- Members must comply with record date requirements for early redemption process

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: September 10, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: September 10, 2025
- **Notice Date**: September 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this affects only specific debenture holders of a private limited company. The suspension is procedural and temporary, related to corporate action completion. No broader market implications expected.
