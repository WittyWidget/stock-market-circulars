## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of subscription facility for SBI Silver ETF Fund of Fund on the BSE StAR MF (Mutual Fund) platform. This allows investors to resume making fresh subscriptions to this fund of fund scheme.

## Key Points

- Subscription facility for SBI Silver ETF Fund of Fund has been resumed on BSE StAR MF platform
- Investors can now make fresh investments in this scheme
- This is a fund of fund that invests in SBI Silver ETF
- The fund provides exposure to silver as an asset class through ETF investment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding subscription facility availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an information circular for investors and distributors.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: October 27, 2025 (date of circular issuance)
- Subscriptions are now open with immediate effect

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational update affecting a single fund of fund scheme.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for investors interested in gaining silver exposure through fund of fund route. Those who were waiting to invest can now make subscriptions.

**Operational Impact**: BSE StAR MF platform participants (distributors, RTAs, AMCs) can now process subscription orders for this scheme.
