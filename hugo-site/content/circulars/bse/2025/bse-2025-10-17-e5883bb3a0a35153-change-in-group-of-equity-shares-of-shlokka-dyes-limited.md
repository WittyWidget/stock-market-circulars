## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of SHLOKKA DYES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544582) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective Tuesday, November 04, 2025. This change follows BSE Notice No. 220251016-39 dated October 16, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: SHLOKKA DYES LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544582
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Segment: SME
- Effective Date: Tuesday, November 04, 2025
- Contact: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822
- Issued by: Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for SHLOKKA DYES LIMITED will change from Trade for Trade (T2T) settlement to Rolling settlement. This change indicates the stock is being moved from a more restrictive trading segment to a regular trading segment, typically allowing intraday trading and delivery-based transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members should note the segment change and update their systems accordingly
- All dealings in the equity shares will be under M Group from the effective date
- Trading Members may contact Mr. Anurag Jain for further clarification or details

## Important Dates

- **October 16, 2025**: Previous notice issued (Notice No. 220251016-39)
- **October 17, 2025**: Current notice date
- **November 04, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer from MT Group to M Group

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - This change is positive for the stock as it moves from restrictive Trade for Trade segment to Rolling segment, which typically provides better liquidity and allows intraday trading. This may improve trading volumes and market participation.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Trading Members need to update their systems and processes to reflect the new group classification. Investors will benefit from increased trading flexibility once the stock moves to Rolling segment.
