## Summary

BSE has announced the transfer of BLT LOGISTICS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544474) equity shares from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment, effective August 26, 2025. This change will shift the company's dealings to M Group under rolling settlement.

## Key Points

- Company: BLT LOGISTICS LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544474
- Current segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: August 26, 2025
- Contact: Mr. Parag Jain (022 2272 8685)

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be transferred from restrictive Trade for Trade segment to more liquid Rolling segment, allowing for normal T+1 settlement cycle instead of immediate delivery requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the segment change and adjust their trading systems and procedures accordingly for dealings in BLT LOGISTICS LIMITED shares.

## Important Dates

- **August 26, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer
- **August 11, 2025**: Notice date
- **August 8, 2025**: Reference to previous notice 20250808-49

## Impact Assessment

Positive impact for shareholders as the move to Rolling segment typically indicates improved liquidity and reduced trading restrictions. The change from Trade for Trade to Rolling settlement will make the stock more accessible to retail and institutional investors.
