## Summary

Venus Pipes & Tubes Limited (Scrip Code: 543528) has listed 61,000 new equity shares on BSE effective Monday, August 11, 2025. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis at Rs. 1700 per share pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 61,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 1690/-
- Issue price: Rs. 1700 per share
- Allotment date: June 19, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 11, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE0JA001018
- Distinctive numbers: 20431111 to 20492110

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities listing
- Lock-in period applicable until February 27, 2027

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: June 19, 2025
- Trading Start Date: August 11, 2025
- Lock-in Period Ends: February 27, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a preferential allotment to promoters with shares under lock-in until 2027. Standard listing procedure with no immediate trading implications for retail investors.
