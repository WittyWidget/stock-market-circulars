## Summary

BSE has approved the listing and trading of 2,945 new equity shares of Grasim Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 500300) resulting from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Rs. 2/- each issued on rights basis. These shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 2,945 new equity shares listed for Grasim Industries Limited
- Shares result from conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up
- Face value: Rs. 2/- per share
- Issued on rights basis
- ISIN: INE047A01021
- Distinctive Numbers: 680456445 to 680459389
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Scrip Code: 500300

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are permitted to trade in these new securities with effect from the trading start date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 15, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: Thursday, October 16, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The listing involves a small number of shares (2,945) from conversion of partly paid to fully paid shares. This is a routine corporate action with no dilution effect as the shares were already partially issued. Trading members can commence trading in these securities from October 16, 2025.
