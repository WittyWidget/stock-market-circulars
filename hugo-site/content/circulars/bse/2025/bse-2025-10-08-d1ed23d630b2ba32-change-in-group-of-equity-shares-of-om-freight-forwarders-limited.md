## Summary

BSE has announced the transfer of equity shares of Om Freight Forwarders Limited (Scrip Code: 544564) from the Trade for Trade segment (T Group) to the Rolling segment (B Group), effective October 24, 2025. This change follows the Exchange's Notice No. 20251007-53 dated October 07, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Om Freight Forwarders Limited
- Scrip Code: 544564
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade (T Group)
- New Segment: Rolling Segment (B Group)
- Effective Date: Friday, October 24, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251008-9
- Contact: Mr. Parag Jain (Tel: 022 2272 8685)
- Issued by: Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for Om Freight Forwarders Limited will change from Trade for Trade settlement to Rolling settlement. In the Trade for Trade segment, each trade results in delivery obligation, while the Rolling segment allows for intraday trading and netting of positions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members should note the segment change and update their systems accordingly
- All trades in the scrip will be settled under Rolling settlement mechanism from October 24, 2025
- Trading Members may contact Mr. Parag Jain for clarifications or additional details

## Important Dates

- **October 07, 2025**: Initial notice (20251007-53) issued
- **October 08, 2025**: Current notice (20251008-9) issued
- **October 24, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer from T Group to B Group

## Impact Assessment

This segment transfer is positive for market participants as it:
- Enhances liquidity by allowing intraday trading
- Enables position netting instead of mandatory delivery on each trade
- Reduces settlement obligations for traders
- Indicates improved compliance or market standing of the company
- May attract more trading interest due to increased flexibility in the Rolling segment
