## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis. The instrument has a face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each with 2,500 units totaling Rs. 125 crores, maturing on September 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each with quantity of 2,500 units
- Scrip Code: 730138, ISIN: INE01C314CM6
- Issue price: Rs. 498,041.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- ICICI Bank Limited serves as issuing and paying agent
- Trading in dematerialized form only with standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: September 2, 2025
- Listing effective date: September 3, 2025
- Redemption date: September 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term commercial paper listing with 22-day maturity period. The instrument provides additional debt market liquidity but has limited significance given its brief tenure.
