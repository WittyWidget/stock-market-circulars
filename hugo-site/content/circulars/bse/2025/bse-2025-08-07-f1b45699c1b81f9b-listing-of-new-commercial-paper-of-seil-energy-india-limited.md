## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 200 crore issued by SEIL Energy India Limited on private placement basis. The papers carry face value of Rs. 5 lakh each with issue price of Rs. 496,166.50 and mature on September 23, 2025.

## Key Points

- 4,000 commercial papers of Rs. 5 lakh face value each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 496,166.50 per paper (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 729980, ISIN: INE460M14784
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Minimum trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Limited serves as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 7, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 7, 2025
- Redemption date: September 23, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides SEIL Energy with short-term funding at competitive rates given the high credit ratings.
