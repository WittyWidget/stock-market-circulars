## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and admission for trading of further securities issued by 10 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be available for trading from Wednesday, August 20, 2025.

## Key Points

- 10 companies are issuing new securities under ESOP/ESOS
- All securities will be listed and admitted for trading from August 20, 2025
- No lock-in period applies to any of the listed securities
- Total shares being listed: 1,310,116 shares across all companies
- Face values range from ₹2 to ₹10 depending on the company

## Companies and Share Details

- **AXIS Bank Ltd**: 121,145 shares (Face Value: ₹2)
- **C.E. Info Systems Limited**: 301,590 shares (Face Value: ₹2)
- **CG Power and Industrial Solutions Limited**: 52,000 shares (Face Value: ₹2)
- **Chalet Hotels Limited**: 90,000 shares (Face Value: ₹10)
- **D.B.Corp Limited**: 16,000 shares (Face Value: ₹10)
- **Gulf Oil Lubricants India Limited**: 15,173 shares (Face Value: ₹2)
- **Infosys Ltd**: 97,651 shares (Face Value: ₹5)
- **NIPPON LIFE INDIA ASSET MANAGEMENT LIMITED**: 546,930 shares (Face Value: ₹10)
- **Nuvama Wealth Management Limited**: 26,252 shares (Face Value: ₹10)
- **Varun Beverages Limited**: 44,375 shares (Face Value: ₹2)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for ESOP securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities availability for trading. No specific compliance actions required beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Trading Date**: Wednesday, August 20, 2025
- **Lock-in Period**: Not applicable (NA) for all securities

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine ESOP listings with relatively small quantities compared to companies' total outstanding shares. The absence of lock-in restrictions means shares are immediately available for trading.
