## Summary

BSE has announced the opening of Offer for Sale (OFS) for Ironwood Education Limited with undersubscription acceptance provisions. This circular provides details about the OFS mechanism and procedures for handling undersubscribed portions of the offer.

## Key Points

- Opening of Offer for Sale for Ironwood Education Limited
- Undersubscription acceptance mechanism applicable
- Specific procedures outlined for OFS participation
- Regulatory framework for handling undersubscribed offers

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Existing OFS framework and undersubscription handling procedures apply.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must follow prescribed OFS procedures
- Compliance with undersubscription acceptance norms required
- Adherence to bidding and settlement timelines

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: September 4, 2025
- OFS opening as per schedule mentioned in detailed circular

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact limited to Ironwood Education Limited shares and participating investors. The undersubscription acceptance provision provides flexibility for partial subscription scenarios, ensuring market efficiency for the OFS process.
