## Summary

BSE has published the post-issue unit holding pattern report for Anantam Highways Trust (ISIN: INE1UA823019). The InvIT has a total of 21,75,00,000 units outstanding, with sponsors and their associates holding 37.54% (8,16,49,861 units) and public holding 62.46% (13,58,50,139 units). All sponsor-held units are mandatorily held with 100% of sponsor units in this category.

## Key Points

- Total outstanding units: 21,75,00,000
- Sponsor group holding: 8,16,49,861 units (37.54%)
- Public holding: 13,58,50,139 units (62.46%)
- 100% of sponsor units are mandatorily held
- No units are pledged or encumbered
- ISIN: INE1UA823019
- Compliance Officer: Chandra Kant Sharma
