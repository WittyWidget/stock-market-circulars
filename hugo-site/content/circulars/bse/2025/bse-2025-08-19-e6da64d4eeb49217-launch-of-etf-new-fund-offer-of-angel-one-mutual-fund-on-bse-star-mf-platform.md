## Summary

ANGEL ONE Mutual Fund will launch ANGEL ONE GOLD ETF New Fund Offer (NFO) on BSE StAR MF Platform from August 20, 2025 to September 2, 2025. The scheme includes both regular and direct plan options with tentative allotment on September 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- NFO period: August 20 to September 2, 2025
- Two scheme options: ANGEL ONE GOLD ETF (Regular and Direct Plan)
- Both schemes categorized as Debt
- Tentative allotment date: September 5, 2025
- Extended trading available till midnight on September 2, 2025 for lumpsum purchases
- Payment cancellation rules apply for uninitiated funds

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client fund initiation by 9:00 AM on T+1 day to avoid transaction cancellation
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on September 4, 2025
- Cheques and OTM payments acceptable during NFO period subject to clear funds receipt
- Transactions without initiated payments will be cancelled with CC and N flag reported to RTA/AMC

## Important Dates

- NFO Start: August 20, 2025
- NFO End: September 2, 2025 (extended till midnight for lumpsum)
- Clear funds deadline: 6:00 PM, September 4, 2025
- Tentative allotment: September 5, 2025
- Payment initiation deadline: 9:00 AM T+1 day
- Pending transaction deadline: 3:00 PM on last NFO day or T+5

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine new fund offering announcement. The launch provides investors with additional gold ETF investment options through BSE StAR MF Platform. No direct impact on existing securities or trading operations.
