## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in two Treasury Bills (T-Bills) effective August 19, 2025, as they are scheduled to mature on the redemption date. Trading members are advised to cease all dealings in these securities.

## Key Points

- Two T-Bills will be suspended from trading: 364TB21825 (ISIN: IN002024Z214) and 91TB210825 (ISIN: IN002025X083)
- Trading suspension effective from August 19, 2025
- Reason for suspension is maturity of the securities
- All trading members must comply with the suspension

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine operational notice for securities reaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified T-Bills from August 19, 2025
- All existing positions should be settled before the suspension date
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and update their systems accordingly

## Important Dates

- **August 18, 2025**: Notice date
- **August 19, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **August 19, 2025**: Redemption/maturity date for both T-Bills

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine suspension due to maturity. The suspension affects only two specific T-Bill securities and does not impact broader market operations. Trading members holding these securities should ensure proper settlement procedures are followed before the maturity date.
