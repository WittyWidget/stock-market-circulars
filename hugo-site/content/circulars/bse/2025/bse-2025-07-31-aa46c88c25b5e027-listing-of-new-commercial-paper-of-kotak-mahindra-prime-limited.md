## Summary

BSE has admitted new Commercial Paper issued by Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited worth Rs. 200 crores (4000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective July 31, 2025. The paper carries CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ ratings with a 7-day tenure.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 4000 units (Total value: Rs. 200 crores)
- Scrip Code: 727700, Scrip ID: KMPL23824
- ISIN: INE916D143S2 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 499,420.50 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Issuing and Paying agent: Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 31, 2025
- **Listing Date**: July 31, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: August 7, 2025
- **Tenure**: 7 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term commercial paper listing. The 7-day tenure indicates temporary funding requirements. High credit ratings (A1+) reflect strong creditworthiness of the issuer.
