## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 900 bonus equity shares of Tata Elxsi Ltd (Scrip Code: 500408) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares will be available for trading effective Tuesday, July 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- 900 bonus equity shares of Rs. 10/- each being listed
- Shares were previously kept in abeyance
- ISIN: INE670A01012
- Trading permitted from July 29, 2025
- Shares distributed across 5 distinct number ranges

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - standard listing procedure for bonus shares previously held in abeyance.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities availability for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Tuesday, July 29, 2025 - Trading commencement
- **Notice Date**: July 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact due to the small number of shares (900) being released. This represents a routine administrative action to list bonus shares that were previously held in abeyance. The impact on Tata Elxsi's stock liquidity and price is expected to be negligible given the small quantity relative to the company's total share capital.
