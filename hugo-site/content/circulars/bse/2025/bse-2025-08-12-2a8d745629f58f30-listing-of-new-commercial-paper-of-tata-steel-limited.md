## Summary

TATA Steel Limited has listed new commercial paper securities worth Rs. 95 crores (19,000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) on BSE's debt segment. The commercial paper is issued on private placement basis with ICRA A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper quantity: 19,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total value: Rs. 95,00,00,000
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,929 per unit
- Credit rating: ICRA A1+, IND A1+
- ISIN: INE081A14GB0
- Scrip code: 730004
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 11, 2025
- Trading effective from: August 12, 2025
- Redemption date: November 7, 2025
- Tenure: Approximately 88 days

## Trading Details

- Market lot: 1 unit
- Issuing and paying agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Contact: BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The short-term debt instrument provides TATA Steel with working capital financing and offers investors a highly-rated short-term investment option.
