## Summary

BSE has revised price bands for 15 securities effective September 2, 2025. The changes range from tightening bands to 2% for some scrips to expanding them to 20% for others, reflecting adjusted risk assessments.

## Key Points

- 15 securities affected by price band revisions
- Changes effective from September 2, 2025
- Price bands range from 2% to 20%
- Star Imaging & Path Lab Ltd receives the highest band at 20%
- Kuber Udyog Ltd and Tamilnadu Steel Tubes Ltd get the most restrictive 2% bands

## Regulatory Changes

Price band percentages revised for equity scrips under surveillance measures to control volatility and protect investor interests.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised price bands
- Clarifications can be sought at bse.surv@bseindia.com
- All trading activities must comply with new band limits from effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: September 1, 2025
- **Effective Date**: September 2, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The revised price bands will limit daily price movements for affected securities, potentially reducing volatility but also constraining trading flexibility. Tighter bands (2%) suggest higher surveillance concerns, while expanded bands (20%) may indicate improved market confidence in certain scrips.
