## Summary

BN Holdings Limited has officially changed its name to BN Agrochem Limited with effect from August 26, 2025, as certified by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs. The company was originally incorporated as Arihant Oil Mills Private Limited.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from BN Holdings Limited to BN Agrochem Limited
- Certificate effective date: August 26, 2025
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L15315MH1991PLC326590
- Original incorporation name: Arihant Oil Mills Private Limited
- Certificate issued by Central Processing Centre, Haryana

## Regulatory Changes

- Formal name change certificate issued under Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014
- Digital signature authentication by Registrar of Companies
- Certificate processed by Mitika Raja, Central Processing Centre

## Compliance Requirements

- Company must display old name along with new name for two years at all required locations per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- Rights and liabilities of stakeholders remain unaffected by name change
- Stakeholders advised to verify latest company status on MCA website

## Important Dates

- **August 26, 2025**: Effective date of name change
- **Two years from August 26, 2025**: Period for displaying both old and new names

## Impact Assessment

- No impact on trading operations or business activities
- Administrative change only affecting company documentation
- Registered address remains: 217, Adani Inspire-BKC, Bandra East, Mumbai-400051
- No effect on stakeholder rights or company liabilities
