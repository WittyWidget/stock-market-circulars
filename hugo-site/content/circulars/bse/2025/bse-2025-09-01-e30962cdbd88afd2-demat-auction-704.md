## Summary

BSE has scheduled a demat auction (Settlement No. 704) for September 1, 2025, covering a wide range of securities across various scrips. The auction includes quantities ranging from single shares to thousands of shares across different companies.

## Key Points

- Settlement number: 704
- Auction date: September 1, 2025
- Total securities listed: Multiple scrips with varying quantities
- Includes major stocks like Bosch, Dr. Reddy's, HDFC Bank, Maruti Suzuki
- Quantities range from 1 share to 27,001 shares per scrip

## Securities Included

### Major Companies
- BOSCH LTD (500530): 5 shares
- DR. REDDY'S LABORATORIES (500124): 10,625 shares
- HDFC BANK (500180): 1 share
- MARUTI SUZUKI (532500): 2 shares
- STATE BANK OF INDIA (500112): 25 shares
- MPHASIS LTD (526299): 4 shares

### Highest Quantities
- Vikas EcoTec (530961): 27,001 shares
- ORIENT GREEN (533263): 18,000 shares
- Alstone Text (539277): 6,551 shares
- SHREE REFRIGERATION (544458): 4,000 shares
- CUBICAL FINANCIAL (511710): 3,305 shares

## Trading Impact

The demat auction will affect trading in these securities as the auctioned shares will be settled through the exchange mechanism. Market participants should be aware of potential price movements in the affected scrips.

## Settlement Process

The auction follows standard BSE demat settlement procedures for securities that have failed to deliver within the prescribed settlement period.
