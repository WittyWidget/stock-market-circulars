## Summary

BSE has notified all trading members that Just Trade Securities Limited (Member No. 6421) will change its name to "Bajaj Capital Markets Limited" effective Tuesday, July 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading member name change from Just Trade Securities Limited to Bajaj Capital Markets Limited
- Member number remains 6421
- Change effective from July 29, 2025
- Notification issued to all BSE trading members

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes - this is an administrative name change notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should update their records to reflect the new name
- Any clarifications can be sought via email to membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Tuesday, July 29, 2025
- **Notice Date**: July 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal operational impact as this is purely an administrative name change. Trading operations and member number remain unchanged. Other trading members may need to update their internal records and systems to reflect the new entity name.
