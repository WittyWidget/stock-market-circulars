## Summary

ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED has listed new Commercial Paper securities on BSE's Debt segment on October 24, 2025. The issuance totals Rs. 200 crores (4000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) on a private placement basis with a maturity period of 91 days.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 4000 Commercial Paper units of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 200 crores
- Scrip Code: 730428
- Scrip ID: IHFC241025
- ISIN: INE071G14HD2
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000/-
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,473.50/-
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading: Only in dematerialized form
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE071G14HD2
- Trading must occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be maintained for trading

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** October 24, 2025
- **Listing Date:** October 24, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** January 23, 2026
- **Maturity Period:** 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper provides short-term funding for ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED and offers institutional investors a debt investment option. The listing expands BSE's debt segment offerings but does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
