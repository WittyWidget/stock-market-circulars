## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Bajaj Housing Finance Limited worth Rs. 700 crores (14,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on private placement basis. The securities are listed on BSE Debt segment effective August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 14,000 units (Rs. 700 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730094, Scrip ID: BHFL26825
- ISIN: INE377Y14BS7
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,76,503 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Central Bank of India acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: August 28, 2025
- Allotment Date: August 26, 2025
- Redemption Date: June 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. Provides additional short-term debt instrument for institutional investors in BSE Debt segment.
