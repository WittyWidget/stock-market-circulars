## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in commercial paper issued by Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited (KMIL) effective August 4, 2025. The suspension is due to the maturity of the commercial paper on the redemption date.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper scrip code: 729916
- ISIN: INE975F14B59
- Trading suspension effective: August 4, 2025
- Reference: DR-687/2025-2026
- Issuer: Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for maturity-related trading suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial paper from August 4, 2025
- All exchange members are required to take note and comply with the suspension

## Important Dates

- **August 1, 2025**: Notice date
- **August 4, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **Redemption Date**: Commercial paper maturity (specific date not mentioned)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine suspension due to instrument maturity. The action affects only the specific commercial paper instrument and does not impact the underlying company's other securities or broader market operations.
