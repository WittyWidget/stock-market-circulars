## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Gujarat Peanut and Agri Products Limited for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will remain available on the SME website for at least 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Gujarat Peanut and Agri Products Limited on August 14, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for public review
- Contains all required disclosures per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments invited within 21-day period
- Document accessible at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This follows standard SME IPO filing process under existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public has 21 days from notice date to submit comments
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Company must comply with Schedule VI disclosure requirements of ICDR Regulations

## Important Dates

- Filing Date: August 14, 2025
- Notice Date: August 19, 2025
- Comment Period Ends: September 9, 2025 (21 days from notice)

## Impact Assessment

Limited immediate market impact as this is a standard SME IPO filing process. Potential impact on SME segment investors interested in agri-business sector. The 21-day comment period allows stakeholders to review the offer document before final approval.
