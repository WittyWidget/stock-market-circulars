## Summary

BSE has announced the application of Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective September 9, 2025. The circular identifies 15 securities being shortlisted for ST-ASM framework and 3 securities moving out of the framework.

## Key Points

- 15 securities shortlisted for Short Term 5/15/30 Days ASM Framework
- 3 securities moving out of ST-ASM Framework
- No securities moving to higher or lower stage ASM
- Framework includes both regular and SME scrips
- T+0 scrips included based on parent company classification

## Regulatory Changes

Implementation of Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure for identified securities with enhanced monitoring and trading restrictions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must comply with ST-ASM framework requirements
- Enhanced surveillance measures apply to shortlisted securities
- Special attention required for SME and T+0 scrips with specific markings

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 9, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on affected securities with potential trading restrictions and enhanced surveillance. Investors and traders in the 15 shortlisted securities will face additional monitoring measures, while 3 securities gain relief from ST-ASM restrictions.
