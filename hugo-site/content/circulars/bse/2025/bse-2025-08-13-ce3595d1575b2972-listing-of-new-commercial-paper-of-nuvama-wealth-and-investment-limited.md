## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited worth Rs. 25 crore (500 securities of Rs. 5 lakh each) on its Debt segment. The securities were issued on private placement basis and are admitted for trading from August 13, 2025.

## Key Points

- 500 Commercial Paper securities of Rs. 5,00,000 each listed on BSE Debt segment
- Issue price: Rs. 4,98,546.50 per security (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ (highest short-term rating)
- ISIN: INE523L14AH8, Scrip Code: 730016
- ICICI Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying Agent
- Trading only in dematerialized form with minimum lot size of Rs. 5 lakhs

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Securities must be traded under specified ISIN number

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 12, 2025
- **Listing Date**: August 13, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: August 28, 2025
- **Maturity Period**: 16 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term debt instrument listing. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk. The short 16-day maturity suggests this is for immediate working capital requirements of Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited.
