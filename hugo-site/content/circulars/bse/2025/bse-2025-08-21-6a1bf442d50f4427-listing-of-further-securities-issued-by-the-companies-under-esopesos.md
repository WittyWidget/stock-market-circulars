## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of further securities issued by 11 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be admitted for trading on the exchange effective Friday, August 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- 11 companies are listing additional securities under ESOP/ESOS programs
- Total of 3,522,428 new shares across all companies
- Securities effective for trading from August 22, 2025
- All listed securities have no lock-in period restrictions
- Face values range from ₹1 to ₹10 per share

## Listed Companies and Details

**Major Banks:**
- HDFC Bank Ltd: 2,102,087 shares (₹1 face value)
- ICICI Bank Ltd: 626,130 shares (₹2 face value)
- ICICI Prudential Life Insurance: 65,115 shares (₹10 face value)

**Other Notable Companies:**
- Vaibhav Global Ltd: 156,277 shares (₹2 face value)
- J.B. Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals: 114,999 shares (₹1 face value)
- Aptus Value Housing Finance: 283,472 shares (₹2 face value)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new listings
- Securities are immediately available for trading
- No additional compliance measures specified

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** August 21, 2025
- **Trading Effective Date:** August 22, 2025
- **Lock-in Period:** None (NA for all securities)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing with minimal market impact. The addition of employee stock options to trading represents normal corporate activity and employee benefit realization. The relatively small number of shares compared to existing float suggests limited impact on stock prices or trading volumes.
