## Summary

BSE notifies trading members about the partial redemption of debentures issued by ASHIANA HOUSING LTD, resulting in a reduced face value per debenture from the original amount to Rs. 764,720.45 per debenture.

## Key Points

- ASHIANA HOUSING LTD (Scrip Code: AHL-8%-31-5-41-PVT, ISIN: INE365D08026, Code: 973220) has completed partial redemption
- New reduced face value: Rs. 764,720.45 per debenture
- Trading with reduced face value begins August 5, 2025
- Reference: DR-688/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. Standard corporate action processing for debenture partial redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the face value change
- All trades from August 5, 2025 onwards must reflect the reduced face value
- Standard settlement procedures apply with new face value

## Important Dates

- **August 4, 2025**: Notice date
- **August 5, 2025**: Effective date for trading with reduced face value

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact confined to holders and traders of ASHIANA HOUSING LTD debentures. The significant reduction in face value will affect trading calculations and settlement amounts for this specific debt instrument.
