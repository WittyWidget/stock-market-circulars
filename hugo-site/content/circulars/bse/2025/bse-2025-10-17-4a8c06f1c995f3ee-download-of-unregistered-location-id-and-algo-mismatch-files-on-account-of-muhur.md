## Summary

BSE has issued notice regarding the download timings for unregistered Location ID and ALGO mismatch files during the Muhurat Trading session scheduled for October 21, 2025. This is a follow-up to previous exchange notices (20250922-21, 20250922-22, 20250922-23) related to the Muhurat Trading session.

## Key Points

- Modified download schedule for UNLC1, UNLC2, and ALGO mismatch files during Muhurat Trading
- UNLC1 and UNLC2 files will be available at 2:30 PM and 3:15 PM
- ALGO mismatch files (<SEG>_ALGO_MM_1) will be available at 2:30 PM
- Trading members can contact their Relationship Manager or BSE Helpdesk for queries

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational adjustment for a special trading session.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Note the revised timings for downloading unregistered Location ID files
- Download UNLC1 and UNLC2 files at 2:30 PM and 3:15 PM
- Download ALGO mismatch files at 2:30 PM
- Contact BSE Helpdesk (022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500) or bsehelp@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **October 21, 2025**: Muhurat Trading session
- **File Download Times**: 2:30 PM and 3:15 PM for UNLC files; 2:30 PM for ALGO mismatch files

## Impact Assessment

Minimal operational impact limited to trading members' file download processes during the special Muhurat Trading session. This is a routine operational notice to ensure smooth functioning of trading operations during the festive trading session. No impact on broader market operations or trading activities.
