## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Highway Infrastructure Limited (Scrip Code: 544477) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (T Group) to the Rolling segment under B Group, effective August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Highway Infrastructure Limited (Scrip Code: 544477)
- Current segment: Trade for Trade (T Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (B Group)
- Effective date: August 28, 2025
- This is a continuation of previous notice 20250811-30 dated August 11, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will move from the restrictive Trade for Trade segment to the more liquid Rolling segment, allowing for normal intraday trading activities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the segment change and adjust their trading systems accordingly. For queries, contact Mr. Parag Jain at 022 2272 8685.

## Important Dates

- **August 28, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer

## Impact Assessment

This represents a positive development for the stock as it moves from the restrictive T Group (where each trade requires separate settlement) to B Group under Rolling segment, which allows for normal intraday trading and better liquidity.
