## Summary

BSE announces the listing of new debt securities issued by Avanti Finance Private Limited on private placement basis, effective from July 30, 2025. The securities carry a 10.90% annual interest rate with quarterly payments and mature on July 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- New debt securities of Avanti Finance Private Limited listed on BSE Debt segment
- Quantity: 2,500 securities with market lot of 1
- Face value and issue price: Rs. 1,00,000 each
- Credit rating: CRISIL BBB/Stable
- ISIN: INE0BNQ07139
- Scrip code: 976962

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Trading members should refer to Placement Memorandum for further details

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: July 29, 2025
- Listing effective date: July 30, 2025
- Interest payment: Quarterly basis
- Redemption date: July 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing. The listing provides additional investment options in the debt segment with a competitive interest rate of 10.90% per annum.
