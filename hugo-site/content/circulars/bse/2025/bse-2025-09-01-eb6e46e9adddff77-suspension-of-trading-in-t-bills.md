## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in three Treasury Bills effective September 2, 2025, as they reach their maturity date. Trading members are advised to cease dealing in these securities.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills will mature on September 2, 2025
- Trading suspension effective from September 2, 2025
- Notice issued under reference DR-706/2025-2026
- All trading members must comply with the suspension

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine operational notice for maturing securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified T-Bills from September 2, 2025
- All market participants must note the suspension and adjust their trading strategies accordingly

## Important Dates

- **September 1, 2025**: Notice date
- **September 2, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date and maturity date for all three T-Bills

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with limited market impact. The suspension affects only three specific T-Bills reaching maturity. Market participants holding these securities will receive redemption proceeds as per standard maturity procedures. No broader market disruption expected as this is standard practice for maturing government securities.
