## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by HDFC Securities Limited on private placement basis, effective August 11, 2025. The instrument is worth Rs. 60 crores (12,000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) with a 91-day maturity period.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (12,000 units)
- Total value: Rs. 60 crores
- Issue price: Rs. 492,219.50 per unit
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730002
- ISIN: INE700G14PG2
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading members must use specified ISIN number

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 11, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 11, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 10, 2025
- Maturity Period: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding for HDFC Securities Limited and offers investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
