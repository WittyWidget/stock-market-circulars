## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in two series of non-convertible debentures of Provident Meryta Private Limited effective August 12, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date for redemption purposes.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to two debenture series: PMPL-ZC-13-4-28-PVT and PMPL-ZC-30-10-29-PVT
- Both series have ISINs INE0NF807017 (974309) and INE0NF807025 (975420) respectively
- No dealings permitted from August 12, 2025
- Suspension is for redemption purposes

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced - standard procedure for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from August 12, 2025
- Members must take note of the suspension and comply with no-dealing restrictions

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: August 12, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: August 12, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only specific debt instruments of one private limited company. Standard corporate action procedure with limited broader market implications.
