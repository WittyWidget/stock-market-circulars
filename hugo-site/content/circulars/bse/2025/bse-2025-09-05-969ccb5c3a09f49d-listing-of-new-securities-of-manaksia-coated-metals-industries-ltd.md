## Summary

BSE has listed 57,90,000 new equity shares of Manaksia Coated Metals & Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 539046) for trading from Monday, September 8, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium to non-promoters through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis.

## Key Points

- 57,90,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at Rs. 64/- premium
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 93634051 to 99424050
- Issue price: Rs. 65/- per share
- ISIN: INE830Q01018

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities availability for trading on the Exchange.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: May 19, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: September 8, 2025
- **Lock-in Period End**: March 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine listing of additional equity shares. The shares are subject to lock-in until March 31, 2026, limiting immediate trading volume impact.
