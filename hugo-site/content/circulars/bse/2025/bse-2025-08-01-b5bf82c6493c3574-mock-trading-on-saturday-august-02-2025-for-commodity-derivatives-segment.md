## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, August 02, 2025. This session is designed for testing and familiarization purposes, allowing trading members to test their systems and applications. The mock trading will run from 10:15 AM to 3:30 PM with no financial obligations or margin requirements.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, August 02, 2025
- Available for Commodity Derivatives segment only
- Members can choose between Mock Trading Session or UAT environment
- Third party trading platforms and in-house systems can participate via IML/ETI APIs
- No new BOLT TWS Release will be implemented
- All trades are for testing only with no financial obligations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine testing session in accordance with SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must comply with testing requirements as per SEBI regulations
- Members using third party platforms or in-house systems should utilize this opportunity for testing
- Active participation in mock trading sessions is requested
- Feedback must be provided by 4:30 PM on the same day

## Important Dates

- **Mock Trading Date**: Saturday, August 02, 2025
- **Log-in Time**: 10:15 AM to 11:00 AM
- **Continuous Trading**: 11:00 AM to 3:30 PM
- **Feedback Deadline**: 4:30 PM on August 02, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a testing session only. No actual trades will be executed, and no margin obligations or settlement processes will apply. The session serves purely for system testing and member familiarization with trading platforms and exceptional market conditions.
