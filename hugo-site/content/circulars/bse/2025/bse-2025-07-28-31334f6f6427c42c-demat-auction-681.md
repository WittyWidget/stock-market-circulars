## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction list for Settlement No. 681 dated July 28, 2025. The auction includes 86 securities from various companies with quantities ranging from 1 share to 197,444 shares. This is a routine settlement process for handling delivery failures and shortages in the demat system.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 681
- Date: July 28, 2025
- Total securities listed: 86 different scrips
- Quantities range from 1 share (multiple stocks) to 197,444 shares (Alstone Text)
- Includes major stocks like Reliance Industries (25 shares), Tata Motors (300 shares), Hero MotoCorp (52 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced - this is a standard operational auction process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants must settle their obligations as per auction procedures
- Members with shortages must participate in the auction or face penalty
- Settlement must be completed as per BSE auction guidelines

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: July 28, 2025
- Settlement Number: 681

Medium impact on affected shareholders and trading members. The auction affects a diverse range of securities from various sectors including banking, automobiles, textiles, and industrial companies.
