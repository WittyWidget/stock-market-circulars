## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Paisalo Digital Limited on private placement basis. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment with effect from October 17, 2025. The issue comprises 100 instruments of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 5 crore, with a maturity date of March 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of face value Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 100 instruments (Rs. 5 crore aggregate)
- Issue price: Rs. 482,147.50 per unit
- Date of allotment: October 16, 2025
- Redemption date: March 24, 2026
- Scrip Code: 730411
- Scrip ID: PDL161025
- ISIN: INE420C14235
- Credit Rating: IVR A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: IDBI Bank Limited
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities to be traded under ISIN INE420C14235
- Contact debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 17, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 24, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 5 months

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term financing to Paisalo Digital Limited and offers debt investment opportunity to institutional investors. The IVR A1+ rating indicates adequate credit quality. Impact is limited to debt segment participants and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
