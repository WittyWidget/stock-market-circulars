## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Rukmani Devi Garg Agro Impex Limited (Scrip Code: 544552) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective Monday, October 20, 2025. This change follows the company's SME IPO listing and represents a transition from restricted to normal trading.

## Key Points

- Company: Rukmani Devi Garg Agro Impex Limited (SME)
- Scrip Code: 544552
- Current segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling settlement (M Group)
- Effective date: Monday, October 20, 2025
- This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251003-53 dated October 3, 2025
- Contact: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for the stock will change from Trade for Trade settlement (where each trade is settled individually on T+1 basis with 100% upfront margin) to Rolling settlement (standard T+2 settlement with regular margin requirements).

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Note the segment change effective October 20, 2025
- Update their systems to reflect the new group classification (M Group)
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain for any clarifications or further details

## Important Dates

- **October 3, 2025**: Initial notice issued (Notice No. 20251003-53)
- **October 6, 2025**: Confirmation notice issued
- **October 20, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer from MT Group to M Group

## Impact Assessment

This is a positive development for the stock as it moves from a restricted trading segment to normal rolling settlement. The change indicates completion of the initial post-IPO stabilization period for this SME stock. Trading will become more liquid with reduced margin requirements, potentially increasing trading volumes and investor participation. Impact is limited to this single SME stock and its trading members.
