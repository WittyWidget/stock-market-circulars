## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Larsen & Toubro Limited on private placement basis. The issue comprises 33,000 securities of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 165 crores, with a 46-day maturity period.

## Key Points

- Issue size: 33,000 securities of Rs. 5,00,000 each (total Rs. 165 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,96,384 per security (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- ISIN: INE018A14LP2
- Scrip code: 730003
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- State Bank of India acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 11, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 12, 2025
- Redemption date: September 26, 2025
- Maturity period: 46 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The issue represents standard corporate treasury operations with no broader market implications.
