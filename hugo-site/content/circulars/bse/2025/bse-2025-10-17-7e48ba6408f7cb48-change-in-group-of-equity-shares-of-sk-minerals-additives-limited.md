## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of SK MINERALS & ADDITIVES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544584) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment effective Tuesday, November 04, 2025. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 220251016-69 dated October 16, 2025. The company's shares will be shifted to M Group for dealings.

## Key Points

- Company: SK MINERALS & ADDITIVES LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544584
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: November 04, 2025
- Segment: SME
- Notice References: Notice No. 20251017-3 and Notice No. 220251016-69 (dated October 16, 2025)

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will transition from Trade for Trade settlement to Rolling settlement mechanism, which allows for intraday trading and T+1 settlement instead of immediate delivery requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Note the change in trading group from MT to M Group
- Update their systems for the new settlement mechanism
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for further details or clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 17, 2025 (Friday)
- **Previous Notice Date**: October 16, 2025 (Thursday)
- **Effective Date**: November 04, 2025 (Tuesday)

## Impact Assessment

This change is positive for market participants as it:
- Increases liquidity by allowing intraday trading
- Removes the restriction of compulsory delivery in Trade for Trade segment
- Enables normal rolling settlement for the scrip
- Provides greater trading flexibility to investors
- May lead to improved price discovery due to enhanced market participation
