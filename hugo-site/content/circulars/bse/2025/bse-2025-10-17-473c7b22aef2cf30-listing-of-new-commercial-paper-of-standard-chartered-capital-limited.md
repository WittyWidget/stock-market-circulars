## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Standard Chartered Capital Limited on private placement basis on its Debt segment effective October 17, 2025. The issuance consists of 3000 Commercial Papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued at Rs. 4,78,644 per unit, maturing on June 5, 2026. The securities carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and will trade exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- 3000 Commercial Papers listed with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Issue price: Rs. 4,78,644 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730407, Scrip ID: SCCL171025
- ISIN: INE403G14TQ4
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for trading members. Standard debt segment trading rules apply.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** October 17, 2025
- **Listing Date:** October 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** June 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine commercial paper listing on the debt segment for institutional investors. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Trading members can contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications. The securities provide short-term investment opportunities in the debt market with approximately 7.5 months maturity period.
