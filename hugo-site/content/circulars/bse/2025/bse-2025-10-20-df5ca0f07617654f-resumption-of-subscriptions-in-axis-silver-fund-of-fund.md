## Summary

Axis Asset Management Company Limited has withdrawn the temporary suspension of subscriptions to units in Axis Silver Fund of Fund. All lumpsum and switch-in applications under the scheme will be accepted effective October 20, 2025. This follows the earlier suspension notice dated October 13, 2025 (Notice no. 71).

## Key Points

- Temporary suspension of subscriptions is being withdrawn
- Resumption effective from October 20, 2025
- Applies to both lumpsum investments and switch-in transactions
- All other terms and conditions of the SID and KIM remain unchanged
- Notice forms integral part of the Scheme Information Document and Key Information Memorandum

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update reversing a previous temporary suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors can resume making lumpsum investments and switch-ins to Axis Silver Fund of Fund
- Fund distributors and platforms should update their systems to accept subscriptions for this scheme
- All scheme-related documents must be read carefully before investing

## Important Dates

- **October 13, 2025**: Original suspension notice (Notice no. 71) issued
- **October 17, 2025**: Date of current notice withdrawing suspension
- **October 20, 2025**: Effective date for resumption of subscriptions

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this affects only a specific mutual fund scheme. Investors who were waiting to invest in Axis Silver Fund of Fund can now resume their investment activities. The suspension period was brief (October 13-20, 2025), minimizing disruption to investors. No impact on equity markets or listed securities.
