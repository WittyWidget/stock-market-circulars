## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited on private placement basis worth Rs. 250 crores. The securities are admitted to dealings on BSE Debt segment with effect from August 14, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total quantity: 5,000 units (Rs. 250 crores total)
- Scrip Code: 730028, Scrip ID: DCBL130825
- ISIN: INE755K14FL9
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 492,691.50 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for commercial paper securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded under ISIN INE755K14FL9

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 13, 2025
- Listing Date: August 14, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 12, 2025
- Tenure: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The securities provide short-term funding option for Dalmia Cement and investment opportunity for institutional investors. IDBI Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying agent.
