## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 10 crore issued by Vivriti Capital Limited on private placement basis, effective August 8, 2025. The instrument carries CRISIL A1+ rating and matures on December 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (200 units)
- Scrip Code: 729993, Scrip ID: VCL7825
- ISIN: INE01HV14896
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 485,197/- per unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Federal Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 7, 2025
- Listing Date: August 8, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates strong creditworthiness of the issuer.
