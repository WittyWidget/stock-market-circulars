## Summary

Axis Finance Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE Debt segment effective October 27, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a quantity of 50,000 units at a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit. The securities carry a 7.23% p.a. interest rate with annual payment frequency and mature on October 24, 2028. The securities are rated CARE AAA and IND AAA.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977243
- Scrip ID: 723AFL28
- ISIN Number: INE891K07AI3
- Quantity: 50,000 units
- Market Lot: 1
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000.00
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000.00
- Interest Rate: 7.23% per annum (paid annually)
- Credit Rating: CARE AAA, IND AAA
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading Mode: Dematerialised form only
- No Put/Call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE891K07AI3
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: October 24, 2025
- Listing Date: October 27, 2025
- Interest Payment: Annually
- Redemption Date: October 24, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The securities are privately placed and will trade only in the debt segment. The high credit rating (AAA) indicates strong credit quality. The listing expands trading options for institutional investors and debt market participants but has no direct impact on equity markets or retail investors. Trading members dealing in debt securities should update their systems with the new scrip details.
