## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Avanti Finance Private Limited issued on private placement basis. The securities worth ₹25 crore (2,500 units of ₹1 lakh each) carry 10.90% annual interest with quarterly payments and mature on July 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2,500 units with face value of ₹1,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 976962 (AFPL-30-07-26-PVT)
- ISIN: INE0BNQ07139
- Interest rate: 10.90% per annum paid quarterly
- Issue date: July 29, 2025
- Maturity: July 30, 2026
- Credit rating: CRISIL BBB/Stable
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities to be traded under specified ISIN number
- Reference to placement memorandum available on BSE website for detailed terms

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: July 29, 2025
- Trading commencement: July 30, 2025
- Interest payment: Quarterly
- Redemption date: July 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing of corporate debt securities. The listing provides additional investment options in the debt segment for institutional and qualified investors.
