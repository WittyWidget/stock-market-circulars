## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that GEPL Capital Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 724) will voluntarily cease operations in the Currency derivative segment effective October 17, 2025. This is a routine notice regarding a member's voluntary business closure in a specific segment.

## Key Points

- GEPL Capital Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 724) is voluntarily closing its business in the Currency derivative segment
- The closure is effective from Friday, October 17, 2025
- Trading members have been notified of this change
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a notification of a member's voluntary business closure.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for other trading members. This is an informational notice regarding GEPL Capital's voluntary closure.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 17, 2025 - GEPL Capital Pvt. Ltd. ceases currency derivative operations

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This affects only one trading member's operations in the currency derivative segment.

**Operational Impact**: No impact on other trading members or market operations. This is a routine member exit from a specific trading segment.

**Trader Impact**: Clients of GEPL Capital Pvt. Ltd. dealing in currency derivatives will need to transfer their positions or close them before the effective date.
