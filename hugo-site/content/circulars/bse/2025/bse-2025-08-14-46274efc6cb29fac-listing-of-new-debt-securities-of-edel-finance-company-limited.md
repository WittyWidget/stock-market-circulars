## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Edel Finance Company Limited issued on private placement basis, effective August 14, 2025. The securities will trade on BSE Debt segment under scrip code 976968.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2,243 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 976968 (EFCL300725)
- ISIN: INE836K07312
- Face Value: ₹1,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: ₹96,052 (at discount)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A/STABLE
- Date of Allotment: August 13, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 30, 2028
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Refer to Placement Memorandum for detailed terms

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: August 14, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: August 13, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: October 30, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing of debt securities. The securities provide institutional investors with a rated debt instrument from Edel Finance Company Limited with approximately 3-year tenure.
