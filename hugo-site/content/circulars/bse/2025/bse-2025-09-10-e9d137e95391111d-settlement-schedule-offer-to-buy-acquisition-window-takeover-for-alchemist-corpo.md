## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement calendar for the Offer to Buy acquisition window (takeover) of Alchemist Corporation Limited. The takeover process will run from September 11-24, 2025, with settlement number 2025083.

## Key Points

- Takeover bidding window: September 11-24, 2025
- Settlement number: 2025083
- Obligation download: October 7, 2025
- Settlement date: October 8, 2025
- Demat shares must be delivered through Early Pay-in mechanism
- Market type "Takeover" to be used in depository systems

## Settlement Details

- **Settlement Type**: Takeover
- **Settlement Number**: 2025083
- **Bid Period**: September 11, 2025 to September 24, 2025
- **Obligation Download**: October 7, 2025
- **Final Settlement**: October 8, 2025

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians must use Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism in both depositories
- Market Type "Takeover" and settlement number 2025083 must be specified in depository systems
- Securities will be earmarked/blocked in client accounts with requisite market type
- No EPN file upload required by Members & Custodians
- Early pay-in must be completed before BSE OTB bidding window closure

## Important Dates

- **September 11, 2025**: Takeover bidding starts
- **September 24, 2025**: Takeover bidding ends
- **October 7, 2025**: Obligation download
- **October 8, 2025**: Final settlement

## Impact Assessment

This circular affects shareholders and trading members involved in Alchemist Corporation Limited. The structured settlement process ensures orderly takeover execution with clear timelines for all participants.
