## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper of Manappuram Finance Limited worth Rs. 200 crores (4000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on the debt segment effective August 19, 2025. The paper was issued on private placement basis with CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- 4000 Commercial Paper units of Rs. 5,00,000 each listed on BSE debt segment
- Issue price: Rs. 464,644.50 per unit (face value Rs. 5,00,000)
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ 
- Scrip code: 730043, Scrip ID: MFL180825
- ISIN: INE522D14OP1
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Axis Bank Limited serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Listing date: August 19, 2025
- Date of allotment: August 18, 2025
- Date of redemption: August 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional debt instrument options for institutional investors with high credit rating backing.
