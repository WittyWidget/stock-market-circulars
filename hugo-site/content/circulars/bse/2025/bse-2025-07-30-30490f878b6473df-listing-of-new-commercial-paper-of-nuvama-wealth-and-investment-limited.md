## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited on private placement basis. Two tranches totaling Rs. 175 crores (500 + 3000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) have been admitted for trading on the BSE Debt segment with ICICI Bank Ltd as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- First tranche: 500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, maturing August 14, 2025 (Scrip Code: 729935)
- Second tranche: 3000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, maturing December 15, 2025 (Scrip Code: 729663)
- Both tranches carry CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ credit ratings
- Trading only in dematerialized form with standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs
- Tick size is 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded under specified ISIN numbers

## Important Dates

- **First Tranche Allotment Date**: July 29, 2025
- **First Tranche Redemption**: August 14, 2025
- **Second Tranche Allotment Date**: July 30, 2025
- **Second Tranche Redemption**: December 15, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: July 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. Provides additional short-term debt investment options for institutional investors. No impact on equity markets or broader regulatory framework.
