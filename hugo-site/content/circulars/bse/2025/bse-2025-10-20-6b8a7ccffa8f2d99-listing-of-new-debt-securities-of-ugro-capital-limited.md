## Summary

UGRO CAPITAL LIMITED has listed new debt securities on BSE's Debt segment effective October 20, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 10,000 per unit, totaling 150,000 units (Rs. 150 crore). The debentures carry an interest rate of 9.75% per annum with monthly interest payments and mature on October 15, 2027.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 977228
- **Scrip ID**: 975UCL27
- **ISIN**: INE583D07612
- **Quantity**: 150,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- **Date of Allotment**: October 16, 2025
- **Interest Rate**: 9.75% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly from November 17, 2025 to October 15, 2027
- **Redemption Date**: October 15, 2027
- **Credit Rating**: IND A+/Rating Watch with Positive Implications
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE583D07612
- Securities must be traded with tick size of 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarifications should contact the debt department at BSE on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915
- Refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: October 20, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: October 16, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: November 17, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: October 15, 2027
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Monthly (17th of each month from November 2025 to October 2027)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine private placement listing that provides additional debt financing options for UGRO CAPITAL LIMITED and investment opportunities for debt market participants.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors and high net-worth individuals participating in the debt segment who may consider these securities offering 9.75% annual returns with IND A+ credit rating.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing procedures apply with demat-only trading and existing BSE infrastructure supporting the transaction.
