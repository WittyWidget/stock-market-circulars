## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of NOVUS LOYALTY LIMITED filed on September 30, 2025, containing disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations. The document is available on the BSE SME website for public comments for a period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by NOVUS LOYALTY LIMITED on September 30, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments sought within 21 days from October 06, 2025
- Comments can be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document publication.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and public are invited to review the draft offer document
- Comments, if any, must be submitted within 21 days from the notice date (October 06, 2025)
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filing Date**: September 30, 2025
- **Notice Date**: October 06, 2025
- **Public Comment Period**: 21 days from October 06, 2025 (deadline: October 27, 2025)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural notice for an SME IPO draft offer document. The impact is limited to potential investors and market participants interested in the NOVUS LOYALTY LIMITED IPO. The 21-day public comment period is a standard regulatory requirement with minimal broader market impact.
