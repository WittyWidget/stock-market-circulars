## Summary

BSE notifies trading members that 20,56,965 equity shares of Ind-Agiv Commerce Ltd. (Scrip Code: 517077) have been listed and are permitted for trading effective October 21, 2025. The shares were issued on a preferential basis to promoter and non-promoter at Rs. 29/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 19/-). The securities rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to lock-in provisions.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 20,56,965 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 29/- per share (including premium of Rs. 19/-)
- Allotment date: May 12, 2025
- Trading commencement: October 21, 2025
- Distribution numbers: 1000001 to 3056965
- ISIN: INE115E01010
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Issued to both promoter and non-promoter on preferential basis

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities availability for trading
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed as per the specified schedule
- Shares subject to lock-in cannot be transferred until lock-in period expires

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: May 12, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 21, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (1,55,000 shares)**: June 29, 2027 (Distribution Nos. 1000001-1155000)
- **Lock-in Expiry (19,01,965 shares)**: June 29, 2026 (Distribution Nos. 1155001-3056965)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a company-specific listing event affecting Ind-Agiv Commerce Ltd. shareholders and potential investors. The preferential issue increases the total paid-up capital of the company. The majority of shares (19,01,965) have a one-year lock-in period until June 2026, while a smaller portion (1,55,000 shares) has a two-year lock-in until June 2027. Trading members can facilitate trades in these securities from October 21, 2025, subject to lock-in restrictions.
