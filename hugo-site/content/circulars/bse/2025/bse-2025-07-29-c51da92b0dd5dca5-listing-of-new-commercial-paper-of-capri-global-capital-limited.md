## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Capri Global Capital Limited on private placement basis. The securities are worth Rs. 5 lakh each with 500 units totaling Rs. 25 crores, carrying CRISIL A1+ rating and maturing in one year.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each with 500 units listed
- Issue price: Rs. 4,60,829.50 per unit (discount to face value)
- CRISIL A1+ credit rating assigned
- One-year maturity (July 29, 2025 to July 29, 2026)
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Yes Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise for trading

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE180C14557
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- All trades subject to 1 paise tick size

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 29, 2025
- Trading Commencement: July 29, 2025
- Redemption Date: July 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding option for Capri Global Capital Limited and investment opportunity for institutional investors in highly-rated commercial paper.
