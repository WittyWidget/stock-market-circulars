## Summary

360 ONE Prime Limited has listed new commercial paper securities worth Rs. 50 crores on BSE's Debt segment effective August 4, 2025. The commercial paper was issued on private placement basis with face value of Rs. 5 lakh each and carries CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper quantity: 1,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total value: Rs. 50 crores
- Scrip Code: 729958, Scrip ID: 360OPL1825
- ISIN: INE248U14RY2
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,80,492 per unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Limited serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 1, 2025
- **Listing Date**: August 4, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by 360 ONE Prime Limited. The listing provides additional short-term debt instrument options for institutional investors in the debt segment.
