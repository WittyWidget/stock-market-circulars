## Summary

BSE announces the addition of six newly listed companies to its indices. PACE DIGITEK LIMITED (544550) will be added to the BSE IPO index effective October 8, 2025. Five SME companies - BHAVIK ENTERPRISES LIMITED (544551), AMEENJI RUBBER LIMITED (544555), KVS CASTINGS LIMITED (544554), M P K STEELS (I) LIMITED (544553), and RUKMANI DEVI GARG AGRO IMPEX LIMITED (544552) - will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective October 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- PACE DIGITEK LIMITED listed on BSE main board on October 6, 2025
- Five companies listed on BSE SME platform on October 6, 2025
- All additions follow standard listing procedures and prior notices
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- No impact on existing index constituents

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index maintenance action following new listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Market participants should note the index composition changes for portfolio management and index-linked products.

## Important Dates

- **October 6, 2025**: Listing date for all six companies on respective BSE platforms
- **October 7, 2025**: Five SME companies added to BSE SME IPO index at market open
- **October 8, 2025**: PACE DIGITEK LIMITED added to BSE IPO index at market open

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. Routine index additions following new listings with no displacement of existing constituents.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant for investors tracking BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO indices, and for index fund managers who may need to adjust holdings.

**Operational Impact**: Standard index rebalancing procedures apply. Changes reflected in corporate action files for automated processing by market participants.
