## Summary

BSE has notified that trading member JUST TRADE SECURITIES LIMITED (Member No. 6421) will change its name to "BAJAJ CAPITAL MARKETS LIMITED" effective Tuesday, July 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading member number 6421 remains unchanged
- Name change from JUST TRADE SECURITIES LIMITED to BAJAJ CAPITAL MARKETS LIMITED
- Administrative change with no impact on trading operations
- Contact membership.ops@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes - purely administrative name update for existing trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their records to reflect the new name of member 6421 in their systems and documentation.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Tuesday, July 29, 2025
- **Notice Date**: July 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact - routine administrative change that does not affect trading operations, member rights, or market functionality. Member number and trading privileges remain unchanged.
