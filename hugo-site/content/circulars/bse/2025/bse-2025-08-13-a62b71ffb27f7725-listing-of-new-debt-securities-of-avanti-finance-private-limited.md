## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Avanti Finance Private Limited issued on private placement basis. The securities carry a 10.9% interest rate and are set to mature on September 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 1,500 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 976062 (AFPL-10.90%-30-09-26-PVT)
- ISIN: INE0BNQ07113 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Credit Rating: CRISIL BBB+/Stable
- Date of Allotment: August 12, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard debt securities listing procedure followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Trading members should refer to Placement Memorandum for further details

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: August 13, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: August 12, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly from August 30, 2025 to September 30, 2026
- **Redemption Date**: September 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing of debt securities. The listing provides additional debt market options for institutional investors with a BBB+ rated instrument offering 10.9% annual returns.
