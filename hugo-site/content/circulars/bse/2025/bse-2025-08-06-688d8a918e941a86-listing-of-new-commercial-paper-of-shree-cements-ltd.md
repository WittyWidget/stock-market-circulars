## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Shree Cements Ltd. issued on private placement basis. The securities worth Rs. 100 crore (2000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) are now available for trading on BSE's debt segment.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (2000 units)
- Scrip Code: 729871, Scrip ID: SCL180725
- ISIN Number: INE070A14802 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 496,293.50/-
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- HDFC Bank Ltd. serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 6, 2025
- Redemption Date: September 22, 2025
- Trading Effective: August 6, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. Provides additional short-term debt instrument for institutional investors with 47-day maturity period.
