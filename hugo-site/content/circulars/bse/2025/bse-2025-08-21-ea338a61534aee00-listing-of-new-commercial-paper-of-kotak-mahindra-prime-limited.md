## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited on a private placement basis. Two series of commercial papers with face value of Rs. 5 lakh each have been admitted for trading on the BSE Debt segment effective August 21, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two series of commercial papers listed with total quantity of 14,000 units
- First series: 10,000 units with redemption on August 28, 2025 (7-day tenure)
- Second series: 4,000 units with redemption on July 6, 2026 (320-day tenure)
- Both series carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings
- Face value of Rs. 5,00,000 per unit for both series
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Securities Details

**Series 1 (KMPL21825):**
- Scrip Code: 730062
- ISIN: INE916D145N8
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,99,411
- Redemption: August 28, 2025

**Series 2 (KMPL21825A):**
- Scrip Code: 730063
- ISIN: INE916D145O6
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,72,419.50
- Redemption: July 6, 2026

## Trading Requirements

- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Kotak Mahindra Bank Ltd

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** August 21, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** August 21, 2025
- **First Series Redemption:** August 28, 2025
- **Second Series Redemption:** July 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The securities provide short-term and medium-term debt investment options for institutional investors in the debt segment.
