## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in debt securities of SML (Scrip Code: 974313, ISIN: INE982X07341) with effect from October 17, 2025. The suspension is due to the maturity of the 12.75% debentures on October 20, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 974313
- ISIN: INE982X07341
- Security: SML-12.75%-20-10-25-PVT (privately placed debentures)
- Coupon Rate: 12.75%
- Maturity Date: October 20, 2025
- Trading Suspension Date: October 17, 2025
- Circular Reference: DR-738/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for debt security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from October 17, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the suspension
- No new trades should be executed in this security

## Important Dates

- **October 16, 2025**: Notice date
- **October 17, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **October 20, 2025**: Debenture maturity date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine suspension affecting a single privately placed debt security approaching maturity. The suspension is procedural and expected.

**Investor Impact**: Minimal - Investors holding these debentures will receive their principal and final interest payment on the maturity date. No trading impact for equity markets.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Standard procedure for maturing debt securities with no broader market implications.
