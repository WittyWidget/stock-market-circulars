## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 1,08,000 new equity shares of Mish Designs Limited (Scrip Code: 544015) effective from Monday, August 25, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium through conversion of warrants to non-promoters on a preferential basis.

## Key Points

- 1,08,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at Rs. 140/- premium
- Issue price: Rs. 150/- per share
- Shares issued to Non Promoters on preferential basis
- Allotment date: January 16, 2025
- Trading commences: August 25, 2025
- ISIN: INE0QI101019
- Distinctive numbers: 2967001 to 3075000

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities are available for trading from August 25, 2025
- Lock-in period applies to all 1,08,000 shares until February 28, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: January 16, 2025
- **Trading Start Date**: August 25, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: February 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this represents a small preferential allotment with lock-in restrictions. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and will increase the company's tradeable float after the lock-in period expires.
