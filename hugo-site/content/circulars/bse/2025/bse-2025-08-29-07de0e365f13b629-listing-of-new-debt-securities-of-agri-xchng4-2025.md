## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of AGRI XCHNG4 2025 issued on private placement basis for trading on the BSE Debt segment effective August 29, 2025. The securities carry an 11.70% annual interest rate with monthly interest payments and redemption date of August 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 150,178,426 securities listed
- Face value and issue price: Rs. 1.00 each
- Credit rating: ICRA A2 (SO)
- Market lot: 100,000 securities
- ISIN: INE2G1D15012
- Scrip code: 977062
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard debt security listing procedure followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN
- Refer to Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for detailed terms
- Contact BSE debt department for clarifications on specified phone numbers

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: August 26, 2025
- Trading effective date: August 29, 2025
- Interest payment period: August 28, 2025 to August 20, 2026 (monthly)
- Redemption date: August 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing. The securities provide institutional investors access to short-term debt instruments with competitive interest rates. Trading volume expected to be limited to institutional participants given the high market lot size of 100,000 securities.
