## Summary

KEERTANA FINSERV LIMITED has announced part redemption of its non-convertible debentures along with interest payment. The debentures will trade with a reduced face value of Rs. 75,000 per debenture effective September 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: KEERTANA FINSERV LIMITED
- Debenture Code: KFL-11.4%-19-6-26-PVT (INE0NES07196)
- ISIN: 976800
- Record Date: September 1, 2025
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and interest payment
- New face value: Rs. 75,000 per debenture

## Regulatory Changes

Trading in the company's debentures will commence with the reduced face value effective from the record date.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the change in face value for settlement purposes under DR-705/2025-2026.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: September 1, 2025
- Effective Date for Reduced Face Value: September 1, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-705/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

The part redemption will reduce the outstanding principal amount of the debentures, affecting the trading value and settlement calculations for market participants dealing in these debt securities.
