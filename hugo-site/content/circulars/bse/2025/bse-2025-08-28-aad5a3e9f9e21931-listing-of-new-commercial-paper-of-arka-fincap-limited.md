## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by ARKA FINCAP LIMITED on private placement basis. The commercial paper has a face value of Rs.5 lakh each with 2000 units totaling Rs.10 crores, carrying CRISIL A1+ credit rating.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs.5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 2000 units (Rs.10 crores total)
- Issue price: Rs.473,490 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+
- Scrip code: 730105, ISIN: INE03W114716
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 lakhs and multiples

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- IndusInd Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Listing effective date: August 28, 2025
- Date of allotment: August 26, 2025
- Date of redemption: May 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding option for ARKA FINCAP LIMITED and investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
