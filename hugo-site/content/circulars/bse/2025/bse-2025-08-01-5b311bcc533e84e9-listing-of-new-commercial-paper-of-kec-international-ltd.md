## Summary

KEC International Ltd. has listed new Commercial Paper securities worth Rs. 75 crores (1,500 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) on the BSE Debt segment. The securities are issued on private placement basis and will be traded in dematerialized form with a maturity period of approximately 3 months.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each totaling 1,500 units
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,159 per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- ISIN: INE389H14LE6, Scrip Code: 729950
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Axis Bank Limited serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 1, 2025
- Listing Date: August 1, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 30, 2025
- Maturity Period: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates strong creditworthiness of the issuer. Trading will be limited to institutional and high-net-worth investors due to the high denomination requirement.
