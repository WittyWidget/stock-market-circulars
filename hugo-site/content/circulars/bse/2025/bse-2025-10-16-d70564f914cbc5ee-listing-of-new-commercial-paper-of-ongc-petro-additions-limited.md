## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by ONGC Petro additions Limited on private placement basis, effective October 16, 2025. The listing comprises 8000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 400 crores. The securities will be traded exclusively in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment with ICICI Bank Ltd serving as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- 8000 units of Commercial Paper listed with face value Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 730394, Scrip ID: OPAL151025
- ISIN Number: INE163N14584
- Issue Price: Rs. 488,561.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Ltd appointed as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE163N14584
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 16, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** October 15, 2025
- **Listing Date:** October 16, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** February 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market impact as it represents a routine addition of short-term debt instruments to the BSE Debt segment. The Commercial Paper is rated CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+, indicating highest safety regarding timely payment of financial obligations. The listing provides ONGC Petro additions Limited access to debt capital markets for short-term funding needs (approximately 4.5 months tenure). Impact is limited to debt market participants and institutional investors in commercial paper instruments.
