## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited issued on private placement basis. The securities are market-linked with a face value of Rs. 100,000 each and will be traded in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 660 securities listed
- Scrip Code: 976954, Scrip ID: NWFL24725
- ISIN: INE918K07PX1 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 96,888 per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum for detailed terms
- Contact debt department for clarifications on specified phone numbers

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 18, 2025
- Listing Date: August 20, 2025
- Interest Payment Date: January 22, 2029 (cumulative)
- Redemption Date: January 22, 2029

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt securities listing for institutional investors. The securities are market-linked with a tenure of approximately 4.5 years and rated AA/Stable by CARE.
