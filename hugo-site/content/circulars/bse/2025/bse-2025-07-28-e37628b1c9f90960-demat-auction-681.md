## Summary

BSE has published Demat Auction Settlement No. 681 dated July 28, 2025, listing 82 securities with their respective auction quantities. The auction covers various stocks across different sectors with quantities ranging from 1 share to over 4,000 shares.

## Key Points

- Settlement number: 681
- Date: July 28, 2025
- Total securities listed: 82
- Quantities range from 1 share (multiple stocks) to 4,498 shares (KELLTON TECH)
- Covers diverse sectors including banking, textiles, automobiles, and technology

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes specified in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must comply with demat auction settlement procedures
- Securities listed must be delivered as per BSE settlement guidelines

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: July 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact as this is a routine demat auction settlement affecting multiple securities. The quantities are relatively small for most stocks, suggesting limited market disruption. However, some stocks like KELLTON TECH (4,498 shares), BOMBAY DYEING (3,720 shares), and HUBTOWN (3,777 shares) have significant quantities that may affect their trading.
