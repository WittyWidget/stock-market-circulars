## Summary

Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 300 crores (6000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE's debt segment. The securities are available for trading in dematerialized form starting August 20, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper issued on private placement basis
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 6000 units (Rs. 300 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,703.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- ISIN: INE831R14FD0
- Scrip code: 730046

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 19, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 20, 2025
- Redemption date: November 18, 2025
- Tenure: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for corporate funding needs. The securities provide short-term investment options for institutional investors in the debt market.
