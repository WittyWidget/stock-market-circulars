## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 50 crore issued by Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective July 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Issue size: Rs. 50 crore (1000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,80,370.50 per unit (face value Rs. 5 lakh)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+
- Scrip code: 729913, ISIN: INE101Q14223
- Issuing and paying agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applies

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: July 25, 2025
- Trading commencement: July 28, 2025
- Redemption date: December 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding option for Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited with 5-month tenure.
