## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of further securities issued by 13 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). The securities will be listed and admitted for trading effective Wednesday, July 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- 13 companies are issuing additional securities under ESOP/ESOS
- Total securities range from 2,221 shares (Oracle Financial) to 1,207,200 shares (Dhani Services)
- Face values vary from ₹1 to ₹10 per share
- All securities are not under lock-in period
- Effective trading date: July 30, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed about the new securities available for trading from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Trading Date**: Wednesday, July 30, 2025
- **Notice Date**: July 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is routine listing of employee stock options. The securities represent employee compensation and are part of normal corporate governance practices. No lock-in restrictions apply to these securities.
