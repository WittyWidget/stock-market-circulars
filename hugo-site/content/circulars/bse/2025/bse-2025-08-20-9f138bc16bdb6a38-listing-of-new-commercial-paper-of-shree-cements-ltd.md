## Summary

Shree Cements Ltd. has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 50 crores (1000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE Debt segment. The commercial paper was issued on private placement basis with 34-day maturity period and carries CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 denomination each
- Quantity: 1000 units (Total value: Rs. 50 crores)
- Scrip Code: 729917, Scrip ID: SCL280725
- ISIN: INE070A14810 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,97,322.50 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 20, 2025
- Listing Date: August 20, 2025
- Redemption Date: September 23, 2025
- Maturity Period: 34 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by an established cement company. The short-term nature (34 days) and private placement basis limits broader market implications. Standard credit ratings indicate normal corporate funding activity.
