## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of Muthoot Fincorp Limited worth Rs. 125 crores (2,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective September 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each issued by Muthoot Fincorp Limited
- Total quantity: 2,500 units (Rs. 125 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,80,268 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+
- Scrip code: 728935, ISIN: INE549K14BX0
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and paying agent: IndusInd Bank Limited

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: September 3, 2025
- Trading commencement: September 4, 2025
- Redemption date: March 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding avenue for Muthoot Fincorp Limited and investment opportunity for debt market participants.
