## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Midland Microfin Limited worth Rs. 50 crore issued on private placement basis. The securities carry 12% interest rate and will mature on November 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- New debt securities of Midland Microfin Limited listed on BSE Debt segment
- Issue size: Rs. 50 crore (50,000 securities of Rs. 10,000 each)
- Interest rate: 12% per annum
- Credit rating: ACUITE A-
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Security Details

- **Scrip Code**: 977047
- **Scrip ID**: 12MML2026
- **ISIN**: INE884Q07780
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10,000
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 10,000
- **Allotment Date**: August 21, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: November 19, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 21, 2025
- **Listing Date**: August 22, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Quarterly from November 19, 2025 to November 19, 2026
- **Redemption Date**: November 19, 2026

## Trading Information

- Trading only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE884Q07780
- Market lot size: 1 security
- Minimum tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call options available

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. The securities provide institutional investors access to Midland Microfin's debt instruments with attractive 12% yield and A- credit rating.
