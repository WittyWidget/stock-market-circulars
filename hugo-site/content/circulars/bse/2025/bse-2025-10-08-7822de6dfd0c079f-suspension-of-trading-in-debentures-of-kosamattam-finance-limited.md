## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of Kosamattam Finance Limited with ISIN INE403Q07DH0 (scrip code 938538) effective from October 13, 2025. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for redemption of these debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Company: KOSAMATTAM FINANCE LIMITED
- ISIN: INE403Q07DH0
- Scrip Code: 938538
- Security Type: Non-Convertible Debentures (KFL-28-10-25-NCD)
- Record Date: October 13, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures
- Trading Suspension Date: October 13, 2025
- Notice Number: DR-734/2025-2026
- Notice Date: October 08, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard suspension notice issued in accordance with existing market regulations for corporate actions involving debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified debentures from October 13, 2025
- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the suspension
- No buy or sell orders should be accepted for scrip code 938538 from the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **October 08, 2025**: Notice issued by BSE
- **October 13, 2025**: Record date for redemption
- **October 13, 2025**: Trading suspension effective from this date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited impact as this affects only holders of specific debentures of Kosamattam Finance Limited. The suspension is temporary and directly related to the redemption process.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders on the record date (October 13, 2025) will be eligible for redemption. No trading will be possible from the suspension date, ensuring clean settlement for the redemption process.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to ensure compliance by blocking trading in the specified security code from October 13, 2025.
