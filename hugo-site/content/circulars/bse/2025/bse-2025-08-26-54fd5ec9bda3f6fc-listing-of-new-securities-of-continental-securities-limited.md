## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 15,00,000 new equity shares of Continental Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 538868) effective from August 28, 2025. These shares were issued through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis to non-promoters.

## Key Points

- 15,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at premium of Rs. 19/-
- Issue price: Rs. 21/- per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE183Q01020
- Distribution numbers: 27753001 to 29253000

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new security listing
- Lock-in period applicable until June 15, 2026
- All 15,00,000 shares subject to lock-in restrictions

## Important Dates

- Allotment dates: May 24, 2025 (7.25 lakh shares) and May 31, 2025 (7.75 lakh shares)
- Trading commencement: August 28, 2025
- Lock-in expiry: June 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine listing of shares issued through warrant conversion. The lock-in period until June 2026 ensures these shares won't immediately impact trading volumes. This represents additional capital raise for Continental Securities Limited through preferential allotment mechanism.
