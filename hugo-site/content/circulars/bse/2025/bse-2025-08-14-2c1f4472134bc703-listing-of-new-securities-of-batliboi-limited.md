## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 1,26,81,963 new equity shares of Batliboi Limited (Scrip Code: 522004) resulting from the merger of Batliboi Environmental Engineering Limited (BEEL) with Batliboi Limited. The new securities will be available for trading from Monday, August 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- 1,26,81,963 new equity shares of Rs. 5/- each face value
- Issued under Scheme of Amalgamation between BEEL and Batliboi Limited
- ISIN: INE177C01022
- Distribution numbers: 34309885 to 46991847
- Allotment date: June 24, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing procedure following completion of amalgamation scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the increased share capital
- Market participants should note the expanded equity base for position calculations
- Standard trading rules and regulations apply to the new securities

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: June 24, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: Monday, August 18, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 14, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The listing significantly increases Batliboi Limited's equity base, which may affect share price dynamics and trading volumes. Existing shareholders receive additional shares as per the amalgamation scheme, potentially improving liquidity in the stock. The merger consolidates operations between the two entities under Batliboi Limited.
