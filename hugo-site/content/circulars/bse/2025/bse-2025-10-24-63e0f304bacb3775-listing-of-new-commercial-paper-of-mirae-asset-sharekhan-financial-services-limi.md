## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Mirae Asset Sharekhan Financial Services Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective October 24, 2025. The issue comprises 1,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 50 crore, with a 91-day tenure.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 730433
- Scrip ID: MASF241025
- ISIN: INE550X14AK0
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,243 per unit
- Total Issue Size: 1,000 units (Rs. 50 crore)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE550X14AK0
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: October 24, 2025
- Listing Date: October 24, 2025
- Redemption Date: January 23, 2026
- Tenure: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term funding to Mirae Asset Sharekhan Financial Services Limited and offers institutional investors a CRISIL A1+ rated investment option with 91-day maturity. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
