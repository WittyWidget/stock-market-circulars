## Summary

BSE announces the addition of two newly listed SME companies to the BSE SME IPO Index. ABRIL PAPER TECH LIMITED (ticker 544500) and SUGS LLOYD LIMITED (ticker 544501) will be included in the index effective at market open on Monday, September 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two companies being added to BSE SME IPO Index
- Both companies listed on BSE SME platform on September 5, 2025
- Index addition effective September 8, 2025 at market open
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes involved - this is a routine index maintenance activity.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index changes are automatically processed through corporate action files.

## Important Dates

- **September 5, 2025**: Companies listed on BSE SME platform
- **September 8, 2025**: Effective date for index addition at market open

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this involves SME segment index additions. The changes primarily affect index tracking funds and products that follow the BSE SME IPO Index. Market participants using this index for benchmarking or product creation should note the composition change.
