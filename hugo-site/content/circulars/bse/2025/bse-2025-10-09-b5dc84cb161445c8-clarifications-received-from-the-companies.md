## Summary

BSE published clarifications received from three listed companies in response to exchange queries regarding unusual price or volume movements in their securities. All three companies - Futuristic Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 534063), Graphite India Limited (Scrip Code: 509488), and Indraprastha Medical Corporation Limited - confirmed they have no material information or pending announcements that would justify the observed market behavior under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.

## Key Points

- **Futuristic Solutions Limited**: Confirmed no information, announcement, or impending material event requiring disclosure; attributes price movement to market-driven factors and external conditions
- **Graphite India Limited**: Responded to query about significant volume increase; confirmed compliance with Regulation 30 and stated no information/announcement that may bear on price/volume behavior
- **Indraprastha Medical Corporation Limited**: Clarified increase in share volume across exchanges; confirmed no information or impending announcement affecting operations/performance
- All three companies affirmed ongoing compliance with SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015
- Companies attribute movements to market conditions rather than company-specific developments

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular represents routine surveillance activity under existing SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015, specifically Regulation 30 concerning disclosure of material events.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed companies must respond to exchange surveillance queries regarding unusual price/volume movements
- Companies must disclose whether any material events, information, or pending announcements exist that could affect security prices
- Continued compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 required
- Prompt disclosure of any future material events/information remains mandatory

## Important Dates

- **October 8, 2025**: BSE sent surveillance queries to Futuristic Solutions Limited and Indraprastha Medical Corporation Limited
- **October 9, 2025**: BSE sent surveillance query to Graphite India Limited; all three companies submitted their clarification responses

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The clarifications confirm absence of undisclosed material information, providing transparency to investors but not indicating any fundamental changes in the companies' business operations or prospects.

**Investor Impact**: Low. Investors receive confirmation that recent price/volume movements are not driven by unreported corporate developments, helping to dispel speculation about undisclosed events.

**Operational Impact**: None. The clarifications are procedural responses to exchange surveillance and do not reflect operational changes at any of the three companies.

**Compliance Impact**: Demonstrates normal functioning of exchange surveillance mechanisms and company compliance with disclosure obligations under SEBI regulations.
