## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Shreeji Shipping Global Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544490 and trading symbol SHREEJISPG with ISIN number INE1B6101010.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Shreeji Shipping Global Limited
- Scrip Code: 544490
- Trading Symbol: SHREEJISPG
- ISIN Number: INE1B6101010
- Listing date and security details to be announced separately

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

Trading members are informed of the upcoming listing and should prepare for trading in the new security once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: August 25, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. Trading members should monitor for the separate notice announcing the actual listing date and trading commencement details.
