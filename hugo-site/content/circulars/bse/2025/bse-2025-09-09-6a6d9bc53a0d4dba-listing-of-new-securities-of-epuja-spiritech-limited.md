## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 29,39,706 new equity shares of EPUJA SPIRITECH LIMITED (Scrip Code: 532092) for trading from September 10, 2025. These shares were issued to non-promoters on preferential basis through conversion of warrants at Rs. 3.40 per share.

## Key Points

- 29,39,706 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at premium of Rs. 2.40/-
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 78639334 to 81579039
- Date of allotment: October 10, 2024
- ISIN: INE807D01030

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 29,39,706 shares until March 31, 2026

## Important Dates

- Trading commencement: September 10, 2025
- Date of allotment: October 10, 2024
- Lock-in period ends: March 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine listing of shares issued through warrant conversion. The lock-in period until March 2026 prevents immediate trading of these shares, limiting supply-side pressure.
