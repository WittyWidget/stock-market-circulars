## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 50 crores issued by Mirae Asset Financial Services (India) Private Limited on private placement basis. The instrument carries CRISIL A1+ rating and has a 3-month maturity period.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 1,000 units (Rs. 50 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730048, ISIN: INE0JRU14255
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,544 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise for trading

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 20, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 20, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding. The CRISIL A1+ rating indicates high credit quality, providing investors with a safe short-term investment option.
