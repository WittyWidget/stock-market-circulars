## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Kosamattam Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 977001, ISIN: INE403Q07GC4) on its Debt segment effective October 24, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit, carrying an interest rate of 10.62% per annum with monthly interest payments, and maturing on February 7, 2028. The securities are rated IND A/Stable and will trade exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Quantity listed: 7,500 securities
- Scrip Code: 977001, Scrip ID: 1062KFLD28
- ISIN: INE403Q07GC4 (further listings under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 99,592.6575 per unit
- Interest Rate: 10.62% per annum (paid monthly)
- Credit Rating: IND A/Stable
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Redemption Date: February 7, 2028
- No put/call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE403Q07GC4
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members can contact the BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 24, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Monthly
- **Redemption Date**: February 7, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to private placement debt securities of a single issuer. The impact is limited to:

- **Debt Market Participants**: Trading members dealing in corporate debt securities can now trade these instruments on BSE's debt platform
- **Investors**: Provides additional investment option in rated corporate debt (IND A/Stable rating) with regular monthly income
- **Liquidity**: Market lot of 1 and tick size of 1 paise allows for flexible trading
- **Company**: Kosamattam Finance Limited gains access to organized debt market platform for its securities

The securities offer a competitive interest rate of 10.62% per annum with monthly payments, making them suitable for income-focused debt investors. The IND A/Stable rating indicates adequate credit quality with stable outlook.
