## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth ₹100 crore issued by Sterlite Technologies Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective August 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of ₹5 lakh denomination each (2000 units)
- Scrip Code: 730116, Scrip ID: STL290825
- ISIN: INE089C14DR2
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue price: ₹4,87,135.50 per unit
- HDFC Bank Limited as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN
- Standard denomination of ₹5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise for trading

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 29, 2025
- **Trading Start**: August 29, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding for Sterlite Technologies with approximately 4-month maturity period.
