## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of SHRI RAM FINANCE CORPORATION PRIVATE LIMITED issued on private placement basis, effective from September 10, 2025. The securities carry a 13.25% per annum interest rate with monthly interest payments and mature on December 9, 2030.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 3,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 977091, Scrip ID: 1325SRFC30
- ISIN Number: INE08E808041
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 (at par)
- Credit Rating: ACUITE A Stable
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum for detailed information

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: September 9, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: September 10, 2025
- Interest Payment Dates: Monthly from October 9, 2025 to December 9, 2030
- Redemption Date: December 9, 2030

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities. The listing provides additional investment options for institutional investors in the debt segment with attractive interest rates.
