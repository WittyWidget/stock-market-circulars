## Summary

BSE has announced that THE WEALTH COMPANY MUTUAL FUND will reopen four schemes for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective October 17, 2025. This follows previous notices 20250923-24 and 20250923-25. The schemes will support SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities.

## Key Points

- Four THE WEALTH COMPANY MUTUAL FUND schemes reopening for transactions
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform for subscription and redemption
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be enabled
- Effective date: October 17, 2025
- Follows reference notices 20250923-24 and 20250923-25

## Schemes Details

**Reopening Schemes:**

1. **THE WEALTH COMPANY ARBITRAGE FUND** - Equity Category
2. **THE WEALTH COMPANY ETHICAL FUND** - Equity Category
3. **THE WEALTH COMPANY FLEXI CAP FUND** - Equity Category
4. **THE WEALTH COMPANY LIQUID FUND** - Liquid Category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding scheme availability on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members and market participants should:
- Note the availability of these schemes for transactions starting October 17, 2025
- Update their systems to reflect the reopening of these schemes
- Enable subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP facilities for these schemes

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 16, 2025
- **Effective Date:** October 17, 2025 - Schemes available for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market-wide impact. It primarily benefits:
- Investors seeking to invest in THE WEALTH COMPANY MUTUAL FUND schemes
- BSE StAR MF Platform participants who can now offer these schemes to clients
- Brokers and distributors who can facilitate transactions in these four schemes

The reopening provides investors with access to diversified investment options across equity (Arbitrage, Ethical, Flexi Cap) and liquid fund categories through systematic investment and withdrawal facilities.
