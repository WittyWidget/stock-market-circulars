## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in a specific Treasury Bill (T-bill) with effect from October 24, 2025, due to its scheduled redemption/maturity. Trading Members are advised not to deal in this instrument from the specified date.

## Key Points

- T-bill with Scrip Code 810573 (ISIN: IN2220150113) - 799MAHSDL25 will mature on the redemption date
- Trading suspension effective from October 24, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-742/2025-2026
- Trading Members must cease all dealings in this T-bill from the suspension date

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for instruments reaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not execute any trades in the specified T-bill (Scrip Code 810573) from October 24, 2025 onwards
- Members should update their trading systems to reflect the suspension
- Ensure clients are informed about the trading suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 23, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: October 24, 2025 (maturity date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative action affecting a single T-bill instrument that has reached maturity. No broader market implications expected.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading Members need to update their systems to prevent trading in this specific scrip code. Standard end-of-life procedure for debt instruments.

**Investor Impact**: Holders of this T-bill will receive redemption proceeds as per standard maturity procedures. No action required from investors beyond normal settlement processes.
