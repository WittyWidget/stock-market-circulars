## Summary

JSW Energy Limited has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 200 crore (4000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE's debt segment. The commercial paper was issued on private placement basis with 90-day tenor and ICRA A1+/IND A1+ credit rating.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each, total 4000 units
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,411.50 per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: ICRA A1+ and IND A1+
- Scrip code: 729992, ISIN: INE121E14375
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Axis Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 8, 2025
- Redemption date: November 6, 2025
- Trading effective: August 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides JSW Energy with short-term funding option and offers investors a rated debt instrument with 90-day maturity.
