## Summary

BSE has listed 49,35,000 equity shares of SELLWIN TRADERS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 538875) issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are permitted to trade from October 15, 2025, and are subject to lock-in until April 15, 2026.

## Key Points

- 49,35,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 3.50/-
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 229922501 to 234857500
- ISIN: INE195F01027
- Issue price: Rs. 5.50/- per share
- Date of allotment: September 13, 2025
- All shares subject to lock-in period

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note:
- New securities are available for trading from October 15, 2025
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 49,35,000 shares until April 15, 2026
- Shares must be treated as ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 13, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 15, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: April 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a company-specific listing event with minimal market-wide impact. The preferential issue increases the equity base of SELLWIN TRADERS LIMITED by 49.35 lakh shares. The lock-in period until April 15, 2026 ensures these shares cannot be traded freely for six months, limiting immediate liquidity impact. Trading members dealing in this scrip should update their systems to reflect the increased share capital and distribution number ranges.
