## Summary

KEC International Ltd has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 75 crores (1,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE debt segment. The commercial paper was issued on private placement basis with a credit rating of CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+, maturing on October 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each, total 1,500 units
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,159/- per unit (discount to face value)
- Scrip Code: 729940, ISIN: INE389H14LD8
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited
- Trading in dematerialised form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialised form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Contact debt department for clarifications on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 31, 2025
- Trading Commencement: July 31, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding option for KEC International Ltd and investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
