## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for Currency Derivatives segment on Saturday, September 06, 2025. This session is designed for testing and familiarization purposes, allowing trading members to test their systems and applications. A new BOLT Pro TWS version 12.03 will be released.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Currency Derivatives segment on September 6, 2025
- New BOLT Pro TWS version 12.03 will be released
- Members can choose between Mock Trading Session or UAT environment for regulatory compliance
- Third party trading platforms and in-house systems can test through IML/ETI APIs
- Trades from mock session will not create any obligations or liabilities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Session aligns with existing SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020 regarding testing requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to actively participate in mock trading sessions
- Members using third party platforms or in-house systems should test their applications
- Feedback must be provided by 4:30 PM on the trading day
- Members must refer to Exchange Notice No 20201201-22 for testing guidelines

## Important Dates

- **September 6, 2025**: Mock trading session date
- **10:15 AM - 11:00 AM**: Log-in period
- **11:00 AM - 3:30 PM**: Continuous trading for Currency and Cross Currency Derivatives
- **3:40 PM**: Trade modification deadline
- **4:30 PM**: Feedback submission deadline

## Impact Assessment

Low operational impact as this is a voluntary mock trading session. No real trading obligations, margin requirements, or pay-in/pay-out obligations. Provides opportunity for system testing and familiarization with new BOLT Pro version.
