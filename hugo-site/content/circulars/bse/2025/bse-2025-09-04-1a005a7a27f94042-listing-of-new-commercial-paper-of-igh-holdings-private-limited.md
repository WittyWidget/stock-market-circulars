## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 350 crores issued by IGH Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis, effective September 4, 2025. The securities will trade in the BSE Debt segment with a 3-month maturity period.

## Key Points

- 7,000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5 lakh face value each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,785 per security (discount to face value)
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Trading only in dematerialised form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Security Details

- **Scrip Code:** 730150
- **Scrip ID:** IGHHP4925
- **ISIN:** INE02FN14622
- **Face Value:** Rs. 5,00,000 each
- **Total Issue Size:** Rs. 350 crores
- **Issuing and Paying Agent:** Axis Bank Ltd

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** September 4, 2025
- **Listing Date:** September 4, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** December 4, 2025
- **Tenure:** 3 months

## Trading Parameters

- Trading segment: BSE Debt
- Market lot: 1
- Minimum trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs
- Form: Dematerialised only

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding avenue for IGH Holdings Private Limited and investment option for institutional investors seeking high-rated commercial paper.
