## Summary

BSE announces the addition of six newly listed companies to BSE indices. PACE DIGITEK LIMITED will be added to the BSE IPO index effective October 8, 2025. Five SME platform listings - BHAVIK ENTERPRISES LIMITED, AMEENJI RUBBER LIMITED, KVS CASTINGS LIMITED, M P K STEELS (I) LIMITED, and RUKMANI DEVI GARG AGRO IMPEX LIMITED - will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective October 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- PACE DIGITEK LIMITED (544550) listed on BSE on October 6, 2025
- Five companies listed on BSE SME platform on October 6, 2025
- All additions are routine inclusions following successful IPO listings
- Changes reflect in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Index queries should be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index maintenance activity.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance action required. Market participants should note the index composition changes for portfolio tracking and rebalancing purposes.

## Important Dates

- **October 6, 2025**: Listing date for all six companies on BSE
- **October 7, 2025**: Effective date for BSE SME IPO index additions (5 stocks)
- **October 8, 2025**: Effective date for BSE IPO index addition (PACE DIGITEK LIMITED)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. These are routine index additions following IPO listings. 

**Index Impact**: Minor adjustments to BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO indices. Index funds and passive investment products tracking these indices will need to acquire these securities.

**Trading Impact**: May see increased trading activity in these stocks around effective dates as index funds rebalance portfolios.
