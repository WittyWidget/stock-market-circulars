## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited on private placement basis. The securities are worth Rs. 37.5 crores (7,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) with a maturity period of 7 days.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 7,500 units (Total value: Rs. 37.5 crores)
- Scrip Code: 729934, Scrip ID: KMIL30725
- ISIN: INE975F14B67
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,99,411/- per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Issuing and Paying agent: Kotak Mahindra Bank Ltd

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: July 30, 2025
- Allotment Date: July 30, 2025
- Redemption Date: August 6, 2025
- Maturity Period: 7 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding for Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited with a very brief 7-day maturity period.
