## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 200 bonus equity shares of Tata Elxsi Ltd (Scrip Code: 500408) that were kept in abeyance. These shares will be available for trading starting Thursday, September 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- 200 bonus equity shares of Rs. 10/- each kept in abeyance
- ISIN: INE670A01012
- Distinctive numbers: 31159421 to 31159620
- Trading commences September 11, 2025
- Notice issued by Deputy Vice President Ashok Kumar Singh

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed about the availability of these securities for trading.

## Important Dates

- **September 11, 2025**: Effective date for trading of the bonus shares

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected due to the small quantity of shares (200) being released from abeyance. This is a routine administrative process for bonus shares that were previously held back.
