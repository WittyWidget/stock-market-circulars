## Summary

BSE has notified all trading members that SEBI has approved the surrender of trading membership of Jeevan Utsav Capital Private Limited (Member no. 6770). This is an informational notice with no action required from other trading members.

## Key Points

- Trading member Jeevan Utsav Capital Private Limited (Member no. 6770) has resigned
- The surrender of trading membership has been approved by SEBI
- Notice issued by BSE Membership Department on October 15, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251015-2

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification of a member's resignation.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements. Trading members are requested to take note of the resignation for information purposes only.

## Important Dates

- **October 15, 2025**: Notice date and effective date of resignation notification

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative matter involving the resignation of a single trading member. No operational changes or compliance actions are required from other market participants. The resignation has been processed through proper regulatory channels with SEBI approval.
