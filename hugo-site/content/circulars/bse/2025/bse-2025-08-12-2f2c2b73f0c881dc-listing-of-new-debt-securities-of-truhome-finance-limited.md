## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Truhome Finance Limited worth ₹20 crore issued on private placement basis. The securities carry a 7.90% annual interest rate and mature on August 8, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 20,000 securities with face value of ₹1,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 977014 (ISIN: INE432R07489)
- Credit Rating: IND AA/Stable
- Interest Rate: 7.90% per annum, paid annually
- Allotment Date: August 8, 2025
- Trading effective from August 12, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard debt security listing procedure followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities to be traded only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading members must refer to Placement Memorandum for detailed terms
- Contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 8, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 12, 2025
- Interest Payment Dates: Annually from August 8, 2026 to August 8, 2028
- Maturity Date: August 8, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing. The securities provide institutional investors with a fixed-income investment option rated AA/Stable. No put/call options available.
