## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that ASHIKA STOCK BROKING LTD. (Member No. 912) will change its name to "ASHIKA STOCK SERVICES LIMITED" effective Monday, September 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading member name change from ASHIKA STOCK BROKING LTD to ASHIKA STOCK SERVICES LIMITED
- Member number remains unchanged (912)
- Administrative notification to all trading members
- Contact provided for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes - purely administrative name change of existing trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from other trading members. This is an informational notice.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Monday, September 8, 2025
- **Notice Date**: September 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is an administrative name change. Trading operations and member services will continue under the new name without disruption to market activities.
