## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 40,000 new equity shares of Exhicon Events Media Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 543895) effective from Monday, August 25, 2025. These shares were issued through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis to non-promoters.

## Key Points

- 40,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 285/-
- Shares issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive numbers: 21538001 to 21578000
- Date of allotment: May 12, 2025
- Issue price: Rs. 295.00 per share
- ISIN: INE0O8901016

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and permission to trade on the exchange.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: May 12, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 25, 2025
- **Lock-in Period End**: March 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this involves a small number of shares (40,000) from warrant conversion. The shares are subject to lock-in until March 1, 2026, limiting immediate trading volume impact.
