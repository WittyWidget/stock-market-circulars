## Summary

BSE has listed 24,681 new equity shares of Concord Control Systems Limited (Scrip Code: 543619) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares are available for trading from October 16, 2025, and are subject to a lock-in period until April 29, 2026.

## Key Points

- 24,681 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value each issued
- Issue price: Rs. 1418.08 per share (premium of Rs. 1408.08)
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Date of allotment: August 11, 2025
- Trading commencement: October 16, 2025
- Distribution numbers: 6300473 to 6325153
- ISIN: INE0N0J01014
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of new securities
- Lock-in requirements must be observed: all 24,681 shares are locked-in until April 29, 2026
- Shares cannot be transferred or sold until the lock-in period expires

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 11, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 16, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: April 29, 2026
- **Circular Date**: October 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine preferential allotment listing with minimal market impact. The small number of shares (24,681) represents a relatively minor addition to the company's equity capital. The lock-in period until April 29, 2026 ensures these shares will not immediately impact liquidity or trading volumes. The high issue price of Rs. 1418.08 suggests positive investor confidence in the company.
