## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Ghushine Fintrrade Ocean Ltd (Scrip Code: 539864) will be transferred to the 'M' group effective October 27, 2025. This group change will affect the trading parameters and surveillance measures applicable to the stock.

## Key Points

- Company: Ghushine Fintrrade Ocean Ltd
- Scrip Code: 539864
- New Group: M Group
- Effective Date: October 27, 2025
- Contact Person: Mrs. Kinjal More (Cell: +91-7304278788)

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be subject to 'M' group trading rules and surveillance measures as per BSE's group classification framework. The M group typically has specific trading parameters and surveillance mechanisms different from other groups.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new group classification
- Members should familiarize themselves with M group trading parameters
- Any clarifications required should be directed to Mrs. Kinjal More at the provided contact number

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 20, 2025
- Effective Date: October 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact**: The reclassification to M group may result in different trading parameters, price bands, and surveillance measures. Trading members and investors should be aware of the modified trading conditions that will apply from October 27, 2025.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to ensure their systems are updated to reflect the group change before the effective date to avoid any disruptions in trading operations.
