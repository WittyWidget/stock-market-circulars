## Summary

BSE announces the listing and admission for trading of further securities issued by 10 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS), effective from Wednesday, August 20, 2025.

## Key Points

- 10 companies are listing additional shares from ESOP/ESOS exercises
- Total of 1,310,116 new shares across all companies
- All securities will be admitted for trading from August 20, 2025
- No lock-in period applies to any of the listed securities
- Face values range from ₹2 to ₹10 per share

## Companies and Share Details

**Major Listings:**
- **NIPPON LIFE INDIA ASSET MANAGEMENT**: 546,930 shares (₹10 face value)
- **Axis Bank**: 121,145 shares (₹2 face value)
- **Infosys**: 97,651 shares (₹5 face value)
- **Chalet Hotels**: 90,000 shares (₹10 face value)
- **C.E. Info Systems**: 301,590 shares (₹2 face value)

**Other Companies:**
- CG Power: 52,000 shares
- Varun Beverages: 44,375 shares
- Nuvama Wealth Management: 26,252 shares
- D.B.Corp: 16,000 shares
- Gulf Oil Lubricants: 15,173 shares

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 19, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 20, 2025
- **Lock-in Period**: Not applicable for any securities

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine ESOP exercises. The additional shares represent a small percentage of existing float for most companies. No dilution concerns as these are previously authorized shares under existing ESOP schemes.
