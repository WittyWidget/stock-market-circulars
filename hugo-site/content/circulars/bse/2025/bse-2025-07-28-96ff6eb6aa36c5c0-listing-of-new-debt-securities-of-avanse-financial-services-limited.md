## Summary

BSE has listed two new debt securities issued by Avanse Financial Services Limited on private placement basis, effective from July 28, 2025. Both securities carry CRISIL AA-/Stable credit rating and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities listed: 9% p.a. (3-year) and 8.9% p.a. (2-year)
- Face value: Rs. 1,00,000 each
- Quantity: 7,500 units each
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL AA-/Stable
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities to be traded under specified ISIN numbers
- Reference to Placement Memorandum available on BSE website

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: July 28, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: July 24, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: 
  - 9% security: Annually from July 24, 2026 to July 24, 2028
  - 8.9% security: Annually from September 10, 2025 to September 10, 2027
- **Redemption Dates**:
  - 9% security: July 24, 2028
  - 8.9% security: September 10, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine corporate debt listing. The securities provide institutional investors with additional investment options in the debt segment with AA- credit rating.
