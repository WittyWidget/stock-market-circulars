## Summary

Times Green Energy (India) Limited (BSE Code: 543310) has announced a rights issue offering 27 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each at a premium of Rs. 70/- per share for every 40 equity shares held. The record date is October 31, 2025, and shares will trade on ex-rights basis from the same date.

## Key Points

- Issue ratio: 27 equity shares for every 40 shares held
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Premium: Rs. 70/- per share
- Total issue price: Rs. 80/- per share
- Full amount payable on application
- Ex-rights trading begins October 31, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-747/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must execute transactions in Times Green Energy shares on ex-rights basis from October 31, 2025
- Shareholders must pay full issue price of Rs. 80/- per equity share at time of application
- Shares traded in dematerialized form under rolling settlement segment

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: October 31, 2025
- **Ex-Rights Date**: October 31, 2025
- **Settlement**: DR-747/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

This rights issue provides existing shareholders of Times Green Energy (India) Limited an opportunity to subscribe to additional shares at Rs. 80/- per share. The ex-rights trading adjustment will affect share price discovery from October 31, 2025. Trading members need to ensure proper handling of ex-rights basis transactions to maintain market integrity. The rights entitlement ratio (27:40) indicates a significant capital raise relative to existing equity base.
