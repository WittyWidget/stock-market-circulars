## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 245 bonus equity shares of ICICI Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 532174) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares of Rs. 2/- face value each are now listed and permitted to trade with effect from Thursday, October 16, 2025. The shares are allocated across four distinctive number ranges under ISIN INE090A01021.

## Key Points

- Total of 245 bonus equity shares being listed
- Face value: Rs. 2/- per share
- Company: ICICI Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 532174)
- ISIN: INE090A01021
- Trading commencement: October 16, 2025
- Four separate batches: 30 shares, 15 shares, 100 shares, and 100 shares
- Distinctive number ranges provided for each batch

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for shares previously held in abeyance.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to recognize these shares as listed and tradeable from October 16, 2025. No specific compliance actions required beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 15, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: October 16, 2025 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

The market impact is negligible given the small quantity of 245 shares relative to ICICI Bank's total outstanding equity. This is an administrative listing action that resolves previously held abeyance shares, likely related to resolution of shareholder documentation or entitlement issues. No material effect on stock price or trading volumes is expected.
