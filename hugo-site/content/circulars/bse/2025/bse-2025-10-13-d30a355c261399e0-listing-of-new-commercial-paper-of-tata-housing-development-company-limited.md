## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Tata Housing Development Company Limited on private placement basis. A total of 3600 Commercial Papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000/- each are listed and admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective October 13, 2025. The securities carry credit ratings of CARE A1+ and IND A1+.

## Key Points

- Total quantity of Commercial Papers listed: 3600 units
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 5,00,000/- per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,389/- per unit
- Scrip Code: 730380
- Scrip ID: THDC101025
- ISIN Number: INE582L14IY2
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE582L14IY2
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department on specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- Listing effective date: October 13, 2025
- Actual/deemed date of allotment: October 10, 2025
- Date of redemption: October 9, 2026
- Circular notice date: October 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides Tata Housing Development Company Limited with short-term funding through commercial paper. The securities are rated investment grade (A1+) and will be traded in the BSE Debt segment. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations. The listing is relevant primarily to debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in commercial paper instruments.
