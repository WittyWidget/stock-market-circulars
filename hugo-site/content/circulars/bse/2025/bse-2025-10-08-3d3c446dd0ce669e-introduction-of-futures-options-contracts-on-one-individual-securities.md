## Summary

BSE has announced the introduction of futures and options contracts for Hitachi Energy India Ltd (Scrip Code: 543187) in the Equity Derivatives segment. These derivative contracts will be available for trading starting October 31, 2025. Contract specifications will be published in the derivatives contract master file at the end of day on October 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Futures and options contracts introduced for Hitachi Energy India Ltd
- Scrip Code in Equity Segment: 543187
- Trading to commence on October 31, 2025
- Contract details to be available in derivatives contract master file on October 30, 2025
- Master file accessible through BSE extranet and BseIndia website

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an expansion of existing derivative trading facilities to include an additional underlying security.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the availability of new contracts
- Members should access and review the derivatives contract master file before trading commences
- Contract master file to be downloaded from BSE extranet or BseIndia website after October 30, 2025

## Important Dates

- **October 8, 2025**: Notice issued
- **October 30, 2025**: Contract details published in derivatives master file (end of day)
- **October 31, 2025**: Trading commences for F&O contracts on Hitachi Energy India Ltd

## Impact Assessment

The introduction of derivative contracts on Hitachi Energy India Ltd provides enhanced trading and hedging opportunities for market participants interested in this stock. This expands the universe of stocks available for derivative trading on BSE. Impact is limited to traders and investors specifically interested in Hitachi Energy India Ltd. The addition does not affect existing contracts or market structure.
