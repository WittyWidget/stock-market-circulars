## Summary

BSE Limited has announced that equity shares of Canara Robeco Asset Management Company Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544580 and trading symbol CRAMC with ISIN number INE218I01013. The exact date of listing and further details about the securities will be communicated through a separate notice.
