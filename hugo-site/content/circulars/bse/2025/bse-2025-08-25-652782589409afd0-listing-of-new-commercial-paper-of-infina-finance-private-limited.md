## Summary

BSE has listed two new commercial paper issues by Infina Finance Private Limited on its debt segment. The instruments, issued on private placement basis, have face values of Rs. 5 lakh each with CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- First issue: 4000 units with redemption date June 9, 2026 (ISIN: INE879F14LH3)
- Second issue: 1000 units with redemption date March 13, 2026 (ISIN: INE879F14LJ9)
- Both issues allotted on August 22, 2025
- Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited serves as issuing and paying agent
- Trading in dematerialized form only with Rs. 5 lakh minimum denomination
- Tick size of 1 paise applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination requirement of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Use specified ISIN numbers for trading identification

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 22, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 25, 2025
- First Issue Redemption: June 9, 2026
- Second Issue Redemption: March 13, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk for investors. Trading restricted to institutional participants due to high minimum denomination requirements.
