## Summary

BSE has listed 2,69,20,537 new equity shares of HIMALAYA FOOD INTERNATIONAL LIMITED (Scrip Code: 526899) issued on rights basis. The shares will commence trading from Tuesday, August 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- 2,69,20,537 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 3.80
- Rights issue basis with allotment date of July 18, 2025
- ISIN: INE552B01010
- Distinctive Numbers: 57872885 to 84793421
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 18, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine rights issue listing for an existing company. The new shares increase the company's equity base and provide additional liquidity.
