## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debentures of EAAA India Alternatives Limited effective August 14, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date of August 17, 2025 for interest payment and redemption purposes.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to EAAAL-GSEC-21-8-25-PVT debentures (ISIN: INE528L07040, Code: 974377)
- No dealings permitted from August 14, 2025 onwards
- Record date set for August 17, 2025
- Purpose includes both redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice reference: DR-695/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. Standard trading suspension procedure for corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from August 14, 2025
- All exchange participants must comply with the trading suspension directive
- Members required to take note of the suspension and communicate to clients

## Important Dates

- **August 13, 2025**: Notice date
- **August 14, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **August 17, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and redemption

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as suspension affects only one company's specific debenture series. Routine corporate action procedure that temporarily restricts trading to ensure proper settlement of interest payments and redemption processes.
