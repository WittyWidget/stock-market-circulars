## Summary

KEC International Ltd. has issued new commercial paper worth Rs. 50 crores (1000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) on private placement basis, which is now listed and admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective September 3, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 1000 units (Rs. 50 crores total)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,449 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- ISIN: INE389H14LF3
- Scrip code: 730133

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying agent: Axis Bank Limited

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: September 2, 2025
- Listing date: September 3, 2025
- Redemption date: November 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by KEC International. The short-term nature (87 days maturity) and private placement basis limit broader market implications. Standard debt instrument with established credit ratings.
