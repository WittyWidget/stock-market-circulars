## Summary

Icodex Publishing Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 544483) equity shares will be transferred from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment effective September 3, 2025. The company's dealings will be shifted under M Group.

## Key Points

- Company: ICODEX Publishing Solutions Limited
- Scrip Code: 544483
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Wednesday, September 3, 2025
- This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20250818-46 dated August 18, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

Transfer from restrictive Trade for Trade segment to more liquid Rolling segment, allowing normal trading mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the segment change and adjust their trading systems accordingly. Contact Mr. Anurag Jain (022-2272 8822) for further details.

## Important Dates

- **September 3, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer

## Impact Assessment

Positive impact on stock liquidity as move from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment typically allows for better price discovery and increased trading activity.
