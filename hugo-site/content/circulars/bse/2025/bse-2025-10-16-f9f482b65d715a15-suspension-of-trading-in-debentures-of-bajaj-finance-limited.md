## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding the suspension of trading in specific debentures of BAJAJ FINANCE LIMITED. The company has fixed a record date of October 23, 2025 for the purpose of redemption and interest payment on its 8.94% debentures maturing on November 7, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from October 23, 2025 onwards.

## Key Points

- Company: BAJAJ FINANCE LIMITED
- Debenture ISIN: INE296A08763 (Code: 952990)
- Debenture Series: BFL-8.94%-7-11-25-PVT
- Record Date: October 23, 2025
- No Dealings From: October 23, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Reference: DR-741/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from October 23, 2025
- All market participants must take note of the trading suspension
- Debenture holders on record as of October 23, 2025 will be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds

## Important Dates

- **October 16, 2025**: Notice issuance date
- **October 23, 2025**: Record date and start of trading suspension
- **November 7, 2025**: Debenture maturity date (as per series nomenclature)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited impact as this affects only a specific series of private placement debentures. The suspension is temporary and tied to the redemption process.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive their final interest payment and principal redemption. Trading is suspended to ensure proper settlement and prevent trades that would not settle before redemption.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in this specific ISIN from October 23, 2025. Standard corporate action processing procedure with no unusual elements.
