## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of Rachit Prints Limited (Exchange ticker: 544503) to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at market open on Tuesday, September 09, 2025. The stock was listed on BSE on Monday, September 08, 2025.

## Key Points

- Rachit Prints Limited (ticker: 544503) added to BSE SME IPO Index
- Stock listed on BSE on September 08, 2025
- Index inclusion effective from market open on September 09, 2025
- Corporate action included in end-of-day file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine index maintenance action.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index composition change will be reflected in overnight corporate action files.

## Important Dates

- **September 08, 2025**: Stock listing date on BSE
- **September 09, 2025**: Effective date for index addition (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine index maintenance action following the listing of a new SME company. Index funds and ETFs tracking the BSE SME IPO Index may need to adjust their portfolios to reflect the new constituent.
