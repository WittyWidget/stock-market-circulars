## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of 360 ONE Prime Limited worth Rs. 175 crores (3,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued on private placement basis. The securities will trade on BSE Debt segment with scrip code 729936 and mature on October 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each (3,500 units)
- Total issue size: Rs. 175 crores
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,604.50 per unit
- Scrip code: 729936, ISIN: INE248U14RX4
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Allotment date: July 29, 2025
- Maturity date: October 28, 2025
- HDFC Bank Limited as issuing and paying agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applies
- Market lot size: 1 unit

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 29, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: July 30, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: October 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The securities provide short-term funding option for 360 ONE Prime Limited and investment opportunity for debt market participants. Standard trading parameters and high credit ratings indicate regular market operations.
