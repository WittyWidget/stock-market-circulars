## Summary

BSE has revised price bands for 16 securities effective August 29, 2025. The changes include increases to 20% price bands for several major stocks including Adani Total Gas, Aditya Birla Fashion and Retail, CESC, Granules India, IRB Infrastructure, Jindal Stainless, Poonawalla Fincorp, and SJVN. Several smaller companies have been assigned lower price bands of 2% to 5%.

## Key Points

- 16 securities will have revised price bands from August 29, 2025
- 8 major companies moved to 20% price band
- 7 companies assigned restrictive 5% price band
- 1 company (Krishival Foods) assigned very restrictive 2% price band
- Changes are part of BSE surveillance measures

## Regulatory Changes

Price band percentages have been revised for the following categories:
- **20% Price Band**: Adani Total Gas, Aditya Birla Fashion and Retail, CESC, Granules India, IRB Infrastructure Developers, Jindal Stainless, Poonawalla Fincorp, SJVN
- **5% Price Band**: Ambo Agritec, Filtron Engineers, IZMO, KBS India, Oceanic Foods, Omni AXs Software, Subam Papers
- **2% Price Band**: Krishival Foods

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must implement revised price bands from August 29, 2025
- All trading systems must be updated to reflect new price band limits
- Members can contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 28, 2025
- **Effective Date**: August 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The price band revisions will affect daily trading limits for these securities. Companies with increased price bands (20%) will have greater intraday price movement flexibility, while those with restrictive bands (2-5%) will face tighter trading ranges, typically indicating heightened surveillance due to volatility or other market concerns.
