## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of SUGS LLOYD LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544501) will be transferred from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment with effect from Friday, September 19, 2025. The company's shares will be shifted to M Group for trading purposes.

## Key Points

- Transfer from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment
- Effective date: Friday, September 19, 2025
- Scrip Code: 544501
- Company classified under SME segment
- Shares will be shifted to M Group

## Regulatory Changes

- Trading mechanism changes from Trade for Trade to Rolling settlement
- Group classification changes from MT Group to M Group

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the segment change for SUGS LLOYD LIMITED
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022 2272 8822) for further details

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: September 5, 2025
- Effective Date: September 19, 2025
- Reference Notice: 20250904-61 dated September 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

- Improved liquidity potential as shares move from restrictive Trade for Trade to Rolling segment
- Enhanced trading flexibility for market participants
- Limited market impact as it affects only one SME stock
