## Summary

Vanta Bioscience Limited (Scrip Code: 540729) has listed 8,00,000 new equity shares on BSE effective October 17, 2025. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis at Rs. 50/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 40/-) and are subject to lock-in until April 29, 2027.

## Key Points

- 8,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 40/-
- Issue price: Rs. 50/- per share
- Allotment to promoters on preferential basis
- Date of allotment: May 6, 2025
- Trading commences: October 17, 2025
- Shares ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE695X01011
- Distinctive Numbers: 6312001 to 7112000

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed about the listing of new securities and lock-in restrictions. The entire 8,00,000 shares are subject to lock-in provisions and cannot be transferred until April 29, 2027.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: May 6, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 17, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: April 29, 2027
- **Notice Date**: October 16, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of preferential shares issued to promoters. The impact on market operations is minimal as the shares are locked-in for approximately 18 months. The preferential issue strengthens the promoter holding and provides capital to the company. No immediate trading impact expected due to lock-in restrictions.
