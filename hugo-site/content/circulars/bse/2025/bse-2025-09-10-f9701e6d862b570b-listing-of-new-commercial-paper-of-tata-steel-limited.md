## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of TATA STEEL LIMITED worth Rs. 245 crores (49,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 49,000 units (total value Rs. 245 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730171, Scrip ID: TSL90925
- ISIN: INE081A14GD6
- Credit Rating: ICRA A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 492,734.50 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Limited serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 9, 2025
- Listing Date: September 10, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 8, 2025
- Tenure: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term funding instrument for institutional investors in Tata Steel's debt securities.
