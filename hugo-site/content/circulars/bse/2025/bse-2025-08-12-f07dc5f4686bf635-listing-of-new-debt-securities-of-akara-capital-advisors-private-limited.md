## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited issued on private placement basis, effective from August 12, 2025. The securities carry a 12.5% interest rate and mature on December 27, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2,850 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 976868, Scrip ID: 125ACAP28
- ISIN: INE08XP07324 (further listings under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: ICRA BBB (Stable)
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 95,410.96

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Trading members should refer to Placement Memorandum for details

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 8, 2025
- Trading Effective: August 12, 2025
- Interest Payment: Monthly
- Redemption Date: December 27, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement debt listing. The securities add to BSE's debt segment offerings but represent a small issuance with limited broader market implications.
