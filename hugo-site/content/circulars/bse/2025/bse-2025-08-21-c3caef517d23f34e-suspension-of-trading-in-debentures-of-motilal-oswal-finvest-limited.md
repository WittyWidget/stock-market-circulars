## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in MOTILAL OSWAL FINVEST LIMITED debentures (INE01WN07094) effective August 28, 2025, due to the company fixing record date for interest payment and redemption.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to MOFL-9.5%-12-9-25-PVT debentures (Code: 975072)
- Record date set for August 28, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings allowed from August 28, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes - standard procedure for debenture record date suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from August 28, 2025
- Trading members required to take note of the suspension notice

## Important Dates

- **August 21, 2025**: Notice date
- **August 28, 2025**: Record date and start of trading suspension

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as suspension affects only one specific debenture series. Standard corporate action process with temporary trading halt to facilitate record date determination for interest payment and redemption.
