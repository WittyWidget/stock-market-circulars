## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis. The instrument comprises 15,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 75 crores, with CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Total quantity: 15,000 units (Rs. 75 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730134, Scrip ID: ABHF20925
- ISIN: INE831R14FF5
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,727.50 per unit
- Allotment date: September 2, 2025
- Maturity date: December 2, 2025
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE831R14FF5
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise for trading
- Trading members can contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 2, 2025
- Trading Effective Date: September 3, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 2, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding option for Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited with high credit ratings ensuring low risk profile.
