## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis. The listing consists of 1300 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 65 crores. The securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective October 17, 2025.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 1300 units of Commercial Paper
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,95,150 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730187
- Scrip ID: BFSL10925
- ISIN: INE01C314CQ7 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE01C314CQ7
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be handled according to BSE Debt segment trading requirements

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Notice Date**: October 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term funding to Bajaj Financial Securities Limited for approximately 55 days. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+, IND A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Trading members dealing in debt securities should update their systems with the new scrip details. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
