## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in three Treasury Bills effective August 5, 2025, as they reach their redemption/maturity date. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills will mature on August 5, 2025
- Trading suspension effective from August 5, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-688/2025-2026
- All trading members must comply with suspension

## Securities Affected

- **182TB70825** (SCRIP CODE: 805023, ISIN: IN002024Y431)
- **364TB70825** (SCRIP CODE: 804952, ISIN: IN002024Z198) 
- **91TB070825** (SCRIP CODE: 805061, ISIN: IN002025X067)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified T-Bills from August 5, 2025
- All market participants must take note of the suspension
- No exceptions to the trading suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 4, 2025
- **Effective Date**: August 5, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: August 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine operational impact on debt market participants. The suspension is standard procedure for maturing T-Bills and ensures orderly settlement. Market participants holding these securities should prepare for redemption procedures.
