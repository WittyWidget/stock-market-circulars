## Summary

BSE has listed two new series of Commercial Paper issued by Adani Enterprises Ltd on private placement basis, effective October 16, 2025. The instruments are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each and carry credit ratings of A1+ from multiple agencies.

## Key Points

- Two series of Commercial Paper listed for Adani Enterprises Ltd
- Series 1 (AEL151025): 200 units with redemption on January 13, 2026, ISIN INE423A14YK8
- Series 2 (AEL28325): 500 units with redemption on March 25, 2026, ISIN INE423A14WX5 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Both series allotted on October 15, 2025
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+, ACUITE A1+
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Market lot size is 1 unit

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: October 15, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Redemption Date (Series 1)**: January 13, 2026
- **Redemption Date (Series 2)**: March 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of short-term debt instruments with minimal market impact. The listing provides additional liquidity options for institutional investors in the debt market. Series 2 represents a further listing under an existing ISIN, indicating ongoing fundraising activity by Adani Enterprises through commercial paper. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
