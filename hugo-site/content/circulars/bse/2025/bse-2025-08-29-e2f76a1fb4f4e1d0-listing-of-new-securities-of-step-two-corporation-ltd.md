## Summary

Step Two Corporation Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531509) has listed 31,47,500 new equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 5/- on a preferential basis to promoters and non-promoters. Trading will commence on September 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- 31,47,500 new equity shares of Rs. 10/- each with Rs. 5/- premium
- Issue price: Rs. 15/- per share
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution Numbers: 5052101 to 8199600
- Allotment Date: April 9, 2025
- ISIN: INE623D01015

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members can commence trading these securities from September 1, 2025
- Lock-in provisions apply to different tranches of shares

## Important Dates

- **Trading Start Date**: September 1, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: April 9, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: 
  - 13,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 5052101 to 6352100): March 25, 2027
  - 18,47,500 shares (Dist. Nos. 6352101 to 8199600): April 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a company-specific preferential allotment. The lock-in periods indicate structured release of shares into the market, with the majority having a shorter lock-in period until April 2026.
