## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in debt securities of SML effective October 17, 2025. The debentures (SCRIP CODE: 974313, ISIN: INE982X07341) carrying 12.75% interest rate are scheduled to mature on October 20, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture Details: SML 12.75% Private Debentures maturing October 20, 2025
- SCRIP CODE: 974313
- ISIN: INE982X07341
- Trading suspension effective: October 17, 2025
- Maturity Date: October 20, 2025
- Notice Reference: DR-738/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for scheduled debt security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified SML debentures from October 17, 2025 onwards
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and communicate to their clients
- No new trades should be executed in SCRIP CODE 974313 after the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **October 16, 2025**: Notice issued
- **October 17, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **October 20, 2025**: Debenture maturity date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with minimal market impact. The suspension affects only holders and potential traders of this specific private debenture issue. The three-day gap between suspension and maturity is standard practice to ensure orderly settlement. No broader market implications or systemic concerns are indicated.
