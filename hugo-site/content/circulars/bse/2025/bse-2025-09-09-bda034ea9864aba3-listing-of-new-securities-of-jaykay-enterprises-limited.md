## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and trading of 79,17,936 new equity shares of Jaykay Enterprises Limited (Scrip Code: 500306) effective September 10, 2025. These shares were issued on a preferential basis to promoters and non-promoters at Rs. 142 per share.

## Key Points

- 79,17,936 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at premium of Rs. 141/-
- Issue price: Rs. 142 per share
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 122369131 to 130287066
- Date of allotment: July 28, 2025
- ISIN: INE903A01025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are listed and permitted for trading from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 28, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: September 10, 2025
- **Lock-in Period**: 
  - 7,893,829 shares locked until March 15, 2027
  - 24,107 shares locked until March 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of preferentially allotted shares. The lock-in provisions ensure gradual market release of the new securities.
