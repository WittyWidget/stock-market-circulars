## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Tata Power Co. Ltd on private placement basis worth Rs. 500 crores. The commercial paper has a face value of Rs. 5 lakh per unit with 10,000 units issued at a discount price of Rs. 4,92,727.50 per unit.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Quantity: 10,000 units (total value Rs. 500 crores)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,727.50 per unit (at discount)
- Scrip Code: 730000
- ISIN: INE245A14KB3
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 11, 2025
- Listing Date: August 11, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 10, 2025
- Tenure: 3 months (91 days)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding for Tata Power's working capital requirements. High credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk for investors.
