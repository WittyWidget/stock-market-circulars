## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 98,64,427 new equity shares of Deccan Bearings Ltd. (Scrip Code: 505703) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will be available for trading from August 22, 2025, and are subject to a lock-in period until March 31, 2027.

## Key Points

- 98,64,427 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par value
- Shares issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Trading commences on August 22, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 10135574 to 20000000
- ISIN: INE498D01012

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability
- Lock-in period compliance required until March 31, 2027
- Standard listing procedures and trading rules apply

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 3, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 22, 2025
- Lock-in Period Ends: March 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine preferential allotment to non-promoters. The shares are subject to a standard lock-in period of approximately 20 months, preventing immediate trading by allottees. The issue represents capital raising activity by Deccan Bearings Ltd. for business expansion or operational requirements.
