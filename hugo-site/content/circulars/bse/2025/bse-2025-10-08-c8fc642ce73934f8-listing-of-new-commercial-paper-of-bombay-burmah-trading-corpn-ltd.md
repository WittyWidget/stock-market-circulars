## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading new Commercial Paper issued by Bombay Burmah Trading Corpn. Ltd. on private placement basis, effective October 8, 2025. The instrument comprises 500 Commercial Papers of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 25 crore, with maturity date of May 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer:** Bombay Burmah Trading Corpn. Ltd.
- **Instrument Type:** Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Total Issue Size:** 500 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000 = Rs. 25 crore
- **Face Value:** Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 4,77,342.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code:** 730360
- **Scrip ID:** BBTC71025
- **ISIN:** INE050A14879
- **Credit Rating:** IND A1+
- **Market Lot:** 1 unit
- **Tick Size:** 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent:** Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for a new debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE050A14879
- Trading shall occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** October 7, 2025
- **Listing Date:** October 8, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** May 5, 2026
- **Tenure:** Approximately 7 months

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine Commercial Paper listing in the debt segment affecting only participants trading in short-term debt instruments.

**Investor Impact:** Provides debt market participants with an additional IND A1+ rated short-term investment option from Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation with 7-month maturity.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members can now trade this instrument on BSE Debt segment in dematerialized form with standard lot size of Rs. 5 lakhs.
