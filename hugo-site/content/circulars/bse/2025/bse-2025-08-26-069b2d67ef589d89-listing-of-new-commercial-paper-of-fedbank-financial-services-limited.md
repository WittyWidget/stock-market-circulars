## Summary

BSE has listed two new commercial paper issues by Fedbank Financial Services Limited on its debt segment. The issues comprise 2,000 and 1,000 papers of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 15 crores, with tenures of approximately 3 months.

## Key Points

- Two commercial paper series listed: FFSL-21-11-25-CP and FFSL-24-11-25-CP
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per paper
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,785 per paper
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Use specified ISIN numbers for trading

## Important Dates

- First issue allotment date: August 22, 2025
- First issue redemption date: November 21, 2025
- Second issue allotment date: August 25, 2025
- Second issue redemption date: November 24, 2025
- Trading effective from: August 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instruments provide short-term funding options for Fedbank Financial Services Limited and investment opportunities for institutional investors in the debt segment.
