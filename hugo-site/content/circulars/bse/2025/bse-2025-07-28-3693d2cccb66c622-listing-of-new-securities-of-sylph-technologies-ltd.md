## Summary

BSE has listed 489,090,000 new equity shares of Sylph Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 511447) issued through a rights offering. The shares are listed with effect from Tuesday, July 29, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 489,090,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at par on rights basis
- ISIN: INE706F01021
- Distribution Numbers: 358666001 to 847756000
- Date of Allotment: July 18, 2025
- Trading commencement: July 29, 2025
- New shares have equal rights with existing shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and should update their systems accordingly for trading operations.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 18, 2025
- **Trading Start Date**: July 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The rights issue substantially increases Sylph Technologies' share count, which may impact liquidity and market capitalization. Existing shareholders who participated in the rights issue maintain their proportional ownership, while those who didn't will face dilution.
