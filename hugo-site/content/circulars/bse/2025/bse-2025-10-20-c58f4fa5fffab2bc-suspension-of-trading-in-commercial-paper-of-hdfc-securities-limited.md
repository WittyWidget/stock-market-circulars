## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper (Scrip Code: 729893, ISIN: INE700G14PD9) issued by HDFC Securities Limited, effective October 21, 2025. The suspension is due to the commercial paper reaching its maturity/redemption date on October 23, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of HDFC Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 729893) will be suspended from trading
- ISIN: INE700G14PD9
- Trading suspension effective from October 21, 2025
- Reason: Instrument approaching maturity/redemption date
- Trading members advised not to deal in this commercial paper
- Notice reference: DR-740/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for debt instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified commercial paper (Scrip Code: 729893) from October 21, 2025 onwards
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance

## Important Dates

- **October 20, 2025**: Notice issued
- **October 21, 2025**: Trading suspension becomes effective
- **October 23, 2025**: Expected redemption/maturity date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with minimal market impact. The suspension affects only holders and potential traders of this specific commercial paper issue. Since the instrument is maturing, this represents normal lifecycle management of debt securities. No broader implications for HDFC Securities Limited's other securities or overall market operations.
