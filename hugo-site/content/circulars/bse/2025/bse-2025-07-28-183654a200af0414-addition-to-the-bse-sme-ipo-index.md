## Summary

BSE announces the addition of Swastika Castal Limited (Exchange ticker: 544452) to the BSE SME IPO Index, effective at market open on July 29, 2025. The stock is being listed on BSE on July 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Swastika Castal Limited listed on BSE effective July 28, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE SME IPO Index on July 29, 2025
- Exchange ticker: 544452
- Action included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index inclusion following the listing of a new SME company.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index addition is automatically processed.

## Important Dates

- July 28, 2025: Listing date of Swastika Castal Limited on BSE
- July 29, 2025: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine index addition for a newly listed SME company. The inclusion will make the stock part of the BSE SME IPO Index for tracking and investment purposes.
