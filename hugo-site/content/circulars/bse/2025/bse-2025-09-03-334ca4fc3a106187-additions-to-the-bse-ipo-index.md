## Summary

BSE announces the addition of two newly listed companies to the BSE IPO Index effective September 5, 2025. Vikran Engineering Limited (ticker: 544496) and Anlon Healthcare Limited (ticker: 544497) will be included following their listing on September 3, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two companies being added to BSE IPO Index
- Both companies listed on BSE on September 3, 2025
- Index addition becomes effective at market open on September 5, 2025
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine index maintenance action following new listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index funds tracking BSE IPO Index may need to adjust portfolios accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **September 3, 2025**: Listing date for both companies
- **September 5, 2025**: Effective date for index addition (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Routine index maintenance with medium impact on index funds and ETFs tracking the BSE IPO Index. Investors and fund managers should note the composition change for portfolio adjustments. No significant market disruption expected.
