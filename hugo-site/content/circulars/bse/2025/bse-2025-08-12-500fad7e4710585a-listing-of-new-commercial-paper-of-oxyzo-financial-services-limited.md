## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 25 crore issued by OXYZO Financial Services Limited on private placement basis. The securities are effective for trading from August 12, 2025, with redemption date of November 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total quantity: 500 units (Rs. 25 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,88,967.50 per unit
- Credit rating: ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 730011 (OFSL110825)
- ISIN: INE04VS14293
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Federal Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 11, 2025
- Listing effective date: August 12, 2025
- Redemption date: November 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding option for OXYZO Financial Services with 3-month maturity period.
