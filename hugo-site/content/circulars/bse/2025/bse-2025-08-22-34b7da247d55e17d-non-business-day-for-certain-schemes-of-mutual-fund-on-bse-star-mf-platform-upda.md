## Summary

BSE announces that Bandhan US Treasury Bond 0-1 year Fund of Fund will observe a non-business day on August 25, 2025 on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Key Points

- Specific fund affected: Bandhan US Treasury Bond 0-1 year Fund of Fund
- Non-business day date: August 25, 2025
- Platform: BSE StAR MF (Mutual Fund) Platform
- Fund house: Bandhan

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Investors and intermediaries should note that transactions for this specific fund will not be processed on August 25, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **August 25, 2025**: Non-business day for Bandhan US Treasury Bond 0-1 year Fund of Fund

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact limited to investors of the specific Bandhan fund. Other mutual fund schemes and market operations remain unaffected. This is a routine operational adjustment for the fund house.
