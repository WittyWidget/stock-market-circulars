## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of SIHORA INDUSTRIES LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544585, symbol SIHORA, and ISIN number INE0SFA01011. The specific date of listing and further details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: SIHORA INDUSTRIES LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544585
- Symbol: SIHORA
- ISIN Number: INE0SFA01011
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed about the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage. Members should await the separate notice containing the listing date and security details.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard listing announcement for a new SME segment company. The impact is limited to trading members who may be interested in trading this security once it becomes available. The announcement does not provide the listing date or detailed security information, which will follow in a subsequent notice. Market participants should monitor for the follow-up circular containing specific listing details and timeline.
