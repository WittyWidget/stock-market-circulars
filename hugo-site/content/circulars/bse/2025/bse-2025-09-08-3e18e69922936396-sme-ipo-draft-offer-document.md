## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of SMR JEWELS LIMITED for public comments. The document was filed on September 6, 2025, and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Public comments are invited within 21 days from the notice date.

## Key Points

- SMR JEWELS LIMITED has filed its Draft Offer Document for SME IPO
- Document filed on September 6, 2025
- Available on BSE SME website for public review
- Complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments period of 21 days from notice date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This follows standard ICDR procedure for SME IPO draft offer documents.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public invited to review and provide comments on the Draft Offer Document
- Comments must be submitted within 21 days from September 8, 2025
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document hosted on www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- Document Filing Date: September 6, 2025
- Notice Date: September 8, 2025
- Comments Deadline: September 29, 2025 (21 days from notice)
- Document Availability: Minimum 21 days on exchange website

## Impact Assessment

Routine SME IPO procedure with standard market impact. Provides transparency and public participation opportunity in the IPO process. Limited direct market impact as this is preliminary documentation stage before actual public offering.
