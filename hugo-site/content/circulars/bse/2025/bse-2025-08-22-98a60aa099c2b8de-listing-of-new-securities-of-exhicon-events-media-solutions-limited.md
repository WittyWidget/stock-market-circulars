## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 40,000 new equity shares of Exhicon Events Media Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 543895) effective from Monday, August 25, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 285/- per share to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 40,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 295/- per share (Rs. 10/- face value + Rs. 285/- premium)
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 21538001 to 21578000
- ISIN: INE0O8901016

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities available for trading
- Lock-in period applicable until March 1, 2026 for all 40,000 shares
- Standard trading rules and regulations apply

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: May 12, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 25, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: March 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected due to small number of shares (40,000) and lock-in restrictions until March 2026. The warrant conversion represents a routine corporate action with no immediate trading implications given the lock-in period.
