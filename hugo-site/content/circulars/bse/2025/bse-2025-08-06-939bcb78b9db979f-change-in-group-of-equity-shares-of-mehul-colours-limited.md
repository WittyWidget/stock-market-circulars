## Summary

BSE has announced the transfer of MEHUL COLOURS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544472) equity shares from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment with effect from Thursday, August 21, 2025. This change will shift the company's dealings under M Group.

## Key Points

- Company: MEHUL COLOURS LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544472
- Transfer from: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- Transfer to: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Thursday, August 21, 2025
- Contact: Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822)

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will move from a more restrictive trading mechanism (Trade for Trade) to a normal rolling settlement system, allowing for more liquid trading.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should note the segment change and adjust their trading systems accordingly. For further details, they may contact the designated BSE official.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: August 06, 2025
- Effective Date: August 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This change will improve liquidity for MEHUL COLOURS LIMITED shares as they move from the restrictive Trade for Trade segment to normal rolling settlement, potentially increasing trading volumes and reducing settlement risk.
