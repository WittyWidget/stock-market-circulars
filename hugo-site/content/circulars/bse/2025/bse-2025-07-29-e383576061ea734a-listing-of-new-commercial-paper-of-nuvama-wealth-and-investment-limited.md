## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited on private placement basis. The securities are now available for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from July 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- 3,000 commercial papers of Rs. 5 lakh face value each issued
- Issue price: Rs. 4,90,822.50 per paper
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- ISIN: INE523L14AE5
- Scrip Code: 729922
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Ltd serves as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 28, 2025
- Trading Start Date: July 29, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt instrument option for institutional investors in the debt segment.
