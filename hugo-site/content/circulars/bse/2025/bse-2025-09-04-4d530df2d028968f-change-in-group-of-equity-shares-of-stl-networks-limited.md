## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of STL Networks Limited (Scrip Code: 544395) will be permitted for trading under 'B' group effective Thursday, September 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: STL Networks Limited
- Scrip Code: 544395
- New Trading Group: 'B'
- Effective Date: Thursday, September 18, 2025
- Notice issued by Deputy Vice President Ashok Kumar Singh

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will move from their current trading group to 'B' group classification, which typically indicates stocks with different liquidity or trading characteristics.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the group change and ensure compliance with 'B' group trading requirements from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Thursday, September 18, 2025
- **Notice Date**: September 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The group change may affect trading patterns, margin requirements, and settlement procedures for STL Networks Limited shares. Trading members should prepare for any operational adjustments required for 'B' group securities.
