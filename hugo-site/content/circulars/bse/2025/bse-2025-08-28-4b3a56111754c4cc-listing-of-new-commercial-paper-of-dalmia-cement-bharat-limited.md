## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited worth Rs. 75 crores (1,500 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) on private placement basis. The commercial paper carries CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings and will mature on November 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 1,500 units (Rs. 75 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,715.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip code: 730107
- ISIN: INE755K14FN5
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- IDBI Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 26, 2025
- Listing effective date: August 28, 2025
- Redemption date: November 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding for Dalmia Cement operations with a tenure of approximately 3 months. High credit ratings indicate low default risk.
