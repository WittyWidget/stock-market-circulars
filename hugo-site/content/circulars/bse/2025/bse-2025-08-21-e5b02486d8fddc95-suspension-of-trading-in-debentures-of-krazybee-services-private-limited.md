## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debentures of KRAZYBEE SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED effective August 29, 2025, due to the company setting a record date for interest payment and redemption of its non-convertible debentures.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to KSPL-12%-13-9-25-PVT debentures (INE07HK07700)
- Record date set for August 29, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Trading members advised not to deal in these debentures from August 29, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - routine corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date
- Trading members required to take note of the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: August 29, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: August 29, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only debentures of a single private limited company. Standard procedure for corporate actions involving debt securities redemption and interest payments.
