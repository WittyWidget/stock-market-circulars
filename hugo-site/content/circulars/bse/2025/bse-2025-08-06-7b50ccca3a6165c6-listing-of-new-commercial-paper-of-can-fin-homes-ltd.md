## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Can Fin Homes Ltd. on private placement basis. The issue comprises 10,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 500 crore, with a 3-month maturity period.

## Key Points

- Issue size: Rs. 500 crore (10,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,752 per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 729966, Scrip ID: CFHL50825
- ISIN: INE477A14DZ6
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise for trading
- HDFC Bank Limited appointed as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 5, 2025
- Listing date: August 6, 2025
- Redemption date: November 4, 2025
- Maturity period: 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides Can Fin Homes with short-term funding at competitive rates given the high credit ratings. Trading will be limited to institutional investors due to high denomination requirements.
