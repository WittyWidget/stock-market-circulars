## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Axis Finance Limited on private placement basis. The securities worth Rs. 125 crores (2500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) are now available for trading on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each issued by Axis Finance Limited
- Total quantity: 2,500 securities worth Rs. 125 crores
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,268 per security
- Market lot size: 1 security
- Scrip Code: 730071
- ISIN: INE891K14PH9

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - routine debt security listing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Securities available for trading with effect from August 22, 2025

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 21, 2025
- Listing Date: August 22, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 20, 2025
- Maturity Period: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt instrument options for institutional investors. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates low credit risk for investors.
