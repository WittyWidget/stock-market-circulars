## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Poonawalla Fincorp Limited issued on private placement basis. The securities carry a face value of ₹1 lakh each with a total quantity of 35,000 units, representing ₹350 crore in debt issuance.

## Key Points

- Securities issued on private placement basis by Poonawalla Fincorp Limited
- Total quantity: 35,000 securities with face value of ₹1,00,000 each
- Interest rate: 7.52% per annum paid annually
- Credit rating: CRISIL AAA/Stable
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Security Details

- **Scrip Code**: 977057
- **Scrip ID**: 752PFL28
- **ISIN**: INE511C07920
- **Issue Price**: ₹1,00,000
- **Date of Allotment**: August 25, 2025
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 25, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from February 25, 2026 to February 25, 2028
- **Redemption Date**: February 25, 2028
- **Trading Commencement**: August 28, 2025

## Trading Information

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- No put/call options available
- Trading members can contact debt department for clarifications
- Placement memorandum available on BSE website

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The AAA credit rating indicates strong creditworthiness, and the 7.52% interest rate reflects current market conditions for corporate bonds.
