## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for an Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window/Takeover) for NACL INDUSTRIES LTD shares. The offer will run from Friday, August 22, 2025 to Monday, September 8, 2025, with an offer price of Rs. 76.70 per share for up to 5,24,62,320 equity shares.

## Key Points

- Security: NACL INDUSTRIES LTD (Symbol: NACLIND, ISIN: INE295D01020)
- Offer size: Up to 5,24,62,320 equity shares
- Offer price: Rs. 76.70 per share
- Face value: Rs. 1.00
- Market lot: 1 share
- Multiple investor categories eligible: FII, FI, MF, IC, Other QIBs, Companies, Individuals

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a scheduled corporate action following existing takeover regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure UCC of investors is registered with correct PAN Number and Category
- Only investors with shares in demat form can participate
- Members must use designated BSE platforms: iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet
- Proper documentation and bid acceptance procedures must be followed

## Important Dates

- **Offer Period**: August 22, 2025 to September 8, 2025
- **Bid Offering Period**: August 22 - September 8, 2025 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: August 22 - September 5, 2025 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Give-up**: September 8, 2025 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up**: September 8, 2025 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

This takeover offer will provide an exit opportunity for NACL Industries shareholders at Rs. 76.70 per share. The 18-day offer period allows sufficient time for participation. Impact is localized to NACL Industries stakeholders and does not affect broader market operations.
