## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of INDEL MONEY LIMITED (ISIN: INE0BUS07BH8, Code: 939891) effective October 24, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption purposes. Trading members are explicitly directed not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Debenture ISIN: INE0BUS07BH8
- Company Code: 939891 (IML-9-11-25-NCD)
- Record date for redemption: October 24, 2025
- No dealings permitted from: October 24, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-742/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational notice for debenture redemption procedures requiring trading suspension to establish record date entitlements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must immediately cease all trading activities in the specified debentures from October 24, 2025
- No buy or sell orders should be executed for debenture code 939891 (INE0BUS07BH8)
- Members must ensure their systems and processes reflect the trading suspension
- Compliance is mandatory for all exchange members

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 20, 2025
- **Record Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Purpose**: Redemption of Non-Convertible Debentures

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete trading halt affects liquidity and investor ability to exit positions before redemption.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders as of the record date (October 24, 2025) will be eligible for redemption proceeds. Investors unable to trade after this date must wait for redemption completion.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update systems to block trades in these securities and communicate the suspension to clients holding or interested in these debentures.

**Financial Impact**: Holders will receive redemption proceeds as per debenture terms; no secondary market exit available post-suspension date.
