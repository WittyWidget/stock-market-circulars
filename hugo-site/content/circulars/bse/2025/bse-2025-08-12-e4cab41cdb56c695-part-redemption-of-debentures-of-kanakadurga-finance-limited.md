## Summary

Kanakadurga Finance Limited has announced part redemption of its non-convertible debentures with a record date of August 16, 2025. The debentures will trade with reduced face value of Rs. 70,833 per debenture effective August 14, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Kanakadurga Finance Limited
- Debenture Code: KDFL-13%-31-1-27-PVT
- ISIN: INE104W07195 (976402)
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Record date: August 16, 2025
- Reduced face value: Rs. 70,833 per debenture
- Effective date: August 14, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-695/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. Standard corporate action procedure for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face value effective from August 14, 2025
- Debentures will trade with reduced face value from the effective date
- Record date compliance for interest payment and redemption eligibility

## Important Dates

- **August 14, 2025**: Effective date for reduced face value trading
- **August 16, 2025**: Record date for part redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this is a routine corporate action affecting only holders of this specific debenture series. The part redemption reduces the outstanding principal amount, with trading continuing at the reduced face value.
