## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of 360 ONE WAM LIMITED worth Rs. 200 crores (4000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from August 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (4000 units)
- Total issue size: Rs. 200 crores
- Scrip Code: 730032, ISIN: INE466L14ET7
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,508 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 14, 2025
- Listing Date: August 18, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term funding instrument for institutional investors in the debt market.
