## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Hinduja Leyland Finance Limited on private placement basis with effect from August 26, 2025. The securities carry 8% annual interest rate and mature on August 25, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 5,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Interest Rate: 8% per annum (paid annually)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AA+/STABLE
- Scrip Code: 977056
- ISIN: INE146O07573
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details
- Contact debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 25, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 26, 2025
- Interest Payment: Annually
- Redemption Date: August 25, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a standard private placement listing of debt securities. The listing provides additional fixed-income investment options for institutional investors.
