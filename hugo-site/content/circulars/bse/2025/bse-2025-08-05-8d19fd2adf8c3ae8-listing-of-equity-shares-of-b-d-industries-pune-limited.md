## Summary

BSE announces that equity shares of B. D. Industries (Pune) Limited will be listed and admitted to trading on the SME segment. The company has been assigned scrip code 544468, symbol BDI, and ISIN INE1LJ801017.

## Key Points

- Company: B. D. Industries (Pune) Limited
- Scrip Code: 544468
- Symbol: BDI
- ISIN: INE1LJ801017
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this time.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: August 5, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a new SME listing. The announcement provides advance notice to trading members to prepare for the new security's admission to trading.
