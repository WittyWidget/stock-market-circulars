## Summary

BSE has listed two new Commercial Paper securities of Bajaj Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective from August 12, 2025. Both securities have face value of Rs. 5 lakh each with quantities of 10,000 units.

## Key Points

- Two commercial paper securities listed on BSE Debt segment
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000/- each for both securities
- Quantity: 10,000 units each
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Central Bank of India

## Securities Details

**Security 1:**
- Scrip Code: 730007
- ISIN: INE377Y14BP3
- Issue Price: Rs. 485,193.50/-
- Allotment Date: August 11, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 9, 2026

**Security 2:**
- Scrip Code: 730008
- ISIN: INE377Y14BQ1
- Issue Price: Rs. 487,414.50/-
- Allotment Date: August 11, 2025
- Redemption Date: January 12, 2026

## Trading Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date:** August 12, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** August 11, 2025
- **Redemption Dates:** February 9, 2026 and January 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing announcement with minimal market impact. The commercial papers provide additional funding options for Bajaj Housing Finance Limited and offer investment opportunities for institutional investors in the debt segment.
