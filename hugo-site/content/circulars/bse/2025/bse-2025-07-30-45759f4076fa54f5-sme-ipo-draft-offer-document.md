## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Kanishk Aluminium India Limited for public comments. The document was filed on July 30, 2025, and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Public comments are sought within 21 days from the notice date.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Kanishk Aluminium India Limited on July 30, 2025
- Document contains all required disclosures under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for public viewing
- 21-day comment period for public feedback

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public can submit comments within 21 days from July 30, 2025
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document hosted on www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- Filing Date: July 30, 2025
- Comment Period: 21 days from July 30, 2025 (deadline: August 20, 2025)
- Document availability: Minimum 21 days on exchange website

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard procedural step for SME IPO processing. The public comment period allows stakeholders to review and provide feedback on the draft offer document before finalization. Impact is limited to investors interested in the SME IPO segment and specifically Kanishk Aluminium India Limited.
