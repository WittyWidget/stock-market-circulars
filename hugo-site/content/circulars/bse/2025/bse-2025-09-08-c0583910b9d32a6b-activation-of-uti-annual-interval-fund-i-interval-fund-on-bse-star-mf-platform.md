## Summary

UTI Mutual Fund is launching UTI ANNUAL INTERVAL FUND – I for a Specified Transaction Period (STP) from September 09-10, 2025 on BSE StAR MF Platform. The fund offers 6 different plan options across Regular and Direct plans with IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, and Growth variants.

## Key Points

- Transaction period: September 09-10, 2025 during normal business hours
- 6 scheme variants available: Regular and Direct plans with different dividend/growth options
- All schemes categorized as DEBT funds with specific ISIN codes
- NAV applicable based on cut-off timing and fund confirmation
- Payment restrictions: MICR Cheque, Nodal and OTM payments not accepted

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes specified in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Clear funds must be received before 2:30 PM on transaction dates
- Funds received after deadline will be refunded to investors after AMC/RTA rejection
- Transaction requests accepted only during specified business hours
- Payment method restrictions must be followed

## Important Dates

- **September 09, 2025**: STP begins, transaction requests accepted
- **September 10, 2025**: STP ends
- **2:30 PM daily**: Clear funds deadline during STP period

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on mutual fund investors seeking interval fund exposure. The short 2-day transaction window creates urgency for interested investors. Payment method restrictions may affect some investors' ability to participate. Overall market impact limited to mutual fund segment.
