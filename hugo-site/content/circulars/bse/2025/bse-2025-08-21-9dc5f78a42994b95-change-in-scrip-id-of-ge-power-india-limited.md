## Summary

BSE has announced a change in the Scrip ID for GE Power India Limited (Scrip Code: 532309) in the BOLT Plus System. The Scrip ID will change from 'GEPIL' to 'GVPIL' effective August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: GE Power India Limited
- Scrip Code: 532309
- Old Scrip ID: GEPIL
- New Scrip ID: GVPIL
- System: BOLT Plus System
- Effective Date: August 28, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

This is an administrative change in the trading system identifier for GE Power India Limited shares on BSE's BOLT Plus trading platform.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new Scrip ID
- All trading activities for GE Power India Limited must use the new identifier 'GVPIL' from August 28, 2025

## Important Dates

- **August 28, 2025**: Effective date for the new Scrip ID 'GVPIL'

## Impact Assessment

This change primarily affects trading members and market participants who need to update their systems and processes. The operational impact is moderate as it requires system updates but does not affect the underlying security or trading mechanics.
