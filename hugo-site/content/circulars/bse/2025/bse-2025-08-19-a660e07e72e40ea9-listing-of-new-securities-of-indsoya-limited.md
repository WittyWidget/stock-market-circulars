## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 1 crore new equity shares of Indsoya Limited (Scrip Code: 503639) issued through a rights offering. The shares will commence trading on August 20, 2025.

## Key Points

- 1,00,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each issued at par value
- Shares issued on rights basis to existing shareholders
- ISIN: INE314N01028
- Distribution numbers: 400001 to 10400000
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 12, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine listing of rights issue shares with minimal market impact. The new shares carry equal rights with existing shares and represent capital raising by the company through existing shareholder participation.
