## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Bajaj Finance Limited on private placement basis. Two series of Commercial Papers totaling 21,000 units (face value Rs. 5 lakhs each) are now admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective October 23, 2025. Both series carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings, with ICICI Bank Limited serving as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Series 1 (BFL-30-12-25-CP)**: 16,000 units with Scrip Code 730425, ISIN INE296A14C97, issued at Rs. 493,947, maturing December 30, 2025
- **Series 2 (BFL-19-1-26-CP)**: 5,000 units with Scrip Code 730426, ISIN INE296A14D05, issued at Rs. 492,099, maturing January 19, 2026
- Both series have face value and paid-up value of Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ for both series
- Allotment date: October 20, 2025
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 20, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 23, 2025
- **Redemption Date (Series 1)**: December 30, 2025
- **Redemption Date (Series 2)**: January 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing of short-term debt instruments (Commercial Paper) with maturity periods of approximately 2-3 months. The listing provides additional liquidity options for institutional investors in the debt segment.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to debt segment trading members who may now trade these instruments. No changes to trading infrastructure or procedures required beyond standard debt segment protocols.

**Investor Impact**: Provides institutional investors access to highly-rated (A1+) short-term debt instruments from Bajaj Finance Limited through the secondary market on BSE's debt platform.
