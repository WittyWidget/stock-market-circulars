## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of RNIT AI Solutions Limited (formerly known as Autopal Industries Limited) with Scrip Code 517286 will be moved to trading under Group 'X' effective Monday, November 10, 2025. This represents a change in the trading group classification for the company's shares.

## Key Points

- Company: RNIT AI Solutions Limited (formerly Autopal Industries Limited)
- Scrip Code: 517286
- New Trading Group: Group 'X'
- Effective Date: Monday, November 10, 2025
- Notice issued by Kinnar Mehta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

The trading group classification for RNIT AI Solutions Limited shares is being changed to Group 'X'. Group 'X' typically contains securities that are listed but may have specific trading restrictions or characteristics different from regular equity groups.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new group classification for Scrip Code 517286
- All trading in these shares must comply with Group 'X' regulations and requirements from the effective date
- Market participants should familiarize themselves with Group 'X' trading rules and restrictions

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 10, 2025 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

The reclassification to Group 'X' may affect trading dynamics for RNIT AI Solutions Limited shares. Group 'X' classification can impact liquidity, margin requirements, and trading parameters. Investors and trading members should review the specific implications of Group 'X' trading rules. The company has also undergone a name change from Autopal Industries Limited, which investors should note for identification purposes.
