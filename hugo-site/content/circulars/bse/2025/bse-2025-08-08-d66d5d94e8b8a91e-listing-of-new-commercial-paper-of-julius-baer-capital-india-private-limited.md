## Summary

Julius Baer Capital (India) Private Limited has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 100 crores (2000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE Debt segment effective August 8, 2025. The paper carries CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings with redemption date of May 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,74,174 per unit
- Total quantity: 2000 units
- Scrip Code: 729990, ISIN: INE824H14SD9
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- HDFC Bank Limited is the Issuing and Paying agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialised form under specified ISIN
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size of 1 paise applicable
- Market lot size: 1 unit

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 8, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 8, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: May 19, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Adds to the debt market instruments available for trading but does not affect broader market operations or equity segments.
