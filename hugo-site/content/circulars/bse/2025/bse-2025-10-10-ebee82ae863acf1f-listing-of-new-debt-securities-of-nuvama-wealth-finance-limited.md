## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of new debt securities issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective October 10, 2025. The securities consist of 20,000 debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 9.0762% per annum, and maturing on December 9, 2027. The securities are rated CARE AA-/Stable and will trade only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited
- Scrip Code: 977196
- Scrip ID: 908NWFL27
- ISIN: INE918K07QB5
- Quantity: 20,000 debentures
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Market Lot: 1
- Interest Rate: 9.0762% per annum (paid annually)
- Credit Rating: CARE AA-/Stable
- Date of Allotment: October 9, 2025
- Maturity Date: December 9, 2027
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Put/Call Options: As per Key Information Document (KID)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard new debt securities listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE918K07QB5
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** October 9, 2025
- **Listing Date:** October 10, 2025
- **Interest Payment:** Annually
- **Redemption Date:** December 9, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides institutional and qualified investors access to a CARE AA-/Stable rated debt instrument from Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited. The securities are privately placed and will not affect retail equity markets. Trading members dealing in debt securities can access these instruments through the BSE Debt segment. The 9.0762% annual interest rate reflects current market conditions for AA- rated financial sector debt with approximately 2-year tenure.
