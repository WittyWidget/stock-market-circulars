## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in two Treasury Bills (T-Bills) effective July 29, 2025, as they are approaching their redemption/maturity date. This is a routine operational measure to prevent trading in securities that are about to mature.

## Key Points

- Two T-Bills will be suspended from trading: 182TB31725 (ISIN: IN002024Y423) and 364TB310725 (ISIN: IN002024Z180)
- Trading suspension takes effect from July 29, 2025
- Suspension is due to securities reaching maturity/redemption date
- Notice reference: DR-683/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine operational suspension due to security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified T-Bills from July 29, 2025
- Trading members should take note of the suspension and adjust their operations accordingly

## Important Dates

- **July 29, 2025**: Effective date for trading suspension of both T-Bills
- **July 28, 2025**: Notice date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine suspension of maturing T-Bills. Trading members need to ensure they do not execute trades in these specific securities after the effective date. The suspension affects only two debt securities in the T-Bills segment.
