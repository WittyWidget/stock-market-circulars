## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Treasury Bill 182TB29825 (ISIN: IN002024Y464) effective August 26, 2025, due to its maturity on the redemption date. Trading members are advised to cease dealing in this T-Bill from the specified date.

## Key Points

- T-Bill 182TB29825 with ISIN IN002024Y464 will mature on August 26, 2025
- Trading suspension effective from August 26, 2025
- Scrip code: 805032
- Notice issued under reference DR-702/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine operational notice for instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified T-Bill from August 26, 2025
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension

## Important Dates

- **August 25, 2025**: Notice date
- **August 26, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date and redemption date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine maturity-based suspension affecting only one T-Bill instrument. Trading members holding positions in this T-Bill should plan for settlement and redemption processes.
