## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Indel Money Limited worth Rs. 2,500 lakhs issued on private placement basis. The securities carry an interest rate of 11.25% per annum with monthly interest payments and mature on April 23, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2,500 securities of Rs. 1 lakh face value each
- Scrip Code: 976928 (ISIN: INE0BUS07BW7)
- Issue Price: Rs. 99,630.14 per security
- Credit Rating: CRISIL BBB+/Stable
- Interest Rate: 11.25% per annum paid monthly
- Trading in dematerialized form only with 1 paise tick size

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities will trade under the specified ISIN number
- Tick size of 1 paise applies
- Refer to Placement Memorandum for detailed terms

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 11, 2025
- Interest Payment Dates: Monthly from August 23, 2025 to April 23, 2027
- Maturity Date: April 23, 2027
- Trading Commencement: August 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing of corporate debt securities. The listing adds to BSE's debt segment inventory but represents standard corporate financing activity with no broader market implications.
