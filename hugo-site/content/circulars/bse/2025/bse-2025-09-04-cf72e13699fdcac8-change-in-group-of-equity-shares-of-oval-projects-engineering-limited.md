## Summary

OVAL PROJECTS ENGINEERING LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544498) will be transferred from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) effective September 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: OVAL PROJECTS ENGINEERING LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544498
- Current segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: September 18, 2025
- Contact person: Mr. Anurag Jain (022-2272 8822)

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be moved from restrictive Trade for Trade segment to more liquid Rolling segment, allowing normal trading mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members need to be aware of the segment change and adjust their trading strategies accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **September 18, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer

## Impact Assessment

The move from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment typically indicates improved compliance or reduced risk concerns, potentially increasing liquidity and trading activity for the stock.
