## Summary

BSE has listed 4,500,000 equity shares of TITAN INTECH LIMITED (Scrip Code: 521005) with effect from October 28, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 4.50 to Non Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. All shares are subject to lock-in until July 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- 4,500,000 equity shares of face value Rs. 1.00 each listed
- Issued at premium of Rs. 4.50 per share (total issue price: Rs. 5.50)
- Shares issued to Non Promoters on preferential basis
- Issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Distinctive Numbers: 323103911 to 327603910
- Date of Allotment: September 13, 2025
- ISIN: INE807M01031
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- All 4,500,000 shares are subject to lock-in restrictions until July 20, 2026
- Shares cannot be traded or transferred during the lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: September 13, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 28, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: July 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification for a specific company's preferential allotment. The impact is limited to TITAN INTECH LIMITED shareholders and potential investors. The lock-in period ensures these shares will not be available for trading until July 2026, preventing immediate dilution impact. Market-wide impact is minimal as this affects only one scrip.
