## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 2,000 crores (40,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 40,000 units (Rs. 2,000 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,788 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Scrip code: 730099, ISIN: INE929O14DY0
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 26, 2025
- Listing date: August 28, 2025
- Redemption date: November 25, 2025
- Tenure: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding option for Reliance Retail Ventures Limited and investment opportunity for debt market participants.
