## Summary

BSE has updated the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective August 14, 2025. Three new securities have been added to LT-ASM, one security moves to a higher ASM stage, and one security exits the framework due to inclusion in ESM framework.

## Key Points

- 3 new securities shortlisted for Long Term ASM Framework
- 1 security moved to higher ASM Stage II
- 1 security moving out of LT-ASM Framework due to ESM inclusion
- Changes effective from August 14, 2025

## New Securities in LT-ASM Framework

**Part A - Securities shortlisted in Long Term ASM:**
- Jayaswal Neco Industries Ltd (522285, INE854B01010)
- Spright Agro Ltd (531205, INE179O01031)
- Vintron Informatics Ltd (517393, INE043B01028)

## Securities Moving to Higher ASM Stage

**Part B - Moving to ASM Stage II:**
- Ambitious Plastomac Company Ltd (526439, INE267C01013)

## Securities Exiting LT-ASM Framework

**Securities moving out due to ESM Framework inclusion:**
- Spice Islands Industries Ltd (526827, INE882D01017)

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** August 14, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Securities under LT-ASM framework face enhanced surveillance measures which may include additional margin requirements, position limits, or other trading restrictions. The movement of securities between different ASM stages reflects BSE's ongoing monitoring of market activity and risk assessment.
