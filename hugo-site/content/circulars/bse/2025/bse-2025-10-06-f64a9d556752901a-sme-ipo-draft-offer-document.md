## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Shree Balaji (Mala) Textiles Limited filed on September 29, 2025 on its SME website for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations and will remain available for at least 21 days for stakeholder feedback.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Shree Balaji (Mala) Textiles Limited on September 29, 2025
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations disclosure requirements
- Available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date (October 6, 2025)
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft document publication as per existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Stakeholders and public may review the Draft Offer Document and submit comments within 21 days
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- The company must maintain the document on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days as per regulatory requirements

## Important Dates

- **September 29, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed
- **October 6, 2025**: Notice date and start of public comment period
- **October 27, 2025**: Last date for public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal market impact as it is a standard procedural announcement for an SME IPO. The impact is limited to:

- Prospective investors in Shree Balaji (Mala) Textiles Limited who wish to review the draft document
- Market participants interested in upcoming SME IPO opportunities
- No immediate impact on existing market operations or listed securities
- Represents early-stage IPO process with final offer document to follow after public comment period
