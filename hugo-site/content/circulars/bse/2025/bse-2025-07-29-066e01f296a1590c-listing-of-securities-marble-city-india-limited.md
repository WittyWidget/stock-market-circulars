## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 877,800 new equity shares of Marble City India Limited (Scrip Code: 531281) issued on preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will commence trading on July 30, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 877,800 equity shares of Rs. 5.00 each issued at premium of Rs. 95.50
- Issue price: Rs. 100.50 per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE807H01023
- Distribution numbers: 22447309 to 23325108

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed about the new securities listing and permitted trading from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: January 24, 2025
- Trading Commencement: July 30, 2025
- Lock-in Period End: February 5-6, 2026 (varying by distribution numbers)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this represents a routine preferential issue listing. The lock-in restrictions on shares until February 2026 will limit immediate trading volume. The preferential issue indicates capital raising by the company for business expansion or working capital requirements.
