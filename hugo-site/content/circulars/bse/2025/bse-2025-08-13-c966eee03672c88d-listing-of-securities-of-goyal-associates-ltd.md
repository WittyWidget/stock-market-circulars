## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 60,00,000 equity shares of Goyal Associates Ltd. (Scrip Code: 530663) issued on preferential basis to non-promoters pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares will be available for trading from August 14, 2025.

## Key Points

- 60 lakh equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at premium of Rs. 1.50/-
- Issue price: Rs. 2.50 per share
- Shares issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Date of allotment: March 31, 2025
- ISIN: INE912B01032
- Distinct numbers: 46937501 to 52937500

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed about the new securities being listed and permitted for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Trading Commencement**: August 14, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: March 31, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: February 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine listing of preferential shares for a single company. The shares are subject to lock-in restrictions until February 2026, which limits immediate trading volume impact.
