## Summary

BSE announces the addition of six newly listed companies to its indices. Three companies will be added to the BSE IPO index effective August 8, 2025, and three SME companies will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective August 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- Six new stocks being added to BSE indices following their listing on August 6, 2025
- Three main board listings added to BSE IPO index
- Three SME platform listings added to BSE SME IPO index
- Changes reflected in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - routine index maintenance following new listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants beyond standard index tracking obligations.

## Important Dates

- **August 6, 2025**: Listing date for all six companies
- **August 7, 2025**: SME stocks added to BSE SME IPO index at market open
- **August 8, 2025**: Main board stocks added to BSE IPO index at market open

## Impact Assessment

**BSE IPO Index Additions (Effective August 8, 2025):**
- National Securities Depository Limited (544467)
- Sri Lotus Developers and Realty Limited (544469) 
- M & B Engineering Limited (544470)

**BSE SME IPO Index Additions (Effective August 7, 2025):**
- B. D. Industries (Pune) Limited (544468)
- Takyon Networks Limited (544471)
- Mehul Colours Limited (544472)

Index funds and ETFs tracking these indices will need to adjust their portfolios accordingly. The additions may result in increased trading activity and fund flows into these newly listed securities.
