## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of India Infradebt Limited (ISIN: INE537P07588, Code: 960203) effective October 28, 2025. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Company: India Infradebt Limited
- Security Type: Non-Convertible Debentures (7% maturing November 12, 2025)
- ISIN: INE537P07588
- Security Code: 960203
- Record Date: October 28, 2025
- No Dealings From: October 28, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Notice Reference: DR-744/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational procedure for debenture redemption and interest payment events.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures of India Infradebt Limited from October 28, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance with the no-dealings restriction
- All trading activity in security code 960203 must cease from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 24, 2025
- **Record Date:** October 28, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Start Date:** October 28, 2025
- **Debenture Maturity Date:** November 12, 2025 (as per security description)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Limited impact as this affects a single privately placed debenture issue. The suspension is procedural and relates to the maturity and redemption process of the instrument.

**Investor Impact:** Debenture holders registered as of the record date (October 28, 2025) will receive interest payment and redemption proceeds. No trading opportunity exists from October 28, 2025 onwards.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in security code 960203 from October 28, 2025. The suspension is aligned with the upcoming redemption on November 12, 2025.
