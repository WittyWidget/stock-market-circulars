## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 3,11,111 new equity shares of Vivo Bio tech Ltd (Scrip Code: 511509) effective from Wednesday, August 6, 2025. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis following conversion of warrants at Rs. 45 per share.

## Key Points

- 3,11,111 equity shares of Rs. 10 face value each issued at Rs. 35 premium
- Shares issued to promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Trading permitted from August 6, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 17164817 to 17475927
- ISIN: INE380K01017

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities available for trading
- All listed shares subject to existing BSE trading rules and regulations
- Lock-in restrictions apply as specified

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: April 8, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 6, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: February 28, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal immediate market impact expected as all 3,11,111 shares are subject to lock-in restrictions until February 28, 2027, preventing immediate trading by promoters. The preferential issue strengthens the company's equity base without affecting free float availability.
