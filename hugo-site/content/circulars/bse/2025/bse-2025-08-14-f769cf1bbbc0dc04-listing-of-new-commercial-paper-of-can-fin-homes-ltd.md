## Summary

Can Fin Homes Ltd has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 500 crores (10,000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) on BSE Debt segment through private placement. The securities are listed with effect from August 14, 2025, and will mature on November 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each issued on private placement basis
- Total quantity: 10,000 units (Rs. 500 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,727.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing of debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Securities will be identified by ISIN Number INE477A14EA7

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 13, 2025
- Listing effective date: August 14, 2025
- Redemption date: November 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term funding instrument for investors in the debt market segment.
