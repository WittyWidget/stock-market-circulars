## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Paisalo Digital Limited worth ₹15 crore (300 units of ₹5 lakh each) issued on private placement basis. The securities will trade in dematerialized form under scrip code 730058 with tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- 300 Commercial Paper units of ₹5,00,000 each listed
- Issue price: ₹4,77,386.50 per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: IVR A1+ 
- ISIN: INE420C14219
- Scrip Code: 730058, Scrip ID: PDL20825
- IDBI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard commercial paper listing procedures followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading allowed only in standard denomination of ₹5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will trade under the specified ISIN number

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 20, 2025
- Listing Date: August 21, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 18, 2026
- Tenor: Approximately 6 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding avenue for Paisalo Digital Limited and investment option for institutional investors in the debt segment.
