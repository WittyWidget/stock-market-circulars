## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 50 crore issued by Mirae Asset Financial Services (India) Private Limited on private placement basis, effective August 20, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5 lakh denomination each (1000 units)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,544 per unit (face value Rs. 5 lakh)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Scrip code: 730048
- ISIN: INE0JRU14255

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applies
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 20, 2025
- Redemption date: November 19, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing in the debt segment. The 91-day maturity instrument provides short-term funding for the issuer with no effect on equity markets.
