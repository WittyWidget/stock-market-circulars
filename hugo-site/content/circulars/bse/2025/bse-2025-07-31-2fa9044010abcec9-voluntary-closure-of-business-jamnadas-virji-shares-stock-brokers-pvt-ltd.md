## Summary

BSE has announced that Jamnadas Virji Shares & Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 339) will voluntarily cease business operations in equity derivative segments effective July 31, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading member voluntarily closing business operations
- Member number: 339
- Cessation applies to equity derivative segments
- Effective date: July 31, 2025
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a voluntary business closure by the member.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements mentioned for other market participants.

## Important Dates

- **July 31, 2025**: Effective date of business cessation

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves the voluntary closure of a single trading member. Other market participants and trading operations remain unaffected.
