## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 25 crore issued by Capri Global Capital Limited on private placement basis. The securities are available for trading on BSE Debt segment effective July 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of face value Rs. 5 lakh each (500 units)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,60,829.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+
- ISIN: INE180C14557
- Scrip Code: 729918
- Redemption date: July 29, 2026
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Yes Bank Limited

## Trading Details

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading segment: BSE Debt

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: July 29, 2025
- Trading commencement: July 29, 2025
- Redemption date: July 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides additional short-term funding avenue for Capri Global Capital Limited and investment option for institutional investors in debt segment.
