## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of MONARCH SURVEYORS AND ENGINEERING CONSULTANTS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544453) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective August 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: MONARCH SURVEYORS AND ENGINEERING CONSULTANTS LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544453
- Current segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (M Group)

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for MONARCH SURVEYORS shares will change from Trade for Trade basis to Rolling settlement, allowing for more flexible trading conditions.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members need to be aware of the segment change and adjust their trading systems accordingly for the new settlement mechanism.

## Important Dates

- **August 12, 2025**: Effective date for transfer to Rolling segment (M Group)

## Impact Assessment

This change will improve liquidity and trading flexibility for MONARCH SURVEYORS shares by moving from the restrictive Trade for Trade segment to the more liquid Rolling segment, potentially benefiting investors with better price discovery and settlement options.
