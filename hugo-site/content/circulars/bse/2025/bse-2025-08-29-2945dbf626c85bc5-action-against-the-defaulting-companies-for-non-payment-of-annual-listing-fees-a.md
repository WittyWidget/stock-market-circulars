## Summary

BSE announces the revocation of trading suspension for two companies - Dalmia Industrial Development Limited and Nihar Info Global Limited - effective September 3, 2025, following their payment of outstanding Annual Listing Fees as required under SEBI LODR Regulations.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension revoked for two companies that paid outstanding Annual Listing Fees
- Action taken in compliance with SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Continuation of previous notices from June 25, 2021 and June 20, 2025
- Different trading arrangements for each company post-revocation

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing requirements under Regulation 14 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must pay Annual Listing Fees as per SEBI LODR Regulation 14
- Trading members advised to note the changes in trading status
- Queries to be directed to bse.sopalf@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **September 3, 2025**: Effective date for revocation of trading suspension for both companies
- **August 29, 2025**: Circular issue date

## Impact Assessment

**Dalmia Industrial Development Limited (Scrip Code: 539900)**:
- Trading suspension revoked
- Will continue in trade-for-trade mode for other reasons

**Nihar Info Global Limited (Scrip Code: 531083)**:
- Trading suspension revoked
- Will resume trading in "X" group

Limited market impact as it affects only two specific companies that have resolved their compliance issues.
