## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of SIHORA INDUSTRIES LIMITED filed on August 11, 2025, for public comments. The document contains all required disclosures under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations and is available on stock exchange websites for at least 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by SIHORA INDUSTRIES LIMITED on August 11, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for public review
- 21-day comment period for stakeholders
- Contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard SME IPO disclosure process under existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public can submit comments within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document hosted on www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- Draft filing date: August 11, 2025
- Notice date: August 13, 2025
- Comment deadline: September 3, 2025 (21 days from notice)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a standard SME IPO documentation process. Affects potential investors and market participants interested in SIHORA INDUSTRIES LIMITED's public offering.
