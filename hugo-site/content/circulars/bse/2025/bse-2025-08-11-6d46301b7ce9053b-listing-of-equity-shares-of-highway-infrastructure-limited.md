## Summary

Highway Infrastructure Limited has filed its post-issue shareholding pattern as of August 9, 2025, following the listing of its equity shares. The company has a total of 71,720,632 shares with promoters holding 70.04% and public shareholders holding 29.96%.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 71,720,632
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 50,231,344 shares (70.0375%)
- Public shareholding: 21,489,288 shares (29.9625%)
- Total shareholders: 37,240 (7 promoters + 37,233 public)
- No partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding
- No shares in locked-in status or pledged by promoters

## Regulatory Changes

None specified in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Filing under Regulation 31(1)(a) for shareholding pattern
- Declaration regarding various security types and encumbrances
- Submission of post-issue shareholding details

## Important Dates

- Post-issue date: August 9, 2025
- Filing date: August 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The listing provides transparency on ownership structure with clear promoter-public distribution. The 29.96% public shareholding ensures adequate free float for trading liquidity. No encumbrances or special securities simplify the capital structure for investors.
