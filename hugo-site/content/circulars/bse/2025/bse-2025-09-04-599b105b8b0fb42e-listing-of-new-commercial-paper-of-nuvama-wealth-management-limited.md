## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Nuvama Wealth Management Limited worth Rs. 35 crores (700 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from September 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper quantity: 700 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total issue size: Rs. 35 crores
- Issue price: Rs. 4,90,846.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730148, Scrip ID: NWML030925
- ISIN: INE531F14FH2
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Trading members can contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 3, 2025
- Trading Commencement: September 4, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The securities provide short-term funding option for Nuvama Wealth Management Limited with 3-month maturity period. High credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk for investors.
