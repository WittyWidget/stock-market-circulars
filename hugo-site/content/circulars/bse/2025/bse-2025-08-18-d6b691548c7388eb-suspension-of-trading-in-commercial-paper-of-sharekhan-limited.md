## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in commercial paper of Sharekhan Limited (ISIN: INE211H14971) effective August 19, 2025, due to the instrument reaching its maturity date.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper with scrip code 728074 will mature on redemption date
- Trading suspension effective from August 19, 2025
- ISIN number: INE211H14971
- Notice reference: DR-697/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine operational suspension due to instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members advised not to deal in the specified commercial paper from August 19, 2025
- All trading members must take note of this suspension

## Important Dates

- **August 18, 2025**: Notice date
- **August 19, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **Redemption date**: Commercial paper maturity (specific date not mentioned)

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this affects only one specific commercial paper instrument. Routine market operation with no broader implications for trading or market structure.
