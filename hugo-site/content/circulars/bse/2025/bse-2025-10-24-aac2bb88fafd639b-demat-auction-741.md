## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities available for Demat Auction under Settlement Number 741 dated October 24, 2025. The auction includes 158 different scrips across various sectors with quantities ranging from 1 share to 78,314 shares depending on the security. This is a routine settlement mechanism to fulfill delivery obligations where sellers have failed to deliver securities.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 741
- Auction Date: October 24, 2025
- Total number of scrips listed: 158
- Quantities range from 1 share (multiple securities) to 78,314 shares (Mehai Techno)
- Includes major stocks like Reliance Industries (10 shares), Infosys (25 shares), ICICI Bank (40 shares), ITC (240 shares)
- Also includes mid-cap and small-cap securities
- Auction conducted to fulfill delivery obligations in settlement process

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard operational circular for demat auction settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with shortages in the listed securities must participate in the auction or arrange delivery
- Members must settle their obligations as per BSE auction settlement procedures
- Participants must ensure sufficient funds/securities for auction settlement

## Important Dates

- **Auction Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: 741
- Specific auction timings and settlement date would be as per BSE's standard auction schedule

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine operational process affecting only specific members with delivery shortages. The quantities involved are relatively small for most securities.

**Trading Impact**: Limited to members who have failed to deliver the listed securities in the normal settlement process. These members will need to purchase from the auction at potentially higher prices.

**Investor Impact**: Minimal direct impact on general investors. Some securities may see temporary price volatility during auction hours.

**Notable Securities Involved**:
- Large-cap: Reliance Industries, Infosys, ICICI Bank, ITC, Larsen & Toubro, HDFC Bank (Axis Bank)
- Mid-cap and small-cap stocks with varying quantities
- Highest quantity shortage: Mehai Techno (78,314 shares), Indian Infotech (34,240 shares), Remedium Life (31,000 shares)
