## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of MIDWEST LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544587) to the BSE IPO Index effective October 28, 2025. The stock was listed on BSE on October 24, 2025, and will be included in the index at the open of trading on October 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- MIDWEST LIMITED (ticker: 544587) listed on BSE on Friday, October 24, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE IPO Index effective at the open on Tuesday, October 28, 2025
- Corporate action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Notice references prior Notice No: 20251023-21

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard index constituent addition following a new listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index providers and index fund managers tracking the BSE IPO Index should incorporate this change into their portfolio rebalancing.

## Important Dates

- **October 24, 2025**: MIDWEST LIMITED listing date on BSE
- **October 28, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine index maintenance action with minimal market impact. The addition affects:

- Index funds and ETFs tracking the BSE IPO Index will need to acquire shares of MIDWEST LIMITED
- May result in modest buying pressure on the stock as index funds rebalance
- No impact on trading operations or other market participants
- Standard procedure following new IPO listings on BSE
