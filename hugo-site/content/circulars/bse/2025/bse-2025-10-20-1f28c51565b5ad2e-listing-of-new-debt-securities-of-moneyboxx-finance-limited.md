## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of MONEYBOXX FINANCE LIMITED issued on private placement basis, effective October 20, 2025. The securities comprise 40,000 units with a face value of Rs. 10,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 10.25% per annum with monthly interest payments. The debentures are rated CRISIL BBB/Stable and will mature on October 16, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 40,000 units
- Scrip Code: 977227
- Scrip ID: 1025MBFL27
- ISIN: INE296Q07142
- Face Value: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 10,000 per unit
- Interest Rate: 10.25% per annum
- Credit Rating: CRISIL BBB/Stable
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Date of Allotment: October 16, 2025
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt security listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE296Q07142
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 16, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 20, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Monthly from November 16, 2025 to October 16, 2027
- **Redemption Date**: October 16, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a specific debt instrument issued through private placement. The securities provide debt market investors with an investment option in MONEYBOXX FINANCE LIMITED at a 10.25% annual return with CRISIL BBB/Stable rating. The monthly interest payment structure may appeal to income-focused investors. No put or call options are available on these securities.
