## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED on its debt segment effective August 4, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each and carry a 6.99% annual interest rate.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 35,500 securities listed
- Scrip Code: 976981, Scrip ID: 699IHFC28
- ISIN: INE071G07850
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Interest Rate: 6.99% per annum
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA/Stable, ICRA AAA/STABLE
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Tick size of 1 paise applies to all transactions

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 1, 2025
- Interest Payment: Annually from November 1, 2025 to November 1, 2028
- Maturity Date: November 1, 2028
- Trading Commencement: August 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing. The AAA credit rating indicates high credit quality. No put/call options are available, providing certainty for investors regarding the investment tenure.
