## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Currency Derivatives segment on Saturday, September 06, 2025. This session is designed for testing and familiarization purposes, allowing trading members to test their systems and platforms without any financial obligations.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Currency Derivatives segment on September 06, 2025
- New BOLT Pro TWS version 12.03 will be released
- Members can choose between mock trading session or UAT environment for regulatory compliance
- Third-party trading platforms and in-house systems can test various functionalities
- No margin obligations or pay-in/pay-out obligations will apply
- Feedback required by 4:30 PM on the same day

## Trading Schedule

- **Log-in**: 10:15 AM to 11:00 AM
- **Continuous Trading for Currency Derivatives**: 11:00 AM to 03:30 PM
- **Continuous Trading for Cross Currency Derivatives**: 11:00 AM to 03:30 PM
- **Trade Modification (Currency)**: 03:40 PM
- **Trade Modification (Cross Currency)**: 03:40 PM

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must participate actively in mock trading sessions
- Members should provide feedback by 4:30 PM on the trading day
- Compliance with SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020
- Reference to Exchange Notice No 20201201-22 for testing requirements

## Important Dates

- **Mock Trading Date**: September 06, 2025 (Saturday)
- **Feedback Deadline**: 4:30 PM on September 06, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine mock trading session with minimal market impact. The session provides an opportunity for members to test their trading systems and familiarize themselves with platform updates. No actual financial transactions or obligations will result from this mock trading activity.
