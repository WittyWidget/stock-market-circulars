## Summary

360 ONE Prime Limited has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 175 crore on BSE's debt segment. The commercial paper was issued on private placement basis with face value of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling 3,500 units.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each listed on BSE debt segment
- Total quantity: 3,500 units (Rs. 175 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,604.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- ISIN: INE248U14RX4
- Scrip code: 729936

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size is 1 paise
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: July 29, 2025
- Listing effective date: July 30, 2025
- Redemption date: October 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The 3-month tenure and high credit ratings indicate standard corporate financing activity with no broader market implications.
