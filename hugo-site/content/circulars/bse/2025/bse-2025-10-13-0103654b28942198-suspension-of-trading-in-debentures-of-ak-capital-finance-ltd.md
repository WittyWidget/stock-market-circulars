## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in debentures (ISIN: INE197P07342, Code: 975868) of A.K. CAPITAL FINANCE LTD. effective October 16, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for interest payment and redemption of its non-convertible debentures. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture ISIN: INE197P07342 (Security Code: 975868)
- Instrument: A.K. CAPITAL FINANCE LTD 9.35% debentures maturing November 1, 2025
- Record date: October 16, 2025
- Trading suspension effective: October 16, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Notice reference: DR-737/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational measure taken for corporate actions involving debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute trades in the specified debentures from October 16, 2025
- Trading members should inform their clients holding these debentures about the suspension
- Members must ensure compliance with the no-dealing restriction

## Important Dates

- **October 13, 2025**: Notice issued by BSE
- **October 16, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and redemption
- **October 16, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **November 1, 2025**: Debenture maturity date (as per security descriptor)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of this specific debenture series (ISIN: INE197P07342). The suspension is temporary and procedural in nature, allowing the company to process redemption and interest payments to eligible holders. This is a routine corporate action with minimal broader market impact. Investors holding these debentures cannot trade them during the suspension period but will receive their redemption proceeds and interest payment as scheduled.
