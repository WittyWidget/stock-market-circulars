## Summary

BSE sought clarifications from Bharat Forge Limited (Scrip Code: 500493) and EPACK Prefab Technologies Limited (Scrip Code: 544540) regarding significant increases in trading volumes of their securities. Both companies confirmed compliance with SEBI Listing Regulations and stated there is no undisclosed price sensitive information or impending announcements affecting their securities.

## Key Points

- Bharat Forge Limited confirmed compliance with SEBI Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements Regulations, 2015
- Bharat Forge stated no undisclosed or price sensitive information exists
- Bharat Forge confirmed no impending announcement or corporate action that could affect price/volume behavior
- EPACK Prefab confirmed investors have latest relevant information as per SEBI Listing Regulations
- EPACK Prefab recently approved Financial Results for Q1 FY2036 (quarter ended June 30, 2035) and Q2/H1 FY2026 (ended September 30, 2025) on October 22, 2025
- EPACK Prefab conducted investor call on October 24, 2025 at 4 PM with related compliances completed
- Both companies assured continuous monitoring and prompt disclosure of material events

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond to stock exchange inquiries regarding unusual trading activity
- Companies must confirm compliance with Regulation 30 of SEBI Listing Regulations
- Companies must disclose any undisclosed price sensitive information or pending corporate actions
- Companies must continuously monitor market developments and inform exchanges of material events

## Important Dates

- October 24, 2025: BSE email requesting clarification (Ref: L/SURV/ONL/PV/AA/2025-2026/599)
- October 24, 2025: Companies' responses submitted
- October 22, 2025: EPACK Prefab Board approved quarterly financial results
- October 24, 2025, 4:00 PM: EPACK Prefab investor call

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine surveillance measure by BSE to ensure market integrity and transparency. Both companies provided standard clarifications confirming no material undisclosed information exists. The volume movement appears to be due to normal market activity rather than any specific corporate developments. EPACK Prefab's recent financial results announcement and investor call may have contributed to increased trading interest. No significant market impact expected as companies confirmed all material information has been disclosed in compliance with listing regulations.
