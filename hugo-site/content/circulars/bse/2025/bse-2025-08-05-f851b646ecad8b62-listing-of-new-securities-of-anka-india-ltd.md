## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 36,154,529 new equity shares of Anka India Ltd (Scrip Code: 531673) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will commence trading on August 6, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 36,154,529 new equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at Rs. 7/- premium
- Issue price: Rs. 17/- per share
- Allotment date: June 11, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 6, 2025
- ISIN: INE067C01025
- Distribution numbers: 15387957 to 51542485

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. Standard listing procedure for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability for trading
- Lock-in restrictions apply to different tranches of shares
- Shares rank equally with existing equity shares

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: June 11, 2025
- **Trading Start Date**: August 6, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: February 28, 2026 and February 28, 2027 (varies by tranche)

## Impact Assessment

Significant equity dilution with addition of 3.62 crore shares. Lock-in periods of 1-2 years will restrict immediate selling pressure. The preferential allotment to non-promoters may indicate capital raising for business expansion. Market impact will depend on existing shareholding pattern and trading volumes.
