## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and trading of 400,000 new equity shares of Mudunuru Limited (Scrip Code: 538743) with effect from Wednesday, August 13, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 400,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at premium of Rs. 10/-
- Issue price: Rs. 12.00 per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE491C01027
- Distinctive Numbers: 31200001 to 31600000

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed about the new securities listing and trading permissions.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: June 2, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 13, 2025
- Lock-in Period End: March 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a small issuance for a single company. The shares are subject to lock-in until March 14, 2026, limiting immediate trading volume impact.
