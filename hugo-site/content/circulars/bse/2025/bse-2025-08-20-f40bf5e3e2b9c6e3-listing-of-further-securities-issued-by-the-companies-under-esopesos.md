## Summary

BSE has listed additional securities for 13 companies issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS), effective from Thursday, August 21, 2025. The listing includes securities from major companies like Wipro, Kotak Mahindra Bank, Federal Bank, and others.

## Key Points

- 13 companies have listed additional securities under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Total securities listed: 1,647,693 shares across all companies
- Effective trading date: August 21, 2025
- Face values range from ₹2 to ₹10 per share
- Only Transcorp International Ltd. has lock-in period until July 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading
- Securities are admitted for trading with immediate effect
- Standard trading rules and regulations apply

## Important Dates

- **Effective Trading Date**: August 21, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: July 30, 2026 (only for Transcorp International Ltd.)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this represents routine ESOP issuances. The additional shares may provide slight increase in liquidity for the respective stocks. Transcorp International's lock-in period until 2026 means those shares won't be tradeable immediately.
