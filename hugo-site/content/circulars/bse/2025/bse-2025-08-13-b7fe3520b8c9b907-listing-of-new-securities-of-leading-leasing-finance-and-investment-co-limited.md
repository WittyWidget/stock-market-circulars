## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 1,25,00,000 new equity shares of Leading Leasing Finance and Investment Co. Limited (Scrip Code: 540360) effective August 14, 2025. The shares were issued through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis to non-promoters.

## Key Points

- 1.25 crore equity shares of Rs. 1/- each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 7.40 per share (Rs. 1 face value + Rs. 6.40 premium)
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE715Q01029
- Distribution numbers: 524565501 to 537065500

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure for warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members can commence trading from August 14, 2025
- Standard compliance requirements for equity trading apply

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: June 25, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 14, 2025
- Lock-in Period Expiry: February 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of shares issued through warrant conversion. The lock-in period until February 2026 restricts immediate trading of all issued shares, limiting potential dilution effects.
