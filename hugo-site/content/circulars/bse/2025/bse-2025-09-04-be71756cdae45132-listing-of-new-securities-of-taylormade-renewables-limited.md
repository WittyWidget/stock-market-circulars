## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 19,822 new equity shares of Taylormade Renewables Limited (Scrip Code: 541228) effective from Friday, September 5, 2025. These shares were issued at Rs. 519/- per share to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 19,822 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 509/-
- Issue price: Rs. 519/- per share
- Allotment date: April 7, 2025
- Trading commencement: September 5, 2025
- ISIN: INE459Z01017
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 12349852 to 12369673

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- Lock-in provisions apply to all shares with varying periods until March 31, 2026 and April 1, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: April 7, 2025
- **Trading Start Date**: September 5, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: March 31, 2026 and April 1, 2026 (varies by share lots)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected due to small number of shares (19,822) and lock-in restrictions on all shares. The warrant conversion represents normal corporate action with no immediate trading implications beyond increased share count.
