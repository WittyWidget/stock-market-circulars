## Summary

L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) Limited has listed new commercial paper securities on BSE's Debt segment. The commercial paper, issued on private placement basis, carries a face value of Rs.5 lakh each with CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs.5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 9,700 units
- Scrip Code: 730085, ISIN: INE128M14AR3
- Issue price: Rs.4,93,424.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall be in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- State Bank of India serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 25, 2025
- Listing Date: August 26, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a standard commercial paper listing for a specific infrastructure company. The listing provides additional short-term debt investment options for institutional investors in the infrastructure sector.
