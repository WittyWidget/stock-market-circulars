## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited worth Rs. 42.5 crores (8,500 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) on private placement basis. The paper carries CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ ratings and matures on September 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total quantity: 8,500 units (Rs. 42.5 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,96,308.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip code: 729640, Scrip ID: BFSL120625
- ISIN: INE01C314BX5
- Issuing and paying agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise for trading
- Market lot size of 1 unit

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: July 29, 2025
- Listing date: July 30, 2025
- Redemption date: September 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides additional short-term funding avenue for Bajaj Financial Securities Limited with a tenor of approximately 44 days.
