## Summary

BSE has announced changes to the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective August 1, 2025. Three securities will be newly shortlisted under the ST-ASM framework, while six securities will move out due to inclusion in other surveillance frameworks.

## Key Points

- 3 securities newly shortlisted in Short Term 5/15/30 Days ASM Framework
- 6 securities moving out of ST-ASM Framework
- No securities moving to higher or lower Stage ASM within the framework
- Changes effective from August 1, 2025

## Securities Added to ST-ASM Framework

- Elegant Floriculture & Agrotech India Ltd (526473)
- Ortin Global Ltd (539287)
- Pondy Oxides & Chemicals Ltd (532626)

## Securities Moving Out of ST-ASM Framework

- Barak Valley Cements Ltd (532916)
- BDH Industries Ltd (524828)
- Garuda Construction and Engineering Ltd (544271) - moved to LT-ASM
- Hind Rectifiers Ltd (504036) - moved to LT-ASM
- Raghunath International Ltd (526813)
- Vashu Bhagnani Industries Ltd (532011) - moved to ESM Framework

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 1, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The changes affect trading conditions for the specified securities, with some moving to more stringent surveillance frameworks (LT-ASM, ESM) while others are newly included in short-term surveillance measures. This reflects BSE's ongoing monitoring of market activities and price movements in these securities.
