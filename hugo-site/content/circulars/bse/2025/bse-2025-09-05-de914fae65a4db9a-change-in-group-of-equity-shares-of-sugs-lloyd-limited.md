## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of SUGS LLOYD LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544501) will be transferred from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) effective Friday, September 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: SUGS LLOYD LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544501
- Current segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: September 19, 2025
- Market segment: SME

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will shift from restrictive Trade for Trade settlement to normal Rolling settlement, allowing for more flexible trading mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should note the change in trading segment and adjust their systems accordingly for the new settlement mechanism.

## Important Dates

- **September 19, 2025**: Effective date for transfer to Rolling segment

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment typically indicates improved liquidity conditions and allows for intraday trading, which may increase trading activity in the stock. This change removes the restrictive settlement requirements previously applicable.
