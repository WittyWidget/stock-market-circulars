## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 500 crores issued by HDFC Securities Limited on private placement basis. The securities will trade on BSE Debt segment with scrip code 730049 and ISIN INE700G14PL2, maturing on November 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each (10,000 units)
- Issue price: Rs. 492,232 per unit (face value Rs. 500,000)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- HDFC Bank Ltd as Issuing and Paying agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded under specified ISIN number INE700G14PL2

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 20, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 20, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding option for HDFC Securities Limited and additional investment avenue for debt market participants. The 3-month tenure and high credit ratings indicate low risk profile.
