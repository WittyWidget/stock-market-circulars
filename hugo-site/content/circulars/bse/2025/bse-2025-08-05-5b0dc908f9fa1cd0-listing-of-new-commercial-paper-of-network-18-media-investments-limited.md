## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Network 18 Media & Investments Limited worth Rs. 25 crores (5,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective August 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 5,000 units (Rs. 25 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,776 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- ISIN: INE870H14VO3
- Scrip code: 729960

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 5, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 5, 2025
- Redemption date: November 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding option for Network 18 Media & Investments Limited with 3-month maturity period.
