## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 1,836,333 new equity shares of Anand Rayons Limited (Scrip Code: 542721) arising from warrant conversion. The shares will be available for trading from Monday, August 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- 1,836,333 equity shares of Rs. 10 face value issued at Rs. 68 premium
- Shares issued to Non Promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Issue price: Rs. 78 per share
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE02GA01012
- Distribution numbers: 18382722 to 20219054

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new share listing
- Lock-in period compliance required for issued shares

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: May 12, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 25, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: February 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine listing of shares from warrant conversion. The lock-in period until February 2026 ensures no immediate supply pressure on the stock.
