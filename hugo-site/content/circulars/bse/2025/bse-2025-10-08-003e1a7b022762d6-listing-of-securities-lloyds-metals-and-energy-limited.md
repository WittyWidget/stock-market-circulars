## Summary

BSE has listed 3,100,000 equity shares of Lloyds Metals and Energy Limited (Scrip Code: 512455) issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The securities are permitted to trade with effect from Thursday, October 9, 2025.

## Key Points

- 3,100,000 equity shares of Rs. 1.00/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 739.00
- Issued to Non-Promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Issue price: Rs. 740.00/- per share
- Distinctive Numbers: 523348233 to 526448232
- Date of Allotment: August 12, 2025
- ISIN: INE281B01032

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and permitted trading from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** August 12, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** October 9, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends:** April 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of preferentially allotted shares following warrant conversion. All 3.1 million shares are subject to lock-in until April 30, 2026, preventing immediate trading impact. The shares represent dilution through preferential allotment to non-promoters.
