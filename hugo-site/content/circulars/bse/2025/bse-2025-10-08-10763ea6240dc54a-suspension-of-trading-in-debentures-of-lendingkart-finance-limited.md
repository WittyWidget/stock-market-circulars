## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debentures of LENDINGKART FINANCE LIMITED (Security Code: INE090W07600/975166) effective October 10, 2025. The suspension is implemented due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the no-dealings date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for LFL-10.69%-XIRR-25-10-25-PVT debentures (ISIN: INE090W07600, Code: 975166)
- Suspension effective from October 10, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Record date set for October 10, 2025
- Notice number: DR-733/2025-2026
- Issued by Marian Dsouza, Assistant Vice President â€“ Listing Operations (CRD)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action-related trading suspension as per existing BSE procedures for debenture redemption and interest payment record dates.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from October 10, 2025
- Trading members must inform their clients holding or intending to trade these debentures about the suspension
- All market participants must take note of the suspension and adjust their systems accordingly

## Important Dates

- **October 8, 2025**: Circular issued
- **October 10, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **October 10, 2025**: No dealings from this date (trading suspension effective)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of specific LENDINGKART FINANCE LIMITED debentures. The suspension prevents secondary market trading but does not affect the underlying redemption and interest payment process.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders cannot exit positions through secondary market from October 10, 2025. However, those holding the debentures on record date will receive scheduled redemption proceeds and interest payment.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to block trades in security code 975166/INE090W07600 and communicate the suspension to affected clients.
