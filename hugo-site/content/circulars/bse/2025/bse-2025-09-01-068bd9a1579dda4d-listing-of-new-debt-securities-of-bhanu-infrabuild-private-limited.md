## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Bhanu Infrabuild Private Limited issued on private placement basis. The securities carry a 16.90% interest rate and will be traded in dematerialized form under ISIN INE247407013.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 1900 securities listed
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Interest Rate: 16.90% per annum
- Credit Rating: IVR BB+ Stable
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: 28/08/2025
- Interest Payment: Monthly from 01/09/2025 to 31/08/2029
- Redemption Date: 31/08/2029
- Effective Date: 01/09/2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine private placement listing of debt securities. The securities are available for trading on BSE Debt segment with standard trading parameters.
