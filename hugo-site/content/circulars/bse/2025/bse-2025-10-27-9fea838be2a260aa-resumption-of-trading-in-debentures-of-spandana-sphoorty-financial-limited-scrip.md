## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in three debenture series of SPANDANA SPHOORTY FINANCIAL LIMITED (Scrip Codes: 975811, 974793, and 974502) effective October 28, 2025. This follows the company's request to resume trading after the record date for optional accelerated redemption was fixed on October 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading will resume in debentures with scrip codes 975811, 974793, and 974502
- Effective date: October 28, 2025
- This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251006-62 dated October 6, 2025
- Previous record date for redemption was fixed as October 8, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-744/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is an operational notice regarding trading status of existing securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note that the three debenture series will be available for trading from October 28, 2025
- All trading activities must comply with standard BSE debt market regulations

## Important Dates

- **October 6, 2025**: Original exchange notice issued (No. 20251006-62)
- **October 8, 2025**: Record date for optional accelerated redemption
- **October 27, 2025**: Notice issued for trading resumption
- **October 28, 2025**: Trading resumes in debentures (scrip codes 975811, 974793, 974502)

## Impact Assessment

The resumption of trading provides liquidity to holders of these debenture series who may wish to exit or enter positions. The impact is limited to debt market participants holding or interested in Spandana Sphoorty Financial Limited's debentures. The company has completed its accelerated redemption process for some debenture holders while maintaining trading for outstanding securities.
