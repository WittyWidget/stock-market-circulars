## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in T-Bills 182TB22825 (ISIN: IN002024Y456) effective August 20, 2025, as the instrument will mature on its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in these T-Bills from the effective date.

## Key Points

- T-Bills 182TB22825 with ISIN IN002024Y456 will mature on redemption date
- Trading suspension effective from August 20, 2025
- Scrip code: 805029
- Notice issued under DR-698/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes - routine operational measure for maturing debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealing activities in the specified T-Bills from August 20, 2025
- Members should update their systems and inform clients about the trading suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 19, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: August 20, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: On or around August 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine suspension for a single maturing T-Bills instrument. Affects debt segment participants who hold or trade this specific treasury bill. No broader market implications expected.
