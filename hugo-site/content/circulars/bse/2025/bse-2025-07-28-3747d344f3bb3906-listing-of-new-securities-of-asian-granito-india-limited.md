## Summary

Asian Granito India Limited (Scrip Code: 532888) has listed 8,48,66,333 new equity shares of Rs. 10/- each on BSE, effective from July 29, 2025. These shares were issued pursuant to a Scheme of Arrangement involving multiple entities including Affil Vitrified Private Limited, Ivanta Ceramics Industries Private Limited, Crystal Ceramic Industries Limited, and several other related companies.

## Key Points

- 8,48,66,333 new equity shares of Rs. 10/- each listed
- Shares issued under comprehensive Scheme of Arrangement
- Multiple entities involved in the restructuring scheme
- ISIN: INE022I01019
- Distribution numbers: 147045317 - 231911649

No specific regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Normal trading rules and regulations apply to the newly listed shares

- **Allotment Date**: July 2, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The listing of approximately 8.49 crore new equity shares significantly increases the company's issued and paid-up capital, potentially affecting stock liquidity and market capitalization. The scheme of arrangement indicates substantial corporate restructuring involving multiple related entities, which may lead to operational synergies and improved business efficiency.
