## Summary

SMR JEWELS LIMITED has filed its Draft Offer Document for SME IPO on September 06, 2025. The document is available for public comments for 21 days as per ICDR Regulations Schedule VI requirements.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed on September 06, 2025
- Contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for at least 21 days
- Public comments invited within 21-day period
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. Standard ICDR compliance process being followed.

- Public has 21 days from September 08, 2025 to submit comments on the Draft Offer Document
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document hosted on www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- September 06, 2025: Draft Offer Document filed
- September 08, 2025: Notice date and start of comment period
- September 29, 2025: Last date for public comments (21 days from notice)

Limited impact as this is a standard SME IPO process. Affects potential investors in SMR JEWELS LIMITED and the SME segment. No immediate market operations impact.
