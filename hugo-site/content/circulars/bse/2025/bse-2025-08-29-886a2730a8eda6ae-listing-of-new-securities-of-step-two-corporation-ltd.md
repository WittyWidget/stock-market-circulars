## Summary

BSE has listed 31,47,500 new equity shares of Step Two Corporation Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531509) issued on a preferential basis to promoters and non-promoters. These shares will commence trading on Monday, September 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- 31,47,500 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 5/-
- Issue price: Rs. 15/- per share
- Allotment date: April 9, 2025
- ISIN: INE623D01015
- Distribution numbers: 5052101 to 8199600
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard listing procedure for preferential issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities for trading
- Lock-in restrictions apply as specified

## Important Dates

- **Trading commencement**: September 1, 2025
- **Allotment date**: April 9, 2025
- **Lock-in expiry**: 
  - 13,00,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 5052101 to 6352100): March 25, 2027
  - 18,47,500 shares (Dist. Nos. 6352101 to 8199600): April 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of preferential shares for a single company. The lock-in periods ensure gradual market availability of these securities.
