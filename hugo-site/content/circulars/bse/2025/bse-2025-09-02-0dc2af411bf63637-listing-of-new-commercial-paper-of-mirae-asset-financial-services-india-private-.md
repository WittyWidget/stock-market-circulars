## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Mirae Asset Financial Services (India) Private Limited on private placement basis. The listing comprises 1000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each, totaling Rs. 50 crores, with a CRISIL A1+ rating and maturity date of December 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5 lakhs each (1000 units) listed on BSE debt segment
- CRISIL A1+ credit rating
- Issue price: Rs. 4,90,905.50 per unit
- Maturity period: 98 days (September 1 to December 8, 2025)
- Trading in dematerialized form only under ISIN INE0JRU14263
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. Standard listing procedures apply.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 1, 2025
- Listing Date: September 2, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs with high credit rating (A1+). The instrument provides institutional investors with a short-term investment option backed by strong credit quality.
