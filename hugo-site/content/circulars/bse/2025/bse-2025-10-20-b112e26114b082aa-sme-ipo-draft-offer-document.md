## Summary

BSE has announced the availability of the Draft Offer Document for Fractal Industries Limited's SME IPO on the exchange website. The document is filed with BSE SME Exchange and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018. The draft document will remain available for at least 21 days for public comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Fractal Industries Limited on October 18, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for public review and comments
- Contains all mandatory disclosures per SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018 Schedule VI
- Public comments can be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Nitinkumar Pujari, AVP, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notification for SME IPO draft offer document filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Draft Offer Document must be hosted on SME website for minimum 21 days
- Document available at: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public stakeholders may submit comments within 21 days from notice date (October 20, 2025)
- Comments to be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date**: October 18, 2025 - Fractal Industries Limited filed Draft Offer Document
- **Notice Date**: October 20, 2025 - BSE circular issued
- **Comment Deadline**: November 10, 2025 - Last date for public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - affects only potential investors and stakeholders interested in Fractal Industries Limited's SME IPO.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to BSE SME segment; routine procedural step in SME IPO process.

**Stakeholder Impact**: Provides transparency for potential investors to review offer details and submit feedback before final offer document is approved.
