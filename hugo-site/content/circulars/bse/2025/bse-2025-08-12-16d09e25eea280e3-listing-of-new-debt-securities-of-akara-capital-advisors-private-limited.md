## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited issued on private placement basis. The securities have a face value of Rs 1 lakh each with 2,850 units listed, carrying a 12.5% annual interest rate and maturing on December 27, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2,850 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 976868 (ACAPL-12.5%-27-12-28-PVT)
- ISIN: INE08XP07324
- Face Value: Rs 1,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs 95,410.96 per security
- Credit Rating: ICRA BBB (Stable)
- Interest Rate: 12.5% per annum (monthly payment)
- Date of Allotment: August 8, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum on BSE website for additional details

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: August 12, 2025
- Date of Allotment: August 8, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 27, 2028
- Interest Payment: Monthly

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine private placement debt listing. The securities will provide additional investment options in the debt segment with ICRA BBB stable rating.
