## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of SK Minerals & Additives Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544584, symbol SKM, and ISIN number INE13YH01017. The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- SK Minerals & Additives Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544584
- Trading Symbol: SKM
- ISIN Number: INE13YH01017
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the new listing and prepare for trading once the listing date is announced
- Members to await separate notice for listing date and security details

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 16, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new company listing announcement for the SME segment. The impact is limited to trading members who will need to update their systems with the new scrip information once the listing date is confirmed. No immediate action is required until the listing date is announced. The announcement provides advance notice to market participants about the upcoming availability of this security for trading.
