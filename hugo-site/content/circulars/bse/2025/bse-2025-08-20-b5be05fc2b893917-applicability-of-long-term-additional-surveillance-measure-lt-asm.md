## Summary

BSE has updated the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective August 21, 2025. The circular includes additions of new securities, movement of existing securities between ASM stages, and removal of one security from the framework.

## Key Points

- Two new securities added to LT-ASM Stage I: Aadi Industries Ltd and Cybele Industries Ltd
- Two securities moved to higher ASM Stage II: Bharat Global Developers Ltd and Stallion India Fluorochemicals Ltd
- One security (Achyut Healthcare Ltd) removed from LT-ASM due to inclusion in ESM Framework
- Consolidated list shows 16+ securities across different ASM stages
- Changes take effect from August 21, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

Updated classification of securities under the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure framework with specific stage assignments and movements between surveillance categories.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must comply with enhanced surveillance requirements for listed securities
- Securities in higher ASM stages subject to stricter trading controls
- Monitoring of price movements and volumes for affected securities

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 21, 2025 - All LT-ASM changes become applicable

## Impact Assessment

Moderate impact on affected securities with enhanced surveillance leading to potential reduced liquidity and increased compliance requirements for market participants trading these stocks.
