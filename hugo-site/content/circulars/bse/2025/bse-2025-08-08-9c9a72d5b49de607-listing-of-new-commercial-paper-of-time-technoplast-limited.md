## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 10 crore issued by Time Technoplast Limited on private placement basis. The securities are rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper face value: Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total quantity: 200 units (Rs. 10 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,90,822.50 per unit
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Scrip Code: 729983, Scrip ID: TTL80825
- ISIN: INE508G14HW3
- Issuing and Paying Agent: IDBI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 8, 2025
- Listing Date: August 8, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 7, 2025
- Tenure: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate good creditworthiness of the issuer. Trading will be limited to institutional and high net worth investors due to high denomination requirement.
