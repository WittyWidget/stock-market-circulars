## Summary

BSE has imposed additional exposure margin on RBL Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 540065, ISIN: INE976G01028) under the Market-Wide Position Limit (MWPL) framework for October 2025. This measure is implemented to manage concentration risk and prevent excessive position buildup in the security.

## Key Points

- RBL Bank Limited is subject to additional exposure margin under MWPL framework
- Scrip Code: 540065
- ISIN: INE976G01028
- Effective for October 2025
- Only one security listed in the annexure

## Regulatory Changes

The exchange has activated MWPL surveillance measures for RBL Bank, which triggers additional margin requirements beyond standard exposure margins. This is a risk mitigation tool used when market-wide positions in a security approach concerning levels.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must collect additional exposure margin from clients holding positions in RBL Bank Limited
- Increased margin requirements apply to both existing and new positions
- Members must ensure adequate margin collection and monitoring systems are in place
- Non-compliance may result in penalty or position squaring off

## Important Dates

- Effective Period: October 2025
- Circular Date: October 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact:** Traders and investors in RBL Bank will face higher margin requirements, increasing the cost of carrying positions. This may lead to reduced trading volumes and position unwinding.

**Market Impact:** The MWPL trigger indicates significant concentration in RBL Bank positions across the market, potentially signaling elevated volatility or corporate developments requiring closer monitoring.

**Operational Impact:** Brokers must adjust margin collection systems and communicate increased requirements to clients holding RBL Bank positions, potentially leading to margin calls.
