## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 90,000 new equity shares of Global Health Ltd (Scrip Code: 543654) under the company's Employee Long Term Share Based Incentive Plan 2024. The shares will be available for trading from August 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- 90,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each fully paid up
- Issued under 'GHL Employee Long Term Share Based Incentive Plan 2024'
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 268680383 to 268770382
- ISIN: INE474Q01031

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard ESOP share listing under existing frameworks.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members can commence trading in these securities from August 26, 2025
- All 90,000 shares are subject to lock-in restrictions
- Standard ESOP compliance requirements apply

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: June 26, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 26, 2025
- **Lock-in Period**: One year from allotment date (until June 26, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The 90,000 shares represent a small addition to Global Health Ltd's equity base and are subject to standard one-year lock-in period. This is a routine corporate action under the company's employee incentive program with no immediate trading implications due to lock-in restrictions.
