## Summary

Manba Finance Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE's Debt segment through private placement. The securities carry a 10.95% annual interest rate with monthly interest payments and mature on October 20, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 5,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 977040, Scrip ID: 1095MBFL27
- ISIN: INE939X07234
- Credit Rating: CARE BBB+/Positive
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Interest Rate: 10.95% per annum (monthly payments)
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE939X07234
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum on BSE website for additional details
- Contact debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 20, 2025
- Listing Date: August 21, 2025
- First Interest Payment: September 20, 2025
- Interest Payment Period: Monthly from September 20, 2025 to October 20, 2027
- Redemption Date: October 20, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing for a single company. The securities are privately placed with limited quantity (5,000 units) and will primarily affect institutional investors in the debt market segment.
