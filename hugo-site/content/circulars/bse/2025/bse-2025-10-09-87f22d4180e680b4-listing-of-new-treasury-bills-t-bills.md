## Summary

BSE announces the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) in the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS category, effective October 10, 2025. The T-Bills have tenors of 91 days, 182 days, and 364 days with maturity dates ranging from January 2026 to October 2026.

## Key Points

- Three new Treasury Bills to be listed on BSE effective October 10, 2025
- All securities listed in G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS
- Market lot of 1 for all three T-Bills
- 91-day T-Bill (Scrip: 805127, ISIN: IN002025X281) maturing January 8, 2026
- 182-day T-Bill (Scrip: 805128, ISIN: IN002025Y289) maturing April 9, 2026
- 364-day T-Bill (Scrip: 805129, ISIN: IN002025Z286) maturing October 8, 2026
- Trading will be suspended two working days prior to maturity/redemption date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new government securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the securities will not be available for trading two working days before maturity/redemption date (T-2 trading days, excluding bank holidays)
- Trading members should contact BSE at 2272 8352/5753/8597 for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: October 10, 2025
- **91-day T-Bill Maturity**: January 8, 2026 (Symbol: 91TB080126)
- **182-day T-Bill Maturity**: April 9, 2026 (Symbol: 182TB90426)
- **364-day T-Bill Maturity**: October 8, 2026 (Symbol: 364TB81026)
- **Trading Suspension**: Two working days before respective maturity dates

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative notification for the listing of government treasury bills. The addition of these short-term government securities provides trading members with additional debt instruments for portfolio management and liquidity operations. No significant operational changes required for market participants.
