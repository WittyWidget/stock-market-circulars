## Summary

BSE announces the introduction of Futures & Options contracts on two individual securities in the Equity Derivatives segment, effective from August 18, 2025. The contracts will be available for Nuvama Wealth Management Ltd (NWML) and Suzlon Energy Ltd (SUEL).

## Key Points

- Two new F&O contracts being introduced: Nuvama Wealth Management Ltd and Suzlon Energy Ltd
- Trading commences August 18, 2025
- Contracts follow standard 7-1-7 strike structure (7 ITM, 1 ATM, 7 OTM strikes minimum)
- Contract details available in derivatives master file from end of day August 14, 2025

## Contract Specifications

**Nuvama Wealth Management Ltd (NWML)**
- Scrip Code: 543988
- Market Lot: 75
- Strike Price Interval: ₹100
- Minimum Strikes: 7-1-7 structure

**Suzlon Energy Ltd (SUEL)**
- Scrip Code: 532667
- Market Lot: 8,000
- Strike Price Interval: ₹1
- Minimum Strikes: 7-1-7 structure

## Important Dates

- **August 14, 2025**: Contract master file generation
- **August 18, 2025**: Trading commencement date

## Impact Assessment

The introduction of F&O contracts on these securities will provide enhanced trading and hedging opportunities for market participants. The significantly different lot sizes (75 vs 8,000) reflect the different price ranges and market capitalizations of the underlying securities.
