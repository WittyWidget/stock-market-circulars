## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of NATRINAI VENTURES LIMITED for public review and comments. The document was filed on August 11, 2025 and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by NATRINAI VENTURES LIMITED on August 11, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Public comments sought within 21 days from notice date
- Document contains all required disclosures under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

No regulatory changes announced. Standard SME IPO process following ICDR Regulations.

- Public can review and submit comments on the Draft Offer Document within 21 days

- Document hosted on www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- Filing Date: August 11, 2025
- Notice Date: August 12, 2025
- Comment Period: 21 days from August 12, 2025 (until September 2, 2025)

Limited impact to SME segment participants and potential investors in NATRINAI VENTURES LIMITED. Standard IPO process with no market-wide implications. Investors and market participants can review the draft document and provide feedback during the comment period.
