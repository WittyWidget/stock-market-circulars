## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in commercial paper of Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited (Scrip Code: 729916, ISIN: INE975F14B59) effective August 4, 2025, due to the instrument reaching its redemption date.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper KMIL-5-8-25-CP will mature on the redemption date
- Scrip Code: 729916
- ISIN: INE975F14B59
- Trading suspension effective from August 4, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-687/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational procedure for maturing debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial paper from August 4, 2025
- All exchange members are required to take note of this suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 1, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective**: August 4, 2025
- **Commercial Paper Maturity**: On redemption date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine suspension due to instrument maturity. Only affects holders and traders of this specific commercial paper. No broader market implications expected.
