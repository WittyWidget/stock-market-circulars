## Summary

BSE has issued the demat auction settlement list for Settlement No. 696 dated August 19, 2025. The list contains securities from various companies with specific quantities that need to be settled through the demat auction process.

- Settlement Number: 696
- Settlement Date: August 19, 2025
- Total securities listed: Over 80 different scrips
- Quantities range from 1 share to over 1.2 million shares
- Includes major companies like Ashok Leyland, Bajaj Finance, Bharti Airtel, Hindalco, Tata Steel

- Settlement Date: August 19, 2025
- All settlements must be completed on the specified date

This is a routine demat auction settlement that affects multiple securities. The settlement process ensures proper delivery of securities that may have failed in regular settlements. The wide range of companies and quantities indicates normal market operations requiring demat auction resolution.
