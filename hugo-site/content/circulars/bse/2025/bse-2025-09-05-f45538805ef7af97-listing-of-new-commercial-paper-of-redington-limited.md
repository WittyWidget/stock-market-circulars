## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Redington Limited on private placement basis worth Rs. 100 crore (2000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) with effect from September 5, 2025. The instrument has a maturity period of 25 days.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- face value each
- Total quantity: 2000 units (Rs. 100 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 497,907.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730161, ISIN: INE891D14A38
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading in dematerialised form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialised form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 4, 2025
- Listing Date: September 5, 2025
- Redemption Date: September 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term commercial paper listing with 25-day maturity. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+) indicate low credit risk for investors.
