## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in two government securities - 823GUJ2025 (ISIN: IN1520150047) and 823MH2025 (ISIN: IN2220150089) - effective September 4, 2025, as they reach their redemption date.

## Key Points

- Two government securities will be suspended from trading
- 823GUJ2025 (Scrip Code: 810584, ISIN: IN1520150047)
- 823MH2025 (Scrip Code: 810589, ISIN: IN2220150089)
- Suspension effective from September 4, 2025
- Reason: Securities reaching maturity on redemption date

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced - this is a routine procedural suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the mentioned government securities from September 4, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the suspension
- No new transactions should be initiated in these securities

## Important Dates

- **September 3, 2025**: Notice issued
- **September 4, 2025**: Trading suspension becomes effective
- **Redemption Date**: Securities mature (specific date not mentioned)

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this is a routine suspension due to securities reaching maturity. Affects only traders dealing in these specific government securities in the debt segment. No broader market implications expected.
