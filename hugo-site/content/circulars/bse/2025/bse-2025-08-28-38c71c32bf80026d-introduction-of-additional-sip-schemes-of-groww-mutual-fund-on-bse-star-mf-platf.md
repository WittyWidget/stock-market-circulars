## Summary

BSE has announced the introduction of additional SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) schemes for GROWW MULTICAP FUND on the BSE StAR MF platform. This addition is effective immediately and expands the mutual fund investment options available to investors on the platform.

## Key Points

- Additional SIP schemes for GROWW MULTICAP FUND introduced on BSE StAR MF platform
- Effective immediately from August 28, 2025
- Communication received from GROWW MUTUAL FUND regarding the addition
- Continuation of previous notice No. 20250107-51

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes involved. This is an operational enhancement to the existing BSE StAR MF platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements mentioned for market participants.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 28, 2025 (immediate effect)
- **Notice Date**: August 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update that enhances investment options for mutual fund investors. The impact is minimal and primarily beneficial for investors seeking systematic investment options in GROWW MULTICAP FUND through the BSE platform.
