## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debentures of DMI Finance Private Limited effective August 7, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date for interest payment and redemption of its non-convertible debentures.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to DMI Finance Private Limited debentures (Code: INE604O07175)
- Debenture series: DMIFFPL-9.75%-22-8-25-PVT (975221)
- No dealings permitted from August 7, 2025
- Suspension related to corporate action processing

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. Standard trading suspension procedure for corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from August 7, 2025
- All exchange participants must comply with the trading restriction
- Members required to take note of the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: August 7, 2025 (for interest payment and redemption)
- **No Dealings From**: August 7, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact restricted to holders and traders of DMI Finance Private Limited debentures. The suspension facilitates proper processing of interest payment and redemption for eligible debenture holders as per the record date mechanism.
