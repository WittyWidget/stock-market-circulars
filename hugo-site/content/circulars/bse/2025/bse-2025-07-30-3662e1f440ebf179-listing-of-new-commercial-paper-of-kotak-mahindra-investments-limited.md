## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited worth Rs. 375 crores (7,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on private placement basis. The commercial paper has a face value of Rs. 5 lakh per unit with an issue price of Rs. 4,99,411 and will mature on August 6, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Quantity: 7,500 units (Total value: Rs. 375 crores)
- Scrip Code: 729934, Scrip ID: KMIL30725
- ISIN: INE975F14B67
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,99,411 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Issuing and Paying agent: Kotak Mahindra Bank Ltd

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 30, 2025
- Trading Commencement: July 30, 2025
- Redemption Date: August 6, 2025
- Maturity Period: 7 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing with very short tenure (7 days). The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk. The small issue size and short maturity make this a standard money market instrument with limited broader market implications.
