## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper of ONGC Petro additions Limited (Scrip Code: 729298, ISIN: INE163N14527) effective October 27, 2025. The suspension is due to the commercial paper reaching its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this instrument from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Company: ONGC Petro additions Limited
- Scrip Code: 729298
- ISIN: INE163N14527
- Instrument: Commercial Paper (OPAL-28-10-25-CP)
- Trading suspension effective from: October 27, 2025
- Notice Reference: DR-743/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice for instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:
- Cease all trading activities in the specified Commercial Paper from October 27, 2025
- Update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- Ensure clients are informed about the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: October 27, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: October 28, 2025 (implied)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a standard operational notice affecting a single debt instrument. The suspension is due to natural maturity of the commercial paper.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Only affects trading members dealing with this specific commercial paper. No broader market implications.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific commercial paper who will receive redemption proceeds on maturity date.
