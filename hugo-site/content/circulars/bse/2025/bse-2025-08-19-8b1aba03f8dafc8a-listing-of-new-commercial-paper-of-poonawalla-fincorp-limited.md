## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of POONAWALLA FINCORP LIMITED on the debt segment. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a total value of Rs. 35 crores (7,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each).

## Key Points

- 7,000 commercial paper units of Rs. 5 lakh each listed on BSE debt segment
- Securities issued on private placement basis
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,123 per unit (face value Rs. 5,00,000)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- ISIN: INE511C14YV3, Scrip Code: 730040
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- ICICI Bank Ltd serves as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 18, 2025
- Listing Date: August 19, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding option for POONAWALLA FINCORP LIMITED and investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
