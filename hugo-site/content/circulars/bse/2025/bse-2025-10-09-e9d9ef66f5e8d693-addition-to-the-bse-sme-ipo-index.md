## Summary

BSE announces the addition of DSM Fresh Foods Limited (Exchange ticker: 544568) to the BSE SME IPO Index effective October 10, 2025. The company is being listed on BSE on October 9, 2025, and will be included in the index at the market open on the following day.

## Key Points

- DSM Fresh Foods Limited (544568) listed on BSE on Thursday, October 9, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE SME IPO Index effective at market open on Friday, October 10, 2025
- This action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Notice references previous communication (Notice No. 20251008-53)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index constituent update following a new SME listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and portfolios benchmarked to the BSE SME IPO Index may need to adjust holdings to reflect the new constituent.

## Important Dates

- **October 9, 2025**: DSM Fresh Foods Limited listing on BSE
- **October 10, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE SME IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine index maintenance action following a new SME IPO listing.

**Operational Impact**: Index tracking funds and ETFs benchmarked to the BSE SME IPO Index will need to incorporate the new constituent. The action is included in the corporate action file to facilitate automated system updates.

**Investor Impact**: Investors following the BSE SME IPO Index should note the new constituent. For additional queries, BSE Index Services can be contacted at bseindex@bseindia.com.
