## Summary

FEDBANK FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED has announced part redemption of its non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE007N07058) along with interest payment. The record date is set for October 30, 2025, and trading in these debentures will commence with a reduced face value from the same date.

## Key Points

- Company: FEDBANK FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED
- Security: FFSL-8.50%-RESET RATE-14-8-28 (Code: 975918)
- ISIN: INE007N07058
- Record Date: October 30, 2025
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 68,750/- per debenture
- Effective Date: October 30, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-746/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trading in the company's debentures will be conducted with the reduced face value effective from October 30, 2025
- The debentures will trade at the new face value of Rs. 68,750/- per debenture from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **October 24, 2025**: Notice date
- **October 30, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and part redemption
- **October 30, 2025**: Effective date for reduced face value trading (Settlement DR-746/2025-2026)

## Impact Assessment

**Impact on Debenture Holders**: Existing holders of FEDBANK FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED debentures will receive partial redemption proceeds along with interest payment. The face value per debenture will be reduced to Rs. 68,750/-, affecting the nominal value of holdings.

**Trading Impact**: From October 30, 2025, all trading in these debentures will reflect the reduced face value, which trading members and investors must account for in their transactions and valuations.

**Market Segment**: This action is specific to the debt segment and affects holders of the particular ISIN INE007N07058.
