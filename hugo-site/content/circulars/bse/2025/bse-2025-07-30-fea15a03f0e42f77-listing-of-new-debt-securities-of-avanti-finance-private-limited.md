## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Avanti Finance Private Limited on its debt segment effective July 30, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2,500 securities listed
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Interest Rate: 10.90% per annum
- Credit Rating: CRISIL BBB/Stable
- ISIN: INE0BNQ07139
- Scrip Code: 976962

## Security Details

- **Issue Date**: July 29, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: July 30, 2026
- **Interest Payment**: Quarterly
- **Trading**: Dematerialized form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Market Lot**: 1

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum on BSE website
- Contact debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 29, 2025
- **Listing Date**: July 30, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: July 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The securities offer competitive interest rate of 10.90% with BBB/Stable rating, providing institutional investors another debt investment option.
