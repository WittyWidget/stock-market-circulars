## Summary

BSE has announced updates to the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective October 09, 2025. Six securities have been newly shortlisted for inclusion in the ST-ASM framework, while four securities are moving out of the framework. No securities are being moved to higher or lower ASM stages during this update.

## Key Points

- 6 securities newly added to Short Term 5/15/30 Days ASM Framework
- 4 securities moving out of ST-ASM Framework
- Changes effective from October 09, 2025
- No securities moving to higher or lower ASM stages
- Some securities moving out due to inclusion in other surveillance frameworks (LT-ASM, ESM)

## Securities Added to ST-ASM (Stage I)

**New Additions:**
1. Futuristic Solutions Ltd (534063, INE241F01011)
2. Indbank Merchant Banking Services Ltd (511473, INE841B01017)*
3. Indraprastha Medical Corporation Ltd (532150, INE681B01017)*
4. Orbit Exports Ltd (512626, INE231G01010)*
5. Rajnish Retail Ltd (530525, INE786J01033)
6. Transglobe Foods Ltd (519367, INE213P01027)

*As per NSE listing

## Securities Exiting ST-ASM Framework

**Moving Out:**
1. Consolidated Construction Consortium Ltd (532902, INE429I01024)
2. Dhanvantri Jeevan Rekha Ltd (531043, INE239F01015) - Moving to ESM Framework
3. Sujala Trading & Holdings Ltd (539117, INE029H01016) - Moving to LT-ASM Framework
4. Tirupati Foam Ltd (540904, INE115G01015)

## Regulatory Framework

The Short Term ASM framework is designed to alert investors about securities displaying abnormal price movements or unusual trading patterns. Securities under this framework are subject to:
- Enhanced surveillance monitoring
- Stage-wise progression (5/15/30 days)
- Potential trading restrictions
- Additional disclosure requirements

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** October 09, 2025
- **Circular Date:** October 08, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact:**
- Securities added to ST-ASM will face stricter surveillance and potential liquidity constraints
- Investors should exercise caution when trading these securities
- Higher margins or trade-for-trade segments may be applicable

**Market Participants:**
- Traders and investors holding positions in affected securities should review their portfolios
- Brokers need to update their systems to reflect new surveillance classifications
- Enhanced due diligence required for transactions in listed securities

**Transition Notes:**
- Securities marked with specific symbols indicate SME scrips (@) or T+0 scrips (~)
- Some securities transitioning to stricter frameworks (LT-ASM, ESM) indicate persistent concerns

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must adhere to revised trading parameters for affected securities
- Brokers should communicate changes to clients holding or trading these securities
- Enhanced reporting and monitoring requirements apply to securities under ST-ASM
- Investors should be aware of applicable margins and settlement obligations
