## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of SHIVCHEM AGRO LIMITED on its SME platform for public comments. The document was filed on September 29, 2025 and contains disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations. Public comments are invited within 21 days from the notice date.

## Key Points

- Company: SHIVCHEM AGRO LIMITED
- Draft Offer Document filed on September 29, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Contains disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available for minimum 21 days for public review
- Comments to be submitted at equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice as per ICDR Regulations for SME IPO process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public can review the Draft Offer Document hosted on BSE SME website
- Comments, if any, must be submitted within 21 days from October 06, 2025 (deadline: October 27, 2025)
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document Filing Date: September 29, 2025
- Notice Date: October 06, 2025
- Public Comment Period: 21 days from October 06, 2025
- Comment Deadline: October 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact - applies only to SHIVCHEM AGRO LIMITED's proposed SME IPO. This is a standard procedural step in the IPO process allowing public scrutiny of the draft offer document. No immediate market impact. Investors interested in the SME IPO can review the document and provide feedback during the comment period.
