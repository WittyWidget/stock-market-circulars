## Summary

BSE has announced updates to the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective July 31, 2025. The circular identifies securities being newly included in LT-ASM, those moving between ASM stages, securities exiting the framework, and provides a consolidated list of all securities under various ASM stages.

## Key Points

- 5 securities newly shortlisted for Long Term ASM Framework
- 1 security (Esha Media Research Ltd) moving to higher ASM Stage II
- 1 security (Times Guaranty Ltd) moving out of LT-ASM due to inclusion in ESM Framework
- No securities moving to lower ASM stages or direct Stage IV
- Consolidated list shows securities across ASM Stages I through IV

## Regulatory Changes

- Implementation of revised LT-ASM classifications effective July 31, 2025
- Securities marked with special indicators for T+0 scrips and various framework inclusions
- Stage-wise categorization of securities based on surveillance requirements

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must comply with enhanced surveillance measures for listed securities
- Special attention required for securities moving between different ASM stages
- Monitoring of securities marked for specific trading frameworks (ESM, GSM, IBC, Trade for Trade)

## Important Dates

- **July 31, 2025**: Effective date for all LT-ASM framework changes

## Impact Assessment

**Newly Included Securities (Stage I):**
- Befound Movement Ltd (511585)
- Infobeans Technologies Ltd (543644)
- Landmarc Leisure Corporation Ltd (532275)
- Sharda Cropchem Ltd (538666)
- Stratmont Industries Ltd (530495)

**Stage Changes:**
- Esha Media Research Ltd moving to Stage II indicates increased surveillance
- Times Guaranty Ltd exiting LT-ASM due to ESM Framework inclusion

These measures aim to enhance market integrity through additional monitoring and surveillance of securities that may pose higher risks to market stability.
