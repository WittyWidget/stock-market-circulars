## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited on private placement basis. The securities are worth Rs. 25 crores (5,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) with a 7-day tenure from July 31 to August 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 5,000 units (Rs. 25 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,99,411 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 729946, ISIN: INE975F14B75
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Kotak Mahindra Bank Ltd

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: July 31, 2025
- Trading commencement: July 31, 2025
- Redemption date: August 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term commercial paper listing with 7-day maturity. Provides short-term funding option for Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited and investment opportunity for institutional investors in highly-rated paper.
