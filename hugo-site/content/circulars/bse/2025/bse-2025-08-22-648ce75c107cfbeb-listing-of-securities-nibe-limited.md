## Summary

NIBE LIMITED (Scrip Code: 535136) has listed 204,705 equity shares of Rs. 10 each issued at a premium of Rs. 500 to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. Trading commences on August 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- 204,705 equity shares of Rs. 10 each issued at premium of Rs. 500
- Issued to promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 14296569 to 14501273
- ISIN: INE149O01018

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members can commence trading from August 25, 2025
- Lock-in period applies until February 24, 2027

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: April 26, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 25, 2025
- Lock-in Period Ends: February 24, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of shares from warrant conversion. The lock-in period until February 2027 ensures these shares cannot be traded immediately, limiting supply pressure on the stock.
