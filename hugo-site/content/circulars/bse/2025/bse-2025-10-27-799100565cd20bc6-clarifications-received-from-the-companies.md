## Summary

BSE surveillance sought clarifications from three companies regarding price/volume movements in their scrips. Utkarsh Small Finance Bank and Zelio E-Mobility Limited confirmed that all necessary disclosures have been made and movements are market-driven. NOCIL Limited announced an upcoming earnings call for Q2 & H1 FY26.

## Key Points

- **Utkarsh Small Finance Bank Limited**: Confirmed all disclosures including ongoing right issue have been made pursuant to SEBI LODR Regulation 30; no undisclosed information affecting price/volume behavior; movement is purely market-driven
- **NOCIL Limited**: Announced earnings conference call scheduled for November 3, 2025 at 11:30 AM IST to discuss Q2 & H1 FY26 operational and financial performance
- **Zelio E-Mobility Limited**: Response to BSE surveillance query regarding significant volume movement (response appears incomplete in source)
- All clarifications provided in response to BSE surveillance queries under SEBI LODR Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond to stock exchange surveillance queries regarding unusual price/volume movements
- Disclosures must be made under Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Earnings call intimations must be provided under Regulation 30(6) of SEBI LODR

## Important Dates

- **October 27, 2025**: Date of clarifications from companies
- **November 3, 2025, 11:30 AM IST**: NOCIL Limited earnings call for Q2 & H1 FY26
