## Summary

BSE has permitted trading in 2,945 new equity shares of GRASIM INDUSTRIES LTD. (Scrip Code: 500300) resulting from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Rs. 2/- each issued on rights basis. These shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Trading permitted effective October 16, 2025
- Total new shares: 2,945 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 2/- each
- Scrip Code: 500300
- ISIN: INE047A01021
- Distinctive Numbers: 680456445 to 680459389
- Shares arise from conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up
- Shares issued on rights basis
- New shares carry same rights as existing equity shares (pari-passu)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that they are permitted to trade in these new securities from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 15, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date:** October 16, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The listing involves only 2,945 shares resulting from conversion of partly paid to fully paid shares, representing a very small addition to Grasim Industries' existing equity base. This is a routine corporate action with no material effect on trading or market operations.
