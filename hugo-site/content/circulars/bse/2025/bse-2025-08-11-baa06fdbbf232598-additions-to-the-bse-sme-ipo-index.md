## Summary

BSE announces the addition of two newly listed SME companies to the BSE SME IPO Index. BLT LOGISTICS LIMITED (ticker 544474) and ESSEX MARINE LIMITED (ticker 544475) will be included in the index effective at market open on August 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two companies being added to BSE SME IPO Index
- Both companies listed on BSE SME platform on August 11, 2025
- Index inclusion effective August 12, 2025 at market open
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine index maintenance activity.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index changes are automatically processed through corporate action files.

## Important Dates

- August 11, 2025: Companies listed on BSE SME platform
- August 12, 2025: Effective date for index inclusion at market open

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a routine index addition following standard procedures for newly listed SME companies. The addition provides these stocks with increased visibility within the SME IPO index framework.
