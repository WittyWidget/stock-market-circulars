## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL) debt securities with ISIN INE114A07638 (SAIL-AK-XII-8.80%-26-10-25-PVT) effective October 23, 2025. The suspension is due to the maturity of these bonds on October 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- Debt security affected: SAIL-AK-XII-8.80%-26-10-25-PVT
- ISIN: INE114A07638
- Scrip Code: 972513
- Maturity Date: October 26, 2025
- Trading suspension effective: October 23, 2025
- Notice Number: DR-741/2025-2026
- Issuer: Steel Authority of India Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for debt security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute trades in the specified SAIL debt securities from October 23, 2025 onwards
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- No new positions should be created in this security

## Important Dates

- **October 21, 2025**: Notice issued
- **October 23, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **October 26, 2025**: Maturity date of debt securities

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine suspension affecting a single debt security issue. The suspension prevents trading errors and ensures orderly maturity settlement.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Only holders and potential traders of this specific SAIL bond series are affected. The suspension is procedural and follows standard market practice for maturing debt securities.

**Investor Impact**: Bondholders will receive maturity proceeds as per the terms of the instrument. No trading opportunity loss as the security is at maturity.
