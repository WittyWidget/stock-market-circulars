## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 2,69,20,537 new equity shares of Himalaya Food International Limited (Scrip Code: 526899) issued through a rights offering. The shares are permitted to trade on the exchange effective Tuesday, August 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- 2,69,20,537 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 3.80
- Rights basis issuance with allotment date of July 18, 2025
- ISIN: INE552B01010
- Distinctive numbers: 57872885 to 84793421
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and trading permissions.

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 18, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The rights issue increases the company's equity base but does not affect broader market operations or trading procedures.
