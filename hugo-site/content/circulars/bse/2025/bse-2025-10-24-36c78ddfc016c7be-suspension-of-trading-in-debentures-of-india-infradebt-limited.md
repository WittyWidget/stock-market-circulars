## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) of India Infradebt Limited (ISIN: INE537P07588, Code: 960203) effective October 28, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture Code: IIL-7%-12-11-25-PVT (960203)
- ISIN: INE537P07588
- Record Date: October 28, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- No dealings permitted from: October 28, 2025
- Notice Reference: DR-744/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational suspension aligned with corporate action timelines for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from October 28, 2025
- Members must ensure clients are informed about the trading suspension
- No fresh positions should be taken in these debentures

## Important Dates

- **October 24, 2025**: Notice issued by BSE
- **October 28, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment; trading suspension effective from this date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High for holders of these specific debentures. Liquidity is completely frozen from the suspension date. Investors holding these securities cannot exit positions through secondary market trading.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds and interest payment as per the terms. However, no trading flexibility exists from October 28, 2025 onwards.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trading in these debentures and communicate the suspension to affected clients.
