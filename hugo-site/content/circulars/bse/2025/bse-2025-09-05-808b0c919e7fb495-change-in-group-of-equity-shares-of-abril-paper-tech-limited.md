## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of ABRIL PAPER TECH LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544500) will be transferred from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment, shifting to M Group effective September 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: ABRIL PAPER TECH LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544500
- Current segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- SME category stock

## Regulatory Changes

Transfer from Trade for Trade segment to Rolling segment represents a change in trading restrictions, allowing for more flexible trading arrangements.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the segment change and adjust their trading systems accordingly for the new group classification.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: September 5, 2025
- Effective Date: September 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The change from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment typically indicates improved liquidity conditions and removes settlement restrictions, making the stock more accessible for regular trading activities.
