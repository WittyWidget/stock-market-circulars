## Summary

GIC Housing Finance Ltd has successfully listed new commercial paper securities worth Rs. 175 crores (3,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on the BSE debt segment. The commercial paper was issued on private placement basis and will be available for trading from August 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Quantity: 3,500 units (Total value: Rs. 175 crores)
- Scrip Code: 729974
- ISIN: INE289B14JU1
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,82,259 per unit
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 6, 2025
- **Trading Start Date**: August 7, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 27, 2026

## Trading Details

- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Ltd
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term funding for GIC Housing Finance Ltd and offers investment opportunities for institutional investors in the debt market segment.
