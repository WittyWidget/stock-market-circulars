## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of Bajaj Housing Finance Limited on its debt segment effective August 13, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with face value of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling 17,000 units.

## Key Points

- 17,000 commercial paper units of Rs. 5,00,000 each listed
- Scrip Code: 730013, Scrip ID: BHFL12825
- ISIN Number: INE377Y14BR9
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,812.50 per unit
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Central Bank of India serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 12, 2025
- Listing Date: August 13, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt instrument options for investors in the debt segment with high credit rating (A1+).
