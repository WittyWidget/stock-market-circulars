## Summary

BSE has listed Commercial Paper (CP) issued by Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited on private placement basis. The instrument comprises 5,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 250 crore. The CP is rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, with allotment and listing effective from 8th October 2025 and maturity on 15th October 2025. The issuing and paying agent is Kotak Mahindra Bank Ltd.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited
- **Instrument Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 250 crore (5,000 units Ã— Rs. 5 lakh)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,99,396.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730357
- **Scrip ID**: KMPL071025
- **Detail Name**: KMPL-15-10-25-CP
- **ISIN**: INE916D145V1 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Trading**: Only in dematerialized form
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE916D145V1
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denominations of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- The tick size for trading is 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarifications may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: 8th October 2025
- **Listing Date**: 8th October 2025
- **Redemption Date**: 15th October 2025
- **Tenure**: 7 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine short-term commercial paper listing with a very short tenure of 7 days. Commercial papers are standard money market instruments used by financial institutions for short-term funding requirements.

**Operational Impact**: None for general market participants. Only relevant to debt market trading members and institutional investors dealing in commercial papers. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+) indicate minimal credit risk for investors.
