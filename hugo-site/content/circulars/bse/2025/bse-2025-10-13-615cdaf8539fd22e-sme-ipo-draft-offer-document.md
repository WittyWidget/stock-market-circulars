## Summary

BSE has announced the availability of the Draft Offer Document for AJWA PROJECTS LIMITED's SME IPO, filed on October 09, 2025. The document is available on the exchange's website for public review and comments for a period of 21 days as per regulatory requirements.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by AJWA PROJECTS LIMITED on October 09, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available for public review for minimum 21 days from notice date
- Hosted on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard filing under existing ICDR (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Draft Offer Document must remain available on exchange website for at least 21 days
- Document must contain all disclosures specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments period of 21 days from October 13, 2025 (notice date)
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **October 09, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed
- **October 13, 2025**: Notice date and start of public comment period
- **November 03, 2025**: End of 21-day public comment period (approximately)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine SME IPO filing with no immediate impact on existing market operations.

**Segment**: SME segment only

**Investor Impact**: Informational for potential investors interested in AJWA PROJECTS LIMITED's upcoming SME IPO. Provides opportunity for public to review draft prospectus and submit comments before final offer document is filed.

**Operational Impact**: None for existing market participants. Standard procedural filing for new listing candidate.
