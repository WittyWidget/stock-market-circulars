## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in Treasury Bill 91TB010825 (ISIN: IN002025X059) effective July 30, 2025, due to its maturity on the redemption date.

## Key Points

- T-Bill 91TB010825 with ISIN IN002025X059 will mature on July 30, 2025
- Trading suspension takes effect from July 30, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-684/2025-2026
- Scrip code: 805058

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine operational notice for debt security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified T-Bill from July 30, 2025
- All market participants should update their systems to reflect the trading suspension

## Important Dates

- **July 29, 2025**: Notice date
- **July 30, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date and T-Bill maturity date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only one T-Bill reaching maturity. Standard operational procedure for debt securities with no broader market implications.
