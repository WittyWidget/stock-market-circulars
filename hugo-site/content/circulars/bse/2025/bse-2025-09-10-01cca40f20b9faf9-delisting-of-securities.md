## Summary

BSE has announced the delisting of 22 debt scrip codes from various companies, effective September 11, 2025. These securities were previously suspended and are being delisted due to maturity and/or record dates fixed for redemption.

## Key Points

- 22 debt scrip codes will be delisted effective September 11, 2025
- All securities were previously suspended from trading
- Delisting is due to maturity and/or redemption record dates
- Affects debt securities from 19 different companies
- Trading members are advised to take note of these delistings

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes - this is routine delisting procedure for matured/redeemed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must take note of the delisted scrip codes
- No trading will be permitted in these securities after September 10, 2025
- Members should update their systems to reflect the delisted status

## Important Dates

- **September 11, 2025**: Effective date for delisting of all 22 debt scrip codes
- **September 10, 2025**: Last trading day for these securities

## Impact Assessment

Routine administrative action with minimal market impact. The delisting affects debt securities that were already suspended from trading due to maturity or redemption processes. This represents normal market operations for expired debt instruments and does not indicate any negative developments for the underlying companies.
