## Summary

BSE has announced surveillance measures for securities under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC) framework, effective August 14, 2025. The circular outlines various stages of surveillance and provides consolidated lists of affected securities.

## Key Points

- Lakshmi Precision Screws Ltd (Scrip Code: 506079) moves to Stage 1 of Additional Surveillance Measure under IBC
- Multiple securities remain under Stage 0 (Receipt of corporate announcement/disclosure)
- No securities are moving between Stage I and Stage II currently
- Consolidated list includes 14+ companies under various IBC surveillance stages

## Regulatory Changes

- Implementation of staged surveillance framework for IBC-related companies
- Enhanced monitoring for securities under insolvency resolution process
- Categorization based on IBC proceeding status and corporate disclosures

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies under IBC surveillance must comply with enhanced disclosure requirements
- Trading members must be aware of surveillance status when dealing with affected securities
- Regular monitoring and reporting of corporate announcements for Stage 0 securities

## Important Dates

- **August 14, 2025**: Effective date for all surveillance measure changes
- Ongoing monitoring for securities in various IBC stages

## Impact Assessment

- Enhanced scrutiny for securities under insolvency proceedings
- Potential impact on trading volumes and liquidity for affected stocks
- Increased transparency requirements for companies in financial distress
- Market participants need to factor in surveillance status for investment decisions
