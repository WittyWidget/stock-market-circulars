## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Asirvad Microfinance Limited on its Debt segment effective October 15, 2025. The securities consist of 2,500 units of non-convertible debentures issued on private placement basis, carrying an interest rate of 8.55% per annum with a three-year tenure.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2,500 units of debt securities
- Scrip Code: 977211
- Scrip ID: 855AML27
- ISIN: INE516Q08489
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Interest Rate: 8.55% per annum (paid annually)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AA-/Stable
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Trading: Dematerialised form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE516Q08489
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all trades
- Clarifications can be sought from BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 14, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 15, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually on October 14 (2026-2027)
- **Redemption Date**: October 14, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it involves private placement of debt securities for a specific issuer. The listing expands the debt securities available on BSE's platform and provides liquidity options for investors holding Asirvad Microfinance debentures. The CRISIL AA-/Stable rating indicates high credit quality. Impact is limited to debt market participants, institutional investors, and existing stakeholders of Asirvad Microfinance Limited.
