## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debentures of RENEW WIND ENERGY (JAMB) PRIVATE LIMITED effective August 11, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date for interest payment and redemption of its non-convertible debentures.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to RENEW WIND ENERGY (JAMB) PRIVATE LIMITED debentures (ISIN: INE361P07013, Code: 975843)
- Record date set for August 11, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Trading members advised not to deal in these debentures from August 11, 2025
- Debenture carries 10.80% interest rate with maturity date August 22, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from the effective date
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: August 11, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: August 11, 2025
- **Debenture Maturity**: August 22, 2025
- **Circular Date**: August 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this affects only one company's debt securities. The suspension is temporary and related to routine corporate action for interest payment and redemption. Trading members holding or planning to trade these specific debentures need to adjust their strategies accordingly.
