## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Adani Enterprises Ltd on private placement basis on the BSE Debt segment effective October 28, 2025. The instrument consists of 100 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each, with credit ratings of ACUITE A1+ and CARE A1+.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Quantity: 100 units (total value Rs. 5 crore)
- Scrip Code: 729140
- Scrip ID: AEL28325
- Detail Name: AEL-25-03-26-CP
- ISIN: INE423A14WX5 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: ACUITE A1+, CARE A1+
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,85,435
- Market Lot: 1
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE423A14WX5
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded with tick size of 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 28, 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: October 27, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: October 28, 2025
- Date of Redemption: March 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper is issued on private placement basis and will be traded on the BSE Debt segment. The short-term nature of the instrument (approximately 5-month tenure) and private placement basis limits its broader market significance. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the listing for their records.
