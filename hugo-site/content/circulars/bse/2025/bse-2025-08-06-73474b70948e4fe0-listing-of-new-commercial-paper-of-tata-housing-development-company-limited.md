## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 80 crore (1,600 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued by Tata Housing Development Company Limited on private placement basis, effective August 6, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 1,600 units (Rs. 80 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,675 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip code: 729969
- ISIN: INE582L14IS4
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 5, 2025
- Listing effective date: August 6, 2025
- Redemption date: November 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides additional short-term funding option for Tata Housing Development Company Limited. HDFC Bank Ltd serves as the issuing and paying agent.
