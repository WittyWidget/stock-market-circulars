## Summary

BSE has announced updates to the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective August 20, 2025. The circular details securities being added to LT-ASM, movements between different ASM stages, and securities moving out of the framework.

## Key Points

- One new security added to LT-ASM Framework: Cargosol Logistics Ltd (543621)
- Jeena Sikho Lifecare Ltd moved to ASM Stage II within LT-ASM Framework
- Retina Paints Ltd (543902) removed from LT-ASM Framework
- No securities moved to Direct Stage IV LT-ASM Framework
- Consolidated list includes 18+ securities across different ASM stages

## Regulatory Changes

- Enhanced surveillance measures applied to securities showing price volatility or other risk factors
- Securities under LT-ASM subject to additional margin requirements and trading restrictions
- Framework includes multiple stages (I, II) with varying levels of surveillance intensity

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure compliance with enhanced margin requirements for LT-ASM securities
- Additional surveillance measures include price bands, margin requirements, and monitoring
- Members must maintain adequate risk management for securities under surveillance

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 20, 2025 - All LT-ASM framework changes take effect

## Impact Assessment

- Investors in affected securities will face enhanced margin requirements and potential trading restrictions
- Securities under LT-ASM may experience reduced liquidity due to additional compliance requirements
- Market participants must adjust risk management strategies for listed securities
- Removal of Retina Paints from framework may improve its trading conditions
