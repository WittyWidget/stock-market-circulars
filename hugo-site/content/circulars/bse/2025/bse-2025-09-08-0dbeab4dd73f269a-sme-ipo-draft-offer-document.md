## Summary

MSAFE EQUIPMENTS LIMITED has filed its Draft Offer Document on September 05, 2025, containing all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The document is made available on BSE SME website for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by MSAFE EQUIPMENTS LIMITED on September 05, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for public review
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public can review the Draft Offer Document on www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Comments must be submitted within 21 days from September 08, 2025
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document filing date: September 05, 2025
- Notice date: September 08, 2025
- Comment submission deadline: September 29, 2025 (21 days from notice)

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO process. The impact is primarily on potential investors and market participants interested in MSAFE EQUIPMENTS LIMITED's public offering. The 21-day comment period allows for public scrutiny before the IPO proceeds to the next stage.
