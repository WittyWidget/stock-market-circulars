## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and admission for trading of further securities issued by six companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be available for trading effective from Friday, October 17, 2025. The listing includes shares from AAVAS Financiers Limited, ICICI Lombard General Insurance Company Limited, NIIT Learning Systems Limited, Suprajit Engineering Ltd., Suzlon Energy Ltd., and Tejas Networks Limited. None of the newly listed shares are subject to lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- Six companies are listing further securities issued under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Trading Members are informed that these securities are admitted for trading on BSE
- Effective date for trading: Friday, October 17, 2025
- None of the listed securities have lock-in restrictions (all marked as NA)
- Total of 1,331,677 shares across all six companies
- Largest issuance: Suzlon Energy Ltd. with 1,248,000 shares (Face Value: Rs. 2)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for securities issued under existing ESOP/ESOS frameworks.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the listing of these new securities in their systems
- Updated scrip details must be incorporated for trading purposes from October 17, 2025
- Members should ensure proper identification using the provided ISIN and Scrip Codes

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 16, 2025
- **Listing and Trading Effective Date:** October 17, 2025 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low. This is a routine corporate action involving employee stock option exercises. The number of shares being listed represents a small fraction of the outstanding equity for each company.
