## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Spandana Sphoorty Financial Limited on private placement basis, effective October 27, 2025. The securities are rated IND BBB+/Negative by India Ratings and will trade in dematerialized form only on the BSE Debt segment with scrip code 977245.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Spandana Sphoorty Financial Limited
- **Scrip Code**: 977245
- **Scrip ID**: 11SSFL28
- **ISIN**: INE572J07760
- **Quantity**: 25,000 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- **Interest Rate**: 11% per annum (paid monthly)
- **Allotment Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: April 30, 2028
- **Credit Rating**: IND BBB+/Negative
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Put/Call Options**: As per Key Information Document (KID)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE572J07760
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 27, 2025
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: April 30, 2028

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis with limited market-wide implications.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant for debt market investors and institutional participants interested in NBFC sector exposure. The BBB+ rating with negative outlook indicates moderate credit quality with some concerns.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to include the new scrip for trading on the debt segment. The monthly interest payment structure provides regular income for investors.
