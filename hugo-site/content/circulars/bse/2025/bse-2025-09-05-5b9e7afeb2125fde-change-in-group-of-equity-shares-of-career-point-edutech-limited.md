## Summary

BSE has announced that Career Point Edutech Limited (Scrip Code: 544499) equity shares will be permitted for trading under Group 'B' effective Friday, September 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- Career Point Edutech Limited scrip code: 544499
- Current trading group classification changing to Group 'B'
- Change effective from September 19, 2025
- Notice issued by Deputy Vice President Ashok Kumar Singh

## Regulatory Changes

The company's equity shares will be reclassified from their current trading group to Group 'B' on BSE, which typically indicates specific trading parameters and surveillance measures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the group change and ensure compliance with Group 'B' trading requirements from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: September 5, 2025
- Effective Date: September 19, 2025 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

This group change may affect the trading dynamics of Career Point Edutech Limited shares, potentially involving different surveillance parameters and trading conditions under Group 'B' classification.
