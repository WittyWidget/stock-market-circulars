## Summary

BSE has listed two new Commercial Paper securities of Lodha Developers Limited on private placement basis, effective September 5, 2025. Both securities have face value of Rs. 5 lakh each with ICRA A1+ credit rating.

## Key Points

- Two Commercial Paper series listed: LDL-20-3-26-CP and LDL-3-12-25-CP
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per security for both series
- Quantity: 1,000 securities each (total Rs. 50 crores per series)
- Credit Rating: ICRA A1+ for both securities
- Issue dates: September 4, 2025 for both
- Redemption dates: March 20, 2026 and December 3, 2025 respectively
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Yes Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applies
- Trading members must use designated scrip codes and ISIN numbers

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: September 5, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**: 
  - LDL-20-3-26-CP: March 20, 2026
  - LDL-3-12-25-CP: December 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term funding options for Lodha Developers and investment opportunities for institutional investors in high-rated commercial paper.
