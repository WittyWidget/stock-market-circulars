## Summary

BSE has listed 2648 new debt securities issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective 06 October 2025. The securities will trade on BSE Debt segment under scrip code 977189 (NWFL300925) with ISIN INE918K07QA7. The securities carry a CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable rating, have a face value of ₹100,000, and mature on 31 August 2035.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2648 securities listed
- Scrip Code: 977189 | Scrip ID: NWFL300925
- ISIN: INE918K07QA7
- Face Value: ₹100,000 per security
- Issue Price: ₹100,000 per security
- Market Lot: 1
- Credit Rating: CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable
- Date of Allotment: 30 September 2025
- Redemption Date: 31 August 2035
- Trading mode: Dematerialised form only
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call options

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form under the specified ISIN
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx
- Contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **30 September 2025**: Date of Allotment
- **06 October 2025**: Listing and trading commencement date
- **31 August 2035**: Redemption date

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this is a routine private placement debt listing. Only affects debt market participants interested in high-rated corporate bonds with long-term maturity. The securities are traded in large denominations (₹100,000), indicating institutional investor focus. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
