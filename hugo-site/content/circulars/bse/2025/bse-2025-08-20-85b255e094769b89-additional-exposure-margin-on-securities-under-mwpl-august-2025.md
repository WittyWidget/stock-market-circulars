## Summary

BSE has imposed additional exposure margin on RBL Bank Limited (ISIN: INE976G01028, Scrip Code: 540065) under the Market Wide Position Limit (MWPL) framework for August 2025.

## Key Points

- Additional exposure margin applied to RBL Bank Limited
- Security falls under MWPL surveillance framework
- Measure implemented as risk management tool
- Affects trading positions and margin requirements

## Regulatory Changes

Implementation of additional exposure margin requirements specifically for RBL Bank Limited under existing MWPL framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must maintain additional margins for positions in RBL Bank Limited
- Enhanced monitoring of position limits required
- Risk management systems to be updated accordingly

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: August 2025 (specific date not mentioned in content)
- Applicable throughout August 2025

## Impact Assessment

Increased margin requirements will raise trading costs for RBL Bank Limited, potentially reducing speculative activity and position sizes. This measure aims to control excessive volatility and ensure market stability for the specific security.
