## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of subscriptions for Tata Silver ETF Fund of Fund, which had been temporarily suspended. This allows investors to resume making fresh investments in the fund.

## Key Points

- Subscriptions in Tata Silver ETF Fund of Fund have been resumed
- The fund was previously under temporary suspension
- Normal subscription operations have been restored

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the lifting of temporary suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Fund houses and intermediaries can process subscription requests for the Tata Silver ETF Fund of Fund as per normal procedures.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: October 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Low impact circular affecting only investors interested in Tata Silver ETF Fund of Fund. This is a positive development for investors who wish to invest in this specific fund, as subscription channels have been reopened. No broader market implications.
