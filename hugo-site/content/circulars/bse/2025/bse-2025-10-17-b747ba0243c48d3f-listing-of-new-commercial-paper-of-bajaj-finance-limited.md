## Summary

Bajaj Finance Limited has listed new Commercial Paper instruments on BSE's Debt segment effective October 17, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each. A total of 12,000 units have been listed under ISIN INE296A14C55 with credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 12,000 units of Commercial Paper
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,099 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730398
- Scrip ID: BFL151025A
- ISIN: INE296A14C55 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities to be traded under the specified ISIN number INE296A14C55

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 17, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: October 16, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: October 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: January 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification for commercial paper issued by Bajaj Finance Limited on a private placement basis. The impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect broader market operations. The 91-day commercial paper provides short-term funding for the company and offers investment opportunities for institutional investors in the debt segment.
