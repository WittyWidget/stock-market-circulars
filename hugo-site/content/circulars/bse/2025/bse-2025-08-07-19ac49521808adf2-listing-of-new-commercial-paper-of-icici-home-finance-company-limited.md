## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 285 crore issued by ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED on private placement basis. The securities will trade on BSE Debt segment from August 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5 lakh denomination each
- Total quantity: 5,700 units (Rs. 285 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,800.50 per unit
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Scrip code: 729978, ISIN: INE071G14GZ7
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- ICICI Bank Limited as issuing and paying agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size of 1 paise applicable
- Trading members must use specified ISIN number

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 7, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 7, 2025
- Redemption date: November 6, 2025
- Maturity period: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides additional short-term investment option for institutional investors in BSE debt segment.
