## Summary

Poonawalla Fincorp Limited has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 350 crores (7,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE's debt segment. The commercial paper is issued on private placement basis with 91-day maturity period.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Quantity: 7,000 units (Total value: Rs. 350 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730040
- ISIN: INE511C14YV3
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,123 per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Trading starts: August 19, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard commercial paper listing process followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 18, 2025
- Trading Start Date: August 19, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 17, 2025
- Maturity Period: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding avenue for Poonawalla Fincorp and investment option for institutional investors in the debt segment.
