## Summary

BSE announces that Kotak Active Momentum Fund will reopen for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective August 28, 2025. The fund will be available for subscription, redemption, and systematic investment plans (SIP, STP, SWP).

## Key Points

- Fund reopens for ongoing transactions on August 28, 2025
- Available services include subscription, redemption, SIP, STP & SWP facility
- Six scheme variants available covering regular and direct plans with different dividend options
- All schemes are equity category funds with unique ISIN codes

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a resumption of previously available services.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of BSE must be informed of the fund availability for client transactions.

## Important Dates

- **August 28, 2025**: Effective date for fund availability on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine operational update regarding mutual fund availability. Provides investors with renewed access to Kotak Active Momentum Fund through BSE's mutual fund platform with comprehensive investment options including systematic plans.
