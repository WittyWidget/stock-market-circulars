## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Bajaj Finance Limited on private placement basis. The securities are worth Rs. 5 lakhs each with 500 units listed, totaling Rs. 25 crores. The commercial paper carries CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ ratings.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 500 units (Total value: Rs. 25 crores)
- Scrip Code: 729887
- ISIN: INE296A14C14
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 469,457.00 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: July 28, 2025
- Allotment Date: July 25, 2025
- Redemption Date: July 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The listing provides additional short-term debt instrument options for institutional investors in the debt segment.
