## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 25 crores issued by Capri Global Capital Limited on private placement basis. The securities will trade on BSE Debt segment with effect from August 13, 2025.

## Key Points

- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 500 units
- Issue price: Rs. 4,77,395.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+
- ISIN: INE180C14565
- Scrip code: 730017
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: YES BANK LIMITED

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 13, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 13, 2025
- Redemption date: March 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding instrument for institutional investors with CRISIL A1+ rating indicating high credit quality.
