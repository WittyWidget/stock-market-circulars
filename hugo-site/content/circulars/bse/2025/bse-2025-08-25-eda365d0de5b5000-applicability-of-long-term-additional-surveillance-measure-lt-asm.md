## Summary

BSE has updated the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective August 26, 2025. Seven securities have been newly shortlisted for LT-ASM, while one security (Ladam Affordable Housing Ltd) moves to higher Stage II ASM. The circular also provides a consolidated list of all securities under various ASM stages.

## Key Points

- Seven new securities added to Long Term ASM Framework
- One security moved to higher ASM Stage II
- No securities moved to lower stages or direct Stage IV
- No securities moved out of LT-ASM framework
- Consolidated list includes securities across ASM Stages I through IV

## Regulatory Changes

- Implementation of Long Term ASM framework for newly identified securities
- Stage progression for existing securities based on surveillance criteria
- Enhanced monitoring and trading restrictions for affected securities

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must comply with LT-ASM trading conditions
- Enhanced disclosure and reporting requirements for affected securities
- Investors should be aware of additional surveillance measures

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 26, 2025
- All changes to LT-ASM framework become applicable from this date

## Impact Assessment

The LT-ASM framework may result in:
- Increased margin requirements for affected securities
- Enhanced monitoring of trading activities
- Potential impact on liquidity and trading volumes
- Additional compliance burden for market participants
- Investor awareness of heightened surveillance on these securities
