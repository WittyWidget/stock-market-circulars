## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Navi Finserv Limited on private placement basis worth Rs. 40 crore (800 units of Rs. 5 lakh each). The securities are listed on BSE Debt segment effective August 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 800 units (Rs. 40 crore total)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,88,307.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1, IND A1
- Scrip code: 729972, ISIN: INE342T14DY5
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialised form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size is 1 paise for price movements

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 6, 2025
- Listing effective: August 7, 2025
- Redemption date: November 6, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The 3-month tenure and investment grade rating indicate standard corporate financing activity.
