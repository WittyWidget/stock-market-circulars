## Summary

BSE has listed two new Commercial Paper instruments issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis, effective October 10, 2025. The instruments are listed on the BSE Debt segment with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each and carry credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+.

## Key Points

- Two Commercial Paper instruments listed for trading on BSE Debt segment
- First instrument: 500 units (Scrip Code 730237, ISIN INE01C314CS3) with redemption on December 15, 2025
- Second instrument: 800 units (Scrip Code 730208, ISIN INE01C314CP9) with redemption on December 11, 2025
- Both instruments issued on private placement basis with allotment date October 9, 2025
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit for both instruments
- Issue prices: Rs. 494,113.50 (first instrument) and Rs. 494,461.00 (second instrument)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ for both instruments
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Market lot size is 1 unit for both instruments

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 9, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date (Instrument 1)**: December 15, 2025
- **Redemption Date (Instrument 2)**: December 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of short-term debt instruments (Commercial Paper) on private placement basis. The instruments have short maturity periods (approximately 2 months) and are intended for institutional investors. The listing expands the available debt securities on BSE but does not affect broader market operations or trading patterns.
