## Summary

BSE has published an updated list of 19 approved Independent Software Vendors (ISVs) that are registered with the exchange to provide front-end trading services to trading members. The list includes vendor names, codes, addresses, and contact details.

## Key Points

- 19 ISVs are currently registered with BSE for providing front-end services
- Each vendor has assigned vendor codes ranging from 1 to 70
- Major technology providers include TCS, FIS Solutions, ION Trading, and Symphony Fintech
- Synapsewave Innovations Private Limited (formerly 63 Moons Technologies Limited) has been updated following business transfer
- Complete contact information provided for all vendors including addresses and phone numbers

## Regulatory Changes

Business transfer notification: 63 Moons Technologies Limited has transferred its ISV business to Synapsewave Innovations Private Limited, resulting in updated company name in the registry.

## Compliance Requirements

ISVs must maintain their registration with BSE to continue providing front-end trading services to members. Trading members can engage services from any of the listed approved vendors.

## Important Dates

No specific dates mentioned in this informational circular.

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is an informational update of existing approved vendors. Provides transparency for trading members seeking technology solutions and ensures compliance with BSE's vendor approval requirements.
