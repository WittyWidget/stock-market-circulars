## Summary

BSE has updated the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective August 01, 2025. Five securities have been newly shortlisted under LT-ASM Stage I, while one security (Remi Edelstahl Tubulars Ltd) has been moved out due to inclusion in ESM Framework.

## Key Points

- 5 new securities added to LT-ASM Stage I framework
- 1 security removed from LT-ASM due to ESM inclusion
- No securities moved between ASM stages
- No securities added to Direct Stage IV LT-ASM
- Consolidated list shows multiple securities across different ASM stages

## Regulatory Changes

Enhanced surveillance measures applied to newly identified securities that may pose higher risks to market integrity and investor protection.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must comply with LT-ASM requirements for affected securities
- Enhanced due diligence and monitoring required for securities under surveillance
- Specific trading restrictions may apply based on ASM stage

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 01, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The LT-ASM framework affects trading conditions for the specified securities, potentially including enhanced margin requirements, position limits, or other trading restrictions designed to mitigate risks associated with these securities.
