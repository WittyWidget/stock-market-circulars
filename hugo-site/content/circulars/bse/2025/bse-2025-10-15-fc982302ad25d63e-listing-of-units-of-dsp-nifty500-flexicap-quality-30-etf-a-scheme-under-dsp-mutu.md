## Summary

BSE announces the listing of DSP Nifty500 Flexicap Quality 30 ETF units effective October 16, 2025. This open-ended scheme tracks the Nifty500 Flexicap Quality 30 Index and will be listed in B Group with 6,564,592 units. The ETF is managed by DSP Asset Managers Private Limited and will trade in dematerialized form only with a market lot of 1 unit.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 544581
- ISIN: INF740KA1WU9
- BOLT System ID: FLEXIADD
- Total Units: 6,564,592
- Face Value: Rs.10.00 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs.10.56 per unit
- Date of Allotment: October 10, 2025
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Group: B
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only (as per SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000)

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The circular follows existing SEBI guidelines requiring mutual fund units to be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades in this ETF are executed in dematerialized form only
- Trades must be effected in unit market lot (1 unit)
- Market makers appointed must fulfill their obligations as per exchange requirements

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 16, 2025 (Thursday)
- **Trading Commencement**: October 16, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine ETF listing that adds another investment product tracking the Nifty500 Flexicap Quality 30 Index to the exchange. The listing expands investment options for market participants interested in quality-focused flexicap exposure.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Trading members need to update their systems with the new scrip details. Ten market makers have been appointed to ensure adequate liquidity.
