## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 400 crores (8,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued by Jamnagar Utilities & Power Private Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective August 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,752 per unit (discount to face value)
- Total issue size: Rs. 400 crores (8,000 units)
- Maturity period: 91 days (redemption on November 18, 2025)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Scrip code: 730044, ISIN: INE936D14386
- ICICI Bank Limited as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise for trading
- Market lot size of 1 unit

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 19, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 19, 2025
- Redemption date: November 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk. Trading restricted to institutional participants due to high denomination requirements.
