## Summary

BSE announces the listing of DSP Nifty500 Flexicap Quality 30 ETF units effective October 16, 2025. The open-ended scheme replicates the Nifty500 Flexicap Quality 30 Index and will trade in B Group with 6,564,592 units available. The ETF is managed by DSP Asset Managers Private Limited with 11 designated market makers to ensure liquidity.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 544581 (FLEXIADD)
- **ISIN**: INF740KA1WU9
- **Total Units**: 6,564,592 units
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 10.56 per unit
- **Allotment Date**: October 10, 2025
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Group**: B Group
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Investment Manager**: DSP Asset Managers Private Limited
- **Registered Office**: Mafatlal Centre, 10th Floor, Nariman Point, Mumbai 400 021

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. The listing complies with SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, requiring mutual fund units to trade exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- **Trading Members**: Must ensure all trades in this ETF are executed in dematerialized form only
- **Market Lot**: Trades must be in unit market lot (1 unit minimum)
- **Settlement**: Standard settlement procedures apply for B Group securities

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 16, 2025 (Thursday)
- **Trading Commencement**: October 16, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine ETF listing that adds another investment vehicle tracking the Nifty500 Flexicap Quality 30 Index. With 11 market makers appointed, adequate liquidity should be available.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need to update their systems with the new scrip details. The ETF follows standard trading rules for B Group securities.

**Investor Impact**: Provides investors with another passive investment option for exposure to quality stocks across market capitalizations within the Nifty 500 universe.
