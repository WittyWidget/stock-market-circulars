## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Nexus Select Trust worth Rs. 15 crores (3000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) on private placement basis. The securities carry IND A1+ credit rating and mature in 3 months.

## Key Points

- 3000 Commercial Paper units of Rs. 5,00,000 each listed on BSE Debt segment
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,842.50 per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: IND A1+ 
- ISIN: INE0NDH14080, Scrip Code: 730005
- ICICI Bank Limited appointed as Issuing and Paying agent
- Trading in dematerialized form only with tick size of 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities to be traded under specified ISIN and scrip codes

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 11, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 12, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 10, 2025
- Maturity Period: 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The securities provide short-term debt investment option for institutional investors. Standard private placement with established issuer and reputable paying agent ensures smooth settlement process.
