## Summary

BSE has listed 15,66,000 new equity shares of Bondada Engineering Limited (Scrip Code: 543971) effective from Monday, August 11, 2025. These shares were issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis through conversion of warrants at an issue price of Rs. 351.20 per share.

## Key Points

- 15,66,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at premium of Rs. 349.20
- Shares issued to Non-Promoter on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Trading permitted from August 11, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE0Q8P01029
- Distinctive Numbers: 110027486 to 111593485

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing process for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability for trading
- Lock-in period compliance required for allotted shares

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: May 31, 2025
- Trading Start Date: August 11, 2025
- Lock-in Period Ends: February 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine listing of shares through warrant conversion. The lock-in period until February 2026 ensures these shares cannot be immediately traded, providing price stability.
