## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and trading permission for 100 bonus equity shares of Tata Elxsi Ltd (Scrip Code: 500408) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares will be available for trading from September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- 100 bonus equity shares of Rs. 10/- each are being listed
- Shares were previously kept in abeyance
- ISIN: INE670A01012
- Distinctive numbers: 31177361 to 31177460
- Trading permitted from Wednesday, September 10, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - routine listing of previously held abeyance shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities availability for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Trading Start Date**: September 10, 2025
- **Notice Date**: September 9, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact due to the small number of shares (100 shares) being released from abeyance. This is a routine administrative action for bonus shares that were previously held.
