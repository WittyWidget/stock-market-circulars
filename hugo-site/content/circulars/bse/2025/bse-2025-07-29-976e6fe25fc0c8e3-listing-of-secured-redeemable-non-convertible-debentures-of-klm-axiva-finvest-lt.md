## Summary

KLM Axiva Finvest Limited has listed 10 series of secured, redeemable, non-convertible debentures (NCDs) on BSE. The debentures have face values of Rs. 1,000 each (except Series X with Rs. 2,000) and carry coupon rates ranging from 9.50% to 11.00% per annum. Total securities listed across all series amount to 13,38,661 NCDs with maturity periods extending from September 2026 to February 2032.

## Key Points

- 10 series of secured NCDs listed with varying interest payment frequencies (monthly, annually, cumulative)
- Coupon rates range from 9.50% (Series I) to 11.00% (Series IX)
- Face value of Rs. 1,000 per NCD for most series, Rs. 2,000 for Series X
- Total of 13,38,661 NCDs listed across all series
- All debentures allotted on July 28, 2025
- Credit rating: ACUITE BBB/Stable
- Market lot size: One NCD

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- NCDs are now available for trading on BSE
- Standard listing compliance requirements apply
- Interest payments scheduled as per respective series terms

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 28, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: September 1, 2025 (for most series)
- **Maturity Dates**: Range from September 1, 2026 (Series I) to February 27, 2032 (Series X)
- **Trading Commencement**: Effective from listing date

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine debt securities listing. The NCDs provide additional investment options for debt investors seeking secured instruments with varying maturity profiles and interest payment frequencies. The BBB/Stable credit rating indicates moderate credit quality, suitable for conservative debt investors.
