## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Birla Group Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis in the Debt segment. The securities are available for trading from October 28, 2025. The issue consists of 2,000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 100 crore.

## Key Points

- Issuer: Birla Group Holdings Private Limited
- Security Type: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,592.50 per unit
- Total Quantity: 2,000 units
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Scrip Code: 730440
- Scrip ID: BGHP281025
- Detail Name: BGHPL-27-1-26-CP
- ISIN: INE09OL14IB2
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE09OL14IB2
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE Debt Department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: October 28, 2025
- Listing Date: October 28, 2025
- Redemption Date: January 27, 2026
- Tenure: 91 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The securities are rated A1+ by both CRISIL and ICRA, indicating highest safety for short-term instruments. The listing expands debt market trading options for institutional investors and provides short-term funding to Birla Group Holdings Private Limited. Impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity markets.
