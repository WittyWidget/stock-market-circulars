## Summary

Kanishk Aluminium India Limited has filed its Draft Offer Document for SME IPO on July 30, 2025. The document is available on BSE SME website for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days as per ICDR Regulations.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Kanishk Aluminium India Limited on July 30, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available for public review and comments for at least 21 days
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned - standard IPO filing process under existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public to submit comments within 21 days from notice date (by August 20, 2025)
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document hosted on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- Filing Date: July 30, 2025
- Comment Period: 21 days from July 30, 2025
- Comment Deadline: August 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on SME segment as new company seeking listing. Standard regulatory process for SME IPOs with public consultation period allowing stakeholder feedback before final approval.
