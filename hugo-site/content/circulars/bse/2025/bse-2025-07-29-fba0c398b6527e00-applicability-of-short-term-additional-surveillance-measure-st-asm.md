## Summary

BSE has updated the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective July 30, 2025. Four new securities have been added to the ST-ASM framework, while two securities are moving out due to inclusion in other surveillance frameworks.

## Key Points

- 4 securities newly shortlisted in Short Term 5/15/30 Days ASM Framework
- 2 securities moving out of ST-ASM Framework
- No securities moving to higher or lower stage ASM within the framework
- Changes effective from July 30, 2025

## Securities Added to ST-ASM Framework

**New Additions (Stage I):**
- Brightcom Group Ltd (532368, INE425B01027)
- IIRM Holdings India Ltd (526530, INE670C01026)
- The Phosphate Company Ltd (542123, INE398C01016)
- Vimta Labs Ltd (524394, INE579C01029)

## Securities Removed from ST-ASM Framework

- Mirc Electronics Ltd (500279, INE831A01028)
- Panth Infinity Ltd (539143, INE945O01019) - moved to ESM Framework

## Regulatory Changes

The ST-ASM framework continues to operate with 5/15/30 days surveillance stages, with securities subject to additional monitoring and trading restrictions based on their risk profile.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** July 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The changes affect trading in the specified securities through enhanced surveillance measures. Securities in ST-ASM framework face additional monitoring, margin requirements, and trading restrictions designed to curb excessive speculation and volatility.
