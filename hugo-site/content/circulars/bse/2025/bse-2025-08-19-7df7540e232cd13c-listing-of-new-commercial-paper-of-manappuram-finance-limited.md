## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Manappuram Finance Limited on private placement basis. The instrument consists of 4,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 200 crore, with redemption period of approximately one year.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- face value each
- Total quantity: 4,000 units (Rs. 200 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 464,644.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Scrip Code: 730043, ISIN: INE522D14OP1
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Axis Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 18, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 19, 2025
- Redemption date: August 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. Provides additional short-term funding avenue for Manappuram Finance Limited and investment option for institutional investors in highly-rated commercial paper.
