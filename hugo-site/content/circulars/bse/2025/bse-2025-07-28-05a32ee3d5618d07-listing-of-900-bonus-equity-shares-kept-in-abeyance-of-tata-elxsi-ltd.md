## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 900 bonus equity shares of Tata Elxsi Ltd (Scrip Code: 500408) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares will be available for trading from Tuesday, July 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- 900 bonus equity shares of Rs. 10/- each are being released from abeyance
- Shares belong to Tata Elxsi Ltd with scrip code 500408
- ISIN: INE670A01012
- Trading commences from July 29, 2025
- Shares are distributed across 5 distinctive number ranges

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine listing of previously issued bonus shares that were held in abeyance.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities availability for trading. No specific compliance actions required beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 28, 2025
- Trading Commencement: July 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected due to the small quantity of shares (900) being released. This represents a routine administrative action to clear shares from abeyance status, allowing their normal trading on the exchange.
