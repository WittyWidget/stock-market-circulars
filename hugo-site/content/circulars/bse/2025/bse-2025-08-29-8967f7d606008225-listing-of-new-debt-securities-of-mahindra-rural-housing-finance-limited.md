## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Mahindra Rural Housing Finance Limited (MRHFL) worth ₹25 crore issued on private placement basis. The securities carry a 7.25% annual interest rate and mature on August 27, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 25,000 securities with face value of ₹1,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 977059, ISIN: INE950O07511
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA/Stable
- Issue Price: ₹1,00,000 per security
- Date of Allotment: August 28, 2025
- Interest Rate: 7.25% per annum (paid annually)
- Maturity Date: August 27, 2027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form
- Tick size for trading is 1 paise
- Trading members should refer to Placement Memorandum for detailed information

## Important Dates

- **August 28, 2025**: Date of Allotment
- **August 28, 2026 to August 27, 2027**: Interest payment period
- **August 27, 2027**: Redemption date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The securities are rated AAA/Stable by CRISIL, indicating high credit quality. The listing provides additional funding avenue for Mahindra Rural Housing Finance Limited's operations.
