## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper (CP) issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis, effective October 13, 2025. Two series of CPs have been admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, rated CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+.

## Key Points

- **First Series (ISIN: INE01C314CT1)**: 2,000 CPs (Scrip Code: 730246) allotted on October 10, 2025, maturing on December 16, 2025 with issue price of Rs. 4,91,761
- **Second Series (ISIN: INE01C314CZ8)**: 3,400 CPs (Scrip Code: 730383) allotted on October 10, 2025, maturing on November 10, 2025 with issue price of Rs. 4,97,259
- Trading only in dematerialized form with market lot size of 1
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 10, 2025 (both series)
- **Listing Date**: October 13, 2025
- **Redemption Date (Series 1)**: December 16, 2025
- **Redemption Date (Series 2)**: November 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of short-term Commercial Paper instruments on the debt segment for institutional investors. The instruments have short tenures (31 and 67 days respectively) and are rated at the highest short-term rating category (A1+), indicating strong credit quality. Trading will be limited to the debt segment with no impact on equity markets.
