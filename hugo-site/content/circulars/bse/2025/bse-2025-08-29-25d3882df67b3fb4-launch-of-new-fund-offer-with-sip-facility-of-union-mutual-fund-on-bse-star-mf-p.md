## Summary

UNION Mutual Fund is launching UNION DIVERSIFIED EQUITY ALL CAP ACTIVE FOF (Fund of Funds) with SIP facility on BSE StAR MF Platform from September 1-15, 2025. The NFO offers 6 plan variants across regular and direct plans with different dividend options.

## Key Points

- NFO period: September 1-15, 2025
- Six scheme variants available (Regular/Direct plans with Growth/IDCW options)
- Allotment date: September 22, 2025
- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on September 19, 2025
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date
- Extended trading till midnight on September 15, 2025 for lumpsum purchases only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure client funds are initiated by 9:00 AM on T+1 day or transactions will be cancelled
- Pending transactions remain in system till 3:00 PM on last day of funds payout or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Clear funds required in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on September 19, 2025 for unit allotment
- Switch transactions must receive funds on or before allotment date for consideration

## Important Dates

- NFO Start Date: September 1, 2025
- NFO End Date: September 15, 2025 (extended till midnight for lumpsum)
- Funds Deadline: September 19, 2025 (6:00 PM)
- Allotment Date: September 22, 2025
- SIP Registration Deadline: September 15, 2025 (3:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a standard mutual fund product launch. Provides additional investment option for retail investors seeking diversified equity exposure through fund-of-funds structure. No direct impact on stock prices or market operations.
