## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 2,968 bonus equity shares of ICICI Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 532174) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares will be available for trading from Wednesday, July 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- 2,968 bonus equity shares of Rs. 2/- each being listed
- Shares were previously kept in abeyance
- ISIN: INE090A01021
- Trading commences July 30, 2025
- Shares distributed across 5 different distinctive number ranges

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing of previously declared bonus shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are now listed and permitted for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: July 30, 2025 (Wednesday)
- **Notice Date**: July 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a small number of bonus shares (2,968) relative to ICICI Bank's total share capital. These shares were already allocated but kept in abeyance, now being made available for trading.
