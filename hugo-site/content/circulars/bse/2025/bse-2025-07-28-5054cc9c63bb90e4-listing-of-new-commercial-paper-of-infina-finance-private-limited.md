## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 30 crore issued by Infina Finance Private Limited on private placement basis, effective from July 28, 2025. The securities will trade in dematerialized form with a face value of Rs. 5 lakh each.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper quantity: 6,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total issue size: Rs. 30 crore
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,087 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 729915, ISIN: INE879F14LD2
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities listed and admitted to dealings on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: July 25, 2025
- Listing date: July 28, 2025
- Redemption date: October 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding avenue for Infina Finance Private Limited. Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited serves as issuing and paying agent.
