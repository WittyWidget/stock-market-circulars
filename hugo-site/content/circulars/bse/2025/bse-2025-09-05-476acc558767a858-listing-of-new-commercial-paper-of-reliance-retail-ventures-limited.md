## Summary

BSE has listed Commercial Paper worth ₹1,675 crore issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from September 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- 33,500 Commercial Paper units of ₹5 lakh each listed
- Issue price: ₹4,92,715.50 per unit (face value ₹5 lakh)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: ₹5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of ₹5 lakhs and multiples
- ICICI Bank Limited appointed as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: September 5, 2025
- Allotment Date: September 4, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The Commercial Paper provides short-term funding option for Reliance Retail Ventures Limited with 3-month tenure.
