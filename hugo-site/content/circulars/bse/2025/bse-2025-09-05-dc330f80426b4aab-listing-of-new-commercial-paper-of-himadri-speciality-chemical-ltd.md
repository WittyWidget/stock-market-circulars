## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 100 crores issued by Himadri Speciality Chemical Ltd. on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from September 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- 2000 units of commercial paper with face value Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total issue size: Rs. 100 crores
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,375.50 per unit
- Credit rating: ICRA A1+
- ISIN: INE019C14631
- Scrip Code: 730156
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 4, 2025
- Listing Date: September 5, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding avenue for Himadri Speciality Chemical Ltd. with 90-day maturity period.
