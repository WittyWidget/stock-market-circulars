## Summary

BSE has announced the availability of the Draft Offer Document of NOVUS LOYALTY LIMITED on its SME platform. The document was filed on September 30, 2025 and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The document is available for public comments for a period of at least 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by NOVUS LOYALTY LIMITED on September 30, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice as per ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Interested parties and public may review the Draft Offer Document on the BSE SME website
- Comments, if any, must be submitted within 21 days from October 06, 2025
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Draft Offer Document Filed:** September 30, 2025
- **Notice Date:** October 06, 2025
- **Public Comment Period:** 21 days from October 06, 2025 (until October 27, 2025)

## Impact Assessment

This notice has limited market impact as it pertains to a single SME company's IPO process. The availability of the draft offer document is a standard regulatory requirement under ICDR Regulations. Impact is primarily limited to potential investors, analysts, and stakeholders interested in NOVUS LOYALTY LIMITED's upcoming IPO on the SME platform.
