## Summary

BSE has admitted three new trading members to the exchange effective October 20, 2025. The new members have been granted trading rights across different segments: Bondnest Capital India Securities Private Limited (Member No. 6963) for Debt segment, Tecstox Securities Private Limited (Member No. 6964) for Cash and Equity Derivatives segments, and Siddhartha Commodities Private Limited (Member No. 5175) for Equity Derivatives segment. All memberships are subject to BSE's Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations.

## Key Points

- Three new trading members admitted to BSE on October 20, 2025
- Bondnest Capital India Securities Private Limited (Member No. 6963) - Debt segment, New Membership
- Tecstox Securities Private Limited (Member No. 6964) - Cash/Equity Derivatives segments, New Membership
- Siddhartha Commodities Private Limited (Member No. 5175) - Equity Derivatives segment, New Membership
- All members granted trading rights only on BSE Ltd.
- Membership subject to BSE Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations
- Queries to be directed to membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an informational notice about new member admissions.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for existing trading members. This is an informational notice only.

## Important Dates

- **October 20, 2025**: Effective date of admission for all three new trading members
- **Notice Date**: October 20, 2025
- **Notice Number**: 20251020-32

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative notice with minimal market impact. The admission of new trading members increases market participation and liquidity potential across Debt, Cash, and Equity Derivatives segments. Existing trading members are not required to take any action. The notice serves purely informational purposes to make the trading community aware of new participants in the exchange ecosystem.
