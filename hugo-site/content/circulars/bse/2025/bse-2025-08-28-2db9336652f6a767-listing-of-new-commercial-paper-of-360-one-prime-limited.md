## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of 360 ONE Prime Limited on the debt segment. Two series of commercial papers totaling Rs. 13.5 crores have been admitted to trading with HDFC Bank Limited as the issuing and paying agent.

## Key Points

- Two series of commercial papers listed on BSE debt segment
- Series 1: 700 units of Rs. 5 lakh each (Rs. 3.5 crores) maturing November 24, 2025
- Series 2: 2000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each (Rs. 10 crores) maturing November 25, 2025
- Both series carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings
- Trading effective from August 28, 2025
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as issuing and paying agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Trading members must ensure compliance with debt segment trading rules

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 26, 2025
- Trading Start Date: August 28, 2025
- Redemption Date (Series 1): November 24, 2025
- Redemption Date (Series 2): November 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The securities provide short-term debt investment options for institutional investors. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations expected.
