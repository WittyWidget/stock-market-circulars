## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 79,50,000 new equity shares of Cura Technologies Limited (Scrip Code: 532332) effective from August 28, 2025. The shares comprise 72 lakh shares from a merger scheme and 7.5 lakh shares issued to Strategic Resolution Applicant under an NCLT-approved resolution plan.

## Key Points

- 72,00,000 equity shares allotted from merger with Catalog IT Solutions Private Limited
- 7,50,000 equity shares issued at par to Successful Resolution Applicant and Strategic Investors
- All shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Issue price of Rs. 10/- per share for all new securities
- Trading permitted from Thursday, August 28, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

- New ISIN: INE117B01020 for the expanded share capital
- Updated share capital structure post-merger and resolution plan implementation
- NCLT Hyderabad Bench approval obtained for resolution plan under IBC

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems for the new securities
- Lock-in compliance: 7.5 lakh shares locked until August 31, 2026
- Merger shares have no lock-in restrictions

## Important Dates

- **February 13, 2025**: Allotment date for merger shares
- **June 21, 2025**: Allotment date for resolution plan shares
- **August 28, 2025**: Trading commencement date
- **August 31, 2026**: Lock-in expiry for resolution plan shares

## Impact Assessment

- Significant equity dilution of approximately 79.5 lakh shares
- Capital infusion through resolution plan may strengthen company finances
- Lock-in on 7.5 lakh shares may limit immediate trading volume
- Merger completion indicates corporate restructuring activity
