## Summary

BSE has announced that the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has approved the surrender of trading membership of Jeevan Utsav Capital Private Limited (Member no. 6770). All trading members of the exchange have been notified to take note of this change.

## Key Points

- Trading member: Jeevan Utsav Capital Private Limited
- Member number: 6770
- Action: Surrender of trading membership
- Approval authority: SEBI
- Notice number: 20251015-2
- Notice date: 15 October 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative action involving the resignation of a single trading member from the exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to take note of the resignation of Jeevan Utsav Capital Private Limited (Member no. 6770) and update their records accordingly.

## Important Dates

- **15 October 2025**: Notice date and effective date of approval

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative matter with minimal impact on market operations. The resignation of a single trading member does not affect overall market functioning or other participants. Trading members should simply update their internal records to reflect that Member no. 6770 is no longer an active trading member of BSE.
