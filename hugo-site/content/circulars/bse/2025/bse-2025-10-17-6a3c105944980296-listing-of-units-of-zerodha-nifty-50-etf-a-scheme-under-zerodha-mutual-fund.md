## Summary

BSE announces the listing of Zerodha Nifty 50 ETF units effective October 20, 2025. The ETF is an open-ended scheme tracking the Nifty 50 Index, managed by Zerodha Asset Management Private Limited. A total of 5,788,600 units will be listed in the B Group with scrip code 544586, trading in dematerialized form only with a market lot of 1 unit.

## Key Points

- **Effective Date**: October 20, 2025
- **Total Units**: 5,788,600
- **Scrip Code**: 544586
- **ISIN**: INF0R8F01166
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 9.99 per unit
- **Date of Allotment**: October 14, 2025
- **Trading Group**: B Group
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **BOLT System ID**: NIFTYCASE
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. The listing follows SEBI guidelines dated February 16, 2000, requiring mutual fund units to be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure all trades are executed in dematerialized form only
- Trades must be effected in unit market lot (1 unit)
- Members must comply with standard ETF trading protocols

## Important Dates

- **October 14, 2025**: Date of allotment
- **October 17, 2025**: Notice date
- **October 20, 2025**: Listing and commencement of trading

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine ETF listing that adds another passive investment option tracking the Nifty 50 Index. 

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing procedures apply with four designated market makers ensuring liquidity:
- Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited
- East India Securities Limited
- iRage Broking Services LLP
- Fyres Securities Private Limited

**Investor Impact**: Provides investors with an additional low-cost passive investment vehicle for Nifty 50 exposure through Zerodha's platform.
