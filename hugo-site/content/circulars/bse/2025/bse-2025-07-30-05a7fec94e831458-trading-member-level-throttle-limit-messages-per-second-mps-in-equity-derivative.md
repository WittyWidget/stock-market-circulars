## Summary

BSE has extended the cut-off time for Messages Per Second (MPS) assignment and reassignment at Trading Member/ETI ID level from the regular time to 6:30 PM specifically for July 30, 2025. This is a partial modification to the previous exchange notice dated May 27, 2025.

## Key Points

- Extension applies to Trading Member level throttle limit configurations
- Affects MPS assignment/reassignment at Trading Member/ETI ID level
- Cut-off time extended to 6:30 PM for July 30, 2025 only
- Modifies previous notice 20250527-65 dated May 27, 2025
- Applies to Equity Derivatives segment

## Regulatory Changes

Temporary extension of operational cut-off time for MPS configuration changes, providing additional time for trading members to make necessary adjustments.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members can utilize the extended window until 6:30 PM on July 30, 2025 for MPS assignment and reassignment activities at Trading Member/ETI ID level.

## Important Dates

- **July 30, 2025**: Extended cut-off time to 6:30 PM for MPS assignment/reassignment
- **May 27, 2025**: Original notice date being modified

## Impact Assessment

Provides operational flexibility for trading members to adjust their system configurations with extended deadline, ensuring smooth trading operations in the equity derivatives segment.
