## Summary

BSE announces the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) in the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS category, effective September 05, 2025. The T-Bills have varying maturities from 91 days to 364 days.

## Key Points

- Three new Treasury Bills to be listed on September 05, 2025
- All T-Bills classified under G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS
- Market lot size of 1 for all three securities
- Trading restrictions apply two working days before maturity

## New Treasury Bills Listed

| Scrip Code | ISIN | Symbol | Maturity Date | Days to Maturity |
|------------|------|---------|---------------|------------------|
| 805112 | IN002025X232 | 91TB041225 | December 04, 2025 | 91 days |
| 805113 | IN002025Y230 | 182TB50326 | March 05, 2026 | 182 days |
| 805114 | IN002025Z237 | 364TB30926 | September 03, 2026 | 364 days |

## Trading Restrictions

- Securities will not be available for trading two working days prior to maturity/redemption date
- Restriction applies to Maturity Date (T) minus two trading days (excluding bank holidays)

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: September 05, 2025
- **T-Bill Maturities**: December 04, 2025; March 05, 2026; September 03, 2026

Routine addition of government treasury bills to BSE's debt segment. These securities provide short to medium-term investment options for institutional and retail investors. No significant market impact expected as this is standard government securities listing.
