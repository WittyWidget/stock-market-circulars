## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Birla Group Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis with effect from October 20, 2025. The instrument consists of 4000 units with face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each, carrying CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings, with a 91-day tenure maturing on January 19, 2026.

## Key Points

- **Issuer:** Birla Group Holdings Private Limited
- **Instrument Type:** Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity:** 4000 units
- **Face Value:** Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 4,91,664.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code:** 730422
- **Scrip ID:** BGHP201025
- **ISIN:** INE09OL14IA4
- **Credit Rating:** CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot:** 1 unit
- **Trading:** Dematerialized form only
- **Issuing and Paying Agent:** Axis Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE09OL14IA4
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** October 20, 2025
- **Listing Date:** October 20, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** January 19, 2026
- **Tenure:** 91 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term funding to Birla Group Holdings Private Limited and offers investment opportunity to institutional investors in the debt segment. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the new listing for their records and trading operations.
