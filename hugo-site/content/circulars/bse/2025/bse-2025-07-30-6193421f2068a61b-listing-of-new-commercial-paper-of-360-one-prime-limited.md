## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by 360 ONE Prime Limited on private placement basis, effective July 30, 2025. The securities are admitted for trading on the BSE Debt segment with a total value of Rs. 17.5 crores.

## Key Points

- 3,500 Commercial Paper units of Rs. 5 lakh each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,604.50 per unit (face value Rs. 5,00,000)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip Code: 729936, ISIN: INE248U14RX4
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- HDFC Bank Limited serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 29, 2025
- Listing Date: July 30, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt investment option for institutional investors with 3-month maturity period.
