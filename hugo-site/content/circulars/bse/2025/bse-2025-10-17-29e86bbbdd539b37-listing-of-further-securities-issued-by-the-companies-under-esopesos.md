## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and admission for trading of further securities issued by 7 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Scheme (ESOS). The new securities will be listed and available for trading with effect from Monday, October 20, 2025. All listed securities have no lock-in period restrictions.

## Key Points

- Total of 7 companies listing additional shares under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Combined total of 1,371,966 new shares being listed
- Yes Bank has the largest issuance with 1,245,046 shares (face value ₹2)
- KEI Industries listing 48,000 shares (face value ₹2)
- Godrej Consumer Products listing 28,261 shares (face value ₹1)
- All securities have no lock-in period (marked as NA)
- Effective trading date: October 20, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for securities issued under existing ESOP/ESOS frameworks.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading
- Companies have complied with ESOP/ESOS issuance and listing requirements
- Securities can be traded without restrictions from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 17, 2025
- **Listing & Trading Effective Date**: October 20, 2025 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Period**: None (NA for all securities)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal to negligible. The issuance represents small incremental additions to existing share capital of listed companies under employee benefit schemes.

**Liquidity Impact**: Marginal increase in floating stock, with Yes Bank seeing the largest absolute increase of 1.24 million shares. The incremental supply is unlikely to materially affect trading volumes or price discovery.

**Operational Impact**: Routine processing for trading members. No special operational requirements or restrictions apply to these securities.
