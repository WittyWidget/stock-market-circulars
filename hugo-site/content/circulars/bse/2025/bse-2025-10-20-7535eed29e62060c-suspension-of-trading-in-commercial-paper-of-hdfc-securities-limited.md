## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in a Commercial Paper issued by HDFC Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 729893, ISIN: INE700G14PD9) effective from October 23, 2025. The suspension is due to the commercial paper reaching its redemption date. Trading Members are advised not to deal in this instrument from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of HDFC Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 729893) to be suspended from trading
- ISIN: INE700G14PD9
- Suspension effective from October 23, 2025
- Reason: Maturity on redemption date
- Notice issued under reference DR-741/2025-2026
- Trading Members instructed not to deal in the aforesaid Commercial Paper

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice for suspension of trading in a maturing debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not execute trades in the specified Commercial Paper (ISIN: INE700G14PD9) from October 23, 2025 onwards
- Trading Members are required to take note of this suspension and update their trading systems accordingly

## Important Dates

- **October 20, 2025**: Notice Date
- **October 23, 2025**: Effective date of trading suspension
- **October 23, 2025**: Redemption Date (maturity)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders and traders of this specific Commercial Paper instrument. This is a routine suspension tied to the natural maturity of the debt security. No broader market impact is expected as this is a single commercial paper issue reaching its scheduled redemption. Trading Members need to ensure compliance by updating their systems to prevent inadvertent trading in this suspended instrument.
