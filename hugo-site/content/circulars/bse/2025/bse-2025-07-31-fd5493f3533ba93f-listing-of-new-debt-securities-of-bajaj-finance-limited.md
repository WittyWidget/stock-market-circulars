## Summary

BSE announces the listing of new debt securities issued by Bajaj Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective July 31, 2025. The securities carry a 7.07% annual interest rate and mature on September 21, 2028.

## Key Points

- New debt securities of Bajaj Finance Limited listed on BSE Debt segment
- Issue quantity: 100,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 976971, Scrip ID: 707BFL28
- ISIN Number: INE296A07TN6
- Credit Rating: CRISIL-AAA/STABLE
- Face Value and Issue Price: Rs. 100,000 each

## Security Details

- **Interest Rate**: 7.07% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Annually from September 21, 2026 to September 21, 2028
- **Maturity Date**: September 21, 2028
- **Allotment Date**: July 30, 2025
- **Put/Call Option**: Not applicable

## Trading Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum on BSE website

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: July 31, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: July 30, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: September 21, 2026
- **Maturity Date**: September 21, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing of high-rated corporate debt securities. The AAA rating indicates strong creditworthiness and the securities provide institutional investors with a fixed-income investment option.
