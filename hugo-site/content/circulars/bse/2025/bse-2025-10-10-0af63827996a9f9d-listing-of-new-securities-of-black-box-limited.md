## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 25,000 new equity shares of Black Box Limited (Scrip Code: 500463) effective from Monday, October 13, 2025. These shares were issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis at Rs. 417/- per share pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are subject to lock-in until April 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- 25,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 415/-
- Issue price: Rs. 417.00 per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Date of allotment: September 5, 2025
- Trading commencement date: October 13, 2025
- Distinctive numbers: 169987511 to 170012510
- ISIN: INE676A01027
- Scrip Code: 500463
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following preferential allotment procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities are available for trading from October 13, 2025
- All 25,000 shares are subject to mandatory lock-in until April 25, 2026
- Lock-in applies to distinctive numbers 169987511 to 170012510

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 5, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 13, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: April 25, 2026
- **Notice Date**: October 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The issuance represents a small preferential allotment of 25,000 shares issued pursuant to warrant conversion. The shares are locked-in for approximately 6.5 months until April 2026, preventing immediate trading by allottees. This is a routine corporate action with no significant effect on overall market liquidity or trading patterns for Black Box Limited.
