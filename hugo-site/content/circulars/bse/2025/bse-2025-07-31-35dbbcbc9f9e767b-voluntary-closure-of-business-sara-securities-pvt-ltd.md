## Summary

Sara Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6348) will voluntarily cease business operations in equity derivative segments effective August 1, 2025. This is a routine member closure notification with no broader market implications.

## Key Points

- Sara Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 6348) ceasing operations
- Voluntary closure of business
- Affects equity derivative segments only
- Effective date: August 1, 2025
- Contact: membership.ops@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is an individual member business closure.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the closure for awareness. No specific compliance actions required from other members.

## Important Dates

- **August 1, 2025**: Effective date for cessation of business operations

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a single member's voluntary business closure. Other trading members and market operations remain unaffected.
