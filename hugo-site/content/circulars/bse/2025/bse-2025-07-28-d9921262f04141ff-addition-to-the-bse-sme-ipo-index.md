## Summary

Swastika Castal Limited (Exchange ticker 544452) will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of Tuesday, July 29, 2025. The stock is being listed on BSE effective Monday, July 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Swastika Castal Limited (ticker 544452) being listed on BSE on July 28, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE SME IPO Index on July 29, 2025
- Addition included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Reference to previous Notice No. 20250726-1

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements mentioned for market participants.

## Important Dates

- **July 28, 2025**: Listing of Swastika Castal Limited on BSE
- **July 29, 2025**: Addition to BSE SME IPO Index effective at market open

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine index addition following the listing of a new SME company. The addition to the BSE SME IPO Index will provide the stock with index exposure and potential inclusion in index-based investment products.
