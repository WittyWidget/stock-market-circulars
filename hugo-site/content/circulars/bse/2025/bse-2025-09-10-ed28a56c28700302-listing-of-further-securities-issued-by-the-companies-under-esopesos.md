## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and admission for trading of further securities issued by 15 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS), effective from Thursday, September 11, 2025. These are additional shares issued to employees under existing stock option schemes.

## Key Points

- 15 companies are listing additional securities under ESOP/ESOS
- Total of 976,770 new shares being listed across all companies
- All securities will be available for trading from September 11, 2025
- None of the listed securities are under lock-in period
- Face values range from ₹1 to ₹10 depending on the company

## Company Details

### Major Listings by Volume:
- Fedbank Financial Services Limited: 291,253 shares (₹10 face value)
- GODAWARI POWER AND ISPAT LTD: 213,355 shares (₹1 face value)
- Tejas Networks Limited: 108,699 shares (₹10 face value)
- NIIT Ltd: 83,359 shares (₹2 face value)
- Brainbees Solutions Limited: 76,000 shares (₹2 face value)

### Other Companies:
- Quick Heal Technologies Limited: 56,575 shares
- INTELLECT DESIGN ARENA LIMITED: 42,489 shares
- City Union Bank Ltd: 40,780 shares
- Five-Star Business Finance Ltd: 19,990 shares
- STOVE KRAFT LIMITED: 15,000 shares
- ICICI Prudential Life Insurance: 11,476 shares
- Max Healthcare Institute Limited: 9,588 shares
- Star Health and Allied Insurance: 5,300 shares
- Cyient Limited: 2,106 shares
- Vardhman Textiles Limited: 500 shares

## Important Dates

- **Trading Commencement**: Thursday, September 11, 2025
- **Notice Date**: September 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing with minimal market impact. The additional shares represent employee stock options being exercised and converted to tradeable securities. The volumes are relatively small compared to existing market capitalization of these companies and should not significantly affect stock prices or trading patterns.
