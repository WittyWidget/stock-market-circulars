## Summary

BSE has issued updates on the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective August 08, 2025. Five securities are being added to the ST-ASM framework, while two securities are moving out. The circular provides consolidated lists of all securities under different stages of ST-ASM.

## Key Points

- 5 securities newly shortlisted for ST-ASM framework including Franklin Industries Ltd, Incon Engineers Ltd, MFS Intercorp Ltd, and Sarda Energy & Minerals Ltd
- 2 securities moving out of ST-ASM framework: Benara Bearings and Pistons Ltd and Sainik Finance & Industries Ltd
- Sainik Finance & Industries Ltd is moving out due to inclusion in Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) Framework
- No securities are being moved to higher or lower stage ASM within the framework
- Consolidated list shows 18+ securities currently under ST-ASM Stage I

## Regulatory Changes

Implementation of Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure for newly identified securities to monitor unusual price movements and trading patterns.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must comply with ST-ASM requirements for affected securities
- Enhanced monitoring and reporting obligations for securities under surveillance
- Adherence to specific trading conditions as per ST-ASM framework

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 08, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The surveillance measures will impact trading liquidity and investor behavior for the affected securities. Securities entering ST-ASM may experience reduced trading volumes due to additional compliance requirements, while those exiting may see normalized trading conditions.
