## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 1,07,250 new equity shares of Spectrum Electrical Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 544386) issued to promoters on a preferential basis. The shares will be available for trading from Thursday, August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- 1,07,250 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 1110/-
- Issue price: Rs. 1120/- per share
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE01EO01010
- Distribution numbers: 15606591 to 15713840

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are listed and permitted for trading on the exchange.

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 24, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 28, 2025
- Lock-in Period Ends: February 28, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves preferential allotment to promoters with a lock-in period until February 2027. The shares cannot be traded by promoters during the lock-in period, limiting immediate market supply.
