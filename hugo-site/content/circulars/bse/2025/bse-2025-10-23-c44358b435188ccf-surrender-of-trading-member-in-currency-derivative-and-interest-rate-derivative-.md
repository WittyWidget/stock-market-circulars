## Summary

BSE has approved the surrender of trading membership of Pace Stock Broking Services Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 3013) in the Currency derivative and Interest rate derivative segments, effective October 23, 2025. This is an administrative action regarding the voluntary exit of a trading member from specific segments.

## Key Points

- Pace Stock Broking Services Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 3013) has surrendered its trading membership
- Surrender applies to Currency derivative segment
- Surrender applies to Interest rate derivative segment
- Approval granted by BSE effective October 23, 2025
- Trading members can contact membership.ops@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification regarding membership status.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for other trading members. This is an informational notice only.

## Important Dates

- **October 23, 2025**: Effective date of membership surrender approval

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative matter involving the voluntary withdrawal of a single trading member from specific derivative segments. No operational changes required for other market participants. The notice is informational in nature and does not affect trading operations or market structure.
