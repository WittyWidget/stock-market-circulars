## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 19,822 new equity shares of Taylormade Renewables Limited (Scrip Code: 541228) effective from Friday, September 5, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 19,822 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 509/-
- Issue price: Rs. 519/- per share
- Allotment date: April 7, 2025
- ISIN: INE459Z01017
- Distribution numbers: 12349852 to 12369673
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Trading permitted from September 5, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed about the listing and permitted trading of these new securities.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: April 7, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: September 5, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry Dates**: March 31, 2026 and April 1, 2026 (varies by share blocks)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected due to the small number of shares (19,822) being listed. The shares are subject to lock-in periods ranging until March 31, 2026 and April 1, 2026, which will limit immediate trading volume. This is a routine corporate action following warrant conversion.
