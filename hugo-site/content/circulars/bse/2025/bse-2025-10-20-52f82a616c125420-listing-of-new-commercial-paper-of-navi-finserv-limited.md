## Summary

BSE has listed two new Commercial Paper instruments issued by Navi Finserv Limited on private placement basis, effective October 20, 2025. The instruments have a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each and are rated IND A1+ by credit rating agencies. HDFC Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent for both instruments.

## Key Points

- **First Instrument (Scrip Code 730419, ISIN: INE342T14EO4)**: 300 units of Commercial Paper issued at Rs. 477,849, allotted on October 17, 2025, maturing on April 15, 2026
- **Second Instrument (Scrip Code 730420, ISIN: INE342T14EQ9)**: 1,200 units of Commercial Paper issued at Rs. 456,314, allotted on October 17, 2025, maturing on October 16, 2026
- Trading will be conducted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination is Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Market lot size is 1 unit for both instruments
- Credit rating: IND A1+ for both instruments
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on specified phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 17, 2025 (both instruments)
- **Listing Date**: October 20, 2025 (both instruments)
- **Redemption Date (First Instrument)**: April 15, 2026
- **Redemption Date (Second Instrument)**: October 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market impact. The listing adds Rs. 150 crore (Rs. 15 crore from first instrument + Rs. 60 crore from second instrument) of short-term debt instruments to the BSE Debt segment. The instruments provide short-term funding to Navi Finserv Limited and offer investment opportunities for institutional investors in highly-rated commercial paper. No impact on equity markets or trading members' operations beyond standard debt trading procedures.
