## Summary

ECL Finance Limited has listed new commercial paper securities on BSE's Debt segment through private placement. The securities have a face value of Rs. 5 lakh each with 500 units issued, carrying CRISIL A1+ and IVR A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- 500 commercial paper units of Rs. 5,00,000 each listed on BSE Debt segment
- Scrip Code: 729277, Scrip ID: ECLF25425
- ISIN: INE804I14XW9 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IVR A1+
- Issue price: Rs. 4,80,589.50 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- ICICI Bank Limited appointed as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 29, 2025
- Listing Date: July 30, 2025
- Redemption Date: January 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding. The securities provide additional debt investment options for institutional investors with high credit rating ensuring low risk profile.
