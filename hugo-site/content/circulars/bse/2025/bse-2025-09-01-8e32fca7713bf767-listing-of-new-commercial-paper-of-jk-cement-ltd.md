## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by J.K. Cement Ltd on private placement basis, effective September 1, 2025. The issue consists of 1000 securities of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 50 crores.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Quantity: 1000 units
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 50 crores
- Issue Price: Rs. 492,770.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+
- Scrip Code: 730118
- ISIN: INE823G14AV3

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applies
- IDBI Bank Limited serves as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 29, 2025
- Listing Date: September 1, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 27, 2025
- Tenor: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The instrument provides J.K. Cement with working capital financing at competitive rates given the A1+ rating.
