## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 200 crore issued by Godrej Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis. The commercial paper carries CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ ratings with 91-day maturity period.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5 lakh face value each, totaling 4,000 units
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,715.50 per unit (discount to face value)
- Allotment date: July 30, 2025
- Redemption date: October 29, 2025
- Scrip Code: 729941, ISIN: INE02JD14625
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading permitted only in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: July 31, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: July 30, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: October 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding avenue for Godrej Housing Finance Limited and investment option for institutional investors in highly-rated commercial paper.
