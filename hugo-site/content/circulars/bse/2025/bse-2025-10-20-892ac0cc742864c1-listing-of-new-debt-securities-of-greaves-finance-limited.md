## Summary

BSE announces the listing and admission to trading of new debt securities issued by Greaves Finance Limited on a private placement basis, effective October 20, 2025. The securities are non-convertible debentures with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000, carrying an interest rate of 10.50% p.a. with monthly interest payments, and a maturity date of October 20, 2027.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977225
- Scrip ID: 1050GFL027
- ISIN: INE1QWF07030
- Quantity: 2,500 debentures
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000
- Interest Rate: 10.50% per annum
- Credit Rating: IND A-/Stable
- Market Lot: 1
- Date of Allotment: October 20, 2025
- Trading: Only in dematerialized form
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE1QWF07030
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** October 20, 2025
- **Listing Date:** October 20, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates:** Monthly from November 20, 2025 to October 20, 2027
- **Redemption Date:** October 20, 2027
- **Put/Call Option:** Not Available

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The securities are issued on a private placement basis with a limited quantity of 2,500 debentures. The listing provides institutional and qualified investors access to rated debt instruments from Greaves Finance Limited. The monthly interest payment structure and 2-year tenure make these suitable for fixed-income portfolios seeking regular cash flows. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
