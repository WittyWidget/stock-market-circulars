## Summary

OXYZO Financial Services Limited has listed new commercial paper on BSE's debt segment effective August 12, 2025. The securities are issued on private placement basis with face value of Rs.5 lakhs each, maturing on November 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- 500 commercial papers of Rs.5 lakhs each listed on BSE debt segment
- Issue price: Rs.488,967.50 per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 730011, ISIN: INE04VS14293
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples
- Federal Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 11, 2025
- Listing effective: August 12, 2025
- Redemption date: November 10, 2025
- Tenure: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term debt instrument listing. Provides additional investment option for institutional investors in high-rated commercial paper with 91-day maturity.
