## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of SHLOKKA DYES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544582) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective Tuesday, November 04, 2025. This change follows the Exchange's earlier notice dated October 16, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: SHLOKKA DYES LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544582
- Current Classification: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Classification: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Segment: SME
- Notice Number: 20251017-2
- Reference Notice: 220251016-39 dated October 16, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be shifted from MT Group (Trade for Trade segment) to M Group (Rolling segment). This represents a relaxation of trading restrictions, as Rolling segment allows for normal T+2 settlement and intraday trading, unlike Trade for Trade which requires full upfront payment and delivery.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should:
- Note the effective date of November 04, 2025 for the segment transfer
- Update their systems to reflect the new group classification
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain on Tel. No. 022-2272 8822 for further details or clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: Friday, October 17, 2025
- **Effective Date**: Tuesday, November 04, 2025
- **Reference Notice Date**: Thursday, October 16, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from Trade for Trade segment to Rolling segment is a positive development for SHLOKKA DYES LIMITED. This change will:

- Improve stock liquidity by allowing intraday trading
- Enable T+2 settlement cycle instead of compulsory delivery
- Reduce trading restrictions on market participants
- Potentially increase trading volumes and price discovery
- Indicate improved compliance and market standing of the company

This move suggests the company has met the necessary criteria for normal rolling settlement, representing enhanced market access for investors.
