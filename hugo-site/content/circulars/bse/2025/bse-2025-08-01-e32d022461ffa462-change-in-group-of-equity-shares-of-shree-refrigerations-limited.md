## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of SHREE REFRIGERATIONS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544458) will be transferred from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) effective Monday, August 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: SHREE REFRIGERATIONS LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544458
- Current Group: MT Group (Trade for Trade segment)
- New Group: M Group (Rolling segment)
- Effective Date: Monday, August 18, 2025
- Contact: Mr. Anurag Jain (022-2272 8822)

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for SHREE REFRIGERATIONS LIMITED shares will change from Trade for Trade basis to Rolling settlement, allowing for more flexible trading and settlement cycles.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members need to be aware of the segment transfer and adjust their trading systems accordingly for the new group classification.

## Important Dates

- **August 18, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer

## Impact Assessment

The transfer from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment typically indicates improved liquidity prospects and removes the restriction of compulsory delivery-based trading, potentially increasing trading activity in the stock.
