## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 3,00,00,000 new equity shares of Shivamshree Businesses Limited (Scrip Code: 538520) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will be available for trading from Monday, August 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- 3 crore equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at premium of Rs. 0.50/-
- Issue price: Rs. 1.50 per share
- Shares issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Date of allotment: May 6, 2025
- ISIN: INE857P01021
- Distribution numbers: 45650001 to 75650000

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed about the new securities listing
- Lock-in period compliance required until March 20, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: May 6, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 25, 2025
- **Lock-in Period End**: March 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a standard preferential issue listing. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to lock-in restrictions until March 2026, preventing immediate market circulation.
