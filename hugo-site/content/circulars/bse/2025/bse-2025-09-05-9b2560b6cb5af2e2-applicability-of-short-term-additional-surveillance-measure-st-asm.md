## Summary

BSE has issued updates to the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective September 8, 2025. Two securities are being newly shortlisted under ST-ASM, while ten securities are moving out of the framework due to inclusion in other surveillance measures.

## Key Points

- 2 securities newly shortlisted in Short Term 5/15/30 Days ASM Framework
- 10 securities moving out of ST-ASM Framework
- No securities moving to higher or lower stage ASM within ST-ASM framework
- Changes effective September 8, 2025
- Consolidated list includes 14 securities across different ASM stages

## Regulatory Changes

**Securities entering ST-ASM Framework:**
- Kairosoft AI Solutions Ltd (Scrip Code: 506122)
- Moschip Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 532407)

**Securities exiting ST-ASM Framework:**
- 10 securities moving to other frameworks including ESM, GSM, LT-ASM, and SME categories
- Notable exits include Coffee Day Enterprises Ltd, Sangal Papers Ltd, and SPEL Semiconductor Ltd

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure compliance with ST-ASM trading restrictions
- Enhanced surveillance measures apply to shortlisted securities
- Special margin requirements and position limits may apply
- Monitoring of price movements and volumes for listed securities

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** September 8, 2025
- All changes to ST-ASM framework take effect from this date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects trading liquidity and investor behavior for specific securities under surveillance. The relatively small number of securities involved limits broader market impact.

**Operational Impact:** Trading restrictions and enhanced monitoring requirements for the listed securities. Investors in these stocks will face additional compliance requirements and potential trading limitations.
