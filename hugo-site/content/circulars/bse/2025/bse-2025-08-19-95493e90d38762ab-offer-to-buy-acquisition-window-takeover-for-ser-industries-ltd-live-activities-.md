## Summary

BSE has announced the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window/Takeover) for SER INDUSTRIES LTD shares. The takeover offer will run from August 20 to September 3, 2025, targeting up to 2,57,294 equity shares at Rs. 35.00 per share.

## Key Points

- Security: SER INDUSTRIES LTD (Symbol: SERIND, ISIN: INE358F01013)
- Offer Size: Up to 2,57,294 equity shares
- Offer Price: Rs. 35.00 per share (Face Value: Rs. 10.00)
- Market Lot: 1 share
- Opening Date: August 19, 2025
- Closing Date: September 3, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This follows existing OTB operational framework as per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 and 20170203-29.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure UCC of investors is registered with correct PAN and category
- Members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- UCC database must be updated with accurate investor information
- Members must use designated BSE platforms (iBBS, RTRMS, Extranet)

## Important Dates

- **Bid Offering Period**: August 20 - September 3, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: August 20 - September 2, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up Last Day**: September 3, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Take-up Last Day**: September 3, 2025 (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on SER Industries shareholders and trading members. The takeover affects a specific stock with defined share quantity limits. Trading members need to prepare systems and ensure compliance for participation in the acquisition window activities.
