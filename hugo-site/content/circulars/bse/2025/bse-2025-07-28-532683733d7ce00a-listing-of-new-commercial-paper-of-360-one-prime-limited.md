## Summary

360 ONE Prime Limited has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 150 crores (3000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE's Debt segment. The securities were issued on private placement basis with allotment date of July 25, 2025 and redemption date of October 24, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (3000 units total)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,604.50 per unit
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Scrip Code: 729908, ISIN: INE248U14RV8
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Trading members to contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 25, 2025
- Listing Date: July 28, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk for investors.
