## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Avanse Financial Services Limited worth ₹75 crores issued on private placement basis. Two series of securities have been listed with different interest rates and maturity periods, both rated CRISIL AA-/Stable.

## Key Points

- Two series of debt securities listed on BSE Debt segment
- Series 1: ₹75 crores at 9% p.a. maturing on 24/07/2028
- Series 2: ₹75 crores at 8.9% p.a. maturing on 10/09/2027
- Both securities have face value of ₹1,00,000 each
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size is 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under specified ISIN numbers
- Refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: 24/07/2025
- **Listing Date**: 28/07/2025
- **Interest Payment**: Annually
- **Series 1 Redemption**: 24/07/2028
- **Series 2 Redemption**: 10/09/2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of debt securities on private placement basis. The listing provides additional investment options for institutional investors in the debt market segment.
