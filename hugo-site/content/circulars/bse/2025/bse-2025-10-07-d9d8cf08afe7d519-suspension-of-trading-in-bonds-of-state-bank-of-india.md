## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in a specific bond series of State Bank of India (SBI-5.83%-26-10-30-PVT, ISIN: INE062A08264, Code: 960145) effective October 9, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of bonds and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these bonds from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Bond series affected: SBI-5.83%-26-10-30-PVT (Private)
- ISIN: INE062A08264
- Scrip Code: 960145
- Record date: October 9, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of bonds and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from October 9, 2025
- Reference: DR-732/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified SBI bond series from October 9, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and communicate to clients holding or intending to trade these bonds

## Important Dates

- **October 7, 2025**: Circular issued
- **October 9, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **October 9, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of this specific private bond series of State Bank of India. Investors holding these bonds will receive their final interest payment and principal redemption based on holdings as of the record date. The suspension affects only one bond issue and does not impact SBI's equity shares or other debt instruments. Market impact is minimal as this is a routine corporate action for maturing bonds.
