## Summary

BSE has listed two series of Commercial Paper issued by Redington Limited on private placement basis, effective October 20, 2025. The first series comprises 4,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each (total Rs. 200 crores) maturing on December 16, 2025, and the second series comprises 2,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each (total Rs. 100 crores) maturing on January 9, 2026. Both instruments are rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, with HDFC Bank Ltd acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Two new commercial paper series listed on BSE Debt segment effective October 20, 2025
- Series 1: 4,000 units at Rs. 5,00,000 face value each (Scrip Code: 730417, ISIN: INE891D14A87)
- Series 2: 2,000 units at Rs. 5,00,000 face value each (Scrip Code: 730418, ISIN: INE891D14A95)
- Allotment date: October 17, 2025 for both series
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ for both instruments
- Trading only in dematerialized form in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification under existing BSE debt segment regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof must be maintained
- Market lot size is 1 unit
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 17, 2025 (both series)
- **Listing Date**: October 20, 2025 (both series)
- **Redemption Date (Series 1)**: December 16, 2025
- **Redemption Date (Series 2)**: January 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing of short-term commercial paper by an established company. The instruments provide additional short-term debt investment options for institutional investors.

**Operational Impact**: No significant operational changes required. Trading members can trade these instruments following standard BSE debt segment procedures.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to institutional investors and entities participating in commercial paper markets. The strong credit ratings (A1+) indicate high credit quality and low default risk for the short tenure.
