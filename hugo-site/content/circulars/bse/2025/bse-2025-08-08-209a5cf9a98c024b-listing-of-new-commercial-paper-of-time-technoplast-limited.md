## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of Time Technoplast Limited on its debt segment effective August 8, 2025. The commercial paper was issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each, totaling 200 units (Rs. 10 crores).

## Key Points

- Commercial paper quantity: 200 units of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Total issue size: Rs. 10 crores
- Issue price: Rs. 4,90,822.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 729983
- ISIN: INE508G14HW3
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- IDBI Bank Limited acts as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 8, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 8, 2025
- Redemption date: November 7, 2025
- Maturity period: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding option for Time Technoplast Limited and offers investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
