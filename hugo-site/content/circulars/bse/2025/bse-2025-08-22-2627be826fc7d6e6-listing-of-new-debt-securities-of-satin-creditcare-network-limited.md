## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of SATIN CREDITCARE NETWORK LIMITED issued on private placement basis. The securities carry 11.5% interest rate and mature in 2032.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 6,000 securities with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each
- Total issue size: Rs. 60 crores
- Interest rate: 11.5% per annum
- Credit rating: ICRA A
- Scrip code: 977048
- ISIN: INE836B08301
- Trading starts effective August 22, 2025

## Security Details

- **Issue Date**: August 21, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: August 21, 2032
- **Interest Payment**: Annually from August 21, 2026 to August 21, 2032
- **Market Lot**: 1 security
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE836B08301
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum on BSE website for detailed terms
- No put/call options available

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 21, 2025
- **Listing Date**: August 22, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: August 21, 2026
- **Maturity Date**: August 21, 2032

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt securities listing with minimal market impact. Provides additional funding avenue for Satin Creditcare Network Limited at competitive interest rates.
