## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 12,60,000 new equity shares of Desh Rakshak Aushdhalaya Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531521) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will commence trading on October 27, 2025, and are subject to lock-in restrictions until April 2026.

## Key Points

- 12,60,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 12/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 22/- per share
- Allotted to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 5002601 to 6262600
- Date of allotment: September 9, 2025
- ISIN: INE971E01016
- Majority of shares (11,80,000) subject to lock-in until April 30, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities available for trading from October 27, 2025
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed:
  - 11,80,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 5002601-6182600): locked until April 30, 2026
  - 80,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 6182601-6262600): locked until April 29, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 9, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 27, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: April 29-30, 2026 (depending on share category)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a routine preferential allotment representing capital raising by Desh Rakshak Aushdhalaya Ltd. The lock-in period indicates regulatory compliance with preferential issue norms. The shares will increase the company's equity base by 12,60,000 shares at Rs. 22/- per share, raising approximately Rs. 2.77 crores. Trading members should update their records to reflect the new securities and associated lock-in restrictions.
