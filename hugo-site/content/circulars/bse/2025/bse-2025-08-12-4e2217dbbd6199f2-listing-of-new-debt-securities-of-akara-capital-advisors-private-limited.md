## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Akara Capital Advisors Private Limited issued on private placement basis, effective August 12, 2025. The securities carry 12.5% interest rate and mature on December 27, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2,850 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 976868, Scrip ID: 125ACAP28
- ISIN: INE08XP07324
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 95,410.96
- Credit Rating: ICRA BBB (Stable)
- Interest Rate: 12.5% per annum (monthly payments)
- Maturity Date: December 27, 2028
- No put/call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Trading members must refer to Placement Memorandum for detailed information
- Contact debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 8, 2025
- Listing Date: August 12, 2025
- Maturity Date: December 27, 2028
- Interest Payment: Monthly

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement debt listing. The securities are targeted at institutional investors and will have limited retail participation due to high face value of Rs. 1 lakh per unit.
