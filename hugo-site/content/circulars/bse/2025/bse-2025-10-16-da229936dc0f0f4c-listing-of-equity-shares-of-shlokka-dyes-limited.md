## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of equity shares of SHLOKKA DYES LIMITED on the exchange. This circular informs market participants about the addition of a new security available for trading.

## Key Points

- Company Name: SHLOKKA DYES LIMITED
- Security Type: Equity Shares
- Action: Listing on BSE
- Circular Date: October 16, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should update their systems to reflect the newly listed security
- Trading members should ensure proper risk management for the new listing

## Important Dates

- Circular Issue Date: October 16, 2025
- Listing effective from the date specified in the circular

## Impact Assessment

This listing expands the universe of tradable securities on BSE. The impact is primarily relevant to:
- Investors interested in the textile/dyes manufacturing sector
- Market participants who need to update their trading systems
- The company's existing shareholders who will gain liquidity

The market-wide impact is limited as this is a standard listing procedure for a single company.
