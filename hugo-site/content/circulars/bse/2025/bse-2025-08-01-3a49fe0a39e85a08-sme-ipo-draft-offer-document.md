## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Infinity Infoway Limited for public review and comments. The document was filed on July 31, 2025, and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Infinity Infoway Limited on July 31, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Contains all disclosures per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

No regulatory changes announced. Standard procedural filing under existing ICDR Regulations.

- All comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

- **Draft Document Filed**: July 31, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 1, 2025
- **Comment Deadline**: August 22, 2025 (21 days from notice)

Routine SME IPO process with standard public consultation period. Limited immediate market impact as this is preliminary documentation phase. Potential future impact depends on IPO success and company performance post-listing.
