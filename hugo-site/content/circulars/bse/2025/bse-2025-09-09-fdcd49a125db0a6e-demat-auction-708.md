## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction settlement list for Settlement No. 708 dated September 9, 2025. The list contains securities and their respective quantities that are subject to demat auction settlement, covering over 100 different scrips across various market segments.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 708
- Settlement Date: September 9, 2025
- Total securities listed: Over 100 scrips
- Quantities range from 1 share to 24,875 shares depending on the security
- Mix of large-cap, mid-cap and small-cap securities included
- Both equity shares and other securities covered

## Securities Covered

Major securities in the auction include:
- BAJAJ FINANC (10 shares)
- BHARAT HEAVY (1 share)
- COAL INDIA L (10 shares)
- DABUR INDIA (5 shares)
- TATA STEEL L (200 shares)
- MAHINDRA & M (50 shares)
- BERGER PAINT (100 shares)
- THERMAX LTD (6 shares)

Highest quantity: CROPSTER AGR (24,875 shares)
Lowest quantity: Multiple securities with 1 share each

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: September 9, 2025
- Auction Settlement Number: 708

## Impact Assessment

This demat auction settlement affects shareholders and trading members dealing with the listed securities. The auction mechanism helps in settling delivery obligations where physical delivery cannot be completed through normal settlement processes. Impact is typically limited to specific holders of these securities who may need to participate in the auction process.
