## Summary

BSE announces the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) in the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS segment effective October 24, 2025. The T-Bills include 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day maturity instruments with specific scrip codes and ISIN numbers.

## Key Points

- Three new Treasury Bills to be listed on BSE from October 24, 2025
- 91-day T-Bill (Scrip: 805133, ISIN: IN002025X307) maturing January 23, 2026
- 182-day T-Bill (Scrip: 805134, ISIN: IN002025Y305) maturing April 24, 2026
- 364-day T-Bill (Scrip: 805135, ISIN: IN002025Z302) maturing October 22, 2026
- All securities have a market lot size of 1
- Trading will be suspended two working days prior to maturity/redemption date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for new government securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new scrip codes and symbols for these T-Bills
- Trading members must ensure these securities are not traded two working days before maturity date (T-2 trading days, excluding bank holidays)
- Securities will be available for trading in G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS segment

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: October 24, 2025
- **91TB220126 Maturity**: January 23, 2026 (Trading stops January 21, 2026)
- **182TB23426 Maturity**: April 24, 2026 (Trading stops April 22, 2026)
- **364T221026 Maturity**: October 22, 2026 (Trading stops October 20, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative listing of government treasury bills that provides additional short-term debt investment instruments for market participants. The listing expands the available universe of government securities for debt investors and trading members.
