## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that Vaibhav Stock & Derivatives Broking Private Limited (Member No. 6750) will voluntarily cease operations in the Currency derivative segment effective August 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- Member firm voluntarily closing currency derivative business operations
- Effective date: Friday, August 8, 2025
- BSE Member Number: 6750
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a voluntary business closure by the member firm.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the cessation. No specific compliance actions required for other members.

## Important Dates

- **August 8, 2025**: Effective date for cessation of currency derivative operations

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves only one member firm voluntarily exiting the currency derivative segment. Other trading members and market operations remain unaffected.
