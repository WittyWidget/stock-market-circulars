## Summary

Taurus Mutual Fund has announced a change in its Registrar & Transfer Agent (RTA) from Kfintech to Computer Age Management Services Ltd (CAMS), effective Monday, September 15, 2025.

## Key Points

- Current RTA: Kfintech
- New RTA: Computer Age Management Services Ltd (CAMS)
- Effective date: Monday, September 15, 2025
- Change applies to all Taurus Mutual Fund schemes

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes involved - this is an operational change in service provider.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members are requested to take note of the RTA change
- No specific action required from investors
- Fund operations will continue seamlessly under new RTA

## Important Dates

- **September 15, 2025**: Effective date for RTA change from Kfintech to CAMS

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact expected as this is a back-office operational change. Investor services and fund operations will continue normally under the new RTA arrangement. CAMS is an established RTA service provider in the mutual fund industry.
