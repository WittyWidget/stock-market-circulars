## Summary

Panchatv Bharat Limited has filed its Draft Offer Document on BSE SME platform on August 20, 2025. The document contains all required disclosures under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations and is available for public comments for a minimum period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Panchatv Bharat Limited on August 20, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for public review
- Contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comment period of at least 21 days

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public can submit comments on the Draft Offer Document within 21 days
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document must remain available on exchange website for minimum 21 days

## Important Dates

- Filing Date: August 20, 2025
- Comment Deadline: September 10, 2025 (21 days from filing)
- Document Availability: Minimum 21 days on BSE SME website

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard procedural filing for SME IPO process. Impact is limited to potential investors and market participants interested in Panchatv Bharat Limited's public offering. The public comment period allows for stakeholder feedback before final approval.
