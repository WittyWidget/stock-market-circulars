## Summary

BSE has introduced daily SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) facility for six Edelweiss Mutual Fund offshore equity schemes on the BSE StAR MF platform, effective immediately from September 9, 2025.

## Key Points

- Daily SIP facility introduced for 6 Edelweiss offshore equity fund schemes
- Implementation is with immediate effect
- Available on BSE StAR MF platform
- All schemes are offshore equity funds focusing on different geographical regions

## Affected Schemes

1. EDELWEISS ASEAN EQUITY OFF-SHORE FUND
2. EDELWEISS EUROPE DYNAMIC EQUITY OFF-SHORE FUND
3. EDELWEISS EMERGING MARKETS OPPORTUNITIES EQUITY OFF-SHORE FUND
4. EDELWEISS GREATER CHINA EQUITY OFF-SHORE FUND
5. EDELWEISS US VALUE EQUITY OFFSHORE FUND
6. EDELWEISS US TECHNOLOGY EQUITY FUND OF FUND

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is an operational enhancement to existing mutual fund trading infrastructure.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements mentioned for market participants.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 9, 2025 (immediate effect)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is an operational enhancement providing additional investment flexibility for investors in Edelweiss offshore equity schemes. The introduction of daily SIP facility may increase investor participation in these international equity funds.
