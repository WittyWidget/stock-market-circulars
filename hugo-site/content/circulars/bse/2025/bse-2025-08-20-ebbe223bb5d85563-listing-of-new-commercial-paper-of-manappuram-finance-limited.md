## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial papers worth Rs. 350 crore issued by Manappuram Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective August 20, 2025. The papers carry CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ credit ratings with a 3-month maturity period.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total quantity: 7,000 units (Rs. 350 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,576 per unit
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Scrip code: 730051, ISIN: INE522D14OQ9
- Issuing and paying agent: Axis Bank Ltd
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size maintained at 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 20, 2025
- Listing effective: August 20, 2025  
- Redemption date: November 18, 2025
- Maturity period: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. Provides short-term funding avenue for Manappuram Finance Limited and investment option for institutional investors in the debt segment.
