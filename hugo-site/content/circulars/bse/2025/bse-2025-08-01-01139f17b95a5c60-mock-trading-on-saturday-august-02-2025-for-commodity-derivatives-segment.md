## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, August 02, 2025. The session is designed for testing trading systems and familiarization with various market functionalities including exceptional market conditions.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, August 02, 2025
- Log-in time: 10:15 AM to 11:00 AM
- Continuous trading: 11:00 AM to 03:30 PM
- No new BOLT TWS release
- Members can choose between mock trading session or UAT environment for regulatory compliance
- Trading members using third-party platforms or in-house systems can test various functionalities
- No margin obligations, pay-in/pay-out obligations, or legal rights/liabilities from mock trades

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is in accordance with existing SEBI circular no. SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020 and Exchange Notice No 20201201-22 dated December 1, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members are encouraged to participate actively in mock trading sessions
- Feedback must be provided by 4:30 PM on the mock trading day
- Members using third-party trading platforms should utilize this opportunity for testing
- Testing should cover various functionalities including risk-reduction mode and trading halt scenarios

## Important Dates

- **August 02, 2025**: Mock trading session date
- **4:30 PM on August 02, 2025**: Deadline for feedback submission

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a testing session with no financial obligations. The mock trading serves operational purposes for system testing and member familiarization with trading platforms and exceptional market conditions. No actual trades or financial settlements will occur.
