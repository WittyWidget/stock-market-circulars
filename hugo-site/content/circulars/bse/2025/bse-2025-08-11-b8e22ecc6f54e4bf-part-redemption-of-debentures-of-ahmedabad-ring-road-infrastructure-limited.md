## Summary

Ahmedabad Ring Road Infrastructure Limited has announced part redemption of its non-convertible debentures with a record date of August 16, 2025. Trading in these debentures will commence with a reduced face value of Rs. 64,018 per debenture effective from August 14, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Ahmedabad Ring Road Infrastructure Limited
- Debenture code: ARRIL-20%-30-6-26-PVT (ISIN: INE091I07011, BSE code: 975947)
- Record date: August 16, 2025
- Reduced face value: Rs. 64,018 per debenture
- Effective date: August 14, 2025 (Settlement No. DR-695/2025-2026)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a standard corporate action notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note that trading in the company's debentures will be conducted at the reduced face value from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **August 14, 2025**: Effective date for trading with reduced face value
- **August 16, 2025**: Record date for part redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action that affects the trading parameters of the specified debentures. The face value reduction reflects the partial redemption of principal amount. Trading members and investors holding these debentures should adjust their records accordingly.
