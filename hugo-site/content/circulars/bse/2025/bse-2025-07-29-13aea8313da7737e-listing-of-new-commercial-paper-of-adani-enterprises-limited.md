## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Adani Enterprises Limited on private placement basis. The securities are listed on BSE Debt segment with effect from July 29, 2025. Total issue size is Rs. 26.5 crores (530 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) with a maturity period of 91 days.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Quantity: 530 units
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 26.5 crores
- Scrip Code: 729927
- ISIN: INE423A14XW5
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,123 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 28, 2025
- Listing Date: July 29, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 27, 2025
- Maturity Period: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding requirements. The securities are rated A1+ indicating high credit quality and low default risk.
