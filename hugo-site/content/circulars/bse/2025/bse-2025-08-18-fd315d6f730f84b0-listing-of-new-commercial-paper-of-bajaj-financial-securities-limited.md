## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis. The instruments are worth Rs. 475 crores (9,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) with CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings, maturing on November 13, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 9,500 units (total value Rs. 475 crores)
- Issue Price: Rs. 492,173.52 per unit
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000/-
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip Code: 730034
- ISIN: INE01C314CJ2
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: August 18, 2025
- Allotment Date: August 14, 2025
- Listing Date: August 18, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instruments provide short-term funding for Bajaj Financial Securities Limited and offer investment opportunities for institutional investors in highly-rated commercial paper with 3-month maturity.
