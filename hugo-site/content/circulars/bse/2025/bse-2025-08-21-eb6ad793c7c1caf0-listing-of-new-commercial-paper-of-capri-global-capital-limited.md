## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 75 crores (1,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued by Capri Global Capital Limited on private placement basis, effective from August 21, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 1,500 units (Rs. 75 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 481,045.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Security Details

- Scrip Code: 730054
- Scrip ID: CGCL21825
- ISIN: INE180C14573
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Yes Bank Limited

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 21, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 17, 2026
- Trading Commencement: August 21, 2025

## Trading Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Minimum trading lot: Rs. 5 lakhs
- Available on BSE Debt segment
- Contact debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides additional short-term funding avenue for Capri Global Capital Limited with strong credit rating.
