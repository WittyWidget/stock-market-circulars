## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by BOBCARD LIMITED on private placement basis totaling Rs. 250 crore (5,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each). The instruments are listed on BSE Debt segment effective October 17, 2025, with a maturity date of December 24, 2025. The securities carry an IND A1+ credit rating and will trade only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: Rs. 250 crore (5,000 units)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,94,008.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730414
- Scrip ID: BL171025
- ISIN: INE027214886
- Credit rating: IND A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities to be traded under ISIN Number INE027214886

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 17, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 24, 2025
- **Tenor**: Approximately 68 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides BOBCARD LIMITED with short-term funding through the debt market and offers institutional investors an additional investment option in highly-rated commercial paper. Trading members dealing in debt instruments should note the trading parameters. No broader market implications expected.
