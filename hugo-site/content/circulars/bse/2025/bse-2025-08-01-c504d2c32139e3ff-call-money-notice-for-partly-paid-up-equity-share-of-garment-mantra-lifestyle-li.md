## Summary

BSE has activated the Call Money Module for Garment Mantra Lifestyle Limited's first call on partly paid equity shares. Shareholders must pay Rs 0.30 per partly paid equity share during the 15-day issue period from August 4-18, 2025.

## Key Points

- First call money notice for Garment Mantra Lifestyle Limited (GARMENTCM)
- Call amount: Rs 0.30 per partly paid equity share
- Total partly paid shares: 39,14,97,795 shares
- Market lot: 1 share
- Registrar: BIGSHARE SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED

## Regulatory Changes

Implementation follows Exchange notice no. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021, and SEBI circular dated December 8, 2020 regarding call money procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members and Self-Certified Syndicate Banks (SCSBs) must use IBBS platform
- Only SCSBs and Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts can submit call money details
- Intermediaries must note the activation and assist shareholders

## Important Dates

- Issue Opening Date: Monday, August 4, 2025
- Issue Closing Date: Monday, August 18, 2025
- Bidding Period: 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. daily
- Issue Duration: 15 days

## Impact Assessment

Direct impact on Garment Mantra Lifestyle Limited shareholders holding partly paid shares who must make mandatory payment. Operational impact on trading members and banks facilitating the call money collection process through BSE's IBBS platform.
