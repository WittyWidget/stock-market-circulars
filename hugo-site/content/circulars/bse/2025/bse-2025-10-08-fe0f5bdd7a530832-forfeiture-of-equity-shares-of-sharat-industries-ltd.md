## Summary

Sharat Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 519397) has forfeited 2,41,174 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each that were partly paid-up due to non-payment of allotment/call monies by shareholders. The forfeited shares have distinctive numbers ranging from 28206146 to 28447319.

## Key Points

- Company: Sharat Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 519397)
- Number of shares forfeited: 2,41,174 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Reason: Non-payment of allotment/call monies
- Status: Partly paid-up shares
- Distinctive numbers: 28206146 to 28447319

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a company-specific corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the forfeiture for their records and to update their systems accordingly. Shareholders holding the forfeited shares will lose their rights to these shares.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 8, 2025
- Effective Date: October 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This forfeiture affects shareholders who failed to meet their payment obligations on partly paid-up shares. The forfeited shares representing approximately 2.41 lakh shares may impact the company's shareholding pattern. Trading members need to update their records to reflect the cancellation of these share certificates. The action is routine for companies dealing with non-payment on partly paid shares and has limited broader market impact.
