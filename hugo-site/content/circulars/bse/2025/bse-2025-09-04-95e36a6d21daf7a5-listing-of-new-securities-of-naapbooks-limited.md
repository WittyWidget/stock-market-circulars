## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 72,000 new equity shares of Naapbooks Limited (Scrip Code: 543351) issued through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will commence trading on September 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- 72,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 51/-
- Issued to non-promoters through preferential allotment pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 10714201 to 10786200
- ISIN: INE0GOA01018

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities listing
- Lock-in period compliance required for issued shares

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** July 9, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** September 5, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends:** March 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a small number of shares (72,000) issued through warrant conversion. The lock-in period until March 2026 prevents immediate trading of these shares, limiting supply impact on the market.
