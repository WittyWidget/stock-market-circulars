## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by SBICAP Securities Limited on private placement basis. The listing includes 5 tranches totaling 10,000 securities with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, all maturing on December 9, 2025.

## Key Points

- 5 tranches of Commercial Paper listed under ISIN INE212K14CB9
- Total quantity: 10,000 securities (2,000 + 1,000 + 2,000 + 2,000 + 3,000)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- Issue prices: Rs. 491,930 and Rs. 491,881.5
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded under scrip code 730177 and scrip ID SSL090925

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: September 9, 2025
- Listing effective date: September 10, 2025
- Redemption date: December 9, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The securities provide short-term funding options for SBICAP Securities Limited with high credit ratings indicating low risk. State Bank of India serves as the Issuing and Paying agent.
