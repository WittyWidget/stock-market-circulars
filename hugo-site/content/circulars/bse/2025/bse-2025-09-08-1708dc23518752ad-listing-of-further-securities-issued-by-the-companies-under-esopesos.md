## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of additional securities issued by 13 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be admitted for trading on the exchange effective Tuesday, September 9, 2025. All listed securities have no lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 13 companies are listing additional securities under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Total securities being listed: over 7 million shares across all companies
- Effective trading date: September 9, 2025
- No lock-in restrictions apply to any of the listed securities
- Companies span various sectors including technology, financial services, and consumer goods

## Securities Details

**Technology & IT Services:**
- 3i Infotech Limited: 49,500 shares (₹10 face value)
- Mphasis Limited: 1,710 shares (₹10 face value)
- Tata Elxsi Ltd: 1,924 shares (₹10 face value)

**Financial Services:**
- ABANS Financial Services Limited: 30,367 shares (₹2 face value)
- Aditya Birla Capital Ltd: 260,685 shares (₹10 face value)
- Angel One Limited: 30,418 shares (₹10 face value)
- Capital Small Finance Bank Ltd: 39,250 shares (₹10 face value)
- Go Digit General Insurance Limited: 10,830 shares (₹10 face value)

**Other Sectors:**
- Ather Energy Limited: 6,513,163 shares (₹1 face value)
- Avalon Technologies Limited: 86,674 shares (₹2 face value)
- Godrej Properties Limited: 218 shares (₹5 face value)
- Tata Consumer Products Limited: 3,192 shares (₹1 face value)
- V.I.P. Industries Ltd: 25,142 shares (₹2 face value)

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** September 8, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** September 9, 2025
- **Lock-in Period:** None (NA for all securities)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal - routine ESOP/ESOS listings with relatively small quantities compared to existing share capital of most companies. The largest addition is from Ather Energy with over 6.5 million shares.

**Liquidity Impact:** Slight increase in available shares for trading, but unlikely to significantly affect market liquidity given the distributed nature across multiple companies and sectors.

**Investor Impact:** Positive for employees exercising stock options, providing immediate liquidity without lock-in restrictions.
