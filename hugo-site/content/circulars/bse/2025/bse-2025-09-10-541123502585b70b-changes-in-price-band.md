## Summary

BSE has revised price bands for 13 securities effective September 11, 2025. The price bands range from 2% to 20%, with most securities having restrictive bands of 5% or lower to control volatility.

## Key Points

- 13 scrips affected by price band revisions
- Changes effective from September 11, 2025
- Price bands range from 2% (most restrictive) to 20%
- Majority of securities (8 out of 13) have 5% price bands
- Notice issued under BSE surveillance department

## Regulatory Changes

Price band modifications for the following securities:
- Ahmedabad Steelcraft Ltd: 5% band
- Covance Softsol Ltd: 20% band (highest)
- Cropster Agro Ltd: 5% band
- Fynx Capital Ltd: 2% band (most restrictive)
- Himalaya Food International Ltd: 5% band
- Refex Renewables & Infrastructure Ltd: 2% band (most restrictive)
- S.A.L. Steel Ltd: 5% band
- Sarveshwar Foods Ltd: 10% band
- Scarnose International Ltd: 5% band
- Sea TV Network Ltd: 2% band (most restrictive)
- Smruthi Organics Ltd: 10% band
- Toyam Sports Ltd: 5% band
- Vama Industries Ltd: 5% band

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must adhere to revised price bands from September 11, 2025
- All trades in affected securities must comply with new price movement limits
- Members can seek clarifications at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: September 10, 2025
- Effective Date: September 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The price band revisions will limit daily price volatility for these securities. Securities with 2% bands (Fynx Capital, Refex Renewables, Sea TV Network) face the most restrictive trading conditions, while Covance Softsol with 20% band has the most trading flexibility. This measure enhances market stability and protects investors from excessive price fluctuations.
