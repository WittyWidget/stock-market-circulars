## Summary

BSE will introduce futures and options contracts for two individual securities - Nuvama Wealth Management Ltd and Suzlon Energy Ltd - in the Equity Derivatives segment starting August 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two new underlying securities added to F&O segment: Nuvama Wealth Management Ltd (543988) and Suzlon Energy Ltd (532667)
- Trading commences August 18, 2025
- Contract details will be available in derivatives master file on August 14, 2025
- Master file accessible through BSE extranet and website

## Regulatory Changes

Expansion of equity derivatives trading to include two additional underlying securities with standard F&O contract specifications.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new contracts availability
- Members should access updated contract master file before trading begins
- Standard derivatives trading rules and risk management apply

## Important Dates

- **August 14, 2025**: Contract master file with details generated
- **August 18, 2025**: F&O trading begins for both securities

## Impact Assessment

Moderate positive impact providing additional hedging and trading opportunities for market participants in these two specific securities. Enhances derivatives market depth and liquidity for Nuvama Wealth Management and Suzlon Energy stocks.
