## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of further securities issued by 9 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be listed and admitted for trading effective Tuesday, August 26, 2025. All listed shares are not under any lock-in period.

## Key Points

- 9 companies issuing additional securities under ESOP/ESOS
- Total of 1,302,526 new shares across all companies
- Trading commences August 26, 2025
- No lock-in restrictions on any of the listed securities
- Face values range from ₹1 to ₹10 per share

## Company Details

**Major Issuances:**
- Paradeep Phosphates Limited: 989,657 shares (₹10 face value)
- Black Box Limited: 103,950 shares (₹2 face value)
- Edelweiss Financial Services: 76,684 shares (₹1 face value)
- CreditAccess Grameen: 45,725 shares (₹10 face value)

**Other Companies:**
- Prataap Snacks Limited: 27,504 shares
- Jana Small Finance Bank: 25,031 shares
- IdeaForge Technology: 11,626 shares
- Lupin Limited: 7,189 shares
- Dr. Reddy's Laboratories: 4,160 shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing of employee stock option exercises.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading. No additional compliance requirements specified.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** August 25, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** August 26, 2025
- **Lock-in Period:** None applicable

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as these are routine ESOP exercises. The additional shares represent small percentages of existing share capital for most companies. No trading restrictions apply, allowing immediate liquidity for option holders.
