## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 50 crore issued by Muthoottu Mini Financiers Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from July 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Total Quantity: 1000 units (Rs. 50 crore)
- Scrip Code: 729913
- ISIN: INE101Q14223
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,80,370.50 per unit
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 25, 2025
- Trading Start Date: July 28, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding instrument for institutional investors with CARE A1+ rating indicating high credit quality.
