## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 100 crore issued by SBICAP Securities Limited on private placement basis, effective August 29, 2025. The instruments carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ ratings with 91-day maturity.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5 lakh denomination each (2000 units)
- Total issue size: Rs. 100 crore
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,171.50 per unit (face value Rs. 5 lakh)
- Scrip Code: 730113, ISIN: INE212K14BZ0
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- State Bank of India acting as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise for trading
- Trading members must use specified ISIN number

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 28, 2025
- Listing Date: August 29, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 27, 2025
- Maturity Period: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk. Trading will be limited to institutional investors due to high denomination requirements.
