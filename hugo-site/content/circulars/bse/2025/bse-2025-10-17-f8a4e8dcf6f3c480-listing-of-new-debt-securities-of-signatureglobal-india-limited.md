## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and admission to trading of new debt securities issued by SIGNATUREGLOBAL (INDIA) LIMITED on a private placement basis. The securities were allotted on October 16, 2025, and commenced trading on the BSE Debt segment on October 17, 2025. The issuance comprises 87,500 units with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying an 11% per annum interest rate and maturing on January 15, 2029.

## Key Points

- Quantity issued: 87,500 units
- Face value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Scrip Code: 977218
- Scrip ID: 11SGIL29
- ISIN: INE903U07087
- Credit rating: CARE A+/Stable
- Interest rate: 11% per annum (paid quarterly)
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Date of allotment: October 16, 2025
- Trading mode: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE903U07087
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- Queries may be directed to the BSE debt department at contact numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Date of allotment**: October 16, 2025
- **Listing date**: October 17, 2025
- **Interest payment dates**: Quarterly from January 15, 2026 to January 15, 2029
- **First interest payment**: January 15, 2026
- **Redemption date**: January 15, 2029

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine debt security listing announcement for privately placed securities. The issuance will not have significant impact on broader market operations as it is limited to the debt segment.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Trading members dealing in debt securities need to be aware of the new listing for their clients interested in debt instruments. The securities will trade in dematerialized form with standard BSE debt segment procedures.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant only for investors interested in debt securities with CARE A+/Stable rating, offering 11% annual return with quarterly interest payments over a 3+ year tenure until January 2029.
