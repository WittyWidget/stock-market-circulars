## Summary

Unifi Mutual Fund has revised the minimum number of instalments for Systematic Transfer Plan (STP) under Direct/Regular Plan of Growth Option of Unifi Liquid Fund from 12 instalments to 6 instalments, effective from a date to be announced in 2025.

## Key Points

- Minimum STP instalments reduced from 12 to 6 for Unifi Liquid Fund
- Change applies to both Direct and Regular Plans under Growth Option
- Monthly frequency with minimum amount of Rs. 1000 and multiples of Re. 1/-
- STP instalments can be scheduled on any day except 29th, 30th and 31st
- Application forms will be modified accordingly

## Regulatory Changes

Addendum to the Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM) of Unifi Liquid Fund to reflect the revised STP parameters.

## Compliance Requirements

- Unifi Asset Management Private Limited to update all relevant scheme documents
- Application forms to be modified to reflect new minimum instalment requirements
- All other terms and conditions of SID/KIM remain unchanged

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: To be announced in 2025
- Document Date: August 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Low impact change that makes STP facility more accessible by reducing minimum commitment from 12 to 6 instalments. This administrative modification affects only Unifi Mutual Fund investors and does not impact broader market operations or other mutual fund schemes.
