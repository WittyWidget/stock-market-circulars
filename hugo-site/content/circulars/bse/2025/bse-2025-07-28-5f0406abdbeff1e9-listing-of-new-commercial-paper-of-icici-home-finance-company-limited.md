## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by ICICI Home Finance Company Limited on private placement basis. The issue consists of 1000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 50 crores, with a maturity period of approximately 3 months.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 1000 units (Total value: Rs. 50 crores)
- Issue Price: Rs. 493,069.50 per unit
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Scrip Code: 729897
- ISIN: INE071G14GY0
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt instrument listing under existing BSE debt segment rules.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Securities will be traded under BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 28, 2025
- Listing Date: July 28, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 24, 2025
- Maturity Period: Approximately 88 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by a established NBFC. The short-term nature (3 months) and private placement basis limits broader market implications. Standard debt market operations continue unchanged.
