## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of further securities issued by 8 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS). These shares will be listed and admitted for trading effective Monday, August 11, 2025. The companies include major banks like AXIS Bank and ICICI Bank, along with technology and industrial companies.

## Key Points

- 8 companies are listing additional shares under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Total of 796,377 new shares across all companies
- Trading begins Monday, August 11, 2025
- No lock-in period applies to any of the listed shares
- Face values range from Rs. 2 to Rs. 10 per share

## Company Details

**AXIS Bank Ltd. (532215)**: 188,445 shares at Rs. 2 face value
**Cyient Limited (532175)**: 2,802 shares at Rs. 5 face value
**Greaves Cotton Ltd. (501455)**: 51,850 shares at Rs. 2 face value
**ICICI Bank Ltd. (532174)**: 132,624 shares at Rs. 2 face value
**RBL Bank Limited (540065)**: 1,374,693 shares at Rs. 10 face value
**ROSSARI BIOTECH LIMITED (543213)**: 375 shares at Rs. 2 face value
**Safari Industries (India) Ltd. (523025)**: 85,369 shares at Rs. 2 face value
**Sasken Technologies Limited (532663)**: 21,220 shares at Rs. 10 face value

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 8, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: Monday, August 11, 2025
- **Lock-in Period**: None applicable

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing with minimal market impact. The shares represent employee stock options being exercised and converted to tradeable equity. No significant dilution is expected given the relatively small quantities compared to existing share capital of these established companies.
