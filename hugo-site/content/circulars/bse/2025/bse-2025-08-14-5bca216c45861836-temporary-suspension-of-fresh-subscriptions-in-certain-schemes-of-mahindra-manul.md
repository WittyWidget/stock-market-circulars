## Summary

Mahindra Manulife Investment Management has temporarily suspended fresh subscriptions in the Asia Pacific REITs Fund of Fund (FOF) effective July 31, 2025, as a precautionary measure to avoid breaching RBI's overseas investment limits. The suspension affects lumpsum subscriptions, fresh systematic registrations including STPs, and switch-ins.

## Key Points

- Temporary suspension of fresh subscriptions in Mahindra Manulife Asia Pacific REITs FOF
- Effective date: July 31, 2025
- Measure taken to avoid breach of overseas investment limits set by RBI
- Previous suspension occurred in January 2022, resumed in July 2022
- Existing SIPs/STPs registered before effective date will continue
- Intra-scheme and intra-plan switches remain unaffected

## Regulatory Changes

The suspension is implemented pursuant to:
- SEBI's email dated January 28, 2022
- AMFI communication dated January 30, 2022
- SEBI letter reference no. SEBI/HO/OW/IMD-ll/DOF3/P/25095/2022 dated June 17, 2022
- Compliance with RBI overseas investment limits

## Compliance Requirements

- AMC must ensure no breach of industry-wide overseas investment limits
- Incremental investments by designated employees must be in schemes with equivalent or higher risk-o-meter ratings
- Necessary changes to be made in Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM)
- Addendum becomes integral part of scheme documents

## Important Dates

- **July 31, 2025**: Effective date of suspension
- **February 1, 2022**: Reference date for headroom calculation
- **January 2022**: Previous suspension period
- **July 1, 2022**: Previous resumption date

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects new investors seeking exposure to Asia Pacific REITs through this FOF. Existing investors with registered SIPs/STPs can continue their investments. The temporary nature and specific scope limit broader market impact, though it restricts access to a specialized investment avenue for international real estate exposure.
