## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Sterlite Technologies Limited worth Rs. 100 crores (2000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) issued on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from August 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 2000 units (Rs. 100 crores total)
- Issue price: Rs. 487,135.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 730116, Scrip ID: STL290825
- ISIN: INE089C14DR2
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: August 29, 2025
- Date of redemption: December 26, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The Commercial Paper provides short-term funding for Sterlite Technologies Limited with a maturity period of approximately 4 months. HDFC Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying agent.
