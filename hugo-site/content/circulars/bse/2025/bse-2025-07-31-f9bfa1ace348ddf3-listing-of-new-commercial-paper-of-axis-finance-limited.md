## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Axis Finance Limited worth Rs. 50 crores (1000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on private placement basis. The commercial paper carries CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings and will mature on October 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 1000 units (Rs. 50 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,364.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip code: 729947, Scrip ID: AXFL30725
- ISIN: INE891K14PG1
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Axis Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: July 30, 2025
- Listing date: July 31, 2025
- Redemption date: October 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates low credit risk for investors.
