## Summary

AMBIT FINVEST PRIVATE LIMITED has set October 10, 2025 as the record date for part redemption of its non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE985V07108, Code: 976947) and payment of interest. The face value of the debentures will be reduced to Rs. 91,666 per debenture, effective from October 10, 2025 under settlement number DR-733/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- ISIN: INE985V07108
- BSE Code: 976947
- Security: 8.25% Non-Convertible Debentures maturing July 28, 2028
- Part redemption accompanied by interest payment
- Trading members notified of reduced face value trading

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trading in the debentures will be conducted with reduced face value effective from October 10, 2025
- Settlement number DR-733/2025-2026 applies to transactions at the reduced face value

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** October 10, 2025
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value:** October 10, 2025
- **Settlement Number:** DR-733/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

This part redemption reduces the outstanding face value of AMBIT FINVEST PRIVATE LIMITED's debentures from the original amount to Rs. 91,666 per debenture. Debenture holders will receive partial principal repayment along with interest payment. Trading members need to ensure their systems reflect the updated face value for accurate pricing and settlement of trades executed on or after October 10, 2025.
