## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Gabion Technologies India Limited on its SME platform for public comments. The document was filed on September 30, 2025 and contains all disclosures required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The public comment period is 21 days from the notice date.

## Key Points

- Company: Gabion Technologies India Limited
- Draft Offer Document filed: September 30, 2025
- Complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Public comments invited
- Notice Number: 20251006-8
- Segment: SME

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard SME IPO disclosure process.

## Compliance Requirements

- The Draft Offer Document is hosted on www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public stakeholders may submit comments within 21 days from October 06, 2025
- Comments should be sent to: equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document Filing Date: September 30, 2025
- Notice Date: October 06, 2025
- Public Comment Deadline: October 27, 2025 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market impact and is relevant primarily to:
- Potential investors interested in Gabion Technologies India Limited SME IPO
- Market participants wishing to provide feedback on the draft offer document
- The company and its advisors preparing for the IPO

No immediate trading or compliance impact on existing market participants.
