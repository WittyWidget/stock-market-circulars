## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited issued on private placement basis. The securities carry a 9% annual interest rate and will mature on June 11, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 16,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Face Value: ₹1,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: ₹1,00,000 per security
- Interest Rate: 9% per annum, paid annually
- Credit Rating: CARE AA/Stable
- ISIN: INE918K07PY9
- Scrip Code: 977024

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialised form
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Trading members should refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 12, 2025
- Listing Date: August 13, 2025
- Maturity Date: June 11, 2027
- Interest Payment: Annually

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis. The listing expands BSE's debt segment offerings but has no broader operational or regulatory implications for market participants.
