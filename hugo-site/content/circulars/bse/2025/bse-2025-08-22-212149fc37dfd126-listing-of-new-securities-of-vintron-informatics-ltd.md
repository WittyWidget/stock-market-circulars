## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 93,14,997 new equity shares of Vintron Informatics Ltd. (Scrip Code: 517393) issued through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis to promoters and non-promoters. The shares will commence trading on August 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- 93,14,997 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at Rs. 4/- premium
- Issue price: Rs. 5/- per share
- Allotment date: May 22, 2025
- ISIN: INE043B01028
- Distribution numbers: 122519134 to 131834130
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- Lock-in provisions apply to the allotted shares as specified

## Important Dates

- **Trading commencement**: August 25, 2025
- **Allotment date**: May 22, 2025
- **Lock-in expiry**: February 28, 2026 (39,81,667 shares) and February 28, 2027 (53,33,330 shares)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of new shares for a single company. The lock-in provisions indicate controlled release of shares into the market over the next 2-3 years.
