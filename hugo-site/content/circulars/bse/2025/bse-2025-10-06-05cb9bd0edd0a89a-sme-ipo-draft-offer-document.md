## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document of NOVUS LOYALTY LIMITED filed on September 30, 2025 for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will be available on the exchange website for at least 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by NOVUS LOYALTY LIMITED on September 30, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comment period: 21 days from October 06, 2025
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Stakeholders wishing to provide comments on the Draft Offer Document must submit them within 21 days from October 06, 2025 to the email address equity.ipo@bseindia.com.

## Important Dates

- **Draft Document Filing Date**: September 30, 2025
- **Notice Date**: October 06, 2025
- **Comment Period**: 21 days from October 06, 2025 (deadline: October 27, 2025)

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard procedural notice for an SME IPO process. The impact is limited to potential investors and stakeholders of NOVUS LOYALTY LIMITED who wish to review the draft offer document and submit comments during the public consultation period.
