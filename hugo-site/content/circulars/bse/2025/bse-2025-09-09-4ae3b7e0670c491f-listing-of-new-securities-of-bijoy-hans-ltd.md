## Summary

BSE has listed 45,00,000 new equity shares of Bijoy Hans Ltd (Scrip Code: 524723) issued to promoters on a preferential basis. The shares are priced at Rs. 12.50 each (face value Rs. 10 + premium Rs. 2.50) and will commence trading from September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- 45,00,000 new equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at Rs. 12.50/-
- Issued to promoters on preferential basis with Rs. 2.50 premium
- Trading effective from Wednesday, September 10, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 3000022 to 7500021
- ISIN: INE491D01017

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities listing
- Lock-in period compliance required for all 45,00,000 shares until April 30, 2027

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** May 27, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** September 10, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends:** April 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This preferential allotment increases Bijoy Hans Ltd's share capital by 45 lakh shares. The 2-year lock-in period ensures these shares cannot be traded by promoters until April 2027, maintaining stability in promoter holding. The premium of Rs. 2.50 over face value indicates positive market sentiment for the company's prospects.
