## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in non-convertible debentures of NUVAMA WEALTH FINANCE LIMITED (ISIN: INE918K07IT4, Code: 973960) effective August 8, 2025, due to the company setting a record date for debenture redemption.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to NUVAMA WEALTH FINANCE LIMITED debentures
- ISIN code: INE918K07IT4 (973960)
- Trading members advised not to deal in these debentures
- Notice issued under DR-691/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - standard operational procedure for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the specified debentures
- Trading members required to take note of the suspension

## Important Dates

- Record Date: August 8, 2025
- No Dealings From: August 8, 2025
- Notice Date: August 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only one company's debt instrument. Standard procedure for debenture redemption with temporary trading suspension to facilitate corporate action processing.
