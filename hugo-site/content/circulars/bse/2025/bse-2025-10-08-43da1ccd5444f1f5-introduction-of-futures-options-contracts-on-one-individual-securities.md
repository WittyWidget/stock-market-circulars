## Summary

BSE has announced the introduction of futures and options contracts for Hitachi Energy India Ltd (scrip code 543187) in the Equity Derivatives segment. The contracts will be available for trading from October 31, 2025. Contract specifications will be published in the derivatives contract master file on October 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Futures and options contracts to be introduced for Hitachi Energy India Ltd
- Underlying scrip code in equity segment: 543187
- Trading to commence on October 31, 2025
- Contract details will be available in derivatives master file generated end of day October 30, 2025
- Contract master file accessible through BSE extranet and BseIndia website
- Notice applicable to all trading members

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an expansion of existing derivative product offerings by adding one new underlying security to the equity derivatives segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new contracts available from October 31, 2025
- Members should review contract specifications from the derivatives contract master file
- Members need to ensure their systems are updated to support trading in these new contracts
- Risk management frameworks should be adjusted to account for the new derivative contracts

## Important Dates

- **October 8, 2025**: Notice date
- **October 30, 2025**: Derivatives contract master file with contract details to be generated (end of day)
- **October 31, 2025**: Trading in futures and options contracts for Hitachi Energy India Ltd commences

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The introduction of F&O contracts on Hitachi Energy India Ltd will provide enhanced trading and hedging opportunities for market participants interested in this stock. This may increase liquidity and trading volumes for the underlying equity.

**Operational Impact**: Low to Medium - Trading members need to update their systems and familiarize themselves with the new contract specifications. The impact is limited as it involves adding one security to existing derivative infrastructure.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Investors and traders gain additional derivative instruments for speculation, hedging, and arbitrage strategies specific to Hitachi Energy India Ltd. This enhances risk management capabilities for holders of the underlying stock.
