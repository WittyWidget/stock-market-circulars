## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 12,62,328 new equity shares of Pondy Oxides & Chemicals Limited (Scrip Code: 532626) effective September 9, 2025. These shares were issued through preferential allotment to promoters and non-promoters pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 12,62,328 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each issued at premium of Rs. 248.50/-
- Issue price: Rs. 253.50/- per share
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 28824690 to 30087017
- Date of allotment: June 23, 2025
- ISIN: INE063E01053

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and trading permissions effective from the specified date.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: June 23, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: September 9, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (Majority)**: March 31, 2027 (1,183,432 shares)
- **Lock-in Expiry (Remaining)**: March 31, 2026 (78,896 shares)

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases the company's equity base and provides additional liquidity. The significant lock-in period until March 2027 for majority shares indicates long-term commitment from allottees. Impact is company-specific with minimal broader market implications.
