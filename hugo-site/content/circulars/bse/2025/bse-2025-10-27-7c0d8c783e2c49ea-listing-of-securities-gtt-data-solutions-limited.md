## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 3,582,068 equity shares of GTT Data Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 530457) issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis. The shares will be available for trading from October 28, 2025, with a lock-in period until April 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- 3,582,068 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 120/-
- Issue price: Rs. 130.00 per share
- Allotted to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinctive Numbers: 19161916 to 22743983
- ISIN: INE959B01017
- Date of Allotment: April 18, 2025
- Trading commences: October 28, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification following preferential share allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to update their systems for the new securities
- All 3,582,068 shares are subject to lock-in restrictions and cannot be transferred until April 30, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: April 18, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 28, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Expiry**: April 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a company-specific listing event. The preferential allotment increases GTT Data Solutions Limited's equity base by 3,582,068 shares. The lock-in period until April 30, 2026 ensures these shares will not create immediate selling pressure. Trading members need to be aware of the additional securities for accurate position tracking and settlement.
