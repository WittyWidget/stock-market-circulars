## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 2,945 new equity shares of Grasim Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 500300) pursuant to conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Rs. 2/- each issued on rights basis. Trading members are permitted to trade in these securities effective October 16, 2025.

## Key Points

- 2,945 equity shares being listed
- Shares result from conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up
- Face value: Rs. 2/- per share
- Originally issued on rights basis
- ISIN: INE047A01021
- Distinctive Numbers: 680456445 to 680459389
- Shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are permitted to trade in the newly listed securities through the normal trading process. No specific compliance actions required.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 15, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date:** October 16, 2025 (Thursday)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a routine administrative listing of a small number of shares (2,945) resulting from conversion of partly paid to fully paid shares from a previous rights issue. The shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares of Grasim Industries Ltd. and represent a negligible percentage of the company's total equity base.
