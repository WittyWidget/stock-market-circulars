## Summary

GIC Housing Finance Ltd. has listed new commercial paper securities worth Rs. 15 crores (3,000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) on BSE's debt segment. The commercial papers are issued on private placement basis with maturity on May 22, 2026.

## Key Points

- 3,000 commercial paper units of Rs. 5,00,000 each listed
- Scrip Code: 730019, ISIN: INE289B14JV9
- Issue price: Rs. 4,75,042.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Allotment date: August 13, 2025
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Ltd. acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Listing effective: August 14, 2025
- Allotment date: August 13, 2025  
- Redemption date: May 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk. The 9-month tenure provides short-term debt instrument option for investors.
