## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 13,83,000 new equity shares of Precision Wires India Ltd (Scrip Code: 523539) issued through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will commence trading from September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- 13,83,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at premium of Rs. 150/-
- Issue price: Rs. 151.00 per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 178657976 to 180040975
- ISIN: INE372C01037

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure for warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability for trading
- Lock-in period compliance required until March 23, 2026
- Standard trading and settlement procedures applicable

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 29, 2025
- Trading Commencement: September 10, 2025
- Lock-in Period Ends: March 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this represents a standard warrant conversion resulting in additional share capital. The lock-in period until March 2026 ensures these shares cannot be traded immediately, limiting any potential dilution effect on existing shareholders.
