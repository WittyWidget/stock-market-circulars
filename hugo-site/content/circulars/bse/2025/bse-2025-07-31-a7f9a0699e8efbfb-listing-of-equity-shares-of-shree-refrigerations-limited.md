## Summary

BSE Limited has announced that equity shares of SHREE REFRIGERATIONS LIMITED will be listed and admitted to trading on the Exchange's SME segment in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544458 and symbol SHREEREF.

## Key Points

- Company Name: SHREE REFRIGERATIONS LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544458
- Symbol: SHREEREF
- ISIN: INE0FMZ01045
- Segment: SME
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the upcoming listing and should prepare for trading activities once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 31, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a standard new company listing on the SME segment. The actual listing date and trading details will be provided in a subsequent notice.
