## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of Redington Limited worth Rs. 100 crore (2000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective September 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper issue size: Rs. 100 crore (2000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,97,907.50 per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+ (highest short-term rating)
- Trading only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE891D14A38
- Standard trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: September 5, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: September 30, 2025
- **Maturity Period**: 26 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing with short 26-day maturity. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Standard trading arrangements with no special conditions or restrictions.
