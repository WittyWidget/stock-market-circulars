## Summary

BSE has updated the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective August 05, 2025. Seven securities have been newly shortlisted for LT-ASM, while no securities are being moved to higher or lower ASM stages, moved to direct stage IV, or removed from the framework.

## Key Points

- 7 securities newly shortlisted in Long Term ASM Framework
- No securities moving to higher or lower ASM stages
- No securities moving out of LT-ASM framework
- Consolidated list includes securities across different ASM stages (I, II, III, IV)
- Framework applies surveillance measures based on trading patterns and risk assessment

## Regulatory Changes

Implementation of Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure on identified securities to monitor and control unusual trading activities and price movements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities under LT-ASM subject to additional surveillance parameters
- Enhanced monitoring of trading activities for listed securities
- Compliance with surveillance framework requirements for affected companies

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 05, 2025 - New LT-ASM framework implementation

## Impact Assessment

The surveillance measures will impose additional trading restrictions and monitoring on the identified securities. This may affect liquidity and trading volumes for the affected stocks. Investors should be aware of the enhanced surveillance parameters when trading these securities.
