## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 100 bonus equity shares of Tata Elxsi Ltd (Scrip Code: 500408) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares will be available for trading from Wednesday, September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- 100 bonus equity shares of Rs. 10/- each previously kept in abeyance
- ISIN: INE670A01012
- Distinctive numbers range from 31177361 to 31177460
- Trading permitted from September 10, 2025
- Notice issued by Deputy Vice President Ashok Kumar Singh

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities availability for trading.

## Important Dates

- **September 10, 2025**: Effective date for trading of the bonus shares

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves only 100 shares that were previously issued but kept in abeyance. This is an administrative listing rather than a new issuance.
