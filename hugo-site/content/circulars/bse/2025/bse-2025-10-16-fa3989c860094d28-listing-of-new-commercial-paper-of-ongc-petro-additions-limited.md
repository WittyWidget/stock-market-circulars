## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by ONGC Petro Additions Limited on private placement basis effective from 16th October 2025. The listing comprises 8000 units of Commercial Papers with face value of Rs.5,00,000 each, totaling Rs.400 crores. The instruments are rated CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ and will mature on 27th February 2026.

## Key Points

- 8000 Commercial Paper units listed on BSE Debt segment
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs.5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs.4,88,561.50 per unit (discount of Rs.11,438.50)
- Scrip Code: 730394
- Scrip ID: OPAL151025
- ISIN: INE163N14584
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under existing debt market framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE163N14584
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should contact BSE Debt Department at specified numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: 16th October 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: 15th October 2025
- Listing Effective Date: 16th October 2025
- Redemption Date: 27th February 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market impact as it represents a routine short-term debt instrument listing. The Commercial Papers provide a 135-day investment option for institutional investors seeking highly-rated short-term debt instruments. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates strong creditworthiness. The discount-based issuance at Rs.4,88,561.50 against face value of Rs.5,00,000 offers investors a return through the discount mechanism. Impact is limited to debt market participants and does not affect equity markets or broader market operations.
