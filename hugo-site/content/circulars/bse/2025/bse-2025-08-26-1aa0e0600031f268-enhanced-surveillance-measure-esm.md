## Summary

BSE has updated its Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework effective August 28, 2025. The update includes addition of 3 new securities to ESM, movement of 13 securities to higher ESM stages, and a consolidated list of all securities currently under ESM framework with their respective stages.

## Key Points

- 3 new securities added to ESM framework: IZMO Ltd, Oceanic Foods Ltd, and Subam Papers Ltd
- 13 securities moved to higher ESM stages including AGS Transact Technologies, Containerway International, and others
- No securities moved to lower ESM stages
- No securities removed from ESM framework
- Consolidated list shows securities across ESM Stage I and Stage II

## Regulatory Changes

- Enhanced surveillance measures will apply to newly added securities
- Existing securities under ESM will face stricter surveillance with higher stage classifications
- Framework continues focus on risk management and market integrity

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must comply with enhanced surveillance requirements for affected securities
- Trading in ESM securities subject to additional monitoring and restrictions
- Brokers and investors should be aware of enhanced surveillance conditions

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 28, 2025 - All ESM framework changes take effect

## Impact Assessment

- **Trading Impact**: Securities under ESM face enhanced monitoring which may affect trading volumes and investor participation
- **Market Risk**: Higher surveillance stages indicate increased regulatory scrutiny for risk management
- **Investor Awareness**: Market participants need to understand ESM implications for affected securities
- **Operational Impact**: Exchanges and brokers must implement enhanced monitoring systems for ESM securities
