## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of SIHORA INDUSTRIES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544585) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment effective Tuesday, November 04, 2025. This change follows Exchange Notice No. 220251016-70 dated October 16, 2025. The company's shares will be shifted to M Group as part of this transition.

## Key Points

- Company: SIHORA INDUSTRIES LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544585
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Tuesday, November 04, 2025
- Segment: SME
- Notice references continuation of Exchange Notice No. 220251016-70 dated October 16, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will transition from a Trade for Trade settlement mechanism to a Rolling settlement mechanism. This represents a change in the trading and settlement framework applicable to the scrip.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should:
- Note the change in trading segment effective November 04, 2025
- Adjust their systems and processes to accommodate the new M Group classification
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain on Tel. No. 022-2272 8822 for further details or clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: Friday, October 17, 2025
- **Effective Date**: Tuesday, November 04, 2025
- **Previous Notice**: October 16, 2025 (Notice No. 220251016-70)

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact**: The shift from Trade for Trade (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) will change the settlement cycle and trading mechanism for SIHORA INDUSTRIES LIMITED shares. Rolling settlement typically allows for more liquid trading compared to Trade for Trade, which requires full upfront delivery. This change may improve liquidity and trading flexibility for the scrip.

**Market Participants**: Trading members dealing in this scrip need to update their internal systems and processes to reflect the new group classification before the effective date.
