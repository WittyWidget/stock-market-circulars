## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) Limited worth Rs. 48.5 crores on private placement basis. The securities will trade on BSE Debt segment from August 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- 9,700 Commercial Paper units of Rs. 5 lakh each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 493,424.50 per unit (face value Rs. 5 lakh)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip code: 730085, ISIN: INE128M14AR3
- State Bank of India is the Issuing and Paying agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 25, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 26, 2025
- Redemption date: November 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding option for L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) Limited operations.
