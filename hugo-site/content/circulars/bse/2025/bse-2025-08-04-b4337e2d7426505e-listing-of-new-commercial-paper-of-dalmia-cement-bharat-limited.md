## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited worth Rs. 175 crores (3,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective August 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 3,500 units (Rs. 175 crores total)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,885 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip code: 729956, ISIN: INE755K14FK1
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- IDBI Bank Ltd serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 1, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 4, 2025
- Redemption date: October 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding option for Dalmia Cement and investment opportunity for institutional investors in highly-rated commercial paper.
