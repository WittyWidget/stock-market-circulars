## Summary

BSE mandates all Investment Advisers to submit compliance confirmation for Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 requirements through specified online form by August 31, 2025.

## Key Points

- References SEBI Circular SEBI/HO/ITD-1/ITD_VIAP/P/CIR/2025/111 dated July 31, 2025
- References BSE Circular No. 20250807-12 dated August 07, 2025
- Compliance confirmation required for point no.5 (1) of the circular
- Online submission mandatory through Microsoft Forms platform

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced - this is a compliance confirmation requirement for existing Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 obligations.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Investment Advisers must submit compliance confirmation
- Use designated online form at https://forms.office.com/r/MAa9t56PP1
- Confirm compliance with point no.5 (1) of referenced SEBI circular
- Ensure all disabilities act requirements are being followed

## Important Dates

- **Submission Deadline**: August 31, 2025
- **Reference Circular Date**: July 31, 2025 (SEBI)
- **BSE Circular Date**: August 07, 2025

## Impact Assessment

High compliance impact for Investment Advisers who must ensure they meet accessibility requirements and submit mandatory confirmation. Non-compliance may result in regulatory action. Medium market impact as this affects operational procedures rather than trading activities.
