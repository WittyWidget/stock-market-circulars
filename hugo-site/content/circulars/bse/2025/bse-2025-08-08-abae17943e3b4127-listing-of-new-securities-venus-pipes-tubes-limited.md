## Summary

BSE has listed 61,000 new equity shares of Venus Pipes & Tubes Limited (Scrip Code: 543528) effective August 11, 2025. These shares were issued to promoters on a preferential basis at Rs. 1700 per share pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 61,000 equity shares of Rs. 10 each issued at premium of Rs. 1690
- Issue price: Rs. 1700 per share
- Allotted to promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 20431111 to 20492110
- Allotment date: June 19, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 11, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard listing procedure for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and trading permissions.

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: June 19, 2025
- Trading Start Date: August 11, 2025
- Lock-in Period Ends: February 27, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal immediate market impact as all 61,000 shares are subject to lock-in until February 27, 2027. The preferential allotment to promoters strengthens their holding without affecting free float for trading.
