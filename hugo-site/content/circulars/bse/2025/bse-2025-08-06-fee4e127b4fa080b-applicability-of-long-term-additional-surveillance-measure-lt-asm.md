## Summary

BSE has updated the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective August 7, 2025. One new security, Shree Pacetronix Ltd (Scrip Code: 527005), has been added to the LT-ASM framework. No securities are moving between ASM stages or exiting the framework.

## Key Points

- Shree Pacetronix Ltd (INE847D01010) newly added to LT-ASM framework
- No securities moving to higher or lower ASM stages
- No securities moving directly to Stage IV LT-ASM
- No securities exiting the LT-ASM framework
- Consolidated list shows 19+ securities currently under various ASM stages

## Regulatory Changes

- Implementation of LT-ASM framework updates per BSE notice 20210604-41
- Securities under LT-ASM subject to additional trading restrictions and surveillance
- Framework includes multiple stages (I, IV) with varying levels of oversight

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must comply with additional surveillance measures for listed securities
- Enhanced monitoring and reporting requirements for securities under LT-ASM
- Adherence to specific trading protocols for affected scrips

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 7, 2025
- Changes applicable from market opening on the effective date

## Impact Assessment

- Limited market impact with only one new security added
- Increased trading restrictions and surveillance for Shree Pacetronix Ltd
- Continued oversight for existing securities in the framework
- Framework designed to address price manipulation and ensure market integrity
