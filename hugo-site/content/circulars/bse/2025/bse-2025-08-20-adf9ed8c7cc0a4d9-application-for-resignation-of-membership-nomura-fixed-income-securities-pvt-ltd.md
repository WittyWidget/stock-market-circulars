## Summary

Nomura Fixed Income Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member no. 4064) has formally applied for resignation of its BSE membership in accordance with exchange rules and regulations. Trading members have 15 days to raise objections.

## Key Points

- Member applying: Nomura Fixed Income Securities Pvt. Ltd. (Member no. 4064)
- Application submitted under BSE Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations
- 15-day objection period for trading members
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members with objections must submit them in writing within 15 days
- Objections must be sent to BSE Membership department
- Deadline for objections: September 3, 2025

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: August 20, 2025
- Objection Deadline: September 3, 2025 (15 days from notice)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine membership resignation process. The resignation will reduce the number of active trading members by one but is unlikely to affect market operations or liquidity significantly.
