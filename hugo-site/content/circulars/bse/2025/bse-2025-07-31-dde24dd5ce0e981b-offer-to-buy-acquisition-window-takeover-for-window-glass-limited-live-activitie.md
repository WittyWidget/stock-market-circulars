## Summary

BSE announces the schedule for Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for Window Glass Limited shares, running from Thursday, July 31, 2025, to Wednesday, August 13, 2025. The offer is for up to 1,75,684 equity shares at Rs. 717.50 per share.

## Key Points

- Security: Window Glass Limited (WINDOW, INE902K01018, Scrip Code 999949)
- Offer size: Up to 1,75,684 equity shares
- Offer price: Rs. 717.50 per share
- Face value: Rs. 10.00
- Market lot: 1 share
- Opening date: July 31, 2025
- Closing date: August 13, 2025

## Trading Schedule

- **Bid Offering Period**: July 31 - August 13, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up**: July 31 - August 12, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up (last day)**: August 13, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Take-up (last day)**: August 13, 2025 (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)
