## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs 225 crore issued by Bajaj Finance Limited on private placement basis. The securities will trade on BSE Debt segment with effect from September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of face value Rs 5,00,000 each (45,000 units)
- Total issue size: Rs 225 crore
- Issue price: Rs 4,92,159.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730179, Scrip ID: BFL090925
- ISIN Number: INE296A14C22
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 9, 2025
- Listing Date: September 10, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 9, 2025
- Maturity Period: 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The Commercial Paper provides short-term funding for Bajaj Finance Limited and offers investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
