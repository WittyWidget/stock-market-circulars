## Summary

360 ONE WAM LIMITED has listed 5,500 units of new commercial paper on BSE's debt segment through private placement, each with face value of Rs. 5 lakhs, totaling Rs. 275 crores. The securities carry CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper quantity: 5,500 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total issue size: Rs. 275 crores
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,508 per unit
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 730108, ISIN: INE466L14EU5
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 26, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 28, 2025
- Redemption date: November 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding option for 360 ONE WAM LIMITED with 3-month maturity. No significant operational changes for market participants.
