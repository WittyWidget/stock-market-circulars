## Summary

BSE has announced the allocation of shares to anchor investors in the public issue of Game Changers Texfab Limited. This circular provides details of the anchor investor portion allocation prior to the opening of the public issue.

## Key Points

- Public issue of Game Changers Texfab Limited has completed anchor investor allocation
- Anchor investor portion allocated before the public issue opens to retail and institutional investors
- Details of anchor investor allocation as per SEBI regulations
- Anchor lock-in period and other terms apply as per SEBI ICDR Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard disclosure as per SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2018.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants to note the anchor investor allocation details
- Anchor investors subject to lock-in requirements as per SEBI regulations
- Company and merchant bankers to comply with SEBI ICDR Regulations

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: October 27, 2025
- Anchor investor allocation completed
- Public issue dates to be announced separately

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Anchor investor allocation indicates institutional interest in the IPO and provides price discovery ahead of the public issue opening.

**Investor Impact:** Medium - Important information for investors considering participating in the public issue, as anchor allocation demonstrates institutional demand and helps establish issue pricing.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Standard IPO process milestone with no operational changes for market participants.
