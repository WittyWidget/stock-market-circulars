## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper of Can Fin Homes Limited issued on private placement basis. The instrument consists of 10,000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each, totaling Rs. 500 crores, with a tenure of 3 months from July 28, 2025 to October 27, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 10,000 units (total value Rs. 500 crores)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,727.50 per unit
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Scrip Code: 729926
- ISIN: INE477A14DY9
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applicable
- Trading members must comply with BSE debt segment requirements

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 28, 2025
- Listing Date: July 29, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 27, 2025
- Trading Commencement: July 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding option for Can Fin Homes Limited and offers investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt market segment.
