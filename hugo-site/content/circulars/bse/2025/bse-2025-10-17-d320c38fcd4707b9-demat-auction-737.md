## Summary

BSE has announced demat auction settlement no. 737 dated October 17, 2025. The circular lists 100 securities (50 pairs) with their respective scrip codes, names, and quantities available for auction. This includes major stocks like HDFC Bank (29 shares), Bajaj Auto (1 share), ITC (1 share), and Tata Steel (1 share), as well as numerous mid and small-cap securities with varying quantities ranging from 1 to 74,441 shares.

## Key Points

- Settlement number: 737
- Auction date: October 17, 2025
- Total of 100 securities listed across multiple sectors
- Quantities range from 1 share (multiple securities) to 74,441 shares (Welcure Drugs)
- Includes major blue-chip stocks and smaller securities
- Largest quantity: Welcure Drug (74,441 shares)
- Other significant quantities: Centrum Capital (20,000 shares), Jaiprakash Power (9,000 shares), Blue Jet Healthcare (7,500 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard operational circular for demat auction proceedings.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members and clearing members with pending delivery obligations must participate in the auction
- Members must settle their outstanding delivery obligations through the auction mechanism
- Auction participation is mandatory for members with shortages in the listed securities

## Important Dates

- **Auction Date**: October 17, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: 737

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects trading members and clearing members with pending delivery obligations across 100 securities. The auction mechanism ensures settlement of failed trades and maintains market integrity.

**Operational Impact**: Clearing members with shortages in the listed securities must participate in the auction to fulfill their delivery obligations. The wide range of quantities and diverse securities indicates scattered delivery failures across multiple brokers.

**Stock-Specific Impact**: Securities with larger auction quantities (Welcure Drug - 74,441 shares, Centrum Capital - 20,000 shares, Jaiprakash Power - 9,000 shares) may experience temporary price volatility during auction proceedings.
