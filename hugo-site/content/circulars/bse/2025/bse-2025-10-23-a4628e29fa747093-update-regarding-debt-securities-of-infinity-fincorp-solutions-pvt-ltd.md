## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding debt securities of Infinity Fincorp Solutions Pvt Ltd (Scrip Code: 976594). Following receipt of call money from debenture holders, the ISIN and paid-up value of the listed debt securities will be revised effective October 24, 2025. This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20250806-43 dated August 6, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company has received call money from debenture holders on specified debt securities
- ISIN will change from INE00VD07024 to INE00VD07032
- Paid-up value will increase from Rs. 10,000/- per debenture to Rs. 1,00,000/- per debenture
- Changes apply to Scrip Code: 976594
- This update is in continuation of previous notice dated August 6, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative update reflecting the receipt of call money and corresponding changes to debt security parameters.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised ISIN and paid-up value for trading purposes
- Systems should be updated to reflect the new ISIN (INE00VD07032) and paid-up value
- Debenture holders should be aware of the increased paid-up value per debenture

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 23, 2025
- **Effective Date:** October 24, 2025 (Friday) - New ISIN and paid-up value applicable from this date
- **Reference Notice:** August 6, 2025 (Exchange Notice No. 20250806-43)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - affects specific debt securities of Infinity Fincorp Solutions Pvt Ltd. The 10x increase in paid-up value (from Rs. 10,000 to Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture) reflects the call money received and will impact trading and settlement of these securities.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members dealing with these specific debt securities must update their systems with the new ISIN and paid-up value. Debenture holders will see their holdings reflected under the new ISIN with revised face value.

**Affected Parties:** Trading members, debenture holders of Infinity Fincorp Solutions Pvt Ltd, and market participants dealing with Scrip Code 976594.
