## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Krazybee Services Private Limited worth ₹42.5 crore issued on private placement basis. The securities carry a 10.65% interest rate and mature on August 12, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 42,500 securities with face value of ₹1,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 977022, ISIN: INE07HK07825
- Interest Rate: 10.65% per annum paid monthly
- Credit Rating: CARE A Stable
- Issue Price: ₹1,00,000 per security
- Date of Allotment: August 12, 2025
- Securities tradeable only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities to be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Members can refer to Placement Memorandum on BSE website for detailed information

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: August 13, 2025
- Date of Allotment: August 12, 2025
- Interest Payment: Monthly
- Redemption Date: August 12, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The securities provide institutional investors access to Krazybee Services' debt instruments with monthly interest payments and CARE A Stable rating.
