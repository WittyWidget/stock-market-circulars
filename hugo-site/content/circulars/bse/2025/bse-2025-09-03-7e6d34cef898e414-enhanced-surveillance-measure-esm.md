## Summary

BSE has updated its Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) Framework effective September 4, 2025, adding 4 new securities to ESM monitoring and moving 8 existing securities to higher ESM stages. The circular provides comprehensive listings of all securities under various ESM stages.

## Key Points

- 4 new securities added to ESM Framework: Ambitious Plastomac Company Ltd, Kaushalya Infrastructure Development Corporation Ltd, Shankar Lal Rampal Dye-Chem Ltd, and Vaxfab Enterprises Ltd
- 8 securities moved to higher ESM stages including Concord Drugs Ltd, Landmarc Leisure Corporation Ltd, and others
- No securities moved to lower ESM stages or exited the framework
- Consolidated list shows securities across different ESM stages (Stage I and Stage II)
- Framework includes both regular securities and SME scrips

## Regulatory Changes

- Enhanced surveillance measures applied to securities showing unusual price movements or trading patterns
- Staged approach with escalating surveillance levels based on continued irregular activity
- Framework aligned with NSE for certain securities as indicated

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities under ESM face additional disclosure requirements
- Enhanced monitoring of price movements and trading volumes
- Increased scrutiny of corporate announcements and compliance
- Trading members must ensure awareness of ESM status for affected securities

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 4, 2025 - All ESM framework changes become applicable

## Impact Assessment

- Affected securities may experience reduced liquidity due to enhanced surveillance
- Investors should exercise additional caution when trading ESM securities
- Companies under ESM framework face heightened regulatory oversight
- Stage progression indicates continued concern about trading patterns
- No relief provided as no securities moved to lower stages or exited framework
