## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of MONARCH SURVEYORS AND ENGINEERING CONSULTANTS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544453) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment, shifting to M Group classification effective August 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: MONARCH SURVEYORS AND ENGINEERING CONSULTANTS LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544453
- Current segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: August 12, 2025
- Contact person: Mr. Prasad Bhide (022-2272 8388)

## Regulatory Changes

The stock will move from restrictive Trade for Trade segment to more liquid Rolling segment, allowing for normal intraday trading and settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the segment change and update their systems accordingly for the new trading mechanics effective from the specified date.

## Important Dates

- **August 12, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer
- **July 29, 2025**: Notice date
- **July 28, 2025**: Reference to previous exchange notice 20250728-56

## Impact Assessment

Positive impact for investors as the move to Rolling segment typically indicates improved liquidity and allows for normal trading compared to the restrictive Trade for Trade segment. This change suggests the company meets criteria for regular trading segment classification.
