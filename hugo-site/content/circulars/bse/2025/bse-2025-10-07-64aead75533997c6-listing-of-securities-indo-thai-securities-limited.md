## Summary

BSE has listed 2,000,000 equity shares of Indo Thai Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 533676) issued on preferential basis to non-promoters pursuant to warrant conversion. The shares are available for trading from October 8, 2025, with a lock-in period until April 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- 2 million equity shares of face value Rs. 1.00 each issued at premium of Rs. 36.60
- Issue price: Rs. 37.60 per share
- Allotted to non-promoters on preferential basis through warrant conversion
- Allotment date: July 30, 2025
- Distinguished numbers: 118590191 to 120590190
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE337M01021

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of new securities
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 2 million shares until April 30, 2026
- Shares issued pursuant to warrant conversion on preferential basis must comply with SEBI regulations

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 30, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 8, 2025 (Wednesday)
- **Lock-in Expiry**: April 30, 2026
- **Notice Date**: October 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific listing event for Indo Thai Securities Limited. The preferential allotment of 2 million shares increases the company's equity base and may lead to minor dilution for existing shareholders.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing process with no changes to trading mechanisms. The lock-in period prevents immediate selling pressure from allottees.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant only to Indo Thai Securities Limited shareholders and potential investors. The warrant conversion indicates fund-raising activity by the company, with proceeds likely used for business operations or expansion.
