## Summary

Paisalo Digital Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE's Debt segment effective August 11, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 100,000 each and carry an interest rate of 9.75% per annum.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 3,400 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 977004, Scrip ID: 975PDL28
- ISIN Number: INE420C07148
- Credit Rating: IVR AA/Stable
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Interest Rate: 9.75% per annum (quarterly payments)
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE420C07148
- Trading members should refer to Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for detailed information
- Queries can be directed to debt department at specified contact numbers

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 8, 2025
- Listing Date: August 11, 2025
- Interest Payment Dates: Quarterly from November 8, 2025 to August 8, 2028
- Redemption Date: August 8, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities. The listing provides additional investment options for institutional investors in the debt market segment.
