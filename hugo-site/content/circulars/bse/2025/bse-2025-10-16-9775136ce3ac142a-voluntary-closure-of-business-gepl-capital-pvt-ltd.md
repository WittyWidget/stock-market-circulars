## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that GEPL Capital Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 724) will voluntarily cease its business operations in the Currency derivative segment effective October 17, 2025. This is a voluntary closure initiated by the member.

## Key Points

- GEPL Capital Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 724) is closing its Currency derivative segment operations
- Closure is voluntary and initiated by the member
- Effective date: Friday, October 17, 2025
- Other trading members have been informed of this closure
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a member-specific operational closure.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for other trading members. This notice is informational only.

## Important Dates

- **October 16, 2025**: Notice date
- **October 17, 2025**: Effective date of business closure in Currency derivative segment

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this closure is minimal as it affects only one trading member's operations in the Currency derivative segment. Other market participants are not required to take any action. This is a routine administrative notification regarding a member's voluntary business closure decision.
