## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 200 crore issued by Birla Group Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis. The securities are available for trading on BSE Debt segment from September 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5 lakh denomination each (4,000 units)
- Total issue size: Rs. 200 crore
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,785 per unit (face value Rs. 5 lakh)
- Scrip Code: 730151, Scrip ID: BGHPL4925
- ISIN: INE09OL14HU4

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applicable
- Axis Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 4, 2025
- Trading Commencement: September 4, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term funding option for Birla Group Holdings and adds to available debt securities for institutional investors.
