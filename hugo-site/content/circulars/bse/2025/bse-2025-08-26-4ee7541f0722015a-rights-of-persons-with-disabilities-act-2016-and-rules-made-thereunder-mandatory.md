## Summary

BSE requires all trading members to submit confirmation of compliance with Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 requirements by August 31, 2025, following SEBI circular guidelines.

## Key Points

- Trading members must confirm compliance with point no.5 (1) of SEBI circular SEBI/HO/ITD-1/ITD_VIAP/P/CIR/2025/111
- Submission deadline is August 31, 2025
- Confirmation must be sent via email to membership.ops@bseindia.com
- This follows previous BSE circular 20250806-17 dated August 6, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced - this is a follow-up requiring compliance confirmation for existing Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading members must submit compliance confirmation
- Email submission to membership.ops@bseindia.com
- Must address specific point no.5 (1) from the referenced SEBI circular
- Mandatory compliance with Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 and associated rules

## Important Dates

- **August 31, 2025**: Deadline for compliance confirmation submission
- **July 31, 2025**: Original SEBI circular date
- **August 6, 2025**: Previous BSE circular date

## Impact Assessment

Administrative impact on trading members who must ensure compliance documentation and submit confirmations. No direct market trading impact, but non-compliance could result in regulatory action against trading members.
