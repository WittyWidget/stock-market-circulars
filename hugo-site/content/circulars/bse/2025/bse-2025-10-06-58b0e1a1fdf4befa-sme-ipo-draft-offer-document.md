## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Jain International Power Limited filed on September 30, 2025 for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will be available on the BSE SME website for a minimum period of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Jain International Power Limited on September 30, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Comments can be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard notification for SME IPO draft document availability.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants and public can review the Draft Offer Document
- Comments, if any, must be submitted within 21 days from October 06, 2025
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document Filing Date: September 30, 2025
- Notice Date: October 06, 2025
- Comment Period Deadline: October 27, 2025 (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a standard SME IPO process notification. Relevant only to prospective investors interested in Jain International Power Limited IPO and market participants tracking SME listings. The public comment period is a regulatory requirement under ICDR Regulations.
