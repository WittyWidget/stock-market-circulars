## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Redington Limited on private placement basis. The issue consists of 2000 units of Rs.5 lakh each, totaling Rs.100 crores, with a 25-day tenure from September 4, 2025 to September 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Issue size: Rs.100 crores (2000 units of Rs.5,00,000 each)
- Issue price: Rs.497,907.50 per unit (face value Rs.5,00,000)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 730161, ISIN: INE891D14A38
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading allowed only in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: September 4, 2025
- Trading commencement: September 5, 2025
- Redemption date: September 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term debt instrument listing. The commercial paper has strong credit ratings (A1+) and short 25-day maturity, indicating low risk profile for institutional investors.
