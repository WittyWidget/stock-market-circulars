## Summary

ASM Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 526433) has forfeited 25,792 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each that were partly paid-up due to non-payment of allotment/call monies. The forfeited shares have distinctive numbers ranging from 10974209 to 11000000.

## Key Points

- Company: ASM Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 526433)
- Number of shares forfeited: 25,792 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10/- each
- Share type: Partly paid-up equity shares
- Reason: Non-payment of allotment/call monies
- Distinctive numbers: 10974209 to 11000000

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the forfeiture for their records and trading considerations.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: August 6, 2025
- Effective Date: August 6, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The forfeiture represents a relatively small number of shares and is a routine corporate action for recovering unpaid amounts on partly paid-up shares. This action primarily affects the specific shareholders who failed to make payments and has minimal impact on overall market operations or the company's trading.
