Stakeholders can submit comments within 21 days from the notice date.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by SHIVCHEM AGRO LIMITED on September 29, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
