## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debt securities of Infrastructure Leasing and Financial Services Limited (ILFS) with coupon rate 8.69% maturing on August 25, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities effective August 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- Debt securities suspension due to approaching maturity date
- SCRIP CODE: 952662
- ISIN: INE871D07OJ9
- Coupon rate: 8.69%
- Notice reference: DR-700/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - routine operational suspension due to security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified ILFS debt securities
- Prohibition applies to SCRIP CODE 952662 with ISIN INE871D07OJ9
- Compliance mandatory for all BSE trading members

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 21, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective**: August 22, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: August 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this is a routine suspension affecting only specific debt securities of ILFS approaching maturity. Trading members holding positions in these securities must ensure compliance with the suspension directive.
