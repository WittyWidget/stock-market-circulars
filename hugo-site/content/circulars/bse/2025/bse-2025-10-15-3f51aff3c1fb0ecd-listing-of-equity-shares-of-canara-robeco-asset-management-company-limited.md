## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Canara Robeco Asset Management Company Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company has been assigned scrip code 544580 with trading symbol CRAMC and ISIN number INE218I01013. The exact listing date and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Canara Robeco Asset Management Company Limited
- Scrip Code: 544580
- Trading Symbol: CRAMC
- ISIN Number: INE218I01013
- Listing status: Approved, awaiting final listing date announcement
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed to note the listing approval and await the separate notice for the actual listing date and complete security details before commencing trading activities in CRAMC shares.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 15, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice
- Security Details Announcement: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This listing adds a new asset management company to the BSE equity segment, expanding investment opportunities in the AMC sector. Trading members should prepare their systems for the new scrip once the final listing date is announced. The impact is moderate as it represents a standard IPO listing process with pending final date confirmation.
