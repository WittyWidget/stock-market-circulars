## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Birla Group Holdings Private Limited worth Rs. 125 crores (2500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective August 21, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 125 crores (2500 units)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,075/- per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- ISIN: INE09OL14HS8
- Scrip Code: 730067
- Scrip ID: BGHP210825

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applicable
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: August 21, 2025
- Allotment Date: August 21, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 20, 2025
- Tenure: 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding requirements. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk for investors.
