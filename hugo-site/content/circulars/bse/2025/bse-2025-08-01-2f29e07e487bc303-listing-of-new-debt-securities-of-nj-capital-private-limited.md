## Summary

NJ Capital Private Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE's Debt segment effective August 1, 2025. The securities carry a 9.80% annual interest rate with a 3-year maturity period and ICRA A+ credit rating.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 15,000 securities with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 976978, ISIN: INE0MT207015
- Interest Rate: 9.80% per annum, paid annually
- Credit Rating: ICRA A+ 
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Market Lot: 1 security
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedures followed for private placement debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0MT207015
- Refer to Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for detailed terms
- Contact BSE debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 30, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 1, 2025
- Interest Payment Dates: Annually from July 25, 2026 to July 25, 2028
- Redemption Date: July 25, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt securities listing. The securities provide institutional investors access to ICRA A+ rated debt instruments with competitive 9.80% yield. No put/call options available, indicating standard fixed-term investment structure.
