## Summary

BSE received clarifications from TBO Tek Limited and Bajaj Finance Limited regarding unusual volume movements in their shares. Both companies confirmed compliance with disclosure regulations and stated no undisclosed price sensitive information exists.

## Key Points

- TBO Tek Limited (Scrip Code: 544174) clarified that all material information requiring disclosure has been duly intimated to exchanges
- Bajaj Finance Limited (Scrip Code: 500034) confirmed no undisclosed price sensitive information or impending announcements
- Both companies reaffirmed commitment to timely disclosure under SEBI Listing Regulations
- Companies stated they are unaware of specific reasons for volume spurt

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must continue prompt disclosure of all material events under SEBI Listing Regulations
- Maintain transparency in all price sensitive information disclosures

## Important Dates

- September 4, 2025: TBO Tek clarification date
- September 5, 2025: Bajaj Finance clarification date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as both clarifications confirm no material undisclosed information. Routine surveillance measure responses with no operational changes for investors or market participants.
