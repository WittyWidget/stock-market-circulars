## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 100 crore issued by Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from September 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5 lakh denomination each
- Total quantity: 2,000 units (Rs. 100 crore)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Issue price: Rs. 4,90,846.50 per unit
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- ISIN: INE523L14AI6
- Scrip Code: 730146

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 3, 2025
- Trading Start Date: September 4, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 3, 2025
- Maturity Period: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The instrument provides institutional investors with a 91-day investment option backed by strong credit ratings.
