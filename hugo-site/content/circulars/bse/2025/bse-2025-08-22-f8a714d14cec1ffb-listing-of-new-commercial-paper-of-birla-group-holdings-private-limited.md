## Summary

BSE announces the listing of new Commercial Paper issued by Birla Group Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis, effective August 22, 2025. The instrument carries a face value of Rs. 5 lakh each with total quantity of 1000 units.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 1000 units (Rs. 50 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,160.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- ISIN: INE09OL14HS8 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Scrip Code: 730067, Scrip ID: BGHP210825
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard commercial paper listing process followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Use specified ISIN number for all transactions

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 22, 2025
- Trading Effective Date: August 22, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 20, 2025
- Tenure: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding option for Birla Group Holdings and investment opportunity for debt market participants. Axis Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying agent.
