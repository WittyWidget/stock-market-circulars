## Summary

BSE has announced the partial redemption of debentures of ASHIANA HOUSING LTD, resulting in a reduced face value per debenture. The debentures will trade with the new reduced face value starting August 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- ASHIANA HOUSING LTD (Scrip Code: AHL-8%-31-5-41-PVT, ISIN: INE365D08026, Code: 973220) is conducting partial redemption of debentures
- New reduced face value: Rs. 764,720.45 per debenture
- Trading with reduced face value begins August 5, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-688/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action involving partial redemption of existing debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face value for trading purposes
- All trades in these debentures must reflect the new face value from the effective date
- Trading members are required to take note of this change

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 4, 2025
- **Effective Date**: August 5, 2025 (trading with reduced face value begins)

## Impact Assessment

This partial redemption affects holders of ASHIANA HOUSING LTD debentures by reducing the outstanding principal amount. The significant reduction in face value (to Rs. 764,720.45) indicates a substantial partial redemption. Trading members and investors need to adjust their systems and calculations to reflect the new face value for accurate pricing and settlement.
