## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited worth Rs. 550 crores (11,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from August 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper issued on private placement basis of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total quantity: 11,000 units (Rs. 550 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730041, ISIN: INE929O14DV6
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,727.50 per unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 18, 2025
- Listing Date: August 19, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CARE and CRISIL) indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer.
