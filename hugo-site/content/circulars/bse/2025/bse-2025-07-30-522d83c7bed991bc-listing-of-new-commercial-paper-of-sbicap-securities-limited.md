## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs 150 crore issued by SBICAP Securities Limited on private placement basis. The securities will trade on BSE Debt segment with effect from July 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs 5 lakh face value each, total 3000 units
- Issue price: Rs 4,92,292 per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- ISIN: INE212K14BW7, Scrip Code: 729930
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination of Rs 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- State Bank of India serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 29, 2025
- Listing Date: July 30, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 27, 2025
- Maturity Period: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer. The short-term nature (90 days) provides liquidity options for institutional investors in the debt market segment.
