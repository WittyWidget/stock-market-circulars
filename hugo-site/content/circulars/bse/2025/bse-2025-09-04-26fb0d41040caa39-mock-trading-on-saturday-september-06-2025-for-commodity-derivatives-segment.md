## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, September 06, 2025. This session is designed for testing and familiarization purposes, allowing trading members to test their systems and applications. The new BOLT Pro TWS version 12.03 will be released during this session.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for September 06, 2025
- Login: 10:15 AM - 11:00 AM
- Continuous Trading: 11:00 AM - 03:30 PM
- New BOLT Pro TWS version 12.03 release
- Members can test third-party platforms and in-house systems
- Testing includes risk-reduction mode, trading halt, and exceptional market conditions
- No margin obligations or pay-in/pay-out requirements
- Feedback required by 4:30 PM

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Session conducted in accordance with existing SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020 and Exchange Notice No 20201201-22 dated December 1, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members may participate in mock trading or UAT environment to fulfill regulatory requirements
- Active participation encouraged for system testing
- Mandatory feedback submission by 4:30 PM on the trading day
- Members using third-party platforms should utilize this opportunity for comprehensive testing

## Important Dates

- **September 06, 2025**: Mock trading session
- **10:15 AM - 11:00 AM**: Login window
- **11:00 AM - 03:30 PM**: Continuous trading session
- **4:30 PM**: Feedback submission deadline

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact as this is a mock trading session with no actual financial transactions or obligations. The session serves as a routine system maintenance and testing opportunity for market participants to ensure their trading systems are functioning properly and to familiarize themselves with the new BOLT Pro TWS version 12.03.
