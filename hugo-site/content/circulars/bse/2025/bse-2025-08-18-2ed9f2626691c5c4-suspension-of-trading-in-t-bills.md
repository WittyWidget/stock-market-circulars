## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in two Treasury Bills (T-Bills) effective August 19, 2025, as they approach their maturity/redemption date. The suspension affects 364TB21825 (ISIN: IN002024Z214) and 91TB210825 (ISIN: IN002025X083).

## Key Points

- Two T-Bills will be suspended from trading: 364TB21825 and 91TB210825
- Trading suspension effective from August 19, 2025
- Suspension reason: T-Bills maturing on redemption date
- Notice reference: DR-697/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced - this is a standard operational procedure for maturing debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified T-Bills from August 19, 2025
- Trading members should take note of the suspension and adjust their operations accordingly

## Important Dates

- **August 18, 2025**: Notice date
- **August 19, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **Redemption Date**: Maturity date for both T-Bills (specific date not mentioned)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine operational measure for maturing debt instruments. Only affects traders dealing specifically in these two T-Bills in the debt segment. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
