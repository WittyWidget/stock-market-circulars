## Summary

BSE has listed 24,681 new equity shares of Concord Control Systems Limited (Scrip Code: 543619) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares are permitted to trade on the Exchange with effect from Thursday, October 16, 2025. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 24,681 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 1,408.08/-
- Issue price: Rs. 1,418.08 per share
- Allotted to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Distinctive Numbers: 6300473 to 6325153
- ISIN: INE0N0J01014
- Shares rank pari-passu with old equity shares
- All shares subject to lock-in until April 29, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed until April 29, 2026 for all 24,681 shares

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: August 11, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 16, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: April 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a small preferential allotment representing a limited number of shares. The lock-in period until April 29, 2026 ensures these shares will not be immediately available for sale in the open market, preventing any immediate dilution impact on existing shareholders.
