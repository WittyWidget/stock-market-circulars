## Summary

Ganesh Housing Corporation Limited has officially changed its name to Ganesh Housing Limited effective August 14, 2025. The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to the name change under CIN L45200GJ1991PLC015817.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from "Ganesh Housing Corporation Limited" to "Ganesh Housing Limited"
- Certificate effective date: August 14, 2025
- Corporate Identification Number remains: L45200GJ1991PLC015817
- Company limited by shares status unchanged
- Rights and liabilities of stakeholders remain unaffected

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate name change process completed through the Ministry of Corporate Affairs Central Processing Centre.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company must display old name alongside new name for 2 years at all required locations per Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders advised to verify latest company status on MCA website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21
- Updated mailing address: Ganesh Corporate House, 100 Feet Hebatpur - Thaltej Road, Near Sola Bridge, off S.G. Highway, Ahmedabad-380054, Gujarat

## Important Dates

- **August 14, 2025**: Effective date of name change
- **Next 2 years**: Period during which old name must be displayed alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is purely an administrative name change. The company's business operations, financial obligations, and shareholder rights remain unchanged. Trading may continue under updated company name with same CIN and business fundamentals intact.
