## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in bonds of Tourism Finance Corporation of India Ltd (TFCI) with ISIN INE305A09232 (Code: 952989) effective October 24, 2025. The suspension is implemented due to the record date set for redemption of bonds and payment of interest on the 8.81% bonds maturing November 9, 2025.

## Key Points

- Bond ISIN: INE305A09232 (Code: 952989)
- Bond Description: TFC-8.81%-9-11-25-PVT
- Record Date: October 24, 2025
- No Dealings From: October 24, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Bonds and Payment of Interest
- Trading members advised not to deal in these bonds

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational circular for bond redemption procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any deals in the specified TFCI bonds from October 24, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and inform their clients accordingly
- Bondholders as on record date (October 24, 2025) will be eligible for interest payment and redemption proceeds

## Important Dates

- **October 20, 2025**: Circular issued (Notice No 20251020-37, DR-742/2025-2026)
- **October 24, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and redemption
- **October 24, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **November 9, 2025**: Bond maturity date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low to medium. The suspension affects only specific bonds of Tourism Finance Corporation of India Ltd with maturity approaching in November 2025. This is a standard procedure for bonds nearing redemption.

**Investor Impact**: Medium for bondholders. Investors holding these bonds as of the record date will receive interest payment and redemption proceeds. No trading allowed from October 24, 2025, preventing any last-minute transactions.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems to block trades in this specific ISIN and inform clients holding or interested in these bonds about the suspension and upcoming redemption.
