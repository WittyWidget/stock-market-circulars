## Summary

BSE has announced that JIO BLACKROCK MUTUAL FUND will reopen its JIOBLACKROCK FLEXI CAP FUND - Direct Plan Growth scheme for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective October 17, 2025. The scheme will also support SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities.

## Key Points

- JIO BLACKROCK FLEXI CAP FUND - Direct Plan Growth (ISIN: INF22M001093) reopening for transactions
- Scheme category: Equity
- Platform: BSE StAR MF Platform
- Facilities available: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- This follows previous Notice No. 20250922-10

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update regarding scheme availability.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of BSE StAR MF Platform should update their systems to enable transactions for the specified scheme effective October 17, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 16, 2025
- **Effective Date:** October 17, 2025 - Scheme reopens for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact. It affects only investors and distributors dealing with JIO BLACKROCK MUTUAL FUND's Flexi Cap Fund Direct Plan. The reopening provides investors access to ongoing investment and redemption facilities through the BSE platform. No broader market implications or changes to trading mechanisms are involved.
