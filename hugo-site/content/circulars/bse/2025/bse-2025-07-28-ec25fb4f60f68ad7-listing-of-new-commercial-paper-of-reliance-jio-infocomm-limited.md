## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities worth Rs. 145 crore issued by Reliance Jio Infocomm Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective July 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: Rs. 145 crore (29,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,812.50 per unit (face value Rs. 5,00,000)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Scrip code: 729907, ISIN: INE110L14UF6
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Axis Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: July 25, 2025
- Trading commencement: July 28, 2025
- Redemption date: October 24, 2025
- Maturity period: 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this represents routine short-term debt funding for Reliance Jio. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Standard commercial paper listing with no operational changes for market participants.
