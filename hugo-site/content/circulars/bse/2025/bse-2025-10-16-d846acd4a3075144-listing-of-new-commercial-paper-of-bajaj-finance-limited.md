## Summary

Bajaj Finance Limited has listed three series of Commercial Papers (CP) on BSE Debt segment on private placement basis, effective October 16, 2025. The total issuance comprises 28,500 units with an aggregate value of Rs. 1,425 crores. All three series carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and mature in January 2026. ICICI Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Three series of Commercial Papers listed on BSE Debt segment with effect from October 16, 2025
- Face value of Rs. 5,00,000/- per unit for all series
- Date of allotment: October 15, 2025
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal impact on broader market operations. The listing provides short-term funding to Bajaj Finance Limited through the commercial paper route. The high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. The short tenure (approximately 90 days) and private placement nature limit the broader market impact. This listing is relevant primarily for debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in money market instruments.
