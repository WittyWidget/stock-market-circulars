## Summary

BSE announces the listing of new debt securities issued by NJ Capital Private Limited on private placement basis, effective August 1, 2025. The securities carry a 9.80% annual interest rate and mature on July 25, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 15,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 976978, Scrip ID: 980NJCPL28
- ISIN: INE0MT207015
- Face Value: ₹1,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: ₹1,00,000 per security
- Credit Rating: ICRA A+
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities to be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Refer to Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for complete details

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 30, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 1, 2025
- Interest Payment Dates: Annually from July 25, 2026 to July 25, 2028
- Redemption Date: July 25, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement debt security listing. The securities provide institutional investors with a fixed-income investment option with ICRA A+ rating and 9.80% annual returns.
