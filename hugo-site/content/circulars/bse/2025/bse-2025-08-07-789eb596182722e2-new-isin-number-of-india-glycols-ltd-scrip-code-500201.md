## Summary

BSE has issued a new ISIN number INE560A01023 for India Glycols Ltd (Scrip Code: 500201) following the sub-division of equity shares from Rs.10/- to Rs.5/-. The new ISIN will be effective for trades executed on and from August 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: India Glycols Ltd (Scrip Code: 500201)
- New ISIN Number: INE560A01023
- Corporate Action: Sub-division of equity shares
- Share denomination change: From Rs.10/- to Rs.5/-
- Effective date: August 12, 2025 (Ex-Date)
- Reference: DR-693/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

The company has undergone equity share sub-division, requiring issuance of a new ISIN number to distinguish the new Rs.5/- shares from the original Rs.10/- shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must use the new ISIN number INE560A01023 for all trades in India Glycols Ltd equity shares executed on or after August 12, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **August 12, 2025**: Ex-date for new ISIN number implementation
- **August 7, 2025**: Notice date
- **July 31, 2025**: Previous related notice (20250731-17)

## Impact Assessment

This corporate action will require system updates by trading members and custodians to reflect the new ISIN number and updated share denomination. Investors holding shares will see their holdings adjusted to reflect twice the number of shares at half the face value.
