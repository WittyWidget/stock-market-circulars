## Summary

BSE announces the listing of 1000 bonus equity shares of Ipca Laboratories Ltd (Scrip Code: 524494) that were kept in abeyance. These shares will be listed and permitted for trading effective Monday, September 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- 1000 bonus equity shares of Rs. 1/- each kept in abeyance
- ISIN: INE571A01038
- Distinctive numbers: 1171453 to 1172452
- Trading permitted from September 1, 2025
- Notice issued by Deputy Vice President Ashok Kumar Singh

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine listing of previously issued bonus shares that were kept in abeyance.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading. No specific compliance actions required beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **September 1, 2025**: Effective date for listing and trading of bonus shares

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a small number of bonus shares (1000 shares) that were previously issued but kept in abeyance. The listing enables these shares to be traded normally.
