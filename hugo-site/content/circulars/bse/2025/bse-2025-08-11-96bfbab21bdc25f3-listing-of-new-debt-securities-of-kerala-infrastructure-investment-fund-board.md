## Summary

BSE announces the listing of seven new debt securities issued by Kerala Infrastructure Investment Fund Board (KIIFB) on private placement basis. The securities carry a 9.67% per annum interest rate with quarterly payments and maturity dates ranging from 2029 to 2035.

## Key Points

- Seven new debt securities listed with scrip codes 977006 to 977012
- Face value and issue price: ₹1,00,000 per security
- Interest rate: 9.67% per annum paid quarterly
- Credit rating: Acuité AA(CE), INDIA AA(CE)
- Quantity: 21,429 securities for each series
- Market lot: 1 security
- Securities will trade in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must handle securities in dematerialized form only
- Tick size for trading is 1 paise
- Reference to placement memorandum available on BSE website for detailed terms

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 8, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 11, 2025
- First interest payment: November 8, 2025
- Maturity dates: August 8, 2029 through August 8, 2035 (different series)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as these are institutional debt securities with high face value (₹1 lakh) targeting sophisticated investors. The securities provide infrastructure funding mechanism for Kerala state projects with competitive interest rates for the current market environment.
