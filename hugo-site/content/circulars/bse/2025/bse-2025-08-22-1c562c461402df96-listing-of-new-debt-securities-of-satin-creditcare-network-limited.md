## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Satin Creditcare Network Limited worth ₹60 crores (6000 securities at ₹1 lakh face value each) issued on private placement basis. The securities carry 11.5% annual interest rate and mature on August 21, 2032.

## Key Points

- New debt securities worth ₹60 crores listed on BSE Debt segment
- Issue details: 6000 securities, face value ₹1 lakh each, issue price ₹1 lakh
- Interest rate: 11.5% per annum paid annually
- Credit rating: ICRA A
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Security Details

- **Scrip Code**: 977048
- **Scrip ID**: 115SCNL32
- **ISIN**: INE836B08301
- **Allotment Date**: August 21, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: August 21, 2032
- **Interest Payment**: Annually from August 21, 2026 to August 21, 2032

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: August 22, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: August 21, 2026
- **Redemption Date**: August 21, 2032

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The securities provide institutional investors access to ICRA A-rated debt instrument with competitive 11.5% yield.
