## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Tata Power Co. Ltd on private placement basis effective October 10, 2025. The issue consists of 10,000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 500 crores. The securities are rated CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ with a redemption date of December 24, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Tata Power Co. Ltd
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 500 crores (10,000 units Ã— Rs. 5,00,000)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,94,011 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730374
- **Scrip ID**: TPCL101025
- **Detail Name**: TPCL-24-12-25-CP
- **ISIN**: INE245A14KI8
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: ICICI Bank Limited
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE245A14KI8
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: October 10, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: October 10, 2025
- **Date of Redemption**: December 24, 2025
- **Tenure**: 75 days (approximately 2.5 months)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing that provides Tata Power with working capital financing. The commercial paper market remains active with regular issuances.

**Investor Impact**: Low. Relevant only for debt segment investors and institutional participants dealing in money market instruments. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates strong creditworthiness.

**Operational Impact**: None for equity market participants. Debt segment trading members can contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers for clarifications.
