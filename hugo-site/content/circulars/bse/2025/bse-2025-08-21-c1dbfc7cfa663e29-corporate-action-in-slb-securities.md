## Summary

BSE has announced corporate actions for 14 companies in SLB securities, including 13 dividend declarations and 1 bonus issue. The actions span from August 22, 2025 to September 19, 2025, with corresponding shut periods that will affect trading.

## Key Points

- 13 companies declaring dividends with record dates between August 22 - September 19, 2025
- Godfrey Phillips India Ltd. announcing bonus issue with record date September 16, 2025
- TVS Motor Company undergoing scheme of arrangement with foreclosure date August 25, 2025
- All actions include specific shut periods ranging from 1-4 days
- Ex-dates generally coincide with record dates, with some exceptions

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Standard corporate action procedures apply for SLB securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will be unavailable for lending/borrowing during specified shut periods
- Market participants must note ex-dates for dividend/bonus entitlements
- TVS Motor Company scheme of arrangement requires foreclosure compliance by August 25, 2025

## Important Dates

- **August 22, 2025**: AGI Greenpac dividend record date and shut period start
- **August 25, 2025**: TVS Motor scheme of arrangement record date and foreclosure
- **August 28, 2025**: Route Mobile dividend record date
- **September 2-3, 2025**: Multiple dividend record dates (Gabriel India, EPL, Patanjali Foods, Prestige Estates)
- **September 16, 2025**: Godfrey Phillips bonus issue record date
- **September 19, 2025**: Gujarat Alkalis dividend record date

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact**: Securities will be unavailable for SLB transactions during shut periods, potentially affecting liquidity and borrowing costs. **Market Impact**: Dividend announcements may influence stock prices around ex-dates. **Operational Impact**: Market participants need to adjust SLB positions before shut periods commence to avoid settlement issues.
