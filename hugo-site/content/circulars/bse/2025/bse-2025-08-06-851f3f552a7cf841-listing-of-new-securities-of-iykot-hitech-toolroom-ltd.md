## Summary

BSE has authorized trading in 40,99,746 new equity shares of Iykot Hitech Toolroom Ltd (Scrip Code: 522245) effective August 7, 2025. These shares result from the conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid up shares of Rs. 5/- each, issued on rights basis.

## Key Points

- 40,99,746 equity shares approved for trading
- Conversion from partly paid to fully paid up shares
- Face value: Rs. 5/- per share
- Shares issued on rights basis
- ISIN: INE079L01013
- Shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are permitted to trade in the mentioned securities from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 7, 2025 (Thursday)
- **Notice Date**: August 6, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid shares. The new shares carry the same rights as existing equity shares and represent completion of a rights issue process.
