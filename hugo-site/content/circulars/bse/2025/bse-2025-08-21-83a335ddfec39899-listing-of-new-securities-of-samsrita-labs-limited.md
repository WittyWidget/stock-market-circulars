## Summary

Samsrita Labs Limited (Scrip Code: 539267) has listed 79,41,004 new equity shares on BSE, effective August 22, 2025. The shares were issued at par value to non-promoters on a preferential basis and are subject to lock-in until February 28, 2026.

## Key Points

- 79,41,004 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par value
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 14808707 to 22749710
- Date of allotment: July 18, 2025
- ISIN: INE579N01018

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed about the new securities listing and trading permissions.

## Important Dates

- **Trading Start Date**: August 22, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: July 18, 2025
- **Lock-in Period End**: February 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine equity listing with minimal market impact. The preferential issue increases the company's equity base and provides additional liquidity, though the lock-in period restricts immediate trading of these shares.
