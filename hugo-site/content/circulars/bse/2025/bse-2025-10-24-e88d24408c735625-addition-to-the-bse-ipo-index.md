## Summary

MIDWEST LIMITED (Exchange ticker 544587) is being added to the BSE IPO Index effective at the open of Tuesday, October 28, 2025. The stock is being listed on BSE on Friday, October 24, 2025. This action has been included in the end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE) for October 24, 2025.

## Key Points

- MIDWEST LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544587) listed on BSE effective Friday, October 24, 2025
- Stock will be added to BSE IPO Index effective at the open of Tuesday, October 28, 2025
- Action included in tonight's end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Notice references prior Notice No: 20251023-21
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index inclusion following a new listing.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index tracking funds and products may need to adjust their portfolios to reflect the addition of MIDWEST LIMITED to the BSE IPO Index.

## Important Dates

- **October 24, 2025**: Listing date for MIDWEST LIMITED on BSE
- **October 28, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard index maintenance action following a new IPO listing. The addition will primarily affect:

- Index tracking funds and ETFs that replicate the BSE IPO Index
- Passive investment products benchmarked to the BSE IPO Index
- MIDWEST LIMITED stock may see additional demand from index funds rebalancing

The BSE IPO Index tracks newly listed companies, and additions are routine following successful IPO listings.
