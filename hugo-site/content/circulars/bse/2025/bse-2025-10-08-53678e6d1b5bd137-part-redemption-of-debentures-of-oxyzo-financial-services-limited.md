## Summary

OXYZO FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED has announced part redemption of its non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE04VS07388, BSE Code: 976577) along with interest payment. The record date has been set as October 12, 2025, and trading in these debentures will commence with reduced face value from October 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: OXYZO FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED
- Security: Non-Convertible Debentures (OFSL-9.75%-27-3-27-PVT)
- ISIN: INE04VS07388
- BSE Code: 976577
- Record Date: October 12, 2025
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- New Reduced Face Value: Rs. 80,952/- per debenture
- Effective Date: October 10, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-733/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notice for part redemption of debentures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trading in the debentures will be conducted with reduced face value effective from October 10, 2025
- Debenture holders on record as of October 12, 2025 will be eligible for interest payment and part redemption

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| October 8, 2025 | Notice Date |
| October 10, 2025 | Effective date for trading with reduced face value (Settlement DR-733/2025-2026) |
| October 12, 2025 | Record Date for interest payment and part redemption |

## Impact Assessment

**Debenture Holders Impact:** Debenture holders will receive partial redemption proceeds and interest payment. The face value of their holdings will reduce to Rs. 80,952/- per debenture post-redemption.

**Market Impact:** Limited to specific debenture holders of this ISIN. Trading will continue with adjusted face value from October 10, 2025. No impact on equity markets or broader debt market operations.
