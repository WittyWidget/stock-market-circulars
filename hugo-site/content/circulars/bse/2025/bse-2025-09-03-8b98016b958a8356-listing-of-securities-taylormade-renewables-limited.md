## Summary

Taylormade Renewables Limited (Scrip Code: 541228) has listed 225,248 new equity shares on BSE effective September 4, 2025. These shares were issued at Rs. 243 per share to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 225,248 equity shares of Rs. 10 face value issued at Rs. 233 premium
- Shares issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Issue price: Rs. 243 per share
- Distribution numbers: 12124604 to 12349851
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE459Z01017

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and trading permissions.

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: January 9, 2025
- Trading Commencement: September 4, 2025
- Lock-in Period End: September 1, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this represents a routine listing of additional shares through warrant conversion. The shares have already completed their lock-in period and are freely tradable.
