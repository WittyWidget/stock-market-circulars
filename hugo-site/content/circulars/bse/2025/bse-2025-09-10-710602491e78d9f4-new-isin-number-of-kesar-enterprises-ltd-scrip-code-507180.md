## Summary

BSE has announced a new ISIN number for KESAR ENTERPRISES LTD (Scrip Code: 507180) following the sub-division of equity shares from Rs.10/- to Re.1/-. The new ISIN number INE133B01027 will be effective for trades from September 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: KESAR ENTERPRISES LTD (Scrip Code: 507180)
- New ISIN Number: INE133B01027
- Share sub-division from Rs.10/- to Re.1/- per share
- Continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20250901-10 dated September 1, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

New ISIN number assignment following completion of equity share sub-division process as previously notified.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new ISIN number for trades in the subdivided equity shares.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 18, 2025 (Ex-Date)
- **DR Number**: 718/2025-2026
- **Notice Date**: September 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine administrative change affecting the identification structure of Kesar Enterprises equity shares. No immediate market impact expected beyond technical system updates required by trading members.
