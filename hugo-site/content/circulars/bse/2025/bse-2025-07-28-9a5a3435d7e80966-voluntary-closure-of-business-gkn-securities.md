## Summary

GKN Securities (Member No. 6507) will voluntarily cease trading operations across Cash, Equity derivative and Currency derivative segments effective July 29, 2025. This is a voluntary business closure decision by the trading member.

## Key Points

- GKN Securities (Member No. 6507) ceasing all trading operations
- Affects Cash, Equity derivative and Currency derivative segments
- Voluntary closure decision by the member
- Contact membership.ops@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a business decision by the trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the cessation. No specific compliance actions required from other members.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Tuesday, July 29, 2025 - GKN Securities ceases trading operations

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a single trading member's voluntary business closure. Other trading members and market operations remain unaffected.
