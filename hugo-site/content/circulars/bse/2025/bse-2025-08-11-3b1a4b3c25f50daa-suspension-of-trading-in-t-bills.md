## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in three Treasury Bills (T-Bills) effective August 12, 2025, as they approach their redemption/maturity date. Trading members are advised not to deal in these specific T-Bills from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills will mature on their respective redemption dates
- Trading suspension effective from August 12, 2025
- Notice issued under reference DR-693/2025-2026
- Applies to all Trading Members of the Exchange

## Treasury Bills Affected

| SCRIP CODE | ISIN | Particulars |
|------------|------|-------------|
| 805026 | IN002024Y449 | 182TB14825 |
| 805064 | IN002025X075 | 91TB140825 |
| 804955 | IN002024Z206 | 364TB15825 |

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine operational measure for maturing securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not deal in the mentioned T-Bills from August 12, 2025
- All debt market participants should note the suspension
- Standard maturity procedures to be followed

## Important Dates

- **August 11, 2025**: Notice issued
- **August 12, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **Redemption Date**: Maturity date for the T-Bills (specific dates not mentioned)

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this is routine procedure for maturing government securities. Debt market participants holding these T-Bills should prepare for redemption procedures. No broader market disruption expected as this follows standard maturity protocols.
