## Summary

INDIGO 044 has listed new debt securities worth Rs. 4,59,71,396.69 on BSE's Debt segment effective August 5, 2025. The securities carry a 12.50% annual interest rate with monthly interest payments and mature on June 20, 2032.

## Key Points

- Face value: Rs. 4,59,71,396.69
- Interest rate: 12.50% per annum
- Monthly interest payment schedule
- Maturity date: June 20, 2032
- Credit rating: CRISIL AA(SO)
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities to be traded under ISIN Number INE2DDW15018
- Refer to Placement Memorandum for detailed terms

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: July 31, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 5, 2025
- Redemption date: June 20, 2032

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement debt listing. The securities provide institutional investors with a high-yield debt instrument backed by CRISIL AA(SO) rating.
