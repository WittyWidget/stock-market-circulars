## Summary

BSE announces the listing of new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 200 crore issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis. The securities are listed on BSE Debt segment effective August 21, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each, total 4000 units
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,171.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip Code: 730055, Scrip ID: BFSL20825
- ISIN: INE01C314CL8
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialised form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples
- Tick size of 1 paise applicable
- Market lot size: 1 unit

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 20, 2025
- Listing Date: August 21, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding option for Bajaj Financial Securities Limited with approximately 3-month tenure.
