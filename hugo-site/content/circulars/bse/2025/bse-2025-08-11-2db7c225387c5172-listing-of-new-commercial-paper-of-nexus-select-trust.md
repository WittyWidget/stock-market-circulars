## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Nexus Select Trust on private placement basis. The issue comprises 4,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 200 crores, with a 3-month maturity period and IND A1+ credit rating.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper quantity: 4,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total issue size: Rs. 200 crores
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,764 per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: IND A1+ 
- ISIN: INE0NDH14072
- Scrip Code: 730001
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 8, 2025
- Listing date: August 11, 2025
- Redemption date: November 7, 2025
- Maturity period: 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The securities provide short-term funding option for Nexus Select Trust with favorable rating. Standard trading parameters apply with no special conditions affecting broader market operations.
