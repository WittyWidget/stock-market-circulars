## Summary

BSE has listed new perpetual debt securities of Aditya Birla Capital Ltd issued on private placement basis. The securities carry an interest rate of 8.4232% per annum and have a face value of Rs. 10 crore each.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 200 securities listed
- Face Value: Rs. 10,00,00,000 per security
- Interest Rate: 8.4232% per annum (annually payable)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AA+, IND AA+
- ISIN: INE674K08042
- Scrip Code: 977034
- Trading only in dematerialised form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Refer to Placement Memorandum for detailed terms

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 18, 2025
- Listing Date: August 20, 2025
- Interest Payment: Annual
- Redemption Date: December 31, 2999 (Perpetual)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing. The perpetual nature and high face value indicate institutional investor targeting. The AA+ rating reflects strong creditworthiness of the issuer.
