## Summary

BSE has announced that trading member Pawankumar Parmeshwarlal Choudhary (Member No. 550) has successfully converted his individual membership into a corporate entity named "Pawan Parmeshwar Capital Pvt. Ltd." (Member No. 550). This conversion has been approved by SEBI and the name change will be effective from Monday, October 20, 2025.

## Key Points

- Individual member Pawankumar Parmeshwarlal Choudhary (Member No. 550) has converted to corporate entity
- New corporate entity name: Pawan Parmeshwar Capital Pvt. Ltd.
- Member number remains unchanged: 550
- SEBI has approved the conversion
- Change effective from October 20, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a membership structure conversion from individual to corporate entity, which is a permitted transition under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of this name change for their records and future correspondence. No action required from other members.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 17, 2025
- **Effective Date**: Monday, October 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is an administrative change with minimal market impact. The conversion from individual to corporate membership structure is a routine process. The member number remains the same, ensuring continuity in trading operations and client relationships. Other trading members should update their records to reflect the new entity name for any future interactions or transactions involving Member No. 550.
