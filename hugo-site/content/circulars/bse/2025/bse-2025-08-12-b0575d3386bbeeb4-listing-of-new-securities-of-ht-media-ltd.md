## Summary

24,835 equity shares of HT Media Ltd (Scrip Code: 532662) have been listed and permitted for trading on BSE effective August 13, 2025. These shares were issued pursuant to a scheme of arrangement between HT Mobile Solutions Limited (transferor) and HT Media Limited (transferee) under the Companies Act, 2013.

## Key Points

- 24,835 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each listed
- Trading commenced on August 13, 2025
- Shares issued under scheme of arrangement between HT Mobile Solutions Limited and HT Media Limited
- ISIN: INE501G01024
- Distribution numbers: 285021042 to 285045876
- Allotment date: February 4, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing following completion of a corporate restructuring scheme approved under Sections 230-232 of the Companies Act, 2013.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing. No specific compliance actions required beyond standard trading procedures for the newly listed shares.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: February 4, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 13, 2025 (Wednesday)
- **Notice Date**: August 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this involves a relatively small number of shares (24,835) being listed following a corporate restructuring. The listing enables trading of shares issued through the scheme of arrangement, providing liquidity to shareholders of the transferor company.
