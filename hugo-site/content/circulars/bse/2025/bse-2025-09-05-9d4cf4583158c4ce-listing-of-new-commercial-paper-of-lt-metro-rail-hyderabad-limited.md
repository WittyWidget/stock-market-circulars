## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted new commercial paper securities issued by L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) Limited on private placement basis to the debt segment effective September 5, 2025. The issue comprises 13,800 commercial papers with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, maturing on November 21, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Quantity: 13,800 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,93,587 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730158
- Scrip ID: LTMR40925
- ISIN Number: INE128M14AS1
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading allowed only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: State Bank of India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE128M14AS1
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed for all trades

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: September 5, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: September 5, 2025
- Allotment Date: September 4, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard listing of commercial paper on private placement basis in the debt segment. The securities are short-term instruments (78-day tenure) with high credit ratings. Trading is restricted to institutional participants given the Rs. 5 lakh minimum denomination requirement. No impact on equity markets or retail investors.
