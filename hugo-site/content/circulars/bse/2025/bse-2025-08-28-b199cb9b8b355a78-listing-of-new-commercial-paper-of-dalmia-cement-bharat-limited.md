## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited on private placement basis. The commercial paper has a face value of Rs. 5 lakh each with 1,500 units totaling Rs. 75 crore, maturing on November 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 1,500 units (Rs. 75 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 492,715.50 per unit
- Scrip code: 730107 (DCBL260825)
- ISIN: INE755K14FN5
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- IDBI Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 26, 2025
- Listing effective date: August 28, 2025
- Redemption date: November 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. Provides short-term funding option for Dalmia Cement with 91-day maturity period. The A1+ credit rating indicates strong creditworthiness of the issuer.
