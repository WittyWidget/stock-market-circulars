## Summary

BSE has announced that TAKYON NETWORKS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544471) equity shares will be transferred from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment with effect from Thursday, August 21, 2025. The shares will be shifted under M Group for trading purposes.

## Key Points

- Company: TAKYON NETWORKS LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544471
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: August 21, 2025
- Notice continuation from previous notice dated August 5, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for TAKYON NETWORKS LIMITED shares will change from Trade for Trade basis to Rolling settlement, allowing for more flexible trading arrangements.
