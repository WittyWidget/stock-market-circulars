## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited on private placement basis. Two series of commercial papers with face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each have been admitted for trading on the BSE Debt segment with effect from July 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two series of commercial papers listed: 500 units (NWIL29725) and 3000 units (NWIL160625)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit for both series
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ for both instruments
- ICICI Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent
- Trading only in dematerialized form with standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded under specified ISIN numbers

## Important Dates

- Series 1 (NWIL29725): Allotment 29/07/2025, Redemption 14/08/2025
- Series 2 (NWIL160625): Allotment 30/07/2025, Redemption 15/12/2025
- Trading effective from July 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of commercial paper securities. The listing provides additional short-term debt instruments for institutional investors and enhances liquidity in the debt segment.
