## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of IGH Holdings Private Limited issued on private placement basis. The securities are worth Rs. 5 lakh each with 2000 units totaling Rs. 100 crores, maturing on November 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 2000 units (Rs. 100 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,978 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Scrip code: 730014, ISIN: INE02FN14598
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Axis Bank Limited is the issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 13, 2025
- Redemption date: November 12, 2025
- Trading effective: August 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing on BSE debt segment. The securities provide short-term funding option for IGH Holdings Private Limited with 3-month maturity period.
