## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of MIZUHO CAPSAVE FINANCE PRIVATE LIMITED effective August 22, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to debentures with ISIN INE0DBJ07143 (Code: 974175)
- Debenture carries 9.35% interest rate with maturity on September 6, 2025
- Record date set for August 22, 2025
- No dealings permitted from August 22, 2025 onwards
- Reference number: DR-700/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational measure for debenture redemption processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from August 22, 2025
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and comply accordingly

## Important Dates

- **August 22, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **August 22, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **September 6, 2025**: Debenture maturity date

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this affects only one debenture issue nearing maturity. Standard procedure for redemption processing with minimal disruption to overall debt market operations.
