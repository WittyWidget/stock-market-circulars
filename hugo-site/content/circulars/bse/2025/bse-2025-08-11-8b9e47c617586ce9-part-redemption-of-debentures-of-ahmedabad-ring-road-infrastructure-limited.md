## Summary

Ahmedabad Ring Road Infrastructure Limited has announced part redemption of its non-convertible debentures along with interest payment. The debenture face value will be reduced from current value to Rs. 64,018 per debenture, effective August 14, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Ahmedabad Ring Road Infrastructure Limited
- Debenture Code: ARRIL-20%-30-6-26-PVT (INE091I07011)
- BSE Code: 975947
- Record Date: August 16, 2025
- Effective Date: August 14, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-695/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

Trading in the company's debentures will commence with reduced face value from the effective date.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the change in face value for trading purposes
- Debentures will trade with reduced face value of Rs. 64,018 per debenture from August 14, 2025

## Important Dates

- **August 14, 2025**: Effective date for reduced face value trading
- **August 16, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and part redemption

## Impact Assessment

This corporate action will significantly impact debenture holders through partial redemption and reduced face value. Trading members need to adjust their systems to reflect the new face value for accurate pricing and settlement.
