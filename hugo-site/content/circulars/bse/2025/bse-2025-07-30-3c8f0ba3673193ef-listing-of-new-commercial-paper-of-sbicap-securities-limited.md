## Summary

BSE announces the listing of new commercial paper issued by SBICAP Securities Limited worth Rs. 150 crores (3,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on private placement basis, effective July 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total issue size: 3,000 units (Rs. 150 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,292 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- ISIN: INE212K14BW7
- Scrip code: 729930

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise
- State Bank of India acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: July 29, 2025
- Trading commencement: July 30, 2025
- Redemption date: October 27, 2025
- Maturity period: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding option for SBICAP Securities Limited and investment opportunity for institutional investors in debt segment.
