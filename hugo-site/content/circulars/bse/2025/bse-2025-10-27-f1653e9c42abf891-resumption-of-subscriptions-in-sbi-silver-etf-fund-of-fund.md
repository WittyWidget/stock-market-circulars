## Summary

BSE has issued a circular announcing the resumption of subscription facility in SBI Silver ETF Fund of Fund. This follows a temporary suspension period and allows investors to resume making fresh subscriptions into this fund of fund scheme that invests in silver exchange traded funds.

## Key Points

- Subscriptions in SBI Silver ETF Fund of Fund have been resumed
- The fund of fund invests in silver ETF underlying schemes
- Normal subscription operations are now active for investors
- This is a routine operational update for the specific fund scheme

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced in this circular. This is an operational notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Fund houses and distributors should update their systems to reflect the resumption of subscriptions
- Investment platforms should enable subscription functionality for this scheme
- Investor communications should be updated to reflect availability of subscriptions

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: October 27, 2025
- **Effective Date**: Subscriptions resumed as per the circular date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This affects only investors specifically interested in SBI Silver ETF Fund of Fund.

**Operational Impact**: Fund distributors and platforms need to enable subscription functionality for this specific scheme.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for investors who were waiting to invest in this silver ETF fund of fund, as they can now make fresh subscriptions into the scheme.
