## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 100 crore issued by Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited on private placement basis. The instrument carries face value of Rs. 5 lakh per unit with 2,000 units issued, maturing in 15 days.

## Key Points

- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 100 crore (2,000 units)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,98,596.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Scrip Code: 730016
- ISIN: INE523L14AH8
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- ICICI Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 13, 2025
- Listing Date: August 14, 2025
- Redemption Date: August 28, 2025
- Maturity Period: 15 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding avenue for Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited with high credit ratings indicating low risk profile.
