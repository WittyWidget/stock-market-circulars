## Summary

BSE has issued a new ISIN number INE07S101038 for Pavna Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 543915) following the sub-division of equity shares from Rs.10 per share to Re.1 per share. The new ISIN will be effective for trades from September 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: PAVNA INDUSTRIES LTD (Scrip Code: 543915)
- New ISIN Number: INE07S101038
- Share sub-division: From Rs.10/- to Re.1/- per equity share
- Effective date for new ISIN: September 1, 2025 (Ex-Date)
- Reference: Continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20250819-37 dated August 19, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

Implementation of new ISIN number system following completion of equity share sub-division process as per corporate action requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems with the new ISIN number
- All trades from September 1, 2025 onwards must use the new ISIN INE07S101038
- Ensure proper handling of the sub-divided shares in trading and settlement systems

## Important Dates

- **September 1, 2025**: Ex-date for sub-division and effective date for new ISIN
- **August 19, 2025**: Original notice date for the sub-division announcement
- **August 28, 2025**: New ISIN announcement date

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact limited to Pavna Industries shareholders and trading systems. The 10:1 sub-division will increase share liquidity and make shares more accessible to retail investors. Trading members need to update their systems to handle the new ISIN and share denomination changes.
