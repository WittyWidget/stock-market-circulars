## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 100 crore issued by SBICAP Securities Limited on private placement basis. The instrument has a face value of Rs. 5 lakh per unit with 2000 units issued, carrying CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ ratings.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each (2000 units)
- Total issue size: Rs. 100 crore
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,171.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730113, ISIN: INE212K14BZ0
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading permitted only in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- State Bank of India appointed as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 28, 2025
- Listing Date: August 29, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 27, 2025
- Maturity Period: 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The Commercial Paper provides short-term funding option for SBICAP Securities and investment avenue for institutional investors in the debt segment.
