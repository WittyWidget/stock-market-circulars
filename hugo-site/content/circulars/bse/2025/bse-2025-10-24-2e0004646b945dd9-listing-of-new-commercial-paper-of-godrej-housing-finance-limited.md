## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Godrej Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis in the BSE Debt segment with effect from October 24, 2025. The issue comprises 2,000 securities of Rs. 5,00,000/- each, totaling Rs. 100 crore, with a maturity of 91 days.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper on private placement basis
- Quantity: 2,000 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000/- per unit
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 100 crore
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,328.50/- per unit
- Scrip Code: 730430
- Scrip ID: GHFL231025
- ISIN: INE02JD14641
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE02JD14641
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment following standard debt market procedures

## Important Dates

- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: October 23, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: January 22, 2026
- **Tenure**: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing in the debt segment. The securities are rated A1+ by both CRISIL and ICRA, indicating highest safety for short-term debt instruments.

**Operational Impact**: Limited to debt segment trading members who deal in commercial paper. Standard trading procedures apply with dematerialized trading in Rs. 5 lakh denominations.

**Investor Impact**: Provides institutional and qualified investors another short-term investment option from a housing finance company with top credit ratings.
