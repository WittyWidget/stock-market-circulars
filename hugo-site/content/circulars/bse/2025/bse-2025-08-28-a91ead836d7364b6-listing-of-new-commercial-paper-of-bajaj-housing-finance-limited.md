## Summary

BSE has listed commercial paper worth ₹700 crores issued by Bajaj Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of ₹5,00,000 face value each
- Quantity: 14,000 units (total value ₹700 crores)
- Issue Price: ₹4,76,503 per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip Code: 730094
- ISIN: INE377Y14BS7
- Market Lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of ₹5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applies
- Central Bank of India acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 26, 2025
- Listing Date: August 28, 2025
- Redemption Date: June 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional debt market liquidity and investment options for institutional investors in the housing finance sector.
