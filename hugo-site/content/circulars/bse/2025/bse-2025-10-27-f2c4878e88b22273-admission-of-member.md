## Summary

BSE has admitted Thar Share Brokers Private Limited (Member No. 6965) as a new Trading Member with trading rights on BSE Ltd. The member has been granted access to Cash/Equity Derivatives segments and is subject to the Exchange's Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations.

## Key Points

- New member: Thar Share Brokers Private Limited
- Member Number: 6965
- Membership Type: New Membership (trading rights only on BSE Ltd.)
- Authorized Segments: Cash/Equity Derivatives
- Notice Number: 20251027-19
- Category: Others - General

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notice regarding member admission.

## Compliance Requirements

The new member must comply with all Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations of the Exchange as applicable to Trading Members.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 27, 2025
- Admission Effective: October 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative notice with minimal market impact. It expands the BSE trading member base by one entity with no changes to trading rules or regulations. Trading members requiring clarification may contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.
