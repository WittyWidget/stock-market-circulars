## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Poonawalla Fincorp Limited on private placement basis. The instrument consists of 2,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 125 crore, with a 3-month maturity period from August 26, 2025 to November 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper worth Rs. 125 crore (2,500 units Ã— Rs. 5 lakh each)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,135 per unit (face value Rs. 5 lakh)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Scrip Code: 730104, ISIN: INE511C14YX9
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 26, 2025
- Listing Date: August 28, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 25, 2025
- Maturity Period: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides additional funding options for Poonawalla Fincorp and offers investment opportunities for institutional investors seeking short-term, highly-rated debt instruments.
