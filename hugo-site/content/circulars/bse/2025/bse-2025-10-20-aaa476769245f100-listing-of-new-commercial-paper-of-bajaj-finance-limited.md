## Summary

BSE has listed 16,500 new Commercial Papers issued by Bajaj Finance Limited on private placement basis with effect from October 20, 2025. The securities are rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued at Rs. 4,92,099, and redemption scheduled for January 16, 2026. ICICI Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- **Issuer:** Bajaj Finance Limited
- **Security Type:** Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Quantity:** 16,500 units
- **Face Value:** Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 4,92,099 per unit
- **Scrip Code:** 730423
- **Scrip ID:** BFL171025
- **ISIN:** INE296A14C89
- **Credit Rating:** CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot:** 1 unit
- **Trading Segment:** BSE Debt
- **Issuing and Paying Agent:** ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE296A14C89
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Securities admitted to dealings on BSE Debt segment effective October 20, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** October 17, 2025
- **Listing Date:** October 20, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** January 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The securities provide short-term funding for Bajaj Finance Limited with a 91-day maturity period. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt market participants and institutional investors interested in highly-rated short-term debt instruments. No broader market or regulatory implications.
