## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by ICICI Home Finance Company Limited on private placement basis. The securities worth Rs. 50 crores (1000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) are now admitted to trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from July 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each with 1000 quantity listed
- Scrip Code: 729897, Scrip ID: IHFC250725
- ISIN Number: INE071G14GY0 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 493,069.50 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard commercial paper listing process followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 28, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: July 28, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Maturity Period**: Approximately 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by an established NBFC. The listing provides short-term funding avenue for ICICI Home Finance Company Limited and offers investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
