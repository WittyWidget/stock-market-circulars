## Summary

GEPL Capital Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 724) will cease to conduct business in the Currency derivative segment with effect from Friday, October 17, 2025, due to voluntary closure of business. This is an administrative notification to all trading members of the exchange.

## Key Points

- GEPL Capital Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 724) is voluntarily closing its currency derivatives business
- Cessation effective from October 17, 2025
- Notice issued to all trading members of BSE
- Contact: membership.ops@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a business closure notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for other trading members. This is an informational notice about a member's voluntary exit.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 16, 2025
- **Effective Date:** October 17, 2025 - GEPL Capital Pvt. Ltd. ceases currency derivative operations

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a single trading member's voluntary decision to exit the currency derivatives segment. No systemic implications or changes to market operations for other participants.
