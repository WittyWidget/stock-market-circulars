## Summary

BSE has approved the listing and trading of 3,64,344 new equity shares of Omega Interactive Technologies Ltd. (Scrip Code: 511644) with effect from Friday, October 10, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 118/- per share to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are subject to lock-in until May 31, 2026.

## Key Points

- 3,64,344 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each listed and permitted for trading
- Issue price: Rs. 128.00/- per share (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 118 premium)
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Distinctive Numbers: 1911933 to 2276276
- Date of allotment: July 23, 2025
- Trading commences: October 10, 2025
- ISIN: INE113B01029
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the listing of these new securities
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed until May 31, 2026 for all 3,64,344 shares

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 23, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 10, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: May 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market impact. The preferential allotment represents warrant conversion to non-promoters, adding approximately 3.64 lakh shares to the tradable equity base. The lock-in period until May 31, 2026 ensures these shares will not be immediately available for sale. Impact is limited to Omega Interactive Technologies Ltd. shareholders and does not affect broader market operations.
