## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Paisalo Digital Limited on private placement basis. The listing comprises 700 instruments with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 35 crores. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from October 15, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Paisalo Digital Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (privately placed)
- **Quantity**: 700 instruments
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,81,822.50 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Scrip Code**: 730387
- **Scrip ID**: PDL141025
- **ISIN**: INE420C14227
- **Credit Rating**: IVR A1+
- **Standard Denomination**: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: IDBI Bank Ltd

## Trading Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading permitted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Trading members must use the specified ISIN number for all transactions
- Minimum tick size for price movements is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 14, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 15, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: March 25, 2026
- **Tenor**: Approximately 5 months

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine short-term debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides Paisalo Digital Limited with working capital financing at competitive rates (implied yield approximately 8% annualized based on issue price). The IVR A1+ rating indicates adequate safety for short-term debt investors. Trading activity is expected to be limited to institutional participants in the debt market.
