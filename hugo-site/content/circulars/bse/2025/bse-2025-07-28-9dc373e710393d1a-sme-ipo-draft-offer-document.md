## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of OM METALLOGIC LIMITED for its SME IPO, filed on July 27, 2025. The document is available on BSE's website for a mandatory 21-day public comment period as per ICDR Regulations.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by OM METALLOGIC LIMITED on July 27, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This follows standard ICDR regulatory requirements for SME IPO draft documents.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public and stakeholders can review the Draft Offer Document
- Comments must be submitted within 21 days (by August 18, 2025)
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Company must address public comments before final filing

## Important Dates

- **July 27, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed
- **July 28, 2025**: Notice published and public comment period begins
- **August 18, 2025**: Last date for public comments (21 days from notice)

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard procedural step in the SME IPO process. The public comment period allows for transparency and regulatory compliance before the company proceeds with its public offering on the BSE SME platform.
