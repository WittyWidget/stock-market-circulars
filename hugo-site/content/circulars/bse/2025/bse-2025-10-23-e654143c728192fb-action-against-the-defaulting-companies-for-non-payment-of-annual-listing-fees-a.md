## Summary

BSE has issued a notice confirming that no further action will be taken against RNIT AI Solutions Ltd. (formerly Autopal Industries Ltd., Scrip Code: 517286) as the company has complied with Annual Listing Fees (ALF) payment requirements under Regulation 14 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. This notice follows the Exchange's previous communications regarding companies that failed to pay outstanding Annual Listing Fees.

## Key Points

- RNIT AI Solutions Ltd. (Scrip Code: 517286) has cleared outstanding Annual Listing Fees
- Company was previously identified as defaulting on ALF payments
- BSE will not take further action against this company
- Company was formerly known as Autopal Industries Ltd.
- Trading members notified of compliance status

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This notice confirms compliance with existing requirements under Regulation 14 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed companies must pay Annual Listing Fees as per Regulation 14 of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015
- Companies that clear outstanding ALF payments will have enforcement actions withdrawn
- Trading members should note the updated compliance status of affected companies
- For clarifications, members may contact bse.sopalf@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **October 23, 2025**: Notice date confirming compliance
- **October 15, 2025**: Previous exchange notice (20251015-16) referenced
- **June 25, 2021**: Original exchange notice (20210625-43) on ALF enforcement
- **March 03, 2020**: Exchange notice (20200303-40) on ALF requirements

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a positive administrative update for a single company that has resolved its fee payment obligations.

**Operational Impact**: RNIT AI Solutions Ltd. avoids potential penalties or suspension actions for non-payment of listing fees. Normal trading operations continue without disruption.

**Investor Impact**: Low impact for investors in RNIT AI Solutions Ltd. The company's compliance removes any regulatory overhang related to listing fee payments.
