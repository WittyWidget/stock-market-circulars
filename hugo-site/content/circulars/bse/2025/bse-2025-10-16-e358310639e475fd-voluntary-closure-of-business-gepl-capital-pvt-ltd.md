## Summary

GEPL Capital Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 724), a trading member of BSE, will cease operations in the Currency derivative segment effective Friday, October 17, 2025, due to voluntary closure of business. This is an administrative notification to all trading members of the exchange.

## Key Points

- GEPL Capital Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 724) is voluntarily closing its business
- Closure applies to Currency derivative segment only
- Effective date: October 17, 2025 (Friday)
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a member-initiated voluntary business closure.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance actions required from other trading members. This is an informational notice only.

## Important Dates

- **October 17, 2025**: Effective date of cessation of business in Currency derivative segment

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a single trading member voluntarily exiting the Currency derivative segment. No disruption to market operations or trading activities is expected. Other market participants remain unaffected.
