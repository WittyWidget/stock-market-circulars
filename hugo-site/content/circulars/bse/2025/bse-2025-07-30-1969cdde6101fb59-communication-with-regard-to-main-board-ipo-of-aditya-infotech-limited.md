## Summary

BSE has issued a clarification regarding the Main Board IPO of Aditya Infotech Limited, emphasizing that the company is not affiliated with Aditya Birla Group despite the similarity in names. The IPO is open for subscription from July 29-31, 2025.

## Key Points

- Aditya Infotech Limited IPO is not affiliated with Aditya Birla Group
- IPO subscription period: July 29, 2025 to July 31, 2025
- Clarification issued by merchant bankers to avoid investor confusion
- Investors advised to refer to Red Herring Prospectus dated July 23, 2025
- Circular issued for information of IPO applicants

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a clarification notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors must refer to the Red Herring Prospectus dated July 23, 2025 before making investment decisions
- Due diligence required to distinguish between companies with similar names

## Important Dates

- IPO Subscription Period: July 29, 2025 to July 31, 2025
- Red Herring Prospectus Date: July 23, 2025
- Notice Date: July 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This clarification helps prevent potential investor confusion and mis-investment due to name similarity with the well-known Aditya Birla Group. It ensures informed decision-making during the IPO subscription period and protects investor interests by providing clear company identification.
