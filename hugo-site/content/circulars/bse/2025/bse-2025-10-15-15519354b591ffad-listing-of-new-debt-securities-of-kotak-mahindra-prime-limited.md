## Summary

BSE has listed two new debt securities issued by Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited on private placement basis, effective October 15, 2025. The first security (ISIN: INE916DA7TF3) has a face value of Rs. 1,00,000, maturity date of October 14, 2030, and carries an interest rate of 7.2640% p.a. with CRISIL-AAA/STABLE and ICRA AAA/STABLE ratings. The second security (ISIN: INE916DA7TA4) is a further listing under an existing ISIN, with face value of Rs. 1,00,000, maturity date of June 19, 2028, and interest rate of 7.1554% p.a. with CRISIL AAA/stable rating.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities of Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited listed on BSE Debt segment
- First security: 40,000 units with scrip code 977209, ISIN INE916DA7TF3, 7.2640% interest rate, maturing October 14, 2030
- Second security: 20,000 units with scrip code 976816, ISIN INE916DA7TA4 (further listing), 7.1554% interest rate, maturing June 19, 2028
- Both securities rated AAA by CRISIL and/or ICRA indicating highest credit quality
- Securities to be traded only in dematerialized form
- Market lot is 1 unit for both securities
- Tick size for trading is 1 paise
- Date of allotment for both securities: October 14, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for detailed information
- Any clarifications required should be directed to BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: October 15, 2025
- **Allotment Date (both securities)**: October 14, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates (ISIN INE916DA7TF3)**: Annually from October 14, 2026 to October 14, 2030
- **Redemption Date (ISIN INE916DA7TF3)**: October 14, 2030
- **Interest Payment Dates (ISIN INE916DA7TA4)**: Annually from June 19, 2026 to June 19, 2028
- **Redemption Date (ISIN INE916DA7TA4)**: June 19, 2028

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine private placement listing that expands the debt securities available for institutional investors. The AAA ratings indicate high credit quality instruments.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members need to be aware of the new scrip codes and ISIN numbers for trading purposes. The securities follow standard trading protocols with dematerialized form requirement and 1 paise tick size.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant primarily for institutional investors and debt market participants seeking AAA-rated fixed income instruments with tenures of approximately 3 years (2028 maturity) and 5 years (2030 maturity). The interest rates of 7.1554% and 7.2640% reflect prevailing market conditions for AAA-rated corporate bonds.
