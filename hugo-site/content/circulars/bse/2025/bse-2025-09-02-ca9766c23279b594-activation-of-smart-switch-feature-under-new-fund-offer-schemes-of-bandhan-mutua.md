## Summary

BSE has activated the SMART Switch feature for BANDHAN Overnight Fund schemes under their New Fund Offer on the BSE StAR MF platform. This enables automated switching functionality for both Regular and Direct plans with Growth options of the BANDHAN Overnight Fund.

## Key Points

- SMART Switch feature activated for BANDHAN Overnight Fund on BSE StAR MF platform
- Covers both Regular Plan and Direct Plan with Growth options
- Part of New Fund Offer schemes from BANDHAN Mutual Fund
- Feature enables automated switching between fund schemes

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is an operational enhancement to the BSE StAR MF platform for existing mutual fund switching capabilities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Mutual fund distributors and participants on BSE StAR MF platform should note the availability of SMART Switch feature for the specified BANDHAN Overnight Fund schemes
- No additional compliance requirements specified

## Important Dates

- Activation Date: September 2, 2025 (based on circular date)
- No specific implementation deadlines mentioned

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a platform feature enhancement that provides investors with automated switching capabilities for BANDHAN Overnight Fund schemes. The feature facilitates easier fund management but does not introduce any structural changes to the market or trading mechanisms.
