## Summary

BSE has listed commercial paper worth Rs. 175 crore issued by Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited on private placement basis. The securities carry CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings and will mature on October 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5 lakh face value each, totaling 3,500 units
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,885 per unit (discount of Rs. 7,115)
- Scrip Code: 729956, ISIN: INE755K14FK1
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+
- IDBI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 1, 2025
- Listing Date: August 4, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 29, 2025
- Tenure: 89 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates strong creditworthiness of the issuer. The short-term nature (89 days) provides liquidity options for institutional investors seeking money market instruments.
