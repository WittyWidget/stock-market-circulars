## Summary

BSE has announced a demat auction for Settlement No. 736 dated October 16, 2025. The auction covers 69 scrips with quantities ranging from 1 share to 100,000 shares across various sectors including banking, automobile, infrastructure, consumer goods, energy, and financial services.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 736
- Date: October 16, 2025
- Total scrips involved: 69
- Highest auction quantity: Vantage Know (100,000 shares), Enbee Trade (98,000 shares)
- Lowest auction quantity: Multiple scrips with 1 share each
- Major scrips include: Motherson Sumi (6,797 shares), Reliance Power (6,700 shares), Nippon Life (14,613 shares), Nippon India (4,408 shares), PTC India Financial (4,301 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine settlement auction notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Brokers and trading members must participate in the auction to fulfill delivery obligations
- Participants must ensure adequate funds/securities are available for auction settlement
- Settlement obligations must be completed as per BSE settlement calendar
- Non-fulfillment may result in penalties as per BSE rules

## Important Dates

- Auction Date: October 16, 2025
- Settlement Number: 736
- Specific auction timing and settlement completion dates to be followed as per BSE settlement schedule

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The auction affects 69 securities across diverse sectors. While most quantities are relatively small, some scrips have significant auction volumes that may impact short-term price discovery.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Brokers with short positions in these securities must participate in the auction. Large quantities in specific scrips (Vantage Know, Enbee Trade, Motherson Sumi, Reliance Power) may require significant capital deployment.

**Investor Impact:** Medium - Investors expecting delivery of these securities may face settlement delays if auction participation is insufficient. Price volatility may occur in auction scrips during the settlement period.
