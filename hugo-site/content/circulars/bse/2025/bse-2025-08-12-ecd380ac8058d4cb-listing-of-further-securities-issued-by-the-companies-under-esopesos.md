## Summary

BSE has listed additional securities from 11 companies issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS), which will be available for trading from August 13, 2025. The securities range from 1,000 shares (Arvind Fashions) to 4,385,250 shares (Suzlon Energy).

## Key Points

- 11 companies have issued additional securities under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- All securities will be admitted for trading on August 13, 2025
- No lock-in period applies to any of the listed securities
- Total securities range from small allocations to significant volumes
- Companies span various sectors including financial services, technology, and manufacturing

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced - this is a routine listing notification under existing ESOP/ESOS framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities availability
- Securities can be traded normally from the effective date
- Standard trading and settlement procedures apply

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 13, 2025 - Securities admitted for trading
- **Notice Date**: August 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine ESOP issuances. The securities represent employee compensation and typically have limited immediate trading activity. Suzlon Energy has the largest allocation with over 4.3 million shares, which could have modest impact on its trading volumes.
