## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 600 crore issued by ICICI Securities Primary Dealership Limited on private placement basis. The securities are listed on BSE Debt segment with effect from August 14, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- denomination each
- Total quantity: 12,000 units (Rs. 600 crore)
- Scrip Code: 730020, Scrip ID: ISPD14825
- ISIN: INE849D14HW8
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 484,354 per unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Ltd as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Use specified tick size of 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: August 14, 2025
- Allotment Date: August 14, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 10, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt market listing with minimal market impact. Provides additional short-term debt instrument for institutional investors. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
