## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debentures of IKF Finance Limited effective August 4, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date for interest payment and redemption of its non-convertible debentures.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to IKF Finance Limited debentures (Code: IKFFL-9.75%-11-8-25-PVT)
- ISIN: INE859C07154 (975026)
- Suspension effective from August 4, 2025
- Record date set for August 4, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members advised not to deal in the specified debentures from August 4, 2025
- Trading members must take note of the suspension

## Important Dates

- **August 4, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and redemption
- **August 4, 2025**: Trading suspension begins

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this affects only one company's debt securities. Routine corporate action requiring temporary trading halt to facilitate interest payment and debenture redemption process.
