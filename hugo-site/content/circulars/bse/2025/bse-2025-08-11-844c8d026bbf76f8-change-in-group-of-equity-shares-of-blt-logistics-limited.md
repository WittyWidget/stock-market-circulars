## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of BLT LOGISTICS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544474) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment, shifting to M Group classification effective August 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: BLT LOGISTICS LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544474
- Current classification: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New classification: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: Tuesday, August 26, 2025
- Segment: SME

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism will change from Trade for Trade (where each trade must be settled individually) to Rolling settlement (where trades are settled on a net basis at regular intervals).

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should note the change in trading segment and adjust their systems accordingly. For queries, Trading Members can contact Mr. Parag Jain on Tel. No. 022 2272 8685.

## Important Dates

- **August 26, 2025**: Effective date for transfer to Rolling segment

## Impact Assessment

This change is likely to improve liquidity for BLT LOGISTICS LIMITED shares as Rolling segment allows for more flexible trading compared to Trade for Trade segment. The shift indicates improved trading confidence in the stock.
