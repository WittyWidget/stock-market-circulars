## Summary

Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE's Debt segment effective August 28, 2025. The securities were issued on a private placement basis with quantity of 585 units, face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, and carry CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable rating.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 976954, Scrip ID: NWFL24725
- ISIN Number: INE918K07PX1 (Further Listings under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 97,141.00
- Date of Allotment: August 25, 2025
- Date of Redemption: January 22, 2029
- Trading only in dematerialised form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form
- Securities will be traded under the specified ISIN Number
- Trading members should refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details

## Important Dates

- Listing Effective Date: August 28, 2025
- Allotment Date: August 25, 2025
- Redemption Date: January 22, 2029

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities. The listing expands Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited's debt market presence and provides additional trading opportunities in the debt segment.
