## Summary

360 One Wam Limited has listed new Commercial Paper of Rs. 5 lakhs denomination each (total 2000 units) on BSE Debt segment effective August 8, 2025. The instruments are issued on private placement basis with CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ ratings.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total quantity: 2000 units (Rs. 10 crores total value)
- Scrip Code: 729988, Scrip ID: 360OWL7825
- ISIN: INE466L14ER1
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,604.50 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 7, 2025
- Trading Start Date: August 8, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 6, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding instrument for institutional investors with 3-month maturity period.
