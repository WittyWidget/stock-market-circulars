## Summary

ECL Finance Limited has listed new Commercial Paper securities worth Rs.25 crores on BSE's Debt segment effective July 30, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with face value of Rs.5 lakhs each.

## Key Points

- 500 Commercial Paper units of Rs.5,00,000 each listed
- Scrip Code: 729277, Scrip ID: ECLF25425
- ISIN: INE804I14XW9 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IVR A1+
- Issue Price: Rs.480,589.5 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples
- ICICI Bank Limited serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 29, 2025
- Listing Date: July 30, 2025
- Redemption Date: January 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt instrument options for institutional investors in ECL Finance Limited securities.
