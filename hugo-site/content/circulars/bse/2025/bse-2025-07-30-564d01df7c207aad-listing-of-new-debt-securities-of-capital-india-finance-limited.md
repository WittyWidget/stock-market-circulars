## Summary

Capital India Finance Limited has listed new debt securities worth ₹50 crore on BSE Debt segment through private placement. The securities carry a 9.55% annual interest rate with a 3-year tenure maturing on July 28, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 5,000 securities with face value of ₹1,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 976963 (CIFL-9.55%-28-7-28-PVT)
- ISIN: INE345H07054
- Credit Rating: IVR A/STABLE
- Issue Price: ₹1,00,000 per security
- Date of Allotment: July 28, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt security listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading members should refer to Placement Memorandum for detailed terms

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: July 30, 2025
- Interest Payment: Annually from July 28, 2026 to July 28, 2028
- Redemption Date: July 28, 2028
- No put/call options available

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing. The securities expand BSE's debt segment offerings and provide investors with a fixed-income instrument rated IVR A/STABLE with competitive 9.55% yield.
