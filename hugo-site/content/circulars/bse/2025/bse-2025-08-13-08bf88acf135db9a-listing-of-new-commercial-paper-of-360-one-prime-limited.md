## Summary

360 ONE Prime Limited has listed new commercial paper securities on BSE's Debt segment effective August 13, 2025. The commercial paper was issued on private placement basis with face value of Rs.5 lakhs each, carrying high credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper worth Rs.5,00,000 face value each listed on BSE Debt segment
- Total quantity: 500 securities with market lot of 1
- Issue price: Rs.498,579.50 per security
- Scrip Code: 730006, Scrip ID: 360OP11825
- ISIN: INE248U14SB8 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- HDFC Bank Limited serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size set at 1 paise
- Trading members must ensure compliance with debt segment trading rules

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 12, 2025
- Listing Date: August 13, 2025
- Redemption Date: August 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term commercial paper listing with 16-day tenure. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk. Standard debt segment trading procedures apply with no changes to existing market operations.
