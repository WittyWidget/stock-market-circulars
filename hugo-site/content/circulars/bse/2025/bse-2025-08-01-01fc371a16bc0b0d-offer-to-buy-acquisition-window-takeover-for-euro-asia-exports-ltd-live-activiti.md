## Summary

BSE announces the schedule for Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for EURO ASIA EXPORTS LTD shares. The offer period runs from Monday, August 4, 2025 to Monday, August 18, 2025, covering up to 34,64,900 equity shares at Rs. 10.00 per share.

## Key Points

- Security: EURO ASIA EXPORTS LTD (Symbol: EUROASIA, ISIN: INE535P01015, Scrip Code: 530929)
- Offer size: Up to 34,64,900 equity shares
- Offer price: Rs. 10.00 per share (Face Value: Rs. 10.00)
- Market lot: 1 share
- Bid offering period: August 4-18, 2025 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- Give-up/Take-up period: August 4-14, 2025 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- Final day take-up extended until 4:00 PM on August 18, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure UCC of investors is registered with correct PAN Number and Category
- Members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Trading Members and Custodian Members must follow operational instructions per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 and 20170203-29
- Access to iBBS platform, RTRMS, and Extranet required for participation

## Important Dates

- **Opening Date**: Monday, August 4, 2025
- **Closing Date**: Monday, August 18, 2025
- **Bid Offering Period**: August 4-18, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: August 4-14, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Final Give-up**: August 18, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Final Take-up**: August 18, 2025 (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact limited to EURO ASIA EXPORTS LTD shareholders and participating trading members. The takeover offer provides an exit opportunity for existing shareholders at the offered price of Rs. 10.00 per share. Trading members need to ensure system readiness and compliance with UCC requirements for smooth participation in the acquisition window.
