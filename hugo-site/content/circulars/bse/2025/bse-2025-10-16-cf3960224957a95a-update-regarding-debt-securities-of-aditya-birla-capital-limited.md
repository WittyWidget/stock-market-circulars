## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding debt securities of Aditya Birla Capital Limited (Scrip Code: 977103). Following receipt of call money from debenture holders, the ISIN and paid-up value of the listed debt securities will be revised effective October 17, 2025. The paid-up value per debenture will increase from Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 1,00,000, and a new ISIN will be assigned.

## Key Points

- Continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20250915-28 dated September 15, 2025
- Company has received call money from debenture holders
- New ISIN assigned: INE674K07127 (replacing INE674K07085)
- Paid-up value doubled from Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Changes apply to Scrip Code: 977103

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is an administrative update reflecting corporate action on existing debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the new ISIN and paid-up value for trading purposes
- Update systems and records to reflect the revised ISIN: INE674K07127
- Ensure correct paid-up value of Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture is reflected in all transactions

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 17, 2025 (Friday) - New ISIN and paid-up value become effective

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Affects specific debt security holders of Aditya Birla Capital Limited. The doubling of paid-up value indicates call money has been received, increasing the investment amount per debenture.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members and debenture holders must update their records with the new ISIN and revised paid-up value. Existing positions will need to be tracked under the new identifier from October 17, 2025 onwards.
