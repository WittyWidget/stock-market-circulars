## Summary

BSE has announced the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for ANKA INDIA LTD (ANKIN). The offer will run from Tuesday, October 14, 2025 to Wednesday, October 29, 2025. Trading Members and Custodian Members can facilitate bids for up to 69,24,902 equity shares at an offer price of Rs. 17.00 per share through the iBBS and RTRMS platforms.

## Key Points

- **Security**: ANKA INDIA LTD (Symbol: ANKIN, Scrip Code: 531673, ISIN: INE067C01025)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 69,24,902 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 17.00 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Bidding Period**: October 14-29, 2025 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: October 10-29, 2025 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Give-up**: October 28, 2025 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- **Last Day Take-up**: October 28, 2025 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure investor UCC is registered and/or updated with correct PAN Number and Category in the Exchange UCC database
- Trading Members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Members should refer to Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions
- For Leased Line URL access settings, refer to Exchange Circular No. 20170503-12
- If no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet

## Impact Assessment

This takeover offer impacts shareholders of ANKA INDIA LTD who wish to tender their shares during the acquisition window. Trading Members and Custodians must ensure their systems and investor registrations are updated to facilitate smooth bidding. The 16-day bidding window provides adequate time for investors to participate in the offer at the fixed price of Rs. 17.00 per share, representing a 70% premium over the face value of Rs. 10.00.
