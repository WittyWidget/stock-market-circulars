## Summary

BSE has notified trading members about the name change of trading member JAYPEE CAPITAL SERVICES LTD. (Member No. 948) to "JAYPEE CAPITAL LIMITED" effective Wednesday, July 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading member name change from JAYPEE CAPITAL SERVICES LTD. to JAYPEE CAPITAL LIMITED
- Member number 948 remains unchanged
- Change is purely administrative in nature
- Contact provided for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes - this is an administrative name change notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from other trading members. This is an informational notice.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Wednesday, July 30, 2025
- **Notice Date**: July 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact as this is purely an administrative name change. Trading operations and member number remain unchanged. Other market participants should update their records accordingly.
