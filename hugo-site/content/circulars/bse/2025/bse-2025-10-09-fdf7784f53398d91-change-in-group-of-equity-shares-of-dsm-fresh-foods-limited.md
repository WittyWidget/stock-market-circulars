## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of DSM FRESH FOODS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544568), an SME IPO listing, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective October 27, 2025. This follows the earlier notice dated October 8, 2025, and represents a positive development allowing normal rolling settlement instead of trade-for-trade basis.

## Key Points

- Company: DSM FRESH FOODS LIMITED - SME IPO
- Scrip Code: 544568
- Current Classification: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Classification: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Monday, October 27, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251009-3
- Reference Notice: 20251008-53 dated October 8, 2025
- Contact: Mr. Anurag Jain, Tel: 022-2272 8822

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will transition from restrictive Trade for Trade (T2T) settlement to regular Rolling settlement mechanism. This change allows:

- Intraday trading opportunities (previously restricted under T2T)
- Normal delivery-based settlement cycles
- Enhanced liquidity and price discovery
- Removal of 100% upfront margin requirement for buyers

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members:**
- Update internal systems to reflect the group change from MT to M Group
- Adjust trading strategies and client communications accordingly
- Contact BSE officials for clarification if needed before October 27, 2025

**For Investors:**
- No specific action required
- Aware that trading mechanics will change from T2T to rolling settlement

## Important Dates

- **October 8, 2025**: Initial notice issued (20251008-53)
- **October 9, 2025**: Confirmation notice issued (20251009-3)
- **October 27, 2025**: Effective date for group transfer from MT to M Group

## Impact Assessment

**Positive Impact:**
- Improved liquidity for DSM FRESH FOODS LIMITED shares
- Greater flexibility for traders and investors
- Transition indicates the stock has stabilized post-IPO and met BSE's criteria for normal trading

**Market Participants Affected:**
- Trading members dealing in DSM FRESH FOODS LIMITED
- Retail and institutional investors holding or interested in the stock
- Market makers and liquidity providers

**Operational Impact:**
Medium - Trading members need to update systems and processes, but the transition is straightforward and allows for better market efficiency.
