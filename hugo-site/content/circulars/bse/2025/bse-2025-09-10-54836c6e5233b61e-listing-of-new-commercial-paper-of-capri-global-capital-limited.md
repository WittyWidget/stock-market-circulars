## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Capri Global Capital Limited on private placement basis. The securities are worth Rs. 5,00,000 each with 100 quantity, totaling Rs. 5 crore. The papers are rated CRISIL A1+ and will mature on December 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total quantity: 100 units (Rs. 5 crore total value)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,90,642 per unit
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+
- Scrip code: 730178, Scrip ID: CGCL100925
- ISIN: INE180C14599
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Yes Bank Ltd serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: September 10, 2025
- Listing effective date: September 10, 2025
- Redemption date: December 10, 2025
- Maturity period: 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The securities provide short-term funding option for Capri Global Capital Limited and investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
