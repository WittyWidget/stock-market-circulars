## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Vivriti Capital Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective September 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper issued on private placement basis
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,85,135 per unit
- Quantity: 500 units (total value Rs. 25 crores)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+
- Scrip code: 730162, Scrip ID: VCL4925
- ISIN: INE01HV14946
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Federal Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: September 4, 2025
- Trading commencement: September 5, 2025
- Date of redemption: January 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt instrument for institutional investors with high credit rating (A1+).
