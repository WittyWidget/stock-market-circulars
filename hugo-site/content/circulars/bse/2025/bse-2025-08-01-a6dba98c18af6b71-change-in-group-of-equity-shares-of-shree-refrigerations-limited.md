## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of SHREE REFRIGERATIONS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544458) will be transferred from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment effective Monday, August 18, 2025. The company's dealings will be shifted under M Group.

## Key Points

- Company: SHREE REFRIGERATIONS LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544458
- Current segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: Monday, August 18, 2025
- This is a continuation of exchange notice no. 20250731-58 dated July 31, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for SHREE REFRIGERATIONS LIMITED shares will change from Trade for Trade basis to Rolling settlement, allowing for more flexible trading arrangements.
