## Summary

BSE has issued the list of securities subject to Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective August 5, 2025. Seven securities have been shortlisted for inclusion in the framework, while no securities are moving between ASM stages or exiting the framework.

## Key Points

- 7 securities newly shortlisted for Long Term ASM Framework
- No securities moving to higher or lower ASM stages
- No securities moving out of the framework
- Consolidated list includes securities across different ASM stages (I, II, III, IV)
- Framework applies additional surveillance measures to manage trading risks

## Regulatory Changes

- Implementation of Long Term ASM framework for identified securities
- Enhanced monitoring and surveillance measures for listed companies
- Structured approach with different ASM stages based on risk assessment

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities under LT-ASM framework subject to additional surveillance measures
- Enhanced disclosure and monitoring requirements for affected companies
- Trading restrictions and special settlement procedures may apply

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 5, 2025
- Framework applies from market opening on the effective date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects trading conditions for specific securities under surveillance

**Operational Impact**: Companies included in the framework face enhanced scrutiny and potential trading restrictions. Investors should be aware of additional surveillance measures when trading these securities.

**Risk Management**: Framework designed to protect investor interests through enhanced monitoring of securities exhibiting unusual price movements or trading patterns.
