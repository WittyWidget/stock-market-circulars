## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of ADITYA BIRLA DIGITAL FASHION VENTURES LIMITED issued on private placement basis. The securities carry 8.5% interest rate with 2-year maturity and will trade on BSE Debt segment from August 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 10,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Face value: ₹1,00,000 per security (total issue size ₹10 crore)
- Interest rate: 8.5% per annum paid annually
- Credit rating: CRISIL AA-
- ISIN: INE0M8D08024
- Scrip code: 977013

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialised form
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Trading members should refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: August 8, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 11, 2025
- Interest payment dates: August 8, 2026 to August 8, 2027 (annually)
- Redemption date: August 8, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement debt listing. The securities will provide additional investment options in the debt segment for institutional investors.
