## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 300 crore (6000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued by Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited on private placement basis. The securities are effective for trading on BSE debt segment from August 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- 6000 commercial paper units of Rs. 5,00,000 each listed
- Scrip Code: 730088, ISIN: INE916D145P3
- Issue price: Rs. 4,71,604 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities in dematerialized form only
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Kotak Mahindra Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 26, 2025
- Trading Effective Date: August 26, 2025
- Redemption Date: July 21, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt instrument option for investors in the BSE debt segment.
