## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 150 crores issued by Aditya Birla Lifestyle Brands Limited on private placement basis. The instruments carry CRISIL A1+ rating and will mature on October 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- 3,000 Commercial Paper units of Rs. 5 lakh each listed on BSE Debt segment
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,711 per unit (face value Rs. 5,00,000)
- ISIN: INE14LE14012, Scrip Code: 729953
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Allotment date: July 31, 2025
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise for trading

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 31, 2025
- Listing Date: August 1, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding avenue for Aditya Birla Lifestyle Brands Limited with approximately 3-month tenure.
