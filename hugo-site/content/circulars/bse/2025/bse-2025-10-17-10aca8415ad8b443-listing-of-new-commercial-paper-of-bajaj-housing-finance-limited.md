## Summary

Bajaj Housing Finance Limited has listed 15,000 new Commercial Papers on BSE's Debt segment effective October 17, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each and will be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 15,000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 730415
- Scrip ID: BHFL161025
- ISIN: INE377Y14BT5
- Issue Price: Rs. 492,606.50
- Face/Paid-up Value: Rs. 5,00,000
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1
- Trading: Only in dematerialized form
- Standard Denomination: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Central Bank of India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE377Y14BT5
- Trading shall occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: January 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term funding for Bajaj Housing Finance Limited with a maturity period of 91 days. The high credit rating (CRISIL A1+, IND A1+) indicates strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors in commercial papers.
