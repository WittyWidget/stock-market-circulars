## Summary

ZERODHA MULTI ASSET PASSIVE FOF will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP, STP & SWP facilities on BSE StAR MF Platform effective August 20, 2025.

## Key Points

- ZERODHA MULTI ASSET PASSIVE FOF - DIRECT PLAN GROWTH reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available facilities: Subscription, Redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Scheme Category: FOF (Fund of Funds)
- ISIN: INF0R8F01117
- Platform: BSE StAR MF Platform

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine reopening of an existing mutual fund scheme for ongoing transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

Members can facilitate ongoing transactions in the specified scheme through BSE StAR MF Platform starting August 20, 2025.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: August 20, 2025
- Notice Date: August 19, 2025
- Reference Notice: 20250724-31

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine operational matter affecting only one mutual fund scheme. Provides additional investment option for investors seeking multi-asset passive fund of funds exposure through BSE platform.
