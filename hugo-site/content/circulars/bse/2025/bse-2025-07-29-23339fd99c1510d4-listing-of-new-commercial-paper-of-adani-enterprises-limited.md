## Summary

Adani Enterprises Limited has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 26.5 crores (530 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE debt segment. The paper carries CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ ratings with 91-day maturity period.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 530 units (Rs. 26.5 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,123 per unit
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 729927, ISIN: INE423A14XW5
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: July 28, 2025
- Listing date: July 29, 2025
- Redemption date: October 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding option for Adani Enterprises with 91-day maturity. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
