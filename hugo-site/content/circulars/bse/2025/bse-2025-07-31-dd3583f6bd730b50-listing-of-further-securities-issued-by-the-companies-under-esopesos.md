## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of additional securities from 12 companies issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be admitted for trading on the exchange effective Friday, August 1, 2025. All listed securities have no lock-in period restrictions.

## Key Points

- 12 companies issuing additional securities under ESOP/ESOS programs
- Total of 2,275,406 new shares across all companies
- All securities effective for trading from August 1, 2025
- No lock-in period applicable for any of the listed securities
- Face values range from ₹1 to ₹10 per share

## Company Details

**Major Issuances:**
- L&T Finance Limited: 1,238,524 shares (₹10 face value)
- Axis Bank Ltd: 323,974 shares (₹2 face value)
- 360 ONE WAM Limited: 344,361 shares (₹1 face value)
- Le Travenues Technology Limited (ixigo): 111,644 shares (₹1 face value)

**Other Companies:**
- Campus Activewear Limited: 16,208 shares
- Geojit Financial Services Limited: 96,667 shares
- ICICI Prudential Life Insurance: 52,467 shares
- JANA Small Finance Bank: 25,032 shares
- KFin Technologies Limited: 20,626 shares
- Tata Technologies Limited: 11,137 shares
- Lloyds Metals and Energy: 9,541 shares
- Kirloskar Industries: 1,425 shares

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** July 31, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date:** August 1, 2025
- **Lock-in Period:** Not applicable for all securities

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing with minimal market impact. The securities represent employee stock options being converted to tradeable shares, which is a normal corporate activity. The absence of lock-in restrictions means these shares can be traded immediately, though the impact on individual stock prices will depend on the quantum relative to existing float and employee selling behavior.
