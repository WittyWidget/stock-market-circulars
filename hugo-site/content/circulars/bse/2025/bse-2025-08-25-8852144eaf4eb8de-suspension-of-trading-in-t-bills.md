## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Treasury Bill 182TB29825 (ISIN: IN002024Y464, Scrip Code: 805032) effective August 26, 2025, due to the instrument reaching its redemption/maturity date.

## Key Points

- Single T-Bill affected: 182TB29825 with scrip code 805032
- ISIN: IN002024Y464
- Trading suspension effective from August 26, 2025
- Reason: Maturity on redemption date
- Notice reference: DR-702/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine administrative action for maturing debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified T-Bill from August 26, 2025
- All trading activity in this instrument must cease before the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** August 25, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective:** August 26, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date:** August 26, 2025 (implied)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only one Treasury Bill reaching natural maturity. This is a standard operational procedure for debt instruments in the BSE debt segment. Trading members should ensure compliance with the trading suspension to avoid any operational issues.
