## Summary

BSE has published Settlement No. 699 dated August 22, 2025, listing securities available for demat auction. The settlement includes 104 different scrips from various companies with quantities ranging from 1 to 27,119 shares.

## Key Points

- Settlement number: 699
- Settlement date: August 22, 2025
- Total securities listed: 104 different scrips
- Quantity range: 1 to 27,119 shares per scrip
- Notable high-quantity auctions: India Cement (27,119), Aerpace Industries (24,000), Vintron Info (13,013)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned - this is a routine settlement auction listing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants must comply with BSE demat auction procedures
- Settlement obligations for listed securities must be fulfilled
- Standard auction settlement timelines apply

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: August 22, 2025
- Settlement Number: 699

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on affected securities and market participants. The auction involves a diverse range of companies across sectors including banking (ICICI Bank, Bank of India), steel (Tata Steel), pharmaceuticals (Cipla), and various industrial companies. Participants holding positions in these securities may need to participate in the auction process to fulfill settlement obligations.
