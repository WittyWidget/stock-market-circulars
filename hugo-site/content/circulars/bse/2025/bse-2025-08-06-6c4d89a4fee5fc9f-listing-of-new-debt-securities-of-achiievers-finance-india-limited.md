## Summary

BSE has listed six new debt securities of ACHIIEVERS FINANCE INDIA LIMITED issued on private placement basis, effective August 6, 2025. The securities have face values of Rs. 100,000 each with varying interest rates from 11.75% to 12.50% per annum and maturity periods ranging from 2027 to 2031.

## Key Points

- Six debt securities listed with total quantity of 201 units
- All securities have face value of Rs. 100,000 each
- Interest rates range from 11.75% to 12.50% per annum
- Credit ratings: IVR BB+/Stable, IVR BB/Stable, and IVR BB-/STABLE
- Date of allotment: August 2, 2025
- Securities will trade only in dematerialised form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Securities Details

- AFIL-12%-01-08-30-PVT: 98 units, 12% p.a., monthly interest, matures Aug 1, 2030
- AFIL-11.75%-01-08-28-PVT: 28 units, 11.75% p.a., monthly interest, matures Aug 1, 2028
- AFIL-12.25%-01-08-28-PVT: 14 units, 12.25% p.a., cumulative payment, matures Aug 1, 2028
- AFIL-12%-31-07-27-PVT: 5 units, 12% p.a., annual interest, matures July 31, 2027
- AFIL-12%-31-07-27-PVT: 40 units, 12% p.a., cumulative payment, matures July 31, 2027
- AFIL-12.50%-12-06-31-PVT: 16 units, 12.50% p.a., cumulative payment, matures June 12, 2031

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 2, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 6, 2025
- Maturity Dates: July 31, 2027 to August 1, 2030

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities with relatively small quantities. The securities provide investors with fixed-income options with competitive interest rates.
