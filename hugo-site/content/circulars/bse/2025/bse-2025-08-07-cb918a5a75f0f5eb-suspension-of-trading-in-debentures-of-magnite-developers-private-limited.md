## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debentures of MAGNITE DEVELOPERS PRIVATE LIMITED effective August 11, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to MAGNITE DEVELOPERS PRIVATE LIMITED debentures (Code: MDPL-10%-31-3-27-PVT)
- ISIN: INE0M1Q07023 (974800)
- Suspension effective from August 11, 2025
- Trading members advised not to deal in these debentures

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - standard procedure for debenture corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from August 11, 2025
- Trading members required to take note of the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: August 11, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: August 11, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact limited to holders and traders of MAGNITE DEVELOPERS PRIVATE LIMITED debentures. Standard corporate action procedure with temporary trading suspension to facilitate redemption and interest payment processes.
