## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities worth Rs. 200 crore issued by Godrej Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective August 28, 2025. The securities will trade under scrip code 730103 with ISIN INE02JD14633.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper face value: Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total quantity: 4,000 units (Rs. 200 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,691.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 730103, Scrip ID: GHFL260825
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 26, 2025
- Listing effective: August 28, 2025
- Redemption date: November 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt market listing with minimal impact on overall market operations. Provides additional short-term debt instrument for institutional investors with 91-day maturity period.
