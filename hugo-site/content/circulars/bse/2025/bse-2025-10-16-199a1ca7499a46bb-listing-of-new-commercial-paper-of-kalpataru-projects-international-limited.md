## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Kalpataru Projects International Limited on private placement basis effective October 16, 2025. The instrument comprises 2,000 units with a face value of Rs.5,00,000 each, issued at Rs.493,729 with redemption scheduled for December 30, 2025. The securities carry an IND A1+ credit rating and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- 2,000 units of Commercial Paper listed with scrip code 730403 and scrip ID KPIL151025
- Face value: Rs.5,00,000 per unit; Issue price: Rs.493,729 per unit
- ISIN Number: INE220B14BM0
- Credit Rating: IND A1+ 
- Allotment date: October 15, 2025
- Redemption date: December 30, 2025
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Ltd
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities on BSE Debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE220B14BM0
- Trading shall be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: October 15, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market-wide impact as it pertains to a privately placed short-term debt instrument with a maturity of approximately 2.5 months. The commercial paper provides short-term financing options for Kalpataru Projects International Limited and offers investment opportunities for institutional investors in the debt segment. The IND A1+ rating indicates high credit quality. Trading will be restricted to institutional participants given the Rs.5 lakh minimum denomination requirement.
