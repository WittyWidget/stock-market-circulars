## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 46,40,000 new equity shares of MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED (Scrip Code: 542774) effective from Monday, October 13, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 54/- per share (face value Rs. 1/-) to Promoter and Non-Promoter on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to lock-in requirements.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 46,40,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 55/- per share (including premium of Rs. 54/-)
- Date of allotment: June 26, 2025
- Trading commencement: October 13, 2025
- Distinctive numbers: 168315173 to 172955172
- ISIN: INE08KJ01020
- Issued on preferential basis to Promoter and Non-Promoter
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of new securities
- Lock-in provisions must be adhered to for the specified shares:
  - 27,50,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 168315173-171065172): Lock-in until April 15, 2027
  - 18,90,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 171065173-172955172): Lock-in until April 15, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: June 26, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 13, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (Partial)**: April 15, 2026 (for 18,90,000 shares)
- **Lock-in Expiry (Remaining)**: April 15, 2027 (for 27,50,000 shares)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine listing of preferentially allotted shares for a single company. The share count represents a preferential allotment with lock-in provisions, limiting immediate trading activity.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the new securities and lock-in restrictions. The shares will be available for trading from October 13, 2025, subject to lock-in provisions.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to existing shareholders and investors in MUFIN GREEN FINANCE LIMITED. The lock-in provisions ensure that a significant portion of these shares (27,50,000 shares) will not be available for trading until April 2027.
