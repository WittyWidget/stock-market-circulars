## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by InCred Financial Services Limited on private placement basis, effective October 13, 2025. The securities are market-linked debentures tied to NIFTY 50 INDEX with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit, maturing on December 8, 2027. The securities carry a CRISIL PP-MLD AA-/Stable rating and will trade in dematerialized form only on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- New debt security listed: IFSL-NIFTY 50 INDEX-08-12-27-PVT (Scrip Code: 977202)
- ISIN: INE321N07574
- Total quantity: 2,500 units with market lot size of 1
- Face value and issue price: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Date of allotment: October 8, 2025
- Credit rating: CRISIL PP-MLD AA-/Stable
- Interest type: Market-linked (cumulative)
- Redemption date: December 8, 2027
- No put/call options available
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE321N07574
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 8, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 13, 2025
- **Interest Payment Date**: December 8, 2027 (cumulative)
- **Redemption Date**: December 8, 2027
