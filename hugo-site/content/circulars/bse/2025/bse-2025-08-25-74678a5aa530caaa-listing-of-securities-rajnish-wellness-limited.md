## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 246,873,025 new equity shares of Rajnish Wellness Limited (Scrip Code: 541601) issued through rights offering. The shares will commence trading on August 26, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 246,873,025 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued on rights basis
- ISIN: INE685Z01033
- Distinctive Numbers: 768474661 to 1015347685
- Allotment Date: August 20, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 26, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities availability for trading from the specified date.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 20, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 26, 2025

Limited market impact as this is a routine listing of rights issue shares. The substantial number of new shares may affect liquidity and trading volumes for Rajnish Wellness Limited specifically.
