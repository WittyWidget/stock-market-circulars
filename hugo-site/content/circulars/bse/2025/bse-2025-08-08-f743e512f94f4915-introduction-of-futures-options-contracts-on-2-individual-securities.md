## Summary

BSE announces the introduction of futures and options contracts for two individual securities - Nuvama Wealth Management Ltd and Suzlon Energy Ltd. These derivative contracts will be available for trading in the Equity Derivatives segment starting August 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two new underlying securities added for derivatives trading
- Contracts available in both futures and options format
- Trading to commence on August 18, 2025
- Contract details will be available in derivatives master file by August 14, 2025
- Master file accessible through BSE extranet and website

## Securities Details

- **Nuvama Wealth Management Ltd** (Scrip Code: 543988)
- **Suzlon Energy Ltd** (Scrip Code: 532667)

## Important Dates

- **August 14, 2025**: Contract master file generation with detailed specifications
- **August 18, 2025**: Trading commencement date for both securities

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new contract availability
- Members should review contract specifications from the master file
- Standard derivatives trading rules and procedures apply

## Impact Assessment

The introduction of derivatives on these securities will provide:
- Enhanced hedging opportunities for investors
- Increased liquidity and price discovery
- Additional trading strategies for market participants
- Expanded derivative product offerings in BSE's equity derivatives segment
