## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Vivriti Capital Limited on its debt segment effective July 28, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each, totaling Rs. 10 crores.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total quantity: 200 securities (Rs. 10 crores total)
- Scrip Code: 729914, Scrip ID: VCL250725
- ISIN Number: INE01HV14888
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,84,957.50 per security
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Securities will be traded under ISIN INE01HV14888

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 25, 2025
- Listing Date: July 28, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The securities provide short-term funding option for Vivriti Capital Limited with 4-month maturity period. Trading members gain access to CRISIL A1+ rated commercial paper for debt portfolio diversification.
