## Summary

BSE has announced the availability of the Draft Offer Document for MSAFE EQUIPMENTS LIMITED's SME IPO, filed on September 5, 2025. The document is hosted on BSE SME website for a mandatory 21-day public comment period as per ICDR Regulations.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by MSAFE EQUIPMENTS LIMITED on September 5, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days for public comments
- Comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. Standard ICDR compliance process being followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public can submit comments on the Draft Offer Document within 21 days from notice date
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document available at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document filing date: September 5, 2025
- Notice date: September 8, 2025
- Comment period: 21 days from September 8, 2025 (until September 29, 2025)

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this is a standard SME IPO procedural announcement. Affects only potential investors and stakeholders interested in MSAFE EQUIPMENTS LIMITED. No broader market implications.
