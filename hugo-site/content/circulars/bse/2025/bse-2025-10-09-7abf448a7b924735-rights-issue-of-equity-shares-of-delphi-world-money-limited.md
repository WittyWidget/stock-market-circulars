## Summary

BSE has announced that Delphi World Money Limited (Scrip Code: 533452) has fixed the record date for its rights issue of equity shares. The company will issue 23 equity shares of Rs 10/- each at a premium of Rs 181/- per share for every 49 equity shares held. Trading will be conducted on an ex-rights basis from October 14, 2025, under settlement number DR-735/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Delphi World Money Limited (Scrip Code: 533452)
- Rights ratio: 23 new equity shares for every 49 shares held
- Face value: Rs 10/- per share
- Premium: Rs 181/- per share
- Total issue price: Rs 191/- per share
- Payment terms: Entire issue price payable at the time of application
- Segment: Dematerialized Securities - Rolling Settlement

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action announcement following existing BSE procedures for rights issues.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must execute all transactions in Delphi World Money Limited shares on an ex-rights basis from the specified date
- Trading members are required to take note of the record date and ex-rights date for proper settlement processing
- Investors applying for rights shares must pay the entire issue price of Rs 191/- per share at the time of application

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: October 14, 2025
- **Ex-Rights Date**: October 14, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-735/2025-2026
- **Notice Date**: October 9, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This rights issue will impact existing shareholders of Delphi World Money Limited who hold shares as of the record date. Shareholders will have the opportunity to subscribe to new shares at a ratio of 23:49, requiring payment of Rs 191 per share. The ex-rights trading from October 14, 2025, means the stock will trade without the rights entitlement from that date. Trading members must adjust their systems to handle ex-rights transactions accordingly. The dilution impact depends on the level of subscription by existing shareholders.
