## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Equity Derivatives segment on Saturday, October 25th, 2025. This session is designed for trading members to test their trading applications, including third-party platforms and in-house systems using ETI APIs. The mock session will allow testing of various functionalities including exceptional market conditions, risk-reduction mode, and trading halts. No new BOLT TWS release is planned. All trades from this mock session will not attract any obligations or create rights and liabilities.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, October 25th, 2025
- Applicable to Equity Derivatives segment only
- No new BOLT TWS release planned
- Login window: 1:15 PM to 2:15 PM
- Continuous trading: 2:15 PM to 5:00 PM
- Trade modification: 5:00 PM to 5:15 PM
- Testing covers exceptional market conditions, risk-reduction mode, and trading halts
- Mock trades will not create any obligations, rights, or liabilities
- No margin, pay-in, or pay-out obligations from mock trades
- Feedback requested by 6:00 PM on the same day

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice for a testing session.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are encouraged to participate actively in the mock trading session
- Members using third-party trading platforms or in-house developed systems through ETI APIs should utilize this opportunity for testing
- Feedback must be submitted by 6:00 PM on October 25th, 2025
- Contact BSE Helpdesk (022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500; bsehelp@bseindia.com) or BSE Tech Support (022-22728053; bse.tech@bseindia.com) for queries
- Relationship Managers available for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **October 25th, 2025**: Mock trading session date
  - 1:15 PM - 2:15 PM: Login window
  - 2:15 PM - 5:00 PM: Continuous trading session
  - 5:00 PM - 5:15 PM: Trade modification window
  - By 6:00 PM: Feedback submission deadline

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: None. This is a mock trading session with no real market transactions.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Optional testing session for trading members to familiarize themselves with trading systems and test various functionalities. Particularly useful for members using third-party platforms or in-house systems to ensure their trading applications work correctly under different market conditions including exceptional scenarios like risk-reduction mode and trading halts.

**Member Participation**: Voluntary but encouraged for all trading members to ensure system readiness and identify any potential issues in a controlled environment before actual trading.
