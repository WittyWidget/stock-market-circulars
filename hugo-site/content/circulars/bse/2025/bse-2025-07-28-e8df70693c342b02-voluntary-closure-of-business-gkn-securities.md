## Summary

GKN Securities (Member No. 6507) has voluntarily decided to cease business operations across all trading segments including Cash, Equity derivatives, and Currency derivatives, effective from Tuesday, July 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading member GKN Securities (Member No. 6507) voluntarily closing business
- Cessation applies to Cash, Equity derivative and Currency derivative segments
- Effective date: Tuesday, July 29, 2025
- Reason: Voluntary closure of business
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a business decision by the trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the closure for awareness and operational adjustments if needed.

## Important Dates

- **July 29, 2025**: Effective date for cessation of business operations

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a single trading member's voluntary business closure. Other market participants may need to adjust their operations if they had business relationships with GKN Securities.
