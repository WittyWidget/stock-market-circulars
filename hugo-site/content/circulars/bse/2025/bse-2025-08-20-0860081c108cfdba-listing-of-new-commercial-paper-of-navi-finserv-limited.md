## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Navi Finserv Limited on private placement basis. The securities are worth Rs. 5 lakh each with 1000 units totaling Rs. 50 crores, carrying CARE A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 1000 units
- Scrip Code: 730047
- ISIN: INE342T14EA3
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- Issue price: Rs. 453,630.50 per unit
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities to be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size set at 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 19, 2025
- Listing Date: August 20, 2025
- Redemption Date: August 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding avenue for Navi Finserv Limited and offers investment option for institutional investors in the debt segment.
