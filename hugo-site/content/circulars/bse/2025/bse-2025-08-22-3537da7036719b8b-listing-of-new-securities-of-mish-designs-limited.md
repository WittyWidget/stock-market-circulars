## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 1,08,000 new equity shares of Mish Designs Limited (Scrip Code: 544015) effective from Monday, August 25, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis to non-promoters.

## Key Points

- 1,08,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at Rs. 140/- premium
- Issue price: Rs. 150/- per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 2967001 to 3075000
- ISIN: INE0QI101019

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed about the new securities listing and trading permissions.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: January 16, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 25, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: February 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this represents a small preferential issue for warrant conversion. The lock-in period until February 2026 ensures these shares cannot be immediately traded freely.
