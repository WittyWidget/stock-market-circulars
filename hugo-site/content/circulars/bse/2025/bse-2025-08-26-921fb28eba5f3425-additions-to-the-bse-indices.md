## Summary

BSE announces the addition of five newly listed companies to its indices. Four companies (Patel Retail Limited, Vikram Solar Limited, Shreeji Shipping Global Limited, and Gem Aromatics Limited) will be added to the BSE IPO index effective August 29, 2025. Additionally, LGT Business Connextions Limited will be added to the BSE SME IPO index effective August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Four companies being added to BSE IPO index with effective date August 29, 2025
- One company being added to BSE SME IPO index with effective date August 28, 2025
- All companies recently listed on BSE on August 26, 2025
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Index queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced - this is a routine index maintenance activity following new company listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. This is an informational notice about index composition changes.

## Important Dates

- **August 26, 2025**: Listing date for all five companies on BSE
- **August 28, 2025**: LGT Business Connextions Limited added to BSE SME IPO index
- **August 29, 2025**: Four companies added to BSE IPO index (Patel Retail, Vikram Solar, Shreeji Shipping Global, Gem Aromatics)

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on index composition and potential passive investment flows. Index additions typically result in buying interest from index funds and ETFs tracking these indices. The staggered effective dates (August 28 and 29) allow for orderly market adjustments. This is routine market operations activity that maintains index relevance and accuracy.
