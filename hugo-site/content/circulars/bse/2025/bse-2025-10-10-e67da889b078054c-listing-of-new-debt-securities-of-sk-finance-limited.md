## Summary

SK Finance Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE's Debt segment on a private placement basis, effective October 10, 2025. The securities carry a 9.10% p.a. interest rate with annual interest payments and mature on October 9, 2028. A total of 25,000 securities with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each have been listed under ISIN INE124N07770.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 977195
- **Scrip ID**: 910SKFL28
- **Detail Name**: SKFL-9.10%-09-10-28-PVT
- **ISIN**: INE124N07770
- **Quantity Listed**: 25,000 securities
- **Face Value**: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Credit Rating**: ICRA AA-/STABLE
- **Interest Rate**: 9.10% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Annually
- **Date of Allotment**: October 9, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: October 9, 2028
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Applicable
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE124N07770
- Securities will be traded with a tick size of 1 paise
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: October 10, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: October 9, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: October 9, 2028
- **Interest Payment Frequency**: Annually

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities with limited market-wide impact. The listing provides debt market participants access to SK Finance Limited's securities rated ICRA AA-/STABLE. The 9.10% annual interest rate reflects current market conditions for similar-rated corporate debt. Impact is confined to debt market participants and investors interested in SK Finance Limited's securities.
