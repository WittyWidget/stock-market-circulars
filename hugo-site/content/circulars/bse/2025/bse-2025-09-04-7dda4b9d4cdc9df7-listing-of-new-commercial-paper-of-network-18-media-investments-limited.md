## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 300 crore issued by Network 18 Media & Investments Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to dealings on BSE Debt segment effective September 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- 6,000 units of Commercial Paper at Rs. 5 lakh each (total Rs. 300 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,727.50 per unit (face value Rs. 5,00,000)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- Maturity period: 91 days (September 4 to December 4, 2025)
- Scrip Code: 730145, ISIN: INE870H14VW6
- ICICI Bank Ltd serves as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise for trading
- Market lot size of 1 unit

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 4, 2025
- Trading Commencement: September 4, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. Provides short-term funding avenue for Network 18 Media & Investments Limited and investment option for institutional investors in high-rated commercial paper.
