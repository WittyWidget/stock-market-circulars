## Summary

BSE has announced revised price bands for 68 equity securities, effective from August 18, 2025. The price bands have been adjusted to various levels ranging from 2% to 20%, representing changes from their existing levels to enhance market surveillance and trading control.

## Key Points

- 68 securities will have revised price bands effective August 18, 2025
- Price band revisions range from 2% to 20%
- Changes include both increases and decreases from existing levels
- Notice applies to all trading members of BSE
- Surveillance measure to control volatility and unusual price movements

## Regulatory Changes

- Modified price band structure for listed securities
- Enhanced surveillance measures through adjusted trading bands
- Updated trading parameters for affected scrips

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must implement revised price bands from August 18, 2025
- All trading systems must be updated to reflect new price band limits
- Members can contact bse.surv@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 14, 2025
- **Effective Date**: August 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

- Trading activity may be restricted within new price band limits
- Potential reduction in volatility for securities with tighter bands
- Enhanced market stability through improved surveillance measures
- Investors and traders need to adjust strategies based on new band limits
- Market makers and algorithmic traders must update their systems accordingly
