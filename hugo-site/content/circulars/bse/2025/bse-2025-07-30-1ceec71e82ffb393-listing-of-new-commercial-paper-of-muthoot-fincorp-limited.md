## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of MUTHOOT FINCORP LIMITED worth Rs. 150 crore issued on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to dealings on BSE Debt segment effective July 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (3000 units)
- Total issue size: Rs. 150 crore
- CRISIL A1+ credit rating
- Issue price: Rs. 4,65,450/- per unit
- Scrip Code: 729931, ISIN: INE549K14CB4
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 29, 2025
- Listing Date: July 30, 2025
- Redemption Date: June 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt instrument option for institutional investors. INDUSIND BANK LTD serves as Issuing and Paying agent.
