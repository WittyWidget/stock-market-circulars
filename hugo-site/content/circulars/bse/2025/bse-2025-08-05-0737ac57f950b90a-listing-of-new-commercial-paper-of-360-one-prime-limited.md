## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of 360 ONE Prime Limited on the debt segment effective August 5, 2025. The listing includes two series of commercial papers totaling Rs. 35 crores, issued on private placement basis with maturity dates in February 2026.

## Key Points

- Two series of commercial papers listed with face value of Rs. 5 lakh each
- Series 1: 3,000 units (Rs. 15 crores) maturing February 13, 2026
- Series 2: 4,000 units (Rs. 20 crores) maturing February 20, 2026
- Both series carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings
- Securities allotted on August 4, 2025
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent for Series 2

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Contact debt department for clarifications on specified phone numbers

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: August 5, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: August 4, 2025
- **Redemption Date (Series 1)**: February 13, 2026
- **Redemption Date (Series 2)**: February 20, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The securities provide short-term funding options for 360 ONE Prime Limited and investment opportunities for institutional investors in the debt segment.
