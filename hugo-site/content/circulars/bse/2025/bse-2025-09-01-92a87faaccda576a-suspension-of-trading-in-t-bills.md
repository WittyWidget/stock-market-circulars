## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in three Treasury Bills effective September 2, 2025, as these instruments will mature on their redemption date. Trading members are advised to cease dealing in the specified T-Bills from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills will mature and trading will be suspended from September 2, 2025
- SCRIP CODE 805035 (ISIN: IN002024Y472) - 182TB40925
- SCRIP CODE 804964 (ISIN: IN002024Z230) - 364TB04925  
- SCRIP CODE 805073 (ISIN: IN002025X109) - 91TB040925
- Trading members must not deal in these T-Bills from the effective date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes - this is a routine operational measure for maturing debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all dealing activities in the specified T-Bills from September 2, 2025
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure compliance
- No transactions should be executed in these instruments post-suspension date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: September 1, 2025
- **Effective Date**: September 2, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: September 2, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine suspension due to instrument maturity. Only affects holders and traders of the specific T-Bills mentioned. No broader market implications expected as this is standard procedure for maturing Treasury Bills.
