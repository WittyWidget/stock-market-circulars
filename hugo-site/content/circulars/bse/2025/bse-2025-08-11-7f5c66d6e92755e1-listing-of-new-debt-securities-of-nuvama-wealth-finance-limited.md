## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective August 11, 2025. The securities will trade in dematerialised form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- New debt securities of Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited listed on BSE Debt segment
- Securities issued on private placement basis
- Trading effective from August 11, 2025
- Quantity: 100 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 976954, Scrip ID: NWFL24725
- ISIN: INE918K07PX1
- Credit Rating: CARE PP-MLD AA/ Stable
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000
- Issue Price: Rs. 96,279

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note securities will trade only in dematerialised form
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Trading members should refer to Placement Memorandum for further details

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 6, 2025
- Trading Effective Date: August 11, 2025
- Date of Redemption: January 22, 2029
- Rate of Interest: As per Information Memorandum

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing. The listing provides additional investment options in the debt segment but is unlikely to significantly affect broader market operations.
