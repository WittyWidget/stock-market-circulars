## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 96,100,000 new equity shares of Deep Diamond India Limited (Scrip Code: 539559) issued through a rights issue. The shares will be available for trading from October 10, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 96,100,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 3.16/-
- Total issue price: Rs. 4.16 per share
- Scrip Code: 539559
- ISIN: INE005G01026
- Distinctive Numbers: 48050001 to 144150000
- Date of Allotment: October 6, 2025
- Trading Start Date: October 10, 2025
- New shares rank pari-passu with old equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities
- The shares are available for trading in the equity segment from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 6, 2025
- **Trading Start Date**: October 10, 2025 (Friday)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification following a rights issue by Deep Diamond India Limited. The addition of 96.1 million shares will increase the company's equity capital base. Impact is limited to shareholders and traders of this specific security, with no broader market implications.
