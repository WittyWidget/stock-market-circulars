## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 29,39,706 new equity shares of EPUJA SPIRITECH LIMITED (Scrip Code: 532092) effective from September 10, 2025. These shares were issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion at Rs. 3.40 per share.

## Key Points

- 29,39,706 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at premium of Rs. 2.40/-
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis through warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 78639334 to 81579039
- ISIN: INE807D01030

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. Standard new listing procedure for warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all issued shares
- Standard listing compliance requirements applicable

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 10, 2024
- **Trading Commencement**: September 10, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: March 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Moderate impact on EPUJA SPIRITECH LIMITED's share capital structure. The addition of nearly 30 lakh shares increases available equity base. Lock-in period until March 2026 limits immediate trading impact. Issue price of Rs. 3.40 provides reference point for market valuation.
