## Summary

BSE announces the listing of new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 200 crore issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis, effective August 22, 2025. The securities will trade on BSE's debt segment in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- 4,000 Commercial Papers of Rs. 5 lakh each listed (total Rs. 200 crore)
- Scrip Code: 730072, Scrip ID: BFSL210825
- ISIN: INE01C314CK0
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,171.50 per unit
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Trading tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading permitted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 21, 2025
- Listing Date: August 22, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 20, 2025
- Tenor: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt investment option for institutional investors. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates strong creditworthiness of the issuer.
