## Summary

BSE has listed three new commercial paper securities of Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis, totaling Rs. 40 crores (8,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each). All securities are rated CRISIL A1+ and will trade in dematerialized form with ICICI Bank Limited as the issuing and paying agent.

## Key Points

- Three commercial paper series listed with face value Rs. 5 lakh each
- Total issue size: Rs. 40 crores across 8,000 units
- All securities carry CRISIL A1+ rating
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as issuing and paying agent

## Securities Details

**Series 1 (BFSL-25-09-25-CP):**
- Quantity: 6,000 units
- Issue Price: Rs. 498,220
- Allotment Date: 04/09/2025
- Redemption Date: 25/09/2025
- ISIN: INE01C314CM6
- Scrip Code: 730138

**Series 2 (BFSL-22-09-25-CP):**
- Quantity: 1,000 units
- Issue Price: Rs. 498,381
- Allotment Date: 03/09/2025
- Redemption Date: 22/09/2025
- ISIN: INE01C314CE3

**Series 3 (BFSL-29-09-25-CP):**
- Quantity: 1,000 units
- Issue Price: Rs. 497,787.50
- Allotment Date: 03/09/2025
- Redemption Date: 29/09/2025
- ISIN: INE01C314CN4

## Trading Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: 04/09/2025 (effective today)
- Redemption Dates: 22/09/2025, 25/09/2025, 29/09/2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding avenue for Bajaj Financial Securities Limited with maturities ranging from 18-25 days.
