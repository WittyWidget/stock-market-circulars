## Summary

BSE has listed two new commercial paper issues by Redington Limited on private placement basis, each worth Rs. 400 crores (face value Rs. 5 lakh per unit). The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from August 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two commercial paper issues listed: RL-7-10-25-CP and RL-4-11-25-CP
- Each issue comprises 4,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh face value
- Both issues carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings
- HDFC Bank Ltd serves as issuing and paying agent
- Trading only in dematerialized form with minimum denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs
- Tick size set at 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Securities to be traded under specified ISIN numbers

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: August 8, 2025
- **First Issue Allotment**: August 8, 2025
- **First Issue Redemption**: October 7, 2025
- **Second Issue Allotment**: August 7, 2025
- **Second Issue Redemption**: November 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term financing needs. The listings provide additional debt market liquidity and financing options for institutional investors in high-rated commercial paper.
