## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Ambium Finserve Limited on its debt segment effective September 10, 2025. The securities worth Rs 20 crore were issued on private placement basis with an interest rate of 11.16% per annum.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 20,000 securities with face value of Rs 10,000 each
- Scrip Code: 977092, Scrip ID: 1116AFL26
- ISIN Number: INE0RU307205
- Credit Rating: CARE BBB-(Stable)
- Issue Price: Rs 10,000 per security
- Interest Rate: 11.16% per annum (monthly payment)
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Trading members can contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: September 9, 2025
- Effective Trading Date: September 10, 2025
- Interest Payment Period: October 12, 2025 to December 12, 2026 (monthly)
- Date of Redemption: December 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing. The securities provide investment opportunity in corporate bonds with BBB- rating and monthly interest payments over 15-month tenure.
