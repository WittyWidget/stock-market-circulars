## Summary

BSE announces the availability of Draft Offer Document for Stanbik Agro Limited's SME IPO filed on September 05, 2025. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available for public comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Stanbik Agro Limited on September 05, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments invited within 21-day period
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. Standard IPO process being followed as per existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public has 21 days from notice date to submit comments on the Draft Offer Document
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Company must maintain document availability for at least 21 days on exchange website

## Important Dates

- Draft filing date: September 05, 2025
- Notice date: September 08, 2025
- Comment period deadline: September 29, 2025 (21 days from notice)

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact for SME segment investors and market participants. Standard IPO process with opportunity for public input on company's draft offer document before finalization.
