## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of two newly listed companies to the BSE SME IPO Index. GLOBTIER INFOTECH LIMITED (544494) and NIS MANAGEMENT LIMITED (544495), which were listed on the SME platform on September 2, 2025, will be added to the index effective at market open on September 3, 2025.

## Key Points

- GLOBTIER INFOTECH LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544494) added to BSE SME IPO Index
- NIS MANAGEMENT LIMITED (Exchange ticker: 544495) added to BSE SME IPO Index
- Both companies listed on SME platform on September 2, 2025
- Index addition effective at market open on September 3, 2025
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine index composition update following the listing of new companies on the BSE SME platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Index tracking funds and portfolios benchmarked to the BSE SME IPO Index will need to adjust their holdings to reflect the addition of these two securities.

## Important Dates

- **September 2, 2025**: GLOBTIER INFOTECH LIMITED and NIS MANAGEMENT LIMITED listed on BSE SME platform
- **September 3, 2025**: Both stocks added to BSE SME IPO Index (effective at market open)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact expected as this is a routine addition to the SME IPO Index. The addition will primarily affect index-tracking funds and investors following the BSE SME IPO Index. The corporate action has been communicated through the standard end-of-day file distribution to ensure market participants can make necessary adjustments.
