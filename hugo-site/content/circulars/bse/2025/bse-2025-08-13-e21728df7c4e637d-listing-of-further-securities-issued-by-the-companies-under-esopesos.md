## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of further securities issued by 12 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS), effective from Thursday, August 14, 2025. All listed shares have no lock-in period restrictions.

## Key Points

- 12 companies are listing additional shares under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Total of approximately 1.3 million shares being listed across all companies
- All shares are admitted for trading with immediate effect from August 14, 2025
- No lock-in period applicable for any of the listed securities
- Face values range from ₹1 to ₹10 per share

## Companies and Share Details

- **Aditya Birla Capital Ltd**: 673,752 shares (₹10 face value)
- **APL Apollo Tubes Limited**: 111,462 shares (₹2 face value)
- **Borosil Scientific Limited**: 1,122 shares (₹1 face value)
- **Cipla Ltd**: 2,853 shares (₹2 face value)
- **Delhivery Limited**: 127,201 shares (₹1 face value)
- **Federal Bank Ltd**: 206,736 shares (₹2 face value)
- **One 97 Communications Limited (Paytm)**: 189,104 shares (₹1 face value)
- **Sai Life Sciences Limited**: 19,000 shares (₹1 face value)
- **Shoppers Stop Ltd**: 53,024 shares (₹5 face value)
- **Tata Consumer Products Limited**: 60 shares (₹1 face value)
- **UTI Asset Management Company Limited**: 13,307 shares (₹10 face value)
- **V-Mart Retail Limited**: 13,328 shares (₹10 face value)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading from August 14, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 14, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine ESOP share listings with relatively small quantities. The absence of lock-in periods allows immediate trading flexibility for employees exercising their options.
