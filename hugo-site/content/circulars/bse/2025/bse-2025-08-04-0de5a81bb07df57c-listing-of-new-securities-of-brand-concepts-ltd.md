## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 12,04,077 new equity shares of Brand Concepts Ltd. (Scrip Code: 543442) pursuant to a scheme of amalgamation with IFF Overseas Private Limited. The shares will be available for trading from August 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- 12,04,077 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each being listed
- Shares issued under scheme of amalgamation between IFF Overseas Private Limited (Transferor) and Brand Concepts Limited (Transferee)
- ISIN: INE977Y01011
- Distribution Numbers: 11217801 - 12421877
- Date of allotment: June 21, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and trading permissions.

## Important Dates

- **Trading Start Date**: August 5, 2025 (Tuesday)
- **Allotment Date**: June 21, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The listing increases Brand Concepts Ltd.'s share capital and trading volume. The amalgamation with IFF Overseas Private Limited expands the company's operational scope and may affect share price and trading patterns.
