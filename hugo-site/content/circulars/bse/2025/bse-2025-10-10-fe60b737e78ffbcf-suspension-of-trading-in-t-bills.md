## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in Treasury Bill GS15OCT25C (ISIN: IN001025C054, Scrip Code: 800648) with effect from October 13, 2025, as the security is scheduled to mature on its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this T-bill from the specified date.

## Key Points

- T-bill GS15OCT25C to be suspended from trading effective October 13, 2025
- Scrip Code: 800648
- ISIN: IN001025C054
- Reason: Maturity on redemption date
- Notice issued under reference DR-734/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for securities reaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified T-bill from October 13, 2025 onwards
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and ensure no trades are executed in this security after the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 10, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date:** October 13, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** October 15, 2025 (implied from T-bill identifier GS15OCT25C)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a routine suspension affecting a single Treasury Bill approaching maturity. T-bills are typically held until maturity by institutional investors, so trading suspension has minimal market disruption.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Standard procedure for debt securities reaching maturity. Trading members regularly handle such suspensions as part of normal operations.

**Investor Impact:** Minimal - Investors holding this T-bill will receive redemption proceeds on the maturity date as scheduled. No action required from investors.
