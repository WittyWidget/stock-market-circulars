## Summary

LLOYDS ENTERPRISES LIMITED (BSE Code: 512463) has announced a rights issue offering 1 equity share of Re. 1/- each for every 5 equity shares held by existing shareholders. The issue price is Rs. 39 per share (Re. 1/- face value + Rs. 38/- premium) payable in installments.

## Key Points

- Rights ratio: 1 new share for every 5 existing shares held
- Issue price: Rs. 39 per share (Re. 1/- + Rs. 38/- premium)
- Payment structure: Rs. 19.50 on application, Rs. 19.50 on calls (maximum 2 calls)
- Record date: August 14, 2025
- Ex-rights trading begins: August 14, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must execute transactions on ex-rights basis from August 14, 2025
- All equity share transactions must reflect the rights entitlement adjustment
- Settlement number: DR-695/2025-2026

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: August 14, 2025
- **Ex-Rights Trading Date**: August 14, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-695/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

This rights issue will dilute existing shareholding for non-participating shareholders while providing existing shareholders an opportunity to maintain their proportional stake. The staggered payment structure (application money and calls) provides flexibility for shareholders. Trading will be adjusted to reflect rights entitlements from the ex-date.
