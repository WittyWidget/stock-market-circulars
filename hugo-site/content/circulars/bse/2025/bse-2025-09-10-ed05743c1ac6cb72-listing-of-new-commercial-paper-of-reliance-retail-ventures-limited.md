## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 17,000 crore issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on private placement basis. The instruments are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each (34,000 units)
- Total issue size: Rs. 17,000 crore
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,794.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Scrip code: 730168, ISIN: INE929O14EB6
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 9, 2025
- Listing Date: September 10, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 8, 2025
- Maturity Period: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt instrument option for institutional investors in BSE debt segment.
