## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debt securities of Setco Auto Systems Private Limited effective September 04, 2025, due to the securities reaching their maturity date of September 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- Debt securities with ISIN INE0I0R07029 (SASPL-5%-7-9-25-PVT) will mature on September 7, 2025
- Trading suspension effective from September 04, 2025
- Scrip code: 975859
- Notice reference: DR-708/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine operational suspension due to security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debt securities from September 04, 2025
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and comply accordingly

## Important Dates

- **September 04, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **September 07, 2025**: Debt security maturity date
- **September 03, 2025**: Notice date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only specific debt securities of a private limited company. Routine operational matter with no broader market implications.
