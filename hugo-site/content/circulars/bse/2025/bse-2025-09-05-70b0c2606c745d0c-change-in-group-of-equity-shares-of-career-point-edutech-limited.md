## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Career Point Edutech Limited (Scrip Code: 544499) will be permitted to trade under Group 'B' classification effective Friday, September 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Career Point Edutech Limited
- Scrip Code: 544499
- Current trading group classification will change to Group 'B'
- Change effective from September 19, 2025
- Notice issued by Deputy Vice President Ashok Kumar Singh

## Regulatory Changes

The stock will move to Group 'B' trading classification, which typically indicates stocks that meet certain criteria for liquidity, market capitalization, or other regulatory parameters as defined by BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of this change and must ensure their systems and processes are updated to reflect the new group classification for Career Point Edutech Limited shares.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: Friday, September 19, 2025
- Notice Date: September 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The change in group classification may affect trading parameters, margin requirements, and other operational aspects for this security. Trading members and investors should note this change for their trading and settlement processes.
