## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 100 crores issued by Vivriti Capital Limited on private placement basis. The securities carry CRISIL A1+ rating and will mature on November 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 200 units (Rs. 100 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,88,848 per unit
- ISIN: INE01HV14920
- Scrip Code: 730059
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: The Federal Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialised form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size of 1 paise for trading

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 20, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 21, 2025
- Redemption date: November 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding option for Vivriti Capital and investment opportunity for institutional investors in debt segment.
