## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document for Mother Nutri Foods Limited's SME IPO for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will remain available on the exchange website for a minimum of 21 days.

## Key Points

- Mother Nutri Foods Limited filed Draft Offer Document on September 8, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for public review
- 21-day comment period from notice date
- Contains all mandatory disclosures per ICDR Regulations Schedule VI
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. Standard SME IPO process following existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public can submit comments within 21 days from September 8, 2025
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Company must maintain document availability on exchange website for minimum 21 days

## Important Dates

- Filing Date: September 8, 2025
- Comment Period: 21 days from September 8, 2025
- Comment Deadline: September 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact on broader market operations. Primarily affects potential investors in Mother Nutri Foods Limited SME IPO. Standard regulatory process with no changes to existing market structure or trading mechanisms.
