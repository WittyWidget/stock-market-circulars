## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted for trading further securities issued by 19 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be available for trading from Monday, September 8, 2025. All listed shares have no lock-in period restrictions.

## Key Points

- 19 companies have issued further securities under ESOP/ESOS programs
- Total shares listed range from 2,384 (RateGain) to 2,309,228 (SBFC Finance)
- All securities admitted for trading effective September 8, 2025
- No lock-in period applicable for any of the listed securities
- Face values vary from Re 1 to Rs 10 per share depending on the company

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for ESOP/ESOS allotments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading
- Standard trading rules and regulations apply to these securities
- No special compliance requirements or restrictions

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: September 5, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: Monday, September 8, 2025
- **Lock-in Period**: None (NA for all companies)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The listing represents routine ESOP/ESOS allotments that cause minor dilution across the 19 companies. The share quantities are relatively small compared to existing outstanding shares for most companies.

**Liquidity Impact**: Negligible increase in tradable float. Most issuances are below 1% of typical daily trading volumes for these securities.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for employees receiving stock options. No material impact on existing shareholders given the small dilution percentages.
