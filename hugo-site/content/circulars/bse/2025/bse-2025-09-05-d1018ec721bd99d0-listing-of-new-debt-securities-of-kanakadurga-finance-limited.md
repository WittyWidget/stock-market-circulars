## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Kanakadurga Finance Limited issued on private placement basis, effective from September 5, 2025. The securities carry an 11.55% interest rate and will mature on December 5, 2026.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 20,000 securities with market lot size of 1
- Scrip Code: 977086, Scrip ID: 1155KFL26
- ISIN Number: INE104W07211

- Credit Rating: CARE BBB-/Stable
- Interest Rate: 11.55% per annum paid monthly
- Securities tradeable only in dematerialised form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialised form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum for further details

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: September 3, 2025
- Listing Date: September 5, 2025
- Date of Redemption: December 5, 2026
- Interest Payment: Monthly

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing of debt securities. The listing provides additional investment options in the debt segment for institutional and eligible investors.
