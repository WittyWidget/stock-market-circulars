## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Pahal Financial Services Private Limited issued on private placement basis. The debentures carry 12.25% interest rate with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each and mature on August 14, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2,900 debentures worth Rs. 29 crores
- Interest Rate: 12.25% per annum (half-yearly payment)
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture
- Credit Rating: ICRA BBB-
- ISIN: INE514Q07387
- Scrip Code: 977032
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Standard tick size of 1 paise applies

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 14, 2025
- Interest Payment Dates: Half-yearly from February 14, 2026 to August 14, 2028
- Maturity Date: August 14, 2028
- Trading Effective Date: August 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing. The securities add to BSE's debt segment offerings but represent a relatively small issuance size of Rs. 29 crores.
