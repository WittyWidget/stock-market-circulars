## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED on private placement basis. The instrument comprises 4,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh face value each, totaling Rs. 200 crores, with maturity on November 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 4,000 units (Rs. 200 crores aggregate)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,752 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip Code: 730042, ISIN: INE071G14HB6
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 19, 2025
- **Listing Date**: August 19, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding for ICICI Home Finance with high credit ratings, indicating low credit risk for investors.
