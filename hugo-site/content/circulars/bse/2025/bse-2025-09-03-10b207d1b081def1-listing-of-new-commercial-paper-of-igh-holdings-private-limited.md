## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of IGH Holdings Private Limited on its debt segment effective September 3, 2025. The issue comprises 7,000 securities of Rs. 5 lakh face value each, totaling Rs. 35 crores, issued on private placement basis.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 per security
- Total quantity: 7,000 securities (Rs. 35 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,785 per security
- Scrip code: 730132, ISIN: INE02FN14630
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Axis Bank Ltd acts as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Listing date: September 3, 2025
- Allotment date: September 3, 2025
- Redemption date: December 3, 2025
- Maturity period: 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk. Limited to institutional and high-net-worth investors due to high denomination requirements.
