## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of KOTAK MAHINDRA INVESTMENTS LIMITED (Code: KMIL-8.0415%-26-9-25-PVT, ISIN: INE975F07IL1) effective September 11, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for interest payment and redemption of debentures.

- ISIN Code: INE975F07IL1 (975110)

- Standard record date compliance procedures apply

- **No Dealings From**: September 11, 2025

Limited impact as suspension affects only specific debentures of KOTAK MAHINDRA INVESTMENTS LIMITED. Standard corporate action with clear timeline. Debenture holders will receive interest payment and redemption proceeds based on record date holdings. No broader market implications expected.
