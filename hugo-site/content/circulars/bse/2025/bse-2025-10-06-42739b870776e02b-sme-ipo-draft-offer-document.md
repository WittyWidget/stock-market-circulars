## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Defrail Technologies Limited on its SME platform for public review. The document was filed on September 29, 2025, and contains disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations. Stakeholders can submit comments within 21 days from October 6, 2025.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Defrail Technologies Limited on September 29, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Contains all disclosures specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments invited via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- The Draft Offer Document is hosted on www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Interested parties may submit comments within 21 days from the notice date (October 6, 2025)
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **September 29, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed
- **October 6, 2025**: Notice date - start of public comment period
- **October 27, 2025**: Last date for public comments (21 days from notice)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural announcement for an SME IPO process. Impact is limited to potential investors and stakeholders interested in Defrail Technologies Limited. No market-wide implications.
