## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issues by SBICAP Securities Limited on its Debt segment. The listing includes 5 separate tranches totaling 10,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh denomination each, all maturing on December 9, 2025.

## Key Points

- Total 5 tranches of Commercial Paper listed under ISIN INE212K14CB9
- Combined quantity: 10,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip Code: 730177, Scrip ID: SSL090925
- Issue prices range from Rs. 491,881.5 to Rs. 491,930 per unit
- State Bank of India serves as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size set at 1 paise
- Trading members must contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 9, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 9, 2025
- Trading Commencement: September 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding for SBICAP Securities with a 3-month maturity period. High credit ratings indicate low credit risk for investors.
