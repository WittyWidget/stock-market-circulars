## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 1,425 crore issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective August 14, 2025, with ICICI Bank Limited as the issuing and paying agent.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each, total quantity 28,500 units
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,727.50 per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ (highest short-term rating)
- Scrip code: 730029, ISIN: INE929O14DT0
- Allotment date: August 13, 2025
- Maturity date: November 12, 2025 (91 days tenure)
- Trading only in dematerialised form with minimum lot size of Rs. 5 lakhs

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialised form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Listing date: August 14, 2025
- Allotment date: August 13, 2025
- Redemption date: November 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. Provides additional short-term funding avenue for Reliance Retail Ventures Limited. The high credit ratings indicate strong creditworthiness, making it attractive for institutional investors seeking short-term investment options.
