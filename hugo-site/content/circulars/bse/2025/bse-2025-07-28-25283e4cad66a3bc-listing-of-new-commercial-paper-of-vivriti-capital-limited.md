## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Vivriti Capital Limited on private placement basis. The instruments are worth Rs. 5 lakh each with 200 units totaling Rs. 10 crores, carrying CRISIL A1+ rating and maturing in November 2025.

## Key Points

- 200 Commercial Paper units of Rs. 5,00,000 each listed on BSE Debt segment
- Issue price: Rs. 4,84,957.50 per unit (face value Rs. 5,00,000)
- ISIN: INE01HV14888, Scrip Code: 729914
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: The Federal Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise for trading

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 25, 2025
- Trading Effective Date: July 28, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding option for Vivriti Capital Limited and investment opportunity for institutional investors in highly-rated debt instruments.
