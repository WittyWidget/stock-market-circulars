## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in T-bill 182T101025 (Scrip Code: 805050, ISIN: IN002025Y024) effective October 08, 2025, as the security will mature on October 10, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in this T-bill from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- T-bill 182T101025 with Scrip Code 805050 and ISIN IN002025Y024 will be suspended from trading
- Trading suspension effective from October 08, 2025
- Maturity/Redemption date is October 10, 2025
- Notice issued under DR-731/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for maturing debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified T-bill from October 08, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance

## Important Dates

- **October 07, 2025**: Notice date
- **October 08, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **October 10, 2025**: T-bill maturity/redemption date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine maturity event affecting a single T-bill security. Trading members need to ensure they do not execute trades in this specific instrument from the suspension date. No broader implications for debt market operations.
