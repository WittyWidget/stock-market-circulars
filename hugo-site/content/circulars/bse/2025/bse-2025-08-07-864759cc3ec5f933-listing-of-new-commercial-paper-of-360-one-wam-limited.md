## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 200 crore issued by 360 ONE WAM LIMITED on private placement basis. The securities will trade on BSE Debt segment from August 7, 2025, with HDFC Bank Limited as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5 lakh denomination each (4,000 units)
- Total issue size: Rs. 200 crore
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,695 per unit (face value Rs. 5,00,000)
- Scrip Code: 729971, ISIN: INE466L14EQ3
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Securities to be traded under specified ISIN number

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 6, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 7, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 4, 2025
- Tenure: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The short-term debt instrument provides additional funding avenue for 360 ONE WAM LIMITED and offers investment option for institutional investors in the debt segment.
