## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 9,27,71,952 new equity shares of Easy Trip Planners Limited (Scrip Code: 543272) issued to Non Promoters on a preferential basis. The shares will be available for trading from August 28, 2025, and are subject to an 18-month lock-in period until February 28, 2026.

## Key Points

- 9,27,71,952 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at premium of Rs. 17.22/-
- Issue price: Rs. 18.22 per share
- Issued to Non Promoters on preferential basis
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 3544081237 to 3636853188
- ISIN: INE07O001026
- Date of allotment: April 12, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities for trading operations
- All shares subject to mandatory lock-in restrictions until February 28, 2026
- New shares carry same rights as existing equity shares

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: April 12, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 28, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: February 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This preferential allotment represents a significant increase in share capital for Easy Trip Planners Limited. The 18-month lock-in period provides market stability by preventing immediate selling pressure. The allotment to Non Promoters may impact promoter shareholding percentage and could influence stock liquidity once the lock-in period expires.
