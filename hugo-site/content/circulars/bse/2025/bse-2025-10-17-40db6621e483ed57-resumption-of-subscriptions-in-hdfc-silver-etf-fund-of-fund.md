## Summary

HDFC Mutual Fund has announced the resumption of all subscriptions in HDFC Silver ETF Fund of Fund effective October 17, 2025. This follows a temporary suspension that was communicated via addendum dated October 15, 2025. The decision to resume subscriptions was made considering current market conditions, and all restrictions on subscriptions have been lifted.

## Key Points

- All subscriptions in HDFC Silver ETF Fund of Fund resumed without restrictions from October 17, 2025
- Previous addendum dated October 15, 2025 had temporarily suspended subscriptions
- Decision based on assessment of current market conditions
- All other terms and conditions of the scheme remain unchanged
- Addendum forms integral part of the Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update by the Asset Management Company.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for investors or market participants. This is a standard disclosure required under mutual fund regulations regarding changes to scheme operations.

## Important Dates

- **October 15, 2025**: Previous addendum suspending subscriptions was issued
- **October 17, 2025**: Effective date for resumption of all subscriptions without restrictions
- **October 17, 2025**: Addendum date

## Impact Assessment

**Investor Impact**: Positive for investors who wish to invest in HDFC Silver ETF Fund of Fund, as they can now make fresh subscriptions without any restrictions. The brief suspension (2 days) suggests the AMC was monitoring market conditions, possibly related to silver price volatility.

**Market Impact**: Minimal market-wide impact. This affects only investors specifically interested in this fund of fund scheme that invests in silver ETFs. The quick resumption indicates normalized market conditions for the underlying asset class.

**Operational Impact**: Standard operational update with no changes to existing investments or scheme structure. All existing terms and conditions remain unchanged.
