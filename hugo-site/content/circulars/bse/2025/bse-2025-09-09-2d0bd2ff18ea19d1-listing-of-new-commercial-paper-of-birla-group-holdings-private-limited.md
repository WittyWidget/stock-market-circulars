## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 150 crore issued by Birla Group Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective September 9, 2025.

## Key Points

- Security: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5 lakh denomination each
- Total quantity: 3,000 units (Rs. 150 crore total)
- Scrip Code: 730164, ISIN: INE09OL14HV2
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,874 per unit (face value Rs. 5,00,000)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot size: 1 unit

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: September 9, 2025
- Trading commencement: September 9, 2025
- Redemption date: December 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The Commercial Paper provides short-term funding option for Birla Group Holdings with 90-day maturity period.
