## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 2,60,000 new equity shares of Uravi Defence and Technology Limited (Scrip Code: 543930) with trading commencing on August 25, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs.320/- to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 2,60,000 equity shares of Rs.10/- each listed
- Issue price: Rs.330/- (Rs.10/- face value + Rs.320/- premium)
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE568Z01015
- Trading effective from Monday, August 25, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and permitted trading from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Trading Start Date**: August 25, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: February 18, 19, and March 4, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: February 25-26, 2026 (various tranches)

## Impact Assessment

Moderate impact as this represents additional equity capital for Uravi Defence and Technology Limited through warrant conversion. The lock-in period until February 2026 provides some stability to the stock price by restricting immediate selling of these shares.
