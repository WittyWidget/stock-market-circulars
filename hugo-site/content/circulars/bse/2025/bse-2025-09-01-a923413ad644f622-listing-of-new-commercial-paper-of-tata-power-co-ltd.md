## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Tata Power Co. Ltd on private placement basis worth Rs. 5 crore (10,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each). The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective September 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper with face value Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 10,000 units (Rs. 5 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,794.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip code: 730119, ISIN: INE245A14KD9
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as issuing and paying agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 29, 2025
- Listing date: September 1, 2025
- Redemption date: November 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding option for Tata Power and investment opportunity for institutional investors in highly rated commercial paper.
