## Summary

BSE has announced that trading member ASHIKA STOCK BROKING LTD. (Member No. 912) will change its name to "ASHIKA STOCK SERVICES LIMITED" effective Monday, September 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading member name change from ASHIKA STOCK BROKING LTD. to ASHIKA STOCK SERVICES LIMITED
- Member number remains unchanged: 912
- Change is purely administrative with no impact on trading operations
- Contact available for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is an administrative name change only.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from other trading members. This is an informational notice.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Monday, September 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is purely an administrative name change. Trading operations and member number remain unchanged. Other market participants should update their records to reflect the new name.
