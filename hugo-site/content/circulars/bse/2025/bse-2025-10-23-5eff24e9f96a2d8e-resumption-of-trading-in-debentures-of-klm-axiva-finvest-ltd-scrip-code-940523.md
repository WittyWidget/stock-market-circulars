## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in debentures of KLM AXIVA FINVEST LTD (Scrip Code 940523) effective October 24, 2025. This follows a clarification from the company that the record date of October 17, 2025, previously communicated for redemption purposes, was actually intended for interest payment and not debenture redemption.

## Key Points

- Trading in KLM AXIVA FINVEST LTD debentures (Scrip Code 940523) will resume from October 24, 2025
- Previous notice dated October 13, 2025 (Notice No. 20251013-45) had indicated record date of October 17, 2025 for redemption
- Company has now clarified that the record date was for payment of interest, not redemption of debentures
- Company has requested resumption of trading following this clarification
- Reference: DR-742/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative correction to a previous corporate action notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are requested to note that the debentures will be available for trading from October 24, 2025
- No specific action required from trading members beyond awareness of the trading resumption

## Important Dates

- **October 13, 2025**: Original notice issued regarding record date for redemption
- **October 17, 2025**: Record date (clarified as for interest payment, not redemption)
- **October 23, 2025**: Circular date announcing resumption
- **October 24, 2025**: Trading resumption date for Scrip Code 940523

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine trading resumption affecting a single company's debentures. The clarification corrects an administrative misunderstanding about the purpose of the record date.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Trading members need only note the resumption date. The debentures remain outstanding and trading can proceed normally from October 24, 2025.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to debenture holders of KLM AXIVA FINVEST LTD who may have been concerned about redemption but now understand the record date was for interest payment purposes.
