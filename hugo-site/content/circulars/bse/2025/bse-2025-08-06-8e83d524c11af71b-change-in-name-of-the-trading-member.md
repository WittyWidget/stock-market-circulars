## Summary

BSE has announced that trading member INDMONEY PRIVATE LIMITED (Member No. 6779) will change its name to "INDSTOCKS PRIVATE LIMITED" effective Thursday, August 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading member name change from INDMONEY PRIVATE LIMITED to INDSTOCKS PRIVATE LIMITED
- Member number 6779 remains unchanged
- Change is purely administrative with no operational impact
- Contact provided for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is an administrative name change only.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for other trading members. The name change is handled by BSE administration.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Thursday, August 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact as this is purely an administrative name change. Trading operations and member number remain unchanged. Other market participants should update their records to reflect the new name.
