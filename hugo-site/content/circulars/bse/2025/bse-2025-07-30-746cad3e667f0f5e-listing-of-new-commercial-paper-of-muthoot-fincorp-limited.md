## Summary

MUTHOOT FINCORP LIMITED has listed new Commercial Paper securities on BSE Debt segment effective July 30, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each.

## Key Points

- 3,000 Commercial Paper securities of Rs. 5,00,000/- each listed
- Scrip Code: 729931, ISIN: INE549K14CB4
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,65,450/- per security
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- INDUSIND BANK LTD acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: July 30, 2025
- Allotment Date: July 29, 2025
- Redemption Date: June 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt securities listing. Provides additional short-term funding instrument for institutional investors with high credit rating (CRISIL A1+).
