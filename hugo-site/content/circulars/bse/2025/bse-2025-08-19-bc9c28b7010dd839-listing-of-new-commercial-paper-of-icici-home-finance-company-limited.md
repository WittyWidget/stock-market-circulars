## Summary

BSE announces the listing of new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 200 crore issued by ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED on private placement basis. The securities are listed on BSE Debt segment effective August 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total quantity: 4,000 units (Rs. 200 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,752 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- ISIN: INE071G14HB6
- Scrip Code: 730042

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 19, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 18, 2025
- **Trading Start**: August 19, 2025

## Trading Details

- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Market lot: 1
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding avenue for ICICI Home Finance and investment option for institutional investors in highly-rated commercial paper with 3-month maturity.
