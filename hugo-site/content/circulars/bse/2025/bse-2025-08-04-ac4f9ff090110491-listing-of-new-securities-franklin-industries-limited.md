## Summary

Franklin Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 540190) has listed 48.2 crore new equity shares issued through a rights offering. The shares will commence trading on BSE from August 5, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 48,20,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at par value
- Rights issue allotment completed on July 25, 2025
- ISIN: INE789R01022
- Distinctive numbers: 289200001 to 771200000
- New shares rank equally with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced - routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability
- Standard trading procedures apply from effective date

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 25, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 5, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of rights issue shares. The substantial increase in share count (48.2 crore shares) will affect the company's share structure and may impact per-share metrics, but trading impact expected to be neutral as existing shareholders received proportional rights.
