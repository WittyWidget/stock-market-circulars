## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of AGRI XCHNG 2025 issued on private placement basis. The securities carry a 12.25% per annum interest rate with monthly interest payments and mature on December 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 112,556,075 securities
- Market Lot: 100,000
- Scrip Code: 977051
- ISIN: INE2FAX15017
- Credit Rating: ICRA A- (SO)
- Face Value: Rs. 1.00
- Issue Price: Rs. 1.00
- Interest Rate: 12.25% per annum
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Reference to Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for detailed terms

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 20, 2025
- Interest Payment Period: August 27, 2025 to December 24, 2026 (Monthly)
- Date of Redemption: December 24, 2026
- Effective Trading Date: August 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities. The listing provides additional investment options in the debt segment but is unlikely to significantly affect broader market operations or investor behavior.
