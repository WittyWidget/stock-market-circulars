## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in non-convertible debentures of A.K. Capital Finance Ltd. effective September 2, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date for interest payment and redemption purposes.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to AKCFL-9.95%-18-9-25-PVT debentures (ISIN: INE197P07227, Code: 958287)
- Record date set for September 2, 2025
- Purpose includes both redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Trading members advised not to deal in these debentures from September 2, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes - standard trading suspension procedure for corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in specified A.K. Capital Finance debentures from September 2, 2025
- Members required to take note of the suspension notice

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 22, 2025
- **Record Date**: September 2, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: September 2, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact confined to holders of A.K. Capital Finance debentures. Suspension ensures orderly processing of interest payment and redemption for eligible debenture holders as per record date.
