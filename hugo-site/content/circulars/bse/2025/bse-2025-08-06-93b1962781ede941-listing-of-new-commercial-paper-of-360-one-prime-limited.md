## Summary

BSE has listed two new series of Commercial Papers issued by 360 ONE Prime Limited on private placement basis, with a total value of Rs. 200 crores. Both securities carry high credit ratings and will trade in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Two Commercial Paper series listed on BSE Debt segment effective August 6, 2025
- Series 1: 1000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each (Total: Rs. 50 crores) maturing February 13, 2026
- Series 2: 3000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each (Total: Rs. 150 crores) maturing November 4, 2025
- Both series carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent
- Trading only in dematerialized form with standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: August 6, 2025
- Allotment Date: August 5, 2025
- Redemption Dates:
  - Series 1 (360OPL4825): February 13, 2026
  - Series 2 (OPL50825): November 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. Provides additional short-term funding options for 360 ONE Prime Limited and investment opportunities for institutional investors in highly-rated commercial paper.
