## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 35,000 new equity shares of Global Offshore Services Ltd (Scrip Code: 501848) issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares will be available for trading on the Exchange from Monday, October 13, 2025. These shares were allotted at a premium and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 35,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 82/-
- Issue price: Rs. 92/- per share
- Issued to promoters on preferential basis through warrant conversion
- Distinctive numbers: 30708444 to 30743443
- ISIN: INE446C01013
- Date of allotment: July 8, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Trading commences: October 13, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following SEBI guidelines for preferential allotment and warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the increased share capital of Global Offshore Services Ltd
- Members should note the lock-in details for the newly listed shares
- All 35,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 30708444 to 30743443) are subject to lock-in provisions as per SEBI regulations

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 8, 2025
- **Notice Date**: October 10, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date**: October 13, 2025 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The addition of 35,000 shares represents a small increase in the total share capital of Global Offshore Services Ltd.

**Liquidity Impact**: Negligible immediate impact as all shares are subject to lock-in provisions and were allotted to promoters.

**Operational Impact**: Routine operational update for trading members to note new securities in their systems. No special trading arrangements required.
