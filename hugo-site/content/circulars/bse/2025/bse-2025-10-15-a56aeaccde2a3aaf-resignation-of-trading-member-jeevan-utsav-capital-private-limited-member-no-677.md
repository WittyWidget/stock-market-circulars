## Summary

BSE has notified all trading members that SEBI has approved the surrender of trading membership of Jeevan Utsav Capital Private Limited (Member no. 6770). This is a routine administrative action regarding membership status.

## Key Points

- Trading member: Jeevan Utsav Capital Private Limited
- Member number: 6770
- Action: Surrender of trading membership approved by SEBI
- Notice number: 20251015-2
- Notice date: 15 October 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification of membership status change.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to take note of this membership resignation. No specific compliance actions required.

## Important Dates

- **15 October 2025**: Notice date of membership resignation approval

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine administrative matter involving the voluntary resignation of a single trading member. Other market participants should update their records accordingly but no operational changes are required.
