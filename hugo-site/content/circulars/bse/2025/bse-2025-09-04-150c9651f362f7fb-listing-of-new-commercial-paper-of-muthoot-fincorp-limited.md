## Summary

BSE announces the listing of new Commercial Paper issued by Muthoot Fincorp Limited on private placement basis worth Rs. 125 crore (2,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) with effect from September 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (2,500 units)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,80,268 per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Scrip Code: 728935, ISIN: INE549K14BX0
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Indusind Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 3, 2025
- Listing Date: September 4, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by Muthoot Fincorp Limited. The instrument provides short-term funding option for the company and investment opportunity for debt market participants.
