## Summary

BSE has announced revisions to price bands for 27 securities, effective from August 21, 2025. The price bands range from 2% to 20%, with most securities receiving restrictive bands of 2% or 5%. This measure is part of BSE's surveillance activities to manage volatility and trading risks.

## Key Points

- 27 securities will have revised price bands effective August 21, 2025
- Price bands range from 2% (most restrictive) to 20%
- Most securities (11 out of 27) receive 2% price bands
- 8 securities get 5% price bands
- 4 securities receive 10% price bands
- 4 securities get 20% price bands
- Changes are implemented as part of surveillance measures

## Regulatory Changes

Price band restrictions have been modified for the listed securities, which will limit the maximum price movement (both upward and downward) during trading sessions to the specified percentages.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure compliance with the revised price bands
- All trading systems must be updated to reflect the new price band limits
- Members can seek clarifications at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 21, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The revised price bands will restrict price volatility for 27 securities, potentially reducing liquidity but providing stability. Securities with 2% bands face the most restrictive trading environment.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update their systems and risk management parameters to accommodate the new price band limits before the effective date.
