## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED on private placement basis. The issue consists of 8,000 units of ₹5 lakh each, totaling ₹40 crore, with maturity on September 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of ₹5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 8,000 units (₹40 crore)
- Issue price: ₹496,554.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 730026
- ISIN: INE071G14HA8
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of ₹5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 14, 2025
- Redemption date: September 26, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 14, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The instrument provides investors with a rated short-term investment option backed by ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED.
