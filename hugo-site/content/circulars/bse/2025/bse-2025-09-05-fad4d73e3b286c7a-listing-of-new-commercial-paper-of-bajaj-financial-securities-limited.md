## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited worth Rs. 30 crores (600 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from September 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 600 units (Rs. 30 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 497,868.5 per unit
- Scrip code: 730147, Scrip ID: BFSL3925
- ISIN: INE01C314CN4 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities to be traded under specified ISIN number

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: September 4, 2025
- Listing date: September 5, 2025
- Redemption date: September 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding option for Bajaj Financial Securities Limited. ICICI Bank Limited serves as issuing and paying agent.
