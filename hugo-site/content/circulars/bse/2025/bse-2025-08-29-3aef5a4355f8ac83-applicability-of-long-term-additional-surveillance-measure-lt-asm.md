## Summary

BSE has announced changes to the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective September 1, 2025. The circular details securities being newly included, moved between different ASM stages, and those being removed from the framework.

## Key Points

- 5 securities newly shortlisted for Long Term ASM Framework
- 2 securities moved to higher ASM stages (Stage II and III)
- 5 securities moved to lower ASM Stage I
- 9 securities removed from Long Term ASM Framework
- No securities directly moved to Stage IV

## Regulatory Changes

- Implementation of Long Term ASM Framework changes effective September 1, 2025
- Securities classified into different surveillance stages based on risk assessment
- Movement between stages based on compliance and trading patterns

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities under LT-ASM framework subject to additional surveillance measures
- Enhanced monitoring and reporting requirements for affected securities
- Stricter trading conditions and margin requirements may apply

## Important Dates

- **September 1, 2025**: Effective date for all LT-ASM framework changes

## Impact Assessment

- **Trading Impact**: Securities under higher ASM stages face stricter trading conditions
- **Liquidity Impact**: Enhanced surveillance may affect trading volumes
- **Investor Impact**: Retail and institutional investors need to be aware of surveillance status
- **Market Impact**: Framework ensures better market integrity and investor protection

## Securities Details

### Newly Shortlisted (Part A)
- Ashram Online.Com Ltd (526187)
- Beryl Drugs Ltd (524606)
- Brawn Biotech Ltd (530207)
- CHPL Industries Ltd (539335)
- Gorani Industries Ltd (531608)

### Moved to Higher Stages (Part B)
- Bharat Global Developers Ltd → Stage III
- Cargosol Logistics Ltd → Stage II

### Moved to Lower Stages (Part C)
- Jeena Sikho Lifecare Ltd → Stage I
- JSW Holdings Ltd → Stage I
- Kitex Garments Ltd → Stage I
- Stallion India Fluorochemicals Ltd → Stage I

### Removed from Framework (Part D)
- 9 securities including Garden Reach Shipbuilders, Associated Ceramics, and others
- Removals due to inclusion in other frameworks (Trade for Trade, GSM, ESM, IBC)
