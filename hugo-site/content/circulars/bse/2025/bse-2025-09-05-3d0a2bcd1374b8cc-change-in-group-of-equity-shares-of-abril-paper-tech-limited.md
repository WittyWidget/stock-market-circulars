## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of ABRIL PAPER TECH LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544500) will be transferred from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment with effect from Friday, September 19, 2025. The shares will be shifted under M Group for regular trading.

## Key Points

- Company: ABRIL PAPER TECH LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544500
- Transfer from: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- Transfer to: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Friday, September 19, 2025
- Notice references previous notice no. 20250904-47 dated September 4, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will move from restricted Trade for Trade trading to normal Rolling segment trading, allowing for regular intraday and delivery-based transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should note the change in trading segment and adjust their systems accordingly. Contact Mr. Anurag Jain on Tel. No. 022 2272 8822 for further details.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: September 5, 2025
- Effective Date: September 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Positive impact for ABRIL PAPER TECH LIMITED shareholders as the stock moves from restrictive Trade for Trade segment to normal Rolling segment, improving liquidity and trading flexibility.
