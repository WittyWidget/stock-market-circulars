## Summary

MIRAE ASSET GOLD SILVER PASSIVE FOF scheme has been reopened for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform with effect from September 1, 2025. The scheme includes SIP, STP and SWP facilities.

## Key Points

- MIRAE ASSET GOLD SILVER PASSIVE FOF NFO scheme reopened for ongoing transactions
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform from September 1, 2025
- Includes subscription, redemption, SIP, STP and SWP facilities
- Six scheme variants available (Regular/Direct plans with Growth/IDCW Payout/IDCW Reinvestment options)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine reopening of a mutual fund scheme for ongoing transactions after NFO closure.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Standard mutual fund trading procedures apply.

## Important Dates

- **September 1, 2025**: Effective date for ongoing transactions to commence

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine operational update providing investors access to ongoing transactions in the MIRAE ASSET GOLD SILVER PASSIVE FOF scheme after the NFO period. The availability of SIP, STP and SWP facilities enhances investment flexibility for retail investors interested in gold-silver exposure through fund of funds structure.
