## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 877,800 equity shares of MARBLE CITY INDIA LIMITED (Scrip Code: 531281) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will commence trading on July 30, 2025, and are subject to lock-in provisions until February 2026.

## Key Points

- 877,800 equity shares of Rs. 5.00 each issued at premium of Rs. 95.50
- Issue price: Rs. 100.50 per share
- Allotment date: January 24, 2025
- Trading commencement: July 30, 2025
- ISIN: INE807H01023
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability for trading
- Lock-in restrictions applicable as per regulations
- Shares subject to standard compliance requirements

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: January 24, 2025
- Trading Commencement: July 30, 2025
- Lock-in Expiry: February 5-6, 2026 (varies by distribution number ranges)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of preferential shares for a single company. The lock-in provisions ensure gradual market entry of these securities over the next year.
