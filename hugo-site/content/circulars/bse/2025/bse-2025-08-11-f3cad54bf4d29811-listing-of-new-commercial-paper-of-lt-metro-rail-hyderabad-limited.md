## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by L&T Metro Rail (Hyderabad) Limited on private placement basis. The securities are worth Rs. 5 lakhs each with 12,700 units totaling Rs. 635 crores, carrying CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Quantity: 12,700 units
- Total Value: Rs. 635 crores
- Scrip Code: 729999
- ISIN: INE128M14AQ5
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,857.50 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- State Bank of India acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 8, 2025
- Listing Date: August 11, 2025 (effective from today)
- Redemption Date: November 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a standard commercial paper listing for infrastructure financing. The short-term nature (3-month tenure) and high credit rating indicate low risk debt instrument for institutional investors.
