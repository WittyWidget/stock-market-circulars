## Summary

BSE has announced the live activities schedule for the Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window/Takeover) for WINDOW GLASS LIMITED. The takeover offer will run from Thursday, July 31, 2025, to Wednesday, August 13, 2025, with an offer price of Rs. 717.50 per share for up to 1,75,684 equity shares.

## Key Points

- Security: WINDOW GLASS LIMITED (Symbol: WINDOW, ISIN: INE902K01018, Scrip Code: 999949)
- Offer size: Up to 1,75,684 equity shares
- Offer price: Rs. 717.50 per share
- Face value: Rs. 10.00
- Market lot: 1
- Opening date: July 31, 2025
- Closing date: August 13, 2025

## Trading Schedule

- **Bid Offering Period**: July 31 - August 13, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up**: July 31 - August 12, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up (last day)**: August 13, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Take-up (last day)**: August 13, 2025 (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)
