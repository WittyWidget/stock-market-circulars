## Summary

BSE has assigned a new ISIN number INE142E01022 for BEMCO HYDRAULICS LTD (Scrip Code: 522650) following the sub-division of equity shares from Rs.10/- to Re.1/-. The new ISIN will be effective for trades from the ex-date of August 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: BEMCO HYDRAULICS LTD (Scrip Code: 522650)
- New ISIN: INE142E01022
- Share sub-division: From Rs.10/- to Re.1/- per share
- Ex-date for new ISIN: August 22, 2025
- Reference: DR-700/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

New ISIN number assignment following corporate action of share sub-division as per exchange regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use new ISIN INE142E01022 for trades from August 22, 2025
- Update trading systems and records with new ISIN
- Ensure proper settlement using new ISIN from ex-date

## Important Dates

- **August 22, 2025**: Ex-date - New ISIN becomes effective for trading
- **August 19, 2025**: Notice date
- **August 13, 2025**: Previous exchange notice date (Reference: 20250813-66)

## Impact Assessment

Routine corporate action requiring system updates by trading members. The share sub-division increases the number of shares outstanding while reducing individual share value proportionally. Trading systems must be updated to reflect the new ISIN for proper trade settlement and record keeping.
