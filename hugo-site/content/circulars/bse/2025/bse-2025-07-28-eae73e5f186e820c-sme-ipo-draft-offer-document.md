The document will be available on stock exchange websites for at least 21 days for public review and comments.

- Draft Offer Document filed by BharatRohan Airborne Innovations Limited on July 24, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for public review
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice for SME IPO draft offer document publication.

This notice affects potential investors in BharatRohan Airborne Innovations Limited's upcoming SME IPO.
