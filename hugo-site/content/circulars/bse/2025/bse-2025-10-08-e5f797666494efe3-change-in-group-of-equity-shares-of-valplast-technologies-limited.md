## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of VALPLAST TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544565), an SME IPO, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective October 24, 2025. This change follows the Exchange's Notice No. 20251007-55 dated October 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: VALPLAST TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544565
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Friday, October 24, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251008-2
- Reference Notice: 20251007-55 dated October 7, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will transition from Trade for Trade settlement to Rolling settlement, allowing for more flexible trading and settlement cycles. This move from MT Group to M Group represents a shift to standard rolling settlement procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should:
- Update their systems to reflect the segment change before October 24, 2025
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for further details or clarifications
- Ensure all trading activities comply with Rolling segment requirements from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **October 7, 2025**: Reference notice issued (20251007-55)
- **October 8, 2025**: Current notice issued (20251008-2)
- **October 24, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer

## Impact Assessment

This segment change will provide increased liquidity and trading flexibility for VALPLAST TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED shares. The shift from Trade for Trade (which requires full delivery) to Rolling segment allows for more efficient price discovery and trading opportunities. Trading members and investors should be aware of the different settlement procedures applicable under the Rolling segment (M Group) compared to the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group).
