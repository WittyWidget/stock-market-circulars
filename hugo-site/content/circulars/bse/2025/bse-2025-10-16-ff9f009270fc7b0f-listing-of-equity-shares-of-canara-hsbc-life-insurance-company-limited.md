## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Canara HSBC Life Insurance Company Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544583, symbol CANHLIFE, and ISIN number INE01TY01017. The exact listing date and additional security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Canara HSBC Life Insurance Company Limited
- Scrip Code: 544583
- Trading Symbol: CANHLIFE
- ISIN Number: INE01TY01017
- Listing status: Approved, pending date confirmation
- Segment: Equity
- Notice issued by: Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE should note the scrip code and symbol for future trading once the listing date is announced. No immediate compliance actions required until the listing date is specified.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 16, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This listing will add a new equity security in the insurance sector to BSE's trading platform. Market participants will have access to trade shares of Canara HSBC Life Insurance Company Limited once the listing becomes effective. The company represents a joint venture in the life insurance space, potentially offering investors exposure to this sector. The actual market impact will depend on the listing date, issue size, and market conditions at the time of listing.
