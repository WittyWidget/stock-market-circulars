## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper of Sharekhan Limited on its debt segment. The instrument consists of 2000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 100 crores, issued on private placement basis with a maturity period of approximately 3 months.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 2000 units (Rs. 100 crores aggregate)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,873.97 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- ISIN: INE211H14AD7
- Scrip code: 729991
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 8, 2025
- Listing date: August 11, 2025
- Redemption date: November 6, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding option for Sharekhan Limited and investment avenue for debt market participants.
