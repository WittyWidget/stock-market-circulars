## Summary

BSE has revised price bands for 13 scrips in the equity segment, effective September 11, 2025. The price bands range from 2% to 20%, with most securities receiving tighter surveillance measures through reduced price bands.

## Key Points

- 13 scrips affected across various sectors
- Price bands revised from existing levels
- Changes effective from September 11, 2025
- Covance Softsol Ltd receives the highest price band at 20%
- Four scrips (Fynx Capital, Refex Renewables, Sea TV Network) restricted to 2% price band
- Most other scrips set to 5% price band

## Regulatory Changes

Price band modifications as surveillance measure to control volatility in specific securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must adhere to revised price bands
- Clarifications can be sought at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 11, 2025
- **Notice Date**: September 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Tighter price bands on most affected scrips indicate increased surveillance due to volatility concerns. This will limit intraday price movements and may impact trading volumes and liquidity in these securities.
