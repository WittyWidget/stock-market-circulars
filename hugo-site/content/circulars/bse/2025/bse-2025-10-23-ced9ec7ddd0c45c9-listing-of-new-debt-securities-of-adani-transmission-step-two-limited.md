## Summary

BSE has listed seven new debt securities issued by Adani Transmission Step-Two Limited on private placement basis, effective October 23, 2025. The securities have face values of Rs. 100,000 each, interest rates ranging from 7.70% to 7.99% per annum, and maturity dates spanning from December 2026 to September 2042. All securities carry CRISIL AA+/Stable and IND AA+/Stable credit ratings, with quarterly interest payments and allotment date of October 17, 2025.

## Key Points

- Seven new debt securities listed on BSE Debt segment effective October 23, 2025
- Total quantity of 99,500 debentures issued (combined across all seven securities)
- Face value: Rs. 100,000 per debenture for all securities
- Interest rates: 7.70% to 7.99% p.a. depending on tenure
- Date of allotment: October 17, 2025
- Credit ratings: CRISIL AA+/Stable and IND AA+/Stable
- Interest payment frequency: Quarterly for all securities
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 for all securities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the respective ISIN numbers
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed for trading these securities

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 17, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 23, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: December 31, 2025 (for all securities)
- **Redemption Dates**:
  - ATSTL-7.70%-31-12-26-PVT (Scrip Code 977232): December 31, 2026
  - ATSTL-7.70%-30-9-27-PVT (Scrip Code 977233): September 30, 2027
  - ATSTL-7.70%-30-9-28-PVT (Scrip Code 977235): September 30, 2028
  - ATSTL-7.75%-30-9-29-PVT (Scrip Code 977236): September 30, 2029
  - ATSTL-7.75%-30-9-30-PVT (Scrip Code 977237): September 30, 2030
  - ATSTL-7.80%-30-9-40-PVT (Scrip Code 977238): September 30, 2040
  - ATSTL-7.99%-30-9-42-PVT (Scrip Code 977239): September 30, 2042

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine listing notification for privately placed debt securities. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment and will have limited participation from institutional investors.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Trading members dealing in debt securities can now trade these seven new instruments. The securities follow standard trading protocols with dematerialized trading and 1 paise tick size.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to debt market participants. The securities offer competitive interest rates (7.70%-7.99% p.a.) with strong credit ratings (AA+), providing options for fixed-income investors across various tenures from short-term (2026) to long-term (2042).

**Issuer Profile**: Adani Transmission Step-Two Limited is part of the Adani Group's transmission infrastructure business. The diverse maturity profile suggests strategic debt management with ladder financing approach.
