## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 141/2025-2026 dated October 24, 2025 lists book closure dates and record dates for various corporate actions. The bulletin includes dividend payments, interest payments on non-convertible debentures (NCDs), and NCD redemptions for multiple companies including Aditya Birla Capital Ltd, Ajanta Pharma Ltd, and Bajaj Finance Limited.

## Key Points

- Bulletin covers corporate actions scheduled between November 2025 and October 2035
- Aditya Birla Capital Ltd has multiple NCD interest payment dates throughout November-December 2025
- Ajanta Pharma Ltd has declared First Interim Dividend with record date November 10, 2025
- Bajaj Finance Limited has numerous interest payment and redemption dates for privately placed NCDs extending to 2035
- Record dates determine eligibility for receiving payments or participating in corporate actions

## Important Dates

**Aditya Birla Capital Ltd:**
- November 19, 2025: Interest payment (Code 958433)
- November 21, 2025: Interest payment (Code 973627)
- November 24, 2025: Interest payment (Code 938890)
- December 5, 2025: Interest payment (Code 958487)
- December 8, 2025: NCD Redemption (Code 960355)
- December 9, 2025: Interest payment (Code 977146)
- December 12, 2025: Interest payments (Codes 960364, 974472)
- December 15, 2025: Interest payment (Code 974499)
- December 20, 2025: Interest payment (Code 974407)

**Ajanta Pharma Ltd:**
- November 10, 2025: Record date for First Interim Dividend (Codes 132331, 532331)

**Bajaj Finance Limited:**
- September 30, 2026-2033: Various interest payment dates
- October 1, 2029-2035: Multiple interest payment and redemption dates
- October 3, 2028-2034: Interest payment dates

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors must hold securities as of the record date to be eligible for dividend, interest, or redemption payments
- Securities with trading flag "0" allow normal trading on record date
- Securities with trading flag "1" trade ex-benefit prior to record date
- Companies must ensure timely payment to eligible security holders

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - Regular corporate actions that are routine market operations

**Investor Impact:** Medium - Important for investors holding these securities to track payment dates and eligibility requirements

**Operational Impact:** Low - Standard procedures for brokers and depositories to handle corporate actions

This bulletin serves as a notification mechanism for market participants to track upcoming corporate actions and ensure proper handling of payments and benefits for eligible security holders.
