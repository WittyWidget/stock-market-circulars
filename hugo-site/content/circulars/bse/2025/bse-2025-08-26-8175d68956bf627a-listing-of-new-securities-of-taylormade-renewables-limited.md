## Summary

515,000 equity shares of Taylormade Renewables Limited (Scrip Code: 541228) have been listed and are permitted to trade on BSE effective August 28, 2025. These shares were issued at Rs. 180 per share (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 170 premium) to promoters and non-promoters through preferential allotment pursuant to warrant conversion.

## Key Points

- 515,000 equity shares of Rs. 10 each issued at premium of Rs. 170
- Shares issued to both promoter and non-promoter categories
- Issue price: Rs. 180 per share
- Distribution numbers: 11609604 to 12124603
- Date of allotment: January 8, 2025
- ISIN: INE459Z01017
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure following warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability for trading
- Lock-in restrictions apply as per SEBI guidelines
- Standard compliance requirements for listed securities

## Important Dates

- **Trading Start Date**: August 28, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: January 8, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: 500,000 shares until March 1, 2027; 15,000 shares until March 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The listing represents a routine administrative process following warrant conversion. The relatively small number of shares and existing company operations suggest limited effect on trading volumes or stock price volatility.
