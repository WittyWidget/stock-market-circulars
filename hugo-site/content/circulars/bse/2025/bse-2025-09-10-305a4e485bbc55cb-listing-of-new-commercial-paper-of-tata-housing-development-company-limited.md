## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Tata Housing Development Company Limited on its Debt segment. The securities were issued on private placement basis with face value of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling 6000 units worth Rs. 30 crores.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each listed on BSE Debt segment
- Total quantity: 6000 units (Rs. 30 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730170, Scrip ID: THDCL9925
- ISIN Number: INE582L14IV8
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,543 per unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 9, 2025
- Listing Date: September 10, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding option for Tata Housing Development Company Limited with 90-day maturity period.
