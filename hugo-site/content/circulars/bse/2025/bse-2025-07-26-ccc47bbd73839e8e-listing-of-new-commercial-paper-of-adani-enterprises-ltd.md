## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Adani Enterprises Ltd on private placement basis, effective July 25, 2025. The Commercial Paper has a face value of ₹5 lakh per unit with 100 units totaling ₹5 crore, carrying credit ratings of ACUITE A1+ and CARE A1+, and scheduled for redemption on March 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of ₹5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 100 units (₹5 crore total)
- Issue price: ₹4,76,219.50 per unit
- Credit ratings: ACUITE A1+ and CARE A1+
- Allotment date: July 24, 2025
- Redemption date: March 25, 2026
- Scrip code: 729140
- ISIN: INE423A14WX5
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading permitted in standard denomination of ₹5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for trading is 1 paise
- Securities will trade under ISIN INE423A14WX5

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: July 24, 2025
- Listing effective date: July 25, 2025
- Redemption date: March 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The securities are short-term debt instruments with approximately 8-month tenure, representing standard corporate funding activity. The listing expands BSE's debt segment offerings but is unlikely to significantly affect broader market operations or Adani Enterprises' stock price.
