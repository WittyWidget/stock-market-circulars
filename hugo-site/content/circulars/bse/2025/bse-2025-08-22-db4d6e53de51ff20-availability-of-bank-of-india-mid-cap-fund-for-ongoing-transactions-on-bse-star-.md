## Summary

Bank of India Mid Cap Fund will reopen for ongoing transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform effective August 25, 2025. The fund will offer subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP facilities across all plan variants.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening effective from August 25, 2025
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Includes both Regular and Direct plans
- Offers Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All systematic investment facilities (SIP, STP, SWP) will be available
- Six scheme variants with different ISIN codes

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must ensure proper handling of transactions for the reopened fund schemes on the BSE StAR MF Platform.

## Important Dates

- **August 25, 2025**: Effective date for fund reopening
- **August 22, 2025**: Notice date

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on mutual fund investors seeking mid-cap equity exposure through Bank of India AMC. The reopening provides renewed access to investment opportunities in the mid-cap segment through BSE's mutual fund platform.
