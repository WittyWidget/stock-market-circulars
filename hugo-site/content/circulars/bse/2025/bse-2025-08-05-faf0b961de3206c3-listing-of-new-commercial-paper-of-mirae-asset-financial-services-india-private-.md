## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Mirae Asset Financial Services (India) Private Limited worth Rs. 75 crores (1,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on private placement basis, effective August 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 1,500 units (Rs. 75 crores total)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,460 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+
- ISIN: INE0JRU14248
- Scrip code: 729962
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard commercial paper listing procedure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 5, 2025
- Listing effective date: August 5, 2025
- Redemption date: November 4, 2025
- Tenure: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding option for Mirae Asset Financial Services and investment opportunity for institutional investors in highly-rated (A1+) commercial paper with 91-day maturity.
