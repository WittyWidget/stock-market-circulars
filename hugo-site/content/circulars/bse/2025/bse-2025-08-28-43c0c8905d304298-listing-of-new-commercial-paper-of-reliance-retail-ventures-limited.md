## Summary

BSE has listed Commercial Paper worth ₹20,000 crores issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on private placement basis. The securities will trade on BSE Debt segment from August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- 40,000 Commercial Paper units of ₹5 lakh each listed
- Issue price: ₹4,92,788 per unit (face value ₹5,00,000)
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- ISIN: INE929O14DY0, Scrip Code: 730099
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialised form
- Trading in standard denomination of ₹5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size of 1 paise applicable
- Trading members to contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 26, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 28, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. Provides short-term funding option for Reliance Retail Ventures Limited with 91-day maturity period.
