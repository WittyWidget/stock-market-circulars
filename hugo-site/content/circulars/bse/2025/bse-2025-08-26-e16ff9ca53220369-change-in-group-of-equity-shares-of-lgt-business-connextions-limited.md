## Summary

LGT Business Connextions Limited (Scrip Code: 544489) will be transferred from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) effective September 10, 2025, allowing normal rolling settlement instead of compulsory delivery.

## Key Points

- Company: LGT Business Connextions Limited (Scrip Code: 544489)
- Transfer from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: Wednesday, September 10, 2025
- This follows previous notice 20250825-63 dated August 25, 2025
- SME segment company

## Regulatory Changes

- Trading mechanism changes from compulsory delivery (Trade for Trade) to rolling settlement
- Company moves from MT Group to M Group classification
- Normal trading flexibility restored

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems for the segment change
- Contact Mr. Parag Jain (022 2272 8685) for further details
- Adjust trading strategies and risk management for rolling settlement

## Important Dates

- **September 10, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer
- **August 25, 2025**: Date of previous related notice
- **August 26, 2025**: Current notice date

## Impact Assessment

- Positive impact for investors as rolling settlement provides more trading flexibility
- Reduced settlement risk compared to compulsory delivery
- May increase liquidity and trading volumes for the stock
- Indicates improved compliance or financial standing of the company
