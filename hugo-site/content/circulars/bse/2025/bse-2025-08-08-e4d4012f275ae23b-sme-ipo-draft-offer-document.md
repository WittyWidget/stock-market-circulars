## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of LOGICIEL SOLUTIONS LIMITED for public review. The document was filed on August 06, 2025, and contains all disclosures required under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The document will be available for at least 21 days for public comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by LOGICIEL SOLUTIONS LIMITED on August 06, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for public review
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard SME IPO filing process under existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public can review and submit comments on the Draft Offer Document
- Comments must be submitted within 21 days from August 08, 2025
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document hosted on www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- Filing Date: August 06, 2025
- Notice Date: August 08, 2025
- Comment Deadline: August 29, 2025 (21 days from notice)
- Document Availability: Minimum 21 days from notice date

## Impact Assessment

This SME IPO filing provides investment opportunity in the small and medium enterprise segment. The public comment period allows stakeholders to review the company's disclosures and provide feedback before the IPO process proceeds. Impact is primarily on potential investors interested in SME listings and the company's ability to raise capital through public offering.
