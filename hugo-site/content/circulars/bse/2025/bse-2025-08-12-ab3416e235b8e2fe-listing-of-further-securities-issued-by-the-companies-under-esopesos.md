## Summary

BSE has listed additional securities from 11 companies issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These shares will be available for trading from August 13, 2025, with no lock-in period restrictions.

## Key Points

- 11 companies have issued additional shares under employee stock option schemes
- Total shares listed range from 1,000 (Arvind Fashions) to 4,385,250 (Suzlon Energy)
- All shares are admitted for trading with effect from Wednesday, August 13, 2025
- No lock-in period applies to any of the listed securities
- Face values range from ₹1 to ₹10 per share

## Companies and Share Details

- **Arvind Fashions Limited**: 1,000 shares (Face Value: ₹4)
- **Fedbank Financial Services Limited**: 50,687 shares (Face Value: ₹10)
- **Five-Star Business Finance Ltd**: 6,385 shares (Face Value: ₹1)
- **Go Digit General Insurance Limited**: 18,859 shares (Face Value: ₹10)
- **Hind Rectifiers Ltd**: 21,132 shares (Face Value: ₹2)
- **Monarch Networth Capital Limited**: 31,000 shares (Face Value: ₹10)
- **NIIT Ltd**: 149,999 shares (Face Value: ₹2)
- **Sudarshan Chemical Industries Ltd**: 23,584 shares (Face Value: ₹2)
- **Suzlon Energy Ltd**: 4,385,250 shares (Face Value: ₹2)
- **Wipro Ltd**: 198,402 shares (Face Value: ₹2)
- **Yes Bank Ltd**: 920,629 shares (Face Value: ₹2)

## Important Dates

- **Trading Commencement**: Wednesday, August 13, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as these are routine ESOP/ESOS share listings. The additional shares represent employee stock options being converted to tradeable equity, typically indicating positive employee sentiment and company performance.
