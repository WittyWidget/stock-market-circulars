## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 61,000 equity shares of Venus Pipes & Tubes Limited (Scrip Code: 543528) issued through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis to promoters. The shares will be available for trading from August 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- 61,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 1,690/-
- Issued to promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 20431111 to 20492110
- ISIN: INE0JA001018

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members informed of new securities listing and trading permissions.

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: June 19, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 11, 2025
- Lock-in Period: Until February 27, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a small number of shares (61,000) issued to promoters through warrant conversion. The shares are subject to lock-in until February 2027, limiting immediate trading volume impact.
