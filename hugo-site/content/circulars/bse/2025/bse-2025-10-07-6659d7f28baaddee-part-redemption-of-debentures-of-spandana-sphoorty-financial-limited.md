## Summary

BSE has notified that Spandana Sphoorty Financial Limited (SSFL) has fixed the record date for part redemption of its non-convertible debentures and payment of interest. Trading in the debentures will be conducted with reduced face value effective from October 9, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Spandana Sphoorty Financial Limited
- ISIN: INE572J07745
- Scrip Code: 974793
- Security: SSFL-11.10%-24-4-26-PVT
- Record Date: October 9, 2025
- Reduced Face Value: Rs. 28,575 per debenture
- Effective Date: October 9, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-732/2025-2026
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trading in SSFL debentures will be conducted with reduced face value of Rs. 28,575 per debenture
- The reduced face value is effective from October 9, 2025
- Settlement number DR-732/2025-2026 applies

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: October 9, 2025 - Eligibility date for interest payment and part redemption
- **Effective Date**: October 9, 2025 - Trading commences with reduced face value
- **Notice Date**: October 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This corporate action impacts holders of Spandana Sphoorty Financial Limited's non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE572J07745). Debenture holders on record as of October 9, 2025 will receive interest payment and partial redemption. Post-redemption, the debentures will trade at a reduced face value of Rs. 28,575, affecting pricing and yield calculations for market participants. Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the new face value for accurate settlement.
