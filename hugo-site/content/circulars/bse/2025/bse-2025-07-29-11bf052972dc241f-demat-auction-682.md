## Summary

BSE has published Settlement No. 682 dated July 29, 2025, listing securities available for demat auction. The settlement includes 88 different securities with their respective quantities available for auction, ranging from small quantities of 1 share to larger quantities of up to 25,000 shares.

## Key Points

- Settlement number: 682
- Settlement date: July 29, 2025
- Total securities listed: 88 different scrips
- Quantity range: 1 to 25,000 shares per security
- Includes major companies like Reliance Industries, IDFC First Bank, Biocon, and others

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this settlement notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must refer to this settlement list for demat auction activities
- Trading members should note the specific quantities available for each listed security

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: July 29, 2025
- Settlement Number: 682

## Impact Assessment

This demat auction settlement affects trading in 88 securities across various sectors. The impact varies by security based on the quantities involved, with IDFC First Bank having the highest quantity at 25,000 shares and Cressanda Solutions at 17,990 shares. Other securities have smaller quantities ranging from single digits to a few thousand shares. This represents routine market operations for settling demat obligations.
