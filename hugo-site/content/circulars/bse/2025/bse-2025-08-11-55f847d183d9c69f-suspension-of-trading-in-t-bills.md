## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in three Treasury Bills (T-Bills) that are set to mature on their respective redemption dates. The suspension becomes effective from August 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills will be suspended from trading due to maturity
- Suspension effective from August 12, 2025
- Notice categorized under Corporate Actions in Debt segment
- Trading members advised not to deal in the specified T-Bills

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine operational notice for maturing securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the following T-Bills from August 12, 2025:
  - SCRIP CODE 805026 (ISIN: IN002024Y449) - 182TB14825
  - SCRIP CODE 805064 (ISIN: IN002025X075) - 91TB140825
  - SCRIP CODE 804955 (ISIN: IN002024Z206) - 364TB15825
- Trading members must take note of this suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 11, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: August 12, 2025
- **Reference**: DR-693/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

Routine impact on debt market operations. The suspension of these three T-Bills will not significantly affect overall market functioning as this is a standard procedure for maturing government securities. Trading members need to adjust their portfolios and trading strategies accordingly for the affected securities.
