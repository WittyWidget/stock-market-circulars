## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by PAISALO DIGITAL LIMITED on private placement basis. The securities are listed on BSE Debt segment with effect from October 15, 2025. A total of 700 Commercial Papers with face value of Rs.5,00,000 each have been admitted to trading.

## Key Points

- Securities Description: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis of Rs.5,00,000 each
- Quantity: 700 units
- Market Lot: 1
- Scrip Code: 730387
- Scrip ID: PDL141025
- Detail Name: PDL-25-03-26-CP
- ISIN Number: INE420C14227
- Credit Rating: IVR A1+
- Face Value: Rs.500,000.00
- Paid-up Value: Rs.500,000.00
- Issue Price: Rs.481,822.50
- Issuing and Paying Agent: IDBI Bank Ltd

## Trading Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE420C14227
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Market lot is 1 unit

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 15, 2025
- Actual/Deemed Date of Allotment: October 14, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: October 15, 2025
- Date of Redemption: March 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing notification with minimal impact on broader market operations. The listing provides short-term debt financing instrument for PAISALO DIGITAL LIMITED with approximately 5-month tenure. Trading members can contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications.
