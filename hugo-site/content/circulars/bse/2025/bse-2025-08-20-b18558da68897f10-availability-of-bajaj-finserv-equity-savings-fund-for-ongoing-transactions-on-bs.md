## Summary

BSE announces that BAJAJ FINSERV EQUITY SAVINGS FUND will be available for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform starting August 21, 2025. The fund will offer complete facilities including SIP, STP, and SWP options.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening for ongoing transactions effective August 21, 2025
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Includes subscription and redemption facilities
- SIP, STP & SWP facilities available
- Six different plan options available (Regular and Direct plans with IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, and Growth options)
- All plans categorized under EQUITY scheme category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

Members can facilitate transactions in the specified fund schemes through BSE StAR MF Platform from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 21, 2025 - Fund available for ongoing transactions
- **Notice Date**: August 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine operational notice providing investment opportunity through BSE's mutual fund platform. Enhances investment options for retail and institutional investors seeking equity savings fund exposure through systematic investment plans and other structured investment routes.
