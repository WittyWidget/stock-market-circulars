## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of subscription facilities for SBI Silver ETF Fund of Fund. This circular informs market participants that investors can now subscribe to this fund scheme through the BSE StAR MF platform.

## Key Points

- Subscriptions for SBI Silver ETF Fund of Fund have been resumed
- The announcement applies to the BSE StAR MF (Mutual Fund) platform
- This is a fund-of-fund scheme that invests in SBI Silver ETF
- Investors can now place fresh subscription orders for this scheme

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational announcement regarding the availability of a mutual fund product.

## Compliance Requirements

- Mutual fund distributors and intermediaries should update their systems to reflect the availability of subscriptions
- Ensure proper disclosure to investors regarding the resumption of subscription facility

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: October 27, 2025
- Effective Date: As per the circular issuance date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - This is a product-specific announcement affecting only SBI Silver ETF Fund of Fund investors.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Primarily affects mutual fund distributors and investors interested in silver ETF exposure through fund-of-fund route.

**Investor Impact:** Positive for investors seeking to invest in silver through SBI's fund-of-fund structure, as subscription facility is now available after a period of suspension.
