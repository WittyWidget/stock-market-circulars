## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of UGRO CAPITAL LIMITED (UCL-10.50%-26-9-25-NCD, ISIN: INE583D07315, Code: 938149) effective September 11, 2025, due to the company fixing record date for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to UGRO CAPITAL LIMITED debentures only
- Debenture code: UCL-10.50%-26-9-25-NCD (938149)
- ISIN: INE583D07315
- Trading Members advised not to deal in these debentures
- Suspension is temporary, linked to corporate action processing

## Regulatory Changes

No permanent regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural suspension during corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not deal in the specified UGRO CAPITAL debentures
- All market participants must note the trading restriction
- Compliance with corporate action processing requirements

## Important Dates

- **Record Date:** September 11, 2025
- **No Dealings From:** September 11, 2025
- **Notice Date:** September 9, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as suspension affects only specific debentures of UGRO CAPITAL LIMITED. Temporary measure to facilitate proper settlement of redemption and interest payments to eligible debenture holders as per record date.
