## Summary

BSE has announced Demat Auction Settlement No. 689 dated August 7, 2025, listing 81 securities with their respective quantities to be auctioned. The auction covers a diverse range of companies across various sectors including ports, pharmaceuticals, capital goods, steel, textiles, finance, and technology.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 689
- Settlement Date: August 7, 2025
- Total Securities Listed: 81 companies
- Quantity Range: From 1 share (multiple companies) to 19,526 shares (MONOTYPE IND)
- Highest Quantity: MONOTYPE IND - 19,526 shares
- Notable Large Quantities: INVENTURE GR (6,170), Sri Lotus De (5,270), BELRISE INDU (3,700)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes specified in this auction notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must settle their demat auction obligations as per BSE settlement procedures
- Securities listed must be delivered or purchased as per auction requirements
- Settlement participants must ensure availability of securities or funds for settlement

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: August 7, 2025
- Settlement Number: 689

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on affected securities and their shareholders. The auction primarily affects delivery obligations for specific quantities of shares across multiple companies. Market participants with short positions in these securities will need to arrange for delivery or purchase from the auction. The relatively modest quantities for most securities suggest limited market disruption, though some securities like MONOTYPE IND, INVENTURE GR, and Sri Lotus De have significant quantities that may impact their trading.
