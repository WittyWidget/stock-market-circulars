## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited worth Rs. 75 crores (1,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued on private placement basis. The securities will trade in the BSE Debt segment with effect from August 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 1,500 units (Rs. 75 crores total)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,715.50 per unit
- Scrip code: 730117, ISIN: INE755K14FO3
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- IDBI Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 28, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 29, 2025
- Redemption date: November 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for corporate funding needs. The listing provides additional short-term debt instrument options for institutional investors in the debt segment.
