## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Anubhav Plast Limited filed on September 09, 2025 for public comments. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and will be available on the stock exchange website for at least 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Anubhav Plast Limited on September 09, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website: www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Contains all required disclosures under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public has 21 days from September 10, 2025 to submit comments on the Draft Offer Document
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Company must maintain document availability for at least 21 days

## Important Dates

- Draft filing date: September 09, 2025
- Notice date: September 10, 2025
- Comment deadline: 21 days from September 10, 2025 (October 01, 2025)
- Minimum availability period: 21 days

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural notification for an SME IPO process. The impact is limited to potential investors and market participants interested in Anubhav Plast Limited. No immediate market disruption expected as this is part of the standard IPO approval process.
