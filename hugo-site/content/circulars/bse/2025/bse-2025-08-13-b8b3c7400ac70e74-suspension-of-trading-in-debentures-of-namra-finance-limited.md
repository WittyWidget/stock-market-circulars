## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debentures of NAMRA FINANCE LIMITED (Code: NFL-11.95%-20-8-25-PVT, ISIN: INE229U07129) effective August 14, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing August 15, 2025 as the record date for interest payment and redemption of non-convertible debentures.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to NAMRA FINANCE LIMITED debentures
- ISIN: INE229U07129 (Code: 975678)
- Debenture series: NFL-11.95%-20-8-25-PVT
- No dealings permitted from August 14, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational measure for record date compliance.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from August 14, 2025
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and comply immediately
- All debt market participants must observe the trading restriction

## Important Dates

- **August 13, 2025**: Notice date
- **August 14, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date (no dealings from this date)
- **August 15, 2025**: Record date for interest payment and redemption

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on debt market participants holding or planning to trade NAMRA FINANCE LIMITED debentures. The suspension is temporary and standard procedure for corporate actions involving record dates. Investors cannot exit positions until trading resumes post-record date settlement.
