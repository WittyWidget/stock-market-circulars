## Summary

BSE has listed 93,08,800 new equity shares of Yash Innoventures Limited (Scrip Code: 523650) for trading effective August 19, 2025. These shares were issued pursuant to a scheme of arrangement involving the amalgamation of Yash Shelters Limited with Yash Innoventures Limited.

## Key Points

- 93,08,800 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each now listed for trading
- ISIN: INE823D01011
- Distinguished numbers: 6721151 to 16000000 and 18760001 to 18789950
- Shares issued under amalgamation scheme between Yash Shelters Limited and Yash Innoventures Limited
- Allotment date: April 17, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing following completion of an amalgamation scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading on the exchange.

## Important Dates

- **Trading Start Date**: August 19, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: April 17, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this represents completion of a previously announced amalgamation. The listing provides additional liquidity for existing shareholders of both entities involved in the merger.
