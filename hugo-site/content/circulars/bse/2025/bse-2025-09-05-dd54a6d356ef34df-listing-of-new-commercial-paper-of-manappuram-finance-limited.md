## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of Manappuram Finance Limited issued on private placement basis, effective September 5, 2025. The issue comprises 8,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 400 crores.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 8,000 units (Rs. 400 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,397 per unit
- Scrip code: 730154, Scrip ID: MFL4925
- ISIN: INE522D14OS5
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded under the specified ISIN number

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: September 4, 2025
- Listing effective date: September 5, 2025
- Redemption date: December 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding. The issue provides Manappuram Finance with working capital financing for approximately 3 months. AXIS BANK serves as the issuing and paying agent.
