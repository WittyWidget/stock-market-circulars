## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Midwest Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company has been assigned scrip code 544587, symbol MIDWESTLTD, and ISIN INE0XAD01024. The specific date of listing and further details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Midwest Limited to be listed on BSE equity segment
- Scrip Code: 544587
- Symbol: MIDWESTLTD
- ISIN: INE0XAD01024
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
- Trading members informed of upcoming listing

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip details and await further notice regarding the listing date before commencing trading activities in Midwest Limited shares.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 23, 2025
- **Listing Date:** To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard listing announcement with minimal market-wide impact. It affects only participants interested in trading Midwest Limited shares once the listing becomes effective. The announcement provides preliminary information to prepare trading members for the upcoming listing.
