## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 5,60,000 new equity shares of Dev Labtech Venture Limited (Scrip Code: 543848) issued through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis to promoters. The shares will commence trading on August 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- 5,60,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 52/-
- Shares issued to promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Issue price: Rs. 62.00 per share
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE0NIJ01017
- Distinct numbers: 11303140 to 11863139

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities listing
- Lock-in period compliance required for all 5,60,000 shares until February 28, 2027

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: April 30, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 4, 2025
- Lock-in Period Ends: February 28, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine preferential allotment to promoters with extended lock-in period. The shares represent warrant conversion and will not immediately affect market liquidity due to lock-in restrictions.
