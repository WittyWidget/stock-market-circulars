## Summary

BSE has activated the Call Money Module on its IBBS platform for SEPC Limited's first and final call on partly paid-up equity shares. The call involves 35,00,00,000 partly paid-up equity shares with a call money amount of Rs. 5 per share. The issue opens on October 24, 2025 and closes on November 7, 2025. Cameo Corporate Services Limited is the Registrar to the issue.

## Key Points

- **Company**: SEPC Limited
- **Type of Call**: First and Final Call
- **Security Symbol**: SEPCPPCM
- **Number of Shares**: 35,00,00,000 partly paid-up equity shares
- **Call Amount**: Rs. 5 per share
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Issue Period**: 15 days (October 24 - November 7, 2025)
- **Bidding Hours**: 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM daily
- **Registrar**: Cameo Corporate Services Limited, Chennai
- **SEBI Registration**: INR000003753

## Regulatory Changes

This call money notice is issued in accordance with:
- BSE Exchange Notice No. 20210104-37 dated January 4, 2021
- SEBI Circular dated December 8, 2020

The Call Money Module follows established regulatory framework for collection of call money on partly paid-up shares through the IBBS platform.

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members and SCSBs:**
- Must use the Call Money Module on IBBS platform to submit details of call money notices collected
- Only Self-Certified Syndicate Banks (SCSBs) and Trading Members with 3-in-1 type accounts can access the module
- Must collect call money payments during the specified bidding period (10:00 AM - 5:00 PM)
- Submit collected call money details through the IBBS system

**For Shareholders:**
- Holders of partly paid-up equity shares must pay Rs. 5 per share as call money
- Payment must be made between October 24 and November 7, 2025
- Contact Registrar (Cameo Corporate Services) or intermediaries for payment processing

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects shareholders holding SEPC's partly paid-up shares who must pay the call amount to maintain their shareholding.

**Operational Impact**: Requires Trading Members and SCSBs to facilitate call money collection through the IBBS platform during the 15-day window.

**Financial Impact**: Total call money collection of Rs. 17.50 crores (35 crore shares x Rs. 5 per share) for SEPC Limited.
