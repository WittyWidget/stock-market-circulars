## Summary

ECL Finance Limited has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 25 crores (500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE's debt segment. The papers are issued on private placement basis with CRISIL A1+ and IVR A1+ ratings, maturing on January 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- 500 commercial paper units of Rs. 5,00,000 each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 4,80,589.50 per unit (discount to face value)
- Scrip Code: 729277, Scrip ID: ECLF25425
- ISIN: INE804I14XW9 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+ and IVR A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 29, 2025
- Listing Date: July 30, 2025
- Redemption Date: January 27, 2026
- Maturity Period: ~6 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides ECL Finance with short-term funding while offering investors a rated debt instrument with 6-month maturity. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk for investors.
