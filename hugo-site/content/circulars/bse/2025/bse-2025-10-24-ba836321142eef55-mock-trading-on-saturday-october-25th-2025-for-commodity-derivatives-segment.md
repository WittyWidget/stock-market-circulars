## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, October 25th, 2025. This session is designed for trading members to test their trading applications, including third-party platforms and in-house systems using ETI APIs. The mock trading will cover various functionalities including exceptional market conditions, risk-reduction mode, and trading halts. No actual trading obligations will be created.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, October 25th, 2025
- No new BOLT TWS Release will be implemented
- Trading members can test third-party platforms and in-house systems through ETI APIs
- Session will include testing of exceptional market conditions, risk-reduction mode, and trading halts
- Trades from mock session will not create any margin, pay-in/pay-out obligations, rights, or liabilities
- Members required to provide feedback by 6:00 PM on the same day
- BSE Helpdesk and Tech Support available for queries

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are requested to participate actively in the mock trading session
- Members must provide feedback for the mock trading session by 6:00 PM on October 25th, 2025
- Contact BSE Tech Support (022-22728053, bse.tech@bseindia.com) or Help Desk (022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500, bsehelp@bseindia.com) for any queries

## Important Dates

**October 25th, 2025 (Saturday)**
- Log-in: 1:15 PM to 2:00 PM
- Continuous Trading: 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM
- Trade Modification: 5:00 PM to 5:10 PM
- Feedback deadline: 6:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** None - This is a mock trading session with no real market implications.

**Operational Impact:** Low - Optional testing opportunity for trading members to familiarize themselves with systems and test various functionalities including exceptional market conditions. Members using third-party platforms or in-house developed systems can validate their applications before actual trading scenarios.

**Trading Members:** Beneficial opportunity to test and validate trading systems, risk management protocols, and exceptional market condition handling without any financial obligations or liabilities.
