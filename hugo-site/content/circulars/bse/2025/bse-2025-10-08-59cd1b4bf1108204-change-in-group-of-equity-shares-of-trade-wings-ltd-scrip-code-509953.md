## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Trade Wings Ltd. (Scrip Code: 509953) will be included under the 'X' group effective October 15, 2025. This group change typically indicates companies under surveillance or with specific trading restrictions.

## Key Points

- Trade Wings Ltd. (Scrip Code: 509953) will move to 'X' group
- Change effective from October 15, 2025
- Trading members notified through Notice No 20251008-19
- Contact person: Ms. Sonali Kurhade (Cell: +91-8779628213) for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be traded under BSE's 'X' group framework, which typically applies to companies that:
- Are under surveillance
- Have specific trading restrictions
- Require enhanced monitoring by the exchange

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:
- Note the group change in their systems before October 15, 2025
- Apply applicable 'X' group trading rules and restrictions
- Inform clients holding or trading this scrip about the group change
- Contact Ms. Sonali Kurhade for any clarifications needed

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 8, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The movement to 'X' group will likely result in:
- Enhanced surveillance on trading activities
- Potential restrictions on trading (depending on X group rules)
- Increased scrutiny by exchange authorities
- Possible impact on liquidity and trading volumes
- Investors should exercise caution when trading this scrip
