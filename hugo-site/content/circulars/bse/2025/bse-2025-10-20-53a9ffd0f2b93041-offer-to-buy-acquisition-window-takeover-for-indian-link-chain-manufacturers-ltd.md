## Summary

BSE has announced the complete schedule for the Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for Indian Link Chain Manufacturers Ltd (INLCM). The offer will be open from Thursday, October 23, 2025 to Thursday, November 6, 2025. Trading and Custodian members can facilitate bids for up to 7,93,000 equity shares at an offer price of Rs. 71.00 per share through the iBBS platform.

## Key Points

- **Security Name**: Indian Link Chain Manufacturers Ltd
- **Symbol**: INLCM
- **ISIN**: INE359D01024
- **Scrip Code**: 504746
- **Offer Size**: Up to 7,93,000 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 71.00 per share
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10.00
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Offer Period**: October 23, 2025 to November 6, 2025
- Trading members can bid for investors having shares in demat form only
- UCC of investors must be registered with correct PAN number and category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard offer to buy (takeover) process following existing OTB framework as per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017).

## Compliance Requirements

**For Trading Members and Custodian Members:**

1. Ensure investor UCC is registered and updated with correct PAN number and category in Exchange UCC database
2. Can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
3. Access OTB module through specified URLs (iBBS, RTRMS, or Extranet)
4. Follow operational instructions per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 and 20170203-29
5. Note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet
6. Acceptance of bids files on Extranet available only after confirmation from Merchant Banker/RTA post-issue closure

**Bidding Categories Available:**
- Foreign Institutional Buyer (FII)
- Financial Institution (FI)
- Mutual Fund (MF)
- Insurance Companies (IC)
- Other QIBs (OTH)
- Other NIB (NOH)
- Company (CO)
- Individual (IND)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a company-specific takeover offer affecting only shareholders of Indian Link Chain Manufacturers Ltd. The offer size of 7,93,000 shares represents a specific acquisition target.

**Operational Impact**: Medium for trading members handling INLCM shareholders - Members need to ensure proper UCC registration, access to iBBS/RTRMS platforms, and facilitate bidding during the 15-day offer period.

**Investor Impact**: High for INLCM shareholders - Shareholders have the opportunity to tender their shares at Rs. 71.00 per share during the specified window.
