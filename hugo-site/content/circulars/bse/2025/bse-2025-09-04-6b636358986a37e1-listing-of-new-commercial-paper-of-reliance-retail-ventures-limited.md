## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 245 crore (49,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on private placement basis. The instruments are rated CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ with a 3-month maturity period.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 245 crore (49,000 units)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,715.50 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730143 (RRVL30925)
- ISIN: INE929O14EA8
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Issuing & Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 3, 2025
- Listing Date: September 4, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term debt instrument listing. Provides additional short-term funding avenue for Reliance Retail Ventures Limited with no effect on equity markets or broader trading operations.
