## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debentures of MAGNITE DEVELOPERS PRIVATE LIMITED (Code: MDPL-10%-31-3-27-PVT, ISIN: INE0M1Q07023, Scrip Code: 974800) effective August 5, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date for interest payment and redemption purposes.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to Non-Convertible Debentures of MAGNITE DEVELOPERS PRIVATE LIMITED
- Record date fixed as August 5, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Trading members advised not to deal in these debentures from August 5, 2025
- Notice issued under DR-688/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. Standard procedure for debenture trading suspension around record dates.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from August 5, 2025
- Trading members required to take note of the suspension
- Compliance with corporate action procedures for debt securities

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 1, 2025
- **Record Date**: August 5, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Start**: August 5, 2025
- **Purpose Date**: August 5, 2025 (for redemption and interest payment)

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this affects only holders of specific debentures of MAGNITE DEVELOPERS PRIVATE LIMITED. The suspension is temporary and procedural, related to corporate action processing. No broader market implications expected.
