## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Amanta Healthcare Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544502, symbol AMANTA, and ISIN number INE084K01015. The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Amanta Healthcare Limited
- Scrip Code: 544502
- Symbol: AMANTA
- ISIN: INE084K01015
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

No regulatory changes specified in this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage pending the separate notice with listing date details.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: September 5, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

This is a routine new listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. The listing will provide investors access to Amanta Healthcare Limited equity shares on BSE once trading commences.
