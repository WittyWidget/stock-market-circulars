## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debentures of LENDINGKART FINANCE LIMITED effective October 10, 2025. The suspension is implemented due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for debenture ISIN: INE090W07600 (Code: 975166)
- Debenture series: LFL-10.69%-XIRR-25-10-25-PVT
- Record date set for October 10, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- No dealings permitted from October 10, 2025
- Notice Reference: DR-733/2025-2026
- Notice Number: 20251008-20

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational suspension implemented as per existing debt market regulations governing record date procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from October 10, 2025
- Members should inform clients holding or interested in these debentures about the trading suspension
- No buy or sell orders should be accepted for ISIN INE090W07600 from the suspension date
- Trading members must ensure compliance with the no-dealings restriction

## Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| October 8, 2025 | Circular issued |
| October 10, 2025 | Record date for redemption and interest payment |
| October 10, 2025 | Trading suspension effective date |

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Limited to holders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is temporary and linked to corporate action processing.

**Investor Impact:** Debenture holders will not be able to trade these securities from October 10, 2025. Investors should plan their trading activities accordingly before the suspension date.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to update their systems to block trading in this ISIN. Brokers should communicate the suspension to affected clients to avoid order placement attempts.

**Financial Impact:** Interest rate of 10.69% XIRR indicates existing investors will receive their final interest payment along with redemption proceeds as per the record date.
