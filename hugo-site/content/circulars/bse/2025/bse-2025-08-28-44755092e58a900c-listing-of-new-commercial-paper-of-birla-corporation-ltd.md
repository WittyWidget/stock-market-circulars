## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Birla Corporation Ltd on private placement basis. The issue comprises 1000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 50 crores, with CRISIL A1+ rating and maturity period of approximately 3 months.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Quantity: 1000 units (total Rs. 50 crores)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,531 per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- ISIN: INE340A14155
- Scrip Code: 730101
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 26, 2025
- Listing Date: August 28, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 24, 2025
- Trading Effective: August 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing on private placement basis. The A1+ rating indicates good creditworthiness. Standard 3-month tenure commercial paper for corporate funding needs.
