## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 50 crore issued by Birla Group Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective August 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper quantity: 1,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,160.50 per unit (face value Rs. 5,00,000)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 730067 (BGHP210825)
- ISIN: INE09OL14HS8 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 22, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 22, 2025
- Redemption date: November 20, 2025
- Maturity period: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness of the issuer.
