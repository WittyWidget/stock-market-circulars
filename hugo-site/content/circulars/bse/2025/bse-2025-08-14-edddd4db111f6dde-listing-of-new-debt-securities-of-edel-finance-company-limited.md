## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Edel Finance Company Limited issued on private placement basis, effective August 14, 2025. The securities will trade in dematerialized form under ISIN INE836K07312.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2,243 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 976968, Scrip ID: EFCL300725
- Face Value: ₹1,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: ₹96,052 per security
- Date of Allotment: August 13, 2025
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A/STABLE
- Redemption Date: October 30, 2028

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form
- Tick size for trading is 1 paise
- Trading members should refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details

## Important Dates

- **Listing Effective Date**: August 14, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: August 13, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: October 30, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis. Only affects debt segment trading with specific securities for institutional investors.
