## Summary

BSE has admitted three new trading members to the exchange effective October 20, 2025. The new members include Bondnest Capital India Securities Private Limited (Member No. 6963) for Debt segment, Tecstox Securities Private Limited (Member No. 6964) for Cash and Equity Derivatives segments, and Siddhartha Commodities Private Limited (Member No. 5175) for Equity Derivatives segment.

## Key Points

- Three new trading members admitted to BSE as of October 20, 2025
- Bondnest Capital India Securities Private Limited (Member No. 6963) - Debt segment - New Membership
- Tecstox Securities Private Limited (Member No. 6964) - Cash/Equity Derivatives segments - New Membership
- Siddhartha Commodities Private Limited (Member No. 5175) - Equity Derivatives segment - New Membership
- All members admitted subject to BSE Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations
- Notice Number: 20251020-32
- Notice Category: Others - General Segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative notice regarding new member admissions.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements for existing trading members. The new members must comply with all applicable BSE Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations as per their admission terms.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 20, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 20, 2025 (admission date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative notice with no impact on market operations or existing trading members.

**Operational Impact**: None for existing members. The addition of new trading members may contribute to increased market liquidity and participation across Debt, Cash, and Equity Derivatives segments.

**Contact**: For clarifications, trading members may contact membership.ops@bseindia.com (Amit Kadam, Assistant Vice President, Membership Department).
