## Summary

Goel Construction Company Limited (Exchange ticker: 544504) will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at the open of Wednesday, September 10, 2025, following its listing on BSE on September 9, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company listing effective Tuesday, September 9, 2025
- Index addition effective Wednesday, September 10, 2025 at market open
- Exchange ticker: 544504
- Addition included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - routine index addition following standard procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Standard index tracking and portfolio adjustments may be required for index-linked funds.

## Important Dates

- **September 9, 2025**: Company listing effective date
- **September 10, 2025**: Index addition effective at market open

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine addition to the SME IPO index following the company's listing. Index fund managers tracking the BSE SME IPO Index may need to adjust their portfolios to include the new constituent.
