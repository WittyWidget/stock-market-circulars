## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of SHLOKKA DYES LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544582) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective November 4, 2025. This follows an earlier notice dated October 16, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: SHLOKKA DYES LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544582
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Tuesday, November 4, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251017-2
- Notice Date: Friday, October 17, 2025
- Category: Company related (SME Segment)

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for SHLOKKA DYES LIMITED will change from Trade for Trade settlement to Rolling settlement. The shares will move from MT Group to M Group classification on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members should note the segment transfer and adjust their trading systems accordingly
- All trades in SHLOKKA DYES LIMITED shares after November 4, 2025 must be executed in the Rolling segment
- Trading Members may contact Mr. Anurag Jain (Tel: 022-2272 8822) for further details or clarifications

## Important Dates

- **October 16, 2025**: Initial notice (No. 220251016-39) issued
- **October 17, 2025**: Current notice issued
- **November 4, 2025**: Effective date of segment transfer from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment

## Impact Assessment

This segment transfer will improve liquidity and trading flexibility for SHLOKKA DYES LIMITED shares. The move from Trade for Trade (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) allows for intraday trading and settlement through the normal rolling cycle, rather than requiring compulsory delivery. This is generally a positive development indicating improved compliance or trading activity levels. Trading Members need to update their systems and processes to accommodate the new trading segment.
