## Summary

BSE has revised price bands for 21 securities effective August 13, 2025. The new price band percentages range from 2% to 10%, with most securities receiving either 2%, 5%, or 10% bands. This surveillance measure affects trading limits for these specific scrips.

## Key Points

- 21 securities affected by price band revisions
- Changes effective from August 13, 2025
- Price bands revised to 2%, 5%, or 10% levels
- Notice issued by BSE Surveillance department
- Clarifications available at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

Price band percentages have been adjusted for surveillance purposes:
- 8 securities set to 2% price band
- 7 securities set to 5% price band  
- 6 securities set to 10% price band

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must implement revised price bands from August 13, 2025
- All trading in affected securities must comply with new percentage limits
- Members can seek clarifications from BSE surveillance team if needed

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 12, 2025
- **Effective Date**: August 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The revision primarily affects smaller companies and may indicate increased surveillance due to volatility concerns. The varied percentage allocations (2%, 5%, 10%) suggest different risk assessments for each security. Impact on overall market operations is expected to be minimal given the nature of affected stocks.
