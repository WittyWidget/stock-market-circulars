## Summary

BSE has published the Demat Auction list for Settlement No. 737 dated October 17, 2025. The auction covers 100 scrips (50 pairs listed) with specific quantities ranging from 1 share to 74,441 shares. This auction is part of the standard clearing and settlement process to fulfill delivery obligations where sellers fail to deliver securities.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 737
- Settlement Date: October 17, 2025
- Total scrips listed: 100 (presented in 50 pairs)
- Highest quantity: Welcure Drug (74,441 shares)
- Other significant quantities: Jaiprakash Power (9,000 shares), Centrum Capital (20,000 shares), Blue Jet Healthcare (7,500 shares)
- Auction includes major stocks like HDFC Bank, Bajaj Auto, ITC, Tata Steel, Yes Bank, Hindalco, and others
- Quantities range from as low as 1 share to over 74,000 shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard operational circular for demat auction process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the scrips and quantities listed for auction
- Members with shortages in these scrips must participate in the auction or arrange delivery
- Auction participants must comply with BSE's demat auction procedures and timelines
- Settlement obligations must be fulfilled as per the exchange's clearing and settlement norms

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: October 17, 2025
- Settlement Number: 737
- Auction date and timing: To be conducted as per BSE's standard auction schedule for this settlement

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - The auction involves 100 scrips across various sectors. While most quantities are small, some scrips like Welcure Drug (74,441 shares), Jaiprakash Power (9,000 shares), and Centrum Capital (20,000 shares) have substantial quantities that could impact their market prices during the auction.

**Operational Impact:** Standard clearing and settlement procedure. Members with delivery shortages in these scrips will need to participate in the auction to fulfill their obligations. The wide range of scrips and quantities indicates regular settlement activity across the market.

**Investor Impact:** Shareholders of the listed companies are not directly affected unless they are trading members with pending delivery obligations. The auction ensures market integrity by addressing delivery failures.
