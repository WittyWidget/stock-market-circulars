## Summary

360 ONE MULTI ASSET ALLOCATION FUND will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform effective August 22, 2025. The fund will offer SIP, STP & SWP facilities across six different plan options.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening effective August 22, 2025
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Six scheme variants available (Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options)
- All schemes categorized as HYBRID funds
- SIP, STP & SWP facilities will be available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

Members can begin processing transactions for the specified fund schemes from August 22, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **August 22, 2025**: Effective date for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact - routine fund reopening providing additional investment options for investors through BSE StAR MF Platform. No significant market or operational changes expected.
