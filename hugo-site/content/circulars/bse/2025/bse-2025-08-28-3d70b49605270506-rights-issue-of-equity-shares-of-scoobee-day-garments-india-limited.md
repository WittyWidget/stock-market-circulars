## Summary

SCOOBEE DAY GARMENTS (INDIA) LIMITED has fixed the record date for its rights issue of equity shares. The company will issue 1 equity share of Rs 10/- each at a premium of Rs 80/- per share for every 3 equity shares held. Trading will be conducted on ex-rights basis from September 2, 2025.

## Key Points

- Rights ratio: 1:3 (1 new share for every 3 existing shares held)
- Face value: Rs 10/- per share
- Premium: Rs 80/- per share
- Total issue price: Rs 90/- per share
- Entire issue price payable on application
- Company code: 531234

## Corporate Action Details

- **Purpose**: Issue of equity shares on rights basis
- **Entitlement**: 1 equity share for every 3 equity shares held
- **Payment**: Full issue price of Rs 90/- payable on application
- **Settlement**: Dematerialised securities under rolling settlement

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: September 2, 2025
- **Ex-Rights Date**: September 2, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-706/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

- Existing shareholders entitled to subscribe to new shares at predetermined price
- Trading will reflect ex-rights pricing from September 2, 2025
- Potential dilution effect for non-participating shareholders
- Capital raising initiative for company expansion or debt reduction
