## Summary

BSE has issued a circular regarding the applicability of Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective July 29, 2025. The circular identifies securities newly shortlisted for ST-ASM, those continuing in the framework with stage changes, and provides a consolidated list of all securities currently under ST-ASM.

## Key Points

- 4 securities newly shortlisted for Short Term 5/15/30 Days ASM Framework
- No securities moving to higher or lower ASM stages
- No securities moving out of ST-ASM Framework
- Consolidated list includes 21+ securities across various ASM stages
- Several SME scrips included in the framework
- Some securities marked with special notations for NSE alignment

## Regulatory Changes

- Implementation of Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure on identified securities
- Securities subject to enhanced monitoring and potential trading restrictions
- Framework includes different stages (I, II, III) with varying levels of surveillance
- Special treatment for SME scrips and T+0 scrips

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must adhere to ST-ASM framework requirements for listed securities
- Enhanced due diligence and monitoring for securities under surveillance
- Compliance with specific trading parameters and restrictions as applicable
- Regular review and assessment of securities performance under the framework

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: July 29, 2025 - ST-ASM framework implementation for newly shortlisted securities

## Impact Assessment

- **Trading Impact**: Securities under ST-ASM may face trading restrictions or enhanced monitoring
- **Market Participants**: Investors and traders need to be aware of surveillance measures affecting these securities
- **Liquidity**: Potential impact on liquidity for securities under enhanced surveillance
- **Risk Management**: Enhanced risk assessment required for affected securities
- **SME Segment**: Special focus on SME scrips with additional oversight measures
