## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd (BHEL) on its debt segment. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each, totaling Rs. 500 crores (10,000 units).

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 10,000 units (total value Rs. 500 crores)
- Scrip Code: 729939
- Scrip ID: BHEL300725
- ISIN: INE257A14AA1
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,95,207.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading members must follow standard debt segment procedures

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 30, 2025
- Trading Commencement: July 30, 2025
- Maturity/Redemption Date: September 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The securities provide short-term funding for BHEL operations with a maturity period of approximately 2 months. Trading will be restricted to institutional and qualified investors due to high denomination requirements.
