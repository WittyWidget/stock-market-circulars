## Summary

India Infrastructure Finance Company Limited has listed new debt securities worth Rs. 1,560 crores on BSE's debt segment. The securities carry a 6.99% annual interest rate and have a 5-year tenure until July 31, 2030.

## Key Points

- Securities issued on private placement basis with quantity of 1,56,000 units
- Face value and issue price: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE AAA/Stable and IND AAA/Stable
- ISIN: INE787H08212, Scrip Code: 976973
- Date of allotment: July 31, 2025
- Trading only in dematerialized form with tick size of 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number INE787H08212
- Tick size compliance of 1 paise must be maintained

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 31, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Annually from July 31, 2026 to July 31, 2030
- **Redemption Date**: July 31, 2030
- **Trading Commencement**: August 1, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing. The AAA credit rating indicates high creditworthiness. The securities provide institutional investors with a fixed-income investment option in infrastructure financing sector. No put/call options available, indicating standard debt instrument structure.
