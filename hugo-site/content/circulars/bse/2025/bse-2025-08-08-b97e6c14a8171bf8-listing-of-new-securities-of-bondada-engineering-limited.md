## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 15,66,000 new equity shares of Bondada Engineering Limited (Scrip Code: 543971) effective from Monday, August 11, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 15,66,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at premium of Rs. 349.20/-
- Issue price: Rs. 351.20/- per share
- Allotment date: May 31, 2025
- Distribution numbers: 110027486 to 111593485
- ISIN: INE0Q8P01029
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are permitted for trading on the Exchange.

## Important Dates

- **Trading commencement**: August 11, 2025
- **Allotment date**: May 31, 2025
- **Lock-in period expires**: February 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a standard listing of shares converted from warrants. The lock-in period until February 2026 ensures these shares cannot be immediately traded freely, providing stability to the stock price.
