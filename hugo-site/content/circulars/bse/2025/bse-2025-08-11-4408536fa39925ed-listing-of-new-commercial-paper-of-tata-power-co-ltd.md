## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Tata Power Co. Ltd on private placement basis. The issue consists of 10,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 500 crores, with a 3-month tenure and CRISIL A1+ credit rating.

## Key Points

- Issue size: Rs. 500 crores (10,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,727.50 per unit (face value Rs. 5 lakh)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip code: 730000, ISIN: INE245A14KB3
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 11, 2025
- Redemption date: November 10, 2025
- Trading effective: August 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding avenue for Tata Power and investment option for institutional investors in high-rated corporate debt.
