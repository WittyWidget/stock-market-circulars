## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 250 crore issued by Network 18 Media & Investments Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5 lakh denomination each (5,000 units)
- Total issue size: Rs. 250 crore
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,679 per unit (face value Rs. 5 lakh)
- Maturity: December 10, 2025 (91 days)
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- ICICI Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applicable

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 10, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: September 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding for Network 18 Media & Investments Limited and offers institutional investors a rated investment option with 91-day maturity.
