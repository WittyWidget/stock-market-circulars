## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 100 crore issued by Sharekhan Limited on private placement basis. The instruments have a face value of Rs. 5 lakh each, with 2,000 units listed under scrip code 729991.

## Key Points

- Total issue size: Rs. 100 crore (2,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,873.97 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- HDFC Bank Ltd serves as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading limited to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Securities must be traded under ISIN INE211H14AD7

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 8, 2025
- Listing Date: August 11, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 6, 2025
- Maturity Period: Approximately 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding for Sharekhan Limited and offers investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
