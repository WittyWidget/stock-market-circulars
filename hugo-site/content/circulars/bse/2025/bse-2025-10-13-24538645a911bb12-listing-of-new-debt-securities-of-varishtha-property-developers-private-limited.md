## Summary

BSE has listed and admitted to trading 700 new zero-coupon debt securities issued by Varishtha Property Developers Private Limited on a private placement basis. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment effective October 13, 2025, under scrip code 974848 and ISIN INE0PO508018. These are cumulative securities with a face value of Rs. 100,000 each, rated ICRA BBB/Stable, maturing on November 16, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 700 securities
- Scrip Code: 974848
- Scrip ID: VPDPLZC28
- Detail Name: VPDPL-ZC-16-11-28-PVT
- ISIN: INE0PO508018 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Market Lot: 1
- Credit Rating: ICRA BBB/Stable
- Interest Rate: 0% per annum (zero-coupon cumulative structure)
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE0PO508018
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- Any clarifications should be directed to BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: October 9, 2025
- Listing Date: October 13, 2025
- Date of Payment of Interest: November 16, 2028 (cumulative)
- Date of Redemption: November 16, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The private placement involves a relatively small quantity (700 securities) of zero-coupon debt instruments from a private limited company. The securities are rated investment grade (ICRA BBB/Stable) and have a three-year tenure. Impact is limited to debt market participants interested in private placement securities and does not affect broader equity markets or retail investors.
