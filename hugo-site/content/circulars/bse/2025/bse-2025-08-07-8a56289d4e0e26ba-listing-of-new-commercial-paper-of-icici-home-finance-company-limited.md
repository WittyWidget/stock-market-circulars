## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth ₹285 crores issued by ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED on private placement basis. The commercial paper has a face value of ₹5 lakh per unit with 5,700 units issued, carrying CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of ₹5,00,000 face value each with 5,700 units issued
- Total issue size: ₹285 crores (5,700 × ₹5,00,000)
- Issue price: ₹4,92,800.50 per unit
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 729978, Scrip ID: IHFC70825
- ISIN: INE071G14GZ7
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of ₹5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Market lot size is 1 unit

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 7, 2025
- **Listing Date**: August 7, 2025 (effective from today)
- **Redemption Date**: November 6, 2025
- **Tenure**: 3 months (91 days)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term funding for ICICI Home Finance Company Limited and offers institutional investors a 3-month investment option with high credit ratings. The listing expands the debt market options available on BSE but does not affect broader market operations.
