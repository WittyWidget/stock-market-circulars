## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis, effective from September 10, 2025. Two series of commercial paper have been admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Two new commercial paper series listed for Bajaj Financial Securities Limited
- Series 1: 2000 securities of Rs.5 lakh each, maturing on September 29, 2025
- Series 2: 6000 securities of Rs.5 lakh each, maturing on December 9, 2025
- Both series rated CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+
- ICICI Bank Limited is the issuing and paying agent
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size is 1 paise for both securities
- Securities allotted on September 9, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 9, 2025
- **Listing Date**: September 10, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**: 
  - Series 1 (BFSL3925): September 29, 2025
  - Series 2 (BFSL90925): December 9, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of commercial paper securities. The listing provides additional short-term debt instruments for institutional investors and enhances liquidity options in the debt segment.
