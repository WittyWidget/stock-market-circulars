## Summary

MSAFE EQUIPMENTS LIMITED has filed its Draft Offer Document on September 05, 2025, for SME IPO listing on BSE. The document is available for public review and comments for a mandatory 21-day period as per ICDR Regulations.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by MSAFE EQUIPMENTS LIMITED on September 05, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days for public comments
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public can submit comments on the Draft Offer Document within 21 days from notice date
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document hosted on www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document filing date: September 05, 2025
- Notice date: September 08, 2025
- Comment submission deadline: September 29, 2025 (21 days from notice)

## Impact Assessment

This filing enables MSAFE EQUIPMENTS LIMITED to proceed with its SME IPO process on BSE. The public comment period is a regulatory requirement that allows market participants to review and provide feedback on the company's disclosures before the IPO proceeds.
