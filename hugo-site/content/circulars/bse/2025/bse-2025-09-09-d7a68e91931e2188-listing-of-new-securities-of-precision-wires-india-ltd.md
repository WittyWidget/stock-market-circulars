## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 13,83,000 new equity shares of Precision Wires India Ltd (Scrip Code: 523539) issued on preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares are converted from warrants and will commence trading from September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- 13,83,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at premium of Rs. 150/-
- Issue price: Rs. 151.00 per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 178657976 to 180040975
- ISIN: INE372C01037

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for preferential issue shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability for trading
- Standard listing and trading procedures to be followed
- Lock-in provisions must be maintained as specified

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 29, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: September 10, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: March 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of preferential shares with standard lock-in period. The shares will increase the company's tradeable equity base after the lock-in period expires.
