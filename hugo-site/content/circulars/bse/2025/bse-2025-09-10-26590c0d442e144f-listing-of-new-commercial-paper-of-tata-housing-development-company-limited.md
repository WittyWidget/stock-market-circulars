## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities worth Rs. 300 crores (6000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) issued by Tata Housing Development Company Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- denomination each
- Total quantity: 6000 units (Rs. 300 crores total value)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,543 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip code: 730170, ISIN: INE582L14IV8
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialised form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Market lot size: 1 unit

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: September 9, 2025
- Listing date: September 10, 2025
- Redemption date: December 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt listing with minimal market impact. Provides additional short-term debt instrument for institutional investors. No operational changes required for trading members beyond standard debt trading procedures.
