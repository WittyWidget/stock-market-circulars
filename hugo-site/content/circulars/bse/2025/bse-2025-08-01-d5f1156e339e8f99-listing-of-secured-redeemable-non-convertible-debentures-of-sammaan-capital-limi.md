## Summary

BSE has listed 13 series of Secured Redeemable Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) issued by Sammaan Capital Limited. The NCDs have face value of Rs. 1,000 each with varying coupon rates from 8.42% to 9.95% and different maturity periods ranging from 2 to 10 years. Total securities listed exceed 16 lakh NCDs across all series.

## Key Points

- 13 different series of NCDs with varying terms and conditions
- Face value: Rs. 1,000 per NCD across all series
- Coupon rates range from 8.42% to 9.95% per annum
- Multiple payment frequencies: Annual, Monthly, and Cumulative
- Maturity periods: 2 years (2027), 3 years (2028), 5 years (2030), 7 years (2032), and 10 years (2035)
- Credit rating: CRISIL AA/Stable & ICRA AA/Stable
- Market lot: One NCD
- Allotment date: August 1, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- NCDs must comply with standard BSE listing requirements for debt securities
- Issuer must maintain credit rating standards
- Regular interest payments as per specified frequencies
- Adherence to redemption schedules for staggered redemption series

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 1, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: September 1, 2025 (for monthly series) / August 1, 2026 (for annual series)
- **Maturity Dates**: 
  - Series I & II: August 1, 2027
  - Series IV & V: August 1, 2028
  - Series VII, VIII & IX: August 1, 2030
  - Series X & XI: August 1, 2032
  - Series XII & XIII: August 1, 2035

## Impact Assessment

This listing provides investors with diverse fixed-income investment options across different risk-return profiles and time horizons. The staggered redemption structure in longer-tenure series (7-10 years) offers periodic liquidity. The high credit ratings (AA/Stable) indicate relatively lower credit risk, making these suitable for conservative debt investors seeking regular income.
