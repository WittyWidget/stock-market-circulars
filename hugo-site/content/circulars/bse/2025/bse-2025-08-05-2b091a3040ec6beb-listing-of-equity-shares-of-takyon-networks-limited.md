## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of TAKYON NETWORKS LIMITED will be listed and admitted to trading on the exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544471 and symbol TAKYON with ISIN number INE14OX01013.

## Key Points

- Company Name: TAKYON NETWORKS LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544471
- Symbol: TAKYON
- ISIN Number: INE14OX01013
- Listing status: To be listed in due course
- Separate notice will provide listing date and security details

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this time.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: August 5, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice
- Security details: To be provided in separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact for investors interested in TAKYON NETWORKS LIMITED. The announcement provides advance notice to trading members and market participants about the upcoming listing, allowing them to prepare for trading activities once the shares are officially listed.
