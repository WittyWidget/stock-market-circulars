## Summary

BSE has listed 5.36 million new equity shares of Niraj Cement Structurals Limited (Scrip Code: 532986) issued through preferential allotment pursuant to conversion of warrants. Trading commences on August 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- 5,360,000 equity shares of Rs. 10 each issued at Rs. 53 premium of Rs. 43
- Issued to both promoter and non-promoter entities on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 54334341 to 59694340
- ISIN: INE368I01016

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities listing
- Lock-in provisions apply as per SEBI regulations
- Standard preferential allotment compliance completed

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: March 21, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 5, 2025
- Lock-in expiry for 2.56M shares: February 4, 2027
- Lock-in expiry for 2.8M shares: February 4, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Moderate dilution impact with significant lock-in periods limiting immediate tradeable float. The preferential allotment indicates capital raising for business expansion, with staggered lock-in providing gradual liquidity release over 2-year period.
