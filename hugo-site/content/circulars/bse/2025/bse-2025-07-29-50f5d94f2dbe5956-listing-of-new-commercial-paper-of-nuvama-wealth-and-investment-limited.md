## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited on private placement basis. The securities have a face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each with 3,000 units totaling Rs. 150 crores, maturing on October 27, 2025.

## Key Points

- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 3,000 units (Rs. 150 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,90,822.50 per unit
- Scrip code: 729922, ISIN: INE523L14AE5
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard commercial paper listing process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- ICICI Bank Ltd acts as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: July 28, 2025
- Listing date: July 29, 2025
- Redemption date: October 27, 2025
- Maturity period: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The securities provide short-term funding for Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited and offer investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
