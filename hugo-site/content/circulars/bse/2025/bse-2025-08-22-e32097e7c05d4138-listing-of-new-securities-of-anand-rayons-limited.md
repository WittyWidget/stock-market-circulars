## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 176,666 new equity shares of Anand Rayons Limited (Scrip Code: 542721) effective from Monday, August 25, 2025. These shares were issued at Rs. 78 per share to non-promoters on preferential basis through conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 176,666 equity shares of Rs. 10 face value issued at premium of Rs. 68
- Issue price: Rs. 78.00 per share
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 20831722 to 21008387
- Date of allotment: June 9, 2025
- ISIN: INE02GA01012

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability for trading
- Lock-in period compliance required until February 28, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Trading commencement**: August 25, 2025
- **Allotment date**: June 9, 2025
- **Lock-in period ends**: February 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected given the small number of shares (176,666) and routine nature of warrant conversion. The lock-in period until February 2026 ensures these shares cannot be traded immediately, reducing any potential dilution effect.
