## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited worth Rs. 75 crores issued on private placement basis. The securities will trade in dematerialized form with effect from August 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Issue size: 1,500 commercial papers of Rs. 5 lakh each (total Rs. 75 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,715.50 per security (face value Rs. 5,00,000)
- Scrip code: 730117, Scrip ID: DCBL28825
- ISIN: INE755K14FO3
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issuing and paying agent: IDBI Bank Ltd
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denominations of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities to be traded under specified ISIN number

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 28, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 29, 2025
- Redemption date: November 27, 2025 (3-month maturity)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The securities provide institutional investors with a 3-month investment option backed by Dalmia Cement's strong credit rating.
