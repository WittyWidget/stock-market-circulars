## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of SHLOKKA DYES LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in the SME segment. The company has been assigned scrip code 544582, trading symbol SHLOKKA, and ISIN number INE1EUB01016. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: SHLOKKA DYES LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544582
- Trading Symbol: SHLOKKA
- ISIN: INE1EUB01016
- Segment: SME
- Listing to occur in due course with separate notice for exact date

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed of the upcoming listing and should prepare their systems to facilitate trading in these securities once the listing date is announced.
