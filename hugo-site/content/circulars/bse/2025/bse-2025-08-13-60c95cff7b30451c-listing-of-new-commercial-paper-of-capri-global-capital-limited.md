## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Capri Global Capital Limited issued on private placement basis. The securities are worth Rs. 25 crores (500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) with CRISIL A1+ credit rating and will mature on March 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- 500 Commercial Paper units of Rs. 5,00,000 each listed on BSE Debt segment
- Issue price: Rs. 4,77,395.50 per unit (face value Rs. 5,00,000)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+ 
- ISIN: INE180C14565, Scrip Code: 730017
- YES BANK LIMITED appointed as Issuing and Paying agent
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 13, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 13, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides additional short-term funding avenue for Capri Global Capital Limited and investment option for institutional investors in the debt segment.
