## Summary

BSE announces that FRIEZA 06 2025 (ISIN: INE26SJ15017, Code: 976876) has fixed November 1, 2025 as the record date for part redemption of Pass Through Certificates (PTCs) and payment of interest. Trading in these PTCs will be conducted with a reduced face value of Rs. 0.7476 per PTC effective from October 31, 2025 (Settlement No. DR-747/2025-2026).

## Key Points

- Company: FRIEZA 06 2025
- Security Type: Pass Through Certificates (PTCs) with 9.90% coupon maturing December 17, 2026
- ISIN: INE26SJ15017
- BSE Code: 976876
- Record Date: November 1, 2025
- Purpose: Part redemption of PTCs and payment of interest
- New Face Value: Rs. 0.7476 per PTC
- Effective Date: October 31, 2025
- Settlement Number: DR-747/2025-2026
- Trading Members are informed to note the face value reduction

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure that trading in FRIEZA 06 2025 PTCs reflects the reduced face value of Rs. 0.7476 per PTC from the effective date
- All transactions from October 31, 2025 onwards must be conducted with the new reduced face value
- Trading Members should update their systems and inform clients about the face value reduction

## Important Dates

- **October 27, 2025**: Notice date
- **October 31, 2025**: Effective date for trading with reduced face value (Settlement DR-747/2025-2026)
- **November 1, 2025**: Record date for part redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - This corporate action affects holders of FRIEZA 06 2025 PTCs in the debt segment. The face value reduction from the original value to Rs. 0.7476 per PTC indicates partial redemption.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - PTC holders on record date will receive interest payment and partial redemption proceeds. Post-redemption, investors will hold securities with lower face value.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to update systems to reflect the new face value for accurate pricing and settlement from the effective date.
