## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Sharekhan Limited worth Rs. 45 crores (9,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper quantity: 9,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total value: Rs. 45 crores
- Scrip Code: 730183, Scrip ID: SL90925
- ISIN Number: INE211H14AE5
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,495.91 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 9, 2025
- Listing Date: September 10, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 9, 2025
- Maturity Period: 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The securities provide short-term funding option for Sharekhan Limited and investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt market segment.
