## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of NUVAMA WEALTH FINANCE LIMITED (Code: NWFL-NIFTY-25-8-25-PVT, ISIN: INE918K07IT4) effective August 8, 2025, due to the company's debenture redemption with the same record date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to NUVAMA WEALTH FINANCE LIMITED debentures
- Debenture Code: NWFL-NIFTY-25-8-25-PVT
- ISIN: INE918K07IT4 (973960)
- Purpose: Redemption of Non-Convertible Debentures
- Trading Members advised not to deal in these debentures

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes - standard procedure for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease dealing in the specified debentures from August 8, 2025
- Trading Members must take note of the suspension notice
- No trading permitted in the affected debentures during suspension period

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: August 8, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: August 8, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as suspension affects only specific debentures of NUVAMA WEALTH FINANCE LIMITED. Debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds as per company's redemption schedule. No broader market implications expected.
