## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in commercial paper (ISIN: INE0NDH14064) issued by Nexus Select Trust, effective August 7, 2025, due to the instrument's maturity on August 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper scrip code: 729368
- ISIN: INE0NDH14064
- Maturity date: August 8, 2025
- Trading suspension effective: August 7, 2025
- Issuer: Nexus Select Trust

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational notice for instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified commercial paper from August 7, 2025
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension

## Important Dates

- **August 7, 2025**: Trading suspension becomes effective
- **August 8, 2025**: Commercial paper maturity/redemption date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only one commercial paper issue approaching natural maturity. Standard operational procedure for debt securities reaching redemption date.
