## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of EARLYSALARY SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED worth Rs. 155 crores issued on private placement basis, effective from August 1, 2025. The securities carry a 10.70% interest rate and mature on March 5, 2027.

## Key Points

- New debt securities of EarlySalary Services Private Limited listed on BSE Debt segment
- Issue size: 15,500 securities with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each (total Rs. 155 crores)
- Interest rate: 10.70% per annum with monthly interest payments
- Credit rating: CARE A-/Stable
- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Security Details

- **Scrip Code:** 976976
- **Scrip ID:** 1070ESPL27
- **ISIN:** INE01YL07383
- **Face Value:** Rs. 1,00,000
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 1,00,000
- **Market Lot:** 1

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** July 30, 2025
- **Listing Date:** August 1, 2025
- **Interest Payment:** Monthly from September 5, 2025 to March 5, 2027
- **Redemption Date:** March 5, 2027

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum on BSE website for further details
- For clarifications, contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides EarlySalary Services with additional funding through private placement, while offering institutional investors a medium-term investment option with monthly interest payments.
