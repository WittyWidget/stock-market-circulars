## Summary

BSE has announced the transfer of ICODEX Publishing Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 544483) equity shares from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) effective September 3, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: ICODEX Publishing Solutions Limited
- Scrip Code: 544483
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Wednesday, September 3, 2025
- Segment: SME

## Regulatory Changes

Transfer from restrictive Trade for Trade settlement to more liquid Rolling settlement mechanism, allowing intraday trading and carry forward positions.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the segment change and adjust their trading systems accordingly for the new settlement mechanism.

## Important Dates

- **September 3, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer

## Impact Assessment

Positive impact for ICODEX shareholders as Rolling segment allows greater liquidity and flexibility compared to Trade for Trade segment. Trading members can contact Mr. Anurag Jain (022-2272 8822) for further details.
