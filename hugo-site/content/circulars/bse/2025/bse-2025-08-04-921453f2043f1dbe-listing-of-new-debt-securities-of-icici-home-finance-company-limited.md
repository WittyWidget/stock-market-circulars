## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED issued on private placement basis, effective August 4, 2025. The securities carry a 6.99% annual interest rate with maturity on November 1, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 35,500 securities with face value Rs. 1,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 976981, Scrip ID: 699IHFC28
- ISIN: INE071G07850
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA/Stable, ICRA AAA/STABLE
- Issue allotted on August 1, 2025
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under specified ISIN number
- Reference to Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for detailed terms

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 1, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 4, 2025
- Interest Payment Dates: Annually from November 1, 2025 to November 1, 2028
- Redemption Date: November 1, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement debt listing. The AAA rating indicates high credit quality, providing institutional investors with a secure investment option in the housing finance sector.
