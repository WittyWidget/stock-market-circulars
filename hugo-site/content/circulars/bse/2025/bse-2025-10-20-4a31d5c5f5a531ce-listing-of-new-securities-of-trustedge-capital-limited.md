## Summary

BSE has listed 33,74,428 new equity shares of Trustedge Capital Limited (Scrip Code: 532056) issued on a rights basis. The shares are permitted to trade on the exchange effective from Tuesday, October 21, 2025. The shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 70/- per share (face value Rs. 10/-) and are ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Total new shares listed: 33,74,428 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Issue premium: Rs. 70/- per share
- Issuance method: Rights Basis
- Scrip Code: 532056
- ISIN: INE398H01015
- Distinctive Numbers: 6749901 to 10124328
- Trading commencement date: October 21, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed about the new securities listing and should update their systems accordingly to facilitate trading from October 21, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** October 17, 2025
- **Notice Date:** October 20, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date:** October 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market impact. The rights issue increases Trustedge Capital Limited's equity base by 33.74 lakh shares. Impact is limited to existing shareholders who participated in the rights issue and potential investors in this specific security. No broader market or regulatory implications.
