## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for Currency Derivatives segment on Saturday, August 23rd, 2025. The session is designed for trading members to test their trading applications and systems during various market conditions without any financial obligations.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, August 23rd, 2025
- Available for Currency Derivatives and Cross Currency Derivatives segments
- No new BOLT TWS release will be implemented
- Trading members can test third-party platforms, in-house systems, and ETI APIs
- No margin obligations or pay-in/pay-out requirements
- Trades will not create any rights or liabilities

## Trading Session Schedule

- **Log-in**: 10:15 AM to 11:00 AM
- **Continuous Trading (Currency Derivatives)**: 11:00 AM to 02:00 PM
- **Continuous Trading (Cross Currency Derivatives)**: 11:00 AM to 02:00 PM
- **Trade Modification (Currency)**: 02:10 PM
- **Trade Modification (Cross Currency)**: 02:10 PM

## Compliance Requirements

- Active participation by trading members is requested
- Feedback must be submitted by 3:00 PM on the same day
- Members should contact designated support teams for queries

## Important Dates

- **Mock Trading Date**: August 23rd, 2025 (Saturday)
- **Feedback Deadline**: 3:00 PM on August 23rd, 2025

## Support Contacts

- **BSE Tech Support**: 022-22728053, bse.tech@bseindia.com
- **Help Desk**: 022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500, bsehelp@bseindia.com

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine testing session with minimal market impact. The mock trading serves purely for system testing and familiarization purposes, allowing members to validate their trading infrastructure without financial consequences.
