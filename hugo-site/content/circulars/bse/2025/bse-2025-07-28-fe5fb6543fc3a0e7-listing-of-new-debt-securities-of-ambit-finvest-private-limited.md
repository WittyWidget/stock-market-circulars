## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Ambit Finvest Private Limited worth Rs. 25 crore (2,500 securities of Rs. 1 lakh each) issued on private placement basis. The securities carry 8.25% p.a. interest rate with quarterly payments and mature on July 25, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2,500 securities
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 each
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 each
- Interest Rate: 8.25% per annum
- Credit Rating: ACUTE AA- Stable
- ISIN: INE985V07108
- Scrip Code: 976947

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialised form
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum for further details

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 25, 2025
- Interest Payment: Quarterly from October 25, 2025 to July 25, 2028
- Date of Redemption: July 25, 2028
- Trading Effective: July 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing for a private placement. The securities are rated AA- by ACUTE and offer competitive interest rates for institutional investors.
