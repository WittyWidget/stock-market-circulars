## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Kalpataru Projects International Limited on private placement basis effective October 17, 2025. The issue consists of 4,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh face value each, totaling Rs. 200 crore, with a tenor of approximately 71 days maturing on December 26, 2025. The Commercial Paper carries IND A1+ credit rating and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Kalpataru Projects International Limited
- **Instrument**: Commercial Paper (Private Placement)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Total Units**: 4,000
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 200 crore
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,94,155.50 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730413
- **Scrip ID**: KPIL161025
- **Detail Name**: KPIL-26-12-25-CP
- **ISIN**: INE220B14BN8
- **Credit Rating**: IND A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Axis Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular. This is a standard listing notification for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE220B14BN8
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded on BSE Debt segment only
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 17, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 26, 2025
- **Notice Date**: October 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This listing has minimal market impact as it involves routine debt issuance by a corporate entity for short-term funding needs. The Commercial Paper is relevant only to institutional investors and debt market participants. The IND A1+ rating indicates high credit quality. The 71-day tenor suggests working capital requirements. This does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
