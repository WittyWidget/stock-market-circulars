## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 79,52,239 new equity shares of Deccan Bearings Ltd (Scrip Code: 505703) issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis. The shares will commence trading from Monday, August 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- 79,52,239 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par value
- Shares issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 2183335 to 10135573
- ISIN: INE498D01012

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must comply with lock-in provisions for the new shares
- Shares subject to different lock-in periods based on distribution numbers

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: June 28, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 18, 2025
- Lock-in expiry for 50,52,239 shares: March 31, 2026
- Lock-in expiry for 29,00,000 shares: March 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of preferentially allotted shares. The lock-in provisions ensure controlled supply in the market over the next 1-2 years.
