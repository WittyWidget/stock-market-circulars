## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of subscription facility in Tata Silver ETF Fund of Fund effective October 24, 2025. This allows investors to once again subscribe to units of the fund following an earlier suspension period.

## Key Points

- Subscription facility for Tata Silver ETF Fund of Fund has been resumed
- Effective date: October 24, 2025
- The fund invests in Tata Silver ETF as its underlying scheme
- Normal subscription operations have been restored

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes associated with this circular. This is an operational update regarding fund subscription availability.

## Compliance Requirements

- Fund houses and distributors to update their systems to accept subscriptions
- Investors can now place fresh subscription orders for the fund
- Normal subscription procedures and limits apply

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: October 24, 2025 - Resumption of subscription facility

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low to Medium. This circular primarily affects investors interested in gaining exposure to silver through the ETF Fund of Fund structure. The resumption provides investors access to the silver commodity market through a fund-of-fund vehicle.

**Operational Impact**: Medium. Fund distributors, brokers, and the Asset Management Company need to ensure their systems are updated to process subscription requests. Investors who were waiting during the suspension period can now participate in the fund.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for investors seeking silver exposure through mutual fund structure, as it restores their ability to invest in this particular fund product.
