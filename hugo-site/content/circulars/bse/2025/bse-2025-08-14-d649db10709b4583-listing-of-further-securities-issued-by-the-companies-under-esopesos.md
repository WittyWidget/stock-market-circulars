## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of further securities issued by 11 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS). These securities will be admitted for trading on the exchange effective Monday, August 18, 2025. The total number of shares across all companies is approximately 853,672 shares with varying face values.

## Key Points

- 11 companies are issuing additional securities under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Total shares being listed: 853,672 shares across all companies
- Trading commencement date: August 18, 2025
- All securities are marked as 'NA' for lock-in periods
- Face values range from ₹1 to ₹10 per share

## Company Details

**Major Issuances:**
- ICICI Prudential Life Insurance: 164,008 shares (₹10 face value)
- Aptus Value Housing Finance: 146,875 shares (₹2 face value)
- MOSCHIP Technologies: 91,908 shares (₹2 face value)
- ICICI Lombard General Insurance: 86,747 shares (₹10 face value)
- Laxmi Organic Industries: 81,368 shares (₹2 face value)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new listings
- Securities are available for trading from the specified effective date
- Standard exchange trading rules and regulations apply

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** August 14, 2025
- **Trading Effective Date:** August 18, 2025 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing with minimal market impact. The ESOP securities represent employee compensation and are typically held by employees rather than traded actively. The relatively small number of shares being listed across multiple companies suggests limited liquidity impact on individual stock prices.
