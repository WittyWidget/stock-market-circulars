## Summary

BSE announces the launch of BARODA BNP PARIBAS BUSINESS CONGLOMERATES FUND NFO with SIP facility on BSE StAR MF Platform from September 02-15, 2025. The equity fund offers 6 plan variants with tentative allotment on September 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- NFO period: September 02-15, 2025
- 6 scheme variants available (Regular/Direct plans with Growth/IDCW options)
- SIP facility available during NFO period
- Extended trading till midnight on September 15, 2025 for lumpsum purchases only
- Tentative allotment date: September 22, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - standard NFO launch procedure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Clear funds must be available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on September 19, 2025
- SIP/XSIP registration authentication required before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date
- Transactions without client fund initiation by 9:00 AM on T+1 will be cancelled
- Cheques & OTM payments acceptable subject to clear funds receipt by deadline

## Important Dates

- NFO Start: September 02, 2025
- NFO End: September 15, 2025 (extended till midnight for lumpsum)
- Clear Funds Deadline: September 19, 2025 (6:00 PM)
- Tentative Allotment: September 22, 2025
- SIP Registration Cutoff: September 15, 2025 (3:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine NFO launch. Provides additional investment option for equity investors seeking exposure to business conglomerates. Standard operational procedures apply with clear timelines for fund settlement and allotment.
