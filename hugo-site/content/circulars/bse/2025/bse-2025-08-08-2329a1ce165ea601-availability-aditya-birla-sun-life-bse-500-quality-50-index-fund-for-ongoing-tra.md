## Summary

Aditya Birla Sun Life BSE 500 Quality 50 Index Fund will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP, STP & SWP facilities on BSE StAR MF Platform effective August 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening applies to all 4 variants: Regular/Direct plans with IDCW Payout/Growth options
- All systematic investment facilities (SIP, STP, SWP) will be available
- References previous notice 20250718-19 regarding initial closure
- All variants are equity category funds with separate ISINs

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - operational update on fund availability for transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

- BSE StAR MF Platform members to note the reopening date
- Update systems to enable transactions from August 11, 2025

## Important Dates

- **August 11, 2025**: Effective date for reopening of all transactions

## Impact Assessment

Positive impact for investors seeking exposure to BSE 500 Quality 50 Index through this fund. Enables renewed access to systematic investment options and regular transactions for all plan variants.
