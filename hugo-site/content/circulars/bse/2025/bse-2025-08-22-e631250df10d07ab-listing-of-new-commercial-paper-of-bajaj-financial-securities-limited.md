## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 200 crores (4000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis. The instruments carry CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings and will mature on November 20, 2025.

## Key Points

- 4000 commercial paper units of Rs. 5,00,000 each listed on BSE Debt segment
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,171.50 per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+
- ISIN: INE01C314CK0, Scrip Code: 730072
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent
- Trading only in dematerialized form with tick size of 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall occur in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Standard BSE debt segment trading rules apply

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 21, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 22, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The short-term nature (3-month tenure) and private placement basis limit broader market implications. The high credit ratings indicate low credit risk for investors.
