## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 72,000 new equity shares of Naapbooks Limited (Scrip Code: 543351) effective from Friday, September 5, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium to non-promoters through warrant conversion on a preferential basis.

## Key Points

- 72,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at Rs. 51/- premium
- Issued to Non Promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 10714201 to 10786200
- Issue price: Rs. 61.00/-
- ISIN: INE0GOA01018

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- Shares are subject to lock-in restrictions until March 14, 2026

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 9, 2025
- Trading Commencement: September 5, 2025
- Lock-in Period End: March 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected due to small number of shares (72,000) being listed. The lock-in period until March 2026 ensures these shares cannot be traded immediately, limiting any potential price volatility.
