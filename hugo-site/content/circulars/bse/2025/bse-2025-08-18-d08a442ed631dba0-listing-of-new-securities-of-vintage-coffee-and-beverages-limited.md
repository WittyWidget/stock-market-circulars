## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 28,00,000 new equity shares of Vintage Coffee and Beverages Limited (Scrip Code: 538920) effective from Tuesday, August 19, 2025. These shares were issued at Rs. 25/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 15/-) to non-promoters on preferential basis through warrant conversion.

## Key Points

- 28,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 15/-
- Shares issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Issue price: Rs. 25/- per share
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE498Q01014
- Distinguished numbers: 127283410 to 130083409

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities listing
- Lock-in period applicable until March 15, 2026
- All 28,00,000 shares subject to lock-in restrictions

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: April 29, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 19, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: March 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of preferential allotment shares. The lock-in period until March 2026 prevents immediate trading of these shares, limiting any potential dilution effect on existing shareholders.
