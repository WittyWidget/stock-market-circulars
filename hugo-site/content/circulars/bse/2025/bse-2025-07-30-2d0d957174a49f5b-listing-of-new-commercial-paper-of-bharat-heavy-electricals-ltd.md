## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd (BHEL) on private placement basis. The instrument consists of 10,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 500 crores, with a maturity period of approximately 2 months.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Total Quantity: 10,000 units (Rs. 500 crores total)
- Scrip Code: 729939
- Scrip ID: BHEL300725
- ISIN: INE257A14AA1
- Issue Price: Rs. 495,207.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard commercial paper listing procedure followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size of 1 paise applies
- Trading members must follow BSE debt segment guidelines

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 30, 2025
- Listing Date: July 30, 2025
- Redemption Date: September 26, 2025
- Maturity Period: 58 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides BHEL with working capital financing for approximately 2 months. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness and low default risk.
