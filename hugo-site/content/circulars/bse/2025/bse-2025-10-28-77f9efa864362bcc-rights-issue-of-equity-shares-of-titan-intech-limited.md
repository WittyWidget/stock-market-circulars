## Summary

TITAN INTECH LIMITED (BSE Code: 521005) has announced a rights issue offering 3 equity shares of Re.1/- each at par for every 2 equity shares held. The record date is set for October 31, 2025, and shares will trade on ex-rights basis from the same date. The entire issue price of Re.1/- per share is payable on application.

## Key Points

- Company: TITAN INTECH LIMITED (Code: 521005)
- Rights ratio: 3 new shares for every 2 existing shares held
- Issue price: Re.1/- per share at par
- Payment terms: Full amount payable on application
- Security type: Dematerialised securities in rolling settlement segment
- Settlement number: DR-747/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure transactions in TITAN INTECH LIMITED equity shares are conducted on ex-rights basis from October 31, 2025
- Investors wishing to participate in the rights issue must hold shares as of the record date
- Full payment of Re.1/- per share must be submitted with application

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: October 31, 2025
- **Ex-Rights Date**: October 31, 2025
- **Notice Date**: October 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Shareholder Impact**: Existing shareholders have the opportunity to acquire additional shares at par value. The 3:2 rights ratio represents significant dilution for non-participating shareholders.

**Market Impact**: The stock will trade ex-rights from October 31, 2025, which typically results in price adjustment to reflect the rights entitlement. Shareholders must decide whether to subscribe, sell rights, or allow them to lapse.

**Trading Impact**: All market participants must ensure proper handling of ex-rights trading from the specified date to maintain orderly market operations.
