## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 125 crore issued by Birla Group Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis. The securities will trade in dematerialized form with a face value of Rs. 5 lakh each and mature on November 20, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper quantity: 2,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each
- Total issue size: Rs. 125 crore
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,075 per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 730067, Scrip ID: BGHP210825
- ISIN: INE09OL14HS8
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Axis Bank Limited serves as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 21, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 21, 2025
- Redemption date: November 20, 2025
- Tenure: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by a private company. The short-term debt instrument provides additional liquidity options for institutional investors in the debt market segment.
