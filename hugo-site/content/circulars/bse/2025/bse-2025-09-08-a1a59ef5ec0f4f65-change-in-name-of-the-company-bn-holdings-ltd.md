## Summary

BN Holdings Limited has officially changed its name to BN Agrochem Limited with effect from August 26, 2025, as per the Certificate of Incorporation issued by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs. The company's CIN remains L15315MH1991PLC326590.

## Key Points

- Company name changed from BN Holdings Limited to BN Agrochem Limited
- Certificate effective date: August 26, 2025
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L15315MH1991PLC326590
- Company originally incorporated as Arihant Oil Mills Private Limited
- Certificate issued by Central Processing Centre, Ministry of Corporate Affairs

## Regulatory Changes

- Formal name change approved by Registrar of Companies
- New registered address: 217, Adani Inspire-BKC, G Block, BKC Main Road, Bandra Kurla Complex, Bandra East, Mumbai-400051, Maharashtra

## Compliance Requirements

- Company must display old name (BN Holdings Limited) along with new name for 2 years at all places where company name is required to be displayed as per Section 12 of Companies Act
- All stakeholders advised to verify latest company status on MCA website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **August 26, 2025**: Effective date of name change
- **Next 2 years**: Period during which old name must be displayed alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

- No impact on rights and liabilities of stakeholders
- Administrative change with minimal market impact
- Investors should note the new company name for future reference and documentation
