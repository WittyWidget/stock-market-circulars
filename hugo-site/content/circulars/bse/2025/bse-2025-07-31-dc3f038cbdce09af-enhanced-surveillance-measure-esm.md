## Summary

BSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework effective August 1, 2025, with 6 securities being newly added to ESM, 1 security moving to higher stage ESM, and a comprehensive consolidated list of all securities under various ESM stages.

## Key Points

- 6 securities newly shortlisted for Enhanced Surveillance Measure framework
- 1 security (RSC International Ltd) moving to higher ESM stage
- No securities moving out of ESM framework
- No securities moving to lower ESM stages
- Comprehensive consolidated list includes securities across ESM Stage I and II

## Securities Added to ESM Framework

**New ESM Securities (Effective Aug 1, 2025):**
- Aryavan Enterprise Ltd (539455)
- Bhakti Gems and Jewellery Ltd (540545)
- Deepak Chemtex Ltd# (544036) - SME Scrip
- Omax Autos Ltd (520021)
- Remi Edelstahl Tubulars Ltd (513043)
- Vashu Bhagnani Industries Ltd (532011)

## ESM Stage Changes

**Moving to Higher ESM Stage:**
- RSC International Ltd (530179) - continuing in ESM but moving to higher stage

**Moving to Lower ESM Stage:**
- NIL

**Moving Out of ESM:**
- NIL

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** August 1, 2025
- All changes to ESM framework become applicable from this date

## Impact Assessment

Securities under ESM framework face enhanced monitoring and may have trading restrictions including price bands, margin requirements, and other surveillance measures. The addition of 6 new securities and upgrading of 1 security to higher ESM stage indicates increased regulatory scrutiny for these stocks.
