## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 650,000 equity shares of Athena Global Technologies Limited (Scrip Code: 517429) issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion. Trading will commence on October 28, 2025. The shares were allotted at Rs. 87/- per share (Rs. 10/- face value + Rs. 77/- premium) and are subject to lock-in until April 29, 2027.

## Key Points

- 650,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 77/-
- Issued to promoters on preferential basis through warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution Numbers: 14061589 to 14711588
- Issue price: Rs. 87.00 per share
- ISIN: INE576B01019
- Scrip Code: 517429

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification following SEBI preferential allotment guidelines.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems to reflect the new securities
- All 650,000 shares are subject to mandatory lock-in provisions
- Shares cannot be transferred or sold until the lock-in period expires

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment:** August 11, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date:** October 28, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry Date:** April 29, 2027

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low. The preferential allotment represents additional equity capital for the company but with minimal immediate trading impact due to the lock-in period extending until April 2027. The shares were issued to promoters, indicating no dilution to public shareholders' control. The pari-passu ranking ensures equal rights with existing shares. Trading members need to update security records but no operational changes are required.
