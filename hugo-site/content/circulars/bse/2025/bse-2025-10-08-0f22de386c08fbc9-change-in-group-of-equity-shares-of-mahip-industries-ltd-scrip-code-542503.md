## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Mahip Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 542503) will be transferred to the 'M' group effective October 15, 2025. The 'M' group classification typically indicates enhanced surveillance or special trading conditions.

## Key Points

- Company: Mahip Industries Ltd.
- Scrip Code: 542503
- Action: Transfer to 'M' group
- Effective Date: October 15, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251008-18
- Notice Date: October 8, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares of Mahip Industries Ltd. will be classified under the 'M' group, which is a special surveillance segment. This classification may impose additional trading restrictions and requirements on market participants.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the group change for their trading systems and operations
- Any clarifications required can be directed to Ms. Sonali Kurhade (Cell: +91-8779628213)

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 8, 2025
- **Effective Date:** October 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The reclassification to 'M' group suggests enhanced regulatory oversight for Mahip Industries Ltd. This may result in:
- Reduced liquidity due to potential trading restrictions
- Increased scrutiny from market participants and investors
- Possible impact on investor sentiment and trading volumes
- Enhanced monitoring by exchange authorities
