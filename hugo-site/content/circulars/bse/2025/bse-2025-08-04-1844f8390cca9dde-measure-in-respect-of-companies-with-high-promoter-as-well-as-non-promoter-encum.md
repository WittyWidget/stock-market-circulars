## Summary

BSE has implemented surveillance measures for companies with high promoter and non-promoter encumbrance under Regulation 28(3) of SEBI (SAST) Regulation 2011, effective August 5, 2025. One new security has been added to the framework while no securities are moving out.

## Key Points

- Future Market Networks Ltd (533296) newly included in the surveillance framework
- No securities are moving out of the framework in this update
- Total of 6 securities currently under this surveillance measure
- Some securities may move between different surveillance frameworks (LT ASM, ESM)

## Regulatory Changes

Implementation of surveillance measures under SEBI (SAST) Regulation 2011, Regulation 28(3) for companies with high promoter and non-promoter encumbrance levels.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed companies must comply with encumbrance disclosure requirements
- Securities under this framework subject to enhanced monitoring
- Promoters and non-promoters must maintain transparency regarding encumbrance levels

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The measure affects 6 securities with enhanced surveillance due to high encumbrance levels. This framework helps protect investor interests by monitoring companies with significant pledged shareholdings by promoters and non-promoters. The consolidated list includes companies across various sectors including technology, infrastructure, and healthcare.
