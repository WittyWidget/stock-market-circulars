## Summary

BSE has issued a notice regarding the suspension of trading in debt securities of Aethon Developers Private Limited. This follows a previous notice dated August 6, 2025. Trading in the specified scrip will be suspended starting Monday, October 20, 2025, due to ongoing restructuring of the company.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension effective from Monday, October 20, 2025
- Suspension applies to Scrip Code 976099 with ISIN INE15S307015
- Reason for suspension: Company restructuring
- This is a continuation of notice number 20250806-13 dated August 6, 2025
- Notice applies to debt securities only

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative action taken by BSE in response to the issuer's restructuring situation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the suspension and cease trading in the specified scrip from October 20, 2025
- Trading Members should contact BSE officials for any clarifications or further details
- Investors holding these securities should be informed about the trading suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 15, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date:** October 20, 2025
- **Previous Related Notice:** August 6, 2025 (Notice No. 20250806-13)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** High - Complete suspension of trading eliminates liquidity for holders of these debt securities. Investors will not be able to exit their positions through the secondary market until the suspension is lifted.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members must update their systems to block trades in this scrip and communicate the suspension to clients holding these securities.

**Investor Impact:** Bondholders face liquidity constraints and uncertainty regarding the outcome of the restructuring process. The value and recovery prospects will depend on the restructuring terms.
