## Summary

Bajaj Financial Securities Limited has listed 1300 Commercial Papers on BSE Debt segment on private placement basis. The securities are issued at Rs. 4,95,150 per paper with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, carrying credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+. The instruments have been allotted on October 16, 2025 and will mature on December 10, 2025. ICICI Bank Limited acts as the Issuing and Paying agent.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 1300 Commercial Papers listed
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per paper
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,95,150 per paper
- Scrip Code: 730187
- Scrip ID: BFSL10925
- ISIN: INE01C314CQ7 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members requiring clarification may contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 17, 2025
- Date of Allotment: October 16, 2025
- Listing Date: October 17, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing of short-term debt instruments with minimal market impact. The commercial papers have a tenure of approximately 55 days and are rated A1+ by both CRISIL and India Ratings, indicating highest safety regarding timely payment. The listing provides additional short-term funding options for Bajaj Financial Securities Limited and offers institutional investors a highly-rated short-term investment avenue. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
