## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial papers issued by Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd (BHEL) worth Rs. 500 crores on private placement basis. The papers carry face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each with 58-day maturity period.

## Key Points

- 10,000 commercial papers of Rs. 5 lakhs each totaling Rs. 500 crores
- Issue price: Rs. 4,95,207.50 per paper (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- ISIN: INE257A14AA1
- Scrip Code: 729939
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- ICICI Bank as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Securities admitted to BSE Debt segment dealings

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 30, 2025
- **Listing Date**: July 30, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: September 26, 2025
- **Tenure**: 58 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by a established PSU company. The short-term nature and private placement basis limits broader market implications. The strong credit ratings indicate low credit risk for investors.
