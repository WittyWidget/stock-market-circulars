## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of AGRI XCHNG 2025 issued on private placement basis. The securities have a quantity of 112,556,075 units with face value of ₹1, carrying 12.25% annual interest rate and maturing on December 24, 2026.

## Key Points

- Securities quantity: 112,556,075 units
- Market lot size: 100,000 units
- Scrip code: 977051, Scrip ID: 1225AX25
- ISIN: INE2FAX15017
- Credit rating: ICRA A- (SO)
- Face value and issue price: ₹1.00 each
- Interest rate: 12.25% per annum
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities to be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Members can refer to placement memorandum for additional details
- Contact debt department for clarifications at specified phone numbers

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: August 20, 2025
- Interest payment dates: Monthly from August 27, 2025 to December 24, 2026
- Redemption date: December 24, 2026
- Effective trading date: August 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing. The securities provide institutional investors access to AGRI XCHNG debt instruments with competitive interest rates. No put/call options available, indicating straightforward debt structure.
