## Summary

BSE has listed additional securities issued by six companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be available for trading starting Tuesday, September 2, 2025, with no lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- Six companies have issued additional securities under ESOP/ESOS programs
- Total of 186,811 new shares across all companies
- All securities have no lock-in period restrictions
- Trading commences September 2, 2025
- Face values range from ₹2 to ₹10 per share

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing of employee stock options with minimal market impact. The securities represent employee benefit programs and typically have limited immediate trading volume. No operational or regulatory changes are involved.
