## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 250 crores issued by Tata Housing Development Company Limited on private placement basis. The instrument carries CARE A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings with a 3-month maturity period.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each (5000 units)
- Total issue size: Rs. 250 crores
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,558 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and IND A1+
- ISIN: INE582L14IU0
- Scrip Code: 730115
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applicable

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 28, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 29, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding option for Tata Housing Development Company Limited and investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
