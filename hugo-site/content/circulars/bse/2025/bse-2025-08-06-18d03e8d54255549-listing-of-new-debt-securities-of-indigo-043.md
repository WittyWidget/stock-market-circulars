## Summary

BSE has listed two new debt securities issued by INDIGO 043 on private placement basis, effective August 6, 2025. The securities are Pass Through Certificates (PTCs) with different interest rates and quantities, both maturing on December 20, 2029.

## Key Points

- Two new debt securities listed: INDIGO043-9.08%-20-12-29-PTC and INDIGO043-9.90%-20-12-29-PTC
- Both securities carry CRISIL AAA(SO) credit rating
- Face value and issue price: Rs. 10,00,000 each
- Securities tradable only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Monthly interest payment from August 20, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under specified ISIN numbers
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: August 6, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: August 1, 2025
- **First Interest Payment**: August 20, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 20, 2029
- **Interest Payment Schedule**: Monthly from August 20, 2025 to December 20, 2029

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt securities listing. The securities are privately placed with limited quantities (1,010 and 278 units respectively) and will primarily affect institutional investors in the debt market segment.
