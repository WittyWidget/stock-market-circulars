## Summary

BSE has listed two new series of Commercial Papers issued by 360 ONE Prime Limited on private placement basis. The first series consists of 3,000 papers worth Rs. 5 lakh each (total Rs. 15 crores) maturing on 13 February 2026. The second series consists of 4,000 papers worth Rs. 5 lakh each (total Rs. 20 crores) maturing on 20 February 2026.

## Key Points

- Two Commercial Paper series listed on BSE Debt segment
- Series 1: 3,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, ISIN INE248U14RZ9, Scrip Code 729963
- Series 2: 4,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, ISIN INE248U14RY2, Scrip Code 729958
- Both rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Trading in dematerialised form only with minimum denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs
- Tick size is 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent for Series 2

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: 04 August 2025
- Listing Date: 05 August 2025
- Series 1 Redemption: 13 February 2026
- Series 2 Redemption: 20 February 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The commercial papers provide short-term funding avenue for 360 ONE Prime Limited and investment option for institutional investors in the debt segment.
