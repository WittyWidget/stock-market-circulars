## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 40,000 new equity shares of Exhicon Events Media Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 543895) effective August 25, 2025. These shares were issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis through conversion of warrants at Rs. 295 per share.

## Key Points

- 40,000 equity shares of Rs. 10 face value each
- Issue price: Rs. 295 per share (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 285 premium)
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares arise from warrant conversion
- Trading commences August 25, 2025
- ISIN: INE0O8901016
- Distribution numbers: 21538001 to 21578000

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability
- Lock-in period compliance required until March 1, 2026
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: May 12, 2025
- **Trading Start Date**: August 25, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: March 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected given the small number of shares (40,000) being listed. The lock-in period until March 2026 prevents immediate trading of these shares, further limiting any potential market volatility. This is a routine corporate action following warrant conversion procedures.
