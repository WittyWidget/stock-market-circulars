## Summary

BSE has announced the live activities schedule for TANLA PLATFORMS LTD's buyback offer through the Offer to Buy - Acquisition Window mechanism. The offer is for up to 20,00,000 equity shares at Rs. 875 per share, scheduled from Tuesday, July 29, 2025 to Monday, August 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Security: TANLA PLATFORMS LTD (Symbol: TANLA, ISIN: INE483C01032, Scrip Code: 532790)
- Offer size: Up to 20,00,000 equity shares
- Offer price: Rs. 875.00 per share (Face value: Rs. 1.00)
- Market lot: 1 share
- Offer period: July 29, 2025 to August 4, 2025
- Trading session timings: 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. (Take-up on last day extends to 4:00 p.m.)

## Trading Schedule

- **Bid Offering Period**: July 29 - August 4, 2025 (9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.)
- **Bid Modification/Cancellation**: July 29 - August 4, 2025 (9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.)
- **Give-up/Take-up**: July 29 - August 3, 2025 (9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.)
- **Give-up (last day)**: August 4, 2025 (9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.)
- **Take-up (last day)**: August 4, 2025 (9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure UCC of investors is registered with correct PAN number and category
- Only investors with shares in demat form can participate through trading members
- Members must use designated BSE platforms: iBBS, RTRMS, and Extranet for file access
- Reference to Exchange Notices 20150930-1 and 20170203-29 for operational instructions

## Investor Categories

- Financial Institution (FI)

- Other QIBs (OTH)
- Other NIB (NOH)
- Company (CO)
- Individual (IND)

## Important Dates

- **Opening Date**: Tuesday, July 29, 2025
- **Closing Date**: Monday, August 4, 2025
- **Notice Date**: July 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This buyback offer provides TANLA shareholders an opportunity to sell shares at Rs. 875 per share. Trading members and custodians need to prepare systems and ensure client registrations are updated. The structured timeline allows for orderly participation across different investor categories with specific give-up and take-up windows to manage settlement efficiently.
