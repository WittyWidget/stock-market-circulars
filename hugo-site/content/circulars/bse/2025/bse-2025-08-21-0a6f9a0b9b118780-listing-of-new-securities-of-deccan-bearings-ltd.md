## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 98,64,427 new equity shares of Deccan Bearings Ltd. (Scrip Code: 505703) issued on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will be available for trading from Friday, August 22, 2025, and are subject to a lock-in period until March 31, 2027.

## Key Points

- 98,64,427 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at par value
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Trading commences August 22, 2025
- Distribution numbers: 10135574 to 20000000
- ISIN: INE498D01012

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities are available for trading
- Lock-in restrictions apply until March 31, 2027
- All shares subject to standard equity trading regulations

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 3, 2025
- **Trading Start Date**: August 22, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: March 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This preferential allotment increases Deccan Bearings' equity base by nearly 1 crore shares. The lock-in period until March 2027 provides stability by preventing immediate selling pressure from allottees. The issue at par value indicates confidence in the company's prospects while providing capital for business expansion.
