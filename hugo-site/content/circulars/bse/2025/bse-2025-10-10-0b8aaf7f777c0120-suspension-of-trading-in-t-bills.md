## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Treasury Bill (T-bill) with ISIN IN001025C054 (GS15OCT25C, Scrip Code 800648) effective October 13, 2025, as the instrument will mature on its redemption date of October 15, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in this T-bill from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- T-bill ISIN: IN001025C054 (GS15OCT25C)
- Scrip Code: 800648
- Trading suspension effective: October 13, 2025
- Maturity/Redemption date: October 15, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251010-4
- Segment: Debt
- Reference: DR-734/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational procedure for debt instruments approaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified T-bill (ISIN IN001025C054) from October 13, 2025 onwards
- Members should inform their clients holding positions in this T-bill about the upcoming maturity and trading suspension

## Important Dates

- **October 10, 2025**: Circular issued
- **October 13, 2025**: Trading suspension begins
- **October 15, 2025**: T-bill maturity/redemption date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - affects only holders of this specific T-bill instrument.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - standard procedure for maturing debt instruments. Trading members have sufficient notice (3 days) to adjust positions and inform clients. The suspension prevents trades that could settle after the redemption date, ensuring orderly market operations.
