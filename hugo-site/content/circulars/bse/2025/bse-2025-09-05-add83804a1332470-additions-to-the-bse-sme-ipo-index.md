## Summary

BSE announces the addition of two newly listed companies - Abril Paper Tech Limited (544500) and Sugs Lloyd Limited (544501) - to the BSE SME IPO Index effective at market open on Monday, September 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- Both companies were listed on BSE's SME platform on Friday, September 5, 2025
- Index addition becomes effective at market open on September 8, 2025
- Changes have been included in the end-of-day corporate action file
- Companies were referenced in previous notices 20250904-47 and 20250904-61

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced - this is a routine index maintenance activity.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index changes are automatically processed through corporate action files.

## Important Dates

- September 5, 2025: Companies listed on BSE SME platform
- September 8, 2025: Effective date for index addition at market open

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine index addition for two SME companies. The addition may increase visibility and potential investment flows for these specific stocks within the SME segment.
