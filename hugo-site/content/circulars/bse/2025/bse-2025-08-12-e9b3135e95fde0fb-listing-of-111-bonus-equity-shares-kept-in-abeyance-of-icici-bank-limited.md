## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 111 bonus equity shares of ICICI Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 532174) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares will be permitted to trade with effect from Wednesday, August 13, 2025.

## Key Points

- 111 bonus equity shares of Rs. 2/- each of ICICI Bank Limited
- Shares were previously kept in abeyance
- ISIN: INE090A01021
- Trading permitted from August 13, 2025
- Shares distributed across four distinctive number ranges

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is an administrative listing of previously declared bonus shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and permitted trading.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Wednesday, August 13, 2025 - Trading permitted
- **Notice Date**: August 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a small number of bonus shares (111 shares) of a large-cap stock. This is primarily an administrative action to list shares that were previously kept in abeyance.
