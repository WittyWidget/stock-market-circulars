## Summary

BSE has announced that Trading Member INDMONEY PRIVATE LIMITED (Member No. 6779) will change its name to "INDSTOCKS PRIVATE LIMITED" effective Thursday, August 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading Member INDMONEY PRIVATE LIMITED (Member No. 6779) changing name
- New name: INDSTOCKS PRIVATE LIMITED
- Effective date: Thursday, August 7, 2025
- All trading members have been notified of this change

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a standard administrative name change procedure for a BSE trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should update their records to reflect the new name
- Any queries should be directed to membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- **August 7, 2025**: Name change becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact as this is purely an administrative name change. Trading operations and member number (6779) remain unchanged. Other market participants may need to update their counterparty records for future reference.
