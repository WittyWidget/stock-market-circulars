## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by KEC International Ltd. on private placement basis worth Rs. 75 crores (1500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) for trading on the debt segment effective August 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (1500 units total)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,159/- per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- ISIN: INE389H14LE6
- Scrip Code: 729950
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Axis Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: August 1, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: August 1, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: October 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding option for KEC International with 90-day maturity period. No significant operational changes for trading members.
