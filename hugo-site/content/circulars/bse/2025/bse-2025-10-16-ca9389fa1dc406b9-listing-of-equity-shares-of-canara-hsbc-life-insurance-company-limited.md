## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of Canara HSBC Life Insurance Company Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company has been assigned scrip code 544583, symbol CANHLIFE, and ISIN INE01TY01017. The exact listing date and additional security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Canara HSBC Life Insurance Company Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544583
- Symbol: CANHLIFE
- ISIN: INE01TY01017
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Security details will be provided in subsequent notice

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes specified in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the upcoming listing. No specific compliance actions required at this stage pending the separate notice with listing date and security details.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 16, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice
