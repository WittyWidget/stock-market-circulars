## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Sterlite Technologies Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective August 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total Quantity: 1000 units (Rs. 50 crores total)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,236 per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip Code: 730038
- ISIN: INE089C14DP6
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialised form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- HDFC Bank Limited serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 18, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 18, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 14, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding option for Sterlite Technologies with 88-day maturity period. High credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk.
