## Summary

BSE has issued notice DR-743/2025-2026 suspending trading in six series of non-convertible debentures (NCDs) issued by Sammaan Capital Limited. The suspension takes effect from October 27, 2025, due to the company fixing the record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Six series of NCDs of Sammaan Capital Limited will be suspended from trading
- Record date fixed: October 27, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- No dealings permitted from October 27, 2025
- Notice issued by BSE Listing Operations (Corporate Actions - Debt Segment)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any deals in the listed debentures from October 27, 2025
- Members are required to take note of the suspension and adjust their trading systems accordingly
- All six ISIN codes must be blocked for trading purposes

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 23, 2025
- **Record Date:** October 27, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date:** October 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The suspension affects holders and traders of Sammaan Capital Limited's NCDs across six different series. Impact is limited to debt market participants dealing in these specific securities. The suspension is procedural in nature, related to the redemption process and interest payment, and does not indicate any financial distress or regulatory action against the company. Investors holding these debentures will receive their redemption proceeds and final interest payment as per the terms of the debentures.
