## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 200 crores (4000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) issued by ADITYA BIRLA REAL ESTATE LIMITED on private placement basis. The commercial paper carries CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ credit ratings and will mature on November 20, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (4000 units totaling Rs. 200 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,544/- per unit (discount to face value)
- Scrip Code: 730056, Scrip ID: ABREL21825
- ISIN: INE055A14KI1
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: August 21, 2025
- Allotment Date: August 21, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 20, 2025
- Tenor: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding option for ADITYA BIRLA REAL ESTATE LIMITED and investment opportunity for institutional investors. State Bank of India serves as the issuing and paying agent.
