## Summary

BSE has issued charges for trading members who failed to upload IML/BOLT TWS/BOLT PLUS terminal location information for August 2025. Non-compliant terminals attract charges of Rs 100 per day per terminal for orders placed from unregistered or incorrectly registered locations.

## Key Points

- Charges of Rs 100 per day per terminal apply for orders from unregistered locations
- Charges file for August 2025 (LCS0825.xxxx) available in Extranet EQUITY TRANSACTION FOLDER
- Amounts will be debited to General Charges Account
- Daily location compliance files (LCddmmyy.xxxx) provided via Extranet

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced - reinforcement of existing terminal registration requirements established in previous notices from 2007, 2013, and 2018.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must register all IML/BOLT TWS/BOLT PLUS terminals with BSE
- Upload terminal location details before placing orders from those locations
- Review daily compliance files from Extranet to identify non-compliant terminals
- Pay applicable charges for non-compliance

## Important Dates

- August 2025: Period for which charges are being levied
- September 10, 2025: Notice date and charges file availability

## Impact Assessment

Financial impact on trading members with unregistered terminals. Operational requirement to maintain updated terminal registration records. Enhanced compliance monitoring through daily reporting system.
