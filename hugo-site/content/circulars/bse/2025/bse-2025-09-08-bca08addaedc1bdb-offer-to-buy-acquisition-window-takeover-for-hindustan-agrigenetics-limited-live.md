## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for an Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for HINDUSTAN AGRIGENETICS LIMITED shares, running from September 9-22, 2025. The offer targets up to 11,44,052 equity shares at Rs. 54.00 per share.

## Key Points

- Security: HINDUSTAN AGRIGENETICS LIMITED (Symbol: HINDUST, ISIN: INE092301014, Scrip Code: 519574)
- Offer Size: Up to 11,44,052 equity shares
- Offer Price: Rs. 54.00 per share
- Face Value: Rs. 10.00
- Market Lot: 1 share
- Trading Period: September 9-22, 2025

## Session Timings

- Bid Offering Period: September 9-22, 2025 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- Give-up/Take-up: September 9-19, 2025 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- Give-up (last day): September 22, 2025 (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM)
- Take-up (last day): September 22, 2025 (9:15 AM to 4:00 PM)

This takeover offer provides shareholders of HINDUSTAN AGRIGENETICS LIMITED an opportunity to exit at Rs. 54.00 per share. The structured timeline and multiple investor categories ensure orderly participation in the acquisition process.
