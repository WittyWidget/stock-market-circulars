## Summary

Bank of India Mid Cap Fund will reopen for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions along with SIP, STP & SWP facilities on BSE StAR MF Platform effective August 25, 2025. This follows a previous closure and affects six different plan variants of the fund.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening effective August 25, 2025
- Available for subscription, redemption, SIP, STP & SWP transactions
- Six plan variants included: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All variants are equity category funds
- References previous Notice No. 20250730-11

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - operational resumption of previously suspended fund transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

Members should note the reopening date and ensure systems are updated to process transactions for the specified fund variants from August 25, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **August 25, 2025**: Effective date for fund reopening on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Impact Assessment

Low impact operational change affecting mutual fund platform availability. Investors who were waiting to transact in Bank of India Mid Cap Fund can resume operations from the specified date.
