## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for the Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window - Takeover) for HYPERSOFT TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED. The offer period will run from October 20, 2025 to November 4, 2025. The acquirer is offering to purchase up to 42,25,442 equity shares at Rs. 11.00 per share. Trading members and custodian members can participate through the iBBS platform during specified session timings.

## Key Points

- **Security**: HYPERSOFT TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED (Symbol: HYPERSOFT, ISIN: INE039D01014, Scrip Code: 539724)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 42,25,442 equity shares
- **Offer Price**: Rs. 11.00 per share (Face Value: Rs. 10.00)
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Offer Period**: October 20, 2025 to November 4, 2025
- **Session Timings**: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (except take-up on last day extends to 4:00 PM)
- **Platform**: iBBS (Internet Banking & Bidding System) and RTRMS

## Bidding Categories

The following investor categories can participate:
- Foreign Institutional Buyer (FII)
- Financial Institution (FI)
- Mutual Fund (MF)
- Insurance Companies (IC)
- Other QIBs (OTH)
- Other NIB (NOH)
- Company (CO)
- Individual (IND)

## Important Dates

- **Bid Offering Period**: October 20, 2025 to November 4, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: October 20, 2025 to November 3, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up (Last Day)**: November 4, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Take-up (Last Day)**: November 4, 2025 (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure UCC of investors is registered with correct PAN number and category in the Exchange UCC database
- Trading members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Members and custodians should note that if no securities are accepted, no End of Issue files will be generated in Extranet
- Members should refer to Exchange Notices No. 20150930-1 (September 30, 2015) and No. 20170203-29 (February 3, 2017) for operational instructions

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited market-wide impact as it pertains to a company-specific takeover offer. Only shareholders of HYPERSOFT TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED who wish to tender their shares in the acquisition window are affected. The offer provides an exit opportunity for existing shareholders at Rs. 11.00 per share. Trading members need to facilitate bidding through the iBBS platform for eligible clients during the specified offer period.
