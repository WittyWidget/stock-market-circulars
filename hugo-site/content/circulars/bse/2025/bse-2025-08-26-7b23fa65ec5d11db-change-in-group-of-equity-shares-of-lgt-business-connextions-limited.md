## Summary

LGT Business Connextions Limited (Scrip Code: 544489) will have its equity shares transferred from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) effective September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: LGT Business Connextions Limited
- Scrip Code: 544489
- Current segment: Trade for Trade segment (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: Wednesday, September 10, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

Transfer of equity shares from restrictive Trade for Trade segment to normal Rolling segment, allowing regular trading mechanisms.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the segment change and adjust their trading systems accordingly for the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **September 10, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer

## Impact Assessment

Positive impact for shareholders as the move from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment typically indicates improved liquidity and normal trading conditions. The change allows for regular settlement cycles instead of trade-specific settlements.
