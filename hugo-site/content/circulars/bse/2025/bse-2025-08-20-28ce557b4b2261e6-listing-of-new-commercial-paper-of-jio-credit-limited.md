## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities worth Rs. 525 crore issued by Jio Credit Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective August 20, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 10,500 units (Rs. 525 crore total value)
- Issue price: Rs. 492,660.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Scrip code: 730045
- ISIN: INE282H14048
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size is 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 19, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 20, 2025
- Redemption date: November 14, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding avenue for Jio Credit Limited and investment option for institutional investors in the debt segment.
