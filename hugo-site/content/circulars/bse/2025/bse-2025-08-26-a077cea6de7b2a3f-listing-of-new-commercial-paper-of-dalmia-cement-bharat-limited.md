## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited worth Rs. 125 crore (2,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on private placement basis. The instrument carries CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings and will mature on November 21, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (2,500 units)
- Total issue size: Rs. 125 crore
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,952.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip code: 730084
- ISIN: INE755K14FM7
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- IDBI Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 25, 2025
- Listing date: August 26, 2025
- Redemption date: November 21, 2025
- Tenure: 88 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding option for Dalmia Cement with high credit rating indicating low default risk. Trading will be limited to institutional investors given the high denomination requirement.
