## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures of Shriram Finance Limited effective August 25, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to debentures with ISIN: INE721A08AI1 (Code: 946998)
- Debenture series: SFL-J2-11%-09-09-25-PVT
- Record date: August 25, 2025
- No dealings permitted from August 25, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard procedure for corporate actions involving debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from August 25, 2025
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- Compliance with record date procedures for redemption and interest payment

## Important Dates

- **August 18, 2025**: Notice date
- **August 25, 2025**: Record date and start of trading suspension
- **September 9, 2025**: Debenture maturity date (inferred from series code)

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of the specific debenture series. This is a routine corporate action that temporarily restricts trading to facilitate proper record-keeping for redemption and interest payment processes. The suspension ensures orderly settlement of the debenture obligations without affecting the broader market or other securities of Shriram Finance Limited.
