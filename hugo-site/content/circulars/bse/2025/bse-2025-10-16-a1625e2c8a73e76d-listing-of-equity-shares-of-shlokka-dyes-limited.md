## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of SHLOKKA DYES LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in the SME segment. The company has been assigned scrip code 544582, symbol SHLOKKA, and ISIN number INE1EUB01016. The exact listing date and additional security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: SHLOKKA DYES LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544582
- Symbol: SHLOKKA
- ISIN Number: INE1EUB01016
- Segment: SME
- Listing status: To be listed in due course
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the scrip code, symbol, and ISIN number for SHLOKKA DYES LIMITED and prepare for trading once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 16, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

Low impact announcement. This is a routine listing notification for a single SME company. Trading members will be able to trade the security once the formal listing date is announced. No immediate action required beyond noting the security details for future reference.
