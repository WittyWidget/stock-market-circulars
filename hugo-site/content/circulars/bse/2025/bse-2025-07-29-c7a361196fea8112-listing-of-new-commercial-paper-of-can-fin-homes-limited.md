## Summary

Can Fin Homes Limited has listed new commercial paper worth ₹50 crore on BSE's debt segment. The instruments are issued on private placement basis with face value of ₹5 lakh each and will mature in 3 months.

## Key Points

- 10,000 commercial paper units of ₹5 lakh each listed
- Issue price: ₹4,92,727.50 per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as issuing and paying agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of ₹5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: July 28, 2025
- Trading effective date: July 29, 2025
- Redemption date: October 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The short-term debt instrument provides Can Fin Homes with working capital financing for 3 months. High credit ratings indicate low risk for investors.
