## Summary

BSE Daily Bulletin No. 142/2025-2026 dated October 27, 2025, provides information on book closure dates and record dates for various corporate actions across multiple securities. The bulletin covers NCDs redemptions, commercial paper redemptions, equity dividends, bonus issues, and right issues scheduled between October 30, 2025 and December 23, 2025.

## Key Points

- Bulletin covers corporate actions for 17+ securities across equity, commercial paper, and NCD categories
- Record dates range from October 30, 2025 to December 23, 2025
- Actions include NCD redemptions with interest payments, commercial paper redemptions, equity dividends, bonus issues, and right issues
- Trading flags indicated: RD flag 0 or 1 for different securities
- Multiple securities from Bajaj Financial Securities Limited, Infina Finance Private Limited, and IOT Utkal Energy Services Limited listed

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this bulletin.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors must hold securities on the specified record dates to be eligible for corporate actions
- Trading participants should note the trading flags (0 or 1) for affected securities
- Companies must process payments/allotments according to scheduled dates

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine daily bulletin providing standard corporate action information.
