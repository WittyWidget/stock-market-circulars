## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by HDFC Securities Limited on private placement basis. The issue comprises 6,100 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 305 crores, with a 91-day maturity period from August 12, 2025 to November 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 6,100 units (Rs. 305 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,219.50 per unit
- Scrip code: 730010 (HDFCS12825)
- ISIN: INE700G14PJ6
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 12, 2025
- Listing effective date: August 12, 2025
- Redemption date: November 11, 2025
- Maturity period: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by HDFC Securities Limited. The instrument provides short-term funding option for the company and investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
