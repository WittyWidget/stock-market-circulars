## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Navi Finserv Limited worth Rs. 25 crores on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to dealings on BSE Debt segment effective September 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each with quantity of 500 units
- Total issue size: Rs. 25 crores
- Scrip Code: 730124, Scrip ID: NFL290825
- ISIN Number: INE342T14EB1
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 476,039.50 per unit
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard commercial paper listing process followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities to be traded only in dematerialised form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Trading members to contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 29, 2025
- Listing Date: September 1, 2025
- Date of Redemption: February 26, 2026
- Maturity Period: Approximately 6 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The securities provide short-term investment option for debt market participants with high credit rating (A1+). Standard trading parameters apply with no special provisions or restrictions.
