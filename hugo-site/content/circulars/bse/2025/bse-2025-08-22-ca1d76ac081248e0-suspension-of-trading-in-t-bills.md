## Summary

BSE has issued a notice suspending trading in two Treasury Bills (T-Bills) effective August 25, 2025, as they reach maturity on their redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Two T-Bills will mature on August 25, 2025
- Trading suspension takes effect from August 25, 2025
- Notice number: 20250822-6 under category Corporate Actions - Debt segment
- Trading members must cease all dealings in the specified securities

## Securities Affected

| Scrip Code | ISIN | Particulars |
|------------|------|-------------|
| 804961 | IN002024Z222 | 364TB28825 |
| 805070 | IN002025X091 | 91TB280825 |

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the aforementioned T-Bills from August 25, 2025
- All market participants should take note of the suspension
- Standard maturity procedures to be followed

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 22, 2025
- **Effective Date**: August 25, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: August 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational notice with minimal market impact. The suspension is due to natural maturity of the T-Bills rather than any market disruption or regulatory action. Impact is limited to holders of these specific securities who need to plan for redemption procedures.
