## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on private placement basis effective August 29, 2025. The securities will trade on the BSE Debt segment under ISIN INE918K07PX1 with scrip code 976954.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2,500 securities
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 96,887
- Credit Rating: CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable
- Redemption Date: January 22, 2029
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Trading members can contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 26, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 29, 2025
- Redemption Date: January 22, 2029

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing for private placement. The listing provides additional investment options in the debt segment but does not affect broader market operations or trading practices.
