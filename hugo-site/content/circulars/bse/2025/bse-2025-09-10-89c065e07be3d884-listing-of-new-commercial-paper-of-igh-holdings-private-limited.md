## Summary

IGH Holdings Private Limited has listed new Commercial Paper securities worth Rs. 150 crores on BSE Debt segment through private placement. The securities carry high credit ratings from both CARE (A1+) and CRISIL (A1+) and will be traded in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total quantity: 3,000 units (Rs. 150 crores aggregate value)
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,568 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730181, ISIN: INE02FN14648
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing of commercial paper securities following standard BSE procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Axis Bank Limited serves as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 10, 2025
- **Listing Date**: September 10, 2025 (effective today)
- **Redemption Date**: December 10, 2025
- **Maturity Period**: 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both agencies) indicate strong creditworthiness. The 3-month tenor and private placement nature limit broader market exposure. Trading members can contact BSE debt department for clarifications on phone numbers provided.
