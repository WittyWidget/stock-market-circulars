## Summary

BSE has announced that Trading Member INDMONEY PRIVATE LIMITED (Member No. 6779) will change its name to "INDSTOCKS PRIVATE LIMITED" effective Thursday, August 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading Member Number: 6779
- Current Name: INDMONEY PRIVATE LIMITED
- New Name: INDSTOCKS PRIVATE LIMITED
- Effective Date: August 7, 2025
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes - this is purely an administrative name change for an existing trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should update their records to reflect the new name of Member No. 6779 from August 7, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **August 7, 2025**: Effective date of name change

## Impact Assessment

Minimal operational impact as this is an administrative name change. Trading member operations and member number remain unchanged. Other trading members may need to update internal records and systems to reflect the new entity name.
