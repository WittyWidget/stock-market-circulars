## Summary

BSE has updated the Enhanced Surveillance Measure (ESM) framework effective August 14, 2025. The circular includes one new security being added to ESM, one security moving to a higher ESM stage, and provides a consolidated list of all securities currently under ESM framework across different stages.

## Key Points

- Spice Islands Industries Ltd (526827) newly added to ESM framework
- Galaxy Cloud Kitchens Ltd (506186) moved to higher ESM stage
- No securities moved to lower ESM stages
- No securities removed from ESM framework
- Consolidated list shows 32+ securities across ESM Stage I and Stage II

## Regulatory Changes

- Enhanced surveillance measures applied to new securities based on market behavior patterns
- Stage progression for existing ESM securities based on continued surveillance criteria
- Framework maintains different stages (Stage I and Stage II) with varying levels of surveillance

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must comply with ESM framework requirements for affected securities
- Enhanced monitoring and reporting obligations for securities under ESM
- Adherence to specific trading conditions and restrictions as applicable to each ESM stage

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 14, 2025 - All ESM changes become effective

## Impact Assessment

- Limited market impact as only two securities affected by changes
- Enhanced surveillance may result in increased trading restrictions or margin requirements
- Investors and trading members need to adjust strategies for affected securities
- Continued monitoring framework helps maintain market integrity and investor protection
