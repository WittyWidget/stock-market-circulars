## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in commercial paper issued by HDFC Securities Limited, effective from October 21, 2025. The suspension is due to the scheduled maturity of the instrument on October 23, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of HDFC Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 729893) to be suspended from trading
- ISIN: INE700G14PD9
- Instrument identifier: HDFCSL-23-10-25-CP
- Suspension effective date: October 21, 2025
- Reason: Scheduled maturity on redemption date
- Notice reference: DR-740/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute trades in the specified commercial paper (Scrip Code: 729893, ISIN: INE700G14PD9) from October 21, 2025 onwards
- All trading members must take note of this suspension and update their systems accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 20, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: October 21, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: October 23, 2025 (implied from instrument name HDFCSL-23-10-25-CP)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine suspension for a maturing commercial paper instrument affecting a single debt security.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard procedure for debt instruments reaching maturity. Trading members need to ensure the instrument is removed from tradable securities list.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Holders of this commercial paper will receive redemption proceeds on the maturity date as scheduled. No trading disruption for other HDFC Securities instruments.
