## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 7,50,000 new equity shares of Jyoti Structures Ltd. (Scrip Code: 513250) issued through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will commence trading on September 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- 7,50,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at premium of Rs. 11.20/-
- Shares issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 1189992805 to 1190742804
- ISIN: INE197A01024

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing process for warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities listing
- Lock-in period compliance required until March 11, 2026
- All 7,50,000 shares subject to lock-in restrictions

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: June 18, 2025
- Trading Commencement: September 11, 2025
- Lock-in Period Ends: March 11, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this represents a routine listing of shares from warrant conversion. The preferential allotment increases the company's equity base by 7.5 lakh shares at an issue price of Rs. 13.20 per share.
