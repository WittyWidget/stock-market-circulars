## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by IGH Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis, effective August 13, 2025. The securities are rated CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ with a face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each.

## Key Points

- Total issue: 2000 commercial papers of Rs. 5,00,000 each (Rs. 100 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,978 per paper
- Scrip code: 730014, ISIN: INE02FN14598
- Redemption date: November 12, 2025
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Axis Bank Limited serves as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Listing date: August 13, 2025
- Allotment date: August 13, 2025
- Redemption date: November 12, 2025
- Maturity: 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by a private company. The listing provides additional short-term debt investment options for institutional investors in the BSE debt segment.
