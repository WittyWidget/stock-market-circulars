## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of further securities issued by 13 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS). These new securities will be listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange effective from Thursday, August 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- 13 companies have issued additional securities under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Total of 3,899,356 new shares to be listed across all companies
- HDFC Bank has the largest issuance with 1,807,481 shares
- All securities have face values ranging from Re. 1 to Rs. 10
- None of the securities are under lock-in period

## Company Details

**Major Issuances:**
- HDFC Bank Ltd.: 1,807,481 shares (Face Value: Re. 1)
- HDFC Life Insurance: 753,756 shares (Face Value: Rs. 10)
- ICICI Bank Ltd.: 730,674 shares (Face Value: Rs. 2)
- Star Health Insurance: 100,000 shares (Face Value: Rs. 10)

**Other Companies:**
- Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC, Capital India Finance, CG Power, Global Health, ICICI Prudential Life, Mphasis, Newgen Software, Stove Kraft, V-Guard Industries

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced - this is a routine listing notification for ESOP securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new listings
- Securities will be available for trading from August 7, 2025
- Standard trading rules and regulations apply

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** August 6, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** August 7, 2025
- **Lock-in Period:** Not applicable for any of the securities

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine ESOP issuances. The additional shares may cause slight dilution in existing shareholding percentages but represent normal corporate actions for employee compensation plans.
