## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 9,32,384 new equity shares of Trident Texofab Limited (Scrip Code: 540726) effective from Monday, August 4, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium of Rs. 60/- per share to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion.

## Key Points

- 9,32,384 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 70/- (Rs. 10 face value + Rs. 60 premium)
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE071Y01013
- Distribution numbers: 14063381 to 14995764

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and permitted trading from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: 29/05/2025 (4,50,477 shares) and 04/06/2025 (4,81,907 shares)
- Trading Commencement: Monday, August 4, 2025
- Lock-in Period: Until February 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of new securities through warrant conversion. The lock-in period until February 2026 ensures these shares cannot be traded immediately, limiting any dilution effect.
