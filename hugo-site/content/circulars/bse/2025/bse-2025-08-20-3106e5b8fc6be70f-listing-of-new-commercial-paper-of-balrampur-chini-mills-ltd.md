## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Balrampur Chini Mills Ltd. worth Rs. 100 crores (2000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) issued on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to dealings on BSE Debt segment with effect from August 20, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (2000 units total)
- Scrip Code: 730053, Scrip ID: BCML200825
- ISIN Number: INE119A14872
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 494,241.00/- per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: August 20, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: August 20, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: October 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The 71-day tenure commercial paper provides working capital financing for Balrampur Chini Mills Ltd. with strong credit ratings (A1+) indicating low credit risk.
