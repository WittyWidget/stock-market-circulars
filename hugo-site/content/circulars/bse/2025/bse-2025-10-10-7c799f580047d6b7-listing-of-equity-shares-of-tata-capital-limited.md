## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of TATA CAPITAL LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544574 with trading symbol TATACAP and ISIN number INE976I01016. The exact date of listing and further security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: TATA CAPITAL LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544574
- Trading Symbol: TATACAP
- ISIN Number: INE976I01016
- Listing status: To be listed in due course
- Specific listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of the Exchange are informed about the upcoming listing and should prepare their systems for trading in TATACAP equity shares once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 10, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice
- Security details: To be provided in subsequent communication

## Impact Assessment

This listing will add a significant Tata group financial services company to BSE's equity segment. Trading members need to await the subsequent notice for the exact listing date and complete security details before commencing trading activities. The addition of TATA CAPITAL LIMITED is expected to provide investors with exposure to a major non-banking financial company in the Tata group.
