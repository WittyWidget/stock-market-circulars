## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in non-convertible debentures (NCDs) of INDEL MONEY LIMITED (ISIN: INE0BUS07BH8, Code: 939891) effective October 24, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of these debentures. Trading members are instructed not to deal in these debentures from the specified date.

## Key Points

- Company: INDEL MONEY LIMITED
- Security: Non-Convertible Debentures (IML-9-11-25-NCD)
- ISIN: INE0BUS07BH8
- BSE Code: 939891
- Record Date: October 24, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures
- No Dealings From: October 24, 2025
- Notice Reference: DR-742/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This is a standard administrative action for debenture redemption record date compliance.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures of INDEL MONEY LIMITED from October 24, 2025
- Members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance
- The suspension applies specifically to debentures with ISIN INE0BUS07BH8

## Important Dates

- **October 20, 2025**: Circular issued
- **October 24, 2025**: Record date for redemption; trading suspension effective from this date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects only holders and traders of specific NCDs of INDEL MONEY LIMITED. No broader market implications.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems to block trades in these debentures. Investors holding these debentures should be aware of the redemption process and trading restriction.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders whose names appear on the record date (October 24, 2025) will be eligible for redemption. Secondary market liquidity for these debentures ceases from the suspension date.
