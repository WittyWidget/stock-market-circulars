## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of ICODEX Publishing Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 544483) will be transferred from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) effective September 3, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: ICODEX Publishing Solutions Limited
- Scrip Code: 544483
- Current segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: September 3, 2025
- Notice follows previous notice dated August 18, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The stock will move from restrictive Trade for Trade segment to more liquid Rolling segment, allowing for normal intraday trading and carry forward positions.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the segment change and adjust their trading systems accordingly. Contact Mr. Anurag Jain (022-2272 8822) for further details.

## Important Dates

- **September 3, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer

## Impact Assessment

Positive impact for liquidity as the stock moves to Rolling segment, allowing for normal trading mechanisms including intraday trading and carry forward positions instead of compulsory delivery-based trading.
