## Summary

Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC Limited has introduced Daily Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) facility for two index fund schemes on BSE StAR MF Platform, effective September 1, 2025. This expands investment frequency options for investors in these specific schemes.

## Key Points

- Daily SIP frequency now available for Aditya Birla Sun Life BSE 500 Momentum 50 Index Fund
- Daily SIP frequency now available for Aditya Birla Sun Life BSE 500 Quality 50 Index Fund
- Effective date: Monday, September 1, 2025
- AMC reserves right to modify scheme list and SIP features
- All other terms and conditions remain unchanged

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational enhancement to existing mutual fund investment facilities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Notice forms integral part of Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM)
- Investors must read all scheme related documents carefully
- Standard mutual fund risk disclosures apply

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: September 1, 2025 (Monday)
- Notice Date: August 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a facility enhancement for specific mutual fund schemes that provides additional investment frequency options for retail investors. Does not affect trading operations or broader market structure.
