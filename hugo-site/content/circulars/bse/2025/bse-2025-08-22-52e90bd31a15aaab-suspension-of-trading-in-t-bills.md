## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in two Treasury Bills (T-Bills) effective August 25, 2025, as they approach their redemption date. Trading members are advised to cease dealing in these specific T-Bills from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Two T-Bills will be suspended from trading: 364TB28825 (ISIN: IN002024Z222) and 91TB280825 (ISIN: IN002025X091)
- Trading suspension effective from August 25, 2025
- Suspension due to maturity on redemption date
- Notice applies to all Trading Members of BSE
- Reference number: DR-701/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational procedure for maturing debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not deal in the specified T-Bills from August 25, 2025
- All BSE Trading Members are required to take note of this suspension
- Standard debt market trading rules continue to apply for other securities

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** August 22, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date:** August 25, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date:** August 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine suspension of maturing T-Bills. Affects debt segment participants who may need to adjust their trading strategies for these specific securities. No broader market implications expected as this follows standard maturity procedures for Treasury Bills.
