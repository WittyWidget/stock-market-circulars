## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth ₹125 crore issued by Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited on private placement basis. The commercial paper carries CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ ratings with a 3-month tenure from August 25, 2025 to November 21, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper denomination: ₹5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 2,500 units (₹125 crore total)
- Issue price: ₹4,92,952.50 per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip code: 730084
- ISIN: INE755K14FM7
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of ₹5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- IDBI Bank Ltd acts as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 25, 2025
- Listing effective: August 26, 2025
- Redemption date: November 21, 2025
- Tenure: 88 days (approximately 3 months)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides Dalmia Cement with short-term funding at competitive rates given the high credit ratings. Trading will be limited to institutional investors due to high denomination requirements.
