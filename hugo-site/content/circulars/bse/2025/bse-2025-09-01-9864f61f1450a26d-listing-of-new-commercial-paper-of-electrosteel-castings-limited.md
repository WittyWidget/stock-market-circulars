## Summary

Electrosteel Castings Limited has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 50 crores (1000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) on BSE Debt segment effective September 1, 2025. The securities are issued on private placement basis with CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Issue size: Rs. 50 crores (1000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,471.50 per unit
- Scrip code: 730122, ISIN: INE086A14873
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Trading in demat form only with Rs. 5 lakhs minimum denomination
- HDFC Bank Limited as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard commercial paper listing procedure followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Minimum trading denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applies
- Standard BSE Debt segment trading rules applicable

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 29, 2025
- Listing date: September 1, 2025
- Redemption date: November 27, 2025
- Tenure: 90 days approximately

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides additional funding avenue for Electrosteel Castings Limited and offers investment option for institutional investors in the debt market segment.
