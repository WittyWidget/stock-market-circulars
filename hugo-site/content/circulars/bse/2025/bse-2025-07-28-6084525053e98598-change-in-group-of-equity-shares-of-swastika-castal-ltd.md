## Summary

BSE announces the transfer of Swastika Castal Ltd (Scrip Code: 544452) equity shares from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) effective August 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Swastika Castal Ltd
- Scrip Code: 544452
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Monday, August 11, 2025
- Contact: Mr. Parag Jain (022 2272 8685)

## Regulatory Changes

Transfer from restrictive Trade for Trade segment to more liquid Rolling segment, allowing normal trading activities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the segment change and adjust their trading systems accordingly for the new group classification.

## Important Dates

- **August 11, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer

## Impact Assessment

Positive impact for shareholders as Rolling segment provides better liquidity compared to Trade for Trade segment. Normal trading operations will resume under M Group classification.
