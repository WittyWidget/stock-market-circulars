## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in bonds of Tulsi Palace Resort Private Ltd (scrip code: 974856, ISIN: INE0PKY07014) effective August 4, 2025, following the company's notification of mandatory bond redemption.

## Key Points

- Bond suspension applies to TULSI PALACE RESORT PRIVATE LTD 10.50% bonds maturing May 24, 2026
- Scrip code: 974856, ISIN: INE0PKY07014
- Reason: Mandatory redemption event as notified by the company
- Trading members advised not to deal in these bonds

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes - standard procedure for bond redemption events.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the specified bonds
- All exchange members required to take note of the suspension
- Compliance effective from August 4, 2025

## Important Dates

- **August 1, 2025**: Notice issued
- **August 4, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **May 24, 2026**: Original bond maturity date

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this affects only bondholders of a specific private company. The suspension is procedural following the company's decision to redeem bonds under mandatory redemption provisions.
