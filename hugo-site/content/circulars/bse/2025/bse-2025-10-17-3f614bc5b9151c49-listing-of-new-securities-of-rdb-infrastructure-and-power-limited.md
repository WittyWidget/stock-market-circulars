## Summary

BSE has listed 5,00,000 new equity shares of RDB Infrastructure and Power Limited (Scrip Code: 533285) effective October 20, 2025. These shares were issued at Rs. 40.50 per share (face value Re. 1/- plus premium of Rs. 39.50) to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares and are subject to lock-in until April 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- 5,00,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at premium of Rs. 39.50 per share
- Total issue price: Rs. 40.50 per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Date of allotment: June 25, 2025
- Trading commencement date: October 20, 2025
- Distinctive Numbers: 197634001 to 198134000
- ISIN: INE245L01028
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- All 5,00,000 shares subject to lock-in period

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular. This is a routine listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the listing and permitted to trade these securities from October 20, 2025, subject to applicable lock-in restrictions.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: June 25, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 20, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: April 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard listing of additional equity shares representing warrant conversion for RDB Infrastructure and Power Limited. The 5,00,000 shares represent a small incremental addition to the company's equity capital. Lock-in restrictions until April 30, 2026 prevent immediate trading of these shares, further limiting short-term market impact. This notification is primarily informational for trading members to update their systems.
