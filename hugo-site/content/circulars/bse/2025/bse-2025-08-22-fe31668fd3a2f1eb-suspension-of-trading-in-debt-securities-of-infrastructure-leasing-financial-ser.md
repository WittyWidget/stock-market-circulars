## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debt securities of Infrastructure Leasing & Financial Services Limited (ILFSL) that are approaching maturity. The suspension is effective from August 25, 2025, for securities maturing on August 27, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to ILFSL debt securities with ISIN INE871D07RQ7
- Securities carry 9.90% interest rate and mature on August 27, 2025
- Scrip code affected: 958216
- Trading Members advised not to deal in these securities

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes. This is a routine suspension for securities approaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease dealing in the specified ILFSL debt securities
- Members required to take note of the suspension directive
- Compliance with directive DR-701/2025-2026

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 22, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: August 25, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: August 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this affects only specific debt securities nearing natural maturity. Standard procedure for debt instruments approaching maturity date to prevent unnecessary trading activity in soon-to-expire securities.
