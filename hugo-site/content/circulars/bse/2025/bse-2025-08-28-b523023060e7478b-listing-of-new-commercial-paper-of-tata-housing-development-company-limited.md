## Summary

BSE announces the listing of new commercial paper worth Rs. 50 crores issued by Tata Housing Development Company Limited on private placement basis, effective from August 28, 2025. The commercial paper has a tenure of 91 days with CARE A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- face value each (1000 units)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,583/- per unit (discount of Rs. 7,417/-)
- Scrip Code: 730102, Scrip ID: THDCL26825
- ISIN: INE582L14IT2
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Limited serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: August 28, 2025
- Allotment Date: August 26, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 25, 2025
- Tenure: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding option for Tata Housing Development Company Limited and investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
