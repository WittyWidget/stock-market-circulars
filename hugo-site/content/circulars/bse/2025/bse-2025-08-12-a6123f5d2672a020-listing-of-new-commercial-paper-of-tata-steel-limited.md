## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of TATA Steel Limited worth Rs. 95 crores (19,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective August 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 19,000 units (total value Rs. 95 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730004
- ISIN: INE081A14GB0
- Credit Rating: ICRA A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,929/-
- Allotment Date: August 11, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 7, 2025
- Issuing Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: August 12, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: August 11, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: November 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The high credit rating (A1+) indicates strong creditworthiness of TATA Steel Limited.
