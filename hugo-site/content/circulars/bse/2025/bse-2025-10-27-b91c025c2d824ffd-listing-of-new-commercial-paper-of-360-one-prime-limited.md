## Summary

360 ONE Prime Limited has listed 4000 Commercial Papers on BSE's Debt segment on October 27, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issue price of Rs. 4,91,183, and will mature on January 23, 2026. The instruments carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings and will be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- **Quantity**: 4000 Commercial Papers
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,183 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730436
- **Scrip ID**: OPL241025
- **ISIN**: INE248U14SK9
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Trading Denomination**: Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: HDFC Bank Limited
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE248U14SK9
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: October 24, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 27, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: January 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine commercial paper listing in the debt segment that provides short-term funding to 360 ONE Prime Limited. The 91-day tenor and high credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA) indicate low credit risk.

**Operational Impact**: Affects only BSE debt segment trading members who deal in commercial papers. The standard trading parameters (Rs. 5 lakh denomination, demat-only trading) align with existing debt market practices, requiring no operational changes.
