## Summary

BSE has imposed additional exposure margin on RBL Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 540065, ISIN: INE976G01028) under the Market-Wide Position Limit (MWPL) framework for October 2025. This measure is implemented to manage concentration risk and ensure market stability when securities approach their position limit thresholds.

## Key Points

- RBL Bank Limited is subject to additional exposure margin under MWPL
- Scrip Code: 540065
- ISIN: INE976G01028
- Applicable for October 2025
- Only one security listed in the annexure for this period

## Regulatory Changes

The additional exposure margin is imposed as a risk management measure under the MWPL framework. This typically occurs when the aggregate open interest in a security approaches or exceeds prescribed position limits, requiring enhanced margin coverage to mitigate systemic risk.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure adequate margin coverage for positions in RBL Bank Limited
- Enhanced margin requirements will be applicable for both existing and new positions
- Members must monitor their exposure limits and maintain sufficient collateral
- Risk management systems should be updated to reflect the additional margin requirements

## Important Dates

- Effective Period: October 2025
- Circular Date: October 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The imposition of additional exposure margin on RBL Bank has significant implications:

**For Traders and Investors:**
- Increased capital requirements for maintaining positions in RBL Bank
- Higher trading costs due to elevated margin obligations
- Potential reduction in leverage available for the security

**For Market Participants:**
- Enhanced risk management and monitoring required
- Possible reduction in trading volumes due to higher capital costs
- Need to reassess position sizing and risk limits

**Market Stability:**
- Designed to prevent excessive concentration and reduce systemic risk
- Encourages more prudent position management
- Helps maintain orderly market conditions during periods of heightened activity
