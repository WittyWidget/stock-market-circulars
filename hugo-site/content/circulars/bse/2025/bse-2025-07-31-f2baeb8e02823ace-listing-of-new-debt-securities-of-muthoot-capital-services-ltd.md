## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Muthoot Capital Services Ltd. issued on private placement basis. The securities carry a 10% annual interest rate and will be traded in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 10,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 976965, Scrip ID: 10MCSL2027
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A+/Stable
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Security Details

- **ISIN Number**: INE296G07309
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1,00,000
- **Interest Rate**: 10% per annum
- **Interest Payment**: Monthly
- **Date of Allotment**: July 29, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: July 29, 2027
- **Put/Call Option**: Not Available

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: July 31, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: July 29, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: July 29, 2027

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members can trade these securities on BSE Debt segment
- Securities must be traded in dematerialized form only
- Reference to Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for further details

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. The securities are issued on private placement basis and will provide additional trading opportunities in the debt segment for institutional investors.
