## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of Bajaj Finance Limited on its debt segment effective September 10, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each, totaling 45,000 units worth Rs. 2,250 crores.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 45,000 units (total value Rs. 2,250 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730179, Scrip ID: BFL090925
- ISIN Number: INE296A14C22
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,159.50 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Limited serves as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: September 10, 2025
- Allotment Date: September 9, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 9, 2025 (91-day tenure)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing on private placement basis. Provides additional short-term funding avenue for Bajaj Finance Limited and investment option for institutional investors in the debt segment.
