## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Infina Finance Private Limited on private placement basis on the Debt segment effective October 10, 2025. The issue comprises 1000 units with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, issued at Rs. 4,93,970 per unit with a redemption date of December 15, 2025. The instruments carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings. Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- 1000 Commercial Paper units listed on BSE Debt segment
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,93,970 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730371, Scrip ID: IFPL91025
- ISIN: INE879F14LQ4
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Allotment Date: October 9, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 15, 2025
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE879F14LQ4
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be followed for all transactions
- Contact BSE debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 10, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date:** October 10, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** October 9, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** December 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The Commercial Paper issue is on private placement basis for a relatively short tenure (67 days). The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA) indicate strong creditworthiness. Impact is limited to debt market participants and investors in short-term instruments. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
