## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of additional securities issued by 8 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS). These new securities will be available for trading starting Monday, September 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- 8 companies issuing additional shares under ESOP/ESOS programs
- Total of 8,406,813 new shares to be listed
- All securities will be admitted for trading from September 1, 2025
- No lock-in period applicable for any of the listed securities
- Face values range from Re 1 to Rs 10 per share

## Companies and Share Details

- **Aarti Pharmalabs Limited**: 6,600 shares (Face Value: Rs 5)
- **Carborundum Universal Ltd**: 5,882 shares (Face Value: Re 1)
- **Coromandel International Limited**: 83,160 shares (Face Value: Re 1)
- **Greenply Industries Ltd**: 500 shares (Face Value: Re 1)
- **Keystone Realtors Limited**: 25,814 shares (Face Value: Rs 10)
- **Medplus Health Services Limited**: 79,157 shares (Face Value: Rs 2)
- **Triveni Turbine Limited**: 3,357 shares (Face Value: Re 1)
- **Bajaj Finance Ltd**: 8,195,345 shares (Face Value: Re 1)

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 29, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: September 1, 2025
- **Lock-in Period**: Not applicable for all securities

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing with minimal market impact. The ESOP issuances represent normal employee compensation programs and are not expected to significantly affect trading volumes or stock prices. Bajaj Finance has the largest issuance with over 8 million shares.
