## Summary

BSE has issued an update on the applicability of Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework, shortlisting two securities for inclusion effective August 6, 2025. The circular provides consolidated lists of securities under various ASM stages and movements between frameworks.

## Key Points

- Two securities newly shortlisted for LT-ASM: Avi Products India Ltd (523896) and Mehai Technology Ltd (540730)
- No securities moving to higher or lower ASM stages
- No securities being moved out of LT-ASM framework
- Consolidated list includes 18 securities across different ASM stages
- Various framework transitions marked with special symbols for different surveillance categories

## Regulatory Changes

- Implementation of LT-ASM framework for two additional securities
- Enhanced surveillance measures will apply to trading in these securities
- Securities subject to additional monitoring and reporting requirements

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must comply with LT-ASM trading restrictions
- Enhanced due diligence required for transactions in listed securities
- Adherence to specific settlement and margin requirements for ASM securities

## Important Dates

- **August 6, 2025**: Effective date for LT-ASM applicability to newly shortlisted securities

## Impact Assessment

- Trading liquidity may be reduced for affected securities due to surveillance measures
- Investors should exercise additional caution when trading in LT-ASM securities
- Enhanced monitoring may lead to improved market integrity for these securities
- Limited immediate market impact given only two securities are newly affected
