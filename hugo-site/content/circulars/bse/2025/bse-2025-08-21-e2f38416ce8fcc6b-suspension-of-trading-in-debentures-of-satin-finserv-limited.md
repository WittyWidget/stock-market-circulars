## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of SATIN FINSERV LIMITED effective August 28, 2025, due to the company fixing record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to SATIN FINSERV LIMITED debentures (Code: SFL-10.85%-4-9-25-PVT, ISIN: INE03K307025)
- Record date set for August 28, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- Trading members advised not to deal in these debentures from August 28, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

Temporary trading suspension implemented to facilitate corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from August 28, 2025
- Members requested to take note of the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: August 28, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: August 28, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as suspension affects only one company's debentures. Temporary measure to ensure proper settlement of corporate action related to interest payment and redemption.
