## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Advance Agrolife Limited (Scrip Code: 544562) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (T Group) to the Rolling segment (B Group) effective from Friday, October 24, 2025. This change follows the Exchange's earlier notice dated October 07, 2025.

## Key Points

- Advance Agrolife Limited (Scrip Code: 544562) to move from T Group to B Group
- Transfer effective from Friday, October 24, 2025
- Notice issued in continuation to BSE Notice No. 20251007-45 dated October 07, 2025
- Trading members can contact Mr. Parag Jain (Tel: 022 2272 8685) for further details
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

The security will transition from Trade for Trade settlement (T Group) to Rolling settlement (B Group), allowing for standard T+1 settlement instead of delivery-based trading only.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Update their systems to reflect the group change for Scrip Code 544562
- Prepare for standard rolling settlement procedures from October 24, 2025
- Contact the designated BSE official for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **October 07, 2025**: Initial notice issued (Notice No. 20251007-45)
- **October 08, 2025**: Current notice date
- **October 24, 2025**: Effective date of transfer from T Group to B Group

## Impact Assessment

This change positively impacts the liquidity and trading flexibility of Advance Agrolife Limited shares. Moving from Trade for Trade segment to Rolling segment allows investors to participate in intraday trading and standard settlement cycles, potentially increasing trading volumes and market participation. Trading members should update their risk management and settlement processes accordingly.
