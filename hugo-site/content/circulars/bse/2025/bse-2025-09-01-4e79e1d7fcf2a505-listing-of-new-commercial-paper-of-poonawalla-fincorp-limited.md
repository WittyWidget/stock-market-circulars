## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of POONAWALLA FINCORP LIMITED issued on private placement basis, effective September 1, 2025. The securities comprise 12,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 6,000 crores.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 12,000 units (total value: Rs. 6,000 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730104
- Scrip ID: PFL260825
- ISIN: INE511C14YX9 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,308.50/-
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI BANK LTD acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: September 1, 2025
- Allotment Date: August 29, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. Provides additional short-term debt instrument for institutional investors in POONAWALLA FINCORP LIMITED securities.
