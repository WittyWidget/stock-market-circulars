## Summary

BSE has announced that JIO BLACKROCK MUTUAL FUND will reopen the JIOBLACKROCK FLEXI CAP FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH (EQUITY) for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform. The scheme will be available for subscription and redemption along with Systematic Investment Plan (SIP), Systematic Transfer Plan (STP), and Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) facilities starting October 17, 2025.

## Key Points

- JIO BLACKROCK MUTUAL FUND reopening JIOBLACKROCK FLEXI CAP FUND for transactions
- Scheme: JIOBLACKROCK FLEXI CAP FUND - DIRECT PLAN - GROWTH
- Category: EQUITY
- ISIN: INF22M001093
- Available facilities: Ongoing subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Platform: BSE StAR MF
- Reference to previous Notice No. 20250922-10 regarding scheme closure

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the availability of a mutual fund scheme on the trading platform.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Members and investors can resume transactions in the specified scheme through the BSE StAR MF Platform as per standard mutual fund transaction procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 17, 2025 - Scheme reopens for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational notification affecting a single mutual fund scheme.

**Investor Impact**: Low to moderate for existing and potential investors in JIOBLACKROCK FLEXI CAP FUND. Investors who were unable to transact in this scheme can now resume investments, redemptions, and systematic plans.

**Operational Impact**: BSE StAR MF Platform members can facilitate transactions in this scheme starting October 17, 2025. No system changes or special procedures required beyond normal mutual fund transaction processes.
