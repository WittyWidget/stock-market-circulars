## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Tata Power Co. Ltd issued on private placement basis. The securities are worth Rs. 500 crores (10,000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) with CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings. Trading commenced on July 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 10,000 units (Total value: Rs. 500 crores)
- Scrip Code: 729933
- ISIN: INE245A14KA5
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,727.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialised form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 30, 2025
- Trading Commencement: July 30, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The securities provide short-term funding for Tata Power with a 3-month tenure. High credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk for investors.
