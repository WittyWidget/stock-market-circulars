## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 1,07,250 new equity shares of Spectrum Electrical Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 544386) issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares will be available for trading from Thursday, August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- 1,07,250 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 1110/-
- Issue price: Rs. 1120/- per share
- Shares issued to promoters on preferential basis through warrant conversion
- Distribution numbers: 15606591 to 15713840
- Date of allotment: July 24, 2025
- ISIN: INE01EO01010
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities listing
- Lock-in provisions apply to all issued shares until February 28, 2027

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 24, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 28, 2025
- Lock-in Period Ends: February 28, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this involves preferential allotment to promoters with a lock-in period of approximately 18 months. The shares cannot be traded by allottees until February 2027, preventing immediate dilution effects on existing shareholders.
