## Summary

BSE has approved the listing and trading of 60,00,000 new equity shares of Goyal Associates Ltd. (Scrip Code: 530663) effective August 14, 2025. These shares were issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis through conversion of warrants at Rs. 2.50 per share.

## Key Points

- 60 lakh equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at premium of Rs. 1.50
- Issue price: Rs. 2.50 per share
- Issued to non-promoters through warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distinguished numbers: 46937501 to 52937500
- Allotment date: March 31, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure for preferential issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability for trading
- Lock-in period compliance required until February 14, 2026
- All 60 lakh shares subject to lock-in restrictions

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: March 31, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 14, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: February 14, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of preferential shares for a single company. The lock-in period until February 2026 prevents immediate trading of these shares, limiting short-term market effects.
