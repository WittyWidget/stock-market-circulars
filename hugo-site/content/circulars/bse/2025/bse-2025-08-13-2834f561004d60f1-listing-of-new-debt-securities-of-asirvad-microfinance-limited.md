## Summary

BSE announces the listing of new debt securities issued by Asirvad Microfinance Limited on private placement basis, effective from August 13, 2025. The securities carry a face value of Rs. 1 lakh each with 8.70% annual interest rate.

## Key Points

- Securities quantity: 2,500 units with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 977018, Scrip ID: 870AML28
- ISIN Number: INE516Q08448
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AA-/Stable
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Interest Rate: 8.70% per annum paid quarterly
- Maturity Date: August 12, 2028

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialised form under the specified ISIN
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum on BSE debt section for further details

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 12, 2025
- Trading Effective Date: August 13, 2025
- Maturity Date: August 12, 2028
- Interest Payment: Quarterly basis

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis. The securities are targeted for institutional investors and will have limited retail participation due to high denomination of Rs. 1 lakh per unit.
