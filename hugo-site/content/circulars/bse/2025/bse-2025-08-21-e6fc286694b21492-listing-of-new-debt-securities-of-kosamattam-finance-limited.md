## Summary

BSE has listed two new debt securities issued by Kosamattam Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective August 21, 2025. The securities will trade in dematerialized form with a tick size of 1 paise.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities listed: KFL-10%-30-11-27-PVT and KFL-10%-31-05-28-PVT
- Both securities carry 10% p.a. interest rate with monthly payment
- Face value and issue price: Rs. 10,000 each
- Credit rating: IND A/Stable for both securities
- Allotment date: August 20, 2025
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Security Details

**Security 1:**
- Scrip Code: 977038
- ISIN: INE403Q07GE0
- Quantity: 100,000
- Redemption: November 30, 2027

**Security 2:**
- Scrip Code: 977039
- ISIN: INE403Q07GD2
- Quantity: 50,000
- Redemption: May 31, 2028

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum on BSE website
- Contact debt department for clarifications: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Listing effective date: August 21, 2025
- Allotment date: August 20, 2025
- First security redemption: November 30, 2027
- Second security redemption: May 31, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis. The securities provide additional investment options for debt market participants with stable credit rating.
