## Summary

Kanakadurga Finance Limited has announced part redemption of its non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE104W07195, Code: 976402) with a record date of October 16, 2025. This corporate action includes payment of interest on the debentures. Trading in these debentures will commence with a reduced face value of Rs. 62,500 per debenture effective from October 16, 2025 under settlement DR-737/2025-2026.

## Key Points

- Company: Kanakadurga Finance Limited
- ISIN: INE104W07195
- Debenture Code: KDFL-13%-31-1-27-PVT (976402)
- Record date: October 16, 2025
- Purpose: Part redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Reduced face value: Rs. 62,500 per debenture
- Effective date: October 16, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-737/2025-2026
- Notice number: 20251014-15
- Segment: Debt

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for part redemption of debentures in the debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trading in the debentures will be with reduced face value effective from October 16, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new face value of Rs. 62,500 per debenture
- All transactions from the effective date onwards must be executed at the reduced face value

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 14, 2025
- **Record Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value**: October 16, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-737/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

**Impact on Debenture Holders**: Existing debenture holders will receive partial redemption proceeds along with interest payment. The face value per debenture will be reduced to Rs. 62,500, affecting the denomination of their holdings.

**Trading Impact**: From October 16, 2025, all trading in these debentures will be at the reduced face value. Market participants need to adjust their valuation and trading systems accordingly.

**Market Segment**: Limited to debt segment, specifically affecting holders of Kanakadurga Finance Limited's 13% non-convertible debentures maturing on January 31, 2027. This is a routine corporate action with medium impact on specific debenture holders.
