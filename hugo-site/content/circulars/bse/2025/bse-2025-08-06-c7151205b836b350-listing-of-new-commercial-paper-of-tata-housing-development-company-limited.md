## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 80 crore issued by Tata Housing Development Company Limited on private placement basis, effective August 6, 2025. The instrument will trade on BSE's debt segment in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each (1,600 units)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,675 per unit
- Scrip Code: 729969, ISIN: INE582L14IS4
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applies
- Trading members must contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 5, 2025
- Trading Effective Date: August 6, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The instrument provides investors with a 90-day investment option with CARE A1+ rating.
