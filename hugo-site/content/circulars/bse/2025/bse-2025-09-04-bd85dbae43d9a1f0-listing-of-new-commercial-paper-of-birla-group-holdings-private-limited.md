## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Birla Group Holdings Private Limited worth Rs. 200 crores (4000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) on private placement basis. The paper has a 3-month maturity period from September 4, 2025 to December 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total issue size: 4000 units (Rs. 200 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,785 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730151 (BGHPL4925)
- ISIN: INE09OL14HU4
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Axis Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size of 1 paise applicable
- Trading members must use specified ISIN number

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 4, 2025
- **Listing Date**: September 4, 2025 
- **Redemption Date**: December 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by a private company. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA) indicate strong creditworthiness. Standard trading parameters apply with no special conditions.
