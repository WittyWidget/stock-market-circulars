## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Stanbik Agro Limited for public comments. The document was filed on September 05, 2025, and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. The public comment period is 21 days from the notice date.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Stanbik Agro Limited on September 05, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for public review
- 21-day public comment period from September 08, 2025
- Document complies with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard IPO process notification under existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public invited to review and provide comments on Draft Offer Document
- Comments must be submitted within 21 days of notice date
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document hosted on www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document filing date: September 05, 2025
- Notice date: September 08, 2025
- Public comment deadline: September 29, 2025 (21 days from notice)
- Document availability period: At least 21 days on exchange website

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact confined to SME segment. This is a routine IPO process step affecting one company (Stanbik Agro Limited). The public comment period is part of standard regulatory procedure under ICDR Regulations. No immediate market operations or trading implications for existing listed securities.
