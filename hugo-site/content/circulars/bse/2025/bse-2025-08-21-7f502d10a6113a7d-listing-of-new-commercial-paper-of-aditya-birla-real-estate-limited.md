## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 200 crores (4,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued by Aditya Birla Real Estate Limited on private placement basis. The commercial paper is effective for trading from August 21, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 4,000 units (Total value: Rs. 200 crores)
- Scrip Code: 730056
- Scrip ID: ABREL21825
- ISIN: INE055A14KI1
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,91,544/-
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000/-
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing of commercial paper securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- State Bank of India serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 21, 2025
- Trading Effective Date: August 21, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 20, 2025
- Tenure: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The listing provides short-term funding avenue for Aditya Birla Real Estate Limited and investment option for debt market participants.
