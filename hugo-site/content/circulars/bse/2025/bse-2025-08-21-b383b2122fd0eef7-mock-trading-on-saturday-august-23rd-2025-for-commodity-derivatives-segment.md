## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, August 23rd, 2025. The session is designed for trading members to test their trading applications, including third-party platforms and in-house systems using ETI APIs. No new BOLT TWS release will be implemented.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, August 23rd, 2025
- Available for Commodity Derivatives segment only
- No new BOLT TWS release
- Opportunity to test various functionalities including risk-reduction mode and trading halts
- Active participation requested from all members
- Feedback required by 3:00 PM on the same day

## Trading Schedule

- **Log-in**: 10:15 AM to 11:00 AM
- **Continuous Trading**: 11:00 AM to 2:00 PM
- **Trade Modification**: 2:00 PM to 2:10 PM

## Important Disclaimers

- Mock trades do not create margin obligations
- No pay-in or pay-out obligations
- No rights and liabilities created from mock trading
- Session is purely for testing and familiarization
