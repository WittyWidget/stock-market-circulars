## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Kolte-Patil Developers Ltd on private placement basis, effective October 20, 2025. The securities have a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit with a total quantity of 13,996 units (approximately Rs. 139.96 crore). The securities carry a CARE AA-/Stable credit rating and have a maturity period of 10 years from the allotment date.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977231
- Scrip ID: KPDL161025
- Detail Name: KPDL-15-10-35-PVT
- ISIN Number: INE094I07080
- Quantity: 13,996 units
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Credit Rating: CARE AA-/Stable
- Date of Allotment: October 16, 2025
- Date of Redemption: October 15, 2035
- Trading Mode: Dematerialized form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- No Put/Call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE094I07080
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for further details
- For clarifications, trading members should contact the debt department at BSE (phone: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 20, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: October 16, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 20, 2025 (effective from today)
- **Redemption Date**: October 15, 2035
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Not applicable (NA)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with minimal market impact. The listing of private placement debt securities by Kolte-Patil Developers Ltd expands the available debt instruments on BSE's debt segment. The securities are rated CARE AA-/Stable, indicating high credit quality. The impact is limited to investors interested in corporate debt securities and does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations. Trading members dealing in debt securities should note the new listing for their operations.
