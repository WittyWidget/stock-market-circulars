## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 19,822 new equity shares of Taylormade Renewables Limited (Scrip Code: 541228) effective from Friday, September 5, 2025. These shares were issued at Rs. 519/- each to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 19,822 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 509/-
- Shares issued to non-promoters through warrant conversion
- Issue price: Rs. 519/- per share
- ISIN: INE459Z01017
- Distribution numbers: 12349852 to 12369673
- Date of allotment: April 7, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are now available for trading on the exchange.

## Important Dates

- **Trading Commencement**: September 5, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: April 7, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry Dates**: March 31, 2026 and April 1, 2026 (varying by share lots)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a small number of shares (19,822) from warrant conversion. The lock-in provisions ensure gradual release of shares into the market over time, reducing immediate liquidity impact.
