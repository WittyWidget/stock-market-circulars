## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Muthoot Fincorp Limited on private placement basis with effect from October 24, 2025. The securities are non-convertible debentures with a face value of Rs. 10 crore, carrying a 12% per annum interest rate with monthly payment frequency. The debentures have a 10-year tenure with a call option available after 10 years from the deemed date of allotment.

## Key Points

- **Scrip Code**: 977240
- **Scrip ID**: 12MFL35
- **ISIN Number**: INE549K08582
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10,00,00,000 (Rs. 10 crore)
- **Interest Rate**: 12% per annum
- **Credit Rating**: CRISIL A+/Positive
- **Date of Allotment**: October 23, 2025
- **Market Lot**: 1
- **Quantity**: 50
- **Tick Size**: 1 paise
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that the securities will be traded only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE549K08582
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the debt department at BSE on specified phone numbers (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915)

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: October 23, 2025
- **Interest Payment Dates**: Monthly from November 30, 2025 to October 23, 2035
- **Redemption Date**: October 23, 2035
- **Call Option**: Available from 10 years from the deemed date of allotment

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Affects only debt segment trading members who may trade these specific securities. The securities are available for trading in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

**Investor Impact**: Relevant only to investors holding or interested in Muthoot Fincorp debt instruments. The CRISIL A+/Positive rating indicates adequate safety with positive outlook. The 12% coupon rate with monthly interest payments provides regular income to debenture holders.
