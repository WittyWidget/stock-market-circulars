## Summary

KEC International Ltd. has listed new Commercial Paper securities worth Rs. 50 crores on BSE Debt segment through private placement. The instruments carry CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ ratings and will be traded in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Total quantity: 1000 units (Rs. 50 crores total value)
- Scrip Code: 730133, ISIN: INE389H14LF3
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,449/- per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 2, 2025
- Trading Commencement: September 3, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The short-term debt instrument provides additional funding avenue for KEC International Ltd. and offers investment option for institutional investors in the debt segment.
