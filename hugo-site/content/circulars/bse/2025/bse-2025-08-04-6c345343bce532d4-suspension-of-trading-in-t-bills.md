## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in three Treasury Bills effective August 5, 2025, as they reach their maturity date. The suspended T-Bills are 182TB70825, 364TB70825, and 91TB070825.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills will mature on August 5, 2025
- Trading suspension effective from August 5, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-688/2025-2026
- All trading members advised to cease dealing in these securities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is routine suspension due to maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified T-Bills from August 5, 2025
- All exchange participants must take note of the suspension

## Important Dates

- **August 4, 2025**: Notice date
- **August 5, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date and maturity date

## Impact Assessment

Routine impact on debt market as three T-Bills reach maturity. Trading members need to adjust their portfolios and cease transactions in these specific securities. Standard procedure with minimal market disruption expected.
