## Summary

BSE announces the listing of new Commercial Paper issued by POONAWALLA FINCORP LIMITED on private placement basis. The instrument worth Rs. 100 crores (2000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) is listed on BSE Debt segment effective August 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (2000 units)
- Scrip Code: 729995, Scrip ID: PFL80825
- ISIN: INE511C14YT7
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 492,207.50 per unit
- Face Value: Rs. 500,000 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 8, 2025
- Listing Date: August 11, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 7, 2025
- Maturity Period: 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The Commercial Paper provides short-term funding option for Poonawalla Fincorp and investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
