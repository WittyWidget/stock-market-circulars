## Summary

BSE has issued a new ISIN number INE560A01023 for India Glycols Ltd (Scrip Code: 500201) following the subdivision of equity shares from face value of Rs.10 to Rs.5 each. The new ISIN will be effective for trades executed on and from August 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: India Glycols Ltd (Scrip Code: 500201)
- New ISIN Number: INE560A01023
- Share subdivision: From Rs.10 per share to Rs.5 per share
- Effective date: August 12, 2025 (Ex-Date)
- Document Reference: DR-693/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

The new ISIN number replaces the previous identifier for India Glycols Ltd equity shares following the approved share subdivision. This is a continuation of exchange notice 20250731-17 dated July 31, 2025.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must use the new ISIN number INE560A01023 for all trades in India Glycols Ltd equity shares from the ex-date of August 12, 2025 onwards.

## Important Dates

- **August 12, 2025**: Ex-date - New ISIN becomes effective for trading
- **July 31, 2025**: Original notice date for the subdivision announcement

## Impact Assessment

The share subdivision will double the number of shares while halving the face value, making shares more affordable for retail investors. Trading systems and portfolios will need to reflect the new ISIN and adjusted share quantities from the ex-date.
