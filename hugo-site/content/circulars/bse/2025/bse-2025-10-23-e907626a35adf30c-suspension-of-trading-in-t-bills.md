## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that T-bill 799MAHSDL25 (ISIN: IN2220150113, Scrip Code: 810573) will mature on its redemption date and trading in this instrument will be suspended from October 24, 2025. This is a standard suspension notice issued prior to the maturity of debt securities.

## Key Points

- T-bill 799MAHSDL25 (Scrip Code: 810573) reaching maturity
- ISIN: IN2220150113
- Trading suspension effective from October 24, 2025
- Trading members advised not to deal in this T-bill
- Notice reference: DR-742/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notice regarding the maturity of a debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease all trading activities in the specified T-bill from October 24, 2025
- Members should update their systems to prevent inadvertent trades in the maturing instrument
- No new positions should be taken in scrip code 810573

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 23, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: To be confirmed (maturity date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This affects only a single T-bill instrument and is part of the normal lifecycle of treasury bills. Holders will receive redemption proceeds on maturity.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Trading members need to ensure their systems are updated to reflect the suspension, but this is a routine process for maturing debt securities.

**Segment**: Debt market only, no impact on equity or other segments.
