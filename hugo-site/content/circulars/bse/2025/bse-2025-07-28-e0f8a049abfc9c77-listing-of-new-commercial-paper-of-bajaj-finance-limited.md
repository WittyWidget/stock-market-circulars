## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Bajaj Finance Limited issued on private placement basis. The securities are worth Rs. 5,00,000 each with 500 quantity, totaling Rs. 25 crores, and will be traded on the BSE Debt segment effective July 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each issued by Bajaj Finance Limited
- Total quantity: 500 securities (Rs. 25 crores total value)
- Scrip Code: 729887, Scrip ID: BFL22725
- ISIN: INE296A14C14 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 469,457 per security
- Market Lot: 1
- ICICI Bank Limited serves as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing of debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Trading members can contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 25, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: July 28, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: July 22, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The securities provide additional debt investment options for institutional investors. The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA) indicate strong creditworthiness of Bajaj Finance Limited.
