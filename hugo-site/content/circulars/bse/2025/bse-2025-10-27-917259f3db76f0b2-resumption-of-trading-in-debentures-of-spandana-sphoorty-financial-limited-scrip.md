## Summary

BSE has announced the resumption of trading in debentures of SPANDANA SPHOORTY FINANCIAL LIMITED with scrip codes 975811, 974793, and 974502, effective October 28, 2025. This follows the completion of the optional accelerated redemption process for which the record date was set as October 8, 2025. The company has now requested to resume trading in these outstanding debentures.

## Key Points

- Trading in debentures with scrip codes 975811, 974793, and 974502 will resume from October 28, 2025
- This follows the completion of optional accelerated redemption process
- Record date for redemption was October 8, 2025
- Reference: DR-744/2025-2026
- Continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20251006-62 dated October 06, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational notice regarding trading status.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the resumption of trading in the specified debentures
- Normal trading operations can resume for these securities from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **October 8, 2025**: Record date for optional accelerated redemption
- **October 27, 2025**: Notice date
- **October 28, 2025**: Trading resumption effective date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine operational update affecting specific debentures of one company. Trading members dealing in these securities can resume normal trading activities.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard resumption of trading following completion of corporate action process. No disruption to broader debt market operations expected.
