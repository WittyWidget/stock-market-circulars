## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Equitas Small Finance Bank Limited on private placement basis, effective August 4, 2025. The securities will trade on BSE Debt segment with specific trading parameters and conditions.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 50,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 976979, Scrip ID: 960ESFBL30
- ISIN: INE063P08120, Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000
- Interest Rate: 9.60% per annum, paid annually
- Credit Rating: CARE AA-, IND AA-
- Securities tradeable only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedures applied for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under specified ISIN number
- Refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details

## Important Dates

- Listing Effective Date: August 4, 2025
- Date of Allotment: July 31, 2025
- Date of Redemption: July 31, 2030
- Interest Payment: Annually

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing for institutional investors. The listing expands BSE's debt market offerings but does not affect broader market operations or retail trading significantly.
