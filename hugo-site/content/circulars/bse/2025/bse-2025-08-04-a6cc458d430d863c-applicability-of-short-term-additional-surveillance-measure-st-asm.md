## Summary

BSE has announced updates to the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective August 5, 2025. The circular identifies 4 securities newly shortlisted for ST-ASM Stage I, while 7 securities are moving out of the framework. No securities are moving between ASM stages.

## Key Points

- 4 securities newly shortlisted for ST-ASM Stage I framework
- 7 securities moving out of ST-ASM framework
- No securities moving to higher or lower ASM stages
- Framework includes 5/15/30 days surveillance periods
- SME scrips specifically marked in the listings

## Regulatory Changes

Implementation of updated ST-ASM framework with revised security listings. Securities moving out are due to inclusion in other frameworks including LT-ASM, Trade for Trade, GSM, Pledge, and ESM frameworks.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must comply with ST-ASM framework requirements for listed securities
- Enhanced surveillance monitoring for securities in Stage I
- Adherence to specific trading restrictions for ASM securities

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 5, 2025 - ST-ASM framework changes take effect

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact affecting specific securities under surveillance. Securities newly included will face enhanced monitoring and potential trading restrictions. Securities moving out will be subject to alternative surveillance frameworks. Market participants should adjust trading strategies for affected securities accordingly.
