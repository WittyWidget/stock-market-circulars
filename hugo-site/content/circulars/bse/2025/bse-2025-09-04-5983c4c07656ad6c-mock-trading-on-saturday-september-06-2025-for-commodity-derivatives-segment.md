## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives segment on Saturday, September 06, 2025. This session is designed for testing and familiarization purposes, allowing trading members to test their systems and applications. The mock trades will not create any financial obligations or rights.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, September 06, 2025
- Login: 10:15 AM - 11:00 AM, Continuous Trading: 11:00 AM - 3:30 PM
- New BOLT Pro TWS version 12.03 will be released
- Members can test third-party platforms and in-house systems via IML/ETI APIs
- Testing includes exceptional market conditions, risk-reduction mode, and trading halt scenarios
- Feedback required by 4:30 PM on the same day

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine mock trading session in compliance with SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 dated November 24, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members may participate in either mock trading session or UAT environment to fulfill regulatory requirements
- Members must refer to Exchange Notice No 20201201-22 dated December 1, 2020 regarding testing of trading and risk management software
- Active participation in mock trading sessions is requested
- Feedback submission required by 4:30 PM on September 06, 2025

## Important Dates

- **September 06, 2025**: Mock trading session
- **4:30 PM, September 06, 2025**: Deadline for feedback submission

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a testing session with no financial obligations. Mock trades will not attract margin obligations or create pay-in/pay-out requirements. The session provides opportunity for members to test systems and ensure readiness for regular trading operations.
