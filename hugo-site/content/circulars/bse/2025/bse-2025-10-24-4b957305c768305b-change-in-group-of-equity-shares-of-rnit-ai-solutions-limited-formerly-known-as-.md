## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of RNIT AI Solutions Limited (formerly known as Autopal Industries Limited, Scrip Code: 517286) will be permitted for trading under Group 'X' effective Monday, November 10, 2025. This represents a change in the trading group classification for the company's shares.

## Key Points

- Company Name: RNIT AI Solutions Limited (formerly Autopal Industries Limited)
- Scrip Code: 517286
- New Trading Group: 'X'
- Effective Date: Monday, November 10, 2025
- Notice issued by Kinnar Mehta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be reclassified from their current group to Group 'X' on BSE. Group 'X' typically represents companies that meet specific criteria set by the exchange for surveillance or trading parameters.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the group change and adjust their trading systems and processes accordingly to reflect the new classification effective from the specified date.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Effective Date**: November 10, 2025 (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

The reclassification to Group 'X' may affect trading parameters such as price bands, settlement cycles, or surveillance measures for RNIT AI Solutions Limited shares. Trading members and investors should be aware of the specific characteristics and requirements associated with Group 'X' securities when trading this scrip from November 10, 2025 onwards.
