## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for EURO ASIA EXPORTS LTD shares. The offer will run from August 4-18, 2025, targeting up to 34,64,900 equity shares at Rs. 10.00 per share (face value Rs. 10.00).

## Key Points

- Security: EURO ASIA EXPORTS LTD (Symbol: EUROASIA, ISIN: INE535P01015, Scrip Code: 530929)
- Offer size: Up to 34,64,900 equity shares
- Offer price: Rs. 10.00 per share
- Market lot: 1 share
- Opening date: Monday, August 4, 2025
- Closing date: Monday, August 18, 2025
- Session timings: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (take-up extends to 4:00 PM on last day)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This follows existing OTB operational guidelines as per Exchange Notices 20150930-1 and 20170203-29.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure UCC of investors is registered with correct PAN and category
- Members can only bid for investors having shares in demat form
- Various investor categories eligible: FII, FI, MF, IC, OTH, NOH, CO, IND
- Access through iBBS platform, RTRMS, and Extranet for files

## Important Dates

- **August 4-18, 2025**: Bid offering period (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **August 4-14, 2025**: Give-up/Take-up period (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **August 18, 2025**: Give-up last day (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **August 18, 2025**: Take-up last day (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact limited to EURO ASIA EXPORTS LTD shareholders and trading members. The takeover offer provides exit opportunity for existing shareholders at Rs. 10.00 per share. Trading members need to ensure system readiness and UCC compliance for participating investors.
