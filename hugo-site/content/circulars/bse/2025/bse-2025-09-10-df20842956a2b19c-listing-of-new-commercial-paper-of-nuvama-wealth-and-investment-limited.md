## Summary

BSE has listed two new commercial paper issues by Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited on private placement basis, effective September 10, 2025. The issues comprise 500 units of 3-month paper and 3,000 units of 4-month paper, both with face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each.

## Key Points

- Two commercial paper issues listed on BSE debt segment
- Issue 1: 500 units, face value Rs. 5 lakhs each, maturing December 9, 2025
- Issue 2: 3,000 units, face value Rs. 5 lakhs each, maturing January 9, 2026
- Both issues carry CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ credit ratings
- ICICI Bank Ltd serves as issuing and paying agent
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: September 10, 2025
- Allotment Date: September 9, 2025
- Redemption Date (Issue 1): December 9, 2025
- Redemption Date (Issue 2): January 9, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt investment options for institutional investors. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
