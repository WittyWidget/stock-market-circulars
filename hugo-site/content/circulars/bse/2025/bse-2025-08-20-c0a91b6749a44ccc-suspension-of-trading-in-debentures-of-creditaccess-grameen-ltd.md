## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in two non-convertible debentures of CREDITACCESS GRAMEEN LTD effective August 22, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date of August 23, 2025 for redemption and interest payment purposes.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to two specific NCDs: CAGL-9.10%-7-9-25-NCD and CAGL-7-9-25-NCD
- Record date set for August 23, 2025
- No dealings allowed from August 22, 2025
- Purpose is redemption of debentures and payment of interest

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - standard procedure for debenture redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from August 22, 2025
- Trading members required to take note of the suspension

## Important Dates

- **August 22, 2025**: Trading suspension begins
- **August 23, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this affects only two specific debt instruments of CREDITACCESS GRAMEEN LTD. Standard corporate action procedure with temporary trading suspension to facilitate redemption process.
