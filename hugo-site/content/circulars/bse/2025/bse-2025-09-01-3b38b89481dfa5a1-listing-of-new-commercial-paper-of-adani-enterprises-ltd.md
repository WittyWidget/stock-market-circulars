## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Adani Enterprises Ltd. on private placement basis. The commercial paper worth Rs. 50 crore (100 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) is rated CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+, with HDFC Bank Limited serving as the issuing and paying agent.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 100 units
- Scrip Code: 729977, Scrip ID: AEL6825
- ISIN Number: INE423A14XX3 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,79,596 per unit
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading members can contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 29, 2025
- Listing Date: September 1, 2025
- Redemption Date: March 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The securities provide short-term funding for Adani Enterprises and offer investment options for institutional investors in the debt segment.
