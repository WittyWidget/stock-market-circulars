## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by HDFC Securities Limited worth Rs. 60 crores (12,000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) on private placement basis, effective August 11, 2025. The securities will trade in dematerialized form on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper worth Rs. 60 crores (12,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,219.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading commences August 11, 2025
- Maturity date: November 10, 2025
- ISIN: INE700G14PG2, Scrip Code: 730002
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as issuing and paying agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Trading members can contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 11, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 11, 2025
- Redemption date: November 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by HDFC Securities. The 91-day tenor instrument provides short-term funding for the company and offers investment option for debt market participants.
