## Summary

Mirae Asset Sharekhan Financial Services Limited has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 50 crores (1000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE Debt segment. The securities are issued on private placement basis with CRISIL A1+ rating and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (1000 units total)
- Scrip Code: 729923, ISIN: INE550X14AI4
- Issue price: Rs. 491,544.09 per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- HDFC Bank Ltd is the Issuing and Paying agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading allowed only in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities to be traded under specified ISIN number

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 28, 2025
- Listing Date: July 29, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding instrument for institutional investors with 3-month maturity period.
