## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 13,52,000 new equity shares of GRM Overseas Ltd (Scrip Code: 531449) issued through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will commence trading from September 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- 13,52,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at Rs. 148/- premium
- Issue price: Rs. 150 per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 60000001 to 61352000
- Allotment date: May 28, 2025
- ISIN: INE192H01020

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced through this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities listing
- Lock-in period compliance as specified for different share tranches
- Standard listing compliance requirements apply

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: May 28, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: September 4, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: Various dates between March 6-7, 2026 for different share tranches

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a preferential issue to non-promoters with specified lock-in periods. The shares represent warrant conversion rather than fresh equity dilution, indicating previously planned capital structure adjustment.
