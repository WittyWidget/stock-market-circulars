## Summary

BSE announces the revocation of trading suspension for three companies - ANG Lifesciences India Limited, Vallabh Steels Ltd, and WinPro Industries Limited - following their payment of outstanding Annual Listing Fees. Trading suspension will be lifted effective August 13, 2025, though shares will continue under trade-for-trade mode for other reasons.

## Key Points

- Three companies have paid their outstanding Annual Listing Fees (ALF)
- Trading suspension to be revoked effective August 13, 2025
- Shares will continue in trade-for-trade mode for other compliance reasons
- Action follows SEBI LODR Regulation 14 requirements

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, specifically Regulation 14 regarding Annual Listing Fees payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must maintain current payment of Annual Listing Fees to avoid trading suspension
- Trading Members advised to note the change in trading status
- Continued compliance with other listing requirements remains mandatory

## Important Dates

- **August 13, 2025**: Effective date for revocation of trading suspension
- **August 8, 2025**: Circular issue date

## Impact Assessment

**Positive Impact:**
- Restoration of normal trading liquidity for affected securities
- Improved market access for investors in these companies
- Demonstrates effective compliance enforcement mechanism

**Affected Companies:**
- ANG Lifesciences India Limited (Scrip Code: 540694)
- Vallabh Steels Ltd (Scrip Code: 513397)
- WinPro Industries Limited (Scrip Code: 531337)

Shares will remain under trade-for-trade segment due to other compliance issues, limiting speculative trading while allowing genuine transactions.
