## Summary

BSE announces the reopening of BAJAJ FINSERV EQUITY SAVINGS FUND for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform, effective August 21, 2025. The fund will offer subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP facilities across six different plan options.

## Key Points

- BAJAJ FINSERV EQUITY SAVINGS FUND reopens for ongoing transactions
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform from August 21, 2025
- Offers subscription and redemption facilities
- Includes SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) options
- Six scheme variants available: Regular and Direct plans with IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, and Growth options
- All schemes categorized under EQUITY segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine fund availability announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

Members trading on BSE StAR MF Platform should note the availability of these fund options for client transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 21, 2025 - Fund reopens for ongoing transactions
- **Notice Date**: August 20, 2025
- **Reference Notice**: 20250725-21 (previous notice)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine operational announcement providing additional mutual fund investment options for investors on the BSE platform. The availability enhances investment choices but does not affect broader market operations or regulatory framework.
