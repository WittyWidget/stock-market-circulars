## Summary

Godrej Finance Limited has listed new commercial paper securities worth Rs. 100 crore (2000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE's debt segment effective August 21, 2025. The securities are issued on private placement basis with 3-month maturity.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 2000 units (Rs. 100 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,050.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 730057, Scrip ID: GFL21825
- ISIN: INE02KN14598

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 21, 2025
- Redemption date: November 20, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term financing option for Godrej Finance Limited and investment opportunity for institutional investors in highly-rated commercial paper.
