## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper issued by HDFC Securities Limited (Scrip Code: 729888, ISIN: INE700G14PE7) effective from October 17, 2025. The suspension is due to the instrument reaching its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper from the effective date.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:

- Update their systems to reflect the trading suspension
- Ensure no deals are executed in scrip code 729888 after the effective date

- **Trading Suspension Effective Date**: October 17, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: October 20, 2025 (inferred from CP nomenclature HSFCSL-20-10-25-CP)

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a scheduled maturity of a single commercial paper instrument. No broader market implications.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Routine suspension process that trading members handle regularly for maturing debt instruments. Affects only holders and potential traders of this specific HDFC Securities commercial paper.

**Segment**: Debt market only, specifically commercial paper segment.
