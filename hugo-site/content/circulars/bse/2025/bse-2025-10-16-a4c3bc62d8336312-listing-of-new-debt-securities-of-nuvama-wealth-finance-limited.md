## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on private placement basis, admitted to trading on the BSE Debt segment effective October 16, 2025. The securities are Nifty 50 Index linked instruments with a three-year tenure, maturing on October 13, 2028.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977215 (Scrip ID: NWFL131025)
- ISIN: INE918K07QC3
- Quantity: 1,800 units with market lot of 1
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 97,000 per unit (issued at discount)
- Date of Allotment: October 13, 2025
- Date of Redemption: October 13, 2028
- Rate of Interest: Linked to Nifty 50 Index
- Credit Rating: CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call options available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing announcement following established debt market procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE918K07QC3
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at BSE's debt memorandum portal for complete details
- Queries may be directed to BSE debt department on specified contact numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 16, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date:** October 16, 2025
- **Date of Allotment:** October 13, 2025
- **Date of Redemption:** October 13, 2028
- **Interest Payment Dates:** Not applicable (Nifty 50 Index linked)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This is a routine private placement listing with limited quantity (1,800 units) totaling Rs. 18 crore nominal value. The securities are targeted at institutional investors and will have negligible impact on broader market operations.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members dealing in debt securities need to update their systems with the new scrip details. The index-linked structure may attract investors seeking equity-linked returns with debt instrument characteristics.

**Investor Impact:** Relevant only to institutional investors participating in private placements. The CARE AA/Stable rating indicates strong credit quality with adequate safety for investors.
