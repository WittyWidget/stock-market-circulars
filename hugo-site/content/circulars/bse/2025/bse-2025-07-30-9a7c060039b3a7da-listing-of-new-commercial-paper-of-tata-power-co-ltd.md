## Summary

BSE has listed Tata Power Co. Ltd's new commercial paper issue of Rs. 500 crores on private placement basis. The commercial papers are denominated at Rs. 5 lakh each with a total of 10,000 units, maturing on October 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 10,000 units (Rs. 500 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,727.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip code: 729933, ISIN: INE245A14KA5
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Contact debt department for clarifications on specified numbers

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: July 30, 2025
- Redemption date: October 29, 2025
- Trading effective from: July 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding option for Tata Power with 3-month maturity. ICICI Bank serves as issuing and paying agent, ensuring operational efficiency.
