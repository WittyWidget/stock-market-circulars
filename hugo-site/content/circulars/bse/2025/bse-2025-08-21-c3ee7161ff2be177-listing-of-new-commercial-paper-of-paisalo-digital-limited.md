## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper of PAISALO DIGITAL LIMITED worth Rs. 15 crores (300 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) on its Debt segment. The securities are issued on private placement basis with IVR A1+ credit rating and will be traded in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (300 units)
- Total issue size: Rs. 15 crores
- Issue price: Rs. 4,77,386.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: IVR A1+
- ISIN: INE420C14219
- Scrip Code: 730058
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt instrument listing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- IDBI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: August 21, 2025
- Allotment Date: August 20, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 18, 2026
- Tenor: Approximately 6 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The instrument provides additional debt investment option for institutional investors in the BSE Debt segment.
