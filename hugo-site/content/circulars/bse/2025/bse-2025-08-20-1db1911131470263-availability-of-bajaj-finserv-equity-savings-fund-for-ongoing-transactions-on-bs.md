## Summary

BSE announces the reopening of BAJAJ FINSERV EQUITY SAVINGS FUND for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform effective August 21, 2025. The fund will offer subscription, redemption, and systematic investment facilities across six scheme variants.

## Key Points

- BAJAJ FINSERV EQUITY SAVINGS FUND reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform from August 21, 2025
- Includes subscription and redemption facilities
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan) & SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities available
- Six scheme variants offered: Regular and Direct plans with IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, and Growth options
