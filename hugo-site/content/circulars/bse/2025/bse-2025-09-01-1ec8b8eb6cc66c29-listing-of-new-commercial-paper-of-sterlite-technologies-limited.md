## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper of Sterlite Technologies Limited worth Rs. 125 crore (2,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued on private placement basis. The securities will trade on BSE Debt segment with scrip code 730120.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each issued by Sterlite Technologies Limited
- Total quantity: 2,500 units (Rs. 125 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,83,595/- per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 29, 2025
- Listing effective: September 1, 2025
- Redemption date: January 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding option for Sterlite Technologies and investment avenue for institutional investors in highly-rated commercial paper.
