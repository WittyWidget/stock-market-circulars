## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Truhome Finance Limited worth Rs. 20 crores issued on private placement basis. The securities carry 7.90% per annum interest rate and will mature on August 8, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 20,000 units with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 977014, Scrip ID: 790TFL28
- ISIN: INE432R07489
- Credit Rating: IND AA/Stable
- Interest Rate: 7.90% per annum paid annually
- Trading in dematerialized form only with tick size of 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Refer to Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for detailed terms

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 8, 2025
- Interest Payment Dates: Annually from August 8, 2026 to August 8, 2028
- Redemption Date: August 8, 2028
- Trading Commencement: August 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing of debt securities. The AA/Stable rating indicates good creditworthiness, providing investors with a secure investment option at competitive interest rates.
