## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on its Debt segment effective August 29, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit and carry a CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable credit rating.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2,500 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 976954, Scrip ID: NWFL24725
- ISIN Number: INE918K07PX1 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Issue Price: Rs. 96,887 per unit
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable
- Trading only in dematerialised form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialised form
- Trading members should refer to Placement Memorandum available on BSE website
- Contact debt department for clarifications on specified numbers

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 26, 2025
- Listing Date: August 29, 2025
- Date of Redemption: January 22, 2029

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing. The securities provide institutional investors with a rated investment option backed by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited with a stable credit outlook.
