## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement calendar for the takeover offer of SER Industries Limited, scheduled from August 20 to September 3, 2025, with settlement number 2025072.

## Key Points

- Takeover settlement number: 2025072
- Bid period: August 20, 2025 to September 3, 2025
- Obligation download date: September 10, 2025
- Final settlement date: September 11, 2025
- Demat shares must be delivered through Early Pay-in mechanism
- Market type designation: "(Takeover)" with settlement number 2025072

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. Process follows revised guidelines per Notice No.20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians must use Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism available in both depositories
- Securities must be earmarked/blocked in client accounts with requisite market type and settlement number
- No EPN file upload required for CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities
- Members must refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise stamp duty charges
- Early pay-in must be completed sufficiently in advance before BSE OTB bidding window closure

## Important Dates

- **August 20, 2025**: Bid start date
- **September 3, 2025**: Bid end date
- **September 10, 2025**: Obligation download date
- **September 11, 2025**: Settlement date

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact limited to SER Industries Limited shareholders and trading members involved in the takeover process. Standard settlement procedures apply with specific timelines for early pay-in requirements.
