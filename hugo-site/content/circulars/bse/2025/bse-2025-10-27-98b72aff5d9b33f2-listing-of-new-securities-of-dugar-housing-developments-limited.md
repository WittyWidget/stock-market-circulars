## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 27,00,000 new equity shares of Dugar Housing Developments Limited (Scrip Code: 511634) issued on a preferential basis to Non-Promoters pursuant to warrant conversion. The shares will commence trading on October 28, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 27,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 2/-
- Issue price: Rs. 12/- per share
- Issued to Non-Promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Date of allotment: August 12, 2025
- Distinctive numbers: 7000001 to 9700000
- ISIN: INE919M01026
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and must ensure compliance with applicable lock-in restrictions during trading.

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: August 12, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 28, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (Partial)**: April 30, 2026 (21,60,000 shares)
- **Lock-in Expiry (Partial)**: April 30, 2027 (5,40,000 shares)

## Lock-in Details

- 4,86,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 7000001-7486000): Lock-in until April 30, 2027
- 19,44,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 7486001-9430000): Lock-in until April 30, 2026
- 54,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 9430001-9484000): Lock-in until April 30, 2027
- 2,16,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 9484001-9700000): Lock-in until April 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact expected. This is a standard preferential allotment listing following warrant conversion. The lock-in provisions on all shares will restrict immediate tradability, minimizing potential volatility. The addition represents new equity capital raised by the company from non-promoter investors.
