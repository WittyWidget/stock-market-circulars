## Summary

Sylph Technologies Ltd (Scrip Code: 511447) has listed 489,090,000 new equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at par on rights basis. These shares are permitted to trade on BSE with effect from Tuesday, July 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- 489,090,000 new equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- Issued at par value on rights basis
- ISIN: INE706F01021
- Distinctive Numbers: 358666001 to 847756000
- Date of Allotment: July 18, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed about the new securities listing and trading permissions.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 18, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: July 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The rights issue significantly increases the share capital of Sylph Technologies Ltd. The new shares having pari-passu rights means they carry equal voting and dividend rights as existing shares, which may impact existing shareholders' proportional ownership unless they participated in the rights issue.
