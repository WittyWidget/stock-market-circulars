## Summary

BSE has notified that Bandhan US Equity Fund of Fund will observe a non-business day on October 13, 2025 on the BSE StAR MF Platform. This is a routine operational update affecting transactions for this specific mutual fund scheme.

## Key Points

- Bandhan US Equity Fund of Fund will not process transactions on October 13, 2025
- The non-business day applies only to the BSE StAR MF Platform
- This is a scheme-specific holiday, not a market-wide closure
- Only one mutual fund scheme from Bandhan Mutual Fund is affected

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors and intermediaries should note that no transactions will be processed for Bandhan US Equity Fund of Fund on October 13, 2025
- Market participants should plan their investment/redemption activities accordingly
- Transactions placed on this date will be processed on the next business day

## Important Dates

- **October 13, 2025**: Non-business day for Bandhan US Equity Fund of Fund on BSE StAR MF Platform

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This affects only one fund of fund scheme and is a routine operational matter.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Investors wishing to transact in Bandhan US Equity Fund of Fund on October 13, 2025 will need to defer their transactions to the next business day. No impact on other mutual fund schemes or market segments.

**Investor Impact**: Negligible for most investors. Only those specifically planning transactions in this scheme on October 13, 2025 need to adjust their timing.
