## Summary

TAKYON NETWORKS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544471) equity shares will be transferred from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment under M Group, effective Thursday, August 21, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: TAKYON NETWORKS LIMITED
- Scrip Code: 544471
- Current segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective date: August 21, 2025
- Contact: Mr. Anurag Jain (022-2272 8822)

## Regulatory Changes

Transfer from restrictive Trade for Trade segment to more liquid Rolling segment allows for normal intraday trading and settlement cycles.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the segment change and adjust their trading systems accordingly for the new classification.

## Important Dates

- **August 21, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer

## Impact Assessment

Positive impact on stock liquidity as shares move from restrictive Trade for Trade to Rolling segment, enabling normal trading mechanisms and potentially improved price discovery.
