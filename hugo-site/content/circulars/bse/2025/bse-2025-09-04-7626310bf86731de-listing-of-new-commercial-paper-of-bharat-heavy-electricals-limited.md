## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL) on private placement basis. The instrument is worth Rs. 500 crores (10,000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) with a 3-month maturity period and carries CARE A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 10,000 units (Rs. 500 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,411.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and IND A1+
- Scrip Code: 730149, ISIN: INE257A14AC7
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denominations of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 4, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: September 4, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 3, 2025
- **Maturity Period**: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides BHEL with short-term funding while offering investors a highly-rated debt security with 3-month maturity. The A1+ rating indicates strong creditworthiness and low default risk.
