## Summary

BSE has published the settlement programme for the Futures & Options (F&O) segment for September 2025. The circular provides a detailed schedule mapping each trading date to its corresponding daily settlement date from September 1 to September 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Settlement programme covers the entire month of September 2025
- Each trading day has a corresponding settlement date, typically T+1
- Schedule accounts for weekends and maintains continuous settlement cycles
- Final settlement for September 30 trading will occur on October 1, 2025
- Programme issued by Indian Clearing Corporation Ltd.

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced - this is a standard operational schedule announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- All F&O segment participants must follow the published settlement dates
- Clearing members must ensure funds and securities are available as per the schedule
- Market participants should align their operational processes with the settlement calendar

## Important Dates

- **Effective Period**: September 1-30, 2025
- **First Settlement**: September 2, 2025 (for September 1 trades)
- **Last Settlement**: October 1, 2025 (for September 30 trades)
- **Notice Date**: August 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational announcement with medium impact on market operations. All F&O traders, brokers, and clearing members need to align their settlement processes with this schedule. The predictable settlement cycle supports efficient market operations and risk management.
