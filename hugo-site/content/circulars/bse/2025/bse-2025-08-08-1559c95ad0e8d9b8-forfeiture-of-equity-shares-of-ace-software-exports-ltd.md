## Summary

ACE Software Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531525) has forfeited 34,096 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each that were partly paid-up due to non-payment of allotment/call monies by shareholders.

## Key Points

- 34,096 equity shares forfeited by ACE Software Exports Ltd
- Shares were partly paid-up with face value of Rs. 10/- each
- Forfeiture due to non-payment of allotment/call monies
- Distinctive numbers: 6365905 to 6400000
- Company scrip code: 531525

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the forfeiture for their records and trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: August 8, 2025
- Effective immediately

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this is a company-specific administrative action affecting only partly paid-up shares. The forfeiture reduces the company's issued share capital by the unpaid portion of these shares.
