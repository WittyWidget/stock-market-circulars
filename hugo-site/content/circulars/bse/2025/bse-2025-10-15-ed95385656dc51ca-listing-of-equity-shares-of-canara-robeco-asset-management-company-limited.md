## Summary

BSE has informed trading members that equity shares of Canara Robeco Asset Management Company Limited will be listed and admitted to trading on the exchange in due course. The company has been assigned scrip code 544580 and trading symbol CRAMC with ISIN number INE218I01013. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Canara Robeco Asset Management Company Limited
- Scrip Code: 544580
- Trading Symbol: CRAMC
- ISIN Number: INE218I01013
- Listing status: Approved, pending final listing date announcement
- Segment: Equity

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the new scrip code and symbol for future reference. Further compliance requirements will be specified in the subsequent notice containing the listing date and security details.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 15, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This announcement has minimal immediate impact on existing market operations. The listing of Canara Robeco Asset Management Company Limited will add another asset management company to BSE's equity segment. Investors interested in the mutual fund and asset management sector will have a new investment option once trading commences. The actual market impact will depend on the company's size, pricing, and investor interest, which will be clearer when the listing date and security details are announced.
