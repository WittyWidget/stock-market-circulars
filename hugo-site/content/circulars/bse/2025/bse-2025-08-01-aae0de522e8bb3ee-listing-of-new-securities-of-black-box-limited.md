## Summary

BLACK BOX LIMITED (Scrip Code: 500463) has listed 2,27,626 new equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 415/- to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion. Trading commences August 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- 2,27,626 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at Rs. 415/- premium
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis through warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 169565751 to 169793376
- ISIN: INE676A01027

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities listing
- Standard listing compliance for preferential allotment completed

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: June 16, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 4, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: February 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this represents a small preferential allotment through warrant conversion with standard lock-in provisions. The shares will be subject to lock-in until February 15, 2026, limiting immediate trading volume impact.
