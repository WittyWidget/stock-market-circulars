## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for Swan Energy Limited (CIN: L17100MH1909PLC000294). The company's name has been officially changed from "SWAN ENERGY LIMITED" to "SWAN CORP LIMITED" with effect from July 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Official name change from Swan Energy Limited to Swan Corp Limited
- Certificate issued by Central Processing Centre, Ministry of Corporate Affairs
- Company was originally incorporated as Swan Mills Limited
- Certificate digitally signed by Registrar of Companies
- Registered office address: 6, Feltham House, 2nd Floor, 10 J.N. Heredia Marg, Ballard Estate, Mumbai - 400001

## Regulatory Changes

- Corporate Identification Number (CIN) remains unchanged: L17100MH1909PLC000294
- Company continues as a company limited by shares
- Name change does not affect rights and liabilities of stakeholders

## Compliance Requirements

- Company must display old name "Swan Energy Limited" along with new name "Swan Corp Limited" for a period of two years
- Old name display required at all places where company name is mandatorily displayed under Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders advised to verify latest company status on MCA website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: July 29, 2025
- **Certificate Issue Date**: July 29, 2025 at 10:44:28 IST
- **Dual Name Display Period**: Two years from July 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

- **Operational Impact**: Minimal - core business operations remain unchanged
- **Legal Impact**: Name change officially registered with MCA, all legal documents to reflect new name
- **Stakeholder Impact**: Investors and stakeholders need to update records with new company name
- **Market Impact**: Stock symbol and trading may require updates to reflect new corporate identity
