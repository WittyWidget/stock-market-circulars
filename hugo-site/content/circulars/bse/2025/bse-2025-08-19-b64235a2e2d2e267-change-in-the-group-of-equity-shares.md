## Summary

BSE has announced that Oxford Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 514414) will be moved to Group 'X' trading category effective August 21, 2025. This follows a previous exchange notice from June 24, 2025.

## Key Points

- Oxford Industries Ltd. (Scrip Code: 514414) to be included in Group 'X'
- Change follows continuation from Exchange notice no. 20250624-12 dated June 24, 2025
- All market participants and trading members must take note

## Regulatory Changes

Reclassification of Oxford Industries Ltd. from its current group to Group 'X' trading category, which typically indicates securities with certain risk characteristics or compliance issues.

## Impact Assessment

Movement to Group 'X' typically indicates enhanced surveillance or risk classification, which may affect trading volumes, investor sentiment, and borrowing against the security. This change may impact liquidity and institutional participation in the stock.
