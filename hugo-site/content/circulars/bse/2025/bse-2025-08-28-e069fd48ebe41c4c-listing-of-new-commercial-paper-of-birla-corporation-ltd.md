## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 50 crores issued by Birla Corporation Ltd on private placement basis. The commercial paper has a face value of Rs. 5 lakhs per unit, carries CRISIL A1+ rating, and matures on November 24, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value per unit listed
- Total quantity: 1,000 units (Rs. 50 crores total)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,531 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+
- Scrip code: 730101, ISIN: INE340A14155
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities in dematerialized form only
- Trading permitted only in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 26, 2025
- Listing date: August 28, 2025
- Redemption date: November 24, 2025
- Maturity period: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The A1+ rating indicates strong credit quality, providing institutional investors with a secure short-term investment option.
