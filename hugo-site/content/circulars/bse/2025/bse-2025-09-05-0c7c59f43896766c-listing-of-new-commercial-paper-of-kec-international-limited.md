## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of KEC International Limited on its debt segment. The securities are issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each, totaling 1500 units worth Rs. 75 crores.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each listed on BSE debt segment
- Total quantity: 1500 units (Rs. 75 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,099.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip Code: 730157, ISIN: INE389H14LG1
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Axis Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 4, 2025
- Listing Date: September 5, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. Provides additional short-term funding avenue for KEC International Limited through commercial paper issuance.
