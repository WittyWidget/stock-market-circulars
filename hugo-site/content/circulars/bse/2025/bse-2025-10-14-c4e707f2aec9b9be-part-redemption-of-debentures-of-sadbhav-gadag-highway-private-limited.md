## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that SADBHAV GADAG HIGHWAY PRIVATE LIMITED has fixed the record date for part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures. Trading in these debentures will commence with a reduced face value of Rs. 81,844 per debenture effective October 16, 2025.

## Key Points

- SADBHAV GADAG HIGHWAY PRIVATE LIMITED is conducting part redemption of its Non-Convertible Debentures
- The debenture series is SGHPL-10%-RESET RATE-31-10-28 with ISIN INE04AP07016 (Code: 976067)
- Record date for the redemption: October 16, 2025
- New reduced face value: Rs. 81,844 per debenture
- Effective date of reduced face value: October 16, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-737/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that trading in the debentures will be conducted with the reduced face value effective from October 16, 2025
- Trading members should update their systems to reflect the new face value for settlement purposes

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value**: October 16, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-737/2025-2026

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it relates to a specific debt instrument of a private limited company. The part redemption is a routine corporate action that affects only holders of this particular debenture series. The reduced face value will be applicable for all future trading and settlements from the effective date. Debenture holders on record as of October 16, 2025, will receive the partial redemption amount, reducing the outstanding principal of their holdings.
