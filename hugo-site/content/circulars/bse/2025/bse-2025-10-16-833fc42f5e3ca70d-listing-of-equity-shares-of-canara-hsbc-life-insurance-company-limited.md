## Summary

BSE has notified trading members that equity shares of Canara HSBC Life Insurance Company Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange. The company has been assigned scrip code 544583, symbol CANHLIFE, and ISIN INE01TY01017. The exact date of listing and detailed security information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Canara HSBC Life Insurance Company Limited approved for listing on BSE
- Scrip Code: 544583
- Trading Symbol: CANHLIFE
- ISIN Number: INE01TY01017
- Listing date and security details to be notified separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Note the scrip code, symbol, and ISIN for system configuration
- Await further notice for specific listing date and security details
- Prepare trading systems for the new listing once the date is announced

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Addition of a life insurance company to BSE expands the insurance sector offerings on the exchange. The stock will provide investors with exposure to the joint venture between Canara Bank and HSBC in the life insurance space.

**Operational Impact**: Low to Medium - Trading members need to update their systems with the new scrip details but can await the formal listing date announcement before full operational readiness.
