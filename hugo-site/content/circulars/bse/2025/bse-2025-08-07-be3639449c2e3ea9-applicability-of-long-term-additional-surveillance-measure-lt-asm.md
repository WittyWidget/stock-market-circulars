## Summary

BSE has updated the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective August 8, 2025. The circular details securities being newly added to LT-ASM, securities moving out of the framework, and provides a consolidated list of all securities under various ASM stages.

## Key Points

- 3 securities newly shortlisted in Long Term ASM Framework: Benara Bearings and Pistons Ltd, BGR Energy Systems Ltd, and Mirc Electronics Ltd
- 2 securities moving out of LT-ASM Framework: Ortin Global Ltd and Shree Pacetronix Ltd (both due to inclusion in ESM Framework)
- No securities moving to higher or lower ASM stages within LT-ASM
- No securities moving to Direct Stage IV LT-ASM
- Consolidated list includes securities across different ASM stages (I, II, III, IV)

## Regulatory Changes

- Implementation of Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure on identified securities
- Securities marked with special indicators based on specific criteria (NSE alignment, non-promoter holding, T+0 scrips)
- Movement of securities between different surveillance frameworks (LT-ASM, ESM, GSM, IBC, Trade for Trade)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must comply with LT-ASM requirements for listed securities
- Enhanced monitoring and surveillance measures apply to securities in the framework
- Special trading restrictions and margin requirements may apply to securities under ASM

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 8, 2025 - All LT-ASM framework changes become applicable

## Impact Assessment

- **Trading Impact**: Securities under LT-ASM face enhanced surveillance with potential trading restrictions
- **Market Impact**: Moderate impact on specific securities and their trading patterns
- **Investor Impact**: Investors in affected securities should be aware of additional surveillance measures and potential liquidity constraints
- **Compliance Impact**: Trading members need to ensure adherence to LT-ASM requirements for the updated list of securities
