## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL) on private placement basis. The securities are worth Rs. 500 crores (10,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) with a 3-month tenure, carrying CARE A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Security: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 500 crores (10,000 units)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,411.50 per unit
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- ISIN: INE257A14AC7
- Scrip Code: 730149
- Scrip ID: BHEL040925

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise
- Market lot size of 1 unit

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 4, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 3, 2025
- Trading Effective Date: September 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding for BHEL's operations with a 3-month maturity period. ICICI Bank Limited serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.
