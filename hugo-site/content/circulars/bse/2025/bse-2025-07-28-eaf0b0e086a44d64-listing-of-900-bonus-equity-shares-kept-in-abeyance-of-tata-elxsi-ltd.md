## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 900 bonus equity shares of Tata Elxsi Ltd (Scrip Code: 500408) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares will be available for trading from Tuesday, July 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- 900 bonus equity shares of Rs. 10/- each are being released from abeyance
- ISIN: INE670A01012
- Shares distributed across 5 batches with specific distinctive number ranges
- Trading commences July 29, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard listing notification for previously held bonus shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading. No specific compliance actions required beyond normal trading procedures.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Tuesday, July 29, 2025 - Trading begins for the released bonus shares

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected given the small number of shares (900) relative to Tata Elxsi's total equity base. This represents the release of previously allocated but held bonus shares, providing additional liquidity in small quantities.
