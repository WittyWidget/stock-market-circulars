## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited on private placement basis, effective August 8, 2025. The securities carry an 8.50% annual interest rate with a 2-year maturity period.

## Key Points

- Issue size: ₹50 crore (50,000 securities of ₹1 lakh face value each)
- Interest rate: 8.50% per annum paid annually
- Maturity: August 6, 2027 (2-year tenure)
- Credit rating: CRISIL AA/POSITIVE
- ISIN: INE338I07180
- Scrip code: 977003

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities to be traded only in dematerialized form
- Tick size of 1 paise applicable
- Trading members must refer to placement memorandum for complete details

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 6, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 8, 2025
- Maturity date: August 6, 2027
- Interest payment: Annual basis

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing. The securities add to the corporate bond market liquidity and provide investment options for institutional investors. The AA/POSITIVE rating indicates good credit quality.
