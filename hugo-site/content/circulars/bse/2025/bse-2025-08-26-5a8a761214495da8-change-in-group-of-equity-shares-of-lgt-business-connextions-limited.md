## Summary

BSE has announced the transfer of LGT Business Connextions Limited (Scrip Code: 544489) equity shares from Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to Rolling segment (M Group) effective September 10, 2025. This change follows the exchange's previous notice dated August 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: LGT Business Connextions Limited
- Scrip Code: 544489
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: Wednesday, September 10, 2025
- Market Segment: SME

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be shifted from restrictive Trade for Trade segment to more liquid Rolling segment, allowing normal trading operations under M Group classification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the segment change and adjust their trading systems accordingly. For queries, members can contact Mr. Parag Jain at 022 2272 8685.

## Important Dates

- **September 10, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer
- **August 25, 2025**: Date of previous related notice (20250825-63)
- **August 26, 2025**: Current notice date

## Impact Assessment

This change will likely improve liquidity and trading flexibility for LGT Business Connextions Limited shares by moving from the restrictive Trade for Trade segment to the more liquid Rolling segment. The transfer to M Group indicates improved trading status for the company.
