## Summary

ACE Software Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531525) has forfeited 34,096 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each that were partly paid-up due to non-payment of allotment/call monies by shareholders.

## Key Points

- Company: ACE Software Exports Ltd (Scrip Code: 531525)
- Forfeited shares: 34,096 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Reason: Non-payment of allotment/call monies
- Share type: Partly paid-up equity shares
- Distinctive numbers: 6365905 to 6400000

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the forfeiture action for their records and trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: August 8, 2025
- Forfeiture effective immediately

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this is a company-specific corporate action affecting partly paid-up shares. The forfeiture reduces the company's share capital and may affect existing shareholders' holdings proportionally.
