## Summary

BSE has updated the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective September 10, 2025. The circular includes 10 new securities being added to LT-ASM Stage I, 3 securities moving to higher ASM stages, and 1 security (S.A.L. Steel Ltd) moving out of the framework due to inclusion in ESM framework.

## Key Points

- 10 securities newly shortlisted in Long Term ASM Framework Stage I
- 3 securities moved to higher ASM stages (all to Stage II)
- 1 security (S.A.L. Steel Ltd) moving out due to ESM inclusion
- No securities moving to lower ASM stages or direct Stage IV
- Consolidated list shows securities across different ASM stages

## Regulatory Changes

- Implementation of Long Term ASM framework updates
- Stage progression for existing securities under surveillance
- Integration with other surveillance frameworks (ESM, GSM, IBC)

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must comply with LT-ASM trading restrictions for affected securities
- Enhanced surveillance measures apply to all listed securities
- Adherence to stage-specific trading conditions

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 10, 2025
- All changes to surveillance measures become applicable from this date

## Impact Assessment

- **Trading Impact**: Enhanced surveillance on 10 new securities may affect liquidity and trading patterns
- **Market Operations**: Stricter monitoring and compliance requirements for affected scrips
- **Investor Impact**: Limited trading flexibility for securities under LT-ASM framework
- **Regulatory Impact**: Strengthened market surveillance and risk management measures
