## Summary

Bandhan Mutual Fund will launch BANDHAN SILVER ETF New Fund Offer (NFO) on BSE StAR MF Platform from October 13-15, 2025. The scheme includes both regular and direct plans, with tentative allotment scheduled for October 17, 2025. Members must ensure clear funds are available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on October 16, 2025, for allotment.

## Key Points

- NFO period: October 13-15, 2025
- Two schemes available: BANDHAN SILVER ETF (Regular and Direct Plan)
- Scheme category: Equity
- Tentative allotment date: October 17, 2025
- Extended trading hours: NFO extended till midnight on October 15, 2025, for lumpsum purchases only
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled
- Pending transactions remain in system till 3:00 PM on last NFO pay-out day or T+5, whichever is earlier
- Cheques and OTM payments accepted during NFO period subject to fund clearance
- Switches accepted during NFO period only if funds received before allotment date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard NFO launch announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are available in ICCL Account by 6:00 PM on October 16, 2025
- Transactions without client-initiated funds by 9:00 AM on T+1 day will be cancelled and reported to RTA/AMC with CC and N flag
- Switch transactions will only be considered for allotment if funds for switch-out scheme are received before the NFO allotment date
- Cheques and OTM payments must result in clear funds in ICCL account by 6:00 PM on October 16, 2025

## Important Dates

- **October 10, 2025**: Notice date
- **October 13, 2025**: NFO opening date
- **October 15, 2025**: NFO closing date (extended till midnight for lumpsum purchases)
- **October 16, 2025**: Deadline for clear funds in ICCL Account (6:00 PM)
- **October 17, 2025**: Tentative allotment date
- **T+1 day**: Client must initiate funds by 9:00 AM or transaction will be cancelled
- **T+5 or last NFO pay-out day**: Pending transactions remain in system till 3:00 PM, whichever is earlier

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine mutual fund NFO launch with no broader market implications.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Standard NFO procedures apply. Members trading on BSE StAR MF Platform need to follow established fund settlement timelines and processes for BANDHAN SILVER ETF subscriptions.

**Investor Impact**: Low - Provides investment opportunity in silver-focused ETF for investors interested in precious metals exposure through mutual fund route.
