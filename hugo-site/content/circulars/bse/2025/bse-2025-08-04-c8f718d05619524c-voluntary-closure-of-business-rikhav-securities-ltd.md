## Summary

Rikhav Securities Ltd. (Member No. 3174) has voluntarily ceased its currency derivative trading operations on BSE with immediate effect from August 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Rikhav Securities Ltd. (Member No. 3174) voluntarily closing currency derivative business
- Closure effective immediately from August 4, 2025
- Contact membership.ops@bseindia.com for clarifications
- Notice issued by Amit Kadam, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a voluntary business closure by a trading member.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the closure for awareness purposes. No specific compliance actions required from other members.

## Important Dates

- **August 4, 2025**: Effective date of voluntary closure of currency derivative operations

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a single trading member's voluntary decision to cease operations in the currency derivative segment. Other market participants are unaffected.
