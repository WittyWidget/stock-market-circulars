## Summary

BSE has listed 19,94,17,428 equity shares of Canara Robeco Asset Management Company Limited on October 14, 2025. The listing includes shares with varying lock-in periods for different investor categories, including anchor investors and other shareholders. All shares are in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares listed: 19,94,17,428
- Listing date: October 14, 2025
- All shares are fully paid-up and in demat form
- 3,48,98,051 shares are not under lock-in
- 16,45,19,377 shares are under various lock-in periods
- Anchor investor allocations subject to specific lock-in requirements
- Registered office: Construction House, 4th Floor, 5, Walchand Hirachand Marg, Ballard Estate, Mumbai â€“ 400 001

## Lock-in Details

### Anchor Investors
- 74,78,152 equity shares: 30-day lock-in until November 12, 2025
- 74,78,154 equity shares: 90-day lock-in until January 11, 2026

### Other Categories
- 3,48,98,051 shares: Fully paid-up, not under lock-in (distinctive nos. 1,49,56,307 to 4,98,54,357)
- 5,59,36,588 shares: Lock-in until April 16, 2026 (distinctive nos. 4,98,54,358 to 10,57,90,945)
- 2,03,40,578 shares: Lock-in until April 16, 2027 (distinctive nos. 10,57,90,946 to 12,61,31,523)
- 5,37,42,997 shares: Lock-in until April 16, 2026 (distinctive nos. 12,61,31,524 to 17,98,74,520)
- 1,95,42,908 shares: Lock-in until April 16, 2027 (distinctive nos. 17,98,74,521 to 19,94,17,428)

## Compliance Requirements

- Lock-in details as per pages 119-121 of the company's prospectus must be adhered to
- Shares under lock-in cannot be traded until respective lock-in periods expire
- Distinctive numbers are for presentation purposes only as all shares are in demat form

## Important Dates

- **October 14, 2025**: Listing date
- **November 12, 2025**: 30-day anchor investor lock-in expiry
- **January 11, 2026**: 90-day anchor investor lock-in expiry
- **April 16, 2026**: Lock-in expiry for certain share categories
- **April 16, 2027**: Lock-in expiry for other share categories

## Impact Assessment

This listing adds a new asset management company to BSE's equity segment. The staggered lock-in periods will result in gradual supply of shares to the market over 18 months. The significant portion of freely tradable shares (3,48,98,051 shares or approximately 17.5% of total) provides immediate liquidity. Investors should note the lock-in schedules when assessing trading volumes and price discovery in the initial months post-listing.
