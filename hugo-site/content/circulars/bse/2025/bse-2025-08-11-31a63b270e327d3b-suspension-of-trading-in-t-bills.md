## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in three Treasury Bills (T-Bills) effective August 12, 2025, as they approach their redemption/maturity date. Trading members are advised not to deal in these specific T-Bills.

## Key Points

- Three T-Bills will be suspended from trading: 182TB14825, 91TB140825, and 364TB15825
- Suspension effective from August 12, 2025
- Reason: T-Bills maturing on redemption date
- Applies to debt segment trading
- Trading members must cease dealing in these instruments

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes - standard procedure for maturing debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified T-Bills from August 12, 2025
- Members should update their systems to prevent trading in these instruments
- Compliance with directive DR-693/2025-2026

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 11, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: August 12, 2025
- **T-Bills Maturity**: On respective redemption dates

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this is a routine administrative action for maturing debt instruments. Affects only holders and traders of the three specific T-Bills mentioned. No broader market implications expected.
