## Summary

BSE has listed commercial paper worth Rs. 15 crore issued by Standard Chartered Securities (India) Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to trading on BSE Debt segment effective August 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5 lakh denomination each
- Total quantity: 300 units (Rs. 15 crore total)
- Scrip Code: 729943, ISIN: INE472H14672
- Credit Rating: ICRA A1+
- Issue price: Rs. 4,65,983 per unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 31, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 1, 2025
- Redemption Date: July 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by a financial services company. The ICRA A1+ rating indicates high credit quality. Trading limited to institutional participants due to high denomination requirements.
