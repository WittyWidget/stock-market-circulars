## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Time Technoplast Limited on its Debt segment effective August 4, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a total value of Rs. 15 crores (300 units of Rs. 5 lakh each).

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (300 units)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,81,512.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- ISIN: INE508G14HV5
- Scrip Code: 729957
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - routine listing process for commercial paper securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- IDBI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: August 4, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: August 4, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 2, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The securities provide short-term funding option for Time Technoplast Limited with good credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA), indicating low credit risk for investors.
