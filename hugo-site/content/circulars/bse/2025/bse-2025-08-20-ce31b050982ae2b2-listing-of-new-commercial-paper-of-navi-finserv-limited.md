## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Navi Finserv Limited worth Rs. 50 crore (1000 securities of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued on private placement basis. The securities carry CARE A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings and will trade in the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Quantity: 1000 securities (Total value: Rs. 50 crore)
- Scrip Code: 730047, Scrip ID: NFL19825
- ISIN: INE342T14EA3
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+ and IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,53,630.50 per security
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will trade under ISIN INE342T14EA3

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 19, 2025
- Listing Date: August 20, 2025
- Redemption Date: August 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding option for Navi Finserv Limited and investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
