## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in commercial paper of Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited (scrip code 729916, ISIN: INE975F14B59) effective August 4, 2025, as the instrument matures on the redemption date.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited to be suspended from trading
- Scrip Code: 729916
- ISIN: INE975F14B59
- Suspension effective from August 4, 2025
- Reference: DR-687/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational measure for maturing debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the aforementioned commercial paper from August 4, 2025
- Trading members are requested to take note of the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 1, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: August 4, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: Not explicitly specified but occurs around suspension date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine suspension of a single commercial paper issue due to natural maturity. Standard procedure for debt instruments reaching redemption date.
