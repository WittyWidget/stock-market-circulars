## Summary

BSE has updated the Short Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective August 5, 2025. Four securities are newly shortlisted for ST-ASM Stage I, while seven securities are moving out of the framework. The circular provides consolidated lists of all securities currently under various stages of ST-ASM.

## Key Points

- 4 securities newly shortlisted in ST-ASM Stage I framework
- 7 securities moving out of ST-ASM framework
- No securities moving to higher or lower ASM stages
- Consolidated list shows securities across different ST-ASM stages
- Framework includes both regular and SME scrips

## Regulatory Changes

- Implementation of updated ST-ASM framework from August 5, 2025
- Enhanced surveillance measures for volatile securities
- Automatic movement of securities based on trading patterns

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must comply with ST-ASM trading restrictions
- Enhanced margin requirements for securities under ST-ASM
- Adherence to position limits and delivery requirements

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 5, 2025 - New ST-ASM framework implementation

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - affects trading liquidity and costs for listed securities. **Operational Impact**: Traders and brokers must adjust strategies for securities entering or exiting surveillance framework. Securities moving out may experience increased trading activity due to relaxed restrictions.
