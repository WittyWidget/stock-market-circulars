## Summary

BSE announces the availability of Century Business Media Limited's Draft Offer Document for their SME IPO, filed on September 5, 2025. The document is available for public review and comments for 21 days as per ICDR Regulations.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed on September 5, 2025
- Contains all disclosures specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. Standard compliance with existing ICDR Regulations for SME IPO disclosures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public can review and submit comments on the Draft Offer Document
- Comments must be submitted within 21 days from September 8, 2025
- All comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Important Dates

- Document Filing Date: September 5, 2025
- Notice Date: September 8, 2025
- Comment Deadline: September 29, 2025 (21 days from notice)

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact to SME segment participants and potential investors in Century Business Media Limited. Standard IPO process with no broader market implications. Provides transparency opportunity for public input on the offering.
