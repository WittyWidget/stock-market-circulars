## Summary

BSE announces that BARODA BNP PARIBAS GOLD ETF FUND OF FUND will be available for ongoing transactions on the BSE StAR MF Platform starting August 22, 2025. The fund will support subscription, redemption, and systematic investment plans.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening for ongoing transactions effective August 22, 2025
- Available on BSE StAR MF Platform
- Supports subscription, redemption, SIP, STP & SWP facilities
- Both Regular and Direct plan options available
- Growth option only for both plans

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this notice.

## Compliance Requirements

Members should note the availability of the fund for client transactions from the specified date.

## Important Dates

- **August 22, 2025**: Fund reopening date for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine fund availability notice. Provides additional investment options for investors seeking gold ETF exposure through fund of fund structure via BSE StAR MF Platform.
