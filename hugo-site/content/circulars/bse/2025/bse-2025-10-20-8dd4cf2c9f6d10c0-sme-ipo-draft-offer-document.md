## Summary

BSE has announced the availability of the Draft Offer Document for Fractal Industries Limited's SME IPO. The document, filed on October 18, 2025, contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018. The draft is available on the SME Exchange website for a minimum period of 21 days for public comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Fractal Industries Limited on October 18, 2025
- Document available on www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date (October 20, 2025)
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document contains all disclosures per Schedule VI of SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard procedural notice as per existing SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018 requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Draft Offer Document must be hosted on SME Exchange website for at least 21 days
- Public has opportunity to submit comments on the draft document
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com within the specified timeframe

## Important Dates

- **Filing Date**: October 18, 2025 - Draft Offer Document filed by Fractal Industries Limited
- **Notice Date**: October 20, 2025 - Public comment period begins
- **Comment Deadline**: November 10, 2025 (21 days from notice date) - Last date for submitting public comments

## Impact Assessment

This notice has minimal immediate market impact as it relates to the early stage of a single SME company's IPO process. The draft document stage precedes the actual IPO launch and is primarily informational for potential investors and stakeholders. Public comments may influence the final offer document. Impact is limited to SME segment and stakeholders interested in Fractal Industries Limited specifically.
