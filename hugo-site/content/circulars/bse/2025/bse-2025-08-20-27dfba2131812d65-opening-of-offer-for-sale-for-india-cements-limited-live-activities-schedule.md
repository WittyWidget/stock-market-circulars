## Summary

BSE announces the Offer for Sale (OFS) schedule for India Cements Limited (INDIACEM) for August 21-22, 2025. The OFS comprises 2,01,12,330 shares with a floor price of Rs 368 per share, using price priority allocation methodology.

## Key Points

- Offer Size: 2,01,12,330 shares (face value Rs 10)
- Floor Price: Rs 368 per share
- Retail Offer: 20,11,233 shares (10% reserved for retail investors)
- Non-Retail Offer: 1,81,01,097 shares
- Security Code: 530005, ISIN: INE383A01012
- Market Lot: 1 share, Tick Size: Rs 0.01
- Allocation Method: Price Priority Method

## Trading Schedule

**Day 1 (August 21, 2025 - T Day):**
- Institutional/Mutual Fund/Others/NII bidding: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
- Bid modification and cancellation allowed during same hours

**Day 2 (August 22, 2025 - T+1 Day):**
- Give up/Take up for institutional 0% margin bids: Up to 7:30 AM
- Retail Investor (RI/RIC) bidding: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM
- Unallocated T-day bidders can carry forward bids

## Investor Categories

- **Retail Investors (RI/RIC)**: Bid value up to Rs 2 lakh, 100% upfront margin, settlement T+2
- **Non-Institutional Investors (NII)**: Above Rs 2 lakh retail bids, 100% margin, settlement T+1
- **Institutional Investors**: Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, Others with 100% or 0% margin options

The OFS of over 2 crore shares represents a significant equity offering that may impact India Cements' stock price and trading volumes. Retail investors have dedicated allocation and separate bidding day, while institutional investors get priority access with flexible margin requirements.
