## Summary

KRAZYBEE SERVICES PVT LTD has announced part redemption of its debentures, resulting in a reduced face value of Rs. 62,500 per debenture. The new face value will be effective from August 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: KRAZYBEE SERVICES PVT LTD
- Scrip Code: KSPL-12.10%-20-10-26-PVT (INE07HK07775 / 976114)
- New face value: Rs. 62,500 per debenture
- Action type: Part redemption of debentures
- Reference: DR-674/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face value for trading purposes
- Debentures will trade at the new reduced face value from the effective date

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 31, 2025
- Effective Date: August 1, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This part redemption reduces the face value of KRAZYBEE SERVICES debentures, affecting their trading price and yield calculations. Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the new face value for accurate pricing and settlement.
