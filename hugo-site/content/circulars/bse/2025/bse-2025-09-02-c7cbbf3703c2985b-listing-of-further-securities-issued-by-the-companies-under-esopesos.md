## Summary

This circular provides information about the listing of additional securities issued by companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Ownership Schemes (ESOS).

## Key Points

- Notification of new securities listing under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Standard listing procedure for employee stock options

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes indicated - routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must follow standard listing procedures for ESOP/ESOS securities
- Compliance with SEBI guidelines on employee stock options

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: September 2, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this represents routine listing of employee stock options. These securities are typically issued to employees as part of compensation packages and do not significantly affect market liquidity or pricing.
