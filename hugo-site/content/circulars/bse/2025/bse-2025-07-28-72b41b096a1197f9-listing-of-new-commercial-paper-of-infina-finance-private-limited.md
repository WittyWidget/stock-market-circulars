## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 300 crore issued by Infina Finance Private Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from July 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Issue size: 6,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each (total Rs. 300 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,087 per unit (face value Rs. 5,00,000)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 729915, Scrip ID: IFPL25725
- ISIN: INE879F14LD2
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Issuing and Paying agent: Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: July 25, 2025
- Listing date: July 28, 2025
- Redemption date: October 24, 2025
- Maturity period: 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding option for Infina Finance and investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
