## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper of Rs. 200 crore (4,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued by 360 ONE WAM LIMITED on private placement basis. The securities will trade on BSE Debt segment from August 7, 2025, with a 90-day tenure maturing on November 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper worth Rs. 200 crore (4,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,695 per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip Code: 729971, Scrip ID: OWL60825
- ISIN: INE466L14EQ3
- HDFC Bank Limited is the Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise for trading
- Market lot size of 1 unit

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 6, 2025
- Listing Date: August 7, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 4, 2025
- Tenure: 90 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding for 360 ONE WAM LIMITED and offers investors a highly-rated debt instrument with 90-day maturity. Trading will be limited to institutional and high-value investors due to Rs. 5 lakh minimum denomination.
