## Summary

BSE has issued a new ISIN number INE371F01024 for Chandrima Mercantiles Ltd (Scrip Code: 540829) following the sub-division of equity shares from Rs.10 per share to Re.1 per share. The new ISIN will be effective for trades executed on and from August 20, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: CHANDRIMA MERCANTILES LTD (Scrip Code: 540829)
- New ISIN Number: INE371F01024
- Share sub-division ratio: Rs.10 to Re.1 (10:1 split)
- Continuation of previous notice dated August 14, 2025
- Dividend record number: DR-698/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will now have a face value of Re.1 each instead of Rs.10 each, requiring a new ISIN for identification and trading purposes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems with the new ISIN number
- All trades from ex-date onwards must use the new ISIN INE371F01024
- Systems must be prepared for the transition on the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Ex-Date**: August 20, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 18, 2025
- **Previous Notice**: August 14, 2025 (Notice No. 20250814-42)

## Impact Assessment

This change affects trading and settlement systems for Chandrima Mercantiles shares. Trading members and market participants need to ensure their systems are updated to handle the new ISIN number from the ex-date. The share sub-division will increase the number of shares outstanding while reducing the face value proportionally.
