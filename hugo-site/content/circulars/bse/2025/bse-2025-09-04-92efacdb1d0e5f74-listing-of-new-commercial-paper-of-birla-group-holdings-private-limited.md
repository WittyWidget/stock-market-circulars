## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 200 crore issued by Birla Group Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective September 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5 lakh denomination each, total 4000 units
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,785 per unit (face value Rs. 5,00,000)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 730151, ISIN: INE09OL14HU4
- Axis Bank Ltd serves as issuing and paying agent
- Trading only in dematerialized form with tick size of 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Contact BSE debt department for clarifications on specified phone numbers

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: September 4, 2025
- Trading commencement: September 4, 2025
- Redemption date: December 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. Provides short-term funding option for Birla Group Holdings and investment opportunity for debt market participants.
