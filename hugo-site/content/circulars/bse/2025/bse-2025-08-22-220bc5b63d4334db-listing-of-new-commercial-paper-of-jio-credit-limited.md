## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 100 crore issued by Jio Credit Limited on private placement basis. The commercial paper has a face value of Rs. 5 lakh each with 2000 units listed under scrip code 730045.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 2000 units (Rs. 100 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,827 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- ISIN: INE282H14048
- Scrip code: 730045
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Ltd appointed as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 21, 2025
- Listing date: August 22, 2025
- Redemption date: November 14, 2025
- Tenure: 85 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The instrument provides short-term funding option for Jio Credit Limited and investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
