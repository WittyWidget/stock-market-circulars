## Summary

Aditya Birla Sun Life Mutual Fund has introduced daily Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) frequency for two index funds on the BSE StAR MF platform, effective September 1, 2025. This expands investment options for retail investors in these specific schemes.

## Key Points

- Daily SIP frequency now available for Aditya Birla Sun Life BSE 500 Momentum 50 Index Fund
- Daily SIP frequency now available for Aditya Birla Sun Life BSE 500 Quality 50 Index Fund
- Feature launched on BSE StAR MF platform
- AMC reserves right to modify scheme list and features in future
- All other terms and conditions of existing schemes remain unchanged

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is an operational enhancement to existing mutual fund schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Notice forms integral part of Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM)
- Standard mutual fund investment disclosures apply
- Investors must read all scheme related documents carefully

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 1, 2025 (Monday)
- **Notice Date**: August 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Low market impact as this is an operational enhancement for specific mutual fund schemes. Provides additional flexibility for retail investors in systematic investment planning but does not affect broader market operations or trading mechanisms.
