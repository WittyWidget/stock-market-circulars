## Summary

BSE has announced that units of Anantam Highways Trust, an Infrastructure Investment Trust (InvIT) IPO, will be listed and admitted to dealings on the exchange. The trust has been assigned scrip code 544579, symbol ANANTAM, and ISIN number INE1UA823019. The specific listing date and detailed securities information will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- Trust Name: Anantam Highways Trust (InvIT - IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544579
- Trading Symbol: ANANTAM
- ISIN Number: INE1UA823019
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed about the upcoming listing and should prepare their systems to handle trading in these units once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 15, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This listing provides investors with a new opportunity to invest in highway infrastructure through an InvIT structure. InvITs offer exposure to operational infrastructure assets with potential for regular income distribution. The impact is moderate as it expands investment options in the infrastructure sector, particularly for investors seeking yield-generating assets. Trading members should monitor for the subsequent notice announcing the specific listing date and trading commencement details.
