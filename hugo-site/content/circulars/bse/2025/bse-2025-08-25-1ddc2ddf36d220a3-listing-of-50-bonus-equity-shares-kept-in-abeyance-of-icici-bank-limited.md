## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 50 bonus equity shares of ICICI Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 532174) that were kept in abeyance. These shares will be listed and permitted for trading effective Tuesday, August 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- 50 bonus equity shares of Rs. 2/- each kept in abeyance are being listed
- ISIN: INE090A01021
- Distinctive numbers: 6412577057 to 6412577106
- Effective date: August 26, 2025
- Company: ICICI Bank Limited (Scrip Code: 532174)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and trading permissions.

## Important Dates

- **August 26, 2025**: Effective date for listing and trading of bonus shares

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a small number of bonus shares (50 shares) previously held in abeyance being made available for trading. This is primarily an administrative action for ICICI Bank shareholders.
