## Summary

BSE has published the draft offer document for Gujarat Peanut and Agri Products Limited's SME IPO, filed on August 14, 2025. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available for public comments for 21 days.

## Key Points

- Draft offer document filed by Gujarat Peanut and Agri Products Limited on August 14, 2025
- Document hosted on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Document contains all required disclosures under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. Standard SME IPO process following existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public has 21 days to submit comments on the draft offer document
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Company must comply with all disclosures under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations

## Important Dates

- Filing Date: August 14, 2025
- Notice Date: August 19, 2025
- Comment Period: 21 days from August 19, 2025 (until September 9, 2025)

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a standard SME IPO process. Relevant primarily for potential investors interested in Gujarat Peanut and Agri Products Limited. The public comment period allows stakeholders to provide feedback before the IPO proceeds.
