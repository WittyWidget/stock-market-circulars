## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of SK Minerals & Additives Limited will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544584, symbol SKM, and ISIN number INE13YH01017. The exact listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- SK Minerals & Additives Limited equity shares to be listed on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544584
- Trading Symbol: SKM
- ISIN Number: INE13YH01017
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members should note the company details for future trading
- Members should await separate notice for listing date and security details before commencing trading

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 16, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine new listing on the SME segment with minimal market-wide impact. The announcement provides preliminary information to trading members about the upcoming listing. Investors and traders interested in SME securities should watch for the follow-up notice containing the actual listing date and detailed security information before trading can commence.
