## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 281,974 new equity shares of UNO Minda Limited (Scrip Code: 532539) with effect from Tuesday, October 28, 2025. These shares were issued pursuant to a scheme of amalgamation involving Kosei Minda Aluminum Company Private Limited, Kosei Minda Mould Private Limited, and Minda Kosei Aluminum Wheel Private Limited (Transferor Companies) merging into Uno Minda Limited (Transferee Company).

## Key Points

- 281,974 equity shares of face value Rs. 2/- each to be listed
- Shares issued under scheme of amalgamation approved under sections 230-232 of Companies Act, 2013
- ISIN: INE405E01023
- Distinctive Numbers: 575819072 to 576101045
- Date of Allotment: August 06, 2025
- Trading effective from: October 28, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing approval following corporate restructuring.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities in their systems effective October 28, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 06, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. The listing represents completion of an amalgamation scheme involving subsidiary/group companies. The number of shares (281,974) is relatively small and represents corporate restructuring rather than fresh capital raising. This is a routine corporate action for existing shareholders and creditors of the merged entities.
