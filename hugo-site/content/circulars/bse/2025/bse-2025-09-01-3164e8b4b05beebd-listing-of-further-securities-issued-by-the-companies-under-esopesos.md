## Summary

BSE has listed additional securities for 6 companies issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP/ESOS), effective from Tuesday, September 2, 2025. All listed securities have no lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 6 companies have additional securities listed under ESOP/ESOS
- Total of 187,611 shares across all companies
- No lock-in restrictions apply to any of the listed securities
- Trading commences September 2, 2025

## Companies and Share Details

- **Barbeque-Nation Hospitality Limited**: 5,624 shares (Face Value ₹5)
- **Coforge Limited**: 12,230 shares (Face Value ₹2)
- **ICICI Bank Ltd**: 68,621 shares (Face Value ₹2)
- **Kaynes Technology India Limited**: 77,561 shares (Face Value ₹10)
- **Lodha Developers Limited**: 18,583 shares (Face Value ₹10)
- **Metro Brands Limited**: 4,192 shares (Face Value ₹5)

## Important Dates

- **Effective Trading Date**: September 2, 2025
- **Notice Date**: September 1, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine ESOP share listings with relatively small quantities. No lock-in restrictions provide immediate liquidity to option holders.
