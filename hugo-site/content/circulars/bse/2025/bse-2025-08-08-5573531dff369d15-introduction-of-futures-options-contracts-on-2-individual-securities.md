## Summary

BSE will introduce futures and options contracts for two individual securities - Nuvama Wealth Management Ltd and Suzlon Energy Ltd - in the Equity Derivatives segment starting August 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two new underlying securities added to derivatives trading
- Contracts available from August 18, 2025
- Contract details will be available in derivatives master file from August 14, 2025
- Master file accessible through BSE extranet and website

## Regulatory Changes

Expansion of equity derivatives segment to include:
- Nuvama Wealth Management Ltd (Scrip Code: 543988)
- Suzlon Energy Ltd (Scrip Code: 532667)

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new contract availability
- Members should refer to derivatives contract master file for detailed specifications
- Access contract details through BSE extranet or official website

## Important Dates

- **August 8, 2025**: Notice date
- **August 14, 2025**: Contract details available in master file (end of day)
- **August 18, 2025**: Trading commences for new F&O contracts

## Impact Assessment

The introduction of these contracts will provide additional derivative trading opportunities for market participants. Impact is moderate as it involves only two specific securities but enhances the derivatives product suite available on BSE.
