## Summary

BSE has revised price bands for 20 securities effective October 17, 2025. The revised bands range from 2% (most restrictive) to 20%, replacing their existing levels. This circular updates trading parameters under BSE's surveillance framework to manage price volatility in these scrips.

## Key Points

- 20 securities will have revised price bands effective October 17, 2025
- Price bands range from 2% to 20% depending on the security
- Most restrictive 2% band applied to: Chandrima Mercantiles, Inter Globe Finance, Mirc Electronics, Titan Biotech, and Triton Corp
- 5% band applied to: Gujarat Lease Financing, Lyons Corporate Market, Onix Solar Energy, Raj Packaging Industries, Tasty Dairy Specialities, Titan Intech, and Twamev Construction
- 10% band applied to: Bits Ltd, Earthstahl & Alloys, and Garnet Construction
- 20% band (most relaxed) applied to: Chatterbox Technologies, Earkart, Gujarat Peanut And Agri Products, Jinkushal Industries, and Telge Projects
- Members can seek clarifications at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

Price band adjustments represent enhanced surveillance measures under BSE's market monitoring framework. Tighter price bands (2-5%) indicate heightened surveillance for certain securities, limiting maximum price movement allowed in a single trading day.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must implement revised price bands in their trading systems before market opening on October 17, 2025
- All orders for affected securities must comply with new price band limits
- Members should ensure their risk management systems reflect updated parameters

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Affects trading dynamics for 20 securities by limiting intraday price movement ranges. Tighter bands (2-5%) will reduce volatility but may also impact liquidity and price discovery.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update systems before October 17, 2025. Orders exceeding revised price bands will be rejected, requiring traders to adjust their strategies for affected securities.

**Investor Impact**: Investors in these securities will experience restricted price movements, providing protection against excessive volatility but potentially limiting profit opportunities in volatile market conditions.
