## Summary

Baroda Mutual Fund has communicated a change in the settlement type for the Baroda BNP Paribas Multi Asset Active Fund of Funds (FOF) on the BSE StAR MF platform. The settlement cycle will be extended from T+2 to T+3, effective from September 3, 2025.

## Key Points

- Settlement type changes from T+2 to T+3 for Baroda BNP Paribas Multi Asset Active FOF
- Change applies to transactions on BSE StAR MF platform
- Effective date: September 3, 2025
- Only one scheme affected by this change

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned. This is an operational change in settlement cycle for a specific mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

Members trading in the affected scheme must adjust their settlement processes to accommodate the new T+3 settlement cycle instead of the current T+2 cycle.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: September 2, 2025
- Effective Date: September 3, 2025 (settlement type change becomes operational)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal operational impact expected as the change affects only one mutual fund scheme. Investors and members will experience a one-day extension in the settlement period for transactions in this specific fund, potentially affecting liquidity timing but not overall market operations.
