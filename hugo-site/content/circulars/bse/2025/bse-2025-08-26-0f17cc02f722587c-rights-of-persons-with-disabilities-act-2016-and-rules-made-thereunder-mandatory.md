## Summary

BSE mandates all Research Analysts to submit compliance confirmation for Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 through an online form by August 31, 2025, following SEBI circular requirements.

## Key Points

- All Research Analysts must submit compliance confirmation
- Submission required through Microsoft Forms online portal
- References SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/ITD-1/ITD_VIAP/P/CIR/2025/111 dated July 31, 2025
- References BSE Circular No. 20250807-11 dated August 07, 2025
- Specific compliance with point no.5 (1) of the referenced circular

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced - this is an implementation notice for existing Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 compliance requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- Research Analysts must submit confirmation on compliance with Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016
- Submission must be made through online form at https://forms.office.com/r/VDQGDQVRGx
- Compliance specifically relates to point no.5 (1) of the referenced SEBI circular
- Mandatory for all Research Analysts registered with BSE

## Important Dates

- **August 31, 2025**: Deadline for Research Analysts to submit compliance confirmation
- July 31, 2025: Date of original SEBI circular
- August 07, 2025: Date of previous BSE circular on same subject

## Impact Assessment

Limited operational impact as this is primarily an administrative compliance submission. Research Analysts need to ensure they understand and comply with disability rights requirements and submit confirmation through the specified online portal by the deadline.
