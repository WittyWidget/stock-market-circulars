## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of new debt securities issued by MAGNUM VENTURES LIMITED on private placement basis, effective August 14, 2025. The securities carry an 18% annual interest rate with monthly interest payments and mature on August 31, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 5,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 975493, Scrip ID: 18MVL27
- ISIN Number: INE387I07013 (further listings under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Date of Allotment: August 12, 2025
- Credit Rating: Acuite BB Stable
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities will trade under the specified ISIN number
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details
- Contact debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 12, 2025
- Listing Date: August 14, 2025
- Interest Payment: Monthly
- Redemption Date: August 31, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine private placement debt listing. The securities provide investors with a high-yield debt instrument (18% p.a.) from Magnum Ventures Limited with a BB credit rating.
