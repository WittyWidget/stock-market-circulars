## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 6,12,667 new equity shares of Anand Rayons Limited (Scrip Code: 542721) effective from Monday, August 25, 2025. These shares were issued through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis to non-promoters.

## Key Points

- 6,12,667 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 68/-
- Issue price: Rs. 78/- per share
- Allotment date: May 24, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 25, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 20219055 to 20831721
- ISIN: INE02GA01012

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure for warrant conversion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability for trading
- Lock-in period applicable until February 28, 2026
- All 6,12,667 shares subject to lock-in restrictions

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: May 24, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 25, 2025
- Lock-in Period Ends: February 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine listing of equity shares through warrant conversion. The shares represent additional equity capital for Anand Rayons Limited with a lock-in period ensuring no immediate selling pressure.
