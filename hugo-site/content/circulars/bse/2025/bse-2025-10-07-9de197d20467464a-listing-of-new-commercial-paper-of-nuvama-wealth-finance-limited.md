## Summary

BSE has listed two new Commercial Papers issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective October 7, 2025. Both instruments have face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each and will trade in dematerialized form only on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Two Commercial Papers with 500 units each listed on BSE Debt segment
- First CP: Scrip Code 728382 (NWFL61224), ISIN INE918K14CD7, redemption on November 25, 2025
- Second CP: Scrip Code 730358 (NWFL61025), ISIN INE918K14CS5, redemption on December 31, 2025
- Issue price: Rs. 495,116.50 (first CP) and Rs. 491,431.50 (second CP)
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+/CRISIL A1+ for first CP; CRISIL A1+/ICRA A1+ for second CP
- Date of allotment: October 6, 2025
- Issuing and Paying Agent: ICICI Bank Limited
- Trading only in dematerialized form with standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Market lot size is 1 unit

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date:** October 7, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** October 6, 2025
- **Redemption Date (NWFL61224):** November 25, 2025
- **Redemption Date (NWFL61025):** December 31, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term funding instruments for Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited with maturities ranging from approximately 50 to 86 days. High credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk. Impact limited to debt market participants and investors in commercial papers.
