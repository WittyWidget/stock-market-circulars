## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debentures of CHEMMANUR CREDITS AND INVESTMENTS LIMITED effective August 25, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date for interest payment and redemption of its non-convertible debentures.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to CCIL-10.50%-10-9-25-NCD (ISIN: INE051307AW4, Code: 939315)
- Record date set for August 25, 2025
- Purpose includes both redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Trading members advised not to deal in the specified debentures

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a standard corporate action procedure for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified CCIL debentures from August 25, 2025
- Members are required to take note of the suspension

## Important Dates

- **August 25, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **August 25, 2025**: No dealings from this date
- **August 18, 2025**: Notice date

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this is a routine corporate action for debenture maturity. The suspension affects only the specific CCIL debenture series and is temporary for record date determination. Investors holding these debentures will receive interest payment and redemption proceeds as per the company's announcement.
