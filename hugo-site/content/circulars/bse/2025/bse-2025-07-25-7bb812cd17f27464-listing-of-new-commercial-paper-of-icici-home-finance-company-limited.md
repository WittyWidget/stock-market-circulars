## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED on private placement basis. The instrument consists of 1,500 units with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, totaling Rs. 75 crores. The commercial paper is rated CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ with a tenure of 3 months from July 25, 2025 to October 24, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 1,500 units (Rs. 75 crores total)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,836.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 729897 (IHFC250725)
- ISIN: INE071G14GY0
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt instrument listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded under the specified ISIN number INE071G14GY0

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: July 25, 2025
- Listing effective date: July 25, 2025
- Date of redemption: October 24, 2025
- Tenure: 3 months (91 days)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. The instrument provides short-term funding for ICICI Home Finance Company Limited and offers investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate strong creditworthiness. Trading will be limited to institutional participants due to high denomination requirements.
