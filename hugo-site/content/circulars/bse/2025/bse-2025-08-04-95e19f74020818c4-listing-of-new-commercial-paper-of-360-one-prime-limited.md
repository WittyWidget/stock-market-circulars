## Summary

360 ONE Prime Limited has listed new commercial paper securities on BSE's Debt segment. The securities are issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each, totaling Rs. 50 crores (1000 units). The commercial paper carries dual credit ratings of CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Total Quantity: 1000 units (Rs. 50 crores)
- Scrip Code: 729958
- Scrip ID: 360OPL1825
- ISIN: INE248U14RY2
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,80,492/-
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt instrument listing following existing BSE procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent
- Trading members can contact BSE debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 1, 2025
- **Listing Date**: August 4, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 20, 2026
- **Tenure**: Approximately 6 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The securities provide short-term funding option for 360 ONE Prime Limited and investment opportunity for institutional investors. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk for investors.
