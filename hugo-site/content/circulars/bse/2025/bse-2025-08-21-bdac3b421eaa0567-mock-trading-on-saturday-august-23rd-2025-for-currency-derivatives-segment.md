## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Currency Derivatives segment on Saturday, August 23rd, 2025. This session is designed for trading members to test their trading applications and systems without any financial obligations or liabilities.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, August 23rd, 2025
- Available for Currency Derivatives and Cross Currency Derivatives segments
- No new BOLT TWS release accompanying this session
- Trading members can test third-party platforms, in-house systems, and ETI APIs
- No margin obligations or pay-in/pay-out requirements
- Feedback requested by 3:00 PM on session day

## Trading Sessions Schedule

**Login Period:** 10:15 AM to 11:00 AM
**Continuous Trading (Currency Derivatives):** 11:00 AM to 2:00 PM
**Continuous Trading (Cross Currency Derivatives):** 11:00 AM to 2:00 PM
**Trade Modification (Currency):** 2:10 PM
**Trade Modification (Cross Currency):** 2:10 PM

## Important Notes

- Mock trading is for testing and familiarization purposes only
- No financial obligations, rights, or liabilities will be created
- Active participation from trading members is encouraged
- Opportunity to test various functionalities including exceptional market conditions

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are requested to:
- Actively participate in the mock trading session
- Provide feedback by 3:00 PM on the session day
- Contact relationship managers or support teams for any queries

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational activity with no market or financial impact. The session serves as a testing environment for trading systems and helps ensure system reliability for actual trading operations.
