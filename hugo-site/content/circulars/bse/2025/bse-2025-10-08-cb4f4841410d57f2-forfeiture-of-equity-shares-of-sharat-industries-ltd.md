## Summary

Sharat Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 519397) has forfeited 2,41,174 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each that were partly paid-up. The forfeiture occurred due to non-payment of allotment money and/or call monies by the shareholders. The distinctive numbers of the forfeited shares range from 28206146 to 28447319.

## Key Points

- Company: Sharat Industries Ltd (Scrip Code: 519397)
- Number of shares forfeited: 2,41,174 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Reason: Non-payment of allotment/call monies
- Distinctive numbers: 28206146 to 28447319
- Shares were partly paid-up at the time of forfeiture

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members of BSE are informed of this corporate action for their records and to update their systems accordingly regarding the reduced share count for Sharat Industries Ltd.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 8, 2025
- Forfeiture effective as of the notice date

## Impact Assessment

This forfeiture reduces the total outstanding share capital of Sharat Industries Ltd by 2,41,174 partly paid-up shares. The impact is localized to shareholders who failed to meet their payment obligations on these partly paid shares. The forfeiture has minimal broader market impact as it affects a specific set of shareholders and is a routine corporate action for enforcing payment obligations on partly paid shares.
