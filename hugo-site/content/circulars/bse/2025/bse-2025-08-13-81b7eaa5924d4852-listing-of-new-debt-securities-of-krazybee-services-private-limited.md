## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities worth ₹42.5 crore issued by Krazybee Services Private Limited on private placement basis. The securities carry a 10.65% annual interest rate with monthly interest payments and mature on August 12, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 42,500 securities with face value of ₹1,00,000 each
- Interest Rate: 10.65% per annum paid monthly
- Credit Rating: CARE A Stable
- ISIN: INE07HK07825
- Scrip Code: 977022
- Market Lot: 1 security
- Issue Price: ₹1,00,000 per security
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Refer to Placement Memorandum for complete details

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 12, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 13, 2025
- Interest Payment: Monthly
- Redemption Date: August 12, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing. The securities provide institutional investors access to a rated corporate bond with attractive yield in the current interest rate environment.
