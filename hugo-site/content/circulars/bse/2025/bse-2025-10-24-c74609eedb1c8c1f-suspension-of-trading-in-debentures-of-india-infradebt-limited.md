## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in 7% Non-Convertible Debentures (ISIN: INE537P07588, Code: 960203) of India Infradebt Limited effective October 28, 2025. The suspension is due to the company setting a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture Code: IIL-7%-12-11-25-PVT (960203)
- ISIN: INE537P07588
- Record Date: October 28, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of Debentures and Payment of Interest
- No dealings permitted from: October 28, 2025
- Notice Reference: DR-744/2025-2026
- Issuer: INDIA INFRADEBT LIMITED

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action procedure for debenture redemption and interest payment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures (INE537P07588) from October 28, 2025
- Trading members are required to take note of the suspension and inform relevant stakeholders
- All dealing activities in these debentures must cease from the specified date

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 24, 2025
- **Record Date**: October 28, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: October 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited and specific to holders of India Infradebt Limited's 7% debentures maturing November 12, 2025. The suspension is temporary and procedural in nature.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members dealing in debt securities need to update their systems to block trading in the specified ISIN. Debenture holders will receive their interest payment and redemption proceeds based on the October 28, 2025 record date.

**Investor Impact**: Investors holding these debentures cannot trade them from October 28, 2025 onwards as the debentures are approaching maturity (November 12, 2025) and will be redeemed.
