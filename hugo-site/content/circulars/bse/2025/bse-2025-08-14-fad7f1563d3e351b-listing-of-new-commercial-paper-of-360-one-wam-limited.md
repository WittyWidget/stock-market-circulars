## Summary

360 ONE WAM LIMITED has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 75 crores (1500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE Debt segment effective August 14, 2025. The instrument is issued on private placement basis with CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Issue size: Rs. 75 crores (1500 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each)
- Scrip Code: 730023, Scrip ID: 360OW13825
- ISIN: INE466L14ES9
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,604.50 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: August 14, 2025
- Allotment Date: August 13, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The instrument provides additional debt market liquidity with high credit ratings indicating low risk profile.
