## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 300 crores issued by HDFC Securities Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to dealings on BSE Debt segment with effect from September 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5 lakh denomination each (6,000 units)
- Issue size: Rs. 300 crores (6,000 Ã— Rs. 5 lakhs)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,002.50 per unit
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+
- ISIN: INE700G14PP3
- Scrip code: 730152

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size of 1 paise applicable
- Market lot size of 1 unit

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: September 4, 2025
- Listing date: September 4, 2025
- Redemption date: December 4, 2025
- Maturity period: 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The Commercial Paper provides short-term funding avenue for HDFC Securities Limited and offers investment option for institutional investors in debt segment.
