## Summary

NIPPON INDIA Mutual Fund is launching NIPPON INDIA QUARTERLY INTERVAL FUND - SERIES II on BSE StAR MF Platform with a Specified Transaction Period from August 7-8, 2025. The fund offers 6 scheme variants across IDCW and Growth plans with both regular and direct options.

## Key Points

- 6 debt fund scheme variants available (IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, Growth Plan - both regular and direct)
- Subscription and redemption requests accepted during normal business hours on August 7-8, 2025
- NAV applicable based on cut-off timing and fund confirmation
- MICR Cheque, Nodal and OTM payments not accepted during the STP period
- Clear funds must be received before 2:30 PM on specified dates

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received before 2:30 PM on August 7-8, 2025
- MICR Cheque, Nodal and OTM payment modes restricted during STP period
- Funds not received on time will be refunded after AMC/RTA rejection

## Important Dates

- **August 7, 2025**: STP period begins
- **August 8, 2025**: STP period ends
- **2:30 PM**: Daily cut-off for clear funds receipt

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine mutual fund scheme launch. Provides additional investment options for debt fund investors seeking quarterly interval fund exposure through BSE StAR MF platform.
