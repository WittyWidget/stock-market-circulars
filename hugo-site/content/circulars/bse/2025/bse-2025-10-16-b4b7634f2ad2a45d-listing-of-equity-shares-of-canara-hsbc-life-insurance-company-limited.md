## Summary

BSE has listed 95,00,00,000 equity shares of Canara HSBC Life Insurance Company Limited on October 15, 2025. The listing includes shares with various lock-in periods ranging from no lock-in to 18 months, depending on the investor category. All shares are in demat form.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares listed: 95,00,00,000
- Listing date: October 15, 2025
- All shares are fully paid-up and in demat form
- 78,32,85,000 shares are under lock-in with varying periods
- 16,67,15,000 shares are not under lock-in
- Registered office: 8th Floor, Unit No. 808-814, Ambadeep Building, Kasturba Gandhi Marg, Connaught Place, New Delhi 110001
- Company Secretary: Vatsala Sameer (Membership No.: A14813)

## Lock-in Details

**Lock-in Category 1 (18 months):**
- Shares: 1 to 19,00,02,000 (19,00,02,000 shares)
- Lock-in period: October 15, 2025 to April 17, 2027

**Lock-in Category 2 (6 months):**
- Shares: 19,00,02,001 to 71,25,00,000 (52,24,98,000 shares)
- Lock-in period: October 15, 2025 to April 17, 2026

**Anchor Investor Lock-in (30 days):**
- Shares: 71,25,00,001 to 74,78,92,499 (3,53,92,499 shares)
- Lock-in period: October 15, 2025 to November 13, 2025

**Anchor Investor Lock-in (90 days):**
- Shares: 74,78,92,500 to 78,32,85,000 (3,53,92,501 shares)
- Lock-in period: October 15, 2025 to January 12, 2026

**No Lock-in:**
- Shares: 78,32,85,001 to 95,00,00,000 (16,67,15,000 shares)
- Available for trading immediately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification following IPO regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Lock-in restrictions must be adhered to by respective shareholders
- Distinctive numbers are for presentation purposes only as all shares are in demat form
- Lock-in details are provided on pages 116-118 of the company's prospectus
- IPO classification: Public issue

## Important Dates

- **October 15, 2025**: Listing date
- **November 13, 2025**: End of 30-day anchor investor lock-in
- **January 12, 2026**: End of 90-day anchor investor lock-in
- **April 17, 2026**: End of 6-month lock-in
- **April 17, 2027**: End of 18-month lock-in

## Impact Assessment

This listing represents a significant public offering in the life insurance sector. The structured lock-in periods indicate a phased approach to market liquidity, with approximately 17.5% of shares immediately available for trading. The majority of shares (82.5%) are under various lock-in periods to ensure stability. The listing of Canara HSBC Life Insurance Company Limited adds to the insurance sector representation on BSE and provides investors access to a joint venture between a major Indian public sector bank and a global insurance major.
