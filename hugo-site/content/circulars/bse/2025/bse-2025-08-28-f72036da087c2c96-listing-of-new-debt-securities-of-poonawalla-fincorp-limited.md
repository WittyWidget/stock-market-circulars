## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Poonawalla Fincorp Limited issued on private placement basis. The securities carry 7.52% annual interest rate and mature on February 25, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 35,000 securities
- Face Value: ₹1,00,000 per security
- Total Issue Size: ₹3.5 crores
- Interest Rate: 7.52% per annum
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA/stable
- Scrip Code: 977057
- ISIN: INE511C07920
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Securities to be traded under specified ISIN number
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum for detailed information

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 25, 2025
- Effective Trading Date: August 28, 2025
- Interest Payment Dates: Annually from February 25, 2026 to February 25, 2028
- Redemption Date: February 25, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of high-rated debt securities on private placement basis. The AAA rating indicates strong creditworthiness of the issuer.
