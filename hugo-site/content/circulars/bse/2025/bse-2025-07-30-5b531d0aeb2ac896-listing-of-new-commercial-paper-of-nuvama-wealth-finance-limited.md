## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 55 crore issued by Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective July 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5 lakh denomination each
- Total quantity: 1,100 units (Rs. 55 crore)
- Scrip Code: 729932, ISIN: INE918K14CN6
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,81,050.50 per unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: July 30, 2025
- Allotment Date: July 29, 2025
- Redemption Date: January 27, 2026
- Maturity Period: 6 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by a financial services company. The listing provides additional short-term debt instrument options for institutional investors in the debt segment.
