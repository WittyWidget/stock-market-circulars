## Summary

BSE has announced the addition of two newly listed companies to their respective indices. WeWork India Management Limited (ticker 544570) will be added to the BSE IPO Index effective October 14, 2025, following its listing on October 10, 2025. NSB BPO Solutions Limited (ticker 544571) will be added to the BSE SME IPO Index effective October 13, 2025, after listing on the SME platform on October 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- WeWork India Management Limited (544570) listed on BSE main board on October 10, 2025
- WeWork India to be included in BSE IPO Index from market open on October 14, 2025
- NSB BPO Solutions Limited (544571) listed on BSE SME platform on October 10, 2025
- NSB BPO Solutions to be included in BSE SME IPO Index from market open on October 13, 2025
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)
- Queries can be directed to bseindex@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine index rebalancing following new IPO listings.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance actions required. This is an informational notice for market participants tracking BSE indices.

## Important Dates

- **October 10, 2025**: WeWork India Management Limited listing date on BSE
- **October 10, 2025**: NSB BPO Solutions Limited listing date on BSE SME platform
- **October 13, 2025**: NSB BPO Solutions Limited addition to BSE SME IPO Index (market open)
- **October 14, 2025**: WeWork India Management Limited addition to BSE IPO Index (market open)

## Impact Assessment

Low impact event. These are standard index additions following IPO listings that affect tracking funds and investors following these specific indices. The BSE IPO and BSE SME IPO indices will include these new constituents for index calculation purposes. No immediate market-wide operational or trading impact anticipated.
