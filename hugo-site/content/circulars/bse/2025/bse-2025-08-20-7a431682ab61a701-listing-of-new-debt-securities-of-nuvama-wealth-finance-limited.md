## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited on private placement basis effective August 20, 2025. The securities are market-linked with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 and redemption date of January 22, 2029.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 660 securities
- Scrip Code: 976954
- ISIN: INE918K07PX1 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000
- Issue Price: Rs. 96,888
- Date of Allotment: August 18, 2025
- Credit Rating: CARE PP-MLD AA/Stable
- Market Lot: 1
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialised form
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum on BSE website for further details
- Contact debt department for clarifications at specified phone numbers

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 18, 2025
- Listing Date: August 20, 2025
- Interest Payment Date: January 22, 2029 (Cumulative)
- Redemption Date: January 22, 2029

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis. The securities are market-linked with cumulative interest payment structure and have a strong credit rating of AA/Stable from CARE.
