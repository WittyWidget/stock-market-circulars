## Summary

BSE has listed 35,00,100 new equity shares of Virtuoso Optoelectronics Limited (Scrip Code: 543597) for trading effective August 20, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion.

## Key Points

- 35,00,100 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 245.10/-
- Issue price: Rs. 255.10 per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares issued pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Distribution numbers: 22838657 to 26338756
- ISIN: INE0I0T01010
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and trading permissions.

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: March 2, 2024
- Trading Start Date: August 20, 2025
- Lock-in Period Ends: February 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Routine equity listing with minimal market impact. All 35,00,100 shares are subject to lock-in until February 28, 2026, limiting immediate trading liquidity impact.
