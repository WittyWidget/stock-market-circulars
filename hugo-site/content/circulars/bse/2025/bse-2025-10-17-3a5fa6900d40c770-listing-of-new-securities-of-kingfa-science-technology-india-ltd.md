## Summary

BSE has announced the listing and commencement of trading of 14,40,920 new equity shares of Kingfa Science & Technology (India) Ltd (Scrip Code: 524019) with effect from Monday, October 20, 2025. The shares were issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis at Rs. 3470/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- plus premium of Rs. 3460/-) and are subject to a lock-in period until April 25, 2026.

## Key Points

- 14,40,920 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 3460/- per share
- Total issue price: Rs. 3470/- per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares ranking pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE473D01015
- Distinctive Numbers: 12233787 to 13674706
- Date of allotment: September 24, 2025
- Trading commencement date: October 20, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification for preferential allotment.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the listing of these new securities for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 24, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 20, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: April 25, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine preferential allotment to non-promoters representing additional equity shares being listed. The shares carry a 6-month lock-in period from allotment date, preventing immediate selling pressure. The issue increases the total equity base of Kingfa Science & Technology (India) Ltd.
