## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 2,025 crore (40,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued by Reliance Retail Ventures Limited on private placement basis, effective from August 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper quantity: 40,500 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total issue size: Rs. 2,025 crore
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,727.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Scrip code: 730037
- ISIN: INE929O14DU8
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 14, 2025
- Listing effective date: August 18, 2025
- Redemption date: November 13, 2025
- Tenure: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by a subsidiary of Reliance Industries. The instrument provides short-term funding for the company's operations with no significant implications for broader market operations.
