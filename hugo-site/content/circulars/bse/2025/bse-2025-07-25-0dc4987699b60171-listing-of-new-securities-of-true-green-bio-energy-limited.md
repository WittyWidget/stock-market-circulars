## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 30,35,714 new equity shares of TRUE GREEN BIO ENERGY LIMITED (Scrip Code: 533407) issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion. These shares will commence trading on BSE from Monday, July 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- 30,35,714 new equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 60/-
- Shares issued to non-promoters on preferential basis through warrant conversion
- Issue price: Rs. 70.00 per share
- Distribution numbers: 29921430 to 32957143
- Date of allotment: June 18, 2025
- ISIN: INE672K01025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. Standard listing procedure for preferential allotment following warrant conversion as per existing SEBI regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed about new security availability for trading
- Lock-in compliance must be maintained until February 28, 2026
- Standard trading and settlement procedures apply
- Shares subject to existing company governance and disclosure requirements

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: June 18, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: July 28, 2025 (Monday)
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: February 28, 2026
- **Notice Date**: July 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - Addition of 30.36 lakh shares increases the free float moderately. The preferential allotment to non-promoters may improve liquidity and market participation.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Standard listing process with no operational changes to trading systems or procedures. The 8-month lock-in period provides stability during initial trading period.

**Investor Impact**: Medium - Existing shareholders experience dilution due to new share issuance, but the preferential allotment strengthens the company's capital base and may attract institutional interest.
