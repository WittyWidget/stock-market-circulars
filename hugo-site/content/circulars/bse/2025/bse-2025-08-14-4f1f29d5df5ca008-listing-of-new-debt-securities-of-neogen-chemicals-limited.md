## Summary

BSE announces the listing of new debt securities issued by Neogen Chemicals Limited on private placement basis, effective August 14, 2025. The securities carry a 10.50% interest rate and will be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 20,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 977028, Scrip ID: 1050NCL28
- ISIN Number: INE136S07013
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000 per security
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A-
- Interest Rate: 10.50% per annum

## Security Details

- Issue Price: Rs. 100,000
- Date of Allotment: August 12, 2025
- Interest Payment: Monthly from August 31, 2025 to February 12, 2028
- Redemption Date: February 12, 2028
- Put/Call Options: Both available from July 31, 2027

## Trading Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: August 14, 2025
- First Interest Payment: August 31, 2025
- Put/Call Option Date: July 31, 2027
- Redemption Date: February 12, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. Provides additional financing instrument for Neogen Chemicals Limited and investment option for debt market participants.
