## Summary

BSE has published the Draft Offer Document for MAX MARKETING LIMITED's SME IPO, filed on August 27, 2025. The document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations and is available for a mandatory 21-day public comment period.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by MAX MARKETING LIMITED on August 27, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for public review
- 21-day comment period mandatory under ICDR Regulations
- Contains all required disclosures per Schedule VI requirements
- Public comments to be submitted via email to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This follows standard ICDR Regulations procedure for SME IPO documentation.

## Compliance Requirements

- Document hosted on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Public comments must be submitted within 21 days from notice date
- All comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Company must comply with Schedule VI disclosure requirements

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document Filing Date: August 27, 2025
- Notice Date: August 28, 2025
- Public Comment Deadline: September 18, 2025 (21 days from notice)

## Impact Assessment

Routine SME IPO process with standard regulatory compliance requirements. Limited market impact as this is preliminary documentation phase. Investors and market participants have opportunity to review and comment on the proposed offering during the mandatory review period.
