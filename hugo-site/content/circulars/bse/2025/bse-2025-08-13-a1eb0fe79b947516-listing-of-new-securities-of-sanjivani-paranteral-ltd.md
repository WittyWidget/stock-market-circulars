## Summary

Sanjivani Paranteral Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531569) has listed 80,000 new equity shares on BSE for trading effective August 14, 2025. These shares were issued to promoters on preferential basis through conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 80,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 125.10/-
- Issue price: Rs. 135.10/- per share
- Shares issued to promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE860D01013
- Date of allotment: July 18, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that the new securities are permitted for trading on the exchange.

## Important Dates

- **Trading Start Date**: August 14, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: July 18, 2025
- **Lock-in Period End**: February 17, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a small number of shares (80,000) issued to promoters with a lock-in period until February 2027, preventing immediate trading by allottees.
