## Summary

BSE has admitted two new trading members effective August 6, 2025: ROI NET Securities Private Limited (Member No. 6958) and R J Equity Edge Private Limited (Member No. 6959). Both members have been granted trading rights in Cash/Equity Derivatives segments under new membership terms.

## Key Points

- Two new trading members admitted to BSE on August 6, 2025
- ROI NET Securities Private Limited assigned member number 6958
- R J Equity Edge Private Limited assigned member number 6959
- Both members authorized for Cash/Equity Derivatives trading
- Membership subject to BSE Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - standard membership admission process.

## Compliance Requirements

- New members must comply with all BSE Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations
- Trading activities subject to standard exchange oversight
- Members must adhere to cash and equity derivatives segment requirements

## Important Dates

- **August 6, 2025**: Effective date of membership admission
- **August 21, 2025**: Notice publication date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine administrative process. Addition of new trading members may marginally increase market participation and liquidity in cash and equity derivatives segments. No operational disruptions expected for existing members.
