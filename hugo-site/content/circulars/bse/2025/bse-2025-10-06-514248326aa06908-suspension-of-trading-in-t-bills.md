## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in two Treasury Bills (T-bills) effective October 07, 2025, as they will mature on the redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in these securities from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Two T-bills will be suspended from trading: 364TB091025 (ISIN: IN002024Z263, Scrip Code: 804973) and 91TB091025 (ISIN: IN002025X158, Scrip Code: 805088)
- Trading suspension effective from October 07, 2025
- Reason: Securities maturing on redemption date
- Notice reference: DR-730/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notice for securities reaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified T-bills from October 07, 2025
- Members should update their trading systems to prevent inadvertent trading in these securities

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 06, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: October 07, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: October 09, 2025 (implied)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine operational measure for maturing debt securities. Only affects trading members dealing in the debt segment, specifically those handling these two T-bills. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
