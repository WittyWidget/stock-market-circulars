## Summary

BSE has announced a change in the scrip ID for GE Power India Limited (scrip code 532309) in the BOLT Plus system, effective August 28, 2025. The scrip ID will change from GEPIL to GVPIL.

## Key Points

- Company: GE Power India Limited (Scrip Code: 532309)
- Old Scrip ID: GEPIL
- New Scrip ID: GVPIL
- System: BOLT Plus
- Trading members must take note of this change

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is an administrative update to the trading system identification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are required to take note of the scrip ID change
- Update internal systems and processes to reflect the new scrip ID
- Contact BSE officials for any clarifications needed

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 28, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a technical/administrative change that affects how the stock is identified in BSE's BOLT Plus trading system. While the impact on actual trading operations is minimal, trading members need to update their systems to ensure proper identification and processing of trades for GE Power India Limited.
