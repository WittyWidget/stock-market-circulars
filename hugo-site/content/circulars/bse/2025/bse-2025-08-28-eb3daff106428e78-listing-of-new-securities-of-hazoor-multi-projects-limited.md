## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 17,77,777 new equity shares of Hazoor Multi Projects Limited (Scrip Code: 532467) issued through conversion of warrants. The shares will commence trading on August 29, 2025, and are subject to lock-in restrictions until February 28, 2026.

## Key Points

- 17,77,777 equity shares of Rs. 1/- each issued at Rs. 30/- (including Rs. 29/- premium)
- Shares issued to Non-Promoters on preferential basis through warrant conversion
- Distribution numbers: 218768014 to 220545790
- Date of allotment: March 3, 2025
- ISIN: INE550F01049
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification following warrant conversion procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities availability for trading
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all issued shares until February 28, 2026
- Standard listing compliance requirements applicable

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: March 3, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 29, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: February 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this represents a routine listing of shares from warrant conversion for a single company. The lock-in period ensures no immediate selling pressure on existing shareholders.
