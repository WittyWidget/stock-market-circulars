## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading for Treasury Bill (T-bill) with ISIN IN001025C054 (scrip code 800648, GS15OCT25C) effective October 13, 2025. The suspension is implemented as the T-bill is scheduled to mature on its redemption date. Trading members are advised not to deal in this security from the specified date.

## Key Points

- T-bill ISIN: IN001025C054
- Scrip Code: 800648
- Particulars: GS15OCT25C
- Trading suspension effective date: October 13, 2025
- Reason: Maturity on redemption date
- Circular reference: DR-734/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for securities reaching maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the specified T-bill (ISIN IN001025C054) from October 13, 2025
- Members should ensure no new trades are executed in this security after the suspension date
- Existing positions should be settled as per normal settlement procedures

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 10, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date:** October 13, 2025
- **Redemption/Maturity Date:** October 15, 2025 (implied from security name GS15OCT25C)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low - affects only one T-bill security nearing maturity

**Operational Impact:** Minimal - standard pre-maturity trading suspension procedure

**Affected Parties:** Trading members holding or dealing in T-bill ISIN IN001025C054

This is a routine operational notice applicable to the debt segment. Investors holding this T-bill will receive redemption proceeds on maturity as per standard procedures.
