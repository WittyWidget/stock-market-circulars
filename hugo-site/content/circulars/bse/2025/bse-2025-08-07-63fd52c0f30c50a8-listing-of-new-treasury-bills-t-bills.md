## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of three new Treasury Bills (T-Bills) in the G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS category, effective August 8, 2025. The new listings include 91-day, 182-day, and 364-day T-Bills with specific ISIN codes and trading symbols.

## Key Points

- Three new Treasury Bills to be listed on August 8, 2025
- All T-Bills classified under G GROUP - DEBT INSTRUMENTS
- Market lot size of 1 for all three securities
- Trading restrictions apply two working days before maturity
- Contact numbers provided for member clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine listing announcement following standard procedures for government securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new scrip codes and symbols
- Members must observe trading suspension two working days prior to maturity dates
- Standard debt instrument trading rules apply

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: August 8, 2025
- **91-day T-Bill Maturity**: November 6, 2025 (Trading stops November 4, 2025)
- **182-day T-Bill Maturity**: February 5, 2026 (Trading stops February 3, 2026)
- **364-day T-Bill Maturity**: August 6, 2026 (Trading stops August 4, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is routine addition of government securities to the exchange. Provides additional short to medium-term investment options for institutional and retail investors in the debt market segment.
