## Summary

BSE has listed 16,49,840 new equity shares of Coromandel Engineering Company Limited (Scrip Code: 533167) issued on a preferential basis to promoter and non-promoter entities. The shares are permitted for trading from Thursday, September 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- 16,49,840 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 30.05/-
- Issue price: Rs. 40.05/- per share
- Allotment date: May 8, 2025
- ISIN: INE312J01012
- Distribution numbers: 33233599 to 34883438
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard preferential allotment listing process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability for trading
- Lock-in restrictions apply to allotted shares as per SEBI regulations
- Shares subject to standard listing compliance requirements

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: May 8, 2025
- Trading Commencement: September 4, 2025
- Lock-in Period End: March 15, 2026 (13,00,000 shares) and March 15, 2027 (3,49,840 shares)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this represents a routine preferential allotment. The lock-in periods ensure gradual market availability of shares, preventing immediate supply pressure on the stock price.
