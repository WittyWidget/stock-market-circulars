## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 3,582,068 equity shares of GTT Data Solutions Limited (Scrip Code: 530457) issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis. The shares will be permitted to trade on the Exchange from Tuesday, October 28, 2025. All shares are subject to a lock-in period expiring on April 30, 2026.

## Key Points

- 3,582,068 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at a premium of Rs. 120/-
- Issue price: Rs. 130.00 per share
- Allotment date: April 18, 2025
- Distinguished numbers: 19161916 to 22743983
- ISIN: INE959B01017
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities are available for trading from October 28, 2025
- All 3,582,068 shares are subject to lock-in restrictions and cannot be transferred until April 30, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: April 18, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 28, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: April 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal immediate market impact as all newly listed shares are locked in until April 30, 2026. The preferential allotment to non-promoters represents additional capital raised by GTT Data Solutions Limited at Rs. 130 per share. Trading members should be aware of these new securities for record-keeping purposes, though the lock-in prevents immediate trading of these specific shares.
