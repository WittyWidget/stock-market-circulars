## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of KWICK FORENSIC SOLUTIONS LIMITED on its SME website for public comments. The document was filed on September 30, 2025, and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations. Stakeholders have 21 days from the notice date to submit comments.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by KWICK FORENSIC SOLUTIONS LIMITED on September 30, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx
- Public comments invited within 21 days from October 06, 2025
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Notification issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard procedural notification for draft offer document availability.

## Compliance Requirements

- Draft Offer Document must be available on Stock Exchange website for at least 21 days
- Document must comply with Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Stakeholders may submit comments on the draft document within the specified period

## Important Dates

- **September 30, 2025**: Draft Offer Document filed
- **October 06, 2025**: Notice date and start of public comment period
- **October 27, 2025**: Last date for submitting public comments (21 days from notice date)

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it relates specifically to a single SME IPO. The draft offer document availability is a standard regulatory requirement under ICDR regulations.
