## Summary

BSE has issued an update regarding debt securities of Aditya Birla Capital Limited (Scrip Code: 977103). The company has received call money from debenture holders, resulting in changes to the ISIN and paid-up value of the listed debt securities. These changes will be effective from Friday, October 17, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company has received call money from debenture holders
- ISIN will change from INE674K07085 to INE674K07127
- Paid-up value will increase from Rs. 50,000/- per debenture to Rs. 1,00,000/- per debenture
- Changes effective from October 17, 2025
- This is a continuation of Exchange Notice No. 20250915-28 dated September 15, 2025
- Scrip Code remains 977103

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative update regarding existing debt securities following corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new ISIN (INE674K07127) for trading purposes from October 17, 2025
- Debenture holders should update their records with the new ISIN
- Market participants must use the revised paid-up value of Rs. 1,00,000/- per debenture for all calculations and transactions

## Important Dates

- **October 16, 2025**: Notice date
- **October 17, 2025**: Effective date for new ISIN and paid-up value changes

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium. The change in ISIN and doubling of paid-up value affects holders of these specific debt securities. Trading members need to update their systems to reflect the new ISIN for proper trade execution and settlement.

**Operational Impact**: Existing debenture holders must track the new ISIN for future transactions. The increase in paid-up value from Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture reflects the call money received, which changes the denomination of the securities.
