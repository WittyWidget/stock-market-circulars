## Summary

MIRAE ASSET GOLD SILVER PASSIVE FOF scheme has been reopened for ongoing subscription and redemption transactions on BSE StAR MF Platform effective September 1, 2025. The scheme offers various plan options including Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW variants.

## Key Points

- NFO scheme reopened for ongoing transactions from September 1, 2025
- Available facilities: subscription, redemption, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Six different scheme variants available (Regular/Direct plans with Growth/IDCW options)
- All schemes classified under FOF (Fund of Funds) category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members can facilitate transactions in the specified schemes through BSE StAR MF Platform
- Standard mutual fund transaction compliance applies

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: September 1, 2025
- Notice Date: August 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine operational notice about mutual fund scheme availability. Provides additional investment options for investors interested in gold and silver exposure through fund of funds structure.
