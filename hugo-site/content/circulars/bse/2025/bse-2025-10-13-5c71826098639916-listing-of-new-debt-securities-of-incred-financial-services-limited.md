## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities issued by InCred Financial Services Limited on a private placement basis, effective October 13, 2025. The securities are market-linked debentures tied to the NIFTY 50 INDEX with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 per unit, maturing on December 8, 2027. A total quantity of 2,500 units have been listed with scrip code 977202 and will trade exclusively in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Security Type: Market-Linked Debentures (NIFTY 50 INDEX linked)
- Issuer: InCred Financial Services Limited
- Scrip Code: 977202
- Scrip ID: IFSL81025
- ISIN: INE321N07574
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Issue Size: 2,500 units
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL PP-MLD AA-/Stable
- Date of Allotment: October 8, 2025
- Maturity Date: December 8, 2027
- Interest Type: Cumulative (market-linked)
- Trading: Dematerialized form only
- Tick Size: 1 paise
- Put/Call Option: Not Available

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for new debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE321N07574
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx for complete details
- For clarifications, trading members may contact the BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **October 8, 2025**: Date of Allotment
- **October 13, 2025**: Listing and commencement of trading on BSE Debt segment
- **December 8, 2027**: Interest Payment Date (Cumulative)
- **December 8, 2027**: Redemption Date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a private placement of debt securities with limited quantity (2,500 units) affecting only specific institutional investors and debt market participants.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Standard listing procedure for debt securities with no changes to existing trading infrastructure or processes.

**Investor Impact**: Limited to investors in InCred Financial Services' debt instruments. The market-linked nature tied to NIFTY 50 INDEX means returns are dependent on index performance rather than fixed interest rates, carrying market risk for investors.
