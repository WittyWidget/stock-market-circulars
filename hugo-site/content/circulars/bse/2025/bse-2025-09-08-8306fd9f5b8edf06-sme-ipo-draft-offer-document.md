## Summary

Century Business Media Limited has filed its Draft Offer Document for SME IPO on September 05, 2025. The document is now available on BSE SME website for public comments for a period of 21 days as per ICDR Regulations.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Century Business Media Limited on September 05, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for public review
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. Standard ICDR compliance process being followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public comments to be submitted within 21 days from September 08, 2025
- Comments should be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Draft document accessible at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- Draft filing date: September 05, 2025
- Notice date: September 08, 2025
- Public comment deadline: September 29, 2025 (21 days from notice)

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact to SME segment only. Standard IPO process for small and medium enterprises. Provides opportunity for public to review and comment on the offering before final approval.
