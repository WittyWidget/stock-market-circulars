## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of further securities issued by 10 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be listed and admitted for trading on the Exchange with effect from Wednesday, September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- 10 companies are issuing additional securities under ESOP/ESOS
- Total of 916,750 new shares across all companies
- All securities will be available for trading from September 10, 2025
- No lock-in period applicable for any of the listed securities
- Face values range from Re. 1 to Rs. 10 per share

## Company Details

**Major Companies Included:**
- **Kalyan Jewellers India Limited**: 657,446 shares (largest issuance)
- **Lancor Holdings Ltd**: 555,000 shares
- **Windlas Biotech Limited**: 117,928 shares
- **Zensar Technologies Ltd**: 73,529 shares
- **CG Power and Industrial Solutions Limited**: 71,980 shares
- **ICICI Bank Ltd**: 30,305 shares
- **IndoStar Capital Finance Limited**: 26,250 shares
- **Infosys Ltd**: 21,924 shares
- **Carborundum Universal Ltd**: 6,388 shares
- **Religare Enterprises Ltd**: 3,000 shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification for employee stock option exercises.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities availability
- Standard trading rules and procedures apply to these securities
- No special compliance requirements mentioned

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: September 9, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: September 10, 2025 (Wednesday)
- **Lock-in Period**: Not applicable for any securities

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine ESOP exercise listings. The additional shares represent employee stock option conversions and will increase the free float of the respective companies marginally. No significant dilution concerns as the quantities are relatively small compared to existing share bases for most companies.
