## Summary

BSE has listed two series of Commercial Paper issued by Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited on private placement basis, effective October 24, 2025. The instruments are rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+, with ICICI Bank Ltd acting as the Issuing and Paying Agent. Both series have a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each and will be traded only in dematerialized form.

## Key Points

- Two series of Commercial Paper listed totaling 8,000 units (4,000 units each)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit for both series
- Total value: Rs. 400 crore (Rs. 200 crore per series)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ for both series
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure transactions are conducted only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Members should adhere to the tick size of 1 paise for these securities

**Series 2 (NWI-20-1-26-CP):**

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term funding options for Nuvama Wealth and Investment Limited through the debt market and offers investment opportunities for institutional investors seeking highly-rated short-term instruments (approximately 3-month tenure). The high credit ratings (A1+ from both CRISIL and ICRA) indicate low credit risk. There is no impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
