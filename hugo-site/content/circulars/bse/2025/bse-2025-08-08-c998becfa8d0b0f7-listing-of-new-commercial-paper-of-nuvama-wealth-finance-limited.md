## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited issued on private placement basis. The securities are worth Rs. 5 lakhs each with 2000 units totaling Rs. 100 crores, carrying CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+ ratings.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 2000 units (Rs. 100 crores total)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,90,328 per unit
- Scrip code: 729395, ISIN: INE918K14CI6
- Credit ratings: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- ICICI Bank Limited as issuing and paying agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 7, 2025
- Listing effective date: August 8, 2025
- Redemption date: November 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The securities provide short-term funding option for Nuvama Wealth Finance Limited with high credit ratings ensuring investor confidence.
