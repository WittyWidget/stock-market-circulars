## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debentures of AYE FINANCE LIMITED effective September 4, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date for redemption and interest payment purposes. The suspension applies to specific non-convertible debentures with ISIN INE501X07315.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to AYE Finance debentures (Code: AFL-11.20%-20-9-27-PVT)
- ISIN: INE501X07315 (BSE Code: 974234)
- Suspension effective from September 4, 2025
- Trading members advised not to deal in specified debentures
- Notice issued under DR-708/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational measure for corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease dealing in the specified debentures from September 4, 2025
- All exchange participants must take note of the suspension
- No trading activities permitted until further notice

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: September 5, 2025 (for redemption and interest payment)
- **No Dealings From**: September 4, 2025
- **Notice Date**: September 2, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact restricted to holders and traders of AYE Finance debentures. The suspension is temporary and related to standard corporate action procedures for redemption and interest payment. Trading members dealing in debt instruments should adjust their portfolios accordingly.
