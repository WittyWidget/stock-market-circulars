## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 100 bonus equity shares of Tata Elxsi Ltd (Scrip Code: 500408) that were previously kept in abeyance. These shares will be available for trading from Wednesday, September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- 100 bonus equity shares of Rs. 10/- each being listed
- Shares were previously kept in abeyance
- ISIN: INE670A01012
- Distinctive numbers: 31177361 to 31177460
- Trading permitted from September 10, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine listing of previously issued but held shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities availability for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Wednesday, September 10, 2025 - Trading commences for the bonus shares

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected due to the small number of shares (100) being released. This represents a routine administrative action to make previously issued bonus shares available for trading.
