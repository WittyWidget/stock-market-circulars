## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 400 crores issued by Manappuram Finance Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to dealings on BSE Debt segment effective September 5, 2025, with redemption scheduled for December 3, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (8,000 units totaling Rs. 400 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,397/- per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Scrip Code: 730154, ISIN: INE522D14OS5
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- AXIS BANK appointed as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 4, 2025
- Trading Commencement: September 5, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 3, 2025
- Tenure: Approximately 89 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding option for Manappuram Finance Limited and investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
