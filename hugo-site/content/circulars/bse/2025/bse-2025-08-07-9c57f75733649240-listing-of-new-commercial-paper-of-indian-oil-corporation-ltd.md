## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Indian Oil Corporation Ltd (IOCL) issued on private placement basis. The securities are worth Rs. 5 lakhs each with 20,000 units totaling Rs. 1000 crores, effective from August 07, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (20,000 units)
- Scrip Code: 729979, Scrip ID: IOCL6825
- ISIN Number: INE242A14YF2
- Credit Rating: ICRA A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,926/- (discount to face value)
- Allotment Date: August 06, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 04, 2025
- State Bank of India as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing of debt securities under existing framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialised form under specified ISIN
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applicable
- Trading members must use BSE debt segment for transactions

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date:** August 07, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** August 06, 2025
- **Redemption Date:** November 04, 2025
- **Tenure:** Approximately 90 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding avenue for IOCL and investment opportunity for institutional investors in high-rated corporate debt with 90-day maturity.
