## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by ICICI HOME FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED worth Rs. 200 crores (4000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from August 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of face value Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total quantity: 4000 units (Rs. 200 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,752 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 730042
- ISIN: INE071G14HB6

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 19, 2025
- Trading effective date: August 19, 2025
- Redemption date: November 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. The commercial paper provides short-term funding option for ICICI Home Finance Company with 91-day maturity period.
