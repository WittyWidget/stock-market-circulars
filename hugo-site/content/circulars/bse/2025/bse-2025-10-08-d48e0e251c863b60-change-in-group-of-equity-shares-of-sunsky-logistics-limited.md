## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of SUNSKY LOGISTICS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 544566), an SME IPO listing, will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment (M Group) effective Friday, October 24, 2025. This change follows the exchange's notice dated October 07, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: SUNSKY LOGISTICS LIMITED (SME IPO)
- Scrip Code: 544566
- Current Group: MT Group (Trade for Trade segment)
- New Group: M Group (Rolling segment)
- Effective Date: Friday, October 24, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251008-8
- Continuation of previous notice: 20251007-78 dated October 07, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The trading mechanism for SUNSKY LOGISTICS LIMITED will shift from Trade for Trade settlement (where each trade settles individually) to Rolling settlement (standard T+2 settlement cycle). This transition indicates the stock is moving from a more restrictive trading framework to normal market operations.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should:
- Note the effective date of October 24, 2025 for the group change
- Update their systems to reflect the new group classification
- Contact Mr. Anurag Jain on Tel. No. 022-2272 8822 for further details or clarifications

## Important Dates

- **October 07, 2025**: Initial notice issued (Notice No. 20251007-78)
- **October 08, 2025**: Confirmation notice issued (Notice No. 20251008-8)
- **October 24, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The transfer from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment typically improves liquidity and reduces trading restrictions. This is a positive development for the stock as it moves from a restrictive settlement mechanism to standard rolling settlement.

**Trading Impact**: The change allows for intraday trading and margin benefits that are not available in the Trade for Trade segment, potentially increasing trading volumes and market participation.

**Investor Impact**: Existing and potential investors will benefit from improved liquidity and easier entry/exit from positions once the stock moves to M Group.
