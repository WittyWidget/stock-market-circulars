## Summary

BSE has announced changes to the Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure (LT-ASM) framework effective July 29, 2025. Two securities are being newly included, one security moves to direct Stage IV, and one security is moving out of the framework.

## Key Points

- 2 securities newly shortlisted for LT-ASM: Emerald Leisures Ltd and Shetron Ltd
- Arihant Foundations & Housing Ltd moved directly to Stage IV LT-ASM
- Kreon Finnancial Services Ltd moving out due to inclusion in ESM Framework
- No securities moving to higher or lower ASM stages
- Comprehensive consolidated list of all securities in LT-ASM provided

## Regulatory Changes

- Implementation of Long Term Additional Surveillance Measure for identified securities
- Securities under LT-ASM subject to additional trading restrictions and monitoring
- Framework designed to curb excessive speculation and volatility

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure compliance with LT-ASM trading conditions
- Enhanced monitoring and reporting requirements for affected securities
- Adherence to specific position limits and trading parameters

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: July 29, 2025 - All LT-ASM changes take effect

## Impact Assessment

- **New Inclusions**: Emerald Leisures Ltd and Shetron Ltd will face additional trading restrictions
- **Stage Movement**: Arihant Foundations & Housing Ltd subject to Stage IV LT-ASM conditions
- **Framework Exit**: Kreon Finnancial Services Ltd moves to ESM framework with different surveillance parameters
- **Market Impact**: Enhanced surveillance may reduce speculative trading in affected securities
