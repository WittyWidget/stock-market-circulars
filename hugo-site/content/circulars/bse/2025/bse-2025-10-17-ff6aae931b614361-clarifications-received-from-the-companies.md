## Summary

BSE published clarifications from three companies - Genesys International Corporation Limited (Scrip: 506109), Mangalore Refinery and Petrochemicals Limited (Scrip: 500109), and Pokarna Limited (Scrip: 532486) - responding to surveillance queries regarding significant increase in trading volumes on October 16-17, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Genesys International**: Clarified that volume spike was related to disclosure of order secured from Tata Motor Passenger Vehicles Limited, disclosed on October 17, 2025 after receiving consent from customer based on Non-Disclosure Agreement
- **MRPL**: Confirmed no unpublished price sensitive information pending disclosure under Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR 2015; volume movement is market driven
- **Pokarna Limited**: Responded to BSE surveillance query via email dated October 17, 2025 regarding volume movement (full response text incomplete in source)
- All clarifications issued in response to BSE surveillance team queries under reference L/SURV/ONL/PV/AA/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must respond to stock exchange surveillance queries regarding abnormal price or volume movements
- Disclosure obligations under Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 must be fulfilled
- Material information disclosure subject to customer consent and Non-Disclosure Agreement provisions where applicable

## Important Dates

- **October 16, 2025**: BSE surveillance queries issued (reference L/SURV/ONL/PV/AA/2025-2026/580, 584)
- **October 17, 2025**: Companies submitted clarifications to BSE; Genesys disclosed Tata Motors order information

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - Clarifications confirm either market-driven movements or already disclosed information. No new material price-sensitive information revealed that would affect trading.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Routine surveillance and disclosure compliance process. Demonstrates exchange oversight functioning normally.

**Investor Impact**: Informational only - Provides transparency on reasons for volume spikes, confirming no hidden material developments.
