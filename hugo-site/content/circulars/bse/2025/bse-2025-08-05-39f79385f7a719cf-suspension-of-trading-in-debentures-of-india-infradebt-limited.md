## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in debentures of INDIA INFRADEBT LIMITED (Code: IIL-8.35%-25-8-25-PVT, ISIN: INE537P07513) effective August 8, 2025, due to the company fixing a record date for interest payment and redemption purposes.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to specific debentures of INDIA INFRADEBT LIMITED
- Debenture code: IIL-8.35%-25-8-25-PVT
- ISIN: INE537P07513 (Security ID: 959312)
- Trading members advised not to deal in these debentures
- Suspension relates to corporate action processing

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational measure for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from August 8, 2025
- Trading members must take note of the suspension and comply with no-dealing restrictions
- Proper record-keeping and client communication regarding the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: August 10, 2025 (for interest payment and redemption)
- **No Dealings From**: August 8, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 5, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders and traders of specific INDIA INFRADEBT LIMITED debentures. The suspension is temporary and procedural, allowing for proper processing of interest payments and redemption. Trading will likely resume after the corporate action is completed. Minimal broader market impact expected as this affects only one company's debt securities.
