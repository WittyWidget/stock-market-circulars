## Summary

BSE has announced the settlement schedule for the Offer to Buy - Acquisition Window (Takeover) for National Fittings Limited. The takeover bidding will be conducted from September 10, 2025, to September 23, 2025, with settlement occurring on October 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- Settlement Type: Takeover
- Settlement Number: 2025082
- Bid Period: September 10, 2025 to September 23, 2025
- Obligation Download Date: October 7, 2025
- Settlement Date: October 8, 2025
- Demat shares must be delivered through Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism
- Market Type "(Takeover)" and settlement No. 2025082 to be used in depository system

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. The circular follows existing guidelines as per Notice No. 20200528-32 dated May 28, 2020, regarding settlement under Revised Guidelines of Mechanism for acquisition of shares through Stock Exchange.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members/Custodians must use Early Pay-in of Shares mechanism for demat share delivery
- Securities must be earmarked/blocked in client accounts with requisite market type and settlement number
- Early pay-in effect must be reflected in BSE/ICCL systems before prescribed timelines
- Members must complete early pay-in process sufficiently in advance of BSE OTB Bidding window closure
- No EPN file upload required for CDSL & NSDL early pay-in securities
- Members should refer to MBRSTD_OTB_CM_mmm_SettNo file for client-wise Stamp Duty charges

## Important Dates

- **September 10, 2025**: Bid Start Date
- **September 23, 2025**: Bid End Date
- **October 7, 2025**: Obligation Download Date
- **October 8, 2025**: Settlement Date

## Impact Assessment

This takeover offer will impact National Fittings Limited shareholders who wish to participate in the acquisition window. Trading members and custodians must ensure compliance with early pay-in procedures and timeline requirements. The two-week bidding period provides adequate time for participation, with settlement occurring approximately two weeks after bid closure.
