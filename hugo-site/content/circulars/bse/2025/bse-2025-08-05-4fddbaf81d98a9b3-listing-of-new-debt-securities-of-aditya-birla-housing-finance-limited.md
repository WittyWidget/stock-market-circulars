## Summary

Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited has listed new debt securities worth ₹1,500 crores on BSE's Debt segment. The securities carry a 7.58% annual interest rate with a 10-year maturity period ending July 31, 2035.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 150,000 securities listed
- Face Value: ₹1,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: ₹1,00,000 per security
- Interest Rate: 7.58% per annum
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA/Stable, ICRA AAA/STABLE
- Scrip Code: 976982
- ISIN: INE831R07615

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard debt securities listing process followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size set at 1 paise
- Trading members must refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details
- Contact debt department for clarifications on specified phone numbers

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 4, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 5, 2025
- Interest Payment: Annually
- Redemption Date: July 31, 2035

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement debt securities listing. The AAA credit rating indicates high-quality debt instrument. The 7.58% interest rate reflects current market conditions for housing finance sector bonds.
