## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of HDB Financial Services Limited worth Rs. 50 crores (50,000 securities at Rs. 1 lakh face value each) on private placement basis. The securities carry 7.9611% annual interest rate and will mature on January 5, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 50,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,01,674.90
- Interest Rate: 7.9611% per annum
- ISIN: INE756I07FB6 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CARE AAA/Stable, CRISIL AAA/Stable
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities to be traded under specified ISIN number INE756I07FB6
- Tick size compliance of 1 paise required

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: October 15, 2024
- Interest Payment Dates: Annually from January 5, 2026 to January 5, 2028
- Redemption Date: January 5, 2028
- Effective Trading Date: August 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine debt security listing for an existing issuer. The AAA credit rating indicates high credit quality. Trading members can access additional investment options in the debt segment.
