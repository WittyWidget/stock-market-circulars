## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in commercial paper issued by ONGC Petro additions Limited (Scrip Code: 729298, ISIN: INE163N14527) effective from October 27, 2025. The suspension is due to the commercial paper reaching its redemption date on October 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of ONGC Petro additions Limited to be suspended from trading
- Scrip Code: 729298
- ISIN: INE163N14527
- Redemption Date: October 28, 2025
- Trading suspension effective from: October 27, 2025
- Notice reference: DR-743/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative action taken prior to the maturity of a debt instrument.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must not deal in the aforementioned commercial paper of ONGC Petro additions Limited from October 27, 2025 onwards
- Trading Members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance

## Important Dates

- **October 24, 2025**: Notice date
- **October 27, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date
- **October 28, 2025**: Commercial paper redemption/maturity date

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard operational measure with minimal market impact. The trading suspension occurs one day before the commercial paper's maturity date, which is a routine practice to facilitate smooth redemption. The impact is limited to holders of this specific commercial paper instrument and does not affect broader market operations or the equity shares of ONGC Petro additions Limited.
