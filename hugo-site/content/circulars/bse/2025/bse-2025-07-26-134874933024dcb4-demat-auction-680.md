## Summary

BSE has published Settlement No. 680 for demat auction dated July 25, 2025. The settlement includes a comprehensive list of securities with their respective scrip codes, names, and quantities to be auctioned. This covers 51 different securities across various sectors including banking, pharmaceuticals, automobiles, and industrial companies.

## Key Points

- Settlement number: 680 dated July 25, 2025
- Total of 51 securities listed for demat auction
- Quantities range from 1 share to 26,000 shares depending on the security
- Major companies included: State Bank of India, ICICI Bank, Tata Motors, Dr. Reddy's Labs, Marico, and others
- Covers diverse sectors: banking, pharmaceuticals, automobiles, chemicals, and technology

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced in this circular. This is a standard operational settlement notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members holding positions in the listed securities must settle their obligations as per BSE auction settlement procedures
- Affected members must ensure adequate funds/securities availability for settlement
- Compliance with standard BSE demat auction settlement timelines and procedures

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: July 25, 2025
- Settlement Number: 680

## Impact Assessment

This demat auction settlement affects 51 securities with varying impact levels based on quantities involved. Notable impacts include:
- High quantity auctions: Capri Global (26,000 shares), Kaiser Corporation (2,340 shares), Bank of Baroda (1,538 shares)
- The settlement ensures proper delivery of securities and maintains market integrity
- Routine operational impact on affected members and their clients
- No significant market-wide disruption expected as this is standard settlement procedure
