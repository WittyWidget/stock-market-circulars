## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 5,631 new equity shares of ADVAIT ENERGY TRANSITIONS LIMITED (Scrip Code: 543230) issued to Non-Promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The securities will be available for trading with effect from Monday, October 13, 2025.

## Key Points

- **Number of Shares**: 5,631 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 1776/- per share (premium of Rs. 1766/-)
- **Allotment Basis**: Preferential allotment to Non-Promoters pursuant to warrant conversion
- **Distinctive Numbers**: 10929739 to 10935369
- **Date of Allotment**: August 5, 2025
- **ISIN**: INE0ALI01010
- **Ranking**: Pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of these new securities
- Lock-in period restrictions apply to all 5,631 shares until April 30, 2026
- Shares cannot be transferred or sold until the lock-in period expires

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 10, 2025
- **Date of Allotment**: August 5, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 13, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: April 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. The listing involves only 5,631 shares representing a small addition to the company's equity capital. The locked-in status until April 2026 means these shares will not be available for immediate trading, further limiting any market impact. This is a routine administrative notification for trading members to update their records.
