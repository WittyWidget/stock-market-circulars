## Summary

BSE has listed 95,50,000 new equity shares of Rotographics (India) Limited (Scrip Code: 539922) issued on a preferential basis to promoters and non-promoters. The shares are effective for trading from August 14, 2025.

## Key Points

- 95.5 lakh equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Re. 1/-
- Issue price: Rs. 11/- per share
- Allotment date: April 7, 2025
- ISIN: INE364S01014
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 3601301 to 13151300

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the new securities availability for trading and applicable lock-in restrictions.

## Important Dates

- **Trading commencement**: August 14, 2025
- **Allotment date**: April 7, 2025
- **Lock-in expiry (53 lakh shares)**: February 28, 2027
- **Lock-in expiry (42.5 lakh shares)**: February 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine preferential issue listing. The lock-in provisions ensure controlled supply of shares in the market over the next 2-3 years.
