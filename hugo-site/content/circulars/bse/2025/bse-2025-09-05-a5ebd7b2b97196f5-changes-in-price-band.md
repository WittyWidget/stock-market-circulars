## Summary

BSE has revised price bands for 59 securities effective September 8, 2025. The price bands range from 2% to 20% across different securities, representing a surveillance measure to manage price volatility and trading risks.

## Key Points

- 59 securities will have revised price bands effective September 8, 2025
- Price band percentages range from 2% to 20%
- Changes affect securities across various market segments
- Trading members have been notified of these surveillance measures
- Clarifications can be sought at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

Price band revisions for 59 securities with the following distribution:
- 2% price band: 4 securities (Abate As Industries, Anand Rayons, Rapicut Carbides, Welspun Investments)
- 5% price band: 19 securities
- 10% price band: 10 securities
- 20% price band: 26 securities

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must implement the revised price bands from September 8, 2025
- All trading in affected securities must adhere to the new price band limits
- Members should contact BSE surveillance team for any clarifications

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 8, 2025 - Revised price bands become applicable
- **Notice Date**: September 5, 2025 - Official notification issued

## Impact Assessment

The price band revisions will impact trading volatility for the affected 59 securities. Tighter price bands (2-5%) will restrict intraday price movements for certain securities, while wider bands (20%) allow for greater price discovery. This surveillance measure aims to balance market liquidity with risk management for securities that may have exhibited unusual price behavior.
