## Summary

BSE has updated the trading terms for debt securities of AETHON DEVELOPERS Pvt Ltd, effective August 7, 2025. The securities will change from unsecured NCDs to secured NCDs with a new ISIN code.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code 976099 will continue trading with revised terms
- Security type changes from Unsecured NCDs to Secured NCDs
- New ISIN: INE15S307015 (replacing INE15S308013)
- Changes effective from Thursday, August 7, 2025
- This is a continuation of notice 20250710-6 dated July 10, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

- **Mode of Security**: Changed from Unsecured NCDs to Secured NCDs
- **ISIN Code**: Updated from INE15S308013 to INE15S307015

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must note the revised terms for scrip code 976099
- Updated trading parameters to be implemented from August 7, 2025
- Contact BSE officials for further clarifications if required

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Thursday, August 7, 2025
- **Notice Date**: August 6, 2025
- **Reference Notice**: 20250710-6 dated July 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The change from unsecured to secured debt improves the security profile for investors, potentially affecting trading volumes and pricing. Trading members need to update their systems with the new ISIN and security classification.
