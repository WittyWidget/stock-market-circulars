## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Capri Global Capital Limited on private placement basis. The securities are worth Rs. 5 lakh each with a total issue of 240 units, representing Rs. 12 crores. The commercial paper carries CRISIL A1+ rating and matures on November 21, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each, total 240 units
- Issue price: Rs. 4,90,822.50 per unit
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Scrip Code: 730074, ISIN: INE180C14581
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Yes Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 22, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 22, 2025
- Redemption date: November 21, 2025
- Maturity period: 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The short-term debt instrument provides additional funding options for Capri Global Capital Limited and offers investment opportunities for institutional investors in the debt segment.
