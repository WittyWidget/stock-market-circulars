## Summary

BSE has published the list of securities and quantities for demat auction settlement number 706 dated September 3, 2025. The auction includes 94 different scrips with varying quantities ranging from 1 share to 11,418 shares.

## Key Points

- Settlement number: 706
- Settlement date: September 3, 2025
- Total securities listed: 94 scrips
- Quantities range from 1 to 11,418 shares
- Notable large quantities include One Mobikwik (11,418 shares), UTI Mutual Fund (5,000 shares), and Vimta Labs (3,692 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Parties involved in demat auction settlement must comply with BSE's settlement procedures and timelines for the specified securities.

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: September 3, 2025
- Settlement Number: 706

## Impact Assessment

The demat auction affects holders of the 94 listed securities. Impact is localized to specific scrips and their stakeholders. Large quantity auctions like One Mobikwik (11,418 shares) and UTI Mutual Fund (5,000 shares) may have more significant impact on those particular securities.
