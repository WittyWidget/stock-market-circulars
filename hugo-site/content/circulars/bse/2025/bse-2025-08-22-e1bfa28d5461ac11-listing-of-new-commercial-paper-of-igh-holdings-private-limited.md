## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 400 crore issued by IGH Holdings Private Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on the debt segment effective August 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total quantity: 8,000 units (Rs. 400 crore)
- Scrip Code: 730069
- ISIN: INE02FN14614
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,930 per unit
- Maturity: November 21, 2025 (91 days)
- Issuing and Paying Agent: Axis Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 22, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 22, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding option for IGH Holdings and investment opportunity for institutional investors in highly-rated commercial paper.
