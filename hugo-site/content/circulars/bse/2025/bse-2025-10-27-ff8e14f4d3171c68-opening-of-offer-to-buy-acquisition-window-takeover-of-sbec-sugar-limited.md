## Summary

SBEC Systems (India) Limited, along with six Persons Acting in Concert (PACs), has launched an open offer to acquire up to 1,23,90,009 equity shares representing 26% of the voting share capital of SBEC Sugar Limited at an offer price of Rs 21.19 per equity share. The offer is made pursuant to Regulation 3(2) and Regulation 4 of SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011.

## Key Points

- **Acquirer**: SBEC Systems (India) Limited
- **Target Company**: SBEC Sugar Limited (CIN: L15421UP1991PLC019160)
- **Offer Size**: Up to 1,23,90,009 equity shares (26% of voting capital)
- **Face Value**: Rs 10 per equity share
- **Offer Price**: Rs 21.19 per equity share (cash payment)
- **PACs Involved**: 6 entities/individuals including Moderate Leasing & Capital Services Ltd, A To Z Holdings Private Limited, Longwell Investment Private Limited, Mr. Jayesh Modi, Mrs. Kumkum Modi, and Mr. Umesh Kumar Modi
- **Offer Type**: Not conditional on minimum acceptance level
- **Competing Offer**: No competing offer exists as on date of Letter of Offer

## Regulatory Framework

- Offer made under Regulation 3(2), Regulation 3(3) and other applicable regulations of SEBI (SAST) Regulations, 2011
- Not a competing offer under Regulation 20 of SEBI (SAST) Regulations
- Unconditional offer not subject to minimum acceptance under Regulation 19
- No statutory or other approvals required to complete the acquisition as per the acquirer's knowledge

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - This is a significant takeover event involving acquisition of substantial stake (26%) in a listed sugar company. The offer price of Rs 21.19 per share will serve as a reference point for market valuation.

**Shareholder Impact**: High - Eligible shareholders have an exit opportunity at the disclosed offer price. The unconditional nature of the offer provides certainty of acceptance for tendered shares.

**Corporate Control Impact**: High - Successful completion of this offer will result in significant change in shareholding pattern and potentially impact control dynamics of SBEC Sugar Limited.

**Regulatory Compliance**: The offer is structured in full compliance with SEBI SAST Regulations and provides shareholders with rights under the takeover code.
