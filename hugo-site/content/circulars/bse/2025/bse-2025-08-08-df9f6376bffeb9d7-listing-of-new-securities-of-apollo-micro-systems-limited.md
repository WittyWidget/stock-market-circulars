## Summary

Apollo Micro Systems Limited (Scrip Code: 540879) has listed 2,70,42,894 new equity shares of Re. 1 each on BSE, issued at a premium of Rs. 113 to non-promoters on a preferential basis. These shares will commence trading from August 11, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 2,70,42,894 new equity shares of Re. 1 each listed
- Issue price: Rs. 114 per share (Re. 1 face value + Rs. 113 premium)
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Trading commences: August 11, 2025
- Distribution numbers: 306489561 to 333532454
- ISIN: INE713T01028
- Allotment date: June 2, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. Standard preferential issue listing process followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members can trade these securities from August 11, 2025
- New shares subject to lock-in restrictions until February 28, 2026
- Shares rank equally with existing equity shares

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: June 2, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 11, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: February 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Significant equity dilution for Apollo Micro Systems Limited with addition of 2.7 crore shares representing substantial increase in share capital. The preferential issue to non-promoters may affect promoter shareholding percentage and could impact stock price due to increased supply. Lock-in period until February 2026 provides temporary price stability.
