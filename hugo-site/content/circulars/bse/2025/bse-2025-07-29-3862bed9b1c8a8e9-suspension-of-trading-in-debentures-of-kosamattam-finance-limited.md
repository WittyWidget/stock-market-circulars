## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of KOSAMATTAM FINANCE LIMITED (KFL-ZC-22-8-25-NCD, ISIN: INE403Q07912) effective August 5, 2025, due to the company's debenture redemption process.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to KFL debentures with code 936168
- Record date for redemption set as August 5, 2025
- No dealings permitted from August 5, 2025 onwards
- Notice issued under reference DR-688/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes - standard procedure for debenture redemption process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified KFL debentures from August 5, 2025
- All market participants must note the trading restriction
- Compliance with corporate action procedures required

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: August 5, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: August 5, 2025
- **Notice Date**: July 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as suspension affects only specific debentures of one company. Debenture holders will receive redemption proceeds as per company's redemption schedule. No broader market implications expected.
