## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of MAS Financial Services Limited worth ₹100 crore on private placement basis, effective September 1, 2025. The securities carry a 9.1% per annum interest rate with monthly interest payments and mature on August 29, 2027.

## Key Points

- Face value: ₹10,000 per security
- Total quantity: 100,000 securities (₹100 crore)
- Interest rate: 9.1% per annum
- Credit rating: CARE AA-/Stable
- Market lot: 1 security
- Scrip code: 977064
- ISIN: INE348L07332

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing process for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: August 29, 2025
- Trading effective date: September 1, 2025
- Interest payment dates: Monthly from September 29, 2025 to August 29, 2027
- Redemption date: August 29, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt securities listing. The securities provide institutional investors with a fixed-income option carrying CARE AA-/Stable rating. No put/call options are available.
