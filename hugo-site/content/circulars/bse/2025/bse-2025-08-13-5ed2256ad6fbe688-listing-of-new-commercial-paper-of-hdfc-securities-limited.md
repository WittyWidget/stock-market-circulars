## Summary

HDFC Securities Limited has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 18 crores (3600 securities of Rs. 5 lakh each) on BSE Debt segment. The securities are issued on private placement basis and will trade in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 3600 securities (Rs. 18 crores total value)
- Issue price: Rs. 492,219.50 per security
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- Trading effective from August 13, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Market lot size is 1

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 13, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 13, 2025
- Redemption date: November 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. Provides additional short-term funding avenue for HDFC Securities Limited with 3-month maturity period.
