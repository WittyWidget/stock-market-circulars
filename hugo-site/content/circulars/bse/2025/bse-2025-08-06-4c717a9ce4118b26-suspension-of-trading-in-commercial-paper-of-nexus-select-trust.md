## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper of Nexus Select Trust (Scrip Code: 729368, ISIN: INE0NDH14064) effective August 7, 2025, due to the instrument reaching its maturity date.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Nexus Select Trust to be suspended from trading
- Scrip Code: 729368
- ISIN: INE0NDH14064
- Suspension effective from August 7, 2025
- Reason: Maturity of the commercial paper

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a standard administrative action for maturing debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members are advised not to deal in the aforementioned Commercial Paper effective August 7, 2025
- Trading Members must take note of this suspension notice

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 6, 2025
- **Trading Suspension Date**: August 7, 2025
- **Maturity/Redemption Date**: August 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine suspension due to natural maturity of the commercial paper. Trading Members holding positions in this instrument should complete any necessary transactions before the suspension date.
