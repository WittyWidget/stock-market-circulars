## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs 225 crore issued by Bajaj Finance Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted to trading on BSE Debt segment with effect from September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs 5,00,000 each
- Total quantity: 45,000 units (Rs 225 crore)
- Scrip Code: 730179, Scrip ID: BFL090925
- ISIN: INE296A14C22
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs 4,92,159.50 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: September 9, 2025
- Listing Date: September 10, 2025
- Redemption Date: December 9, 2025 (3-month tenor)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt instrument listing. Provides additional short-term funding avenue for Bajaj Finance and investment option for institutional investors in the debt segment.
