## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Infina Finance Private Limited worth Rs. 100 crore on private placement basis. The instrument carries CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ ratings and will be traded in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5 lakh face value each, totaling 2,000 units
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,966 per unit (discount to face value)
- ISIN: INE879F14LF7, Scrip Code: 730018
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Must use specified ISIN number for trading

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 13, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 14, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 12, 2025
- Tenure: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by a private finance company. The instrument provides short-term funding option for the issuer and investment opportunity for institutional investors in the debt segment.
