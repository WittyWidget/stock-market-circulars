## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of further securities issued by 13 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These new securities will be listed and admitted for trading on the exchange with effect from Wednesday, July 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- 13 companies have issued additional securities under ESOP/ESOS
- Total of 2,063,559 new shares across all companies
- All securities will be available for trading from July 30, 2025
- None of the securities are under lock-in period
- Face values range from ₹1 to ₹10 per share

## Listed Companies and Securities

- **AFFLE (India) Limited**: 37,000 shares (₹2 face value)
- **Avantel Limited**: 7,600 shares (₹2 face value)
- **AXIS Bank Ltd**: 67,820 shares (₹2 face value)
- **CEINSYS Tech Limited**: 400,000 shares (₹10 face value)
- **DCB Bank Limited**: 55,656 shares (₹10 face value)
- **Dhani Services Limited**: 1,207,200 shares (₹2 face value)
- **Eris Lifesciences Limited**: 9,468 shares (₹1 face value)
- **ICICI Lombard General Insurance**: 162,038 shares (₹10 face value)
- **L&T Technology Services**: 4,500 shares (₹2 face value)
- **Oracle Financial Services Software**: 2,221 shares (₹5 face value)
- **Ujjivan Small Finance Bank**: 289,961 shares (₹10 face value)
- **Zinka Logistics Solutions**: 184,545 shares (₹1 face value)
- **Zodiac Energy Limited**: 27,550 shares (₹10 face value)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification for ESOP/ESOS securities.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new listings for their reference and trading operations.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Trading Date**: July 30, 2025 (Wednesday)
- **Notice Date**: July 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine ESOP/ESOS listings representing employee stock options being converted to tradeable securities. The addition of these securities may provide minor liquidity increases for the respective stocks.
