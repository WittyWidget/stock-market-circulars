## Summary

Oberoi Realty Limited has fixed the record date for part redemption of its non-convertible debentures (ISIN: INE093I07066, Code: ORL-7.95-24-10-26-PVT, Scrip Code: 976126). The debentures will trade with a reduced face value of Rs. 53,750 per debenture effective October 9, 2025. The record date for determining eligible debenture holders for interest payment and part redemption is October 9, 2025.

## Key Points

- Part redemption of non-convertible debentures of Oberoi Realty Limited
- ISIN: INE093I07066, Scrip Code: 976126
- Reduced face value: Rs. 53,750 per debenture
- Trading with reduced face value begins October 9, 2025
- Settlement number: DR-732/2025-2026
- Dual purpose: Payment of interest and part redemption of principal

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that debentures will trade with reduced face value from the effective date
- Debenture holders as on record date (October 9, 2025) will be eligible for interest payment and part redemption
- Trading systems must be updated to reflect the reduced face value of Rs. 53,750 per debenture

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: October 9, 2025
- **Effective Date for Reduced Face Value**: October 9, 2025
- **Settlement Number**: DR-732/2025-2026
- **Circular Date**: October 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Debenture Holders**: Holders of Oberoi Realty debentures as on the record date will receive part redemption of principal and interest payment. The face value reduction indicates partial maturity of the debt instrument.

**Trading Impact**: From October 9, 2025, the debentures will trade at the reduced face value of Rs. 53,750, which trading members and systems must account for in pricing and settlement.

**Liquidity**: Part redemption provides liquidity to debenture holders while the instrument continues to trade at reduced face value until full maturity in October 2026.
