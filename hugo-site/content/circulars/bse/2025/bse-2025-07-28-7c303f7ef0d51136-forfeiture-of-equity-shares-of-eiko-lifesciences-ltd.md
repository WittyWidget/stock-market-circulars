## Summary

Eiko Lifesciences Ltd (Scrip Code: 540204) has forfeited 1,39,591 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each that were partly paid-up due to non-payment of allotment/call monies by shareholders.

## Key Points

- Company: Eiko Lifesciences Ltd (Scrip Code: 540204)
- Forfeited shares: 1,39,591 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Reason: Non-payment of allotment/call monies
- Distinctive numbers: 13720855 to 13860445
- Share status: Partly paid-up

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the share forfeiture for their records and trading operations.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: July 28, 2025
- Effective immediately

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this is a company-specific administrative action affecting partly paid-up shares. The forfeiture reduces the total outstanding shares of Eiko Lifesciences Ltd and may have minor impact on the company's capital structure.
