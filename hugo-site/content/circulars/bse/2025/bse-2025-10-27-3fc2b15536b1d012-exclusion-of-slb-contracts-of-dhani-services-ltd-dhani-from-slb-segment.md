## Summary

BSE has announced the exclusion of Dhani Services Ltd (Scrip Code: 532960, SLB Code: DHANI) from the Securities Lending and Borrowing (SLB) segment. This action is consequent to the amalgamation of Dhani Services Limited as referenced in exchange notices 20251024-55 and 20251027-1. No SLB contracts for this security will be available for trading from the effective date.

## Key Points

- Dhani Services Ltd (SLB Code: DHANI, Scrip Code: 532960) to be excluded from SLB segment
- Action taken due to amalgamation of Dhani Services Limited
- No contracts will be available for trading in this security in the SLB segment
- References prior exchange notices 20251024-55 and 20251027-1 regarding the amalgamation

## Regulatory Changes

Removal of DHANI from the list of securities available for Securities Lending and Borrowing operations on BSE.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must:
- Note the exclusion of DHANI from the SLB segment
- Cease all SLB contract trading activities for this security from the effective date
- Refer to the related amalgamation notices (20251024-55 and 20251027-1) for complete context

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** October 27, 2025
- **Effective Ex-Date:** October 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This exclusion affects market participants engaged in Securities Lending and Borrowing activities involving Dhani Services Ltd. The removal is part of the corporate restructuring process due to amalgamation. Traders and institutional investors using SLB mechanisms for DHANI will need to close or settle their positions as no new contracts will be available from October 28, 2025. The impact is limited to SLB segment participants and does not affect regular equity trading in the security.
