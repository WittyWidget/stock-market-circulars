## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 13,83,000 new equity shares of Precision Wires India Ltd (Scrip Code: 523539) issued through conversion of warrants on a preferential basis to non-promoters. The shares will commence trading on September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- 13,83,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at premium of Rs. 150/-
- Issue price: Rs. 151.00 per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis through warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 178657976 to 180040975
- Date of allotment: July 29, 2025
- ISIN: INE372C01037

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure for additional equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities listing
- Lock-in period compliance required until March 23, 2026
- All 13.83 lakh shares subject to lock-in restrictions

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 29, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: September 10, 2025
- **Lock-in Period End**: March 23, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this represents routine additional equity listing through warrant conversion. The lock-in period until March 2026 ensures these shares won't immediately affect market liquidity. Represents capital infusion for the company through preferential allotment to non-promoters.
