## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth ₹25 crore issued by Standard Chartered Securities (India) Limited on private placement basis, effective August 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of ₹5 lakh denomination each
- Total quantity: 500 units
- Issue price: ₹4,83,806 per unit
- Credit rating: ICRA A1+
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: ₹5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Security Details

- **Scrip Code**: 729989
- **Scrip ID**: SCSL80825
- **ISIN**: INE472H14680
- **Face Value**: ₹5,00,000
- **Market Lot**: 1

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 8, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: February 5, 2026
- **Trading Commencement**: August 8, 2025

## Trading Requirements

- Trading exclusively in BSE Debt segment
- Dematerialized form mandatory
- Minimum trading unit: ₹5 lakhs
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Ltd

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding option for Standard Chartered Securities with 6-month tenure.
