## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of Poonawalla Fincorp Limited issued on private placement basis. The securities have a face value of Rs.5,00,000 each with 7000 quantities listed on the debt segment.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs.5 lakh face value each
- Quantity: 7000 units
- Scrip Code: 730040
- ISIN: INE511C14YV3
- Issue Price: Rs.4,92,123 per unit
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- Issuing Agent: ICICI Bank Ltd

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialised form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs.5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applicable

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 18, 2025
- Date of Redemption: November 17, 2025
- Listing Date: August 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding requirements. The securities are rated A1+ indicating high credit quality and low default risk.
