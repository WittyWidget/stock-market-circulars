## Summary

IRB InvIT Fund (Scrip Code: 540526, ISIN: INE183W23014) has successfully listed 54,14,05,000 new units on BSE with effect from October 16, 2025. These units were allotted to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) pursuant to a Qualified Institutional Placement at an issue price of Rs. 60/- per unit. The new units rank pari-passu with existing units of the fund.

## Key Points

- Total units listed: 54,14,05,000 units
- Issue price: Rs. 60/- per unit
- Allotment exclusively to Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs)
- New units rank pari-passu with existing units
- Distinctive numbers: 580500001 to 1121905000
- Scrip Code: 540526
- ISIN: INE183W23014

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular. This is a standard listing notice for securities allotted through Qualified Institutional Placement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed to enable trading of these new units from the effective date
- No specific compliance actions required from market participants
- For clarifications, trading members can contact Ms. Mayuri Visaria at 022-22728901

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** October 15, 2025
- **Trading Commencement Date:** October 16, 2025 (Thursday)
- **Notice Date:** October 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** The listing of 54.14 crore units represents a significant capital raise for IRB InvIT Fund through the QIP route. This indicates strong institutional interest in infrastructure investment trusts and will increase the free float of the security.

**Investor Impact:** The QIP allotment to institutional investors may lead to increased liquidity in the counter. Existing unitholders will see dilution, though the pari-passu ranking ensures equal rights for all units.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members should update their systems to reflect the increased unit base and new distinctive number range. The substantial increase in outstanding units may impact trading volumes and liquidity patterns.
