## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 12,60,000 new equity shares of Desh Rakshak Aushdhalaya Ltd. (Scrip Code: 531521) issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis. These shares will commence trading on the Exchange from Monday, October 27, 2025. The shares were allotted on September 9, 2025, at an issue price of Rs. 22/- per share (face value Rs. 10/- with a premium of Rs. 12/-) and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- Total shares listed: 12,60,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issue price: Rs. 22/- per share (including premium of Rs. 12/-)
- Allotment date: September 9, 2025
- Trading commencement date: October 27, 2025
- Distinctive numbers: 5002601 to 6262600
- ISIN: INE971E01016
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members must note the listing of these new securities and ensure proper handling of trades in these shares starting October 27, 2025. Lock-in restrictions must be observed as specified.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 9, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 27, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: 
  - 11,80,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 5002601 to 6182600): April 30, 2026
  - 80,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 6182601 to 6262600): April 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with limited market impact. The preferential allotment increases the company's equity base by 12,60,000 shares. The lock-in period until April 2026 ensures these shares cannot be immediately traded, providing stability. Impact is confined to Desh Rakshak Aushdhalaya Ltd. and its shareholders, with no broader market or operational implications for other trading members or securities.
