## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of MARWADI CHANDARANA INTERMEDIARIES BROKERS PRIVATE LIMITED on private placement basis. The zero coupon securities worth Rs. 20 lakhs per unit are now available for trading on the BSE Debt segment.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 20,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Zero coupon structure with cumulative payment on maturity
- Credit Rating: IVR A-/Stable
- ISIN: INE0K0Y08011
- Scrip Code: 976985

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities must be traded only in dematerialised form
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Trading members should refer to Placement Memorandum for detailed terms

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 5, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 6, 2025
- Maturity/Redemption Date: August 5, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing of debt securities by a financial intermediary. The securities provide additional investment options in the debt market segment for institutional investors.
