## Summary

BSE announces the addition of two newly listed companies to the BSE IPO Index effective October 20, 2025. Rubicon Research Limited (ticker 544578) and Canara Robeco Asset Management Company Limited (ticker 544580) will be included in the index following their listing on October 16, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two companies being added to BSE IPO Index
- Rubicon Research Limited (Exchange ticker: 544578)
- Canara Robeco Asset Management Company Limited (Exchange ticker: 544580)
- Companies listed on BSE on Thursday, October 16, 2025
- Index addition effective at market open on Monday, October 20, 2025
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine index maintenance action.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Market participants tracking the BSE IPO Index should note the composition changes for portfolio rebalancing purposes.

## Important Dates

- **October 16, 2025**: Listing date for both companies on BSE
- **October 20, 2025**: Effective date for addition to BSE IPO Index (at market open)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine index maintenance action following new listings. The BSE IPO Index tracks recently listed companies, and these additions are expected as part of normal index operations.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal - Index funds and ETFs tracking the BSE IPO Index will need to adjust their holdings to reflect the new constituents. The changes are included in the corporate action file for automated processing.

**Investor Impact**: Limited - Passive investors tracking the BSE IPO Index will gain exposure to these two newly listed companies. Active investors may see minor trading activity around the inclusion date.
