## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 500,001 new equity shares of EPUJA SPIRITECH LIMITED (Scrip Code: 532092) effective from Friday, September 5, 2025. The shares were issued at Rs. 3.40 per share (face value Rs. 1 plus premium of Rs. 2.40) to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 500,001 new equity shares of Rs. 1/- each listed
- Issued at premium of Rs. 2.40 to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Shares arising from conversion of warrants
- Issue price: Rs. 3.40 per share
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE807D01030
- Distribution Numbers: 77609921 to 78109921

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed that these securities are permitted for trading on the Exchange from September 5, 2025.

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 30, 2024
- Trading Commencement: September 5, 2025
- Lock-in Period Ends: March 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this involves a relatively small number of shares (500,001) issued through warrant conversion. The lock-in period until March 31, 2026, means these shares cannot be traded freely until then, limiting immediate supply impact on the stock.
