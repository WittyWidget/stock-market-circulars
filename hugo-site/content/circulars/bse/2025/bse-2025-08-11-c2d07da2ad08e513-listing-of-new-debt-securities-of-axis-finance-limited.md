## Summary

Axis Finance Limited has listed new debt securities on BSE's Debt segment effective August 11, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each, carrying an interest rate of 8.04% per annum.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 10,000 securities with market lot of 1
- Scrip Code: 977005, Scrip ID: 804AFL35
- ISIN: INE891K08224
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA/Stable, IND AAA/Stable
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Interest Rate: 8.04% per annum (paid annually)
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard debt security listing procedures followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities must be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Tick size of 1 paise must be maintained

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 8, 2025
- Listing Date: August 11, 2025
- Interest Payment Dates: Annually from August 8, 2026 to August 8, 2035
- Redemption Date: August 8, 2035

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement debt security listing. The AAA credit rating indicates high credit quality. Trading members can access additional details through BSE's debt memorandum section.
