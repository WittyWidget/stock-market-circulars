## Summary

BSE has listed two new commercial paper securities issued by Navi Finserv Limited on private placement basis. The securities have face values of Rs. 5 lakhs each, with different maturity periods and credit ratings of CARE A1+ and IND A1+.

## Key Points

- Two commercial paper issues listed with total value of Rs. 250 crores
- Securities denominated at Rs. 5 lakhs each with different redemption dates
- Both issues carry CARE A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent
- Trading begins effective July 28, 2025

## Security Details

**First Issue (NFL25725):**
- Quantity: 300 securities
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,88,372
- Allotment Date: July 25, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 24, 2025
- ISIN: INE342T14DT5

**Second Issue (NFL250725):**
- Quantity: 200 securities  
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,84,455
- Allotment Date: July 25, 2025
- Redemption Date: November 24, 2025
- ISIN: INE342T14DS7

## Trading Requirements

- Securities tradeable only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise
- Market lot size of 1

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 25, 2025
- Trading Commencement: July 28, 2025
- First Issue Redemption: October 24, 2025
- Second Issue Redemption: November 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding options for Navi Finserv Limited and investment opportunities for institutional investors in the debt segment.
