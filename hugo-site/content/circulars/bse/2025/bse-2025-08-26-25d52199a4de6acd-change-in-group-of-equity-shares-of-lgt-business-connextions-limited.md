## Summary

BSE has announced that equity shares of LGT Business Connextions Limited (Scrip Code: 544489) will be transferred from the Trade for Trade segment (MT Group) to the Rolling segment with effect from Wednesday, September 10, 2025. The shares will be shifted under M Group for regular dealings.

## Key Points

- Company: LGT Business Connextions Limited
- Scrip Code: 544489
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade (MT Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (M Group)
- Effective Date: September 10, 2025
- This change continues from previous notice 20250825-63 dated August 25, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

- Transfer from restrictive Trade for Trade segment to regular Rolling segment
- Shift from MT Group to M Group classification
- Enhanced liquidity and normal trading mechanics will apply

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the segment change
- Normal rolling settlement procedures will apply from the effective date
- Contact Mr. Parag Jain (022 2272 8685) for further details

## Important Dates

- **September 10, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer
- **August 25, 2025**: Previous related notice issued
- **August 26, 2025**: Current notice date

## Impact Assessment

- **Positive Impact**: Enhanced liquidity and easier trading for investors
- **Trading Impact**: Move from T+T restrictions to regular rolling settlement
- **Market Access**: Improved market access and trading flexibility
- **Investor Benefit**: Better price discovery and reduced trading constraints
