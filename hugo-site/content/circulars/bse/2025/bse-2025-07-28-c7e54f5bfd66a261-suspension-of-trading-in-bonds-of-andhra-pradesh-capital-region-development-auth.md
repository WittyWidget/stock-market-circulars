## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in bonds of Andhra Pradesh Capital Region Development Authority (APCRDA) effective July 30, 2025, due to the record date for interest payment and redemption.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to APCRDA bonds with code INE01E708024 (958177)
- Record date set for July 30, 2025
- Suspension covers both interest payment and bond redemption
- Trading members advised not to deal in these bonds from July 30, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - standard procedure for bond record date events.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in APCRDA bonds from July 30, 2025
- All trading members required to take note of the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Record Date**: July 30, 2025
- **No Dealings From**: July 30, 2025
- **Notice Date**: July 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as suspension affects only APCRDA bonds for specific corporate action. Standard procedure for debt securities during record date events. No broader market implications expected.
