## Summary

Vivo Bio tech Ltd. (Scrip Code: 511509) has listed 4,48,889 new equity shares on BSE with effect from Friday, August 8, 2025. These shares were issued to promoters on preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants at Rs. 45 per share.

## Key Points

- 4,48,889 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 35/-
- Shares issued to promoters on preferential basis through warrant conversion
- Trading permitted from August 8, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 17475928 to 17924816
- ISIN: INE380K01017

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and trading permissions.

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: April 17, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 8, 2025
- Lock-in Period Expires: February 28, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this represents routine listing of additional shares from warrant conversion. All shares are subject to lock-in until February 28, 2027, preventing immediate trading by promoters.
