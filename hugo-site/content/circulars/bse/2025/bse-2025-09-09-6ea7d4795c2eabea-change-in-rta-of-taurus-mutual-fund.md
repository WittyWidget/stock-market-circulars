## Summary

Taurus Mutual Fund has announced a change in its Registrar & Transfer Agent (RTA) from Kfintech to Computer Age Management Services Ltd (CAMS), effective from Monday, September 15, 2025.

## Key Points

- Current RTA: Kfintech
- New RTA: Computer Age Management Services Ltd (CAMS)
- Effective Date: Monday, September 15, 2025
- Notice issued by BSE VP - Mutual Fund and AVP - Mutual Fund

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is an administrative operational change within the mutual fund structure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members are requested to take note of the RTA change
- No specific compliance actions required from members

## Important Dates

- **September 15, 2025**: Effective date for RTA change from Kfintech to CAMS

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational change that may affect mutual fund transaction processing and investor servicing. Investors and intermediaries should be aware of the change for any future transactions or queries related to Taurus Mutual Fund schemes.
