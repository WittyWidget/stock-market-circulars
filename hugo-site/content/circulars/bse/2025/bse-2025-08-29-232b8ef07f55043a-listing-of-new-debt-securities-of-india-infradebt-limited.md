## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of India Infradebt Limited on private placement basis effective August 29, 2025. The securities carry a face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each with a total quantity of 1,50,000 units and bear interest at 7.60% per annum.

## Key Points

- Scrip Code: 977060, Scrip ID: 760IIL37
- ISIN Number: INE537P07869
- Face Value: Rs. 1,00,000 per unit
- Total Quantity: 1,50,000 units
- Interest Rate: 7.60% per annum (paid annually)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL AAA/stable, ICRA AAA/STABLE
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick Size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Trading members must use specified ISIN number
- Refer to Placement Memorandum for detailed terms

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 28, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 29, 2025
- Redemption Date: August 28, 2037
- Interest Payment: Annually

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis. The high credit rating (AAA) indicates low risk profile for investors.
