## Summary

BSE announces the schedule for Offer to Buy – Acquisition Window (Takeover) for ADDI INDUSTRIES LTD shares. The offer covers up to 27,78,399 equity shares at Rs. 75 per share, running from August 28 to September 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- Security: ADDI INDUSTRIES LTD (Symbol: ADDIND, ISIN: INE757C01021, Scrip Code: 507852)
- Offer Size: Up to 27,78,399 equity shares
- Offer Price: Rs. 75.00 per share (Face Value: Rs. 5.00)
- Market Lot: 1 share
- Trading sessions from 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (4:00 PM on last day for take-up)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes specified - operational circular for takeover process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must ensure UCC registration with correct PAN and category
- Only investors with shares in demat form can participate
- Members must use designated iBBS and RTRMS platforms
- Custodian Members must follow bid acceptance procedures through Extranet

## Important Dates

- **Opening Date**: Thursday, August 28, 2025
- **Closing Date**: Thursday, September 11, 2025
- **Bid Offering Period**: August 28 - September 11, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Give-up/Take-up Period**: August 28 - September 10, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Final Give-up**: September 11, 2025 (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
- **Final Take-up**: September 11, 2025 (9:15 AM - 4:00 PM)

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact limited to ADDI INDUSTRIES LTD shareholders and trading members. The takeover offer provides exit opportunity for existing shareholders at Rs. 75 per share. Trading operations will need to accommodate OTB module activities during the specified period.
