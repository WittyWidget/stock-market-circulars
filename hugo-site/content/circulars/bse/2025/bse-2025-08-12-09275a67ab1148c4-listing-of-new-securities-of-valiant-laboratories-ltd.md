## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 1,08,62,500 new equity shares of Valiant Laboratories Ltd. (Scrip Code: 543998) effective from Wednesday, August 13, 2025. These shares were issued at a premium and will rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 1,08,62,500 new equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Issued at a premium of Rs. 65/- per share
- ISIN: INE0JWS01017
- Distribution Numbers: 43450001 to 54312500
- Allotment Date: August 11, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 13, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure for additional equity shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading on the exchange.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 11, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of additional shares for an existing listed company. The new shares will increase the total tradable float of Valiant Laboratories Ltd.
