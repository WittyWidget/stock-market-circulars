## Summary

BSE has published the demat auction settlement No. 683 dated July 30, 2025, listing securities and their respective quantities that are part of the auction settlement process.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 683
- Settlement Date: July 30, 2025
- Total securities listed: 115 different scrips
- Quantities range from 1 share to 16,503 shares depending on the security
- Covers diverse sectors including pharmaceuticals, technology, energy, finance, and manufacturing

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must settle the listed securities as per BSE auction settlement procedures
- Settlement must be completed as per the specified quantities for each scrip code

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: July 30, 2025
- Settlement Number: 683

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational circular with low market impact. The auction settlement affects specific quantities of various securities and is part of BSE's regular settlement process. Individual investors holding these securities in demat form may be affected based on their specific positions.
