## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 45,00,000 new equity shares of Bijoy Hans Ltd (Scrip Code: 524723) issued to promoters on preferential basis. The shares will be available for trading from September 10, 2025, and are subject to lock-in until April 30, 2027.

## Key Points

- 45,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 2.50/-
- Issue price: Rs. 12.50/- per share
- Allotment date: May 27, 2025
- Trading commences: September 10, 2025
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE491D01017
- Distribution numbers: 3000022 to 7500021

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities listing
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all 45,00,000 shares until April 30, 2027
- Shares cannot be traded or transferred during lock-in period

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: May 27, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: September 10, 2025
- **Lock-in Period End**: April 30, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The preferential allotment increases the total share capital of Bijoy Hans Ltd. The 2-year lock-in period ensures promoter commitment while the shares being issued to promoters may strengthen their control over the company. Market impact is expected to be moderate as these are promoter shares with trading restrictions.
