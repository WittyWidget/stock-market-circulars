## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Godrej Finance Limited on private placement basis worth Rs. 22.5 crores (4,500 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each). The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective August 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper quantity: 4,500 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total issue size: Rs. 22.5 crores
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,050.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- ISIN: INE02KN14572
- Scrip Code: 730075
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- ICICI Bank Limited serves as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 22, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 22, 2025
- Redemption date: November 21, 2025
- Tenure: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides short-term funding avenue for Godrej Finance Limited and investment option for debt market participants. The high credit ratings (A1+) indicate low credit risk for investors.
