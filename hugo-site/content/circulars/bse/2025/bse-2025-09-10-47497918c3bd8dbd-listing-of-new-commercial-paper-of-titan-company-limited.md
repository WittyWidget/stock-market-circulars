## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Titan Company Limited on private placement basis worth Rs. 20 crores (40,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each). The securities are listed on BSE Debt segment with effect from September 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each, totaling 40,000 units
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,752/- per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+
- Scrip code: 730176, ISIN: INE280A14484
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- ICICI Bank Limited as Issuing and Paying agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded under specified ISIN number INE280A14484

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: September 9, 2025
- Listing date: September 10, 2025
- Redemption date: December 9, 2025
- Maturity period: 3 months

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding purposes. The instrument provides Titan Company with working capital financing at competitive rates given the A1+ rating. No impact on equity trading or broader market operations.
