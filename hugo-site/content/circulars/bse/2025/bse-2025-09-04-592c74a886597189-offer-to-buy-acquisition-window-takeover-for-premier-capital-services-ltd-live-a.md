## Summary

BSE has announced the schedule for an Offer to Buy (Acquisition Window/Takeover) for Premier Capital Services Ltd shares. The offer period runs from September 4, 2025 to September 18, 2025, with an offer price of Rs. 5.65 per share for up to 96,35,840 equity shares.

## Key Points

- Security: Premier Capital Services Ltd (PREMCAP, ISIN: INE946K01023)
- Offer size: Up to 96,35,840 equity shares
- Offer price: Rs. 5.65 per share
- Face value: Rs. 1.00
- Market lot: 1 share
- Opening date: September 4, 2025
- Closing date: September 18, 2025

## Trading Schedule

### Session Timings:
