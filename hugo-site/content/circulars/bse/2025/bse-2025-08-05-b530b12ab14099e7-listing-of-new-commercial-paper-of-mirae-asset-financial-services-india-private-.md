## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Mirae Asset Financial Services (India) Private Limited worth Rs. 75 crores (1,500 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on private placement basis. The paper carries CRISIL A1+ rating and matures in 3 months.

## Key Points

- Issue size: Rs. 75 crores (1,500 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,460 per unit (discount to face value)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+ (highest short-term rating)
- Scrip code: 729962, Scrip ID: MAFSIP5825
- ISIN: INE0JRU14248
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading restricted to standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 5, 2025
- Listing date: August 5, 2025
- Redemption date: November 4, 2025
- Maturity period: 3 months (91 days)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by a financial services company. The A1+ rating indicates strong creditworthiness and the 3-month tenure provides short-term funding option for the issuer.
