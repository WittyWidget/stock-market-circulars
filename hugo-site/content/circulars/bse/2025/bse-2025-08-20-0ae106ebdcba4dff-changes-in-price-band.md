## Summary

BSE has revised price bands for 27 securities with effect from August 21, 2025. The price bands range from 2% to 20%, with most securities having restrictive bands of 2% to 5%, indicating heightened surveillance measures.

## Key Points

- 27 securities affected by price band revisions
- Changes effective from August 21, 2025
- Price bands range from 2% (most restrictive) to 20%
- Notice issued on August 20, 2025
- Clarifications available at bse.surv@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

- Price band revisions applied as surveillance measure
- Bands set at 2%, 5%, 10%, and 20% levels based on security assessment
- 11 securities restricted to 2% price band (highest surveillance)
- 8 securities set to 5% price band
- 4 securities set to 10% price band
- 4 securities set to 20% price band

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the revised price bands
- All trading must comply with new price band limits from August 21, 2025
- Members can seek clarifications via email to surveillance department

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** August 20, 2025
- **Effective Date:** August 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The price band revisions indicate enhanced surveillance on these securities, likely due to unusual price movements or volatility concerns. Restrictive bands (2-5%) may limit trading activity and price discovery, while wider bands (10-20%) allow more normal trading within controlled limits. Traders and investors should adjust their strategies accordingly for affected securities.
