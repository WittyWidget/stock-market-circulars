## Summary

Indsoya Limited (Scrip Code: 503639) has listed 1,00,00,000 new equity shares of Rs. 5/- each issued at par on rights basis. These shares are permitted to trade on BSE with effect from Wednesday, August 20, 2025.

## Key Points

- 1 crore equity shares of Rs. 5/- each issued at par value
- Shares issued on rights basis to existing shareholders
- New ISIN: INE314N01028
- Distribution numbers: 400001 to 10400000
- Date of allotment: August 12, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 20, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard listing procedure for rights issue shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and trading permissions.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** August 12, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** August 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The rights issue increases Indsoya Limited's equity base by 1 crore shares. The new shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares, maintaining equal rights for all shareholders. This capital raising exercise may strengthen the company's financial position for growth initiatives.
