## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited on private placement basis. Two separate issues have been listed: Rs. 25 crores maturing on August 8, 2025 and Rs. 15 crores maturing on August 14, 2025. Both securities carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- First issue: Rs. 10 crores (2000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) with scrip code 729350, maturing August 8, 2025
- Second issue: Rs. 15 crores (3000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) with scrip code 729954, maturing August 14, 2025
- Both issues rated CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Securities to be traded under specified ISIN numbers (INE916D145E7 and INE916D145M0)

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 1, 2025
- First Issue Redemption: August 8, 2025
- Second Issue Redemption: August 14, 2025
- Trading Effective: August 1, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The short maturity periods (7 and 13 days) indicate working capital requirements rather than significant business expansion or restructuring.
