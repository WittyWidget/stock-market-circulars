## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of VITP Private Limited issued on private placement basis. The securities are listed on the BSE Debt segment with effect from August 6, 2025.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 11,000 securities
- Market Lot: 1
- Scrip Code: 976987
- ISIN Number: INE743G08134
- Credit Rating: ICRA A-(stable)
- Face Value: Rs. 100,000.00
- Issue Price: Rs. 100,000.00
- Interest Rate: 11% per annum
- Date of Allotment: August 4, 2025
- Securities will trade only in dematerialised form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that securities will be traded only in dematerialised form
- Trading members should refer to the Placement Memorandum for further details
- Contact debt department for clarifications on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 4, 2025
- Listing Date: August 6, 2025
- Interest Payment: Annually from August 4, 2026 to August 3, 2055
- Redemption Date: August 3, 2055

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis. The listing provides additional debt instrument options for investors in the BSE Debt segment.
