## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of OXYZO Financial Services Limited worth ₹25 crore issued on private placement basis. The securities carry a 9.90% interest rate and mature on July 24, 2028.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 2,500 securities with face value of ₹1,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 976948, ISIN: INE04VS07446
- Interest Rate: 9.90% per annum paid annually
- Credit Rating: ICRA A+
- Put option available as per Information Memorandum
- Trading only in dematerialised form with tick size of 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes involved - standard debt listing procedure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialised form
- Securities to be identified by ISIN Number INE04VS07446
- Tick size compliance of 1 paise mandatory

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: July 24, 2025
- Listing Date: July 28, 2025
- Interest Payment: Annually
- Redemption Date: July 24, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement debt listing. The securities provide institutional investors with a rated investment option carrying ICRA A+ rating and 9.90% yield.
