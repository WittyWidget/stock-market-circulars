## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 25 crores issued by Capri Global Capital Limited on private placement basis. The securities are rated CRISIL A1+ and will be traded in dematerialized form only.

## Key Points

- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total Issue: 500 units (Rs. 25 crores)
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,60,829.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+
- ISIN: INE180C14557
- Scrip Code: 729918
- Trading starts: July 29, 2025
- Maturity Date: July 29, 2026 (1 year tenure)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1 unit

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 29, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: July 29, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: July 29, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The commercial paper provides short-term funding option for Capri Global Capital Limited and investment opportunity for institutional investors in BSE debt segment.
