## Summary

BSE has published the Demat Auction list for Settlement No. 735 dated October 15, 2025. The auction includes 159 securities spanning multiple sectors including banking, technology, energy, manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, real estate, and consumer goods. Quantities range from single shares to tens of thousands depending on the scrip.

## Key Points

- Settlement Number: 735
- Settlement Date: October 15, 2025
- Total Securities Listed: 159 scrips
- Sectors Covered: Banking, Technology, Energy, Manufacturing, Pharmaceuticals, Real Estate, Consumer Goods, Metals, Telecommunications
- Largest quantity auctions include: SYLPH TECHNO (97,000 shares), FCS SOFTWARE (62,377 shares), Deep Diamond (56,897 shares)
- Major blue-chip stocks included: AXIS BANK (1,623 shares), JSW STEEL (113 shares), TECH MAHINDRA (76 shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine demat auction settlement notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and market participants must note the securities listed for auction
- Clearing members should ensure settlement obligations are met for the listed quantities
- Participants must comply with standard BSE auction settlement procedures
- Delivery obligations must be fulfilled as per BSE settlement timelines

## Important Dates

- Settlement Date: October 15, 2025
- Settlement Number: 735

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The auction affects 159 securities across diverse sectors, indicating settlement failures that require auction resolution. While the number of securities is significant, most quantities are relatively small.

**Operational Impact**: Medium - Clearing members and brokers with positions in the listed securities must participate in the auction process to fulfill delivery obligations.

**Investor Impact**: Low to Medium - Individual investors may face auction charges if their brokers failed to deliver securities. The impact is localized to specific transactions rather than broad market disruption.

**Notable High-Volume Auctions**: SYLPH TECHNO (97,000), STANDARD CAP (24,980), CAPITAL TRUST (22,697), SHARANAM INF (25,765), UTKARSH SMALL (9,000), and MOTISONS JEW (8,900) represent significant quantities requiring auction settlement.
