## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in a non-convertible debenture (IML-10%-9-11-25-NCD, ISIN: INE0BUS07BB1, Code: 939889) issued by INDEL MONEY LIMITED. The suspension is effective from October 24, 2025, in connection with the company's scheduled debenture redemption and interest payment with a record date of October 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading Members are advised not to deal in the specified debenture from October 24, 2025
- The company has fixed October 25, 2025 as the record date for determining eligible holders
- The corporate action involves both redemption of debenture and payment of interest
- Notice issued under reference number DR-742/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard corporate action notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading Members must cease dealing in the specified debenture (ISIN: INE0BUS07BB1) from October 24, 2025
- Trading Members are required to take note of the suspension and communicate to relevant stakeholders
- No transactions should be executed in this security during the suspension period

## Important Dates

- **October 20, 2025**: Circular issued
- **October 24, 2025**: No dealings commence (trading suspension begins)
- **October 25, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment eligibility

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited impact as this affects only one specific debenture security of INDEL MONEY LIMITED. The suspension is temporary and related to routine corporate action procedures.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will be unable to trade this security from October 24, 2025. Only investors holding the debenture as of the October 25, 2025 record date will be eligible for the redemption proceeds and interest payment.

**Operational Impact**: Trading Members need to update their systems to block trading in this specific debenture code (939889) and ensure clients are informed of the suspension period.
