## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by TATA STEEL LIMITED on private placement basis, effective July 30, 2025. The issue comprises 10,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 500 crores, with maturity on October 27, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (10,000 units)
- Scrip Code: 729929, ISIN: INE081A14FZ1
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,92,794.50 per unit
- Credit Rating: ICRA A1+, IND A1+
- Allotment Date: July 29, 2025
- Redemption Date: October 27, 2025
- HDFC Bank Limited as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialised form under ISIN INE081A14FZ1
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise for trading
- Market lot size of 1 unit

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: July 30, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: July 29, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: October 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by an established company. The 90-day tenor and high credit rating (A1+) indicate standard short-term funding operations with low risk profile.
