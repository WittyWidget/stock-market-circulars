## Summary

Virtuoso Optoelectronics Limited (Scrip Code: 543597) has listed 31,50,000 new equity shares on BSE with effect from August 20, 2025. These shares were issued at Rs. 255.10 per share (face value Rs. 10 + premium Rs. 245.10) to promoters and non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 31,50,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 245.10/-
- Shares issued to both Promoter and Non-Promoter on preferential basis
- Securities arise from conversion of warrants
- New shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE0I0T01010
- Distinctive Numbers: 26338757 to 29488756

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities listing and permitted trading from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: March 1, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 20, 2025
- Lock-in Period Ends: February 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this represents a routine listing of shares from warrant conversion. All 31,50,000 shares are subject to lock-in until February 28, 2026, limiting immediate trading volume impact.
