## Summary

BSE has scheduled a mock trading session for the Electronic Gold Receipts (EGR) segment on Saturday, October 11th, 2025. This session is designed for trading members using third-party trading platforms, empaneled vendor systems, or in-house developed systems through ETI APIs to test their trading applications. The mock session will simulate various functionalities including exceptional market conditions, risk-reduction mode, and trading halts. No new BOLT TWS release is scheduled.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for Saturday, October 11th, 2025 for EGR segment
- No new BOLT TWS release for this session
- Session designed for testing trading applications and exceptional market scenarios
- Trades from mock session will not create any obligations, rights, or liabilities
- No margin, pay-in, or pay-out obligations will arise from mock trades
- Members requested to provide feedback by 2:00 PM on the session day
- Active participation from all members is encouraged

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine testing and familiarization exercise.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members using third-party platforms or in-house systems should participate in testing
- Members must provide feedback by 2:00 PM on October 11th, 2025
- Contact BSE Helpdesk (022-45720400/600 & 022-69158500; bsehelp@bseindia.com) or BSE Tech Support (022-22728053; bse.tech@bseindia.com) for queries

## Important Dates

- **October 11th, 2025**: Mock trading session for EGR segment
  - Log-in: 08:15 AM - 09:00 AM
  - Pre-Open Order Entry: 09:00 AM - 09:08 AM
  - Matching Period: 09:08 AM - 09:15 AM
  - Continuous Trading: 09:15 AM - 12:00 PM
  - Block Deal Window: 11:00 AM - 11:15 AM
  - Closing: 12:00 PM - 12:10 PM
  - Post-Closing: 12:10 PM - 12:20 PM
- **Feedback Deadline**: 2:00 PM on October 11th, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: None. This is a mock trading session with no real market impact.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Members have an opportunity to test their trading systems and familiarize themselves with various trading scenarios including exceptional market conditions. This helps ensure system readiness and reduces potential operational issues during actual trading.

**Member Action Required**: Participation is voluntary but encouraged. Members should utilize this session to test trading platforms, provide feedback, and ensure their systems are functioning correctly for the Electronic Gold Receipts segment.
