## Summary

BSE has published the draft offer document for Helloji Holidays Limited's SME IPO, filed on September 6, 2025. The document is available for public comments for 21 days and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations.

## Key Points

- Draft offer document filed by Helloji Holidays Limited on September 6, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for at least 21 days
- Contains all mandatory disclosures per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Public comments invited within 21-day period
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public can review and submit comments on the draft offer document within 21 days
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Company must comply with Schedule VI disclosure requirements of ICDR Regulations

## Important Dates

- Document filed: September 6, 2025
- Notice date: September 9, 2025
- Comment period: 21 days from September 9, 2025
- Comment deadline: September 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this is a standard SME IPO process notification. Affects potential investors in Helloji Holidays Limited and stakeholders interested in the SME segment. No immediate market-wide implications.
