## Summary

BSE has listed two series of commercial paper issued by Standard Chartered Capital Limited on private placement basis, effective October 28, 2025. The first series (ISIN: INE403G14TR2) consists of 1,500 units with face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each, maturing on May 29, 2026. The second series (ISIN: INE403G14TQ4) consists of 2,500 units with the same face value, maturing on June 5, 2026. Both instruments carry CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Two series of commercial paper listed on BSE Debt segment
- Total aggregate value: Rs. 200 crores (Rs. 75 crores + Rs. 125 crores)
- Allotment date: October 27, 2025
- HDFC Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent for both instruments
- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Credit ratings: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+

## Instrument Details

### Series 1 (SCCL-29-5-26-CP)
- Scrip Code: 730442
- ISIN: INE403G14TR2
- Quantity: 1,500 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,80,099
- Redemption Date: May 29, 2026

### Series 2 (SCCL-5-6-26-CP)
- Scrip Code: 730407
- ISIN: INE403G14TQ4 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Quantity: 2,500 units
- Face Value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,79,475
- Redemption Date: June 5, 2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for debt instruments.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under the specified ISIN numbers
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Market lot size is 1 unit
- Members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 27, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 28, 2025
- **Redemption Date (Series 1)**: May 29, 2026
- **Redemption Date (Series 2)**: June 5, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. The listing provides short-term debt investment opportunities for institutional investors in highly-rated commercial paper. Standard Chartered Capital Limited's strong credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+) indicate low credit risk. The announcement affects only debt market participants and has no impact on equity markets or retail investors.
