## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Navi Finserv Limited on private placement basis. The securities are denominated at Rs. 5,00,000 each with 500 units listed, effective September 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5 lakh face value each
- Quantity: 500 units
- Scrip Code: 730124
- Scrip ID: NFL290825
- ISIN Number: INE342T14EB1
- Credit Rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 476,039.50 per unit
- Allotment Date: August 29, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 26, 2026
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise applies
- Trading members can contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Listing Effective Date: September 1, 2025
- Allotment Date: August 29, 2025
- Redemption Date: February 26, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Adds to the pool of short-term debt securities available for trading on BSE debt segment.
