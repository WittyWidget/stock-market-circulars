## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Century Business Media Limited for public comments. The document was filed on September 05, 2025, and contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of the ICDR Regulations.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Century Business Media Limited on September 05, 2025
- Document available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Document contains all required disclosures per ICDR Regulations Schedule VI
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This follows standard ICDR process for SME IPO draft offer documents.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public can review and submit comments on the draft offer document within 21 days
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Company must comply with Schedule VI disclosure requirements of ICDR Regulations

## Important Dates

- Draft Offer Document Filing Date: September 05, 2025
- Notice Date: September 08, 2025
- Public Comment Deadline: September 29, 2025 (21 days from notice)
- Document Availability: Minimum 21 days on BSE SME website

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine procedural notice for an SME IPO process. Impact is limited to potential investors and stakeholders of Century Business Media Limited. The public comment period is a standard regulatory requirement with no immediate market implications.
