## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of AGRI XCHNG4 2025 issued on private placement basis. The securities carry 11.70% p.a. interest rate with monthly interest payments and mature on August 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 150,178,426 securities
- Face Value: Rs. 1.00
- Issue Price: Rs. 1.00
- Interest Rate: 11.70% p.a.
- Credit Rating: ICRA A2 (SO)
- ISIN: INE2G1D15012
- Scrip Code: 977062
- Market Lot: 100,000

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will be traded only in dematerialised form
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise
- Trading members should refer to Placement Memorandum for further details

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 26, 2025
- Interest Payment Period: August 28, 2025 to August 20, 2026 (Monthly)
- Date of Redemption: August 20, 2026
- Effective Trading Date: August 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing. The securities are available for trading on BSE Debt segment with standard trading parameters.
