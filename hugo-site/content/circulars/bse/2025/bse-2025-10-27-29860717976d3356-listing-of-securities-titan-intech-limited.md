## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 4,500,000 equity shares of TITAN INTECH LIMITED (Scrip Code: 521005) issued to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion. The shares will commence trading on October 28, 2025, with a lock-in period until July 20, 2026.

## Key Points

- 4.5 million equity shares of face value Rs. 1.00 each issued at premium of Rs. 4.50
- Issue price: Rs. 5.50 per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis pursuant to warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE807M01031
- Distinctive Numbers: 323103911 to 327603910
- Date of allotment: September 13, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of these new securities
- All 4.5 million shares are subject to lock-in restrictions
- Lock-in period must be respected until the specified expiry date

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 13, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 28, 2025 (Tuesday)
- **Lock-in Expiry**: July 20, 2026
- **Circular Date**: October 27, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market impact. The preferential allotment to non-promoters represents warrant conversion, indicating successful capital raising activity. The 9-month lock-in period provides stability by preventing immediate sale of these shares. The issue represents additional equity capital for TITAN INTECH LIMITED at Rs. 5.50 per share, which includes a premium of Rs. 4.50 over face value. Trading members should update their systems to reflect the expanded share capital and distinctive number ranges.
