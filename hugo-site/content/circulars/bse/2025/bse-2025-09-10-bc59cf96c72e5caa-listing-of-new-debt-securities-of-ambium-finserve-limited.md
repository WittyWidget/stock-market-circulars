## Summary

Ambium Finserve Limited has listed new debt securities worth ₹20 crore on BSE's Debt segment effective September 10, 2025. The securities were issued on private placement basis with an 11.16% annual interest rate and mature on December 12, 2026.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 20,000 securities with face value of ₹10,000 each
- Issue price: ₹10,000 per security (at par)
- Interest rate: 11.16% per annum, payable monthly
- Credit rating: CARE BBB-(Stable)
- Scrip code: 977092, ISIN: INE0RU307205
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing of debt securities following existing BSE procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities to be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum on BSE website for detailed information

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: September 9, 2025
- Listing effective: September 10, 2025
- Interest payment period: October 12, 2025 to December 12, 2026 (monthly)
- Redemption date: December 12, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing. Provides additional investment option for debt market participants seeking medium-term corporate bonds with monthly interest payments. The BBB- rating indicates moderate credit risk.
