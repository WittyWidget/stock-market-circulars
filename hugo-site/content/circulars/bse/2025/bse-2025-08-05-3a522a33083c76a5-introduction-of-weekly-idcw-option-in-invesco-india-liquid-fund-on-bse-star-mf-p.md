## Summary

Invesco Asset Management introduces a Weekly Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal (IDCW) payout facility for Invesco India Liquid Fund on BSE StAR MF platform, effective August 6, 2025. This adds a payout option to the existing weekly IDCW reinvestment option.

## Key Points

- Weekly IDCW payout option introduced for both existing and direct plans of Invesco India Liquid Fund
- Effective date: August 6, 2025
- Record date for weekly IDCW payout: Every Monday (or next business day if Monday is non-business day)
- Units available at applicable NAV of existing weekly IDCW reinvestment option on effective date
- IDCW Transfer plan not available under weekly IDCW option
- Default selection: Reinvestment facility if no choice specified in application

## Regulatory Changes

Necessary amendments will be made to the Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM) to reflect the introduction of the weekly IDCW payout option.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors must specify payout or reinvestment preference when applying for Weekly Dividend option
- All existing terms and conditions of SID and KIM remain unchanged except for this addition
- Standard mutual fund risk disclosures apply

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** August 6, 2025
- **Record Date:** Every Monday (or next business day)
- **Notice Date:** July 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a product enhancement for an existing liquid fund. Provides additional flexibility for investors seeking regular income distribution rather than reinvestment. No impact on stock markets or trading operations.
