## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of Bajaj Housing Finance Limited worth Rs. 250 crores (5000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) issued on private placement basis. The commercial paper carries CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ credit ratings and will mature on October 24, 2025.

## Key Points

- Issue size: Rs. 250 crores (5000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,788 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+
- ISIN: INE377Y14BM0
- Scrip code: 729906
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Central Bank of India acts as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: July 25, 2025
- Listing date: July 28, 2025
- Redemption date: October 24, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by a housing finance company. The listing provides additional short-term debt instruments for institutional investors in the debt segment.
