## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 80,000 new equity shares of Sanjivani Paranteral Ltd (Scrip Code: 531569) issued to promoters through preferential allotment pursuant to warrant conversion. The shares will commence trading on August 14, 2025.

## Key Points

- 80,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 125.10/-
- Issue price: Rs. 135.10/- per share
- Issued to promoters on preferential basis through warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE860D01013
- Distinguished numbers: 12401801 to 12481800

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability for trading
- Lock-in period compliance required for issued shares

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 18, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 14, 2025
- Lock-in Period Expires: February 17, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a small quantity of shares (80,000) issued to promoters with extended lock-in period until February 2027. The preferential allotment represents routine corporate action with no immediate trading implications due to lock-in restrictions.
