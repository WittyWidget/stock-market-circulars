## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Vivriti Capital Limited issued on private placement basis. The securities worth Rs. 175 crores (17,500 units of Rs. 100,000 each) carry an interest rate of 10.10% per annum and will mature on August 19, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 17,500 securities with face value of Rs. 100,000 each
- Scrip Code: 977042, Scrip ID: 1010VCL27
- ISIN Number: INE01HV07601
- Credit Rating: CARE A+/Stable
- Interest Rate: 10.10% per annum paid quarterly
- Date of Allotment: August 19, 2025
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities to be traded under the specified ISIN number
- Reference to placement memorandum available on BSE website for detailed terms

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 19, 2025
- Listing Date: August 21, 2025
- Interest Payment: Quarterly
- Redemption Date: August 19, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine private placement listing. The securities provide institutional investors with a medium-term debt investment option with stable credit rating and competitive interest rate.
