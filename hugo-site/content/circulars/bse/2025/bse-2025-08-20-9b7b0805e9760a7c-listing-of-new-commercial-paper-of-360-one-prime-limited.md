## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of 360 ONE Prime Limited on its Debt segment with effect from August 20, 2025. Two series of commercial papers have been issued on private placement basis with face value of Rs. 5 lakh each.

## Key Points

- Two series of Commercial Paper listed: OPL-19-11-25-CP (2000 units) and OPL-18-11-25-CP (6000 units)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit for both series
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+
- Issue date: August 19, 2025
- Redemption dates: November 19, 2025 and November 18, 2025 respectively
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Limited
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Securities will be traded under specified ISIN numbers

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: August 20, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: August 19, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**: November 18-19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides additional short-term investment options for institutional investors in the debt segment.
