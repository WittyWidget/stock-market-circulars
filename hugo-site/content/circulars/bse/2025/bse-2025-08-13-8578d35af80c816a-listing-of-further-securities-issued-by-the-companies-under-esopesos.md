## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of further securities issued by 12 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These securities will be listed and admitted for trading with effect from Thursday, August 14, 2025.

## Key Points

- 12 companies have issued additional securities under ESOP/ESOS programs
- Total of 1,271,647 shares across all companies
- All shares have face values ranging from ₹1 to ₹10
- None of the shares are under lock-in period
- Effective trading date: August 14, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for employee stock option exercises.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed of the new securities available for trading
- Companies have fulfilled listing requirements for ESOP/ESOS securities
- All securities are freely tradable with no lock-in restrictions

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: August 13, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date**: August 14, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine ESOP/ESOS listings representing employee stock option exercises. The additional shares increase the free float marginally for each company but are unlikely to significantly affect trading volumes or prices.
