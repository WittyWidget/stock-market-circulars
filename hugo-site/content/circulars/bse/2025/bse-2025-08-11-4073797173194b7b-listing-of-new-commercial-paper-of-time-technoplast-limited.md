## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities of Time Technoplast Limited worth Rs. 30 crores (600 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) issued on private placement basis, effective August 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each (600 units)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,81,434/- per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 729996, ISIN: INE508G14HX1
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- IDBI Bank Limited acts as issuing and paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 11, 2025
- Redemption date: February 11, 2026
- Trading commencement: August 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for short-term funding needs. The 6-month tenure indicates standard working capital financing.
