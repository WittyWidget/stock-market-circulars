## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Papers issued by Shree Cements Ltd. on private placement basis. The listing consists of 3000 Commercial Papers with a face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each, admitted to dealings on the BSE Debt segment effective October 10, 2025. The instruments carry dual credit ratings of CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+, with redemption scheduled for December 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- 3000 Commercial Papers listed with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 730373, Scrip ID: SCL101025
- ISIN Number: INE070A14851
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,94,481.00 per unit
- Credit Ratings: CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- HDFC Bank Ltd. acts as Issuing and Paying Agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE070A14851
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities must be traded on BSE Debt segment only
- Trading members requiring clarification should contact BSE debt department on specified phone numbers: 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date**: October 10, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: October 10, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 19, 2025
- **Circular Notice Date**: October 10, 2025
