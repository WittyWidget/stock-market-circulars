## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of SHLOKKA DYES LIMITED will be listed and admitted to dealings on the Exchange in due course. The company will be listed on the SME segment with scrip code 544582, symbol SHLOKKA, and ISIN number INE1EUB01016. The specific listing date and security details will be communicated through a separate notice.

## Key Points

- SHLOKKA DYES LIMITED approved for listing on BSE SME segment
- Scrip Code: 544582
- Trading Symbol: SHLOKKA
- ISIN: INE1EUB01016
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
- Notice issued by Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed to note the company details for future trading activities once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate notice

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal immediate market impact. The addition of SHLOKKA DYES LIMITED to the SME segment expands investment opportunities in the specialty chemicals/dyes sector. Trading members should await the subsequent notice containing the actual listing date and complete security details before commencing trading activities. The announcement provides advance notice to market participants to prepare for the new listing.
