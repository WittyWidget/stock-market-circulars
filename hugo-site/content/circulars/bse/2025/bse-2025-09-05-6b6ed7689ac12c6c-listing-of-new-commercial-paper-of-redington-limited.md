## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper issued by Redington Limited on private placement basis in the Debt segment. The instrument consists of 2000 units of Rs.5,00,000/- each, with ISIN INE891D14A38 and scrip code 730161. The Commercial Paper was allotted on September 4, 2025 and will mature on September 30, 2025. HDFC Bank Ltd serves as the Issuing and Paying Agent.

## Key Points

- Securities Description: Commercial Paper issued on private placement basis
- Face Value: Rs.5,00,000/- per unit
- Total Quantity: 2000 units
- Issue Price: Rs.4,97,907.50/- per unit
- Scrip Code: 730161
- Scrip ID: RL040925
- ISIN: INE891D14A38
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard debt instrument listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE891D14A38
- Trading must be conducted in standard denomination of Rs.5 Lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise must be observed
- For clarifications, trading members should contact BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: September 5, 2025
- Date of Allotment: September 4, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: September 5, 2025
- Date of Redemption: September 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of short-term debt instrument (26-day maturity Commercial Paper) in the BSE Debt segment. The listing affects only debt market participants and institutional investors dealing in commercial papers. The instrument carries high credit ratings (CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+), indicating low credit risk. No impact on equity markets or broader trading operations.
