## Summary

BSE has authorized trading in 1,979 new equity shares of GRASIM INDUSTRIES LTD. (Scrip Code: 500300) resulting from conversion of partly paid shares to fully paid shares of Rs. 2/- each issued on rights basis.

## Key Points

- 1,979 equity shares converted from partly paid to fully paid
- Face value: Rs. 2/- each
- ISIN: INE047A01021
- Distinctive Numbers: 680454466 to 680456444
- Shares are pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - standard listing procedure for converted shares.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are permitted to trade in these new securities with immediate effect.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: August 05, 2025
- Trading Effective Date: Wednesday, August 06, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves conversion of existing partly paid shares to fully paid status. The small number of shares (1,979) represents routine corporate action with no material effect on trading or market operations.
