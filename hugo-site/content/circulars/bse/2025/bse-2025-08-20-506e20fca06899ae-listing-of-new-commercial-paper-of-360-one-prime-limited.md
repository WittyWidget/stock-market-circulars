## Summary

BSE has listed two new commercial paper securities issued by 360 ONE Prime Limited on private placement basis, effective August 20, 2025. Both securities have face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each with CRISIL A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings.

## Key Points

- Two commercial paper issues listed on BSE Debt segment
- Security 1: 2,000 units with redemption date November 19, 2025 (Scrip Code: 730050)
- Security 2: 6,000 units with redemption date November 18, 2025 (Scrip Code: 730052)
- Both securities allotted on August 19, 2025
- HDFC Bank Limited serves as Issuing and Paying agent
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for securities is 1 paise

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: August 20, 2025
- Allotment Date: August 19, 2025
- Redemption Date 1: November 19, 2025 (ISIN: INE248U14SD4)
- Redemption Date 2: November 18, 2025 (ISIN: INE248U14SC6)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing for institutional investors. The securities provide short-term funding options for the issuer with 3-month maturity periods.
