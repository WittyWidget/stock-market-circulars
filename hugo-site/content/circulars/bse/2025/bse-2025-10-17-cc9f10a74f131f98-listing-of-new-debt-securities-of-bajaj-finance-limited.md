## Summary

BSE has listed three new debt securities issued by Bajaj Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective October 17, 2025. The securities are rated CRISIL AAA/Stable and ICRA AAA/Stable, with face values of Rs. 1,00,000 each. The issuances include tenures of 3 years (2028), 5 years (2030), and 10 years (2035) with corresponding interest rates.

## Key Points

- Three new debt securities of Bajaj Finance Limited listed on BSE Debt segment
- Total quantity: 187,500 debentures (50,000 + 62,500 + 75,000)
- All securities allotted on October 15, 2025
- Face value: Rs. 1,00,000 per debenture across all three securities
- Market lot size: 1 unit for all securities
- Credit ratings: CRISIL AAA/Stable and ICRA AAA/Stable
- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Tick size: 1 paise
- No put/call options on any of the securities

## Security Details

### Security 1: BFL-7.45%-15-10-35-PVT
- Scrip Code: 977219
- ISIN: INE296A07TR7
- Quantity: 50,000 debentures
- Interest Rate: 7.45% p.a. (annual payment)
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000
- Maturity Date: October 15, 2035 (10-year tenure)
- Interest Payment Dates: Annually from October 15, 2026 to October 15, 2035

### Security 2: BFL-7.37%-27-9-30-PVT
- Scrip Code: 977157
- ISIN: INE296A07TQ9 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Quantity: 62,500 debentures
- Interest Rate: 7.37% p.a. (annual payment)
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,781.70 (at premium)
- Maturity Date: September 27, 2030 (5-year tenure)
- Interest Payment Dates: Annually from September 27, 2026 to September 27, 2030

### Security 3: BFL-7.07%-21-9-28-PVT
- Scrip Code: 976971
- ISIN: INE296A07TN6 (Further listing under same ISIN)
- Quantity: 75,000 debentures
- Interest Rate: 7.07% p.a. (annual payment)
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,01,319.50 (at premium)
- Maturity Date: September 21, 2028 (3-year tenure)
- Interest Payment Dates: Annually from September 21, 2026 to September 21, 2028

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for privately placed debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure trading only in dematerialized form under specified ISIN numbers
- Members should refer to the Placement Memorandum available at https://www.bseindia.com/markets/debt/memorandum_data.aspx
- For clarifications, trading members may contact BSE debt department at 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 15, 2025 (all three securities)
- **Listing Effective Date**: October 17, 2025
- **First Interest Payment Dates**:
  - Security 1 (10-year): October 15, 2026
  - Security 2 (5-year): September 27, 2026
  - Security 3 (3-year): September 21, 2026
- **Redemption Dates**:
  - Security 1: October 15, 2035
  - Security 2: September 27, 2030
  - Security 3: September 21, 2028

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine debt market listing with minimal impact on broader market operations or equity investors. The listing provides additional fixed-income investment options for institutional and qualified investors in the debt market segment. The AAA credit rating reflects Bajaj Finance's strong creditworthiness. The interest rates (7.07% to 7.45%) reflect current market conditions for highly-rated corporate debt with varying tenures. No impact on equity trading operations or retail investors.
