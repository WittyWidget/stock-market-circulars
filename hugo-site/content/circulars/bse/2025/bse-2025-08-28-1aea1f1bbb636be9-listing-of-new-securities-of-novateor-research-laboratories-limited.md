## Summary

BSE announces the listing of 12,18,000 new equity shares of Novateor Research Laboratories Limited (Scrip Code: 542771) effective from Friday, August 29, 2025. These shares were issued through preferential allotment to promoters and non-promoters pursuant to conversion of warrants.

## Key Points

- 12,18,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 18/-
- Issue price: Rs. 28/- per share
- Allotment date: May 30, 2025
- Distribution numbers: 4834171 to 6052170
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE08JY01013

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification for new securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the new securities for trading purposes
- Lock-in provisions apply as specified in the circular

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: May 30, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 29, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: 
  - 1,80,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 4834171-5014170): February 28, 2027
  - 10,38,000 shares (Dist. Nos. 5014171-6052170): February 28, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Moderate impact on Novateor Research Laboratories Limited as it increases the company's tradable equity base. The staggered lock-in periods will gradually increase liquidity over the next 2-3 years. Investors should note the additional shares in circulation when assessing market capitalization and trading volumes.
