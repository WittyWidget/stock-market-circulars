## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 50,35,000 new equity shares of Sellwin Traders Limited (Scrip Code: 538875) effective from July 29, 2025. These shares were issued at Rs. 5.50 per share through preferential allotment to non-promoters following warrant conversion.

## Key Points

- 50,35,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each issued at premium of Rs. 3.50
- Issue price: Rs. 5.50 per share
- Distribution numbers: 224887501 to 229922500
- Date of allotment: July 5, 2025
- ISIN: INE195F01027
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard listing procedure for preferential allotment shares.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability for trading
- Lock-in period compliance required for all issued shares

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 5, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: July 29, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: February 1, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine equity listing following warrant conversion. The shares are subject to lock-in until February 2026, limiting immediate trading volume. Represents capital raising activity for the company through preferential route.
