## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in debt securities of CHOPPER 06 2024 due to maturity. The debentures (ISIN: INE0Z5915023) carrying 12.5% interest rate will mature on August 30, 2025, and trading will be suspended from August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- SCRIP CODE: 975825
- ISIN: INE0Z5915023
- Interest rate: 12.5%
- Maturity date: August 30, 2025
- Trading suspension effective: August 28, 2025
- Reference: DR-703/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced - this is a standard maturity-based trading suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from August 28, 2025
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension

## Important Dates

- **August 28, 2025**: Trading suspension becomes effective
- **August 30, 2025**: Maturity date of the debentures

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine suspension due to natural maturity of debt securities. Affects only holders and potential traders of this specific CHOPPER debenture issue.
