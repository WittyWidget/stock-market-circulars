## Summary

BSE has listed additional securities issued by 11 companies under their Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Scheme (ESOS) programs. These securities will be available for trading starting Friday, August 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- 11 companies have issued additional securities under ESOP/ESOS schemes
- Total of 3,522,448 new shares across all companies
- Securities will be admitted for trading on August 22, 2025
- All securities listed have no lock-in period restrictions
- Major banks HDFC Bank and ICICI Bank are among the companies

## Company Details

**Major Contributors:**
- HDFC Bank: 2,102,087 shares (Face Value: ₹1)
- ICICI Bank: 626,130 shares (Face Value: ₹2)
- Aptus Value Housing Finance: 283,472 shares (Face Value: ₹2)
- Vaibhav Global: 156,277 shares (Face Value: ₹2)
- J.B. Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals: 114,999 shares (Face Value: ₹1)

**Other Companies:**
- ICICI Prudential Life Insurance: 65,115 shares
- Genesys International Corporation: 42,050 shares
- Sai Life Sciences: 40,403 shares
- Senco Gold: 37,300 shares
- Quick Heal Technologies: 30,128 shares
- Keystone Realtors: 24,487 shares

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date:** August 21, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** August 22, 2025
- **Lock-in Period:** None (NA for all companies)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing that increases the available float for these companies. The impact is generally neutral to slightly positive as it provides liquidity from employee stock option exercises. No immediate significant market movement is expected, but it represents employee confidence in their respective companies.
