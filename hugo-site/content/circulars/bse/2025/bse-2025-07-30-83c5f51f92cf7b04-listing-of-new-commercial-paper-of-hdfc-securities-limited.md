## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper worth Rs. 200 crore issued by HDFC Securities Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective July 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 denomination each
- Total quantity: 4,000 units (Rs. 200 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,280 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- ISIN: INE700G14PF4
- Scrip code: 729928

## Trading Details

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise
- Market lot: 1
- Issuing and Paying Agent: HDFC Bank Ltd

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: July 30, 2025
- Redemption date: October 29, 2025
- Trading commencement: July 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt instrument listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding option for HDFC Securities Limited through 3-month commercial paper.
