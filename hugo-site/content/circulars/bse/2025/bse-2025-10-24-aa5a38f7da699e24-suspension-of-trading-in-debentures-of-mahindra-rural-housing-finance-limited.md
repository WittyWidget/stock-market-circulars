## Summary

BSE has suspended trading in specific non-convertible debentures of Mahindra Rural Housing Finance Limited (MRHFL) effective October 30, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing a record date for redemption of debentures and payment of interest. Trading members are advised not to deal in the specified debentures from the no-dealing date.

## Key Points

- Trading suspended for MRHFL debentures (ISIN: INE950O07412, Code: 974352)
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Record date fixed for October 30, 2025
- No dealings permitted from October 30, 2025
- Specific debenture series: MRHFL-GSEC-14-11-25-PVT
- Circular reference: DR-746/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard corporate action notification requiring temporary trading suspension.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not deal in the specified debentures from October 30, 2025
- Members must take note of the suspension and communicate to clients
- No transactions should be executed in the affected debenture security

## Important Dates

- **October 24, 2025**: Circular issue date
- **October 30, 2025**: Record date for redemption and interest payment
- **October 30, 2025**: No dealings from this date (trading suspension effective)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited impact as this affects only one specific debenture series of Mahindra Rural Housing Finance Limited. The suspension is temporary and related to scheduled corporate action.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive their redemption amount and interest payment as per the record date. Investors cannot trade these debentures from October 30, 2025 onwards.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members need to ensure their systems block trading in the specified ISIN (INE950O07412) from the effective date to prevent inadvertent trades.
