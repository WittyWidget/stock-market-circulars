## Summary

BSE has made available the Draft Offer Document of Access Point India Limited for its SME IPO, filed on September 09, 2025. The document is hosted on BSE SME website for a mandatory 21-day public comment period as per ICDR Regulations.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Document available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Public comments invited within 21 days from notice date
- Comments to be submitted to equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. Standard compliance with existing ICDR Regulations for SME IPO process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Draft Offer Document must remain available for at least 21 days
- All disclosures as per Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations included
- Public comments period mandatory before final offer document

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: September 08, 2025
- Document Filing Date: September 09, 2025
- Comment Period: 21 days from September 08, 2025
- Comment Deadline: September 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine SME IPO process with standard regulatory compliance. Limited market impact as this is preliminary stage of IPO process. Investors and market participants have opportunity to review and comment on the draft offer document before final IPO launch.
