## Summary

HDFC Securities Limited has listed new commercial paper securities on BSE's debt segment effective August 13, 2025. The issue comprises 3,600 commercial papers of Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 18 crores, issued on private placement basis.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper denomination: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- Total quantity: 3,600 units (Rs. 18 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,219.50 per unit
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, ICRA A1+
- ISIN: INE700G14PI8
- Scrip code: 730015

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard commercial paper listing procedure followed.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size of 1 paise for trading
- Trading members to contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: August 13, 2025
- Date of redemption: November 12, 2025
- Trading commencement: August 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. The 3-month maturity instrument provides short-term funding for HDFC Securities and offers investment option for institutional investors in the debt segment.
