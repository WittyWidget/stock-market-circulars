## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper worth Rs. 100 crore issued by Network 18 Media & Investments Limited on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective August 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper of Rs. 5 lakh denomination each
- Total quantity: 2000 units (Rs. 100 crore)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,776 per unit (face value Rs. 5 lakh)
- Credit rating: CARE A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip code: 729981, ISIN: INE870H14VQ8
- ICICI Bank Ltd acts as issuing and paying agent

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size of 1 paise applicable
- Trading members to contact debt department for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 7, 2025
- Redemption date: November 6, 2025
- Trading effective: August 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine commercial paper listing with minimal market impact. Provides short-term funding option for Network 18 Media & Investments Limited with 3-month tenor.
