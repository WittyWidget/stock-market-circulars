## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 3,08,000 equity shares of Himadri Speciality Chemical Ltd (Scrip Code: 500184) effective from October 17, 2025. These shares were issued at Rs. 316 per share (Re. 1 face value + Rs. 315 premium) to non-promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares are subject to lock-in periods and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 3,08,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each issued at premium of Rs. 315/-
- Issue price: Rs. 316.00 per share
- Issued to non-promoters on preferential basis
- Allotted pursuant to conversion of warrants
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE019C01026
- Distinctive Numbers: 493849500 to 494157499
- Date of Allotment: August 13, 2025
- Trading effective date: October 17, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the listing of new securities effective October 17, 2025
- Lock-in restrictions apply to all allotted shares as specified
- Shares cannot be transferred during lock-in period except as permitted by regulations

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 13, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: October 17, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (13,000 shares - Dist. 493849500-493862499)**: April 29, 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry (2,95,000 shares - Dist. 493862500-494157499)**: April 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine listing of preferential allotment shares representing a small addition to the existing equity capital. The lock-in periods ensure gradual supply into the market. Trading members should update their systems to reflect the increased share capital and lock-in restrictions. No impact on trading operations or compliance requirements beyond standard listing procedures.
