## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by HDFC Securities Limited on private placement basis. Two series of commercial paper with face value of Rs. 5,00,000 each have been admitted for trading in the BSE Debt segment effective August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- First series: 4,300 units with redemption date November 25, 2025 (ISIN: INE700G14PM0)
- Second series: 3,000 units with redemption date November 27, 2025 (ISIN: INE700G14PN8)
- Both series carry CARE A1+ and ICRA A1+ credit ratings
- HDFC Bank Ltd acts as Issuing and Paying agent for both instruments
- Trading in dematerialized form only with tick size of 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Securities will be traded under specified ISIN numbers

## Important Dates

- First series allotment date: August 26, 2025
- Second series allotment date: August 28, 2025
- First series redemption: November 25, 2025
- Second series redemption: November 27, 2025
- Trading effective from: August 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt instruments for institutional investors with 3-month maturity periods. No significant operational changes required for market participants.
