## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 49,489,845 new equity shares of Mirc Electronics Ltd (Scrip Code: 500279) issued through a rights offering. The shares will commence trading on August 8, 2025, and rank pari-passu with existing equity shares.

## Key Points

- 49,489,845 new equity shares of Rs. 1.00 each issued at Rs. 9 premium
- Rights issue basis with allotment date of August 1, 2025
- ISIN: INE831A01028
- Distinctive numbers: 306097195 to 355587039
- Trading commences August 8, 2025
- New shares have equal rights as existing shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of the new securities availability for trading.

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 1, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 8, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine listing of rights issue shares. The shares will increase the company's equity base and provide additional liquidity to existing shareholders who participated in the rights offering.
