## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities issued by Manappuram Finance Limited on a private placement basis, effective September 5, 2025. The issuance totals Rs. 400 crore (8,000 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each) with a tenor of 90 days, maturing on December 3, 2025. The securities carry credit ratings of CARE A1+ and CRISIL A1+.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Manappuram Finance Limited
- **Security Type**: Commercial Paper (private placement)
- **Total Issue Size**: Rs. 400 crore (8,000 units)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 4,91,397 per unit
- **Scrip Code**: 730154
- **Scrip ID**: MFL4925
- **Detail Name**: MFL-03-12-25-CP
- **ISIN**: INE522D14OS5
- **Credit Rating**: CARE A1+, CRISIL A1+
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Issuing and Paying Agent**: Axis Bank
- **Trading Mode**: Dematerialized form only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade the securities only in dematerialized form under ISIN INE522D14OS5
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size for the securities is 1 paise
- Clarifications may be obtained from BSE debt department on 22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: September 5, 2025
- **Listing Date**: September 5, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: September 4, 2025
- **Redemption Date**: December 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine commercial paper listing with minimal market-wide impact. The notification is relevant primarily for:
- Debt market participants trading on BSE Debt segment
- Investors in Manappuram Finance Limited debt securities
- Trading members requiring information on newly listed instruments

The short-term nature (90-day tenor) and private placement basis indicate this is a standard working capital financing instrument with limited broader market implications.
