## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper securities issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis. The listing includes 8,500 units of commercial paper worth Rs. 5 lakh each, totaling Rs. 42.5 crores, with maturity period of approximately 44 days.

## Key Points

- Commercial paper issued on private placement basis of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total quantity: 8,500 units (Rs. 42.5 crores)
- Scrip Code: 729640, Scrip ID: BFSL120625
- ISIN Number: INE01C314BX5 (further listing under same ISIN)
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 496,308.50 per unit
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall take place in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 29, 2025
- Listing Date: July 30, 2025
- Redemption Date: September 11, 2025
- Maturity Period: 44 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited. The short-term nature (44 days) and private placement basis limit broader market implications. The listing provides additional short-term funding avenue for the company.
