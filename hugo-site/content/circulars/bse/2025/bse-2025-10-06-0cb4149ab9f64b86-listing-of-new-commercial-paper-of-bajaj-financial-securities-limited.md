## Summary

BSE has listed four series of Commercial Papers issued by Bajaj Financial Securities Limited on private placement basis, effective October 6, 2025. The instruments have face value of Rs. 5 lakhs each with maturities ranging from October 27, 2025 to December 24, 2025, rated CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+.

## Key Points

- Total of 11,000 Commercial Paper units listed across four series
- All instruments issued on October 3, 2025 (allotment date)
- Face value: Rs. 5,00,000 per unit for all series
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+ and IND A1+ for all instruments
- ICICI Bank Limited acts as Issuing and Paying Agent
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Trading Requirements

- Trading permitted only in dematerialized form
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Trading on BSE Debt segment

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 3, 2025
- **Listing Date**: October 6, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**: October 27, 2025 to December 24, 2025 (varies by series)

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt market listing with no significant market impact. The listing provides short-term funding instruments for institutional investors in the debt segment. Trading members can contact BSE debt department at specified phone numbers for clarifications.
