## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of 50,00,000 new equity shares of Nazara Technologies Limited (Scrip Code: 543280) issued on a preferential basis to promoters. The shares will be available for trading from August 6, 2025.

## Key Points

- 50 lakh equity shares of Rs. 4/- each issued at premium of Rs. 986/-
- Issue price: Rs. 990/- per share
- Shares issued to promoters on preferential basis
- Distribution numbers: 87616257 to 92616256
- Date of allotment: June 16, 2025
- ISIN: INE418L01021

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members are informed about the new securities listing
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Lock-in requirements apply as specified

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** June 16, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** August 6, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends:** February 8, 2027

## Impact Assessment

The preferential issue represents a significant equity dilution with total value of approximately Rs. 495 crores. The 20-month lock-in period until February 2027 ensures promoter commitment while the preferential nature suggests strategic capital raising for business expansion.
