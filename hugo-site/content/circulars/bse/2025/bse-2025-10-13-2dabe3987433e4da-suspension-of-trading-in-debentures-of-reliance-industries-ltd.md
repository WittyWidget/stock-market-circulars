## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in specific non-convertible debentures of Reliance Industries Ltd (scrip code 952917, ISIN INE110L07047) effective October 15, 2025. The suspension is due to the company fixing the record date for interest payment and redemption of the 8.25% debentures maturing on October 30, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in these debentures from the suspension date.

## Key Points

- Debenture series: RIL-8.25%-30-10-25-PVT (INE110L07047)
- Scrip code: 952917
- No dealings permitted from: October 15, 2025
- Record date: October 15, 2025
- Purpose: Redemption of debentures and payment of interest
- Circular reference: DR-736/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational notice for corporate action processing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified debentures from October 15, 2025
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension
- Systems should be updated to block trading in scrip code 952917 from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **October 13, 2025**: Circular issued
- **October 15, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date and record date
- **October 30, 2025**: Debenture maturity date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to holders of this specific debenture series. The suspension is procedural and temporary, lasting until redemption on October 30, 2025.

**Investor Impact**: Debenture holders will receive interest payment and principal redemption. No trading or exit opportunity available from October 15, 2025 onwards.

**Operational Impact**: Trading systems must be configured to prevent transactions in this security. Standard corporate action processing for debt securities.
