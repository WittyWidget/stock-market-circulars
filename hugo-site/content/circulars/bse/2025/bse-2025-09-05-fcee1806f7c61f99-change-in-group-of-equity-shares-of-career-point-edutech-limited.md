## Summary

BSE has announced that the equity shares of Career Point Edutech Limited (Scrip Code 544499) will be reclassified and permitted for trading under Group 'B' effective Friday, September 19, 2025. This is an administrative reclassification of the stock's trading group on the exchange.

## Key Points

- Company: Career Point Edutech Limited
- Scrip Code: 544499
- New Group Classification: Group 'B'
- Effective Date: Friday, September 19, 2025
- Notice issued by Ashok Kumar Singh, Deputy Vice President, BSE

## Regulatory Changes

The stock will be reclassified from its current group to Group 'B' for trading purposes. Group classifications on BSE typically reflect factors such as market capitalization, trading volume, and compliance status. This change represents a modification in the stock's trading classification framework.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should:
- Update their systems to reflect the new group classification for scrip code 544499
- Ensure trading platforms are configured for Group 'B' parameters effective September 19, 2025
- Brief relevant trading desk personnel about the classification change

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: September 5, 2025
- **Effective Date**: Friday, September 19, 2025 (reclassification to Group 'B' takes effect)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is primarily an administrative reclassification that changes the internal grouping of the stock on BSE. Trading in the stock will continue without interruption.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Trading members need to update their internal systems and classifications but no significant operational changes are required.

**Investor Impact**: Negligible. Investors can continue to trade the stock normally, with the classification change being transparent to most market participants.
