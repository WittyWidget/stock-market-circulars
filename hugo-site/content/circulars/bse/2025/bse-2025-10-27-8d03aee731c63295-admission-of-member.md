## Summary

BSE Limited has admitted Thar Share Brokers Private Limited (Member No. 6965) as a new trading member of the exchange. The member has been granted trading rights in Cash/Equity Derivatives segments under a new membership arrangement, subject to BSE's Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations.

## Key Points

- New trading member: Thar Share Brokers Private Limited
- Member Number: 6965
- Trading segments: Cash and Equity Derivatives
- Membership type: New Membership
- Trading rights limited to BSE Limited only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine membership admission under existing BSE Rules, Bye-laws and Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from existing trading members. This is an informational notice regarding the expansion of BSE's trading member base.

## Important Dates

- Notice Date: October 27, 2025
- Notice Number: 20251027-19

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The admission of a new trading member increases market participation and liquidity potential but does not affect existing members' operations or market structure. Trading members requiring clarification may contact membership.ops@bseindia.com.
