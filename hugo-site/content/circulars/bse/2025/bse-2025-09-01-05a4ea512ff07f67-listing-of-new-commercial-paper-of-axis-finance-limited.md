## Summary

BSE has listed new Commercial Paper securities of Axis Finance Limited worth Rs. 100 crores (2000 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) issued on private placement basis. The securities are admitted for trading on BSE Debt segment effective September 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000 face value each
- Total quantity: 2000 units (Rs. 100 crores)
- Issue price: Rs. 4,92,531 per unit
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, IND A1+
- Scrip code: 730121, ISIN: INE891K14PJ5
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading must be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Axis Bank Limited serves as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 29, 2025
- Trading commencement: September 1, 2025
- Redemption date: November 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt security listing with minimal market impact. Provides additional short-term funding avenue for Axis Finance Limited through commercial paper market.
