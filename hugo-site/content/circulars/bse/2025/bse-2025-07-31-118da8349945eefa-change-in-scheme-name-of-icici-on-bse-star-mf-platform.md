## Summary

ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Limited has announced a change in the scheme name of ICICI Prudential Child Care Fund (Gift Plan) to ICICI Prudential Children's Fund, effective from August 1, 2025. This is purely an administrative change with all other provisions of the Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM) remaining unchanged.

## Key Points

- Scheme name change from "ICICI Prudential Child Care Fund (Gift Plan)" to "ICICI Prudential Children's Fund"
- Effective date: August 1, 2025
- All other terms and conditions of the scheme remain unchanged
- Notice dated July 21, 2025
- All references to the old name will be replaced with the new name in due course

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes involved - this is an administrative name change only.

## Compliance Requirements

- Asset Management Company to update all documentation with new scheme name
- Investors should note the new scheme name for future reference
- All existing scheme provisions remain valid under the new name

## Important Dates

- **August 1, 2025**: Effective date of scheme name change
- **July 21, 2025**: Date of notice issuance

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact as this is purely an administrative name change. No impact on:
- Scheme operations
- Investment objectives
- Portfolio management
- Investor rights or benefits
- NAV calculations
- Fund performance

Investors can continue with their existing investments without any action required.
