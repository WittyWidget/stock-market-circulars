## Summary

BSE has announced the filing of Draft Offer Document by Mother Nutri Foods Limited for SME IPO. The document is made available on BSE SME website for public comments for a period of 21 days as per ICDR Regulations Schedule VI requirements.

## Key Points

- Mother Nutri Foods Limited filed Draft Offer Document on September 8, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures as specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available on BSE SME website for minimum 21 days
- Public comments invited within 21-day period
- Comments to be submitted at equity.ipo@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard SME IPO filing process under existing ICDR Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public can review and submit comments on Draft Offer Document within 21 days
- Comments must be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Document hosted on www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Important Dates

- Filing Date: September 8, 2025
- Comment Period: 21 days from September 8, 2025
- Comment Deadline: September 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact for SME segment investors and market participants. Provides opportunity for public to review and comment on Mother Nutri Foods Limited's IPO proposal before final approval. Standard procedure for SME listings on BSE.
