## Summary

BSE has announced the listing of further securities issued by 8 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Option Schemes (ESOS). These new shares will be admitted for trading on the exchange effective Monday, September 1, 2025. All listed shares have no lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- 8 companies are listing additional shares under ESOP/ESOS
- Total of 8,401,470 new shares to be listed
- All shares are free from lock-in restrictions
- Trading begins September 1, 2025
- Largest issuance by Bajaj Finance Ltd with 8,195,345 shares

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine ESOP share listings. The additional shares represent employee stock option exercises and will increase the free float of the respective companies. No lock-in restrictions apply, making all shares immediately tradeable.
