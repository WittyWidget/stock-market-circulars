## Summary

Kanishk Aluminium India Limited has filed its Draft Offer Document for SME IPO on July 30, 2025. The document is available on BSE SME website for public review and comments for a period of 21 days as per ICDR Regulations Schedule VI requirements.

## Key Points

- Draft Offer Document filed by Kanishk Aluminium India Limited on July 30, 2025
- Document contains all disclosures specified under Schedule VI of ICDR Regulations
- Available for public review for minimum 21 days on stock exchange websites
- Public comments invited on the draft document
- Document hosted on BSE SME website at www.bsesme.com/PublicIssues/SMEIPODRHP.aspx

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned. This follows existing ICDR Regulations Schedule VI requirements for SME IPO draft offer documents.

## Compliance Requirements

- Public comments must be submitted within 21 days from notice date (by August 20, 2025)
- Comments to be sent to equity.ipo@bseindia.com
- Company must maintain document availability on exchange websites for minimum 21 days

## Important Dates

- Filing Date: July 30, 2025
- Comment Period: 21 days from July 30, 2025
- Comment Deadline: August 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact limited to SME segment. Affects potential investors in Kanishk Aluminium India Limited SME IPO. Standard regulatory process for SME public offerings requiring stakeholder feedback before final approval.
