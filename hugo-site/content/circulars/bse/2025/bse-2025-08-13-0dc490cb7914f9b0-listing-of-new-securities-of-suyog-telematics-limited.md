## Summary

BSE has approved the listing of 22,168 new equity shares of Suyog Telematics Limited (Scrip Code: 537259) issued to promoters on a preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants. The shares will commence trading on August 14, 2025.

## Key Points

- 22,168 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each issued at premium of Rs. 560/-
- Issue price: Rs. 570/- per share
- Allotted to promoters on preferential basis through warrant conversion
- Shares rank pari-passu with existing equity shares
- Distribution numbers: 11167453 to 11189620
- ISIN: INE442P01014

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing of shares pursuant to warrant conversion under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members informed of new securities availability for trading
- Lock-in period applies until February 14, 2027 for all 22,168 shares

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: May 5, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 14, 2025
- Lock-in Period Ends: February 14, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact due to small number of shares and lock-in restrictions. The shares are restricted from trading until February 2027, limiting immediate market supply. Represents routine corporate action for warrant conversion by promoters.
