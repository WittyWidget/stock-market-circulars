## Summary

BSE has announced that Advance Agrolife Limited (Scrip Code: 544562) will be moved from the Trade for Trade segment (T Group) to the Rolling settlement segment (B Group) effective Friday, October 24, 2025. This change follows Exchange Notice No. 20251007-45 dated October 07, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Advance Agrolife Limited
- Scrip Code: 544562
- Current Segment: Trade for Trade (T Group)
- New Segment: Rolling segment (B Group)
- Effective Date: October 24, 2025
- Contact: Mr. Parag Jain, Tel: 022 2272 8685
- Issued by: Hardik Bhuta, Assistant Vice President

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will be transferred from the Trade for Trade settlement mechanism to the Rolling settlement mechanism, indicating improved compliance or trading characteristics that meet the criteria for normal rolling settlement.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading Members should:
- Note the segment change effective October 24, 2025
- Update their systems and processes accordingly
- Contact Mr. Parag Jain for any clarifications or additional details

## Important Dates

- **Notice Date**: October 08, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 24, 2025 (Friday)
- **Reference Notice**: October 07, 2025 (Notice No. 20251007-45)

## Impact Assessment

This is a positive development for Advance Agrolife Limited and its investors. The transfer from T Group (Trade for Trade) to B Group (Rolling segment) indicates that the company has met the necessary criteria for normal trading operations. This change will:

- Increase liquidity as shares can be traded with intraday settlement
- Provide more trading flexibility to market participants
- Remove the restriction of compulsory delivery that applies in Trade for Trade segment
- Generally improve market efficiency for this security
