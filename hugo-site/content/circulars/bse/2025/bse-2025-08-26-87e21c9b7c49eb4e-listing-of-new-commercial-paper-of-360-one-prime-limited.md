## Summary

BSE announces the listing of new Commercial Paper issued by 360 ONE Prime Limited on private placement basis worth Rs. 75 crores (1500 units of Rs. 5 lakhs each) for trading on the BSE Debt segment effective August 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- Commercial Paper quantity: 1500 units of Rs. 5,00,000 each
- Total issue size: Rs. 75 crores
- Issue price: Rs. 4,91,508 per unit (discount to face value)
- Credit rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Scrip code: 730090, Scrip ID: OPL250825
- ISIN: INE248U14SF9
- Trading only in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Tick size: 1 paise
- HDFC Bank Limited appointed as Issuing and Paying agent

## Important Dates

- Allotment date: August 25, 2025
- Listing date: August 26, 2025
- Redemption date: November 24, 2025
- Tenor: 91 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine commercial paper listing. Provides additional short-term debt instrument option for institutional investors in the debt segment.
