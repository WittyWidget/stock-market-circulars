## Summary

BSE has announced that Pace Stock Broking Services Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 3013) will voluntarily cease business operations in the Commodity derivative segment effective October 29, 2025. This is a routine member exit notification.

## Key Points

- Pace Stock Broking Services Pvt. Ltd. (Member No. 3013) is closing operations
- Cessation applies to Commodity derivative segment only
- Closure is voluntary in nature
- Effective date: Wednesday, October 29, 2025
- Contact for clarifications: membership.ops@bseindia.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a member exit notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members are informed of this member's exit for their records. No specific compliance actions required from other members.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: October 29, 2025 - Pace Stock Broking Services Pvt. Ltd. ceases commodity derivatives operations

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a single member voluntary closure in the commodity derivatives segment. No disruption to market operations expected. Other trading members should be aware that this entity will no longer be active in commodity derivatives trading from the effective date.
