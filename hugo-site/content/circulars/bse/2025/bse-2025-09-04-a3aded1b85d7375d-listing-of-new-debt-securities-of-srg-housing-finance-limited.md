## Summary

BSE announces the listing of new debt securities worth ₹50 crore issued by SRG Housing Finance Limited on private placement basis, effective September 4, 2025. The securities carry an interest rate of 11.52% per annum with monthly interest payments and mature on March 1, 2031.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 5,000 securities with face value of ₹1,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 977076, ISIN: INE559N07058
- Interest Rate: 11.52% per annum payable monthly
- Credit Rating: Acuite BBB+
- Issue Price: ₹1,00,000 per security
- Date of Allotment: August 29, 2025
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade these securities only in dematerialized form
- Securities to be traded under ISIN Number INE559N07058
- Refer to Placement Memorandum available on BSE website for detailed terms

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: September 4, 2025
- Allotment Date: August 29, 2025
- Interest Payment: Monthly
- Maturity Date: March 1, 2031

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine private placement listing. The securities add to BSE's debt segment offerings but represent a relatively small issuance size. The BBB+ rating indicates moderate credit quality for investors in the debt market segment.
