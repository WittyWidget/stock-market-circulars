## Summary

BSE has listed new debt securities of Kosamattam Finance Limited (KFL) worth Rs. 50 crores issued on private placement basis, effective September 1, 2025. The securities carry 10% per annum interest rate with quarterly payments and mature on August 29, 2027.

## Key Points

- Quantity: 5,000 securities with face value of Rs. 1,00,000 each
- Scrip Code: 977065 (Scrip ID: 10KFLD27)
- ISIN: INE403Q07GF7
- Credit Rating: IND A/Stable
- Interest Rate: 10% per annum payable quarterly
- Issue Price: Rs. 1,00,000 per security
- Date of Allotment: August 29, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will trade only in dematerialized form
- Tick size is 1 paise
- Trading members can refer to Placement Memorandum for additional details
- Contact debt department (22728352/8597/8995/5753/8915) for clarifications

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: September 1, 2025
- Interest Payment: Quarterly from December 1, 2025 to August 29, 2027
- Maturity Date: August 29, 2027
- Allotment Date: August 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine debt security listing on private placement basis. The listing provides additional debt investment options for institutional investors but does not affect equity markets or broader trading operations.
