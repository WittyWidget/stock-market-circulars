## Summary

BSE has announced that trading member JAYPEE CAPITAL SERVICES LTD. (Member No. 948) will change its name to "JAYPEE CAPITAL LIMITED" effective Wednesday, July 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading member JAYPEE CAPITAL SERVICES LTD. (Member No. 948) is changing its name
- New name will be "JAYPEE CAPITAL LIMITED"
- Change is effective from Wednesday, July 30, 2025
- Contact membership.ops@bseindia.com for clarifications

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is an administrative name change only.

## Compliance Requirements

Trading members should note the name change for their records and future correspondence.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: Wednesday, July 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal impact as this is purely an administrative name change affecting one trading member. No operational changes to trading activities or market operations.
