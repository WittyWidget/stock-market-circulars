## Summary

BSE has published the trading and settlement programme for the Corporate Debt Segment (ICDM) for November 2025. The circular provides detailed settlement schedules for three settlement cycles: T+0 (same-day settlement), T+1 (next-day settlement), and T+2 (two-day settlement). The schedule covers 19 trading days from November 3, 2025 to November 28, 2025, with settlement numbers ranging from 2526144 to 2526162.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule covers period from November 1 to November 30, 2025
- Three settlement cycles provided: T+0, T+1, and T+2
- 19 settlement numbers allocated (2526144 through 2526162)
- Trading days exclude November 1, 5, 8, 9, 15, 16, 22, 23, 29, and 30 (weekends and holidays)
- Participants must check depository settlement deadline times for respective market types
- Securities must be transferred to ICCL account within specified timelines

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine operational schedule announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must adhere to the published settlement schedule
- Securities and funds pay-in must be completed by specified dates
- Participants should refer to ICCL Notice No.20091206-01 dated December 6, 2009 for detailed settlement procedures
- Participants must verify depository settlement deadline times to ensure timely transfer of securities to ICCL account

## Important Dates

**Schedule Period:** November 1-30, 2025

**First Settlement:** November 3, 2025 (Settlement No. 2526144)

**Last Settlement in November:** November 28, 2025 (Settlement No. 2526162)

**Key Settlement Dates:**
- T+0 settlements: Same-day pay-in and pay-out
- T+1 settlements: Next business day pay-in and pay-out
- T+2 settlements: Two business days later for pay-in and pay-out

**Note:** Some T+1 and T+2 settlements for end-of-month trades will spill into December (December 1-2, 2025)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal - This is a routine operational schedule that provides necessary information for participants to plan their debt segment trading and settlement activities.

**Operational Impact:** Low - The schedule follows the standard settlement framework already established for the Corporate Debt Segment. Market participants use these schedules for operational planning and ensuring timely fulfillment of settlement obligations.

**Participant Action Required:** Informational only - Participants should note the dates and ensure their systems and processes are aligned with the published schedule to avoid settlement failures or penalties.
