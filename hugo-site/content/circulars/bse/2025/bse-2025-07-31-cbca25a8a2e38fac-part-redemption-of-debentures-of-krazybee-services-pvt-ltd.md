## Summary

KRAZYBEE SERVICES PVT LTD has announced a part redemption of its debentures, resulting in a reduced face value of Rs. 62,500 per debenture. The trading will commence with the new reduced face value from August 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: KRAZYBEE SERVICES PVT LTD
- Scrip Code: KSPL-12.10%-20-10-26-PVT (INE07HK07775, 976114)
- Reduced face value: Rs. 62,500 per debenture
- Effective date: August 1, 2025
- Reference: DR-674/2025-2026

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note the reduced face value for trading purposes
- Debentures will trade at the new reduced face value from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **August 1, 2025**: Effective date for trading with reduced face value

## Impact Assessment

This is a company-specific corporate action affecting only KRAZYBEE SERVICES PVT LTD debenture holders. The part redemption reduces the face value per debenture, which may affect the trading dynamics of these specific debt securities but has minimal broader market impact.
