## Summary

BSE announces the addition of two newly listed SME companies - ABRIL PAPER TECH LIMITED (ticker 544500) and SUGS LLOYD LIMITED (ticker 544501) - to the BSE SME IPO Index effective September 8, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two companies being added to BSE SME IPO Index
- ABRIL PAPER TECH LIMITED with exchange ticker 544500
- SUGS LLOYD LIMITED with exchange ticker 544501
- Both companies listed on SME platform effective September 5, 2025
- Index addition effective at market open on September 8, 2025
- Changes included in end-of-day corporate action file (*.SDE)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - routine index maintenance activity.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Index composition changes are automatically reflected in trading systems.

## Important Dates

- September 5, 2025: SME platform listing effective date
- September 8, 2025: Index addition effective at market open

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine index maintenance activity for newly listed SME companies. The addition provides these stocks with index inclusion benefits and potential increased visibility among index-tracking investors.
