## Summary

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs has issued a Certificate of Incorporation pursuant to change of name for CHEMO PHARMA LABORATORIES LTD (CIN: L99999MH1942PLC003556). The company name has been officially changed to STARSOURCE MULTITRADE LIMITED with effect from October 1, 2025. This is an administrative corporate action that does not affect the rights and liabilities of stakeholders.

## Key Points

- Original company name: CHEMO PHARMA LABORATORIES LTD
- New company name: STARSOURCE MULTITRADE LIMITED
- Corporate Identification Number (CIN): L99999MH1942PLC003556
- Company type: Company limited by shares
- Certificate issued by: Central Processing Centre, ROC
- Approved by: M. Yadubhushana Rao, Assistant/Deputy/Registrar of Companies
- Certificate digitally signed under rule 9(2) of the Companies (Registration Offices and Fees) Rules, 2014

## Regulatory Changes

The name change has been processed pursuant to rule 29 of the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014. The certificate confirms that the company was originally incorporated as CHEMO PHARMA LABORATORIES LTD and the name change is now legally effective.

## Compliance Requirements

- The company must display the old name (CHEMO PHARMA LABORATORIES LTD) for a period of two years along with its new name (STARSOURCE MULTITRADE LIMITED) at all places where the company is required to display its name
- This requirement is mandatory under Section 12 of the Companies Act
- All stakeholders are advised to verify the latest status of the company and its directors on the Ministry website www.mca.gov.in/MCA21
- The name change does not affect existing rights and liabilities of stakeholders

## Important Dates

- **October 1, 2025**: Effective date of name change
- **October 28, 2025**: Certificate issued by ROC Central Processing Centre
- **October 1, 2027**: End of mandatory two-year period for displaying old name alongside new name

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate administrative action with minimal market impact. The name change does not alter the company's CIN, legal structure, registered address, or stakeholder rights and obligations. Trading operations will continue normally under the new name. Investors and market participants should note the new company name for all future transactions and communications. The registered office remains at 5, Kumud Apartment CHS Limited, Karnik Road Chikan Ghar, Kalyan, Thane-421301, Maharashtra.
