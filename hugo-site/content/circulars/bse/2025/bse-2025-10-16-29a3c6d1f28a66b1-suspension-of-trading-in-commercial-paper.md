## Summary

BSE has announced the suspension of trading in Commercial Paper (Scrip Code: 729888, ISIN: INE700G14PE7) issued by HDFC Securities Limited, effective from October 17, 2025. The suspension is due to the instrument's scheduled maturity on October 20, 2025. Trading members are advised not to deal in this commercial paper from the effective date.

- Commercial Paper of HDFC Securities Ltd (Scrip Code: 729888) to be suspended from trading
- ISIN: INE700G14PE7
- Maturity Date: October 20, 2025
- Trading Suspension Effective Date: October 17, 2025
- Notice Reference: DR-738/2025-2026

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard operational notice for debt instrument maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must not execute any trades in the specified Commercial Paper (Scrip Code: 729888) from October 17, 2025 onwards
- All trading members are required to take note of this suspension and ensure compliance

## Important Dates

- **October 16, 2025**: Notice Date
- **October 17, 2025**: Trading Suspension Effective Date
- **October 20, 2025**: Commercial Paper Redemption/Maturity Date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational matter with minimal market impact. The suspension affects only one specific Commercial Paper instrument issued by HDFC Securities Limited. Trading members holding positions in this instrument should note the maturity date and plan accordingly. No broader market implications or systemic concerns are associated with this notice.
