## Summary

BSE has listed new commercial paper issued by Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited worth Rs. 25 crores (5,000 units of Rs. 5 lakh each) on private placement basis. The commercial paper will be traded on BSE's debt segment with scrip code 729946.

## Key Points

- Securities: Commercial Paper of Rs. 5,00,000/- each
- Total Issue Size: Rs. 25 crores (5,000 units)
- Scrip Code: 729946
- Scrip ID: KMIL31725
- ISIN: INE975F14B75
- Credit Rating: CRISIL A1+, ICRA A1+
- Issue Price: Rs. 4,99,411/-
- Trading in dematerialized form only
- Standard denomination: Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples
- Tick size: 1 paise

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must trade securities only in dematerialized form
- Trading shall be in standard denomination of Rs. 5 lakhs and multiples thereof
- Issuing and Paying agent: Kotak Mahindra Bank Ltd

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: July 31, 2025
- Listing Date: July 31, 2025
- Redemption Date: August 7, 2025
- Tenure: 7 days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine short-term commercial paper listing with 7-day maturity. The instrument provides short-term funding for Kotak Mahindra Investments Limited and offers investment opportunity for institutional investors in highly rated (A1+) commercial paper.
