## Summary

NSE has admitted PNB Housing Finance Limited's privately placed senior bonds (Series LXX) with 7.4342% coupon rate to trading on the capital market segment. The bonds carry ISIN INE572E07233, mature on July 23, 2030, and will trade under symbol 74342PHF30 with market lot size of 1 unit effective July 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Single bond issue from PNB Housing Finance Limited being listed
- 7.4342% fixed coupon rate with 5-year maturity (July 23, 2030)
- Trading symbol: 74342PHF30 in N0 series
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- ISIN Code: INE572E07233

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. Standard listing procedure under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (Regulations 3.1.1 and 2.5.5).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading participants must use designated security code 74342PHF30 for all transactions
- Trading must comply with specified lot size of 1 unit
- Standard capital market segment trading rules apply

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: July 28, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: July 23, 2030
- **Circular Date**: July 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine single bond listing. The admission of PNB Housing Finance bonds provides additional debt instrument options for institutional investors but does not affect broader market operations or regulatory framework. Standard administrative listing with no systemic implications.
