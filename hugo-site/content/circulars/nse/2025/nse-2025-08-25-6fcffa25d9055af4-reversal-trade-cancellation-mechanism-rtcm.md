## Summary

NSE has published the updated list of securities eligible for the Reversal Trade Cancellation Mechanism (RTCM) effective September 2025. The list contains hundreds of securities across various sectors that qualify for this trade cancellation facility.

## Key Points

- Updated RTCM eligible securities list for September 2025
- Contains comprehensive list of stocks eligible for reversal trade cancellation
- Covers securities from various market segments and sectors
- List includes both large-cap and small-cap securities

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes announced - this is a routine update of eligible securities list for the existing RTCM framework.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members to note the updated list of RTCM eligible securities
- Apply RTCM procedures only for securities listed in the published list
- Ensure compliance with existing RTCM guidelines and procedures

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: September 2025 (specific date to be confirmed)
- List applicable for September 2025 trading period

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on market operations as it affects the availability of trade cancellation mechanism for the listed securities. Trading members and market participants need to be aware of which securities are eligible for RTCM to properly utilize this facility when required.
