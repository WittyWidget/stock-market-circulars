## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the suspension of trading in equity shares of Mangalore Chemicals & Fertilizers Limited (Symbol: MANGCHEFER) effective from October 31, 2025. The suspension is being implemented pursuant to Regulation 3.1.2 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A due to a Scheme of Amalgamation.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to MANGCHEFER equity shares in EQ series
- Suspension reason: Scheme of Amalgamation
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Circular reference number: NSE/CML/70979 (2083/2025)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an implementation of existing regulations for corporate restructuring scenarios.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must cease trading in MANGCHEFER equity shares from October 31, 2025
- Trading to stop at closing hours on October 30, 2025
- Members must update their systems to reflect the trading suspension

## Important Dates

- **October 27, 2025**: Circular issued
- **October 30, 2025**: Last day of trading (closes at end of trading hours)
- **October 31, 2025**: Trading suspension becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - Complete trading suspension prevents all buy/sell transactions in the security on NSE platform.

**Investor Impact**: High - Existing shareholders cannot exit positions through NSE; liquidity completely eliminated until amalgamation process concludes.

**Operational Impact**: Members must update trading systems and inform clients about the suspension. The amalgamation scheme will determine the future treatment of shares and potential conversion/exchange ratios for shareholders.
