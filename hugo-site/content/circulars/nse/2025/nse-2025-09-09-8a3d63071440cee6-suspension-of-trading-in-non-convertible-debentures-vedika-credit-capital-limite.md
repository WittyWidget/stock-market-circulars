## Summary

NSE has announced the suspension of trading in non-convertible debentures of Vedika Credit Capital Limited (Symbol: 135VCCL25, ISIN: INE04HY07096) effective September 11, 2025, due to maturity of the instrument.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to Vedika Credit Capital Limited's non-convertible debentures
- Debenture symbol: 135VCCL25
- ISIN number: INE04HY07096
- Reason for suspension: Maturity of the debentures
- Circular issued under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard operational action under existing NSE trading regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from market participants. The suspension is automatic due to instrument maturity.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 11, 2025
- **Circular Date**: September 09, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine suspension due to debenture maturity. Affects only holders and traders of this specific non-convertible debenture series. No broader market implications expected.
