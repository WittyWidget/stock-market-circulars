## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of additional equity shares issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and Employee Stock Purchase Schemes (ESPS) by 15 companies. The securities will be admitted to trading on the Capital Market segment effective August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- 15 companies are issuing additional equity shares under ESOP/ESPS schemes
- All securities will be traded with market lot size of 1
- Securities are pari passu with existing shares
- No lock-in period applicable for these shares
- Trading identification will be by designated security codes only

## Companies and Share Details

**Major Companies Include:**
- Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC Limited: 19,581 shares of Rs. 5 each
- Federal Bank Limited: Details in annexure
- ICICI Bank Limited: Details in annexure
- ICICI Lombard General Insurance: Details in annexure
- IndusInd Bank Limited: Details in annexure
- ITC Limited: Details in annexure
- Dixon Technologies: 11,750 shares of Rs. 2 each
- Borosil Limited: 1,848 shares of Re. 1 each
- Arvind Fashions Limited: 5,000 shares of Rs. 4 each
- Alivus Life Sciences Limited: 52,200 shares of Rs. 2 each

## Regulatory Framework

- Issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Lot sizes specified under Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- All shares issued are pari passu with existing equity shares

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** August 26, 2025
- **Effective Date:** August 28, 2025
- **Allotment Dates:** Various dates between August 1-20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Low to medium - Routine ESOP issuances that increase share count but typically have minimal price impact due to small quantities relative to total outstanding shares.

**Operational Impact:** Standard listing procedure with no operational changes required from market participants.
