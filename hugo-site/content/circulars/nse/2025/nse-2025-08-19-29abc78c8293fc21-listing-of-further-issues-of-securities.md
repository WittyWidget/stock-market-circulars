## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of additional equity shares for two companies effective August 20, 2025. Goldiam International Limited has issued 6,122,722 equity shares under QIP at Rs. 330 per share, while Mahindra Logistics Limited has issued 27,049,301 equity shares under Rights Issue at Rs. 277 per share.

## Key Points

- New shares will be available for trading from August 20, 2025
- Both issues were allotted on August 18, 2025
- Market lot size is 1 for both securities
- All shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- No lock-in restrictions applicable

## Company Details

### Goldiam International Limited (GOLDIAM)
- ISIN: INE025B01025
- Face Value: Rs. 2 per share
- Issue Price: Rs. 330 per share
- Securities Issued: 6,122,722 equity shares under QIP
- Distinctive Number Range: 108974616 to 115097337

### Mahindra Logistics Limited (MAHLOG)
- ISIN: INE766P01016
- Face Value: Rs. 10 per share
- Issue Price: Rs. 277 per share
- Securities Issued: 27,049,301 equity shares under Rights Issue
- Distinctive Number Range: 72131471 to 99180771

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 18, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The listing of additional shares will increase the free float and trading volumes for both companies. Goldiam International's QIP and Mahindra Logistics' rights issue represent significant capital raising exercises that may impact share prices and liquidity.
