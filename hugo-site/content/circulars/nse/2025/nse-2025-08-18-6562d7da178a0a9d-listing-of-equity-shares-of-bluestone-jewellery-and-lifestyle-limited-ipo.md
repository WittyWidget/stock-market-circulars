## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of BlueStone Jewellery and Lifestyle Limited equity shares on the Capital Market segment with effect from August 19, 2025. The company's shares will trade under the symbol BLUESTONE with ISIN INE304W01038 at an issue price of Rs. 517 per share.

## Key Points

- Trading symbol: BLUESTONE
- ISIN: INE304W01038
- Face value: Re. 1/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 517/-
- Total securities: 151,320,372 equity shares
- Market lot: 1 share
- Trading effective from: August 19, 2025
- Pre-open session applicable for IPO category

## Regulatory Changes

The security is admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Trading Regulations Part A. Lot sizes are specified under Regulation 2.5.5.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes
- Security will participate in pre-open session as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012

## Important Dates

- **August 19, 2025**: Trading commencement date
- **Lock-in periods**: Various dates ranging from September 12, 2025 to February 14, 2027 for different shareholding blocks

## Impact Assessment

This listing adds a new equity instrument to NSE trading platform, enabling public trading of BlueStone shares. The company operates in the jewellery and lifestyle sector, with registered offices in Bangalore and Mumbai. The listing provides liquidity to IPO investors and market access for new participants.
