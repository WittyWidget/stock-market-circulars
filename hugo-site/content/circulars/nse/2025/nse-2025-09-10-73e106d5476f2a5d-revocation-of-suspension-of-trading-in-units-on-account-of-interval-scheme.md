## Summary

NSE has revoked the suspension of trading in four Aditya Birla Sun Life mutual fund interval scheme units effective September 11, 2025. The revocation is based on confirmation from Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC Limited regarding continuation of the schemes post closure of STP (Specified Transaction Period).

## Key Points

- Trading suspension revoked for 4 Aditya Birla Sun Life interval scheme units
- Revocation based on AMC confirmation of scheme continuation post STP closure
- All affected units are MF series interval schemes
- Action applies to units with symbols BSLIQPIRG, BSLIFQPIDG, BSLIQPIIDP, and BSLIQPIRDP

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an administrative action reversing previous trading suspensions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members to note the revocation of trading suspension
- Normal trading operations to resume for the specified units

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 11, 2025
- **Circular Date**: September 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this affects specific mutual fund interval scheme units. Positive for unit holders as trading can resume normally. No broader market implications expected.
