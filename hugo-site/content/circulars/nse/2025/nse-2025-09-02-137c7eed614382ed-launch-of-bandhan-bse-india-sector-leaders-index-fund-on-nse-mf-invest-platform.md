## Summary

Bandhan Mutual Fund is launching its BSE India Sector Leaders Index Fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The New Fund Offer (NFO) will be open from September 3, 2025 to September 17, 2025 (11:59 PM). The fund will be available in both Regular and Direct plans with Growth options, with allotment scheduled for September 23, 2025.

## Key Points

- NFO Period: September 3-17, 2025 (closes at 11:59 PM)
- Fund Category: Equity Index Fund
- Available Plans: Regular Plan-Growth (ISIN: INF194KB1KA6) and Direct Plan-Growth (ISIN: INF194KB1KB4)
- Allotment Date: September 23, 2025
- Platform: NSE MF Invest Platform

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard new fund offering launch notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before the NFO closing date
- Standard NFO subscription procedures apply through the NSE MF Invest Platform

## Important Dates

- **September 3, 2025**: NFO opens for subscription
- **September 17, 2025**: NFO closes at 11:59 PM
- **September 23, 2025**: Allotment date for both Regular and Direct plans

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine new fund offering that provides investors with an additional investment option tracking the BSE India Sector Leaders Index. The fund will be available for regular trading on the NSE MF platform after the allotment date. No operational changes required for members beyond standard NFO processing procedures.
