## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has announced that ICICI Prudential Conglomerate Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions along with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective October 28, 2025. Six schemes under this fund (three regular and three direct plans with different dividend options) will be available for transactions.

## Key Points

- ICICI Prudential Conglomerate Fund reopening for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Subscriptions and redemptions will be available from October 28, 2025
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be enabled
- Six equity schemes available: Regular Growth, Regular IDCW Reinvestment, Regular IDCW Payout, Direct Growth, Direct IDCW Reinvestment, and Direct IDCW Payout
- All six schemes fall under the Equity category
- Circular issued by NMF TM Segment department (Reference No: 944/2025)
