## Summary

NSE announces the listing of Connplex Cinemas Limited equity shares on the EMERGE SME Platform effective August 14, 2025. The company will trade under symbol CONNPLEX in Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (TFTS) with settlement type 'W', later shifting to normal rolling segment.

## Key Points

- Security Symbol: CONNPLEX
- Trading commencement: August 14, 2025
- ISIN: INE0EAS01014
- Face Value: Rs. 10/-
- Issue Price: Rs. 177/- per share
- Number of securities: 19,100,000 shares
- Minimum quantity: 800 equity shares
- Initial trading in Series 'ST' (TFTS), later shifting to Series 'SM'
- Market maker spread within 10%
- Pre-open session applicable for IPO category

## Regulatory Changes

Shares will initially trade in Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (TFTS) with settlement type 'W' and subsequently shift to Normal Rolling Segment with settlement type 'N' as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading
- Trading must be in specified lot sizes (800 shares minimum)
- Compliance with pre-open session requirements
- Adherence to market maker spread limits (within 10%)

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: August 14, 2025
- Circular Date: August 13, 2025
- Financial Year: April 2025 - March 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact to SME segment participants and designated market makers. New listing provides investment opportunity in cinema exhibition sector. Market makers (Spread X Securities) will facilitate liquidity with specified spread limits.
