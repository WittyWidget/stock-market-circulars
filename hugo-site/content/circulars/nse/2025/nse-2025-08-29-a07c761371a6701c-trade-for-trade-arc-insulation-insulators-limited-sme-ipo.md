## Summary

NSE circular announcing the transfer of ARC Insulation & Insulators Limited (ARCIIL) securities from trade for trade segment (series: ST) to rolling segment (series: SM) effective September 12, 2025, following SEBI guidelines.

## Key Points

- ARC Insulation & Insulators Limited (Symbol: ARCIIL) will be transferred from trade for trade segment to rolling segment
- Transfer effective from September 12, 2025
- Series change from ST (trade for trade) to SM (rolling segment)
- Action taken pursuant to SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012
- References previous Exchange Circular NSE/CML/69895 dated August 28, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

Transfer of securities from restrictive trade for trade segment to more liquid rolling segment, allowing normal trading operations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements mentioned for market participants.

## Important Dates

- **September 12, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer

## Impact Assessment

Positive impact on liquidity as securities move from restrictive trade for trade segment to normal rolling segment, enabling regular trading operations for ARCIIL shares.
