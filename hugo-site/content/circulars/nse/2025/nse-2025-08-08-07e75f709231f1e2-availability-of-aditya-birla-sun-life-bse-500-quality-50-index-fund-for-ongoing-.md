## Summary

NSE announces the reopening of Aditya Birla Sun Life BSE 500 Quality 50 Index Fund for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The fund will be available for subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment facilities starting August 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- Four variants of the fund will be available: Regular IDCW, Regular Growth, Direct IDCW, and Direct Growth
- All variants are equity category funds with specific ISIN codes
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities will be available
- Fund tracks BSE 500 Quality 50 Index

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are informed about the fund availability for client transactions.

## Important Dates

- **August 11, 2025**: Effective date for fund availability on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine fund availability notification. Provides additional investment options for investors seeking exposure to quality stocks within BSE 500 index through systematic investment facilities.
