## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of further equity shares for 17 companies that were allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP). The securities will be available for trading on the Capital Market segment from September 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- 17 companies have additional equity shares listed under ESOP allocations
- All shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Market lot size is 1 for all securities
- No lock-in period applicable for these shares
- Allotment dates range from August 29, 2025 to September 4, 2025

## Companies and Share Details

- **City Union Bank Limited (CUB)**: 40,780 shares of Re. 1/- each
- **Cyient Limited (CYIENT)**: 2,106 shares of Rs. 5/- each
- **Fedbank Financial Services Limited (FEDFINA)**: 291,253 shares of Rs. 10/- each
- **Brainbees Solutions Limited (FIRSTCRY)**: 76,000 shares of Rs. 2/- each
- **Five-Star Business Finance Limited (FIVESTAR)**: 19,990 shares of Re. 1/- each
- **Godawari Power And Ispat Limited (GPIL)**: Details partially listed
- Additional companies: ICICI Prudential Life Insurance, Intellect Design Arena, Jyoti Structures, Max Healthcare Institute, NIIT Limited, Quick Heal Technologies, Star Health and Allied Insurance, Stove Kraft, Tata Elxsi, Tejas Networks, and Vardhman Textiles

## Regulatory Framework

- Issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Lot sizes specified under Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Securities identified by designated codes for trading purposes

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 11, 2025
- **Circular Date**: September 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as these are routine ESOP share listings representing employee compensation. The additional shares increase the floating stock but typically have limited immediate trading impact as they may be subject to employee holding patterns.
