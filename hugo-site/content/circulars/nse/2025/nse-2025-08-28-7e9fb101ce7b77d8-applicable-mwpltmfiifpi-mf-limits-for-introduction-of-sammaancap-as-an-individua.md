## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has established position limits for SAMMAANCAP as an individual security in the derivatives segment. The circular specifies market-wide position limits and category-wise limits for trading members, FII/FPI investors, and mutual funds.

## Key Points

- SAMMAANCAP introduced as individual security with specific position limits
- Market-wide position limit set at 163,102,628 shares
- Different limits apply to various investor categories
- Effective from August 29, 2025

## Position Limits Structure

- **Market Wide Position Limit**: 163,102,628 shares
- **Trading Member/FII/FPI Category I/MF Limit**: 32,619,800 shares
- **FPI Category II (non-individuals)**: 16,309,900 shares
- **FPI Category II (individuals/family offices/corporates)**: 8,152,800 shares

## Compliance Requirements

- All members must adhere to specified position limits
- Limits apply to different investor categories as outlined
- Trading members must monitor positions against prescribed limits

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 29, 2025 (trade date)
- **Circular Date**: August 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The introduction of SAMMAANCAP with defined position limits provides structured trading framework for institutional and retail participants. The tiered limit structure ensures balanced market participation across different investor categories while maintaining market stability.
