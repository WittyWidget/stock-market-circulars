## Summary

Capitalmind Flexi Cap Fund will be reopened for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform with effect from August 06, 2025. The fund will offer subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic facilities including SIP, STP, and SWP.

## Key Points

- Capitalmind Flexi Cap Fund reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform from August 06, 2025
- Both Direct Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF226401018) and Regular Plan - Growth (ISIN: INF226401026) available
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities included
- Both plans categorized under Equity category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are informed of the fund availability for client transactions.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 06, 2025 - Fund reopening for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

This is an operational update with minimal market impact, providing additional investment options for investors through the NSE MF Invest Platform. The reopening expands available mutual fund choices for equity investments.
