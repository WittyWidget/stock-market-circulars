## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of units of Anantam Highways Trust, an Infrastructure Investment Trust (InvIT) coming through an Initial Public Offering (IPO). The security will be listed with the symbol ANANTAM and ISIN INE1UA823019. The specific date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- New InvIT listing on NSE: Anantam Highways Trust
- Trading symbol assigned: ANANTAM
- ISIN: INE1UA823019
- Listing through IPO route
- Exact listing date to be announced separately
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/70823, dated October 15, 2025
- Circular number: 2022/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational circular notifying members about the forthcoming listing.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: October 15, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low. This is a routine listing announcement for a new InvIT security. The listing expands investment options in the infrastructure sector for investors interested in regular income through infrastructure assets.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Members are informed about the new security and its trading symbol for operational preparedness once the listing date is announced.

**Investor Impact**: Medium for infrastructure-focused investors. InvITs provide exposure to operational infrastructure assets with potential for steady cash flows and distributions.
