## Summary

NSE has issued a circular regarding the listing of equity shares of Career Point Edutech Limited pursuant to a scheme of arrangement. The circular includes detailed guidance on using the XBRL Excel Utility for filing shareholding patterns, which is a mandatory requirement for listed companies.

## Key Points

- Career Point Edutech Limited equity shares to be listed following scheme of arrangement
- XBRL Excel Utility provided for shareholding pattern e-filing
- Two-step process: XBRL/XML file generation and upload to NSE Listing Center
- Requires Microsoft Excel 2007 or above and Chrome browser for reports
- Macros must be enabled for utility functionality

## Regulatory Changes

- New equity listing adds Career Point Edutech Limited to NSE platform
- Standardized XBRL filing process for shareholding patterns continues to apply

## Compliance Requirements

- Career Point Edutech Limited must comply with all listing requirements
- Shareholders and promoters must file shareholding patterns using XBRL utility
- All mandatory fields in red fonts must be completed
- Data must be validated before XML generation
- Generated XML files must be uploaded to NSE Listing Center

## Important Dates

- Circular date: September 3, 2025
- Specific listing date not mentioned in available content

## Impact Assessment

- Medium impact limited to Career Point Edutech Limited and its stakeholders
- Provides new investment opportunity for market participants
- Technical filing requirements may require familiarization with XBRL utility for new users
- Standard compliance procedures apply to newly listed entity
