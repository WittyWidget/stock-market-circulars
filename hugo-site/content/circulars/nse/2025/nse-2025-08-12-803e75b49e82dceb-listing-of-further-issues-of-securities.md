## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further equity shares for 15 companies effective August 13, 2025. These securities are primarily equity shares allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and will be admitted to trading on the Capital Market segment with designated security codes and lot sizes.

## Key Points

- 15 companies getting further equity shares listed on NSE
- All securities effective from August 13, 2025
- Majority are ESOP allotments with small quantities
- Securities identified by designated codes for trading
- All shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- No lock-in restrictions applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (Regulations 3.1.1 and 2.5.5).

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for trading
- Trading must be conducted in specified market lot sizes
- Securities to be treated as pari passu with existing equity shares

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 13, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: Range from July 22, 2025 to August 7, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are small quantity ESOP allotments representing routine corporate actions. The securities will enhance liquidity marginally but quantities are relatively small compared to existing paid-up capital of these companies.
