## Summary

NSE has announced that Jindal Steel & Power Limited will change its name to Jindal Steel Limited effective August 29, 2025. The company will continue trading under the same symbol JINDALSTEL.

## Key Points

- Company name changing from "Jindal Steel & Power Limited" to "JINDAL STEEL LIMITED"
- Trading symbol remains unchanged: JINDALSTEL
- Change effective from August 29, 2025
- No impact on trading operations

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is purely an administrative name change for the listed entity.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Exchange members have been informed of the name change.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is an administrative change. Trading symbol and all trading operations remain unchanged. Investors should note the new company name for future reference and documentation.
