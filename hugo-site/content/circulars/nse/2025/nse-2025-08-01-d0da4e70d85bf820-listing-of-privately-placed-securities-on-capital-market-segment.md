## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed debt securities from three issuances - one from Vedika Credit Capital Limited and two from Aditya Birla Capital Limited. These securities will be admitted for trading on the capital market segment with effect from August 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Three new debt securities admitted to NSE capital market segment
- All securities have market lot size of 1
- Trading will commence from August 4, 2025
- Securities identified by designated codes for trading purposes

## Listed Securities Details

**Vedika Credit Capital Limited:**
- Symbol: 1025VCCL28
- Issue: VCCL 10.25% 2028
- Coupon Rate: 10.25%
- Maturity: July 31, 2028
- ISIN: INE04HY07294

**Aditya Birla Capital Limited (Issue 1):**
- Symbol: 72959ABC28
- Issue: ABCL 7.2959% 2028 Sr D2
- Coupon Rate: 7.3%
- Maturity: September 15, 2028
- ISIN: INE674K07069

**Aditya Birla Capital Limited (Issue 2):**
- Symbol: 74242ABC30
- Issue: ABCL 7.4242% 2030 Sr D3
- Coupon Rate: 7.42%
- Maturity: July 31, 2030
- ISIN: INE674K07077

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for identification
- All trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes (1 unit for all securities)
- Securities comply with NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** August 4, 2025
- **Circular Date:** August 1, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine administrative listing of debt securities. The addition of these privately placed securities provides additional investment options for institutional and qualified investors in the debt market segment.
