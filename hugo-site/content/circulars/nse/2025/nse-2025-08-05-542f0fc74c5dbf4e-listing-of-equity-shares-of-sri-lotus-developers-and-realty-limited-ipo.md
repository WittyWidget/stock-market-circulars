## Summary

NSE announces the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Sri Lotus Developers and Realty Limited following their Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will be listed under the symbol LOTUSDEV with ISIN INE0V9Q01010.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Sri Lotus Developers and Realty Limited
- Trading Symbol: LOTUSDEV
- ISIN: INE0V9Q01010
- Listing date to be announced through separate circular
- Standard IPO listing procedure being followed

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members are informed about the upcoming listing
- Members should prepare for trading activities once listing date is announced
- Standard listing compliance procedures apply

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: August 05, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

Low to medium impact on market operations. This is a routine IPO listing that will add one new equity instrument to NSE's trading platform. Impact limited to investors and traders interested in real estate development sector stocks.
