## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has informed members about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Midwest Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company has been assigned the trading symbol MIDWESTLTD and ISIN INE0XAD01024. The specific listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Midwest Limited
- Trading Symbol: MIDWESTLTD
- ISIN: INE0XAD01024
- Listing Date: To be announced via separate circular
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/70916, Circular No. 2063/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Exchange members are informed for awareness purposes regarding the upcoming listing.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: October 23, 2025
- **Listing Date**: To be announced separately

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. The addition of Midwest Limited to the NSE platform will provide investors with a new equity investment option. Members should note the trading symbol and ISIN for reference once the listing date is announced. No immediate operational changes are required.
