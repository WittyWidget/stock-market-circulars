## Summary

NSE has informed all members about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Glottis Limited through an Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol GLOTTIS with ISIN INE0TQE01026. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Glottis Limited
- Trading Symbol: GLOTTIS
- ISIN: INE0TQE01026
- Listing method: Initial Public Offering (IPO)
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are required to take note of the forthcoming listing and prepare their systems for trading in the new security once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: October 06, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal immediate impact on existing market operations. Members and investors interested in participating in the new listing should await further communication regarding the exact listing date. The addition of a new security expands trading options on the NSE platform.
