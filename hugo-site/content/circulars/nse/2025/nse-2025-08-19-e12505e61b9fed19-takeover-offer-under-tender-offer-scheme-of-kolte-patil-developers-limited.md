## Summary

BREP Asia III India Holding Co VII Pte. Ltd. (Acquirer) along with Blackstone Real Estate Partners Asia III L.P. (PAC 1) and Blackstone Real Estate Partners (Offshore) X.TE-F (AIV) L.P. (PAC 2) have initiated a takeover offer for Kolte-Patil Developers Limited through NSE's web-based bidding platform.

## Key Points

- Takeover offer for up to 2,30,56,825 equity shares at Rs. 329/- per share
- Fixed price offer with face value of Re. 10/- and lot size of 1 equity share
- ISIN: INE094I01018
- JM Financial Limited as Merchant Banker
- MUFG Intime India Private Limited as Registrar
- Order modification/cancellation not permitted for takeover offers

## Regulatory Changes

Follows existing Tender Offer Scheme as per NSE circular NSE/CMTR/52776 dated June 29, 2022.

## Compliance Requirements

- All Capital Market segment trading members eligible to participate
- Members can use existing web-based IPO bidding platform credentials
- New members must use admin terminal (Adminxxxxx format) to create branches and users
- Access through https://eipo.nseindia.com

## Important Dates

- Issue Period: August 22, 2025 to September 8, 2025 (excluding September 5, 2025 due to Id-E-Milad)
- Bidding Time: 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
- Custodial confirmation on closure day: 9:15 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

## Impact Assessment

Specific impact on Kolte-Patil Developers Limited shareholders with opportunity to tender shares at fixed price of Rs. 329/-. Multiple investor categories eligible including FII, Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, Banks, and individual investors.
