## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has announced the forthcoming listing of Anlon Healthcare Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol AHCL with ISIN INE0Y8W01017.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Anlon Healthcare Limited
- Trading Symbol: AHCL
- ISIN: INE0Y8W01017
- Listing Type: Initial Public Offering (IPO)
- Exchange: National Stock Exchange of India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members should note the new security listing and prepare their systems for trading in AHCL shares once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: September 2, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds a new healthcare sector equity to NSE, providing additional investment opportunities. Market impact expected to be limited to IPO participants and healthcare sector investors. Trading systems will need to be updated to accommodate the new security.
