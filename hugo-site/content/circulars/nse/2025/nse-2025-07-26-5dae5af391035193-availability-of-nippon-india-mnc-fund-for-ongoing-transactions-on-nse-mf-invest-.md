## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has announced that Nippon India MNC Fund will reopen for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective July 28, 2025. The fund will be available for subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment plans (SIP), systematic transfer plans (STP), and systematic withdrawal plans (SWP).

## Key Points

- Fund reopening effective from July 28, 2025
- All six variants of Nippon India MNC Fund will be available (Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options)
- Full transaction facilities including SIP, STP, and SWP will be operational
- All schemes fall under the Equity category
- Support available through toll-free number 1800 2100 940 and email support@nseinvest.com

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced - this is a fund availability announcement restoring previously suspended transaction facilities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure proper implementation of the fund availability on NSE MF Invest Platform
- All transaction types (subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, SWP) must be operational from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **July 28, 2025**: Effective date for fund reopening and transaction availability

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - affects only investors interested in Nippon India MNC Fund transactions through NSE platform.

**Operational Impact**: Low - routine restoration of fund transaction facilities with established infrastructure already in place.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing and potential investors seeking exposure to multinational companies through this equity fund, providing renewed access to systematic investment options.
