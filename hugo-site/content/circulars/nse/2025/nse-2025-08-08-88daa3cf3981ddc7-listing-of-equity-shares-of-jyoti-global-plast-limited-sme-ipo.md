## Summary

NSE announces the listing of equity shares of Jyoti Global Plast Limited on the EMERGE SME Platform effective August 11, 2025. The company's shares will trade under symbol JYOTIGLOBL in the Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (TFTS) with settlement type 'W', later shifting to normal rolling segment.

## Key Points

- Symbol: JYOTIGLOBL
- ISIN: INE1M3T01017
- Face Value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Issue Price: Rs. 66/- per share
- Total Securities: 19,820,000 equity shares
- Market Lot: 2,000 equity shares
- Trading starts: August 11, 2025
- Initial Series: ST (TFTS with settlement type 'W')
- Will shift to Series SM (Normal Rolling Segment)

## Regulatory Changes

- Securities will be part of pre-open session for IPO and Other category as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012
- Available for continuous market trading as per Exchange Circular NSE/CMTR/37202
- Market maker spread within 10%

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading
- Trading must be in specified lot sizes of 2,000 equity shares
- Lock-in provisions apply as per Annexure I
- Market maker: L. F. C. Securities Private Limited

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: August 08, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: August 11, 2025
- Financial Year: April 2025-March 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing provides new investment opportunity in the SME segment for plastic manufacturing sector. Limited immediate market impact due to SME platform's smaller investor base. Company operates in MIDC Area Rabale, Navi Mumbai, with registrar services by MUFG Intime India Private Limited.
