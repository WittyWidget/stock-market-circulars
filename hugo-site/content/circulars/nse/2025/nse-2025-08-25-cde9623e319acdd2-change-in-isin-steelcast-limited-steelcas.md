## Summary

NSE announces a change in ISIN code for Steelcast Limited (STEELCAS) effective August 29, 2025, due to share sub-division from Rs. 5/- to Re. 1/-. The new ISIN INE124E01038 will be effective for all trades from the ex-date.

## Key Points

- Company: Steelcast Limited (Symbol: STEELCAS)
- New ISIN: INE124E01038
- Reason: Sub-division of shares from Rs. 5/- to Re. 1/-
- Effective date: August 29, 2025
- Applies to all trades from ex-date onwards

## Regulatory Changes

ISIN code change from existing code to INE124E01038 for Steelcast Limited's equity shares following corporate action of share sub-division.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must update their systems to reflect the new ISIN code for Steelcast Limited equity shares effective August 29, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Ex-date**: August 29, 2025
- **Effective date for new ISIN**: August 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The ISIN change affects trading systems and requires technical updates by exchange members. Share sub-division typically increases liquidity by reducing share price and making shares more accessible to retail investors.
