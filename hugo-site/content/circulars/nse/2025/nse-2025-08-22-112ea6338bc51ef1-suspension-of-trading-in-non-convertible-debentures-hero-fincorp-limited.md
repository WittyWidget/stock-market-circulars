## Summary

NSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of Hero FinCorp Limited (Symbol: 894HFL25, ISIN: INE957N07781) effective August 26, 2025, due to maturity of the securities.

## Key Points

- Hero FinCorp Limited NCDs will be suspended from trading
- Suspension effective from August 26, 2025
- Reason for suspension: Maturity of the debt securities
- ISIN: INE957N07781
- Trading Symbol: 894HFL25

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a standard operational suspension due to security maturity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants must cease trading in the specified NCDs from the effective date
- Settlement of existing positions must be completed as per standard procedures

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: August 22, 2025
- **Effective Date**: August 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this affects only holders of specific Hero FinCorp NCDs reaching maturity. Standard procedure for debt securities at maturity with no broader market implications.
