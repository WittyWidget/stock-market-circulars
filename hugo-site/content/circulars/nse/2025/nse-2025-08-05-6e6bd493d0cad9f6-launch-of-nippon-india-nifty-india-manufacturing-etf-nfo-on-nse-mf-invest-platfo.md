## Summary

NSE announces the launch of Nippon India Nifty India Manufacturing ETF New Fund Offer (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The ETF will be available for transactions from August 06, 2025, to August 20, 2025, with allotment scheduled for August 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two variants available: Regular-Growth and Direct-Growth options
- Both variants share the same ISIN: INF204KC1GH6
- Classified as Equity category fund
- Transaction period: August 06-20, 2025
- Allotment date: August 26, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received in designated Clearing Corporation account on or before NFO closing date
- Funds can be received through any payment mode

## Important Dates

- NFO Start Date: August 06, 2025
- NFO End Date: August 20, 2025
- Allotment Date: August 26, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this is a routine NFO launch for a sector-specific ETF. Provides investment opportunity in India's manufacturing sector through ETF format on NSE's mutual fund platform.
