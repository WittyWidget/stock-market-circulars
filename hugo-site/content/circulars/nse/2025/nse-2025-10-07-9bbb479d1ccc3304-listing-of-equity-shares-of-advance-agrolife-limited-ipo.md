## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Advance Agrolife Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol ADVANCE with ISIN INE1B0W01010. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Advance Agrolife Limited
- Trading Symbol: ADVANCE
- ISIN: INE1B0W01010
- Listing Department: LISTING
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/70629, Circular No. 1940/2025
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange should note the new listing and prepare for trading operations once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: October 07, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced via separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This circular has limited impact on overall market operations. It primarily affects:
- Investors who participated in the Advance Agrolife Limited IPO
- Market participants who will trade the security once listed
- The company's shareholders and stakeholders

The listing adds one new security to the NSE platform in the agro-chemical/agriculture sector.
