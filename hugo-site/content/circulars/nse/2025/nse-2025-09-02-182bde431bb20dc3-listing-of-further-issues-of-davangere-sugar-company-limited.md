## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has announced the listing of 489,146,178 equity shares of Davangere Sugar Company Limited allotted under a rights issue. These shares will be admitted to trading on the NSE Capital Market segment effective September 3, 2025, at an issue price of Rs. 3.05 per share.

## Key Points

- 489,146,178 equity shares to be listed under symbol DAVANGERE
- Shares allotted under rights issue at Rs. 3.05 per share
- Face value and paid-up value: Re. 1 per share
- ISIN: INE179G01029
- Market lot size: 1 share
- Shares will be pari passu with existing equity shares
- No lock-in period applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Members should note the designated security code DAVANGERE for trading purposes. Trading shall be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 share as per NSE regulations.

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: September 1, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: September 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine listing of rights issue shares. The shares are being listed at a significant premium to face value (Rs. 3.05 vs Re. 1), indicating completed capital raising. The pari passu status ensures no differential treatment between new and existing shares.
