## Summary

NSE announces the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Highway Infrastructure Limited on the exchange. The company will trade under the symbol HILINFRA with ISIN INE00RL01028. The actual listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Highway Infrastructure Limited
- Trading Symbol: HILINFRA
- ISIN: INE00RL01028
- Listing date to be announced separately
- IPO shares to be listed on NSE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the exchange are informed about the upcoming listing and should prepare for trading activities once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: August 11, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact as this introduces a new equity security for trading on NSE. The actual market impact will depend on the company's sector performance and investor interest once trading begins.
