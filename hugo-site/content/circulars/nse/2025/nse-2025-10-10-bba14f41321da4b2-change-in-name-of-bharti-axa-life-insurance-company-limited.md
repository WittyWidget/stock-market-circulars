## Summary

NSE has notified all members that Bharti AXA Life Insurance Company Limited will undergo a name change to Bharti Life Insurance Company Limited, effective from October 15, 2025. This is an administrative update with no operational implications.

## Key Points

- Company undergoing name change: Bharti AXA Life Insurance Company Limited
- New company name: Bharti Life Insurance Company Limited
- Effective date: October 15, 2025
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/70765, Circular Ref. No: 1998/2025
- Department: Listing

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a corporate name change notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. Members are informed of the name change for their records and reference.

## Important Dates

- **October 10, 2025**: Circular issued
- **October 15, 2025**: Effective date of name change

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative notification with minimal impact. The name change does not affect:
- Trading operations
- Member obligations
- Market infrastructure
- Insurance policies or operations

Members should update their records to reflect the new company name from October 15, 2025.
