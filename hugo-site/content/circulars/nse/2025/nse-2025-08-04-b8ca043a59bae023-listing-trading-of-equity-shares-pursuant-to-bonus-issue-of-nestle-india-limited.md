## Summary

NSE has approved the listing and trading of equity shares pursuant to the bonus issue of Nestle India Limited. The new shares will be available for trading on the Capital Market segment effective August 12, 2025, with 96,41,57,160 new securities being added.

## Key Points

- Company: Nestle India Limited (Symbol: NESTLEIND)
- ISIN: INE239A01024
- Number of new securities: 96,41,57,160
- Distinctive number range: 964157161 to 1928314320
- Securities will be identified by designated codes for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

None specified - this is a standard listing approval under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for system identification
- Compliance with Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Important Dates

- Record Date: August 8, 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: August 11, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 12, 2025
- Circular Effective Date: August 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The bonus issue will nearly double the outstanding shares of Nestle India Limited, which may impact share price and liquidity. The large volume of new shares (over 96 crores) indicates a significant corporate action that will affect existing shareholders and market participants.
