## Summary

NSE has published the Mutual Fund Service System (MFSS) settlement calendar for November 2025. The calendar outlines settlement schedules for normal subscription orders (T+1 settlement), liquid fund subscriptions (T day settlement), and redemption orders for the month.

## Key Points

- Normal subscription settlements: 19 settlement cycles (2025207 to 2025225) covering November 3-28, 2025
- Liquid subscription settlements: Same 19 cycles with T day unit settlement (same-day payout)
- Settlement numbers range from 2025207 to 2025225
- Normal subscriptions follow T+1 cycle for units payout
- Liquid subscriptions have same-day funds and units settlement
- Calendar accounts for weekends with no settlements on November 1, 2, 5, 8, 9, 15, 16, 22, 23, 29, and 30

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine operational calendar.

## Compliance Requirements

Mutual fund intermediaries and participants should:
- Follow the published settlement schedule for November 2025
- Process subscription orders according to specified payin/payout dates
- Ensure timely settlement of funds and units as per calendar

## Important Dates

**Normal Subscription (T+1):**
- First settlement: November 3, 2025 (Settlement No. 2025207)
- Last settlement: November 28, 2025 (Settlement No. 2025225)
- Units payout occurs T+1 day after subscription order date
- Funds payin/payout occurs on T day

**Liquid Subscription (T day):**
- First settlement: November 3, 2025 (Settlement No. 2025207)
- Last settlement: November 28, 2025 (Settlement No. 2025225)
- Both funds and units settle on same day (T day)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational calendar with minimal market impact. It provides clarity to mutual fund distributors, RTAs, and investors on settlement timelines for November 2025. The calendar maintains consistency with T+1 settlement norms for normal subscriptions and T day settlement for liquid funds, ensuring predictable processing of mutual fund transactions.
