## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and trading of equity shares of VRL Logistics Limited pursuant to a bonus issue. The new shares will be available for trading from August 19, 2025, with a total of 8,74,68,495 bonus shares being listed.

## Key Points

- Company: VRL Logistics Limited (Symbol: VRLLOG)
- Total bonus shares: 8,74,68,495
- ISIN: INE366I01010
- Distinctive number range: 91243496 to 178711990
- Securities will be identified by designated codes for trading

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A for admission of securities to dealings.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must use the designated security codes for trading the new bonus shares on the NSE system.

## Important Dates

- Record Date: August 14, 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: August 18, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 19, 2025
- Circular Effective Date: August 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The bonus issue will increase the number of tradeable shares of VRL Logistics, potentially improving liquidity. Existing shareholders will receive additional shares without dilution of ownership percentage. Market impact expected to be moderate as this is a standard corporate action.
