## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of 14,480 additional equity shares of Party Cruisers Limited (Symbol: PARTYCRUS) allotted under Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP). The securities will be available for trading on the SME EMERGE platform starting August 6, 2025.

## Key Points

- 14,480 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each allotted under ESOP
- Securities allotted on July 15, 2025
- Trading commences August 6, 2025 on SME EMERGE platform
- Market lot size: 1000 shares
- Distinctive number range: 11926122 to 11940601
- All shares have lock-in period until September 30, 2029

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard listing procedure under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities identified by designated code PARTYCRUS on trading system
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes of 1000 shares
- Lock-in restrictions apply as per Annexure A until September 30, 2029

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: July 15, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 6, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: September 30, 2029

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a small number of ESOP shares for an SME company. The shares are subject to lock-in restrictions until 2029, limiting immediate trading volume. Standard administrative listing with no broader market implications.
