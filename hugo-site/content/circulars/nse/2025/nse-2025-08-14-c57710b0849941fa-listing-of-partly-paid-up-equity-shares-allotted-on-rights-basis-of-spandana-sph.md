## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 17,334,362 partly paid-up equity shares of Spandana Sphoorty Financial Limited allotted under rights issue. The shares will trade with symbol SSFLPP under series E1 effective August 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- 17,334,362 partly paid-up equity shares to be listed
- Face value: Rs. 10 per share, paid-up value: Rs. 5 per share
- Issue price: Rs. 230 (Rs. 115 paid on application)
- Trading symbol: SSFLPP, Series: E1
- ISIN: IN9572J01019 (temporary ISIN currently used)
- Market lot size: 1 share
- Distinctive number range: 71305145 to 88639506
- Pari passu with existing shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for identification
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes
- Shares currently credited under temporary ISIN in dematerialized mode as per SEBI circulars

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: August 12, 2025
- Effective trading date: August 18, 2025
- Circular effective date: August 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact for Spandana Sphoorty Financial Limited shareholders and traders. Increases available shares for trading and provides liquidity for rights issue participants. No lock-in restrictions apply to these shares.
