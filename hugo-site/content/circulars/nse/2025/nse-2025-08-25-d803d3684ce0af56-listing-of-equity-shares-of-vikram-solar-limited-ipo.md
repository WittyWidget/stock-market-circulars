## Summary

NSE notifies the listing of equity shares of Vikram Solar Limited on the Capital Market segment effective August 26, 2025. The company's shares will trade under symbol VIKRAMSOLR with ISIN INE078V01014, face value Rs. 10 per share, and issue price of Rs. 332 per share.

## Key Points

- Total securities listed: 361,717,031 equity shares
- Trading symbol: VIKRAMSOLR
- Market lot size: 1 share
- Face value: Rs. 10 per share
- Issue price: Rs. 332 per share
- Will participate in pre-open session for IPO category
- Lock-in periods vary by shareholder category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes
- Compliance with pre-open session requirements for IPO category

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** August 26, 2025
- **Lock-in periods:** Range from September 20, 2025 to August 21, 2028 depending on shareholder category

## Impact Assessment

Adds a new solar energy company to NSE trading platform, providing investors access to renewable energy sector. Company operates in solar panel manufacturing and installations. Limited immediate market impact as standard new listing procedure.
