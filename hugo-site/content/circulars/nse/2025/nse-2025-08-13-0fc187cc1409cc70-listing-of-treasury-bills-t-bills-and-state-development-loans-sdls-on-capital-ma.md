## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of 7 government securities on the capital market segment effective August 18, 2025. The securities include 4 State Development Loans (SDLs) from Jammu & Kashmir, Bihar, Goa, and Telangana, along with 3 Treasury Bills with varying maturities.

## Key Points

- 7 new government securities admitted for trading on NSE capital market segment
- Includes 4 SDLs with coupon rates ranging from 7.12% to 7.43%
- 3 Treasury Bills with maturities from 91 days to 364 days
- All securities will have a standard lot size of 100
- Trading will commence on August 18, 2025

## Securities Details

**State Development Loans:**
- SDL JK 7.35% 2040 (Maturity: Aug 13, 2040)
- SDL BR 7.43% 2055 (Maturity: Aug 13, 2055) 
- SDL GA 7.12% 2036 (Maturity: Aug 13, 2036)
- SDL TS 7.33% 2060 (Maturity: Aug 13, 2060)

**Treasury Bills:**
- 91-day T-bill maturing Nov 13, 2025
- 364-day T-bill maturing Aug 13, 2026
- 182-day T-bill maturing Feb 12, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** August 18, 2025
- **Circular Date:** August 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This listing expands investment opportunities in government securities for market participants, providing additional fixed-income options with varying risk profiles and maturities. The inclusion of long-term SDLs offers attractive yields for institutional investors seeking duration exposure.
