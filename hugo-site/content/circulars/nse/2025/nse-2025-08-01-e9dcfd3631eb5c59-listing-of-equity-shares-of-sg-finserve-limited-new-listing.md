## Summary

NSE announces the listing of equity shares of SG Finserve Limited with trading symbol SGFIN, effective from August 05, 2025. The company will trade in the Normal Market segment with compulsory demat requirements.

## Key Points

- Trading Symbol: SGFIN
- ISIN: INE618R01015
- Face Value: Rs. 10 per share
- Total Securities: 55,895,000 equity shares
- Market Lot Size: 1 share
- Trading Segment: Normal Market (Rolling Settlement)
- Compulsory demat for all investors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedures under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading must be conducted through designated security codes
- Compulsory demat requirement for all investors
- Trading only in specified lot sizes

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: August 01, 2025
- Effective Trading Date: August 05, 2025
- Financial Year: April 1 to March 31

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine new listing. Provides new investment opportunity in the fintech/financial services sector. Standard trading procedures apply with no special conditions or restrictions.
