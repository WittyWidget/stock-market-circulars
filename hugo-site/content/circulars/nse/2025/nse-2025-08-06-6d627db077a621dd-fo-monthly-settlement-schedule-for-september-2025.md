## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has released the settlement schedule for Futures & Options (F&O) segment for September 2025. The schedule covers 22 trading days from September 1-30, 2025, with corresponding settlement dates.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule applies to all F&O segment trades in September 2025
- 22 trading days scheduled for the month
- Settlement typically occurs T+1 (next business day)
- Weekend trades settle on following Monday
- Month-end settlement extends to October 1, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced - this is a routine operational schedule.

## Compliance Requirements

- All F&O segment members must follow the specified settlement dates
- Members should ensure adequate funds and securities for settlement obligations
- Any queries to be directed to fao_clearing_ops@nsccl.co.in

## Important Dates

- September 1, 2025: First trading day, settles September 2
- September 5, 2025: Thursday trade settles Monday September 8 (weekend)
- September 30, 2025: Last trading day of month, settles October 1, 2025
- Contact: 18002660050 (IVR Option 2) for support

## Impact Assessment

Medium operational impact for F&O traders requiring advance planning for settlement obligations. Critical for treasury and risk management teams to align liquidity planning with settlement dates.
