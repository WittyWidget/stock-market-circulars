## Summary

NSE has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of GNG Electronics Limited following their Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol EBGNG with ISIN INE18JU01028. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: GNG Electronics Limited
- Trading Symbol: EBGNG
- ISIN: INE18JU01028
- Listing follows successful IPO completion
- Exact listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes specified in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are informed about the new listing for their awareness and preparation for trading activities once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: July 29, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This listing will add a new equity option in the electronics sector to NSE's trading platform. Market participants should prepare for potential trading activity once the listing becomes effective.
