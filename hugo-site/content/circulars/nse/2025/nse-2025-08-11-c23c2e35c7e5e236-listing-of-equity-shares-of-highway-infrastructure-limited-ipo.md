## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of Highway Infrastructure Limited (symbol: HILINFRA) on the Capital Market segment effective August 12, 2025. The IPO consists of 71,720,632 equity shares of Rs. 5/- face value each, issued at Rs. 70/- per share.

## Key Points

- Company Symbol: HILINFRA
- ISIN: INE00RL01028
- Face Value: Rs. 5/- per share
- Issue Price: Rs. 70/- per share
- Total Securities: 71,720,632 equity shares
- Market Lot: 1 share
- Trading Series: Initially 'BE', later shifted to 'EQ' as per SEBI regulations

## Regulatory Changes

- Securities will be part of pre-open session for IPO and Other category scrips as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012
- Initially available in Series 'BE' and subsequently shifted to Series 'EQ'

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes
- Company must maintain registered office at 57-FA, Scheme No. 94, Pipliyahana Junction, Ring Road, Indore â€“ 452016, Madhya Pradesh

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: August 12, 2025
- Lock-in periods vary: Some shares locked until February 15, 2026, others until February 15, 2027, September 6, 2025, and November 5, 2025
- Financial Year: April 2025 - March 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds a new infrastructure company to NSE's capital market segment. With 71.7 million shares and significant lock-in arrangements, it may provide new investment opportunities in the infrastructure sector while ensuring controlled liquidity during initial trading phases.
