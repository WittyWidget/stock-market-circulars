## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has announced that the Motilal Oswal Special Opportunities Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective August 21, 2025.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Six plan variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All plans are equity category funds
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities will be available
- Each plan has distinct ISIN codes for identification

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements mentioned for members.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 21, 2025 - Fund reopens for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only investors interested in this specific mutual fund. Provides additional investment options through the NSE MF Invest Platform for equity fund investments with systematic investment facilities.
