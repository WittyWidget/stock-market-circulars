## Summary

NSE has admitted further securities of 16 companies to trading on the Capital Market segment effective September 10, 2025. The securities primarily consist of equity shares allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) with market lot size of 1 for all securities.

## Key Points

- 16 companies' further securities admitted to NSE trading
- Effective date: September 10, 2025
- All securities have market lot size of 1
- All shares are pari passu with existing shares
- No lock-in period applicable for any of the securities
- Securities identified by designated codes for trading purposes

## Companies and Security Details

- **Carborundum Universal Limited (CARBORUNIV)**: 6,388 equity shares of Re. 1/- each under ESOP
- **CG Power and Industrial Solutions Limited (CGPOWER)**: 71,980 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each under ESOP
- **E2E Networks Limited (E2E)**: 15,300 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each under ESOP
- **ICICI Bank Limited (ICICIBANK)**: 30,305 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each under ESOP
- **IndoStar Capital Finance Limited (INDOSTAR)**: 26,250 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each under ESOP
- **Infosys Limited (INFY)**: Details provided for equity shares under ESOP
- Additional companies: Kalyan Jewellers, Kiri Industries, Lancor Holdings, Manaksia Coated Metals, Precision Wires, Religare Enterprises, Tata Elxsi, Windlas Biotech, Xpro India, Zensar Technologies

## Regulatory Framework

- Issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Lot sizes specified under Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Securities to be identified only by designated codes for trading

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: September 9, 2025
- **Effective Date**: September 10, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: Various dates from August to September 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine ESOP allotments representing small quantities relative to existing share capital. The admissions facilitate employee stock option exercises and maintain market liquidity for these securities.
