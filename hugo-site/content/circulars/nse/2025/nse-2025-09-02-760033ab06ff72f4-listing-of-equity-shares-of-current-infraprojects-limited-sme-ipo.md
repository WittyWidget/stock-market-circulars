## Summary

Current Infraprojects Limited equity shares are being listed on NSE following their SME IPO. The shareholding pattern shows promoter holding of 70.50% (13,499,000 shares) and public holding of 29.50% (5,649,000 shares) out of total 19,148,000 equity shares.

## Key Points

- Total paid-up equity capital: 19,148,000 shares
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 70.50% (13,499,000 shares)
- Public shareholding: 29.50% (5,649,000 shares)
- Total number of shareholders: 821 (7 promoters, 814 public)
- 100% of promoter shares are locked-in
- 80.30% of total shares held in dematerialized form

## Regulatory Changes

None - This is a standard listing announcement following SME IPO completion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company has filed shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a)
- All mandatory disclosures regarding partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, depository receipts, and encumbrances have been provided
- Lock-in requirements applicable to promoter shareholding (100% locked)

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: September 2, 2025
- Shareholding Pattern Filing Date: September 2, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is an SME segment listing with limited liquidity. The high promoter holding (70.50%) and lock-in of all promoter shares indicates limited free float initially available for trading.
