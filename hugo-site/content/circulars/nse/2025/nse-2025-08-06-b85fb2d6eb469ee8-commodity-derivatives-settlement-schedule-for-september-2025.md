## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has released the settlement schedule for the Commodity Derivatives Segment for September 2025. The schedule provides specific trade dates and corresponding settlement dates for all trading days in September 2025.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule covers all trading days from September 1-30, 2025
- Settlement dates range from September 2, 2025 to October 1, 2025
- Standard T+1 settlement cycle applies for most days
- Weekend adjustments result in some trades settling on the next business day
- September 5th trades settle on September 8th due to weekend

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced - this is a routine operational schedule publication.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE Members must follow the specified settlement dates for commodity derivatives trades
- Members should ensure adequate liquidity and margin requirements are met on settlement dates
- Contact support@nsccl.co.in or call 18002660050 (IVR Option 2) for queries

## Important Dates

- September 1, 2025: First trade date settling September 2
- September 30, 2025: Last trade date settling October 1, 2025
- Weekend adjustments: September 5 trades settle September 8

## Impact Assessment

This schedule is crucial for commodity derivatives market participants for planning cash flows, margin requirements, and settlement obligations. Medium impact as it affects operational planning for all commodity derivatives trades in September 2025.
