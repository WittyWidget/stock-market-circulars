## Summary

NSE announces the listing of equity shares of Parth Electricals & Engineering Limited on the EMERGE SME Platform effective August 11, 2025. The company's shares will trade under symbol 'PARTH' in the Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (TFTS) with settlement type 'W', and will later be shifted to normal rolling segment.

## Key Points

- Company Symbol: PARTH
- ISIN: INE1H7V01011
- Face Value: Rs. 10 per share
- Issue Price: Rs. 170 per share
- Total Securities: 13,667,955 equity shares
- Lot Size: 800 equity shares
- Market Maker Maximum Spread: 10%
- Trading Series: ST (TFTS - Settlement Type 'W'), later shifting to SM (Normal Rolling)

## Regulatory Changes

Shares will initially trade in Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (TFTS) and subsequently be shifted to Normal Rolling Segment as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must note pre-open session applicability for IPO category
- Trading available in continuous market as per Exchange Circular NSE/CMTR/37202 dated March 14, 2018
- Securities identified only by designated codes
- Trading in specified lot sizes only

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: August 11, 2025
- Circular Effective Date: August 11, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact to broader market as this affects SME segment specifically. New listing provides investment opportunity in electrical engineering sector. Market maker arrangement ensures liquidity support with Shreni Shares Limited appointed as market maker.
