## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has admitted privately placed Commercial Papers from 8 companies to trading on its debt market segment effective September 2, 2025. The issuances total approximately Rs 1,850 crores with maturities ranging from September to December 2025.

## Key Points

- 11 Commercial Paper issuances from 8 companies listed for trading
- Total face value of Rs 1,850 crores across all issuances
- Maturities range from 23 days to 91 days
- Face value per security: Rs 5,00,000
- Issue prices range from Rs 4,91,978 to Rs 4,98,054 per security

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. Standard listing under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements. Standard debt market trading regulations apply.

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: September 2, 2025
- Allotment Dates: September 1-2, 2025
- Redemption Dates:
  - September 25, 2025: Kotak Securities
  - October 28, 2025: CEAT Limited
  - November 25, 2025: Godrej Industries
  - November 28, 2025: Ramco Cements, CEAT, Tata Steel
  - December 1, 2025: Godrej Properties, Alembic Pharma, Ramco Cements
  - December 2, 2025: Axis Securities

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as these are routine debt market listings. Provides additional short-term investment options for institutional investors in the debt segment. The CPs offer yields in the range of 7.5-8% annually based on discounted issue prices.
