## Summary

ICICI Prudential Active Momentum Fund will be available for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform starting July 29, 2025. The fund offers both Regular and Direct Growth options with full systematic transaction facilities.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening effective July 29, 2025
- Available for subscriptions and redemptions
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities included
- Two scheme variants: Regular Growth (INF109K1A377) and Direct Growth (INF109K1A385)
- Both schemes categorized as Equity funds
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a platform availability announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

Members can facilitate transactions in the specified fund schemes through NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: July 29, 2025 - Fund becomes available for all transaction types

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine fund availability announcement that provides investors with additional investment options on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The reopening allows for diversified equity fund access with systematic investment facilities.
