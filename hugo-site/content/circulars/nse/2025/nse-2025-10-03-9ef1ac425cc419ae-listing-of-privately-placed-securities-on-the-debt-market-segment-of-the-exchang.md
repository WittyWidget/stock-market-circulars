## Summary

NSE has admitted various privately placed securities to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective October 03, 2025. The circular includes fresh issues of commercial paper from SMFG India Credit Company Limited (2 series), Indian Oil Corporation Limited, and HDFC Securities Limited, along with debentures from Toyota Financial Services India Limited, IIFL Finance Limited, and Trust Investment Advisors Private Limited. Re-issues include commercial paper from ICICI Securities Limited and debentures from LIC Housing Finance Limited.

## Key Points

- Total of 7 fresh issue securities listed (4 commercial papers, 3 debentures)
- Total of 2 re-issue securities listed (1 commercial paper, 1 debenture)
- All securities admitted to trading effective October 03, 2025
- Securities issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations
- Issuers include major financial institutions and corporations

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members may trade these securities on the debt market segment using designated security codes
- Securities are subject to NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations
- All listed securities follow private placement norms

## Important Dates

- **Effective Trading Date**: October 03, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: September 30, 2025 to October 03, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**: Range from October 24, 2025 to September 28, 2026 (commercial papers) and December 15, 2027 to February 28, 2029 (debentures)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification that enables trading of privately placed debt securities on NSE's debt market segment. The impact is limited to debt market participants, institutional investors, and the specific issuers. There is no direct impact on equity markets or retail investors. The listing provides liquidity options for holders of these privately placed instruments.
