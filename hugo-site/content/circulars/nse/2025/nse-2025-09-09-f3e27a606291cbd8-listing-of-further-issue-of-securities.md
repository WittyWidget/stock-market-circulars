## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of 1,640,000 additional equity shares of Pattech Fitwell Tube Components Limited on the SME EMERGE platform, effective September 10, 2025. These shares were allotted under a preferential issue at Rs. 77.5 per share on August 13, 2025.

## Key Points

- 1,640,000 equity shares of face value Rs. 10 each to be listed
- Issue price: Rs. 77.5 per share
- Trading symbol: PATTECH
- Market lot size: 1,500 shares
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- ISIN: INE0NZW01014 (temporary ISIN currently assigned)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. Standard listing procedure for additional equity shares under preferential issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members to use designated security codes for trading
- Trading to be conducted in specified lot sizes of 1,500 shares
- Shares currently credited under temporary ISIN as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012

## Important Dates

- Allotment Date: August 13, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: September 10, 2025
- Lock-in Expiry Date: March 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a small SME company listing additional shares under preferential issue. The lock-in period until March 2026 limits immediate trading impact. Routine administrative listing with no broader market implications.
