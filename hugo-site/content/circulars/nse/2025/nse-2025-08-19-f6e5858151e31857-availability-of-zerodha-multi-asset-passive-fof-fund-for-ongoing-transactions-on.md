## Summary

NSE announces that the Zerodha Multi Asset Passive FOF Fund will be available for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform starting August 20, 2025. The fund will support subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment plans.

## Key Points

- Zerodha Multi Asset Passive FOF Fund reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Supports subscriptions and redemptions
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities included
- Fund category: Equity
- ISIN: INF0R8F01117 (Direct Growth option)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is an operational update about fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements mentioned for members beyond standard mutual fund transaction procedures.

## Important Dates

- **August 20, 2025**: Effective date for fund availability on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine operational announcement about mutual fund availability. Provides additional investment options for retail investors through the NSE MF Invest Platform with systematic investment facilities.
