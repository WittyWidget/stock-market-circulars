## Summary

NSE announces the launch of Angel One Gold ETF New Fund Offer (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be available for transactions from August 20, 2025, to September 02, 2025, with allotment scheduled for September 05, 2025.

## Key Points

- Angel One Gold ETF NFO launching on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Two plan options available: Direct plan and Regular plan, both with Growth option
- Both plans categorized under Debt segment
- Single ISIN (INF1J2R01114) for both plans
- Clear funds must be received in designated Clearing Corporation account by NFO closing date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before NFO closing date (September 02, 2025)
- Transactions must be conducted through NSE MF Invest Platform

## Important Dates

- NFO Opening Date: August 20, 2025
- NFO Closing Date: September 02, 2025
- Allotment Date: September 05, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine NFO launch. Provides additional investment option for investors seeking gold exposure through ETF format. No impact on existing securities or trading operations.
