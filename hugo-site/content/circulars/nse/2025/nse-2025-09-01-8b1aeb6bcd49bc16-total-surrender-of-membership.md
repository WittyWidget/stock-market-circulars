## Summary

NSE has approved the total surrender of membership for STAR BULLS DERIVATIVE TRADING LLP (SEBI Registration: INZ000161752). The surrender was approved on August 22, 2025, with the member's last trade date being April 9, 2021.

## Key Points

- Trading member: STAR BULLS DERIVATIVE TRADING LLP
- SEBI Registration Number: INZ000161752
- Last trade date: April 9, 2021
- Surrender approval date: August 22, 2025
- All members are requested to take note of this surrender

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative notification of membership surrender.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are required to take note of this membership surrender.

## Important Dates

- Last Trade Date: April 9, 2021
- Surrender Approval Date: August 22, 2025
- Circular Date: September 1, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is an administrative matter regarding a single trading member who had already ceased trading activities in 2021. The surrender formalizes the end of an inactive membership.
