## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of further issues of securities for nine companies effective August 04, 2025. The securities include equity shares allotted under ESOP schemes and conversion of warrants on preferential basis. All securities will trade with designated codes and specified lot sizes.

## Key Points

- Securities admitted to NSE Capital Market segment with effect from August 04, 2025
- Includes equity shares from ESOP allotments and warrant conversions
- All securities will be pari passu with existing shares
- Market lot size of 1 for all listed securities
- Various lock-in periods apply based on allotment type

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - standard listing procedure under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for system trading
- Securities must be traded in specified lot sizes
- Lock-in restrictions must be observed where applicable

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 04, 2025
- **Circular Date**: August 01, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry Dates**: Vary by company (ranging from August 20, 2025 to February 15, 2026)

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on affected companies as additional shares become available for trading, potentially affecting liquidity and share price dynamics. Standard corporate action with no systemic market implications.
