## Summary

NSE announces the listing of three privately placed debt securities issued by Torrent Investments Limited on the capital market segment, effective from August 21, 2025. The securities include bonds with coupon rates of 7.90%, 7.96%, and 8.03% maturing between 2028-2030.

## Key Points

- Three debt securities of Torrent Investments Limited admitted to NSE trading
- All securities have market lot size of 1
- Trading to commence from August 21, 2025
- Securities identified by designated codes: 790TIL28, 796TIL29, 803TIL30

## Securities Details

- **TIL 7.90% 2028 Sr 1**: Symbol 790TIL28, ISIN INE939L08013, Maturity 19-Aug-2028
- **TIL 7.96% 2029 Sr 2**: Symbol 796TIL29, ISIN INE939L08021, Maturity 19-Aug-2029  
- **TIL 8.03% 2030 Sr 3**: Symbol 803TIL30, ISIN INE939L08039, Maturity 19-Aug-2030

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading permitted only using designated security codes
- All trades must be in specified lot sizes as per annexure
- Securities subject to NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 21, 2025
- **Circular Date**: August 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves routine listing of privately placed debt securities. Provides additional investment options for debt market participants interested in Torrent Investments Limited paper with varying maturity profiles and competitive coupon rates.
