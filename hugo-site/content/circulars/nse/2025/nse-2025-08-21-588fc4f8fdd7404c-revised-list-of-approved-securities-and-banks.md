## Summary

NSE has issued a revised list of approved securities and banks, specifically updating Annexure 1 which contains 40 equity shares that form part of the non-cash component of liquid assets. The list includes major blue-chip stocks with specified quantity limits and VAR-based haircut requirements.

## Key Points

- 40 equity shares approved for non-cash component of liquid assets
- VAR-based haircut applicable for all listed securities
- Maximum 25% limit per security of total margins for members
- Overall permitted quantities specified across all segments
- FO segment shows N.A for memberwise permitted quantities
- List includes major stocks like RELIANCE, HDFCBANK, ICICIBANK, INFY, ITC

## Regulatory Changes

- Updated approved securities list with current quantity limits
- Maintained VAR-based haircut methodology
- Continued 25% maximum exposure limit per security

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must adhere to specified quantity limits for each approved security
- Maximum 25% of total margins can be in any single security
- VAR-based haircuts must be applied to all approved securities
- Members must comply with overall permitted quantities across all segments

## Important Dates

- Circular dated: August 21, 2025
- Implementation: Immediate effect (date not specified)

## Impact Assessment

- Provides clarity on eligible securities for margin purposes
- Ensures diversification through 25% limit per security
- Maintains liquidity requirements through approved blue-chip stocks
- No significant operational changes as this appears to be a routine update
- Affects risk management and collateral planning for trading members
