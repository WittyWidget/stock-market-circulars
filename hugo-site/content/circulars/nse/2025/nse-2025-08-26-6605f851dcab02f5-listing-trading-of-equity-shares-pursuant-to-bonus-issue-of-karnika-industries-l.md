## Summary

NSE has announced the listing and trading of bonus equity shares of Karnika Industries Limited (KARNIKA) effective from September 02, 2025. The bonus shares have been admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment.

## Key Points

- Company: Karnika Industries Limited (Symbol: KARNIKA)
- ISIN: INE0MGA01012
- Number of bonus securities: 4,95,98,000 shares
- Distinctive number range: 12399501 to 61997500
- Trading effective date: September 02, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The securities have been admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities shall be identified only by their designated codes for trading purposes
- All members must note the new security details for trading operations

## Important Dates

- Record Date: August 29, 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: September 01, 2025
- Trading Commencement: September 02, 2025
- Circular Effective Date: September 02, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The bonus issue will increase the total number of tradeable shares of Karnika Industries Limited by 4,95,98,000 shares. This is a routine corporate action that will dilute the share price proportionally while maintaining shareholders' overall value. Trading systems and market participants need to update their records with the new share count and distinctive number ranges.
