## Summary

NSE has announced the adjustment of market lot for KARNIKA INDUSTRIES LIMITED (Symbol: KARNIKA) in the SME platform following a 4:1 bonus issue. The adjusted market lot will be 1000 shares, effective from August 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: KARNIKA INDUSTRIES LIMITED (Symbol: KARNIKA)
- Corporate Action: Bonus issue with 4:1 ratio
- Adjustment Factor: 5
- New Market Lot: 1000 shares
- Platform: SME (Small and Medium Enterprises)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard market lot adjustment procedure following corporate action.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must load updated security files (security.gz/nnf_security.gz/MII-Security File.gz) in trading applications before trading on effective date
- Files available from Extranet server directory: common/NTNEAT
- MII-Security File.gz also available on NSE website at https://www.nseindia.com/all-reports

## Important Dates

- Ex-date: August 29, 2025
- Effective Date: August 29, 2025
- Record Date: August 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited impact as this affects only one SME platform stock. Members trading KARNIKA must ensure their systems are updated with new market lot specifications to avoid trading disruptions. The 5x adjustment factor reflects the 4:1 bonus ratio implementation.
