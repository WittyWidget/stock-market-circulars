## Summary

Bandhan Multi-Factor Fund of Bandhan Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions along with SIP, STP, SWP facility on NSE MF Invest Platform effective August 01, 2025.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening applies to all six plan variants (Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options)
- All schemes are categorized as Equity funds
- Full transactional facilities including SIP, STP, and SWP will be available
- Effective date is August 01, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is an operational announcement regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

Members need to be aware of the fund's availability for client transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Important Dates

- **August 01, 2025**: Effective date for fund reopening

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact - this is a routine operational matter affecting the availability of one mutual fund scheme for transactions on NSE's platform.
