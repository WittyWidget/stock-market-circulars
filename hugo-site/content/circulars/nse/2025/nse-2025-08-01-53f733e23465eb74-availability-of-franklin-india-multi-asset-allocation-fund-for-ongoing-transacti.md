## Summary

NSE has announced that the Franklin India Multi Asset Allocation Fund of Franklin Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective August 04, 2025.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening includes all six variants: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes are categorized under Equity category
- Facility includes systematic investment plans (SIP), systematic transfer plans (STP), and systematic withdrawal plans (SWP)
- Each plan has unique ISIN codes for identification

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine fund reopening announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

Members can facilitate transactions in the specified fund schemes through the NSE MF Invest Platform starting from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 04, 2025 - Fund reopening for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine fund availability announcement. Provides additional investment options for investors seeking multi-asset allocation strategies through NSE's mutual fund platform.
