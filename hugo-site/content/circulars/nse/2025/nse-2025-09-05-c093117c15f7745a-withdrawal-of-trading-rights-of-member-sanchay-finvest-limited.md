## Summary

NSE has suspended the trading rights of Sanchay Finvest Limited (SEBI Registration Number: INZ000289036) for one day on September 08, 2025, across all segments due to non-adherence to regulatory provisions.

## Key Points

- Member: Sanchay Finvest Limited
- SEBI Registration: INZ000289036
- Suspension duration: One day (September 08, 2025)
- Affected segments: Capital Market and Futures & Options
- Reason: Non-adherence to regulatory provisions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an enforcement action under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and investors must take note of the suspension
- Trading activities of the suspended member will be restricted during the suspension period

## Important Dates

- **September 08, 2025**: One-day suspension effective date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only one trading member for a single day. Demonstrates NSE's continued regulatory oversight and enforcement of compliance requirements among trading members.
