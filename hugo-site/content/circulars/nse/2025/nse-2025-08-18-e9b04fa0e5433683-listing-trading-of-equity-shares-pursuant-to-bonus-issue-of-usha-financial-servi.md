## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and trading of equity shares pursuant to bonus issue of Usha Financial Services Limited (USHAFIN) effective from August 21, 2025. The company will issue 2,17,37,631 new equity shares with ISIN INE0LS001014.

## Key Points

- New equity shares admitted to NSE Capital Market segment
- Total bonus shares: 2,17,37,631
- Distinctive number range: 21737632 to 43475262
- Trading symbol: USHAFIN
- Securities identified by designated codes for system trading

## Regulatory Changes

Admission of additional securities under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading
- Standard NSE trading regulations apply to the new shares

## Important Dates

- Record Date: August 19, 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: August 20, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 21, 2025
- Circular Effective Date: August 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Moderate impact limited to USHAFIN shareholders and traders. Increased share count will affect per-share metrics and may influence trading volumes. Standard bonus issue procedure with no systemic market implications.
