## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that Godha Cabcon & Insulation Limited will undergo a corporate name and trading symbol change effective October 17, 2025. The company will be renamed to Auri Grow India Limited, and its trading symbol will change from GODHA to AURIGROW.

## Key Points

- Existing company name: Godha Cabcon & Insulation Limited
- New company name: Auri Grow India Limited
- Existing trading symbol: GODHA
- New trading symbol: AURIGROW
- Effective date: October 17, 2025
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/70780, Circular No. 2007/2025

## Regulatory Changes

This is a corporate action involving a legal name change and corresponding trading symbol update. The change has been approved and will be implemented in NSE's trading systems. No regulatory framework changes are involved.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their systems to reflect the new company name and symbol
- Trading terminals and backend systems should be updated before October 17, 2025
- Investors should note the symbol change for order placement and portfolio tracking
- Market participants must use the new symbol AURIGROW for all trades from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **October 13, 2025**: Circular issued
- **October 17, 2025**: Name and symbol change becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The symbol change affects trading operations, order routing systems, and investor portfolios. While the underlying security remains the same, all market participants must update their systems and records.

**Operational Impact**: Exchange members need to update trading systems, risk management systems, and client communication. Investors holding positions in GODHA will see their holdings automatically reflected under the new symbol AURIGROW.

**Investor Impact**: Shareholders should be aware of the name and symbol change for accurate portfolio tracking. Any pending orders with the old symbol must be cancelled and re-entered with the new symbol after October 17, 2025.
