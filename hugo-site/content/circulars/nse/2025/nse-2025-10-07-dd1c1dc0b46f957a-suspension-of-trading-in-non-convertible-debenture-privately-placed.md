## Summary

NSE has announced the suspension of trading in a Non-Convertible Debenture (Privately Placed) issued by LIC Housing Finance Limited. The suspension is effective from October 08, 2025, and is due to the redemption of the debenture. This action is taken in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Single NCD with ISIN INE115A07IF7 to be suspended from trading
- Issuer: LIC Housing Finance Limited
- Suspension effective date: October 08, 2025
- Reason: Redemption of the debenture
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/70655, Circular Ref. No: 1947/2025
- Regulatory basis: Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine suspension action under existing regulations for debt securities reaching redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must note the suspension of trading in the specified NCD
- No trading permitted in ISIN INE115A07IF7 from October 08, 2025 onwards
- Members should update their trading systems to reflect the suspension

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: October 07, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: October 08, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The impact is limited to holders of this specific privately placed NCD. This is a routine corporate action with no broader market implications. The suspension is procedural, following the scheduled redemption of the debenture. Investors holding this security will receive redemption proceeds as per the original terms of issuance. No impact on LIC Housing Finance Limited's equity shares or other debt securities.
