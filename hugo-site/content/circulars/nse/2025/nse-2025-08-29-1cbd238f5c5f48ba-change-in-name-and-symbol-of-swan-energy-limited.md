## Summary

NSE has announced that Swan Energy Limited will undergo a name and symbol change effective September 4, 2025. The company's name will change from Swan Energy Limited to SWAN CORP LIMITED, and its trading symbol will change from SWANENERGY to SWANCORP.

## Key Points

- Company name changing from Swan Energy Limited to SWAN CORP LIMITED
- Trading symbol changing from SWANENERGY to SWANCORP
- Changes effective from September 4, 2025
- All exchange members have been notified of the change

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate name and symbol change process.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their systems to reflect the new name and symbol
- Trading systems must be updated by the effective date
- Market participants should note the symbol change for trading purposes

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 4, 2025
- **Circular Date**: August 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine corporate name and symbol change. Traders and investors should update their records and systems to reflect the new symbol SWANCORP when trading the company's shares from September 4, 2025 onwards.
