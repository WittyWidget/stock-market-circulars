## Summary

NSE has announced the launch of Kotak Nifty Chemicals ETF New Fund Offering (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be open for subscription from October 23, 2025 to November 06, 2025, with both Direct and Regular plan options available under the same ISIN. The allotment date is scheduled for November 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- NFO subscription period: October 23, 2025 to November 06, 2025
- Two plan options available: Direct Plan and Regular Plan
- Both plans share the same ISIN: INF174KA1XV0
- Category: Equity ETF
- Allotment date: November 12, 2025
- Clear funds must be received by November 11, 2025 till 12:00 PM
- Funds to be credited to designated Clearing Corporation account

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received through any payment mode in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before November 11, 2025 till 12:00 PM
- Funds acceptance deadline is one day before allotment date

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: October 23, 2025
- **NFO End Date**: November 06, 2025
- **Fund Payment Deadline**: November 11, 2025 (12:00 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: November 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This circular has minimal market-wide impact as it announces a routine NFO launch. The impact is primarily operational for NSE members and mutual fund distributors who will facilitate subscriptions through the NSE MF Invest Platform. For investors, it provides a new investment avenue in the chemicals sector through an index-tracking ETF. No changes to existing trading rules or compliance requirements are introduced.
