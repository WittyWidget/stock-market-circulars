## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced that Barbeque Nation Hospitality Limited will undergo a name and trading symbol change effective October 13, 2025. The company will be renamed to United Foodbrands Limited, and its trading symbol will change from BARBEQUE to UFBL.

## Key Points

- Existing symbol: BARBEQUE
- New symbol: UFBL
- Existing name: Barbeque Nation Hospitality Limited
- New name: United Foodbrands Limited
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/70657, 1949/2025
- Department: Listing

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action involving renaming and symbol modification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their systems to reflect the new company name and trading symbol
- All trading systems and records should be updated before the effective date
- Members should inform their clients about the name and symbol change

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** October 07, 2025
- **Effective Date:** October 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Traders and investors need to be aware of the symbol change to avoid confusion. Historical data and charts will need to be referenced under the old symbol for periods before October 13, 2025.

**Operational Impact:** Medium - Trading members must update their systems, databases, and client communications. Automated trading systems and watchlists need to be reconfigured with the new symbol UFBL.
