## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Regaal Resources Limited following their Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol REGAAL with ISIN INE0MHO01029.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Regaal Resources Limited
- Trading Symbol: REGAAL
- ISIN: INE0MHO01029
- Listing type: IPO (Initial Public Offering)
- Exchange: National Stock Exchange of India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange are informed about the new listing. No specific compliance actions required at this time.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: August 19, 2025
- Listing Date: To be informed through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard new listing announcement that will add Regaal Resources Limited to NSE's equity segment. The actual listing date will be communicated separately, allowing market participants to prepare for trading in the new security.
