## Summary

NSE has received communication from SBI Mutual Fund regarding a revised NFO allotment date for the Magnum Hybrid Long Short Fund under Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) category. The allotment date has been advanced by 3 days from the previously announced date.

## Key Points

- NFO allotment date revised from October 23, 2025 to October 20, 2025
- Applies to Magnum Hybrid Long Short Fund NFO on NSE MF Invest Platform
- SIP/XSIP registrations must be authenticated by clients before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date
- Clear funds through any payment mode must be received in designated Clearing Corporation account on or before October 20, 2025
- Circular issued by NMF TM Segment department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update regarding NFO timeline modification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure SIP/XSIP registration authentication is completed by clients before 3:00 PM on NFO closure date
- Members must ensure clear funds are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before October 20, 2025
- Members should communicate the revised allotment date to their clients

## Important Dates

- **Previous Allotment Date**: October 23, 2025
- **Revised Allotment Date**: October 20, 2025
- **SIP/XSIP Authentication Deadline**: 3:00 PM on NFO closure date
- **Funds Receipt Deadline**: On or before October 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: The 3-day advancement of the allotment date requires members to expedite fund collection and processing activities. This affects operational timelines for brokers and distributors handling NFO applications.

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a scheme-specific timeline change affecting only investors and intermediaries participating in this particular NFO.

**Investor Impact**: Investors interested in this NFO will receive allotment earlier than originally scheduled, which may be beneficial for those seeking faster deployment of funds.
