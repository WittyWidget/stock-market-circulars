## Summary

NSE has approved the segmental surrender of membership by Pace Stock Broking Services Private Limited (SEBI registration: INZ000180832) for the Currency derivatives including IRF segment, effective July 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Member: Pace Stock Broking Services Private Limited
- SEBI Registration: INZ000180832
- Surrendered Segment: Currency derivatives including IRF
- Approval Date: July 29, 2025
- All members notified of the change

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a routine membership surrender approval.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are requested to take note of this membership change
- No specific compliance actions required from other members

## Important Dates

- **July 29, 2025**: Surrender approval date for Currency derivatives including IRF segment
- **July 31, 2025**: Circular notification date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves surrender of membership by a single trading member in the currency derivatives segment. Other members should update their records regarding the member's trading status in the affected segment.
