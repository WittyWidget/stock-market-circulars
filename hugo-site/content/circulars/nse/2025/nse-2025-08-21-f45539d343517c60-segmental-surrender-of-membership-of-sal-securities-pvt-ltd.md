## Summary

NSE has approved the request of Sal Securities Pvt Ltd (SEBI registration: INZ000287438) to surrender its membership in the Currency Derivatives segment including IRF (Interest Rate Futures), effective August 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading member: Sal Securities Pvt Ltd
- SEBI registration number: INZ000287438
- Surrendered segment: Currency Derivatives including IRF
- Approval date: August 19, 2025
- All NSE members notified of the change

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is an administrative approval of a voluntary membership surrender.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are requested to take note of this membership change
- No specific compliance actions required from other members

## Important Dates

- **August 19, 2025**: Surrender approval date
- **August 21, 2025**: Circular notification date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves one trading member voluntarily surrendering membership in a specific segment. Other members and market operations remain unaffected.
