## Summary

NSE announces the availability of UTI Annual Interval Fund-I on NSE MF Invest Platform for a specified transaction period from September 09, 2025 to September 10, 2025. The fund offers 6 scheme variants across Direct and Regular plans with IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, and Growth options.

## Key Points

- UTI Annual Interval Fund-I available for transactions on September 09-10, 2025
- Transaction requests (subscription/redemption) accepted during normal business hours
- NAV applicable as per cut-off timing based on receipt of transaction
- 6 scheme variants available: 3 Direct Plans and 3 Regular Plans
- All schemes categorized as Debt funds
- Each plan has unique ISIN codes

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine fund availability notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must follow NSE MF Invest platform procedures
- Transactions subject to scheme cut-off timings
- Fund confirmation must be uploaded/received for NAV applicability

## Important Dates

- **September 09, 2025**: Transaction period begins
- **September 10, 2025**: Transaction period ends
- Transaction window: Normal business hours on both days

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine mutual fund offering announcement. Provides investment opportunity for interval fund seekers during the specified two-day window. No impact on stock markets or trading operations.
