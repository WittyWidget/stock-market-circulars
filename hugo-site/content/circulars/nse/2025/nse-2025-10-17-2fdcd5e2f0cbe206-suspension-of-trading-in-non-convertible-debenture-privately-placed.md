## Summary

NSE has announced the suspension of trading in one series of Non-Convertible Debentures (Privately Placed) issued by Tata Capital Housing Finance Limited. The suspension is effective from October 20, 2025, and is being implemented due to the redemption of the debentures. This action is taken in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- One NCD series of Tata Capital Housing Finance Limited to be suspended from trading
- ISIN affected: INE033L07EC5
- Suspension effective date: October 20, 2025
- Reason: Redemption of debentures
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine operational measure taken pursuant to existing regulations governing debt market trading.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must note the suspension and cease trading in the specified ISIN from the effective date
- Members should update their trading systems to reflect the suspension
- Investors holding these debentures should be informed about the redemption and trading suspension

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: October 17, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: October 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The impact of this circular is minimal and localized to holders of this specific NCD series. The suspension is due to normal redemption of the debentures, which is a scheduled corporate action. Trading members need to ensure their systems are updated to prevent trades in this ISIN from the effective date. This is a routine administrative action with no broader market implications.
