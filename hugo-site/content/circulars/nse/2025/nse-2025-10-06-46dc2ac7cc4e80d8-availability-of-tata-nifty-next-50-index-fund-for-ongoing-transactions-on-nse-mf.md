## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that the Tata Nifty Next 50 Index Fund will be reopened for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective October 07, 2025. This includes subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment facilities (SIP, STP, SWP) across all plan variants of the scheme.

## Key Points

- Tata Nifty Next 50 Index Fund reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Six scheme variants covered: Regular and Direct plans with IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout, and Growth options
- All schemes fall under Equity category
- Facilities include subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Circular reference: NSE/NMFTM/70625, Circular No. 898/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding the reopening of a previously closed mutual fund scheme for transactions.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational circular notifying members about the availability of the fund for transactions on the platform.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: October 06, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 07, 2025 - Reopening of Tata Nifty Next 50 Index Fund for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine operational update affecting only investors interested in the Tata Nifty Next 50 Index Fund.

**Operational Impact**: Low. Members are informed about the availability of the fund for transactions. Investors can now resume subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment facilities through the NSE MF Invest Platform.

**Investor Impact**: Positive for existing and prospective investors of this fund who can now execute transactions and set up systematic investment plans.
