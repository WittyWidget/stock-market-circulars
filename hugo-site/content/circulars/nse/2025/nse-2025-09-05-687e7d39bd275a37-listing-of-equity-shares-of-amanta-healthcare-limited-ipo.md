## Summary

NSE announces the forthcoming listing of Amanta Healthcare Limited's equity shares following their Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol AMANTA with ISIN INE084K01015. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Amanta Healthcare Limited
- Trading Symbol: AMANTA
- ISIN: INE084K01015
- Listing date to be announced separately
- IPO shares ready for market trading

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are informed about the new listing and should prepare their systems for trading the new security once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: September 05, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

The listing of Amanta Healthcare Limited will provide investors with a new healthcare sector investment opportunity. Market participants should monitor the separate circular for the exact listing date to prepare for trading activities.
