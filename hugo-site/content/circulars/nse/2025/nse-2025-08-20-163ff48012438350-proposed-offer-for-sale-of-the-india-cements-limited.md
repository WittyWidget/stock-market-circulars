## Summary

NSE announces the proposed Offer for Sale (OFS) of The India Cements Limited shares scheduled for August 21-22, 2025. The OFS will offer up to 2,01,12,330 equity shares of face value Re 10/- each at a floor price of Rs. 368 per share through the eOFS platform.

## Key Points

- OFS dates: August 21, 2025 and August 22, 2025
- Floor price: Rs. 368 per equity share
- Issue size: Up to 2,01,12,330 equity shares of face value Re 10/- each
- Symbol: INDIACEM (ISIN: INE383A01012)
- Market lot: 1 equity share
- Tick size: Re. 0.05
- Two categories: Non-retail (IS series) and Retail (RS series for bids â‰¤ Rs 2 lacs)
- eOFS platform URL: https://eofs.nseindia.com
- Bidding session timing: 09:15 hrs to 15:30 hrs on both days
- Carry forward option available for unallocated non-retail bids from Day 1 to Day 2

## Regulatory Changes

Implementation follows the Interoperability among Clearing Corporations along with Exchange Scheme circular NSE/CMTR/62927 dated July 12, 2024.

## Compliance Requirements

- All eligible Capital Market segment trading members can participate
- Members without Admin user IDs must request through ENIT portal (https://enit.nseindia.com/MemberPortal/)
- Admin user requests processed at 11:00 AM, 2:00 PM, and 3:00 PM intervals
- Compliant UCC of self (PRO) and clients must be updated in UCI system one day prior to OFS bidding
- UCCs identified as compliant at 22:00 hrs on previous day are permitted to trade next day
- Members must ensure sufficient collaterals before carry forward orders on T+1 day
- Rejected orders due to insufficient margins cannot be placed again

## Important Dates

- August 20, 2025: Circular date
- August 21, 2025: First OFS bidding session (09:15-15:30 hrs)
- August 22, 2025: Second OFS bidding session and carry forward day (09:15-15:30 hrs)
- Prior day requirement: UCC compliance update needed day before bidding

## Impact Assessment

This OFS provides liquidity opportunity for India Cements Limited shares and allows both retail and non-retail investors to participate. The two-day structure with carry forward mechanism ensures adequate participation opportunity. Trading members need to ensure technical readiness and compliance requirements are met to participate effectively.
