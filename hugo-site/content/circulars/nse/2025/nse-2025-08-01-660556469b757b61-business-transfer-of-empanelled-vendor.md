## Summary

NSE has informed market participants about the business transfer of empanelled vendor services from M/s 63 Moons Technologies Limited to M/s Synapsewave Innovations Private Limited. The vendor provides Non-Neat Front end services to trading members.

## Key Points

- Business transfer from 63 Moons Technologies Limited to Synapsewave Innovations Private Limited
- Vendor provides Non-Neat Front end services to trading members
- Company name change reflects the business transfer
- New contact details and address provided for correspondence

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is an administrative update regarding vendor business transfer.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants should note the change in vendor name and contact details
- Update records to reflect new vendor entity name: Synapsewave Innovations Private Limited

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: Immediate effect from August 01, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal operational impact as this is an administrative change in vendor ownership. Trading members using Non-Neat Front end services should update their vendor contact information but no service disruption is expected.
