## Summary

Bhadora Industries Limited (BHADORA) will be transferred from the trade for trade segment (series: ST) to the rolling segment (series: SM) with effect from August 26, 2025. This follows SEBI guidelines and previous Exchange Circular NSE/CML/69592 dated August 08, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Bhadora Industries Limited
- Symbol: BHADORA
- Transfer from: Trade for Trade segment (Series: ST)
- Transfer to: Rolling segment (Series: SM)
- Authority: SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012

## Regulatory Changes

The stock will move from restricted trading (trade for trade) to normal rolling settlement, allowing for more flexible trading arrangements.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must adjust their trading systems and processes to accommodate the segment change for BHADORA stock.

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: August 26, 2025
- Reference Circular Date: August 08, 2025 (NSE/CML/69592)

## Impact Assessment

This change will improve liquidity and trading flexibility for BHADORA stock by moving it from the restrictive trade for trade segment to normal rolling settlement.
