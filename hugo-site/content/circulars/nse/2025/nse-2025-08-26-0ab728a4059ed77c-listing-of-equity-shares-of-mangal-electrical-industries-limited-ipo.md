## Summary

Mangal Electrical Industries Limited has filed its post-IPO shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. The company has successfully completed its IPO with equity shares now listed and trading.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 27,630,124 shares
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 20,500,000 shares (74.19%)
- Public holding: 7,130,124 shares (25.81%)
- Number of public shareholders: 98,954
- Dematerialized shares: 22,639,020 shares (81.94%)
- Locked-in shares: 2,139,020 shares (29.9998% of public holding)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard post-IPO compliance filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Filed under Reg. 31(1)(a) of SEBI LODR Regulations
- Company has declared no partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, or depository receipts
- No shares are pledged or under non-disposal undertaking
- No differential voting rights or significant beneficial owners

## Important Dates

- Filing date: August 26, 2025
- Post-issue shareholding pattern effective from IPO completion

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds a new equity option for investors in the electrical industries sector. With 74.19% promoter holding, the company maintains strong promoter control while providing 25.81% public float for market liquidity. The high dematerialization rate of 81.94% indicates good market readiness for electronic trading.
