## Summary

NSE announces the listing of Cash Ur Drive Marketing Limited (Symbol: CUDML) equity shares on the EMERGE SME Platform effective August 07, 2025. The company's shares will initially trade in the Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (TFTS) with settlement type 'W' before moving to normal rolling segment.

## Key Points

- Company Symbol: CUDML
- ISIN: INE0WL201014
- Face Value: Rs. 10 per share
- Issue Price: Rs. 130 per share
- Total Securities: 17,645,800 equity shares
- Market Lot Size: 1,000 equity shares
- Market Maker Spread: Within 10%

## Trading Details

- Initial Series: ST (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment - Settlement Type 'W')
- Future Series: SM (Normal Rolling Segment - Settlement Type 'N')
- Available for pre-open session for IPO category
- Continuous market trading available
- Pari Passu: Yes
- Lock-in details as per Annexure I
