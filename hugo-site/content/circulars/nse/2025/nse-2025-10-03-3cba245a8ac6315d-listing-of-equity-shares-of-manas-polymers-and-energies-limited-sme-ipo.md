## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of Manas Polymers and Energies Limited (Symbol: MPEL) on the EMERGE SME Platform effective October 06, 2025. The company issued 7,776,000 equity shares at Rs. 81/- per share with a face value of Rs. 10/-. The security will initially trade in Series 'ST' (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment) and subsequently be shifted to Series 'SM' (Normal Rolling Segment).

## Key Points

- **Symbol**: MPEL
- **ISIN**: INE0U4H01011
- **Total Shares**: 7,776,000 equity shares
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10/-
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 81/- per share
- **Market Lot**: 1,600 equity shares
- **Trading Series**: ST (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment - Settlement Type 'W'), later shifting to SM
- **Market Maker**: Shreni Shares Limited
- **Market Maker Spread**: Within 10%
- **Listing Date**: October 06, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular follows existing regulations:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- SEBI circular no. CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012
- Exchange Circular No. NSE/CMTR/37202 dated March 14, 2018

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security code for trading
- Trading must be in specified lot size of 1,600 equity shares
- Security will participate in pre-open session for IPO and Other category scrips
- Security available for trading in continuous market
- Lock-in requirements as per Annexure I apply

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: October 03, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: October 06, 2025
- **Company Financial Year**: April 2025 - March 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - This is a routine SME IPO listing with no impact on existing market participants.

**Operational Impact**: Low - Standard listing procedures apply. The stock will be available for trading on the EMERGE SME platform with specified market maker arrangements.
