## Summary

NSE has admitted multiple privately placed commercial papers to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective August 29, 2025. The listing includes commercial papers from 10 companies with varying maturity periods ranging from November 2025 to March 2026, with face values of ₹5 lakh each.

## Key Points

- 10 commercial papers from different companies listed on debt market segment
- All securities have face value of ₹5,00,000 each
- Issue prices range from ₹4,80,422.5 to ₹4,92,643 per security
- Maturity periods vary from 3 months to 6+ months
- All securities allotted on August 28, 2025
- Trading effective from August 29, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification under existing Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed companies must comply with ongoing disclosure requirements for debt securities
- Trading members can commence dealings in these securities from the effective date
- Standard debt market trading and settlement procedures apply

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 28, 2025
- **Listing Date**: August 29, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**: Range from November 26, 2025 to March 17, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Moderate positive impact on debt market liquidity with addition of new short-term investment options. The listing provides institutional investors with diversified commercial paper options from established companies across sectors including financial services, real estate, and conglomerates.

**Investor Impact**: Enhanced choice for short-term debt investments with varying risk profiles and maturity periods. The securities offer yields implied by their discount pricing structure.
