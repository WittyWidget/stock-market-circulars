## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session in the Capital Market Segment on Saturday, October 11, 2025. The session includes two trading sessions - one from the Primary Site (10:45 to 13:30) and another from the DR Site (14:00 to 15:15). Members are advised that login with NEAT+ version 7.8.3 will be discontinued from November 01, 2025, and Order Snapshot Recovery via TCP/IP for MTBT broadcast will be available for testing.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for October 11, 2025 with two separate trading sessions
- Trading Session-1 from Primary Site: 10:45 to 13:30 hours
- Trading Session-2 from DR Site: 14:00 to 15:15 hours
- NEAT+ 7.8.3 login to be discontinued from November 01, 2025 (mock)
- Order Snapshot Recovery via TCP/IP for MTBT broadcast available for testing, effective in LIVE from October 13, 2025
- Live re-login timings: 16:00 to 16:30 hours
- All outstanding orders will be cleared before each session
- No financial obligations - trades will not result in any fund pay-in or pay-out

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular provides information about a routine mock trading session for system testing purposes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use NEAT+ version 7.8.3 or 7.8.4 for the mock session
- Only valid and compliant UCC/PAN uploaded and approved before cutoff will be allowed
- Members using NNF software must manually clear orders in their systems
- Members should refer to Exchange circular NSE/MSD/70707 dated October 08, 2025 for Order Snapshot Recovery details
- For connectivity details, refer to circular NSE/MSD/67674 dated April 24, 2025
- For software testing requirements, refer to circular NSE/MSD/46441 and SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234

## Important Dates

- **October 11, 2025**: Mock trading session date
- **October 13, 2025**: Order Snapshot Recovery via TCP/IP effective in LIVE environment
- **November 01, 2025**: NEAT+ 7.8.3 login discontinuation date (mock)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine mock trading session with no impact on regular trading or financial obligations.

**Operational Impact**: Low to medium for members. Members need to:
- Participate in mock session for system testing
- Prepare for transition away from NEAT+ 7.8.3 before November 01, 2025
- Test new Order Snapshot Recovery feature before it goes live on October 13, 2025
- Ensure connectivity parameters and software versions are properly configured

**Member Action Required**: Members should participate in the mock session to test their systems, particularly the new Order Snapshot Recovery feature, and ensure readiness for the upcoming version discontinuation.
