## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has mandated the migration of segregated client collateral reporting from the existing portal to a new NCL portal (https://ims.connect2nsccl.com/NCLMemberPortal/). Members must begin using the new portal for reports starting from trade date August 28, 2025, with a transition period allowing dual reporting until September 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- New portal URL: https://ims.connect2nsccl.com/NCLMemberPortal/
- Reporting process remains the same as current procedures
- Members must use existing admin IDs and reset passwords
- Sub-users need to be created with NCL INSPECTION role
- Technical support available at support@nsccl.co.in

## Regulatory Changes

- Migration from existing portal to new NCL portal for segregated client collateral reporting
- Discontinuation of old portal submissions after September 15, 2025 trade date
- Enhanced portal infrastructure for compliance reporting

## Compliance Requirements

- Clear all segregated client collateral reports through new NCL portal starting August 28, 2025
- Reset admin passwords and create sub-users as per Annexure 1 process
- Continue dual reporting during transition period (August 28 - September 12, 2025)
- Report technical issues with specific subject line format and screenshots
- Maintain existing processes for penalty display and review requests

## Important Dates

- **August 28, 2025**: Start date for new portal reporting (due date August 29, 2025)
- **August 28 - September 12, 2025**: Transition period allowing dual portal reporting
- **September 15, 2025**: Final date - old portal submissions discontinued

## Impact Assessment

- **Operational Impact**: Medium - requires system migration and user training but with adequate transition period
- **Compliance Impact**: Medium - mandatory migration with clear timelines and support structure
- **Business Continuity**: Low risk - dual reporting period ensures smooth transition
- **Technical Requirements**: Members need to reset credentials and configure sub-users for new portal access
