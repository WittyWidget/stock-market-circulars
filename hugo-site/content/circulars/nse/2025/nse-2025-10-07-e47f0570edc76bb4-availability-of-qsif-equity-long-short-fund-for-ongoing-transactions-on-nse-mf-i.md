## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has announced that Quant Mutual Fund's QSIF Equity Long-Short Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective October 08, 2025. The reopening includes facility for Systematic Investment Plans (SIP), Systematic Transfer Plans (STP), and Systematic Withdrawal Plans (SWP) across all six plan variants.

## Key Points

- QSIF Equity Long-Short Fund reopening for ongoing transactions from October 08, 2025
- Six scheme variants available: Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW (Payout/Reinvestment) options
- All schemes categorized under Equity category
- Facilities available: Subscriptions, Redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Applicable to NSE MF Invest Platform

## Scheme Details

**Direct Plan Variants:**
- Growth (ISIN: INF966L30019)
- IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF966L30035)
- IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF966L30043)

**Regular Plan Variants:**
- Growth (ISIN: INF966L30027)
- IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF966L30050)
- IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF966L30068)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update regarding the availability of mutual fund schemes on the platform.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of NSE are informed about the availability of these schemes for client transactions. No specific compliance actions required beyond normal mutual fund transaction protocols.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** October 07, 2025
- **Effective Date:** October 08, 2025 - Schemes available for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This circular affects only investors and distributors interested in Quant Mutual Fund's QSIF Equity Long-Short Fund.

**Operational Impact:** Low. Members can facilitate transactions in these schemes through NSE MF Invest Platform starting October 08, 2025.

**Investor Impact:** Positive for investors seeking exposure to equity long-short strategies, as the fund becomes accessible for ongoing investments and systematic plans.
