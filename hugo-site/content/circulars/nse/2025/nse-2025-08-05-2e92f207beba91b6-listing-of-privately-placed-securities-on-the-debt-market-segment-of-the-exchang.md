## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed securities to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective August 5, 2025. The listing includes 5 commercial papers and 3 other debt securities from various companies, along with one re-issue of commercial paper.

## Key Points

- Fresh issue of 5 commercial papers from NABARD, Godrej Agrovet, 5Paisa Capital, Godrej Industries, and Kotak Securities
- Fresh issue of 3 debt securities from Sundaram Home Finance, Aditya Birla Housing Finance, and Sammaan Capital
- Re-issue of 1 commercial paper from Reliance Industries Limited
- All securities assigned specific ISIN codes and security descriptions
- Face values range from ₹5,000 to ₹5,00,000 depending on the instrument

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification under existing Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities are now available for trading on NSE debt market segment
- Market participants can trade these securities using designated security codes
- Standard debt market trading regulations apply to all listed securities

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 5, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: August 1-5, 2025 (varies by security)
- **Redemption Dates**: Range from October 29, 2025 to August 4, 2035

## Impact Assessment

This listing expands the debt market investment universe with new commercial papers and debentures. The securities offer various maturity profiles from 3 months to 10+ years, providing investors with diverse fixed-income options. The inclusion of major corporate names like Reliance Industries and Godrej entities may attract institutional interest.
