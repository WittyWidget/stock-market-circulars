## Summary

National Stock Exchange has notified the listing of equity shares of Pace Digitek Limited on the Capital Market segment effective October 06, 2025. The company's IPO consists of 215,851,327 equity shares with a face value of Rs. 2/- each, issued at Rs. 219/- per share. The security will trade under the symbol PACEDIGITK with ISIN INE0S3G01027 and a market lot size of 1 share. The scrip will be part of the pre-open session for IPO and other category scrips as per SEBI regulations.

## Key Points

- **Symbol**: PACEDIGITK
- **ISIN**: INE0S3G01027
- **Series**: EQ (Equity)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 2/- per share
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 219/- per share
- **Total Shares**: 215,851,327 equity shares fully paid up
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Listing Date**: October 06, 2025
- **Pre-open Session**: Applicable as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012
- **Pari Passu**: Yes

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard IPO listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify the security by its designated code (PACEDIGITK) for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 1 share
- The security will participate in pre-open session for IPO category scrips
- Lock-in periods apply to various tranches of shares as detailed in Annexure I, ranging from October 30, 2025 to October 03, 2028

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: October 03, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 06, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry Dates**: Various dates ranging from October 30, 2025 to October 03, 2028 for different share tranches
- **Financial Year**: April 2025 - March 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal - This is a routine IPO listing adding one new security to the Capital Market segment. The market lot size of 1 share provides good accessibility for retail investors.

**Operational Impact**: Standard listing procedures apply. Trading members need to update their systems to recognize the new security code and ensure compliance with pre-open session requirements.
