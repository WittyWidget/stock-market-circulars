## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed securities to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective October 08, 2025. The circular lists fresh issues and re-issues of commercial paper from Angel One Limited, Godrej Industries Limited, Godrej Properties Limited, L&T Finance Limited, Axis Securities Limited, and SMFG India Credit Company Limited. Additionally, fresh issues of debt securities (debentures) from Vedika Credit Capital Limited have been listed.

## Key Points

- Total of 6 fresh commercial paper issues admitted to trading
- 2 fresh debt securities (debentures) from Vedika Credit Capital Limited listed
- 2 re-issues of commercial paper from L&T Finance Limited and SMFG India Credit Company Limited
- All securities effective from October 08, 2025
- Face value of commercial papers: ₹5,00,000 each
- Face value of debentures: ₹1,00,000 each
- Maturity periods range from 22 days to 18 months for commercial paper
- Debenture tenures of 18 months with coupon rates of 11.6% and 11.8%

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a notification under existing Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from members. This is an informational circular notifying members of newly admitted securities for trading on the debt market segment.

## Important Dates
