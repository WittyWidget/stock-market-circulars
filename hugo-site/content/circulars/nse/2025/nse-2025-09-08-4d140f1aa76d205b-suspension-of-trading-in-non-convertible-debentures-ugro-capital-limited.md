## Summary

NSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of Ugro Capital Limited (Symbol: 1050UCL25, ISIN: INE583D07315) effective September 11, 2025, due to maturity of the debentures.

## Key Points

- Trading suspension applies to Ugro Capital Limited's Non-Convertible Debentures
- Security Symbol: 1050UCL25
- ISIN Number: INE583D07315
- Reason for suspension: Maturity of debentures
- Circular issued under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard suspension procedure under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from market participants. This is an administrative action by NSE.

## Important Dates

- **September 8, 2025**: Circular issued
- **September 11, 2025**: Trading suspension becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only the specific Non-Convertible Debentures of Ugro Capital Limited due to natural maturity. No impact on equity trading or other securities of the company.
