## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of 4,042,275 additional equity shares of Mos Utility Limited on the SME EMERGE platform. These shares were allotted on a preferential basis at Rs. 58 per share with a face value of Rs. 2 each. The securities will be admitted to trading effective October 07, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Mos Utility Limited
- Trading Symbol: MOS
- Series: SM (SME segment)
- ISIN: INE0N7S01028
- Number of shares: 4,042,275 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 2 per share
- Issue price: Rs. 58 per share
- Allotment date: September 12, 2025
- Distinctive number range: 124677931 to 128720205
- Market lot size: 2000 shares
- Lock-in expiry date: April 10, 2027
- Pari passu: Yes (rank equally with existing shares)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular notifies routine listing procedures under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for trading
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes of 2000 shares
- Shares are subject to lock-in until April 10, 2027
- Temporary ISIN (IN8*********) applies until permanent activation as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012

## Important Dates

- Circular date: October 06, 2025
- Effective trading date: October 07, 2025
- Allotment date: September 12, 2025
- Lock-in expiry: April 10, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a preferential allotment for an SME segment company with shares under lock-in until April 2027. The allotment represents additional equity capital raised by Mos Utility Limited. The shares will not be immediately tradeable due to lock-in restrictions, minimizing near-term liquidity impact on the stock.
