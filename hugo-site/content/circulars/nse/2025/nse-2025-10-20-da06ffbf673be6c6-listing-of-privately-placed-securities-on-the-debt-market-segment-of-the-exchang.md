## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed commercial paper securities on its debt market segment with effect from October 20, 2025. The circular lists 10 commercial paper issues from various companies including L&T Finance Limited, Raymond Lifestyle Limited, ICICI Securities Limited, Tata Projects Limited, JM Financial Services Limited, Deutsche Investments India Private Limited, Godrej Properties Limited (2 issues), Alembic Pharmaceuticals Limited, and HDFC Securities Limited.

## Key Points

- Total of 10 commercial paper securities admitted to dealings on NSE's Debt Market Segment
- All securities were allotted on October 17, 2025
- Effective listing date: October 20, 2025
- Issuance under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations
- Face value of all listed commercial papers: Rs 5,00,000 per security
- Maturity periods range from approximately 84 days to 236 days

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification under existing NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements imposed on market participants. This is an informational circular regarding admission of securities to trading on the debt market segment.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 17, 2025 (all securities)
- **Listing/Trading Effective Date**: October 20, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**: Range from January 9, 2026 to June 10, 2026 (varies by security)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative notification with minimal market impact. The listing of commercial paper securities on the debt market segment provides additional short-term debt instruments for institutional investors and enhances funding options for the issuing companies. No impact on equity markets or retail investors. The circular is purely informational for market participants aware of debt market operations.
