## Summary

Bhadora Industries Limited has been listed on NSE following its SME IPO, with the company filing its post-issue shareholding pattern as of August 7, 2025, under Regulation 31 of SEBI LODR Regulations 2015.

## Key Points

- Company name: Bhadora Industries Limited
- Security type: Equity shares
- Filing date: August 7, 2025 (post-issue)
- Filed under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI LODR 2015
- Standard shareholding pattern disclosure format submitted

## Regulatory Changes

No specific regulatory changes mentioned. This is a standard compliance filing following SME IPO listing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Mandatory shareholding pattern filing under SEBI LODR Regulation 31
- Declaration of various security types (partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, ESOPs, etc.)
- All security types declared as 'NO' indicating standard equity structure
- No shares pledged, locked-in, or under non-disposal undertaking

## Important Dates

- Shareholding pattern as on: August 7, 2025
- Filing requirement: Post-issue disclosure

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine compliance filing for a newly listed SME company. The disclosure provides transparency on ownership structure and confirms no complex securities or encumbrances exist.
