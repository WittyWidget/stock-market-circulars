## Summary

Bank of India Mid Cap Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on NSE MF Invest Platform effective August 25, 2025. The fund will offer SIP, STP, and SWP facilities across six variants including Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening effective from August 25, 2025
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Six scheme variants available (Direct/Regular with Growth/IDCW Payout/IDCW Reinvestment)
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities enabled
- All schemes categorized under Equity fund category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a fund availability update.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are informed about the fund availability for client transactions.

## Important Dates

- **August 25, 2025**: Fund reopening effective date for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine fund reopening. Provides additional investment options for investors seeking mid-cap equity exposure through Bank of India Mutual Fund on the NSE platform.
