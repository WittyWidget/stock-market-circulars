## Summary

NSE announces the listing of Indiqube Spaces Limited equity shares on the Capital Market segment effective July 30, 2025. The company completed its IPO with shares priced at Rs. 237 per share and will trade under symbol 'INDIQUBE' with ISIN INE06ST01018.

## Key Points

- Company Symbol: INDIQUBE
- ISIN: INE06ST01018
- Face Value: Re. 1/-
- Issue Price: Rs. 237/-
- Total Securities: 210,011,507 equity shares
- Market Lot: 1 share
- Trading Effective: July 30, 2025
- Series: EQ (Equity)
- Pari Passu: Yes

## Regulatory Changes

- New equity security added to NSE Capital Market segment
- Security will participate in pre-open session for IPO category as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading
- Trading must occur in specified lot sizes
- Lock-in periods apply to various shareholding categories as detailed in Annexure I

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 30, 2025
- Various lock-in expiry dates ranging from August 26, 2025 to July 29, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Standard IPO listing with medium market impact. Adds new investment option in real estate/workspace sector. Lock-in provisions ensure controlled supply in secondary market initially. Company operates in co-working space segment which may attract investor interest in post-pandemic workspace transformation theme.
