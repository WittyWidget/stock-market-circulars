## Summary

NSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of Navi Finserv Limited effective August 29, 2025. The suspension is due to the maturity of the debt instrument.

## Key Points

- Company: Navi Finserv Limited
- Security Symbol: 10NFL25
- ISIN: INE342T07478
- Reason for suspension: Maturity
- Effective date: August 29, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine administrative action following existing trading regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must cease trading in the specified Non-Convertible Debentures from August 29, 2025
- All pending orders for this security should be managed accordingly before the suspension date

## Important Dates

- **August 26, 2025**: Circular issued
- **August 29, 2025**: Trading suspension becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine suspension due to debt instrument maturity. Only affects holders and traders of this specific Non-Convertible Debenture issue of Navi Finserv Limited.
