## Summary

NSE has admitted 10 commercial paper securities from various companies to trading on the debt market segment with effect from August 11, 2025. The securities are privately placed instruments with maturity periods ranging from September 2025 to February 2026.

## Key Points

- 10 commercial paper securities admitted to NSE debt market trading
- All securities have face value of Rs 5,00,000 each
- Issue prices range from Rs 4,78,638.5 to Rs 4,96,430
- All securities were allotted on August 8, 2025
- Trading effective from August 11, 2025

## Securities Details

**Companies and Securities:**
- 5Paisa Capital Limited - PCL CP 04/02/26 Sr 7 (200 units, redemption Feb 4, 2026)
- ICICI Securities Limited - ISEC CP 07/11/25 (500 units, redemption Nov 7, 2025)
- IIFL Samasta Finance Limited - ISFL CP 06/11/25 (500 units, redemption Nov 6, 2025)
- Indian Oil Corporation Limited - IOCL CP 07/11/25 Sr 752 (40,000 units, redemption Nov 7, 2025)
- Godrej Industries Limited - GIL CP 07/11/25 Sr 67 (1,500 units, redemption Nov 7, 2025)
- Welspun Corp Limited - WCL CP 19/09/25 (1,000 units, redemption Sep 19, 2025)
- IIFL Capital Services Limited - Three series (ISL CP 12/11/25 Sr 31, 18/11/25 Sr 32, 24/11/25 Sr 33, 500 units each)
- Godrej Consumer Products Limited - GCPL CP 07/11/25 Sr 11 (9,000 units, redemption Nov 7, 2025)

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date:** August 8, 2025
- **Trading Commencement:** August 11, 2025
- **Redemption Dates:** September 19, 2025 to February 4, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative circular with minimal market impact. The listing of these commercial paper securities provides additional debt market instruments for institutional investors and enhances market liquidity in the short-term debt segment.
