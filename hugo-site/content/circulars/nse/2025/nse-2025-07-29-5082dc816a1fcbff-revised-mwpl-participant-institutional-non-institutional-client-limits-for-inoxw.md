## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has revised the Market Wide Position Limit (MWPL) and various client position limits for INOXWIND in the Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme. The new limits will be effective from July 30, 2025, replacing the previous limits set in earlier circulars.

## Key Points

- Market Wide Position Limit increased from 67,442,043 to 68,424,919 shares
- Institutional client limit raised from 6,744,204 to 6,842,491 shares
- Participant limit increased from 6,744,204 to 6,842,491 shares
- Non-Institutional client limit revised from 674,420 to 684,249 shares
- Changes apply specifically to INOXWIND symbol in SLB scheme

## Regulatory Changes

This circular modifies previous circulars NCL/CMPT/67763 dated April 30, 2025, and partially modifies NCL/CMPT/68844 dated June 30, 2025. The revision represents an increase in all position limit categories for INOXWIND.

## Compliance Requirements

- All participants and custodians must note the revised limits
- New limits must be implemented from July 30, 2025
- Participants should ensure compliance with the updated position limits in their SLB operations

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: July 30, 2025
- **Circular Date**: July 29, 2025
- **Applicable Period**: July 2025

## Impact Assessment

The increase in position limits provides additional capacity for securities lending and borrowing activities in INOXWIND. This could enhance liquidity and market efficiency for the stock while maintaining appropriate risk management controls through the revised but still structured limit framework.
