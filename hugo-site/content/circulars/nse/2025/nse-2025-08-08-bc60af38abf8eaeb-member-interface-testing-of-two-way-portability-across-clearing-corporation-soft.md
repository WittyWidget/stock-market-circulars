## Summary

NSE has issued a circular regarding member interface testing of two-way portability across clearing corporations under the Software as a Service (SaaS) model. The circular provides support contact information for members.

## Key Points

- Member interface testing for two-way portability across clearing corporations
- Implementation under Software as a Service (SaaS) model
- Support helpline available at 18002660050 (IVR Option 2)
- Email support available at support@nsccl.co.in

## Regulatory Changes

No specific regulatory changes mentioned in the available content.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are expected to participate in the interface testing as per the clearing corporation requirements.

## Important Dates

No specific dates mentioned in the available content.

## Impact Assessment

This testing initiative will enable better portability for members across different clearing corporations, potentially improving operational efficiency and reducing settlement risks through the SaaS model implementation.
