## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed securities to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective July 28, 2025. The listing includes commercial papers from companies like Tata Capital Housing Finance Limited, Godrej Properties Limited, Godrej Agrovet Limited, and others, along with debt securities from NABARD and SIDBI.

## Key Points

- Multiple commercial papers and debt securities listed on NSE debt market segment
- Fresh issues include commercial papers with 3-month tenure (October 2025 redemption)
- Debt securities from NABARD and SIDBI with 3-year tenure (2028 redemption)
- Face value of ₹5,00,000 for commercial papers and ₹1,00,000 for debt securities
- One re-issue of Aditya Birla Capital Limited commercial paper

## Securities Listed

**Commercial Papers (Fresh Issues):**
- Tata Capital Housing Finance Limited - ₹200 crores (4000 units)
- Godrej Properties Limited - ₹150 crores (3000 units)
- Godrej Agrovet Limited - ₹100 crores each for two series (2000 units each)
- Small Industries Development Bank of India - ₹6,000 crores (120,000 units)
- Godrej Industries Limited - ₹75 crores (1500 units)

**Debt Securities:**
- NABARD 6.66% 2028 Sr 26A - ₹7,000 crores (700,000 units)
- SIDBI 6.66% 2028 Sr I - ₹5,925.5 crores (592,550 units)

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** July 28, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** July 25, 2025 (for all securities)
- **Commercial Paper Redemption:** October 23-24, 2025
- **Debt Securities Redemption:** October 2028

## Impact Assessment

This listing expands the debt market segment with significant fresh issuances totaling over ₹19,000 crores across commercial papers and debt securities. The listing provides institutional investors with additional short-term and medium-term investment options in the debt market.
