## Summary

HDFC Mutual Fund's Diversified Equity All Cap Active Fund of Funds (FOF) will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective October 06, 2025. All six plan options (Direct/Regular with Growth/IDCW Payout/IDCW Reinvestment) will be available along with systematic investment facilities including SIP, STP, and SWP.

## Key Points

- HDFC Diversified Equity All Cap Active FOF reopening for ongoing transactions
- Effective date: October 06, 2025
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Six scheme options available across Direct and Regular plans
- All schemes categorized under Equity
- Systematic facilities enabled: SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan)
- Both subscriptions and redemptions will be permitted

No regulatory changes introduced. This is an operational update regarding the reopening of previously closed scheme for transactions.

No specific compliance requirements. This is an informational circular for trading members regarding scheme availability.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** October 03, 2025
- **Effective Date:** October 06, 2025 - Schemes reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions

## Impact Assessment

**Investor Impact:** Investors in HDFC Diversified Equity All Cap Active FOF will be able to perform fresh subscriptions, redemptions, and set up systematic plans starting October 06, 2025.

**Market Impact:** Minimal. This affects only a single fund-of-funds scheme and represents routine fund operations.

**Operational Impact:** NSE MF Invest Platform members should ensure their systems are updated to accept transactions in these six ISINs from the effective date.
