## Summary

Sawaliya Food Products Limited has submitted its shareholding pattern as per Regulation 31(1)(A) following its SME IPO listing. The company has disclosed its post-issue shareholding structure with 9,918,220 total equity shares.

## Key Points

- Total issued equity shares: 9,918,220
- Promoter & Promoter Group holding: 6,722,789 shares (67.78%)
- Public shareholding: 3,195,431 shares (32.22%)
- Number of shareholders: 540 total (5 promoters, 535 public)
- All shares are fully paid-up equity shares
- No partly paid shares, convertible securities, warrants, or ESOPs outstanding

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shareholding pattern filed under Regulation 31(1)(A) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Post-issue shareholding disclosure completed as of August 13, 2025
- Company declared no locked-in shares, pledged shares, or encumbrances

## Important Dates

- Shareholding pattern date: August 13, 2025 (Post Issue)
- Filing under: Regulation 31(1)(A)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine compliance disclosure following SME IPO listing with no market impact. The shareholding pattern shows standard promoter-public distribution for a newly listed SME company with promoters retaining majority control at 67.78%.
