## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has notified the listing and trading of equity shares issued pursuant to a bonus issue by TAC Infosec Limited. The 1,04,79,600 bonus shares will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment with effect from October 17, 2025. The shares will be identified by the symbol TAC and ISIN INE0SOY01013.

## Key Points

- Company: TAC Infosec Limited
- Symbol: TAC
- ISIN: INE0SOY01013
- Number of bonus securities: 1,04,79,600
- Distinctive number range: 10479601 to 20959200
- Trading effective date: October 17, 2025
- Issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Members should note that:
- Securities will be identified only by their designated codes on the trading system
- Trading participants must use symbol TAC for identification
- Standard trading procedures apply from the effective date

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: October 13, 2025
- **Record Date**: October 15, 2025
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: October 16, 2025
- **Trading Effective Date**: October 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine corporate action with limited market impact. The bonus issue will increase the total number of TAC Infosec Limited shares available for trading by 1,04,79,600 units. Existing shareholders as of the record date will receive bonus shares proportionally. The action may temporarily increase liquidity in the stock but does not change the company's fundamental value or market capitalization.
