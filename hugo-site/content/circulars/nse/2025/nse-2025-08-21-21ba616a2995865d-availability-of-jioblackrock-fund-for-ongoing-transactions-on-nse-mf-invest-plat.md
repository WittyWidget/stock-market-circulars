## Summary

NSE announces that five JioBlackRock Mutual Fund schemes will be available for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform starting August 22, 2025. All schemes are equity index funds offering Direct Growth options with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities.

## Key Points

- Five JioBlackRock index funds reopening for ongoing transactions
- All schemes are equity category Direct Growth options
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be available
- Effective date: August 22, 2025
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes involved - this is an operational update about fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members can process ongoing subscriptions and redemptions for listed schemes
- Standard mutual fund transaction procedures apply
- Support available through toll-free number and email

## Important Dates

- **August 22, 2025**: Effective date for ongoing transactions to begin

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine operational notice. Provides additional investment options for investors seeking exposure to Nifty indices through JioBlackRock fund house. The availability of systematic investment facilities enhances accessibility for retail investors.
