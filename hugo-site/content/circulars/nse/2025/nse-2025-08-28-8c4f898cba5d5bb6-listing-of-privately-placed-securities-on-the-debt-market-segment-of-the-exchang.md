## Summary

NSE has listed 10 commercial papers from various companies on its debt market segment effective August 28, 2025. All securities were allotted on August 26, 2025, with redemption dates ranging from October 17, 2025 to January 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- 10 commercial papers listed with face value of ₹5,00,000 each
- Total securities quantity: 52,900 units
- All allotments made on August 26, 2025
- Issue prices range from ₹486,274.5 to ₹495,854
- Redemption periods vary from 2 to 5 months

## Listed Securities

**Angel One Limited (AOL)**
- Series: Sr 41
- Quantity: 900 units
- Issue Price: ₹490,402
- Redemption: November 25, 2025

**HSBC Investdirect Financial Services**
- Series: Sr 21
- Quantity: 1,500 units
- Issue Price: ₹486,274.5
- Redemption: January 27, 2026

**ICICI Securities Limited (ISEC)**
- Quantity: 13,000 units (largest issue)
- Issue Price: ₹492,195.5
- Redemption: November 25, 2025

**Other Companies:** Piramal Finance (2,000 units), Welspun Corp (1,000 units), Aditya Birla Capital (16,000 units - second largest), Godrej Properties (1,500 units), Motilal Oswal Financial Services (4,000 units), TATA Projects (4,000 units), and Bharti Hexacom (5,000 units).

## Important Dates

- **Listing Date:** August 28, 2025
- **Allotment Date:** August 26, 2025
- **Earliest Redemption:** October 17, 2025 (Bharti Hexacom)
- **Latest Redemption:** January 27, 2026 (HSBC Investdirect)

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt market activity with no significant impact on equity markets. These commercial paper listings provide short-term funding options for the issuing companies and investment opportunities for debt market participants.
