## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed securities to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective July 30, 2025. The circular lists commercial papers and other debt securities from various companies with their security codes, ISINs, face values, issue prices, and redemption dates.

## Key Points

- Multiple commercial papers listed with maturity periods ranging from August 2025 to October 2025
- Face values predominantly at â‚¹5,00,000 per security
- Issue prices vary based on discount rates and tenure
- Includes both fresh issues and re-issues
- L&T Finance Limited debt security with 7.1242% coupon rate maturing in 2028

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Listed companies must comply with ongoing disclosure requirements
- Securities admitted for trading on debt market segment
- Designated security codes assigned as per annexure

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: July 30, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: July 29-30, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**: Range from August 5, 2025 to September 25, 2028
- **Shortest Tenure**: HDB Financial Services CP - 7 days
- **Longest Tenure**: L&T Finance debt security - 3+ years

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on debt market participants and institutional investors. Provides additional investment options in commercial papers and corporate debt securities. Companies gain access to debt capital markets for funding requirements. Short-term commercial papers offer liquidity management tools for institutional investors.
