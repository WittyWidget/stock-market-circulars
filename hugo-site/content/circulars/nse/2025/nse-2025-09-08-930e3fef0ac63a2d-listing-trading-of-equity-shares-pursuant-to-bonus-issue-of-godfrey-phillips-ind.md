## Summary

NSE has announced the listing and trading of equity shares pursuant to bonus issue by Godfrey Phillips India Limited. The new shares will be available for trading on NSE Capital Market segment from September 18, 2025.

## Key Points

- 103,987,840 new equity shares to be listed
- Trading symbol: GODFRYPHLP
- ISIN: INE260B01028
- Distinctive number range: 51993921 to 155981760
- Securities identified by designated codes for trading

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading
- Securities to be identified only by designated codes on trading system

## Important Dates

- Record Date: September 16, 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: September 17, 2025
- Trading Commencement: September 18, 2025
- Circular Effective Date: September 18, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on Godfrey Phillips India Limited shareholders and traders. Significant increase in share count (103+ million shares) will affect liquidity and market capitalization calculations. Routine bonus issue with no operational disruptions expected.
