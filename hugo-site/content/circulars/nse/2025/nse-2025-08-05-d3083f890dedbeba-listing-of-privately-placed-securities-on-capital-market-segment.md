## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed debt securities from three companies on the capital market segment effective August 06, 2025. The circular specifies trading symbols, lot sizes, and security details for debentures issued by Sundaram Home Finance Limited, Sammaan Capital Limited, and Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited.

## Key Points

- Three new debt securities admitted to NSE capital market segment
- Trading commences August 06, 2025
- All securities have market lot size of 1
- Securities identified by designated codes for trading purposes
- Coupon rates range from 7.12% to 9.20%
- Maturity periods extend from 2028 to 2035

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing process under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading
- Trading must comply with specified lot sizes
- Securities subject to Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 06, 2025
- **Maturity Dates**:
  - SHFL 7.12% 2030: August 01, 2030
  - SCL 9.20% 2028: August 04, 2028
  - ABHFL 7.58% 2035: July 31, 2035

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine administrative listing of debt securities. The addition of these three privately placed debentures provides additional fixed-income investment options for institutional investors. No significant operational changes required for market participants.
