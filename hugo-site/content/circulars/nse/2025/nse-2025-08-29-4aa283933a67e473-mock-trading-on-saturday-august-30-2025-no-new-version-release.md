## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session in the Capital Market Segment on Saturday, August 30, 2025. The session includes various trading windows with specific timings and serves as preparation for Monday trading. Members must ensure migration to NEAT+ version 7.8.3 before the old version 7.8.2 discontinuation on September 6, 2025.

## Key Points

- Mock trading date: August 30, 2025
- Trading sessions run from 08:45 to 14:00 with multiple windows
- Pre-Open session: 09:00-09:08 with random closure in last minute
- Normal Market: 09:15-10:30
- T+0 Market: 09:30-10:10
- Re-login timings: 13:30-14:00
- All outstanding orders cleared before each session
- No financial obligations from mock trades
- NOTIS application unavailable during session
- Connect2NSE and Extranet remain available

## Regulatory Changes

Mandatory migration to NEAT+ version 7.8.3 required before September 6, 2025 when version 7.8.2 will be discontinued.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must participate using all trading software
- Re-login into live environment to ensure connectivity for Monday, September 1, 2025
- Only valid and compliant UCC/PAN uploaded before cutoff allowed
- NNF software users must manually clear orders in their systems
- Members may use Mock Trading or Simulated Environment to meet SEBI regulatory requirements per circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234

## Important Dates

- **August 30, 2025**: Mock trading session
- **September 1, 2025**: Regular trading resumes
- **September 6, 2025**: NEAT+ version 7.8.2 discontinuation deadline

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact routine mock trading session designed to ensure system readiness and member preparedness. Critical software migration deadline approaching may affect members not yet upgraded to NEAT+ 7.8.3. Session provides essential testing opportunity before regular Monday trading.
