## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has announced that Aarvee Denims & Exports Limited will undergo a name and symbol change effective October 13, 2025. The company will be renamed to VARVEE GLOBAL LIMITED and its trading symbol will change from AARVEEDEN to VGL.

## Key Points

- Company name changing from "Aarvee Denims & Exports Limited" to "VARVEE GLOBAL LIMITED"
- Trading symbol changing from AARVEEDEN to VGL
- Change effective from October 13, 2025
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department
- Circular reference number: 1952/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an administrative update to reflect corporate name change.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the Exchange should update their systems to reflect the new company name and trading symbol from October 13, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: October 07, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative change with minimal market impact. Traders and investors should note the new symbol VGL when trading the company's shares from October 13, 2025. The name change does not affect the company's operations, financials, or shareholder rights.
