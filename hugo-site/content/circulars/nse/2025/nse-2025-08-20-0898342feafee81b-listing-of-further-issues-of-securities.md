## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of securities for 14 companies effective August 21, 2025. The securities include equity shares allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and conversion of warrants issued on preferential basis.

## Key Points

- Effective date: August 21, 2025
- 14 companies involved in further securities listing
- Securities include ESOP allotments and warrant conversions
- All securities will trade with market lot size of 1
- Pari passu status with existing shares

## Companies and Details

### Aavas Financiers Limited (AAVAS)
- Multiple ESOP allotments totaling 4,448 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10 each
- Allotment date: August 12, 2025
- No lock-in applicable

### Black Box Limited (BBOX)
- 5,995 equity shares from warrant conversion
- Face value: Rs. 2 each, Issue price: Rs. 417
- Allotment date: July 14, 2025
- Lock-in until: February 25, 2026

### Brigade Enterprises Limited (BRIGADE)
- ESOP allotments mentioned (details partially shown)
- Face value: Rs. 10 each
- Allotment date: August 13, 2025

## Regulatory Framework

- Listed under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Lot sizes specified under Regulation 2.5.5
- Securities identified by designated codes for trading

## Trading Details

- All securities will have market lot size of 1
- Trading effective from August 21, 2025
- Securities maintain pari passu status with existing shares

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine listings of additional shares from ESOP exercises and warrant conversions. The relatively small quantities involved are unlikely to significantly affect trading volumes or share prices.
