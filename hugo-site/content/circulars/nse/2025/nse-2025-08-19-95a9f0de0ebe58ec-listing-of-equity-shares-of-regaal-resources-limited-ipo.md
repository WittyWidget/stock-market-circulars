## Summary

Regaal Resources Limited has listed its equity shares on NSE following an IPO. The company has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, dated August 19, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company has no partly paid up shares, convertible securities, or warrants outstanding
- No ESOPs granted or depository receipts issued
- Promoter shares have locked-in restrictions as per IPO regulations
- No shares are pledged or under non-disposal undertaking
- No significant beneficial owners identified
- Company has shares in locked-in status post-IPO

## Regulatory Changes

Standard IPO listing compliance with SEBI regulations requiring mandatory shareholding pattern disclosure.

## Compliance Requirements

- Quarterly shareholding pattern filing under Regulation 31
- Compliance with lock-in requirements for promoter shares
- Ongoing disclosure obligations as a listed entity

## Important Dates

- Shareholding pattern filing date: August 19, 2025
- Post-issue status effective from listing date

## Impact Assessment

Routine IPO listing with standard regulatory compliance requirements. No immediate market impact beyond normal IPO listing procedures.
