## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session in the Capital Market Segment on Saturday, September 06, 2025. The session will use NEAT+ version 7.8.3 and includes two trading sessions - one from primary site and another from DR site with detailed timings for various market segments.

## Key Points

- Mock trading date: September 06, 2025
- Two trading sessions: Primary site (10:45-14:15) and DR site (15:00-16:15)
- NEAT+ version 7.8.3 to be used
- Version 7.8.2 will be discontinued after September 06, 2025
- All outstanding orders will be cleared before each session
- No financial obligations from mock trades
- NOTIS application accessible during session-2

## Regulatory Changes

- NEAT+ version 7.8.2 discontinuation effective September 06, 2025
- Only NEAT+ version 7.8.3 available at download path /common/NEATPlus783

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use valid and compliant UCC/PAN uploaded before cutoff
- NNF software users must manually clear orders in their systems
- Members may use mock trading or simulated environment to meet regulatory requirements per SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234
- UCC validation will be skipped during contingency time for order entry

## Important Dates

- September 06, 2025: Mock trading session
- September 06, 2025: NEAT+ version 7.8.2 discontinuation date

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on trading members who need to participate in mandatory system testing and ensure software version compliance. Members using older NEAT+ versions must upgrade to avoid trading disruptions. The mock session allows testing of various market segments including pre-open, normal trading, block deals, and auction markets.
