## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that Sundaram Finance Holdings Limited will undergo a name and symbol change effective October 16, 2025. The company will be renamed to TSF INVESTMENTS LIMITED and the trading symbol will change from SUNDARMHLD to TSFINV.

## Key Points

- Company name changing from "Sundaram Finance Holdings Limited" to "TSF INVESTMENTS LIMITED"
- Trading symbol changing from "SUNDARMHLD" to "TSFINV"
- Changes effective from October 16, 2025
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department
- Circular reference number: 1992/2025
- NSE reference number: NSE/CML/70756

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action involving company name and trading symbol modification as per standard listing procedures.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members are informed of the name and symbol change
- Market participants should update their systems to reflect the new name and symbol from the effective date
- No specific compliance actions required from members beyond awareness and system updates

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: October 10, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 16, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative change with minimal market impact. The name and symbol change is a corporate restructuring action that does not affect the underlying business operations, trading mechanics, or investor holdings. Market participants and trading systems will need to recognize the new symbol TSFINV from October 16, 2025. No disruption to trading is anticipated as this is a standard corporate action handled through established exchange procedures.
