NSE announces the forthcoming listing of Patel Retail Limited equity shares following their IPO. The company will trade under symbol PATELRMART with ISIN INE0R8B01010. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

- Company Name: Patel Retail Limited
- Trading Symbol: PATELRMART
- ISIN: INE0R8B01010
- Listing date to be announced separately
- IPO listing on NSE

No regulatory changes specified in this circular.

Members are informed about the upcoming listing for trading preparation and system updates.

Medium impact for retail sector investors and traders. New equity available for trading on NSE platform. Market participants should prepare for potential trading activity in PATELRMART shares upon listing.
