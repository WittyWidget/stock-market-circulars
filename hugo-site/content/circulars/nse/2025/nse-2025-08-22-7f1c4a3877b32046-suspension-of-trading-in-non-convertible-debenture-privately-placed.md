## Summary

NSE has announced the suspension of trading in three Non-Convertible Debentures (Privately Placed) from L&T Finance Limited, Power Finance Corporation Limited, and State Bank Of India effective August 25, 2025, due to redemption.

## Key Points

- Three Non-Convertible Debentures will be suspended from trading
- All suspensions are due to redemption of the instruments
- Suspension effective from August 25, 2025
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is implementation of existing regulations for debt instrument redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must cease trading in the specified debentures from the suspension date
- No specific compliance actions required beyond cessation of trading

## Important Dates

- **Suspension Date**: August 25, 2025
- **Circular Date**: August 22, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine suspension due to natural redemption of debt instruments. Affects only holders and traders of these specific debentures. No broader market implications expected.
