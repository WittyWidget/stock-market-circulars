## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has notified the listing and admission to trading of bonus equity shares issued by Univastu India Limited. A total of 2,39,91,180 bonus shares will be listed on the Capital Market segment effective October 15, 2025. The shares will trade under the symbol UNIVASTU with ISIN INE562X01013.

## Key Points

- 2,39,91,180 bonus equity shares to be listed
- Trading symbol: UNIVASTU
- ISIN: INE562X01013
- Distinctive Number Range: 11995591 to 35986770
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Members should update their systems to reflect the new securities admitted to trading on the Capital Market segment. Securities should be identified by their designated codes for trading purposes.

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** October 07, 2025
- **Record Date:** October 13, 2025
- **Deemed Date of Allotment:** October 14, 2025
- **Effective Date for Trading:** October 15, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a company-specific event with minimal market-wide impact. The bonus issue increases the number of outstanding shares of Univastu India Limited but does not affect the overall market structure or introduce new regulatory requirements. Trading members need to ensure their systems are updated to accommodate the additional securities.
