## Summary

Groww Mutual Fund is launching the Groww Multi Asset Allocation Fund on NSE MF Invest Platform. The fund will be available for transactions from September 10, 2025 to September 24, 2025 until 11:59 PM, with an allotment date of October 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- Six scheme variants available: Direct and Regular options with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment
- All schemes categorized under Equity category
- Transaction period: September 10-24, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- Allotment date: October 1, 2025
- Clear funds must be received in designated Clearing Corporation account by NFO closing date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before NFO closing date
- Standard NSE MF Invest Platform compliance requirements apply

## Important Dates

- **NFO Opening Date**: September 10, 2025
- **NFO Closing Date**: September 24, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: October 1, 2025
- **Fund Clearing Deadline**: On or before NFO closing date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine new fund offering launch. The multi-asset allocation fund provides additional investment options for retail and institutional investors on the NSE MF platform. No direct impact on stock prices or trading operations.
