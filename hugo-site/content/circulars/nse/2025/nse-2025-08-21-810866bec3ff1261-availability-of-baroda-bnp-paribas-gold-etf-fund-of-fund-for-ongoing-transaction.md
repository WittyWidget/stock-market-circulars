## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that the Baroda BNP Paribas Gold ETF Fund of Fund will be available for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective August 22, 2025. The fund will support subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment facilities.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening for ongoing transactions effective August 22, 2025
- Available facilities: subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP
- Two scheme variants available: Direct-Growth and Regular-Growth
- Both schemes categorized under Debt category
- Platform: NSE MF Invest Platform

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine fund availability notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are informed of the fund availability for client transactions through the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 22, 2025 - Fund reopened for ongoing transactions
- **Circular Date**: August 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine fund reopening. Provides additional investment options for investors seeking gold exposure through ETF Fund of Fund structure. The availability of systematic investment facilities (SIP, STP, SWP) enhances accessibility for retail investors.
