## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session for the Futures & Options segment on Saturday, August 23, 2025. The session includes normal market hours (11:15-12:00) and live re-login period (14:00-14:30). Members must ensure compatibility with NEAT+ versions and follow specific guidelines for participation.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for August 23, 2025
- Trading hours: 11:15 AM to 12:00 PM
- Trade modification end time: 12:10 PM
- Live re-login window: 2:00 PM to 2:30 PM
- NEAT+ version 7.8.2 will be discontinued on September 6, 2025
- NEAT+ version 7.8.3 is the current recommended version
- No financial obligations from mock trades
- NOTIS application will not be accessible during mock session

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes announced. This is a routine mock trading session to ensure system readiness.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must migrate to NEAT+ version 7.8.3 before September 6, 2025
- Only valid and compliant UCC/PAN will be allowed
- Members using NNF software must manually clear orders in their systems
- Participation required using all trading software for Monday readiness
- Must ensure telnet connectivity and proper Box ID setup

## Important Dates

- **August 23, 2025**: Mock trading session
- **September 6, 2025**: Discontinuation date for NEAT+ version 7.8.2
- **August 25, 2025**: Regular trading resumes (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on F&O members who must participate to ensure smooth operations on Monday. The upcoming discontinuation of NEAT+ version 7.8.2 requires timely system upgrades. Members should use this session to test connectivity and system functionality before regular trading resumes.
