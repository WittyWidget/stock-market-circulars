## Summary

NSE has announced that ORTEL Communications Limited (ISIN: INE849L01019) will be moved to Stage II of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework effective October 27, 2025. This escalation imposes stricter trading restrictions including trade-for-trade settlement with no netting off, a price band of 5% or lower, and mandatory Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) of 50% of trade value to be deposited by buyers.

## Key Points

- ORTEL Communications Limited (Symbol: ORTEL) moved to Stage II of GSM effective October 27, 2025
- Trading will be conducted on trade-for-trade basis with no netting off allowed
- Price band restricted to 5% or lower as applicable
- Buyers must deposit Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) of 50% of trade value
- Members advised to take adequate precautions while trading in this security
- Circular reference number: 825/2025, NSE/SURV/70961

## Regulatory Changes

The security transitions from Stage I to Stage II of the GSM framework, which involves:

**Stage I Measures (Previous):**
- 100% margin rate
- 5% or lower price band

**Stage II Measures (New):**
- Trade-for-trade settlement (no netting off)
- 5% or lower price band (continued)
- Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) of 50% of trade value required from buyers

**GSM Framework Overview:**
- Stage III: Trade-for-trade with 5% price band, trading once per week (Monday/first trading day), 100% ASD
- Stage IV: Trade-for-trade with 5% price band, trading once per week, 100% ASD, no upward movement allowed

## Compliance Requirements

**For All NSE Members:**
- Exercise adequate precautions when trading in ORTEL Communications Limited
- Ensure trade-to-trade settlement procedures are followed
- No netting off of positions is permitted

**For Buyers:**
- Must deposit Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) equivalent to 50% of trade value
- Must comply with 5% or lower price band restrictions

**For Queries:**
- Members may contact NSE at surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date:** October 24, 2025
- **Effective Date:** October 27, 2025 (Stage II GSM measures become applicable)

## Impact Assessment

**Trading Impact:**
- Significantly reduced liquidity due to trade-for-trade settlement requirement
- No intraday position squaring or netting off allowed
- Higher capital requirements for buyers due to 50% ASD
- Limited price movement due to 5% price band restriction

**Market Impact:**
- Reduced trading volumes expected in ORTEL Communications Limited
- Higher transaction costs for investors due to ASD requirements
- Potential difficulty in position exits due to restricted trading conditions

**Investor Impact:**
- Existing holders may face challenges in liquidity
- New buyers face additional capital deployment (50% ASD)
- Risk mitigation through enhanced surveillance measures
