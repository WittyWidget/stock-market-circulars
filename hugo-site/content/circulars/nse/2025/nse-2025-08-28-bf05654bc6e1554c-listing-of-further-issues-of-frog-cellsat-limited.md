## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of 25,400 additional equity shares of Frog Cellsat Limited (Symbol: FROG) on the SME EMERGE platform, effective August 29, 2025. These shares were allotted under the Employee Stock Purchase Scheme (ESPS) on August 6, 2025.

## Key Points

- 25,400 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each allotted under ESPS
- Trading symbol: FROG on SME EMERGE platform
- ISIN: INE385O01018 (Series: SM)
- Market lot size: 400 shares
- Distinctive number range: 15528901 to 15554300
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing of additional shares under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members can identify securities by designated codes
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes of 400 shares
- Shares currently credited under temporary ISIN as per SEBI guidelines

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 6, 2025
- **Listing Effective Date**: August 29, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: August 5, 2026
- **Circular Effective Date**: August 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine listing of additional shares under ESPS for an existing SME company. The shares have a one-year lock-in period, limiting immediate trading volume. This represents normal corporate action for employee stock allocation.
