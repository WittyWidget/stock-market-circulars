## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session in the Futures & Options segment on Saturday, August 09, 2025, with no new version release. The session includes primary site and disaster recovery site testing with specific timings for different phases.

## Key Points

- Mock trading from Primary Site: 11:15 to 15:00 hours
- Mock trading from DR Site: 15:45 to 16:15 hours with trade modification until 16:25
- Live Re-login session: 18:00 to 18:30 hours
- NEAT+ version 7.8.2 will be discontinued on September 06, 2025 (mock)
- NEAT+ versions 7.8.2 and 7.8.3 are compatible with new broadcast parameters
- No financial obligations from mock trading transactions

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Members must comply with existing software testing requirements as per SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must participate using all trading software
- Ensure migration to NEAT+ version 7.8.3 before September 06, 2025
- Valid and compliant UCC/PAN must be uploaded and approved before cutoff
- Members using NNF software must manually clear orders in their systems
- Re-login into live environment required for smooth Monday operations

## Important Dates

- **August 09, 2025**: Mock trading session
- **September 06, 2025**: NEAT+ version 7.8.2 discontinuation date
- **August 11, 2025**: Regular trading resumes (Monday)

## Impact Assessment

Medium operational impact as members need to participate in testing to ensure system readiness. Limited market disruption as session occurs on weekend. Critical for disaster recovery preparedness and system version migration planning.
