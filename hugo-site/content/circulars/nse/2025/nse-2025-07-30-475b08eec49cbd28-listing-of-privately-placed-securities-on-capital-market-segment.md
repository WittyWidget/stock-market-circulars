## Summary

NSE announces the listing of L&T Finance Limited's privately placed debt securities on the capital market segment. The 7.1242% 2028 Senior G bonds will be available for trading from July 31, 2025, with designated security code 712LTFL28 and market lot size of 1.

## Key Points

- L&T Finance Limited debt securities admitted to NSE capital market segment
- Security code: 712LTFL28 (Series N0)
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- ISIN Code: INE498L07178
- Issue description: LTFL 7.1242% 2028 Sr G

## Security Details

- **Company**: L&T Finance Limited
- **Coupon Rate**: 7.12% per annum
- **Maturity Date**: September 25, 2028
- **Series**: N0
- **Trading Symbol**: 712LTFL28

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: July 31, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: September 25, 2028

## Trading Information

- Securities will be identified by designated codes for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes as per annexure
- Regulations 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A apply

## Impact Assessment

Routine listing of debt securities with minimal market impact. Provides additional trading opportunities for institutional and retail investors in L&T Finance Limited's debt instruments.
