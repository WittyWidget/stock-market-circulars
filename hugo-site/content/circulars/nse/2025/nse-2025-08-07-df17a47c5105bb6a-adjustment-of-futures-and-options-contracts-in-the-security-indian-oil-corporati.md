## Summary

NSE has issued a circular regarding the adjustment of futures and options contracts for Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOC) following a corporate action. This circular outlines the necessary modifications to existing derivative contracts to account for changes in the underlying security.

## Key Points

- Adjustment applies to IOC futures and options contracts
- Modifications are required due to corporate action in the underlying security
- Standard NSE procedure for contract adjustments
- Affects all outstanding derivative positions in IOC

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory framework changes, but adjustment of existing contract terms as per NSE's standardized procedures for corporate actions.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants holding IOC derivative positions must note the contract adjustments
- Trading systems will reflect the modified contract specifications
- Settlement procedures will be updated accordingly

## Important Dates

- Circular date: August 7, 2025
- Adjustment effective as per the corporate action timeline

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on traders and investors holding IOC derivative positions. The adjustment ensures fair treatment of all market participants while maintaining contract integrity following the corporate action. Standard procedure with established precedent in Indian derivatives market.
