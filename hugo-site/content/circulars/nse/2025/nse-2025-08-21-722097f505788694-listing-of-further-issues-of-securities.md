## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 190,736 additional equity shares of Pelatro Limited on the SME EMERGE platform effective August 22, 2025. These shares were issued under preferential allotment with face value and paid-up value of Rs. 10 each at an issue price of Rs. 367.

## Key Points

- Company: Pelatro Limited (Symbol: PELATRO)
- Number of shares: 190,736 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10 per share
- Issue price: Rs. 367 per share
- Trading platform: SME EMERGE
- Market lot size: 300 shares
- ISIN: INE0VG601013
- Distinctive number range: 10406664 to 10597399

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing process under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading to be conducted using designated security codes only
- Trading in specified lot sizes as per annexure
- Shares currently under temporary ISIN pending activation per SEBI circulars

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: July 22, 2025
- Effective trading date: August 22, 2025
- Lock-in period ends: February 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a small preferential allotment for an SME company. The shares are subject to lock-in restrictions until February 2026, limiting immediate trading volume impact.
