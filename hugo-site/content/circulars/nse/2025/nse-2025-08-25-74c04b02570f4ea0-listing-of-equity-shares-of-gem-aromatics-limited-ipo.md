## Summary

NSE announces the listing of Gem Aromatics Limited equity shares on the Capital Market segment effective August 26, 2025. The company's IPO shares will trade under symbol GEMAROMA with face value Rs. 2 and issue price Rs. 325 per share.

## Key Points

- Trading symbol: GEMAROMA
- ISIN: INE06XZ01023
- Face value: Rs. 2 per share
- IPO issue price: Rs. 325 per share
- Total securities: 52,237,138 equity shares
- Market lot size: 1 share
- Series: EQ (Equity)

## Regulatory Changes

The security will be part of pre-open session for IPO and Other category scrips as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes
- Lock-in periods apply to different tranches of shares as per Annexure I

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 26, 2025
- **Lock-in periods**: Various dates ranging from September 20, 2025 to August 25, 2028
- **Financial Year**: April 2025 - March 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing provides new investment opportunities for market participants. With an issue price of Rs. 325 against face value of Rs. 2, the premium reflects market expectations. Lock-in provisions for promoter shares ensure stability during initial trading period.
