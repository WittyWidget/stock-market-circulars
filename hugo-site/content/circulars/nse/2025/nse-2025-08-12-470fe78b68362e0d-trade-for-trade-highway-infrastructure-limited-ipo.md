## Summary

Highway Infrastructure Limited (HILINFRA) securities will be transferred from the trade for trade segment (series: BE) to the rolling segment (series: EQ) effective August 28, 2025, as per SEBI guidelines.

## Key Points

- Security symbol: HILINFRA (Highway Infrastructure Limited)
- Transfer from trade for trade segment (BE series) to rolling segment (EQ series)
- Change follows SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012
- References previous exchange circular NSE/CML/69608 dated August 11, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

Transfer follows established SEBI guidelines for moving securities between trading segments based on trading behavior and compliance metrics.

## Compliance Requirements

All members must note the segment change and adjust their trading systems accordingly for Highway Infrastructure Limited securities.

## Important Dates

- **August 28, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer from BE to EQ series

## Impact Assessment

The transfer to rolling segment will improve liquidity and trading flexibility for Highway Infrastructure Limited shares, allowing normal settlement cycles instead of trade-for-trade restrictions.
