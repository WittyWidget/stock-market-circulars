## Summary

NSE announces the listing of R&B Denims Limited equity shares under symbol RNBDENIMS on the Capital Market segment effective September 4, 2025. The company's 89,973,440 fully paid-up equity shares with face value of Rs. 2 each will trade in the Normal Market segment with compulsory demat for all investors.

## Key Points

- Trading Symbol: RNBDENIMS
- ISIN: INE012Q01021
- Total shares admitted: 89,973,440
- Face value: Rs. 2 per share
- Market lot: 1 share
- Trading segment: Normal Market (Rolling Settlement)
- Compulsory demat trading for all investors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. Standard listing procedures under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A apply.

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading must be in dematerialized form
- Securities identified by designated code RNBDENIMS
- Trading in specified lot size of 1 share
- Standard rolling settlement applicable

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: September 2, 2025
- Effective Date: September 4, 2025
- Share Holding Pattern filed: August 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine new listing. The stock will be available for trading in the normal market segment with standard settlement procedures. No lock-in restrictions mentioned, indicating shares are freely tradeable from listing date.
