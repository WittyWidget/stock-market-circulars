## Summary

NSE has announced that six schemes of Bandhan US Treasury Bond 0-1 Year Fund of Fund will observe a non-business day on August 25, 2025. All transactions including subscriptions, redemptions, switches, SIPs, STPs, and SWPs will be suspended for these schemes on the NSE MF Invest platform.

## Key Points

- Six Bandhan US Treasury Bond Fund of Fund schemes affected
- Complete suspension of all transaction types on August 25, 2025
- Applies to both Direct and Regular plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- Notification based on communication from Bandhan Mutual Funds

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a routine operational announcement for fund-specific non-business day.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must ensure no transactions are processed for the specified schemes on August 25, 2025
- All transaction types (subscriptions, redemptions, switches, SIPs, STPs, SWPs) must be suspended

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 25, 2025
- **Circular Date**: August 22, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only specific mutual fund schemes for a single day. Investors in these six Bandhan US Treasury Bond Fund of Fund schemes will need to plan transactions accordingly. No impact on equity markets or other mutual fund schemes.
