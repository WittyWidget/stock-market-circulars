## Summary

National Stock Exchange notifies the listing of privately placed debentures of Rare Asset Reconstruction Limited on the capital market segment. The security, RARL 16.50% 2028 Series V, will be admitted to trading effective October 13, 2025, with designated symbol 165RARL28 and market lot size of 1.

## Key Points

- **Issuer**: Rare Asset Reconstruction Limited
- **Security**: RARL 16.50% 2028 Sr V debentures
- **Trading Symbol**: 165RARL28
- **Series**: N0
- **Market Lot Size**: 1 unit
- **Coupon Rate**: 16.5% per annum
- **Maturity Date**: June 30, 2028
- **ISIN Code**: INE03UZ07031
- **Effective Date**: October 13, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code 165RARL28 for system identification
- All trades must be executed in the specified market lot size of 1 unit
- Members should update their systems to reflect the new security listing before October 13, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date**: October 10, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date**: October 13, 2025
- **Security Maturity Date**: June 30, 2028

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact. This is a routine administrative notification for listing of a single privately placed debt security. The listing provides an additional trading avenue for investors holding these debentures. The relatively high coupon rate of 16.5% may attract interest from debt investors seeking higher yields. No immediate action required by general market participants unless specifically interested in this security.
