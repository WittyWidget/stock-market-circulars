## Summary

NSE has revised the minimum gap requirement for monthly frequency Systematic Investment Plans (SIPs) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The minimum gap has been reduced from 30 days to 7 days, providing greater flexibility for investors to start their monthly SIP investments.

## Key Points

- Minimum gap for monthly SIP frequency reduced from 30 days to 7 days
- Change applies to NSE MF Invest Platform
- Effective immediately from September 9, 2025
- All members are required to take note of this change

## Regulatory Changes

- **SIP Minimum Gap Revision**: Monthly SIP frequency minimum gap reduced from 30 days to 7 days
- Change implemented in the SIP Master on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must acknowledge and implement this change
- Update systems to reflect the new 7-day minimum gap for monthly SIPs
- Ensure compliance with revised SIP parameters

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 9, 2025 (immediate effect)
- **Circular Date**: September 9, 2025

## Impact Assessment

- **Positive Impact**: Enhanced flexibility for investors to initiate monthly SIPs with shorter waiting periods
- **Operational Impact**: Members need to update their systems and processes to accommodate the reduced minimum gap
- **Market Impact**: May encourage more frequent SIP participation due to reduced entry barriers
