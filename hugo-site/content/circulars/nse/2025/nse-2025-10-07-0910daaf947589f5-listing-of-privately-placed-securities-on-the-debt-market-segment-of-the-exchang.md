## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed commercial paper securities from six companies to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective October 7, 2025. The securities include commercial paper issues by Godrej Properties Limited, Tata Steel Limited, Indian Oil Corporation Limited, Godrej Industries Limited, Afcons Infrastructure Limited, and L&T Finance Limited, all allotted on October 6, 2025.

## Key Points

- Eight commercial paper securities listed on NSE's Debt Market Segment
- All securities allotted on October 6, 2025
- Trading effective from October 7, 2025
- Total quantity: 126,100 securities with face value of ₹5,00,000 each
- Maturity periods range from 22 days (L&T Finance) to 1 year (Afcons Infrastructure)
- Largest issue: Indian Oil Corporation with 75,000 securities (₹37,500 crore)

## Regulatory Framework

This listing is notified in pursuance of Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities assigned designated security codes as specified
- All securities admitted to dealings on the Debt Market Segment
- Trading commences from October 7, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 6, 2025 (all securities)
- **Listing Date**: October 7, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**: Range from October 28, 2025 to October 6, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative circular with no material impact on equity markets or trading operations. The listing of commercial paper securities in the debt segment provides additional short-term debt instruments for institutional investors but does not affect retail equity trading or market structure.
