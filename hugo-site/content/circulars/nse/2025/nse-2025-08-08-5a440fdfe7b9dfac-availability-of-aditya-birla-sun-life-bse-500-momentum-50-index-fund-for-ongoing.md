## Summary

NSE announces the reopening of Aditya Birla Sun Life BSE 500 Momentum 50 Index Fund for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform effective August 11, 2025. The fund will be available for subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment plans.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening for ongoing transactions from August 11, 2025
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Includes SIP, STP, and SWP facilities
- Four variants available: Regular IDCW, Regular Growth, Direct IDCW, and Direct Growth
- All variants classified under Equity category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements mentioned beyond normal mutual fund transaction processes.

## Important Dates

- **August 11, 2025**: Effective date for reopening of fund transactions

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on mutual fund investors seeking exposure to BSE 500 momentum strategy. Provides additional investment avenue through NSE platform with systematic investment options. Limited broader market impact as it relates to specific fund availability rather than market-wide changes.
