## Summary

NSE will conduct mock bidding sessions on the eOFS (electronic Offer for Sale) platform in live environment on September 6, 2025. Two sessions are scheduled with specific security parameters for three test companies. Participation is encouraged but carries no real obligations.

## Key Points

- Mock sessions scheduled for September 6, 2025
- Two time slots: 11:00 AM to 11:30 AM and 3:00 PM to 4:00 PM
- Live web URL: https://eofs.nseindia.com
- Bidding allowed only at floor price and up to 99.99% of floor price
- Three test securities available: TESTOFS1, TESTOFS2, and TESTOFS3
- No real financial obligations for participants

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced - this is a testing exercise only.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members encouraged to participate actively in mock sessions
- No funds or securities obligations will arise from mock trading
- Members should monitor www.nseindia.com for any revised announcements

## Important Dates

- **September 6, 2025**: Mock bidding sessions
  - Session 1: 11:00 AM to 11:30 AM
  - Session 2: 3:00 PM to 4:00 PM

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is purely a testing exercise. The mock sessions will help members familiarize themselves with the eOFS platform functionality without any real trading consequences. All three test securities have identical parameters with Rs. 100 floor price and Rs. 0.05 tick size.
