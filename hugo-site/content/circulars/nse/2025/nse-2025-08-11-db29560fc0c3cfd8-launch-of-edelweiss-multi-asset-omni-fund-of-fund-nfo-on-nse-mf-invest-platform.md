## Summary

NSE announces the launch of Edelweiss Multi Asset Omni Fund of Fund NFO on NSE MF Invest Platform, available for transactions from August 12, 2025 to August 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- Six variants of Edelweiss Multi Asset Omni Fund of Fund available
- All variants categorized under Equity category
- Transaction period: August 12-26, 2025
- Allotment date: September 3, 2025
- Clear funds must be received before NFO closing date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received in designated Clearing Corporation account on or before NFO closing date
- Funds can be received through any payment mode

## Important Dates

- NFO Start Date: August 12, 2025
- NFO End Date: August 26, 2025
- Allotment Date: September 3, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine NFO launch. Provides additional investment option for investors interested in multi-asset fund of fund strategies through NSE MF platform.
