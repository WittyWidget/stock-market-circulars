## Summary

NSE announces the listing of equity shares of Savy Infra and Logistics Limited on the EMERGE SME Platform effective July 28, 2025. The company will trade under symbol SAVY in the Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (TFTS) with settlement type 'W', with 20,808,480 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value issued at Rs. 120/- per share.

## Key Points

- Company Symbol: SAVY
- ISIN: INE0UCN01017
- Face Value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Issue Price: Rs. 120/- per share
- Total Securities: 20,808,480 equity shares
- Market Lot: 1,200 equity shares
- Trading Series: ST (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment)
- Market Maker: Globalworth Securities Limited
- Registrar: Maashitla Securities Private Limited

## Regulatory Changes

The equity shares will initially trade in Series 'ST' (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment with Settlement Type 'W') and will subsequently be shifted to Series 'SM' (Normal Rolling Segment with Settlement Type 'N') as per SEBI circular no. CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes of 1,200 equity shares
- Security will participate in pre-open session for IPO and Other category scrips
- Market maker maximum permissible spread shall be within 10%
- Continuous market trading as per Exchange Circular No. NSE/CMTR/37202 dated March 14, 2018

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: July 28, 2025
- Circular Date: July 25, 2025
- Financial Year: April 2025-March 2026

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard SME IPO listing that adds a new security to the EMERGE platform. The Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment classification indicates enhanced monitoring measures. The company operates in infrastructure and logistics sector with registered office in Gujarat and corporate office in Mumbai. Market impact is expected to be limited given SME platform scope and surveillance segment classification.
