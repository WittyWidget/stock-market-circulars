## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has issued a circular notifying the suspension of trading in five series of Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs) issued by Sammaan Capital Limited. The suspension is effective from October 20, 2025, and is being implemented in accordance with Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A. All five NCDs are being suspended due to reaching their maturity dates.

## Key Points

- Five series of NCDs issued by Sammaan Capital Limited will be suspended from trading
- Suspension effective date: October 20, 2025
- All suspensions are due to maturity of the debentures
- Action taken under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2
- Circular reference number: 2052/2025, NSE/CML/70894

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard administrative action for matured debt securities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must cease trading in the specified NCDs from October 20, 2025
- No trading orders should be accepted for these securities after the effective date
- Market participants holding these securities should note the maturity-related suspension

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date**: October 17, 2025
- **Effective Date of Suspension**: October 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine suspension affecting only debt securities of one company that have reached maturity. The action is procedural and expected for matured instruments.

**Investor Impact**: Minimal - Investors holding these NCDs will receive their principal and final interest payments as per the maturity terms. The suspension merely prevents further trading of instruments that have completed their lifecycle.

**Operational Impact**: None - Standard process for debenture maturity with no operational disruptions expected for market participants.
