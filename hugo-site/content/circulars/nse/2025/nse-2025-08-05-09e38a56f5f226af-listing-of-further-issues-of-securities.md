## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of further equity shares for 9 companies effective August 6, 2025. These shares are primarily allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and will be available for trading with specified lot sizes and market codes.

## Key Points

- 9 companies approved for listing of additional equity shares
- All shares are allotted under ESOP schemes
- Effective date: August 6, 2025
- Securities will be identified by designated codes for trading
- Most shares have lock-in periods or are not applicable for lock-in

## Companies and Share Details

**Ajanta Pharma Limited (AJANTPHARM)**
- 23,525 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each
- Allotment date: July 28, 2025
- Lock-in until: July 29, 2026

**Angel One Limited (ANGELONE)**
- 92,397 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Allotment date: July 30, 2025
- Lock-in: Not applicable

**Emkay Global Financial Services Limited (EMKAY)**
- 97,810 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each
- Allotment date: July 17, 2025
- Lock-in: Not applicable

**Federal Bank Limited (FEDERALBNK)**
- 168,223 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each
- Allotment date: July 31, 2025
- Lock-in: Not applicable

**Larsen & Toubro Limited (LT)**
- 152,393 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each
- Allotment date: July 29, 2025
- Lock-in: Not applicable

**FSN E-Commerce Ventures Limited (NYKAA)**
- 477,950 equity shares of Re. 1/- each
- Allotment date: July 24, 2025

## Regulatory Requirements

- Securities admitted under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Lot sizes specified under Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- All shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Market lot size of 1 for all listed securities

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 6, 2025
- **Circular Date**: August 5, 2025
- **Various Allotment Dates**: July 17-31, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine ESOP share listings with relatively small quantities compared to existing share capital. The listings provide liquidity for employee stock options and maintain regulatory compliance for the companies involved.
