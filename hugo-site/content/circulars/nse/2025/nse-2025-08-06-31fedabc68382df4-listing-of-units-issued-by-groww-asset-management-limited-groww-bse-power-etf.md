## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of 6,875,558 units of Groww BSE Power ETF (symbol: GROWWPOWER) with effect from August 07, 2025. The ETF units have a face value and issue price of Rs. 10 each, with a market lot size of 1 unit.

## Key Points

- ETF Symbol: GROWWPOWER
- ISIN: INF666M01LM6
- Number of Units: 6,875,558
- Face Value: Rs. 10 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 10 per unit
- Market Lot Size: 1 unit
- Date of Allotment: August 05, 2025
- Asset Management Company: Groww Asset Management Limited
- Trustee: Groww Trustee Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code GROWWPOWER for all transactions
- Trading must comply with the specified lot size of 1 unit
- Market makers appointed: Kanjalochana Finserve Pvt. Ltd (TM code: 90246) and East India Securities (TM code: 08134)

## Important Dates

- Date of Allotment: August 05, 2025
- Listing Effective Date: August 07, 2025
- Circular Effective Date: August 07, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine ETF listing. The listing provides investors access to a power sector focused investment vehicle through NSE's capital market segment. The ETF tracks the BSE Power index and offers diversified exposure to power sector companies.
