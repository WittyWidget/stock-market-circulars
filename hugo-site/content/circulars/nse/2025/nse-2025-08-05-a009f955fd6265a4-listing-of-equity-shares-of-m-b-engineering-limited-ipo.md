## Summary

NSE announces the forthcoming listing of equity shares of M & B Engineering Limited (MBEL) following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company has been assigned the trading symbol MBEL with ISIN INE08N601015.

## Key Points

- Company Name: M & B Engineering Limited
- Trading Symbol: MBEL
- ISIN: INE08N601015
- Listing type: IPO (Initial Public Offering)
- Exact listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are informed about the upcoming listing for their awareness and preparation.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: August 05, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

Low immediate impact as this is a standard new listing announcement. The actual listing date and trading commencement will be communicated separately, allowing market participants to prepare for the new security's trading.
