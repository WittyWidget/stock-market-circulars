## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of additional equity shares for six companies on the Capital Market segment, effective September 02, 2025. All shares were issued under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and will trade without any lock-in restrictions.

## Key Points

- Six companies received approval for listing additional equity shares
- All shares were allotted under ESOP schemes between July-August 2025
- Total of 186,811 new shares to be listed across all companies
- No lock-in restrictions apply to any of the new shares
- Market lot size remains 1 for all securities
- All shares rank pari passu with existing equity shares

## Company Details

**Barbeque Nation Hospitality Limited (BARBEQUE)**
- 5,624 equity shares of Rs. 5/- each
- Allotment date: July 31, 2025
- ISIN: INE382M01027

**Lodha Developers Limited (LODHA)**

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** September 02, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are small quantities of shares being listed under routine ESOP allotments. The shares will be freely tradeable without restrictions and rank equally with existing shares.
