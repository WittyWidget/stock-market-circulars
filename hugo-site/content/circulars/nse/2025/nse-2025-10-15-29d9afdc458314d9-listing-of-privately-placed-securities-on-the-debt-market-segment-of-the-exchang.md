## Summary

NSE has admitted various privately placed securities to dealings on its Debt Market Segment with effect from October 15, 2025. The circular notifies the listing of commercial papers (both fresh issues and re-issues) and other debt securities from multiple issuers including Godrej & Boyce Manufacturing Co Ltd, Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI), Axis Securities Limited, Angel One Limited, Kisetsu Saison Finance (India) Private Limited, and Aditya Birla Housing Finance Limited.

## Key Points

- Four fresh commercial paper issues listed with tenures ranging from 3 to 6 months
- One commercial paper re-issue from Axis Securities Limited
- Two debt securities (debentures) listed with longer tenures (2028 and 2036)
- All securities assigned designated ISIN codes for trading
- Total fresh CP issuance: 35,200 securities worth approximately ₹1,760 crore face value
- Re-issued CP: 300 securities worth ₹15 crore face value
- Debentures issued: 38,000 securities worth ₹380 crore face value

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Market participants can trade these securities on the Debt Market Segment using the designated ISIN codes
- Debentures allotted in dematerialized mode have been credited under temporary ISINs as per SEBI circular SEBI/HO/DDHS/CIR/P/2020/198 dated October 05, 2022
- Issuers must comply with ongoing disclosure and listing obligations for privately placed debt securities

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: October 15, 2025 (listing commencement)
- **Allotment Dates**: October 14-15, 2025
- **CP Redemption Dates**: January 13, 2026 to April 15, 2026
- **Debenture Redemption Dates**: August 11, 2028 and January 21, 2036

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative circular with minimal market impact. The listing provides:

- **Liquidity**: Enhanced secondary market liquidity for these privately placed debt instruments
- **Price Discovery**: Transparent pricing mechanism on the exchange platform
- **Investor Access**: Institutional investors can trade these securities on the debt market segment
- **Market Depth**: Adds to the breadth of debt instruments available for trading

The circular is informational in nature and primarily relevant to debt market participants, institutional investors, and the issuing companies. No impact on equity markets or general trading operations.
