## Summary

NSE has issued a circular notifying members about a scheme name change for ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund on the NSE MF Invest platform. The ICICI Prudential Child Care Fund (Gift Plan) will be renamed to ICICI Prudential Children's Fund effective August 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- Scheme name change affects ICICI Prudential Child Care Fund (Gift Plan)
- New name: ICICI Prudential Children's Fund
- Change applies to NSE MF Invest platform
- All other provisions of the Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM) remain unchanged
- References to the existing name will be replaced with the revised name in due course

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is purely an administrative name change for the mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members are requested to take note of the scheme name change
- All references to the existing scheme name should be updated to the new name
- The notice-cum-addendum forms an integral part of the SID and KIM

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 1, 2025
- **Notice Date**: July 31, 2025
- **ICICI Prudential Notice Date**: July 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a low-impact administrative change with no effect on fund operations, investment strategy, or member trading activities. The change is purely cosmetic to update the scheme name while maintaining all existing fund characteristics and documentation.
