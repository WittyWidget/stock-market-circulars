## Summary

The Groww Nifty Next 50 Index Fund of Groww Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities on NSE MF Invest Platform effective August 26, 2025.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening for ongoing transactions after temporary closure
- Six variants available: Direct and Regular options with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment
- All systematic investment facilities (SIP, STP, SWP) will be available
- Covers equity category index fund tracking Nifty Next 50 index

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational notification regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

Members can facilitate transactions in the specified fund variants through NSE MF Invest Platform from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 26, 2025 - Fund reopens for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine operational matter affecting one specific index fund's transaction availability. Provides investors access to Nifty Next 50 index exposure through Groww Mutual Fund.
