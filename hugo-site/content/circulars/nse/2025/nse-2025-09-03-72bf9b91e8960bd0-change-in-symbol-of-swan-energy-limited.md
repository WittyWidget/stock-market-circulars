## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has announced that Swan Energy Limited's trading symbol will change from SWANENERGY to SWANCORP in the Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme, effective September 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Old symbol: SWANENERGY
- New symbol: SWANCORP
- Change applies to Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme
- All existing contracts will trade under the new symbol
- Reference to previous circular NSE/CML/69907 dated August 29, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

Trading symbol modification in the Securities Lending and Borrowing Scheme as per NSE Clearing Limited directive.

## Compliance Requirements

Participants and custodians must update their systems to recognize the new trading symbol and ensure all contracts transition to the new symbol.

## Important Dates

- **September 4, 2025**: Effective date for symbol change
- All contracts will trade under new symbol SWANCORP from this date

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on market participants who need to update their trading systems and records. The change is administrative and should not affect the underlying security's trading or market operations significantly.
