## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has admitted 1,059 equity shares of Aavas Financiers Limited (Symbol: AAVAS) to trading on the Capital Market segment effective October 17, 2025. These shares were allotted under the company's Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) on October 6, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Aavas Financiers Limited (Symbol: AAVAS)
- Number of securities listed: 1,059 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10 per share
- Paid-up value: Rs. 10 per share
- ISIN: INE216P01012 (Series: EQ)
- Distinctive number range: 79165754 to 79166812
- Market lot: 1 share
- Pari passu: Yes (rank equally with existing shares)
- Lock-in: Not applicable

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code (AAVAS) for trading these securities
- Shares are currently credited under temporary ISIN (IN8*********) in accordance with SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 dated August 02, 2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012 dated September 11, 2012 regarding activation of ISIN for additional issue of shares
- All shares allotted in dematerialized mode only

## Important Dates

- Date of allotment: October 6, 2025
- Circular date: October 16, 2025
- Effective listing date: October 17, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing of ESOP shares with minimal market impact. The quantity of 1,059 shares represents a negligible percentage of the company's outstanding equity, and such listings are standard corporate actions for employee compensation. No changes to trading regulations, lot sizes (remains at 1), or other market parameters. The shares are pari passu with existing equity, meaning they carry identical rights and rank equally with all other outstanding shares of the company.
