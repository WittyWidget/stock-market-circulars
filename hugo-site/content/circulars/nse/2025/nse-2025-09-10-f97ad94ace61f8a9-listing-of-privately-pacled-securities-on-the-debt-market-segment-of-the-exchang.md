## Summary

NSE has admitted 10 new privately placed commercial paper securities to its debt market segment for trading effective September 10, 2025. The securities are issued by 7 companies including Aditya Birla Money Limited, Blue Star Limited, Angel One Limited, Clix Capital Services Private Limited, Tata Steel Limited, Godrej Industries Limited, and Deutsche Investments India Private Limited.

## Key Points

- 10 commercial paper securities admitted to NSE debt market segment
- All securities have face value of â‚¹5,00,000 each
- Total issue size ranges from 500 to 49,000 securities per company
- Maturity periods range from 3 months to 6 months
- All securities were allotted on September 9, 2025
- Trading commences September 10, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. Standard debt market trading rules apply.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 9, 2025 (all securities)
- **Trading Start Date**: September 10, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**: Range from December 5, 2025 to March 16, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Expands debt market trading options for institutional investors. Provides additional short-term investment instruments with varying maturity profiles. No significant impact on broader market operations as these are standard commercial paper listings.
