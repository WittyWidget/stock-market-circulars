## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further issues of equity shares for 11 companies effective August 25, 2025. The securities include shares allotted under ESOP schemes and conversion of warrants issued on preferential basis.

## Key Points

- 11 companies have additional equity shares being listed
- Most allotments are under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP)
- Jyoti Structures shares result from warrant conversion on preferential basis
- All securities will trade with market lot size of 1
- Various lock-in periods apply depending on the nature of allotment

## Company Details

**Aditya Birla Capital Limited (ABCAPITAL)**
- 434,583 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each under ESOP
- Allotment date: August 19, 2025
- No lock-in applicable

**Arvind Limited (ARVIND)**
- 30,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each under ESOP
- Allotment date: August 8, 2025
- No lock-in applicable

**Axis Bank Limited (AXISBANK)**
- 108,502 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each under ESOP
- Allotment date: August 19, 2025
- No lock-in applicable

**Jyoti Structures Limited (JYOTISTRUC)**
- 1,000,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each from warrant conversion
- Issue price: Rs. 13.2
- Allotment date: May 28, 2025
- Lock-in until: February 28, 2026

**Metropolis Healthcare Limited (METROPOLIS)**
- 5,580 equity shares of Rs. 2/- each under ESOP
- Allotment date: July 8, 2025
- Graded lock-in expiry on July 7, 2026

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 25, 2025
- **Circular Date**: August 22, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine listing of additional shares from corporate actions. The share additions represent normal business operations through employee incentive schemes and warrant conversions, with minimal market impact expected given the relatively small quantities compared to existing share capital.
