## Summary

National Stock Exchange has announced the launch of Axis Income Plus Arbitrage Passive Fund of Funds (FOF) New Fund Offer (NFO) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be open for subscriptions from October 28, 2025 to November 11, 2025 until 11:59 PM, with allotment scheduled for November 12, 2025.

## Key Points

- Six scheme variants available: Direct and Regular plans with IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, and Growth options
- All schemes categorized as Debt funds
- NFO period: October 28, 2025 to November 11, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- Allotment date: November 12, 2025
- Payment deadline: November 12, 2025 (12:30 PM)
- Fund house: Axis Mutual Fund
- Circular reference: NSE/NMFTM/70964, 942/2025

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account through any payment mode
- Payment must be received on or before November 12, 2025 till 12:30 PM
- Transactions to be processed through NSE MF Invest Platform only

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: October 28, 2025
- **NFO End Date**: November 11, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: November 12, 2025
- **Payment Deadline**: November 12, 2025 (12:30 PM)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch notification with minimal market impact. It provides investment opportunities for investors seeking debt fund exposure through a fund of funds structure focused on arbitrage strategies. The circular is informational for NSE members operating on the MF Invest Platform and requires standard operational compliance for fund collection and processing. Support is available through toll-free number 1800 2100 940 and email support@nseinvest.com.
