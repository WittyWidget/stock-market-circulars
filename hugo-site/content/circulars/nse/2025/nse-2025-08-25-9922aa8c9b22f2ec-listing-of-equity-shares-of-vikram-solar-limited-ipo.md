## Summary

NSE announces the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Vikram Solar Limited following its IPO. The company will trade under symbol VIKRAMSOLR with ISIN INE078V01014. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Vikram Solar Limited
- Trading Symbol: VIKRAMSOLR
- ISIN: INE078V01014
- Listing type: IPO (Initial Public Offering)
- Exchange: National Stock Exchange of India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are informed about the upcoming listing. No specific compliance requirements mentioned beyond awareness.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: August 25, 2025
- Listing Date: To be informed through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This listing will add a new equity security to NSE's trading platform. Impact is company-specific and limited to Vikram Solar Limited stakeholders and potential investors. The solar energy sector may see increased trading activity upon listing.
