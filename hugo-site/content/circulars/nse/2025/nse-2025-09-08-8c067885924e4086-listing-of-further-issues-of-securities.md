## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further securities for 15 companies effective September 09, 2025. These securities are primarily equity shares allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) with face values ranging from Re. 1 to Rs. 10.

## Key Points

- 15 companies getting further securities listed on NSE Capital Market segment
- All securities are equity shares allotted under ESOP schemes
- Trading effective from September 09, 2025
- Securities identified by designated codes with specified lot sizes
- All shares are pari passu with existing shares
- No lock-in restrictions applicable

## Companies and Securities Details

**Major Companies Include:**
- **3i Infotech Limited (3IINFOLTD)**: 49,500 equity shares of Rs. 10 each
- **Aditya Birla Capital Limited (ABCAPITAL)**: 260,685 equity shares of Rs. 10 each
- **Angel One Limited (ANGELONE)**: 30,418 equity shares of Rs. 10 each
- **Ather Energy Limited (ATHERENERG)**: 6,513,163 equity shares of Re. 1 each
- **Abans Financial Services Limited (AFSL)**: 30,367 equity shares of Rs. 2 each
- **Avalon Technologies Limited (AVALON)**: Details partially provided

**Other Companies:** Capital Small Finance Bank Limited, Go Digit General Insurance Limited, Godrej Properties Limited, Lokesh Machines Limited, MphasiS Limited, Pondy Oxides & Chemicals Limited, TATA CONSUMER PRODUCTS LIMITED, Tata Elxsi Limited, and VIP Industries Limited

## Regulatory Framework

- Issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Lot sizes specified under Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Trading Regulations
- Securities to be identified only by designated codes for trading

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: September 08, 2025
- **Effective Date**: September 09, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: Range from August 29, 2025 to September 02, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine administrative listing of ESOP-based equity shares with minimal market impact. Increases tradeable float for affected companies and provides liquidity to employee shareholders. No significant operational changes for trading members or market participants.
