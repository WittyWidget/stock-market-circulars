## Summary

NSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debenture (Privately Placed) of Alpha Alternatives Financial Services Private Limited effective August 19, 2025. The suspension is due to the redemption of the debenture.

## Key Points

- Company: Alpha Alternatives Financial Services Private Limited
- ISIN: INE0L6807039
- Suspension effective from August 19, 2025
- Reason: Redemption of the debenture
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine suspension under existing NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must cease trading in the specified debenture from the suspension date
- Members should update their trading systems accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: August 18, 2025
- **Suspension Effective Date**: August 19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves suspension of a single privately placed debenture due to normal redemption process. No broader market implications expected.
