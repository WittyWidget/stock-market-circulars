## Summary

Vijaypd Ceutical Limited's equity shares will be admitted to trading on the National Stock Exchange EMERGE SME Platform effective October 07, 2025. The company has issued 19,528,686 equity shares at Rs. 35/- per share with a face value of Rs. 10/-. Trading will commence in Series 'ST' (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment with Settlement Type 'W') and will subsequently shift to Series 'SM' (Normal Rolling Segment with Settlement Type 'N').

## Key Points

- Symbol: VIJAYPD
- ISIN: INE0WL901019
- Total securities: 19,528,686 equity shares
- Face value: Rs. 10/- per share
- Issue price: Rs. 35/- per share
- Market lot size: 4,000 equity shares
- Market maker maximum permissible spread: Within 10%
- Trading type: Continuous Market
- Initial trading series: ST (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment - Settlement Type 'W')
- Distinctive number range: 1 to 19,528,686

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This circular implements existing regulations:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A for listing
- Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A for lot sizes
- SEBI circular no. CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012 for pre-open session
- Exchange Circular No. NSE/CMTR/37202 dated March 14, 2018 for continuous market trading
