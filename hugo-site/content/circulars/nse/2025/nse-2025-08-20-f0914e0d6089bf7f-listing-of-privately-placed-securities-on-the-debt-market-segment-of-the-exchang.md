## Summary

NSE has listed 10 commercial papers from various companies on its debt market segment effective August 20, 2025. The securities include issues from financial services companies, housing finance firms, and industrial companies with redemption periods ranging from November 2025 to February 2026.

## Key Points

- 10 commercial papers admitted to debt market segment trading
- Total value of securities listed exceeds ₹37,500 crores (75,000 securities × ₹5 lakh face value)
- All securities allotted on August 19-20, 2025
- Redemption dates range from November 14, 2025 to February 18, 2026
- Face value of ₹5,00,000 per security for all listed instruments

## Companies and Securities Listed

- **Tata Capital Housing Finance Limited**: 5,000 securities, redemption Nov 18, 2025
- **CEAT Limited**: 1,000 securities, redemption Nov 18, 2025
- **Aditya Birla Money Limited**: 2,000 securities, redemption Nov 18, 2025
- **ICICI Securities Limited**: 12,000 securities, redemption Nov 18, 2025
- **Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited**: 11,000 securities, redemption Nov 18, 2025
- **Godrej Industries Limited**: 1,500 securities, redemption Nov 18, 2025
- **Cholamandalam Investment and Finance Company Limited**: 22,000 securities, redemption Feb 18, 2026
- **Kotak Securities Limited**: 7,500 securities, redemption Nov 19, 2025
- **Jio Credit Limited**: 10,500 securities, redemption Nov 14, 2025
- **Angel One Limited**: 5,000 securities, redemption Nov 19, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 20, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: August 19-20, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**: November 14, 2025 to February 18, 2026

## Impact Assessment

This listing provides additional debt market liquidity and investment options for institutional investors. The diverse range of issuers from financial services, housing finance, and industrial sectors offers portfolio diversification opportunities in the short-term debt market.
