## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of equity shares of Laxmi India Finance Limited following its IPO, effective from August 05, 2025. The company's shares will trade under symbol LAXMIINDIA with ISIN INE06WU01026, face value Rs. 5/-, and IPO issue price of Rs. 158/-.

## Key Points

- Total equity shares: 52,267,875 fully paid-up shares of Rs. 5/- each
- Trading symbol: LAXMIINDIA
- ISIN: INE06WU01026
- IPO issue price: Rs. 158/-
- Market lot size: 1 share
- Shares will be part of pre-open session for IPO category
- Various lock-in periods apply to different share tranches

## Regulatory Changes

None specified - standard IPO listing procedure under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes
- Shares subject to pre-open session requirements per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: August 05, 2025
- Lock-in periods vary:
  - Some shares locked until February 05, 2026
  - Some shares locked until February 05, 2027
  - Some shares locked until August 30, 2025
  - Some shares locked until October 29, 2025
  - Some shares are free from lock-in

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact as this introduces a new equity security to NSE trading. The listing adds liquidity to the market and provides investment opportunities. The company operates in the finance sector with registered office in Jaipur, Rajasthan. Registrar services provided by MUFG Intime India Private Limited.
