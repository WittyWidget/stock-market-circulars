## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further equity shares of Inox Wind Limited pursuant to a rights issue. A total of 104,110,712 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each were allotted at an issue price of Rs. 120 per share on August 21, 2025, and will be available for trading from August 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- 104,110,712 new equity shares of Inox Wind Limited to be listed
- Face value: Rs. 10 per share
- Issue price: Rs. 120 per share
- Allotment date: August 21, 2025
- Trading symbol: INOXWIND
- ISIN: INE066P01011
- Market lot size: 1
- Distinctive number range: 2065200599 to 2169311310

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard listing procedure under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulations 3.1.1 and 2.5.5.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading participants must use designated security codes for system identification
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes
- Shares currently credited under temporary ISIN as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/21/2012 and CIR/MRD/DP/24/2012

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 21, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 25, 2025
- **Circular Effective Date**: August 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The rights issue significantly increases Inox Wind's equity base with over 104 million new shares. This will impact the company's market capitalization and may affect trading volumes. Existing shareholders who participated in the rights issue will see their holdings reflected in trading from August 25, 2025. The shares are pari passu with existing equity shares and have no lock-in restrictions.
