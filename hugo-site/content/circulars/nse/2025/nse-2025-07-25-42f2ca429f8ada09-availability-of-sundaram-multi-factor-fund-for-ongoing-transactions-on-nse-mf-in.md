## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has announced that Sundaram Multi-Factor Fund will be available for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform starting July 28, 2025. The fund will support subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment facilities including SIP, STP, and SWP across six different scheme variants.

## Key Points

- Sundaram Multi-Factor Fund reopening for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Effective date: July 28, 2025
- Six scheme variants available: Direct and Regular options with IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout, and Growth plans
- All schemes categorized under Equity category
- Support for SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan)
- Complete ISIN codes provided for all six scheme variants

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a platform availability notification for an existing mutual fund scheme.

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members informed about fund availability
- Members can facilitate transactions for investors starting July 28, 2025
- Standard mutual fund transaction compliance applies

## Important Dates

- **July 28, 2025**: Effective date for fund availability on NSE MF Invest Platform
- **July 25, 2025**: Circular issue date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update with minimal market impact. The availability of Sundaram Multi-Factor Fund on NSE's platform provides additional investment options for retail investors but does not affect broader market operations or regulatory framework. The impact is limited to Sundaram Mutual Fund investors and NSE MF platform users.
