## Summary

NSE announces the introduction of futures and options contracts on Sammaan Capital Limited (SAMMAANCAP) effective August 29, 2025, subject to SEBI approval and fulfillment of Quarter sigma computation cycle eligibility criteria for August 2025.

## Key Points

- New F&O contracts on SAMMAANCAP will be available for trading from August 29, 2025
- Introduction based on SEBI stock selection criteria per circular SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD-2/P/CIR/2024/116 dated August 30, 2024
- Market lot and strike scheme details to be communicated on August 28, 2025
- Contract files and quantity freeze details will be updated before trading commencement

## Regulatory Changes

Expansion of derivative trading universe to include Sammaan Capital Limited based on SEBI's prescribed stock selection criteria.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must load updated contract.gz and NSE_FO_contract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz files in trading applications before August 29, 2025
- Files available from faoftp/faocommon directory on Extranet server
- MII contract files accessible at https://www.nseindia.com/all-reports-derivatives

## Important Dates

- **August 28, 2025**: Market lot and strike scheme details to be communicated
- **August 29, 2025**: F&O contracts on SAMMAANCAP become available for trading

## Impact Assessment

Provides additional derivative trading opportunities for market participants interested in Sammaan Capital Limited. Limited impact as it involves only one stock addition to the F&O segment.
