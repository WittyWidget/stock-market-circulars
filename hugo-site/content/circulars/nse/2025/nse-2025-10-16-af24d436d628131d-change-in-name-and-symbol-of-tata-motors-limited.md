## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced that Tata Motors Limited will undergo a name and symbol change effective October 24, 2025. The company's trading symbol will change from TATAMOTORS to TMPV, and the company name will change from "Tata Motors Limited" to "Tata Motors Passenger Vehicles Limited".

## Key Points

- Current trading symbol TATAMOTORS will be replaced with new symbol TMPV
- Company name changing from "Tata Motors Limited" to "Tata Motors Passenger Vehicles Limited"
- Changes will be effective from October 24, 2025
- All exchange members have been notified of this change
- Circular reference number: 2041/2025
- NSE circular reference: NSE/CML/70875

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a corporate action involving rebranding and identity change of the listed entity.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their systems to reflect the new symbol TMPV effective October 24, 2025
- All trading and settlement systems should be configured for the symbol transition
- Market participants should update company name references in their records and systems
- Investors and traders should note the symbol change to avoid confusion in trading activities

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 24, 2025 - Name and symbol change becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: High - This change affects one of India's largest and most widely traded automotive stocks. The symbol change from TATAMOTORS to TMPV may cause temporary confusion among retail investors and requires updates across trading platforms, watchlists, and market data systems.

**Operational Impact**: All brokers, trading members, and market infrastructure providers must update their systems before October 24, 2025. The name change to "Tata Motors Passenger Vehicles Limited" suggests a strategic repositioning or corporate restructuring, potentially indicating a focus on the passenger vehicle business segment.

**Investor Impact**: Existing shareholders are not required to take any action, but should be aware of the new symbol for trading purposes. Historical data and charts will need to be accessed using the appropriate symbol based on the date.
