## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Canara Robeco Asset Management Company Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol CRAMC with ISIN INE218I01013. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Canara Robeco Asset Management Company Limited
- Trading Symbol: CRAMC
- ISIN: INE218I01013
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/70820, dated October 15, 2025
- Circular Number: 2020/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational circular regarding a new listing.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: October 15, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This listing adds a new security in the asset management sector to the NSE platform. Canara Robeco Asset Management Company Limited's listing will provide investors with exposure to the mutual fund and asset management industry. The impact is moderate as it expands investment opportunities in the financial services sector, though it does not affect existing market operations or trading rules.
