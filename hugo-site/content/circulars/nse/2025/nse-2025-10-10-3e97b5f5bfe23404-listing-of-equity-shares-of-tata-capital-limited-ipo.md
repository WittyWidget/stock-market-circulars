## Summary

National Stock Exchange has notified the listing of equity shares of Tata Capital Limited on the Capital Market segment effective October 13, 2025. The company has issued 4,244,869,037 equity shares of Rs. 10/- face value at an issue price of Rs. 326/- per share. The security will trade under the symbol TATACAP with ISIN INE976I01016 and will be part of the pre-open session for IPO category scrips.

## Key Points

- **Symbol**: TATACAP
- **ISIN**: INE976I01016
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10/-
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 326/-
- **Total Shares**: 4,244,869,037 equity shares
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Series**: EQ (Equity)
- **Trading Start Date**: October 13, 2025
- **Pre-open Session**: Applicable as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify the security by its designated code (TATACAP) for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 share
- The security will participate in the pre-open session designated for IPO and other category scrips
- Lock-in periods apply to certain share categories as specified in Annexure I

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: October 10, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 13, 2025 (trading commencement)
- **Financial Year**: April 2025 - March 2026
- **Lock-in Expiry Dates** (for applicable shares):
  - November 7, 2025 (for specific share blocks)
  - January 6, 2026 (for specific share blocks)
  - April 10, 2026 (for anchor investor shares)
  - April 10, 2027 (for promoter shares)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The listing of Tata Capital Limited, a major non-banking financial company under the Tata Group, adds a significant financial services player to the exchange. With an issue size of approximately Rs. 138,054 crore at issue price (4.24 billion shares Ã— Rs. 326), this represents one of the larger IPOs.

**Investor Impact**: The diverse lock-in structure shows different categories of shareholders including promoters (lock-in until April 2027), anchor investors (lock-in until April 2026), and free-floating shares. Approximately 67.6 million shares (1.59% of total) are immediately available for free trading.

**Operational Impact**: Members must update their trading systems to include the new security code and comply with pre-open session requirements for IPO category scrips.
