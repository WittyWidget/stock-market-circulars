## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed commercial paper securities from 10 companies on the Debt Market Segment effective October 28, 2025. The circular lists securities from EPL Limited, Godrej Industries Limited, Godrej Properties Limited, HDFC Securities Limited, Nxtra Data Limited, Blue Star Limited, Barclays Investments & Loans (India) Private Limited, and Hero Housing Finance Limited with their respective security codes, ISINs, and redemption dates.

## Key Points

- 13 commercial paper securities admitted to debt market segment
- All securities allotted on October 27, 2025
- Effective listing date: October 28, 2025
- Face value of all securities: Rs. 500,000
- Redemption dates range from January 2026 to October 2026
- Total quantity across all issues: 20,000 securities
- Securities issued by EPL Limited (1,000 units), Godrej Industries (1,500 units), Godrej Properties (4,000 units), HDFC Securities (2,000 units), Nxtra Data (5,000 units), Blue Star (2,000 units), Barclays Investments & Loans (1,500 units), and Hero Housing Finance (2,000 units)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine notification under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from market participants. This is an informational circular notifying members of newly listed securities available for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: October 27, 2025 (all securities)
- **Listing Date**: October 28, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**:
  - January 20, 2026: GIL CP Sr 104
  - January 22, 2026: GPL CP Sr 80, NDL CP
  - January 23, 2026: EPL CP, GPL CP Sr 81, HSL CP, BSL CP Sr 23, HHFL CP Sr 8
  - June 5, 2026: BILIPL CP Sr 550
  - October 27, 2026: HHFL CP Sr 9

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a standard operational notification for debt market participants. The listing of these commercial paper securities expands the available debt instruments for trading but does not affect equity markets or broader market operations. Impact limited to debt market participants and the specific issuers mentioned.
