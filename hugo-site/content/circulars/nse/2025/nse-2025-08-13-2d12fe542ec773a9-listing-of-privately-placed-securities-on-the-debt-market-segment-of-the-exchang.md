## Summary

NSE has admitted various privately placed securities to the debt market segment for trading effective August 13, 2025. The circular lists fresh issues and re-issues of commercial papers and other debt securities from multiple companies including The India Cements Limited, Godrej Industries Limited, NHPC Limited, and others.

## Key Points

- Multiple commercial papers and debt securities admitted for trading on NSE debt market
- Fresh issues include commercial papers from TICL and GIL, debt securities from Alpha Alternatives and NHPC
- Re-issues include commercial papers from Barclays Investments and Kotak Securities
- All securities effective for trading from August 13, 2025
- Securities assigned specific ISIN codes and security descriptions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced - this is a routine listing notification under existing NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations 3.1.1.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants - this is an informational circular about new securities available for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 13, 2025 - Securities admitted to trading
- **Allotment Dates**: August 8-13, 2025 for various securities
- **Redemption Dates**: Range from November 11, 2025 to November 8, 2029

## Impact Assessment

Provides additional debt market trading opportunities for investors and market participants. Expands the universe of tradeable debt securities on NSE platform, supporting market liquidity and investment options in corporate debt instruments.
