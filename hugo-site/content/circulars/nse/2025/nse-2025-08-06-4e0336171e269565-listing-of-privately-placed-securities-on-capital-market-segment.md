## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of privately placed debt securities from two companies on the capital market segment. IIFL Home Finance Limited's 8.00% Senior Debentures maturing in 2027 and Summit Digitel Infrastructure Limited's 7.1450% Debentures maturing in 2032 will commence trading from August 7, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two new debt securities admitted to NSE capital market segment
- Trading commences August 7, 2025
- Both securities have market lot size of 1
- Securities identified by designated codes for trading purposes

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for system trading
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes
- Compliance with Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 7, 2025
- **IIFL Maturity**: August 4, 2027
- **Summit Digitel Maturity**: August 5, 2032

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves routine listing of privately placed debt securities. The addition provides investors with two new fixed-income investment options with different maturity profiles and coupon rates. IIFL offers higher coupon (8.00%) with shorter tenure, while Summit Digitel offers lower coupon (7.1450%) with longer tenure.
