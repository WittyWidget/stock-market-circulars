## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and trading of equity shares of HDFC Bank Limited pursuant to a bonus issue. The new shares will be available for trading on NSE Capital Market segment from August 29, 2025. A total of 7,67,70,39,761 new equity shares will be listed with distinctive number range 8841665596 to 16518705356.

## Key Points

- HDFC Bank Limited bonus issue shares to be listed on NSE
- Symbol: HDFCBANK
- ISIN: INE040A01034
- Total new shares: 7,67,70,39,761
- Distinctive number range: 8841665596 to 16518705356
- Securities identified by designated codes for trading

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned. This is a standard listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.1.

## Compliance Requirements

- All members must use designated security codes for trading
- Securities to be identified only by designated codes on trading system
- Compliance with NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Important Dates

- Record Date: August 27, 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: August 28, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 29, 2025
- Circular Effective Date: August 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

High market impact expected due to HDFC Bank's significant market presence and the large number of bonus shares being issued. The bonus issue will increase the total number of tradeable shares substantially, potentially affecting liquidity and trading volumes. Investors holding HDFC Bank shares as of record date will receive additional shares as per the bonus ratio.
