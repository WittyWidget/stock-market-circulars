## Summary

NSE introduces Futures and Options contracts on Sammaan Capital Limited (SAMMAANCAP) effective August 29, 2025. The security will have a lot size of 4,300 shares with specific strike schemes and tick sizes defined.

## Key Points

- New F&O contracts for Sammaan Capital Limited (SAMMAANCAP)
- Lot size: 4,300 shares
- Trading commencement: August 29, 2025
- Futures contracts: Same tick size as underlying CM segment
- Options contracts: Tick size of Re 0.05
- Strike scheme provided in Annexure A with narrow and wide range specifications

## Regulatory Changes

- Addition of SAMMAANCAP to F&O segment
- Implementation of specific strike schemes with step values ranging from 2.5 to 10
- Reserve strike mechanisms defined for different contract periods

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must load updated contract.gz and NSE_FO_contract_ddmmyyyy.csv.gz files before trading
- Contract files available from faoftp/faocommon directory on Extranet server
- Alternative access via NSE website at derivatives reports section
- Quantity freeze details to be referenced from contract file

## Important Dates

- **August 29, 2025**: Trading commencement for SAMMAANCAP F&O contracts
- Contract files must be updated before effective trading date

## Impact Assessment

- Expands derivative trading opportunities for market participants
- Provides hedging and speculation tools for Sammaan Capital Limited
- Requires technical updates to trading systems for proper contract handling
- Limited market impact as it affects only one new security addition
