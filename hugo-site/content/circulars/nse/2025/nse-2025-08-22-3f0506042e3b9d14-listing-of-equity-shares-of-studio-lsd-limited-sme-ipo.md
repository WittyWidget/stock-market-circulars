## Summary

Studio LSD Limited has completed its SME IPO and submitted its post-issue shareholding pattern as of August 21, 2025. The company has filed the shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) of SEBI LODR Regulations 2015, showing promoter and promoter group holding with locked-in shares but no pledged shares or other encumbrances.

## Key Points

- Company: Studio LSD Limited
- Security type: Equity Shares
- Filing date: Post-IPO as of August 21, 2025
- Promoter & Promoter Group: 3 shareholders holding 3,760 shares
- No partly paid-up shares, convertible securities, or warrants issued
- No ESOPs outstanding or depository receipts issued
- Locked-in shares present for promoter group
- No pledged shares or non-disposal undertakings

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a standard post-IPO compliance filing.

## Compliance Requirements

- Shareholding pattern filed under Regulation 31 of SEBI LODR Regulations 2015
- Company required to declare various securities-related disclosures
- Standard post-IPO disclosure requirements fulfilled

## Important Dates

- Shareholding pattern as on: August 21, 2025
- Filing type: Post Issue of IPO

## Impact Assessment

Routine compliance disclosure for newly listed SME company. Standard shareholding pattern submission with typical post-IPO structure showing promoter holdings with locked-in provisions. No unusual encumbrances or complex securities structures identified.
