## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of Hero FinCorp Limited's privately placed debt securities on the capital market segment. The listing includes 7.99% Senior bonds maturing in August 2027, effective from August 08, 2025.

## Key Points

- Hero FinCorp Limited's debt securities admitted to NSE Capital Market segment
- Security symbol: 799HFL27, Series: N0
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Trading to commence from August 08, 2025

## Security Details

- **Issue Description**: HFL 7.99% 2027 Sr 073
- **Coupon Rate**: 7.99%
- **Maturity Date**: August 06, 2027
- **ISIN Code**: INE957N07849
- **Trading Symbol**: 799HFL27
- **Series**: N0
- **Market Lot**: 1

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 08, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: August 06, 2027

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities identified by designated codes for trading
- Trading to be conducted in specified lot sizes
- Compliance with NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of privately placed debt securities from Hero FinCorp Limited. The listing expands available debt instruments for institutional and qualified investors in the capital market segment.
