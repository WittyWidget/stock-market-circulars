## Summary

NSE announces the listing of further equity shares for 9 companies effective July 31, 2025. The listings include shares allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and preferential issues, with securities to be traded using designated codes and specified lot sizes.

## Key Points

- 9 companies have further equity shares admitted for trading on NSE Capital Market segment
- Most shares are allotted under ESOP schemes with no lock-in restrictions
- Emmbi Industries has preferential issue shares with lock-in until March 15, 2027
- All securities will trade in market lot of 1 share
- Trading effective from July 31, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities to be identified only by designated codes for trading
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes
- Market participants must update their systems for new listings

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: July 31, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (Emmbi Industries only)**: March 15, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine further issues under ESOP and preferential allotments. The additional shares represent relatively small quantities compared to existing paid-up capital for most companies. Only Emmbi Industries shares have lock-in restrictions, while others are freely tradeable.
