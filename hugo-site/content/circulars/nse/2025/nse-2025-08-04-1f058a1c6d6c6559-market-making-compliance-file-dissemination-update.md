## Summary

NSE has issued an update regarding market making compliance file dissemination procedures. This circular provides revised guidelines for trading members and market makers regarding the submission and dissemination of compliance-related documentation.

## Key Points

- Updated procedures for market making compliance file submissions
- Revised dissemination mechanisms for compliance documentation
- Enhanced reporting requirements for market makers
- Modified timelines for compliance file submissions

## Regulatory Changes

The circular introduces updates to existing market making compliance framework, focusing on:
- File dissemination protocols
- Documentation requirements
- Submission procedures
- Compliance monitoring mechanisms

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must adhere to updated file dissemination procedures
- Market makers required to follow revised compliance documentation standards
- Timely submission of required compliance files
- Maintenance of proper documentation as per updated guidelines

## Important Dates

- Circular issued: August 4, 2025
- Implementation details as specified in the circular

## Impact Assessment

This update affects trading members and market makers who must adjust their compliance procedures according to the revised dissemination requirements. The changes are primarily procedural and aimed at improving the efficiency and transparency of market making compliance processes.
