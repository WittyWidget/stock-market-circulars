## Summary

NSE announces the listing of units issued by Angel One Asset Management Company Limited (Angel One Gold ETF) effective September 10, 2025. The ETF will trade under symbol AONEGOLD with 6,870,634 units having face value and paid-up value of Rs. 10/- each.

## Key Points

- Symbol: AONEGOLD
- ISIN: INF1J2R01114
- Total units: 6,870,634
- Face value per unit: Rs. 10/-
- Issue price: Rs. 10/-
- Market lot: 1 unit
- Date of allotment: September 5, 2025
