## Summary

NSE announces the listing of equity shares of M & B Engineering Limited (MBEL) on the Capital Market segment effective August 06, 2025. The company's IPO shares will trade at Rs. 385/- issue price with symbol MBEL and ISIN INE08N601015.

## Key Points

- Trading symbol: MBEL
- ISIN: INE08N601015
- Face value: Rs. 10/-
- Issue price: Rs. 385/-
- Total securities: 57,148,215 equity shares
- Market lot: 1 share
- Series: EQ (Equity)
- Pre-open session applicable for IPO category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. Standard listing procedure under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Company must comply with SEBI LODR regulations
- Registrar: MUFG Intime India Private Limited
- Company Secretary: Ms. Palak Dilipbhai Parekh
- Financial year: April 2025-March 2026

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 06, 2025
- **Lock-in periods**: Varying from September 2025 to August 2028 for different share categories
- **Circular Date**: August 05, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - New equity listing provides additional investment opportunity in engineering sector. Company operates from Ahmedabad with established business operations.

**Trading Impact**: Shares will participate in pre-open session for IPO category as per SEBI guidelines. Standard trading rules and lot size of 1 share applicable.
