## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of further equity shares of Committed Cargo Care Limited on the SME EMERGE platform. The shares were allotted on preferential basis pursuant to conversion of warrants and will be available for trading from August 11, 2025.

## Key Points

- Total 575,000 equity shares to be listed (525,000 + 50,000)
- Shares allotted through warrant conversion on preferential basis
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 10 each
- Issue price: Rs. 81 per share
- Market lot size: 800 shares
- Symbol: COMMITTED
- ISIN: INE597Z01014

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members to use designated security codes for transactions
- Trading permitted only in specified lot sizes of 800 shares
- Securities currently in dematerialized mode under temporary ISIN pending activation

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 11, 2025
- **First Allotment Date**: May 29, 2025 (525,000 shares)
- **Second Allotment Date**: June 18, 2025 (50,000 shares)
- **Lock-in Expiry**: February 15, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves a small SME company with limited trading volume. The additional shares increase the company's free float but remain subject to lock-in restrictions until February 2026, limiting immediate trading impact.
