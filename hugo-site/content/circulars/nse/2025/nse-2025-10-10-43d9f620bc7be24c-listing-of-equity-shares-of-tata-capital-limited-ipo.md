## Summary

NSE has announced the forthcoming listing of Tata Capital Limited equity shares on the exchange following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company has been assigned the trading symbol TATACAP with ISIN INE976I01016. The exact date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Tata Capital Limited equity shares will be listed on NSE
- Trading symbol assigned: TATACAP
- ISIN: INE976I01016
- This is an IPO listing
- Specific listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational circular notifying members about the upcoming listing.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: October 10, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This listing announcement has moderate market impact as it involves a Tata group company entering the public markets. Members and market participants should note the new trading symbol TATACAP for when trading commences. The listing will expand the universe of tradeable securities on NSE and may attract investor interest given the Tata group's market reputation.
