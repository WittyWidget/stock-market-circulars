## Summary

The National Stock Exchange announces that Jindal Steel & Power Limited (symbol: JINDALSTEL) will change its name to Jindal Steel Limited effective August 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company symbol remains unchanged: JINDALSTEL
- Old name: Jindal Steel & Power Limited
- New name: Jindal Steel Limited
- Change effective from August 29, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes - this is a corporate name change notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance actions required from members beyond noting the name change.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is purely an administrative name change. Trading symbol JINDALSTEL remains unchanged, ensuring continuity in trading operations.
