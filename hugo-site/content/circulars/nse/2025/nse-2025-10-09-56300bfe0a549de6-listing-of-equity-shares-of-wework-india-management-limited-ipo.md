## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of WeWork India Management Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company has been assigned the trading symbol 'WEWORK' with ISIN INE085001019. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: WeWork India Management Limited
- Trading Symbol: WEWORK
- ISIN: INE085001019
- Listing Type: IPO (Initial Public Offering)
- Listing date to be announced via separate circular
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/70715, Circular No. 1977/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced by this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for members. This is an informational circular about a forthcoming listing.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: October 09, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This circular has medium market impact as it introduces a new equity security for trading on NSE. WeWork India Management Limited will become available for trading once the listing date is announced. Members and investors should watch for the follow-up circular announcing the specific listing date. The security will expand the universe of tradeable stocks in the market.
