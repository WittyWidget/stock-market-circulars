## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India Limited has announced the suspension of trading in 6 non-convertible debentures (NCDs) issued by Sammaan Capital Limited, effective from October 27, 2025. The suspension is being implemented due to the maturity of these debt securities, as per NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2.

## Key Points

- 6 NCDs of Sammaan Capital Limited to be suspended from trading
- Suspension effective date: October 27, 2025
- Reason: Maturity of the debt securities
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/70930, dated October 23, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This action is taken in pursuance of existing NSE (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members and market participants should note the suspension and cease trading in the specified NCDs from October 27, 2025
- Holders of these NCDs should coordinate with the issuer for redemption procedures upon maturity

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** October 23, 2025
- **Effective Date:** October 27, 2025 (suspension becomes effective)

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative action with minimal market impact. The suspension is due to natural maturity of the debt instruments rather than any default or regulatory concern. Impact is limited to holders of these specific NCDs who will need to follow standard redemption procedures. No effect on Sammaan Capital Limited's other securities or broader debt market operations.
