## Summary

Bank of India Mutual Fund is launching the Bank of India Mid Cap Fund NFO on NSE MF Invest Platform. The fund will be available for transactions from July 31, 2025, to August 14, 2025, till 11:59 PM, with an allotment date of August 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- NFO period: July 31, 2025 to August 14, 2025 (till 11:59 PM)
- Six scheme variants available (Direct/Regular with Growth/IDCW options)
- All schemes fall under Equity category
- Systematic registrations (SIP, STP, SWP) available only after NFO reopening
- Clear funds must be received before NFO closing date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in designated Clearing Corporation account on or before NFO closing date
- Members responsible for facilitating transactions during NFO period

## Important Dates

- NFO Start Date: July 31, 2025
- NFO End Date: August 14, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- Allotment Date: August 22, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine NFO launch. The fund expands investment options for mid-cap equity exposure through NSE's mutual fund platform. No impact on existing securities or trading operations.
