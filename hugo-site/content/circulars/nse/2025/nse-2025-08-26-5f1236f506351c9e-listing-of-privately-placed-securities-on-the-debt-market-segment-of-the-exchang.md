## Summary

NSE has admitted 10 privately placed commercial papers from various companies to dealings on the Debt Market Segment with effect from August 26, 2025. The securities include commercial papers from IIFL Finance, Tata Capital, Godrej Properties, CEAT, Sundaram Finance, Angel One, Piramal Enterprises, Aditya Birla Capital, Godrej Agrovet, and Piramal Finance.

## Key Points

- 10 commercial papers admitted to NSE debt market segment
- All securities allotted on August 25, 2025
- Trading commences August 26, 2025
- Maturity periods range from October 2025 to August 2026
- Face value of â‚¹5,00,000 per security across all issues
- Total quantity of 50,800 securities across all companies

## Securities Details

### Short-term (3-4 months)
- CEAT Limited: 1,000 securities, maturing October 29, 2025
- IIFL Finance, Godrej Properties, Aditya Birla Capital, Godrej Agrovet: maturing November 24, 2025

### Medium-term (6 months)
- Angel One, Tata Capital, Piramal Enterprises, Piramal Finance: maturing February-March 2026

### Long-term (12 months)
- Sundaram Finance: 14,000 securities, maturing August 24, 2026

## Regulatory Framework

Listing conducted under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: August 25, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: August 26, 2025
- **Maturity Dates**: October 29, 2025 to August 24, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Routine listing announcement with minimal market impact. Provides additional investment options for debt market participants and enhances liquidity in the commercial paper segment.
