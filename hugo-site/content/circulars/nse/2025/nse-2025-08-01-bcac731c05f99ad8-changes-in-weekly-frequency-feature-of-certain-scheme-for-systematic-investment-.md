## Summary

Sundaram Mutual Fund has modified the weekly frequency feature for Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) and Systematic Transfer Plan (STP) on NSE MF Invest Platform. The change expands processing from Wednesdays only to all working days of the week, effective August 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Weekly SIP/STP requests previously processed only on Wednesdays
- New system will process weekly requests on all working days (Monday to Friday)
- Change applies to all schemes of Sundaram Mutual Fund on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Modification affects Scheme Information Document (SID) and Key Information Memorandum (KIM)

## Regulatory Changes

**Previous System:**
- Weekly frequency SIP/STP requests processed on Wednesday of every week
- If Wednesday was not a business day, processing moved to next business day
- Investors could choose any day (Monday-Friday) but default was Wednesday

**New System:**
- Weekly frequency SIP/STP requests processed on all working days of the week
- Greater flexibility in processing schedule

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members must note the changes for client servicing
- All other terms and conditions of SID/KIM/SAI remain unchanged
- Changes incorporated into relevant fund documents

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** August 4, 2025
- **Circular Date:** August 1, 2025
- **Addendum Date:** July 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact:**
- Improved flexibility for investors in weekly SIP/STP processing
- Enhanced operational efficiency for fund processing
- Better alignment with market working days

**Market Impact:**
- Minimal market impact as this is an operational enhancement
- Affects only Sundaram Mutual Fund schemes
- Positive for investor convenience and fund operations
