## Summary

NSE announces the upcoming listing of equity shares of Aditya Infotech Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol CPPLUS with ISIN INE819V01029. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Aditya Infotech Limited
- Trading Symbol: CPPLUS
- ISIN: INE819V01029
- Listing type: IPO (Initial Public Offering)
- Exact listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the exchange are informed about the forthcoming listing and should prepare for trading operations once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: August 04, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This listing will add a new equity security to NSE's trading platform, providing investors with additional investment opportunities. The impact is moderate as it represents routine market expansion through new listings.
