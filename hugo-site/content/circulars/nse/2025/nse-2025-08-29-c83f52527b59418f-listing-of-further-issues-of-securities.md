## Summary

NSE announces the listing of further equity shares for eight companies allotted under Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) schemes. The securities will be admitted for trading on the Capital Market segment effective September 1, 2025. Total securities being listed exceed 8.3 million shares across all companies.

## Key Points

- Eight companies getting further equity shares listed under ESOP allotments
- Largest allotment: Bajaj Finance Limited with 8,195,345 shares
- All securities are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Market lot size of 1 for all listed securities
- No lock-in restrictions applicable
- Trading identification by designated security codes only

## Company Details

**Major Allotments:**
- **Bajaj Finance Limited (BAJFINANCE)**: 8,195,345 shares of Re. 1/- each (August 25, 2025)
- **Coromandel International Limited (COROMANDEL)**: 83,160 shares of Re. 1/- each (August 20, 2025)
- **Medplus Health Services Limited (MEDPLUS)**: 79,157 shares of Rs. 2/- each (August 2, 2025)

**Smaller Allotments:**
- **Aarti Pharmalabs Limited (AARTIPHARM)**: 6,600 shares of Rs. 5/- each (August 12, 2025)
- **Carborundum Universal Limited (CARBORUNIV)**: 5,882 shares of Re. 1/- each (July 31, 2025)
- **Greenply Industries Limited (GREENPLY)**: 500 shares of Re. 1/- each (July 16, 2025)

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 1, 2025
- **Circular Date**: August 29, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: Range from July 16, 2025 to August 25, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - routine ESOP listings with no restrictions on trading. Bajaj Finance's large allotment of 8.2M shares represents the most significant addition to tradeable float. All shares are immediately tradeable with no lock-in periods, potentially affecting short-term liquidity and price dynamics for the respective stocks.
