## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has informed members about the forthcoming listing of Om Freight Forwarders Limited equity shares following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol OMFREIGHT with ISIN INE1BZC01019. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Om Freight Forwarders Limited
- Trading Symbol: OMFREIGHT
- ISIN: INE1BZC01019
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Circular issued by Listing Department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for exchange members. This is an informational circular about a new listing.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: October 07, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine IPO listing notification that adds one new security to the exchange. Members are informed for awareness purposes. No operational changes or compliance obligations are imposed.
