## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further equity shares for six companies through preferential allotment, effective August 28, 2025. The circular covers shares of Cura Technologies Limited, Easy Trip Planners Limited, Spectrum Electrical Industries Limited, Suven Life Sciences Limited, and Zota Health Care Limited.

## Key Points

- Six companies have additional equity shares listed through preferential allotment
- All securities will trade with market lot size of 1
- Shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Various lock-in periods apply ranging from February 2026 to February 2027
- Total new shares being listed: approximately 103 million shares across all companies

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Companies must comply with lock-in requirements as specified
- Trading permitted only in designated lot sizes
- Securities identified by designated codes for system trading

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 28, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry Dates**:
  - Easy Trip Planners: February 28, 2026
  - Zota Health Care: February 26, 2026
  - Cura Technologies: August 31, 2026
  - Suven Life Sciences: March 9, 2026
  - Spectrum Electrical: February 28, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Moderate impact on individual stock liquidity with significant increase in share count for some companies. Easy Trip Planners has the largest addition with 92.8 million new shares, representing substantial dilution. Lock-in periods provide temporary supply constraints but will eventually increase tradable float.
