## Summary

NSE has approved the segmental surrender of membership for Krchouksey Share and Securities Private Limited (SEBI registration: INZ000181135) from derivatives and currency segments including IRF, effective July 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading member: Krchouksey Share and Securities Private Limited
- SEBI registration number: INZ000181135
- Surrendered segments: Derivatives and Currency (including IRF)
- Surrender approval date: July 25, 2025
- All members notified of the change

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an administrative approval for voluntary membership surrender in specific trading segments.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are requested to take note of this membership change
- No specific compliance actions required from other members

## Important Dates

- **July 25, 2025**: Surrender approval date
- **July 29, 2025**: Circular notification date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a single member's voluntary surrender of segmental membership. No effect on trading operations or market structure for other participants.
