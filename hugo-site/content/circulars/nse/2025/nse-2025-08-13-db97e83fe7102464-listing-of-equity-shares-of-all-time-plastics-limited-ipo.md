## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of equity shares of All Time Plastics Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The shares will commence trading on August 14, 2025, with the trading symbol 'ALLTIME' and ISIN INE0GV601021.

## Key Points

- Company: All Time Plastics Limited
- Trading Symbol: ALLTIME
- Series: EQ (Equity)
- ISIN: INE0GV601021
- Face Value: Rs. 2 per share
- IPO Issue Price: Rs. 275 per share
- Total Securities: 65,507,778 equity shares
- Market Lot: 1 share
- Trading Commencement: August 14, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The security will be included in the pre-open session for IPO and Other category scrips as per SEBI circulars CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for system trading
- Trading shall be conducted in specified lot sizes as per annexure
- Lock-in periods apply to different categories of shares with varying dates from September 2025 to August 2028

## Important Dates

- **Trading Commencement**: August 14, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry Dates**: 
  - September 10, 2025 (2,180,249 shares)
  - November 9, 2025 (2,180,253 shares) 
  - December 31, 2025 (4,032,258 shares)
  - February 14, 2026 (1,064,800 shares)
  - August 14, 2026 (32,738,360 shares)
  - August 14, 2028 (13,101,600 shares)

## Impact Assessment

This listing adds a new equity security to NSE's capital market segment, expanding investment opportunities in the plastics manufacturing sector. The significant lock-in periods for various share categories will affect liquidity and trading volumes in the initial years post-listing.
