## Summary

NSE has approved the segmental surrender of membership by DB (International) Stock Brokers Ltd. (SEBI registration: INZ000179035) for the IRF (Interest Rate Futures) segment, effective July 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- DB (International) Stock Brokers Ltd. surrendered its IRF segment membership
- SEBI registration number: INZ000179035
- Surrender approval date: July 29, 2025
- All NSE members notified of this change

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an administrative approval of a member's voluntary segment surrender.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are requested to take note of this membership change
- No specific compliance actions required from other members

## Important Dates

- **July 29, 2025**: Surrender approval date for IRF segment membership
- **July 31, 2025**: Circular notification date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only one trading member's participation in the IRF segment. No operational disruptions expected for other market participants.
