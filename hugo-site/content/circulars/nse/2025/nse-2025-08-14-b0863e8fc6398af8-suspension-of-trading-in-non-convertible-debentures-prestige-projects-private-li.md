## Summary

NSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures of Prestige Projects Private Limited (Symbol: 1175PPPL27, ISIN: INE757O07049) effective August 21, 2025. The suspension is due to the exercise of a call option by the issuer.

## Key Points

- Single NCD security affected: Prestige Projects Private Limited
- Symbol: 1175PPPL27
- ISIN: INE757O07049
- Suspension reason: Exercise of Call option
- Action taken under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard suspension procedure under existing NSE trading regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must cease trading in the specified NCD security
- Members should update their trading systems to reflect the suspension
- No new positions can be created in this security

## Important Dates

- **August 14, 2025**: Circular issued
- **August 21, 2025**: Trading suspension effective date

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this affects only one NCD security. The suspension is due to a normal corporate action (call option exercise) rather than regulatory concerns. Investors holding these debentures will be subject to the call option terms as per the original issue documentation.
