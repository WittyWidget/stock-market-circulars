## Summary

NSE has admitted 10 commercial papers from various companies to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective August 19, 2025, under Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Key Points

- 10 commercial papers from different companies listed on debt market segment
- All securities are commercial papers with face value of ₹5,00,000
- Allotment dates range from August 18-19, 2025
- Redemption dates vary from September 29, 2025 to November 18, 2025
- Issue prices range from ₹4,92,087 to ₹4,96,430

## Listed Securities

**August 18, 2025 Allotment:**
- Bajaj Auto Credit Limited - 10,000 units, redemption Nov 17, 2025
- ICICI Securities Limited - 17,000 units, redemption Nov 17, 2025
- Aditya Birla Capital Limited - 11,000 units, redemption Nov 17, 2025
- CEAT Limited - 1,000 units, redemption Nov 17, 2025
- Godrej Industries Limited - 1,500 units, redemption Nov 17, 2025
- Nuvoco Vistas Corporation Limited - 12,000 units, redemption Sep 29, 2025
- PNB Housing Finance Limited - 16,500 units, redemption Nov 17, 2025

**August 19, 2025 Allotment:**
- Kotak Securities Limited - 10,000 units, redemption Nov 18, 2025
- Axis Securities Limited - 3,000 units, redemption Nov 18, 2025
- HSBC Investdirect Financial Services (I) Limited - 1,200 units, redemption Nov 18, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced - routine listing notification under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants - informational circular only.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** August 19, 2025
- **Allotment Dates:** August 18-19, 2025
- **Earliest Redemption:** September 29, 2025 (Nuvoco Vistas)
- **Latest Redemption:** November 18, 2025 (Kotak Securities, Axis Securities, HSBC)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine administrative listing of privately placed commercial papers. Provides additional short-term debt instruments for institutional investors in the debt market segment.
