## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of two series of privately placed non-convertible debentures issued by Vedika Credit Capital Limited for trading on the capital market segment, effective August 25, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities series admitted for trading: VCCL 11.8% 2027 Sr B and VCCL 11.6% 2027 Sr A
- Trading symbols: 118VCCL27A and 116VCCL27 respectively
- Market lot size: 1 for both securities
- Securities classified under N0 series

## Security Details

**Series B (118VCCL27A):**
- Coupon Rate: 11.8%
- Maturity: August 21, 2027
- ISIN: INE04HY07302

**Series A (116VCCL27):**
- Coupon Rate: 11.6%
- Maturity: February 21, 2027
- ISIN: INE04HY07310

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: August 25, 2025
- Series A Maturity: February 21, 2027
- Series B Maturity: August 21, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing of debt securities that provides additional investment options for institutional and qualified investors. The listing expands the debt market offerings but has minimal impact on broader market operations.
