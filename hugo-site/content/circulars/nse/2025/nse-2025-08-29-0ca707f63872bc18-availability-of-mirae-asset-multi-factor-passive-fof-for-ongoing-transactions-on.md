## Summary

Mirae Asset Multi Factor Passive FOF of Mirae Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions along with SIP, STP, SWP facility on NSE MF Invest Platform effective September 1, 2025.

## Key Points

- Six scheme variants available covering Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW options
- All schemes fall under Equity category
- Full range of investment facilities including SIP, STP, and SWP will be available
- Schemes were previously closed and are now being reopened for ongoing transactions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational announcement regarding scheme availability.

## Compliance Requirements

Members should note the availability of these schemes for client transactions starting September 1, 2025.

## Important Dates

- **September 1, 2025**: Effective date for reopening of ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine operational matter providing additional investment options for clients on the NSE MF Invest Platform without affecting broader market operations or regulatory framework.
