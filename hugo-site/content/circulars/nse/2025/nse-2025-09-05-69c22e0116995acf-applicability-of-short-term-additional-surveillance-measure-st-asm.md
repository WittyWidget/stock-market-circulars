## Summary

NSE has announced changes to the Short-Term Additional Surveillance Measure (ST-ASM) framework effective September 08, 2025. Three securities are being added to Stage-I of ST-ASM, while five securities are being excluded from the ASM framework entirely. The consolidated list shows 26 securities currently under ASM Stage-I surveillance.

## Key Points

- 3 securities added to ST-ASM Stage-I: Ganga Forging Limited, Moschip Technologies Limited, and Readymix Construction Machinery Limited
- No securities in ST-ASM Stage-II currently
- No movement between Stage-I and Stage-II
- 5 securities excluded from ASM framework with specific reasons
- Total 26 securities remain under ASM Stage-I surveillance

## Regulatory Changes

**Securities Added to ST-ASM Stage-I:**
- GANGAFORGE (Ganga Forging Limited) - INE691Z01023
- MOSCHIP (Moschip Technologies Limited) - INE935B01025
- READYMIX (Readymix Construction Machinery Limited) - INE10QL01011

**Securities Excluded from ASM Framework:**
- AFFORDABLE (Affordable Robotic & Automation Limited)
- ASTRON (Astron Paper & Board Mill Limited) - moved to LTASM
- COFFEEDAY (Coffee Day Enterprises Limited) - moved to ESM
- NEWJAISA (Newjaisa Technologies Limited) - moved to LTASM
- SHREERAMA (Shree Rama Multi-Tech Limited) - moved to ESM

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must ensure compliance with ST-ASM framework requirements for newly added securities
- Enhanced surveillance measures apply to all securities under Stage-I including margin requirements and position limits
- Market participants should review exposure to affected securities

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** September 08, 2025
- All changes to surveillance measures become applicable from market opening on this date

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:**
- Enhanced surveillance on 3 new securities may lead to reduced liquidity and increased volatility
- Removal of 5 securities from ASM provides relief from restrictive trading conditions
- Overall ASM list maintains 26 securities under heightened surveillance

**Operational Impact:**
- Brokers need to update risk management systems for newly added securities
- Clients with positions in affected securities should be informed of changed trading conditions
- Two securities moved to more stringent LTASM framework, two moved to ESM (Enhanced Surveillance Measure)
