## Summary

NSE announces the listing of Anondita Medicare Limited equity shares on the EMERGE SME Platform with trading symbol 'ANONDITA' effective September 01, 2025. The company's shares will initially trade in the Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment (TFTS) with 'W' settlement type before moving to normal rolling segment.

## Key Points

- Trading Symbol: ANONDITA
- ISIN: INE0VTV01012
- Face Value: Rs. 10 per share
- Issue Price: Rs. 145 per share
- Total Securities: 18,086,618 equity shares
- Lot Size: 1,000 equity shares
- Market Maker: Mansi Share and Stock Broking Private Limited
- Maximum Permissible Spread: Within 10%

## Regulatory Changes

- Initial trading in Series 'ST' (Trade for Trade Surveillance Segment with Settlement Type 'W')
- Subsequent shift to Series 'SM' (Normal Rolling Segment with Settlement Type 'N') as per SEBI guidelines
- Pre-open session applicable for IPO category as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading
- Trading must be in specified lot sizes of 1,000 shares
- Market maker spread must remain within 10% limit
- Compliance with TFTS settlement requirements initially

## Important Dates

- **September 01, 2025**: Trading commencement date
- **Financial Year**: April 2025 - March 2026

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact expected as this is a standard SME listing on the EMERGE platform. The TFTS classification indicates heightened surveillance measures, suggesting potential volatility concerns. The small issue size and specialized healthcare sector focus limit broader market implications.
