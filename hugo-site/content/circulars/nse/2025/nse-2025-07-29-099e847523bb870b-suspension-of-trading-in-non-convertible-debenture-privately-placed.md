## Summary

NSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debentures (privately placed) of three companies effective July 30, 2025. The suspension is due to redemption of these debt instruments.

## Key Points

- Three NCDs will be suspended from trading on July 30, 2025
- All suspensions are due to redemption of the debentures
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A
- Circular effective from the suspension date

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. Standard procedure for suspending trading in debt instruments upon redemption.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease trading in the specified NCDs from July 30, 2025
- No trading activity permitted in these instruments post-suspension date

## Important Dates

- **July 30, 2025**: Suspension date for all three NCDs
- **July 29, 2025**: Circular issue date

## Impact Assessment

Routine administrative action with limited market impact. Affects holders and traders of specific NCDs:
- LIC Housing Finance Limited (INE115A07HU8)
- L&T Finance Limited (INE027E07BX4) 
- Sammaan Capital Limited (INE148I08207)

No broader market implications expected as this is standard procedure for maturing debt instruments.
