## Summary

NSE has suspended trading in a Non-Convertible Debenture issued by Prestige Projects Private Limited effective August 21, 2025, due to redemption of the instrument.

## Key Points

- Single Non-Convertible Debenture (privately placed) affected
- ISIN: INE757O07049
- Issuer: Prestige Projects Private Limited
- Suspension effective from August 21, 2025
- Reason: Redemption of the debenture

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine administrative action under existing NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations Part A, Regulation 3.1.2.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. The suspension is automatically effective from the stated date.

## Important Dates

- **August 21, 2025**: Trading suspension becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only one privately placed debt instrument. The suspension is due to natural redemption rather than any adverse event.
