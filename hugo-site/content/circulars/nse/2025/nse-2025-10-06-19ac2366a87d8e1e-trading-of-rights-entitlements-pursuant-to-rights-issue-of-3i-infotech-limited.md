## Summary

NSE has admitted Rights Entitlements (REs) of 3i Infotech Limited for trading on the Capital Market segment. Trading will commence from October 07, 2025 and continue until October 20, 2025. The REs will trade in the Trade to Trade segment with compulsory demat for all investors. A total of 37,708,165 Rights Entitlements will be available for trading.

## Key Points

- Symbol: 3IINFO-RE, Series: BE, ISIN: INE748C20012
- Total Rights Entitlements: 37,708,165
- Market lot: 1
- Trading exclusively in Trade to Trade segment
- Compulsory demat requirement for all investors
- Trading members must inform clients about RE commencement with proper ISIN identification
- Contract notes must include disclaimer about RE purchase rights and expiry
- REs not subscribed or renounced by Issue Closing Date will lapse and be extinguished

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes. This circular implements existing regulations under NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A (Regulation 3.1.1 for security admission and Regulation 2.5.5 for lot sizes).

## Compliance Requirements

**Trading Members must:**
- Inform all clients about commencement of RE trading with correct ISIN (INE748C20012)
- Ensure clients understand REs are not ordinary shares of the company
- Include prominent disclaimer in contract notes/bills for RE purchases stating:
  - Purchase of REs only provides right to participate in ongoing Rights Issue by making application with requisite money OR renounce REs before issue closes
  - REs neither subscribed nor renounced by Issue Closing Date will lapse and be extinguished
- Ensure all RE trades are settled in demat form only

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date:** October 07, 2025
- **Trading Period Start:** October 07, 2025
- **Trading Period End (Last Date for Renunciation):** October 20, 2025
- **Suspension Date:** October 21, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - Affects only shareholders and prospective investors of 3i Infotech Limited. The 14-day trading window provides liquidity for shareholders who wish to renounce their rights.

**Operational Impact:** Standard corporate action requiring trading members to update systems for new symbol 3IINFO-RE and ensure proper client communication and disclaimer mechanisms are in place.

**Investor Impact:** Shareholders of 3i Infotech have the opportunity to trade their rights entitlements during the specified period. Investors purchasing REs must understand they need to apply with requisite funds before issue closure or the REs will lapse.
