## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has admitted the equity shares of Markolines Pavement Technologies Limited for trading on the Capital Market segment (Normal Market - Rolling Settlement) effective October 14, 2025. The company's equity shares will trade under the symbol 'MARKOLINES' with ISIN INE0FW001016. A total of 22,040,920 equity shares of face value Rs. 10/- each will be listed, with a market lot of 1 share. Trading will be in compulsory demat mode for all investors.

## Key Points

- **Symbol**: MARKOLINES
- **ISIN**: INE0FW001016
- **Series**: EQ (Equity)
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10/- per share
- **Paid-up Value**: Rs. 10/- per share
- **Total Securities**: 22,040,920 equity shares
- **Distinctive Number Range**: 1 to 22,040,920
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Trading Mode**: Compulsory demat for all investors
- **Segment**: Normal Market (Rolling Settlement)
- **Pari Passu**: Yes

## Lock-in Details

- **Free for Trading**: 22,004,520 equity shares (Distinctive Nos. 1 to 22,004,520)
- **Locked-in until March 30, 2026**: 36,400 equity shares (Distinctive Nos. 22,004,521 to 22,040,920)

## Regulatory Changes

This listing is in pursuance of:
- Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A (admission to dealings)
- Regulation 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A (lot sizes)

## Compliance Requirements

- All trading in MARKOLINES equity shares must be conducted in compulsory demat mode
- Securities will be identified on the trading system only by their designated codes
- Trading must be in the specified market lot size of 1 share
- Lock-in restrictions apply to 36,400 shares until March 30, 2026

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a routine new listing notification affecting only investors interested in Markolines Pavement Technologies Limited. The company operates in the pavement technologies sector.

**Trading Impact**: Investors will be able to trade MARKOLINES equity shares on NSE from October 14, 2025. The majority of shares (99.83%) will be freely tradable, while a small portion (0.17%) remains locked-in until March 30, 2026.
