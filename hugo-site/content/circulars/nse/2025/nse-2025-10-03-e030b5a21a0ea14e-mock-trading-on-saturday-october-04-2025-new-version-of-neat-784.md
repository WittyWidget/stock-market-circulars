## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session in the Futures & Options segment on Saturday, October 04, 2025, featuring the release of new NEAT+ version 7.8.4. The session will test both primary site and disaster recovery (DR) site operations. NEAT+ version 7.8.3 will be discontinued on November 01, 2025.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session scheduled for October 04, 2025 in F&O segment
- New NEAT+ version 7.8.4 available from October 03, 2025 at 18:00 hrs
- Primary site mock trading: 11:15 hrs to 14:00 hrs with contingency testing from 12:45 hrs to 14:00 hrs
- DR site mock trading: 15:00 hrs to 16:00 hrs, with trade modification until 16:10 hrs
- Live re-login session: 18:00 hrs to 18:30 hrs
- NEAT+ 7.8.3 and 7.8.4 compatible with new broadcast parameters
- Existing broadcast parameters via NNF will be discontinued from October 04, 2025
- Connect2NSE and Extranet unavailable from 11:00 am to 02:00 pm
- Extranet API unavailable from 11:00 am to 11:30 am

## Regulatory Changes

- NEAT+ version 7.8.3 will be discontinued on November 01, 2025 (mock)
- Existing market data broadcast parameters to be discontinued from October 04, 2025
- Members must migrate to new market data broadcast parameters (refer NSE/MSD/69328 and NSE/MSD/69966)
- UCC/PAN validation requirements apply (refer NSE/ISC/51754 and NSE/ISC/52722), but will be skipped during contingency time

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must download and install NEAT+ version 7.8.4 from /faoftp/faocommon/NEATPlus784
- Members using direct connection via NNF must migrate to new broadcast parameters on priority
- Members using NNF software must manually clear outstanding orders in their systems
- Only valid and compliant UCC/PAN uploaded and approved before cutoff will be allowed
- Members may use mock trading or simulated environment to meet SEBI regulatory requirements (SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234)

## Important Dates

- **October 03, 2025, 18:00 hrs**: NEAT+ 7.8.4 available for download
- **October 04, 2025**: Mock trading session
  - 11:15-14:00 hrs: Primary site trading (contingency 12:45-14:00 hrs)
  - 15:00-16:00 hrs: DR site trading
  - 16:10 hrs: Trade modification end time
  - 18:00-18:30 hrs: Live re-login session
- **November 01, 2025**: NEAT+ 7.8.3 discontinuation date (mock)

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Members must ensure timely migration to NEAT+ 7.8.4 and new broadcast parameters. The mock session provides critical testing opportunity for disaster recovery procedures and system compatibility. Members using NNF connections face mandatory migration from existing broadcast parameters.

**System Impact**: Connect2NSE and Extranet services will be unavailable during specified periods, affecting member access to exchange systems. All outstanding orders will be cleared before each session.

**Financial Impact**: No financial obligations from mock trading - no fund pay-in or pay-out will result from trades executed during the session.
