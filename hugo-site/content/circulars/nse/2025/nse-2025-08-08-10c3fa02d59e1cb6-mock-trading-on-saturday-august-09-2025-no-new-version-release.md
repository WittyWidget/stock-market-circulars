## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session in the Capital Market Segment on Saturday, August 09, 2025. The session includes both primary site and disaster recovery (DR) site trading with specific timings for various market segments including pre-open, normal trading, block deals, and T+0 markets.

## Key Points

- Mock trading scheduled for August 09, 2025
- No new software version release for this session
- Two trading sessions: Primary Site (10:45-16:45) and DR Site (15:30-18:30)
- Re-login testing window from 18:00-18:30
- All outstanding orders will be cleared before each session
- NOTIS application will not be accessible during mock session
- Connect2NSE and Extranet will have limited availability

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is a routine mock trading session for system testing purposes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure migration to NEAT+ version 7.8.3 before September 06, 2025 discontinuation of version 7.8.2
- Only valid and compliant UCC/PAN uploaded and approved before cutoff will be allowed
- Members using NNF software must manually clear orders in their systems
- Members should participate using all trading software and complete re-login testing

## Important Dates

- **August 09, 2025**: Mock trading session
- **September 06, 2025**: Discontinuation deadline for NEAT+ version 7.8.2
- **Trading Session 1**: 10:45 - 16:45 (Primary Site)
- **Trading Session 2**: 15:30 - 18:30 (DR Site)
- **Re-login Testing**: 18:00 - 18:30

## Impact Assessment

Medium operational impact on trading members who need to participate in mock session for system validation. Limited impact on normal market operations as this is a weekend session. Members need to ensure system readiness and software version compliance. No financial obligations from mock trading activities.
