## Summary

NSE has announced a scheduled maintenance downtime for the NSE MF Invest Platform on October 25, 2025, from 10:00 hrs to 11:00 hrs (1 hour duration). Members are advised to refrain from carrying out any activities and transactions during this period and plan their activities accordingly.

## Key Points

- Platform affected: NSE MF Invest Platform
- Downtime date: October 25, 2025
- Start time: 10:00 hrs
- End time: 11:00 hrs
- Duration: 1 hour
- Reason: Scheduled maintenance activity
- Members must avoid all activities and transactions during the downtime window
- Members connecting via API should inform their technical team/vendor

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must refrain from carrying out any activities and transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform during the specified maintenance window
- Members using API connectivity should inform their technical teams and vendors about the scheduled downtime
- Members should plan their activities to accommodate the system unavailability

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** October 23, 2025
- **Maintenance Window:** October 25, 2025, 10:00 hrs to 11:00 hrs

## Impact Assessment

Minimal operational impact expected due to the short 1-hour maintenance window and advance notice provided. Members have sufficient time to plan their mutual fund investment activities around the downtime. The maintenance is scheduled during business hours but the limited duration should allow members to reschedule transactions before or after the window. No market-wide impact anticipated as this affects only the MF Invest Platform operations.
