## Summary

NSE has announced a change in ISIN code for Pavna Industries Limited (PAVNAIND) effective September 01, 2025, due to sub-division of equity shares from face value of Rs. 10/- to Re. 1/-. The new ISIN INE07S101038 will be effective for all trades from the ex-date.

## Key Points

- Company: Pavna Industries Limited
- Symbol: PAVNAIND
- New ISIN: INE07S101038
- Reason: Sub-division of equity shares from Rs. 10/- to Re. 1/-
- Effective date: September 01, 2025 (Ex-date)

## Regulatory Changes

ISIN code change from existing code to INE07S101038 to reflect the sub-division of equity shares with reduced face value.

## Compliance Requirements

- All members must update their systems with the new ISIN code
- Trading systems must reflect the new ISIN from September 01, 2025
- Settlement processes must use the new ISIN for trades executed on or after the ex-date

## Important Dates

- **August 28, 2025**: Circular notification date
- **September 01, 2025**: Ex-date - New ISIN becomes effective for all trades

## Impact Assessment

Routine administrative change affecting trading and settlement systems. Members need to update their systems to ensure smooth trading operations. The sub-division will increase the number of shares outstanding while reducing the face value, making shares more affordable for retail investors.
