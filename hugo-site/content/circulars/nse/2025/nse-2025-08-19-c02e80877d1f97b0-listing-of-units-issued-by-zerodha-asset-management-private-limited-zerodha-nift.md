## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of units issued by Zerodha Asset Management Private Limited for the Zerodha Nifty 8-13 Yr G-Sec ETF. The listing becomes effective from August 20, 2025, with trading symbol LTGILTCASE.

## Key Points

- ETF Symbol: LTGILTCASE
- ISIN: INF0R8F01133
- Total Units: 5,201,094
- Face Value: Rs. 10 per unit
- Issue Price: Rs. 29.34 per unit
- Market Lot: 1 unit
- Date of Allotment: August 14, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security code LTGILTCASE
- Trading must comply with specified lot size of 1 unit
- Market makers: Kanjalochana Finserve Private Limited and East India Securities Limited

## Important Dates

- **August 14, 2025**: Date of allotment
- **August 20, 2025**: Effective date for trading commencement

## Impact Assessment

Low immediate market impact. The listing adds a new government securities ETF option for investors seeking exposure to 8-13 year government bonds. Provides additional investment avenue in the fixed income segment through ETF structure.
