## Summary

NSE has notified the commencement of trading in Rights Entitlements (REs) of Inox Wind Limited with effect from August 6, 2025. The rights entitlements will be traded under symbol INOXWI-RE in the Trade to Trade segment with compulsory demat.

## Key Points

- Symbol: INOXWI-RE (Inox Wind Limited-RE)
- ISIN: INE066P20011
- Total Rights Entitlements: 103,914,054
- Market lot: 1
- Trading in Trade to Trade segment with compulsory demat
- Series: BE

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - standard notification for rights entitlements trading as per existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must inform clients about RE trading commencement and ISIN
- Contract notes must include disclaimer that REs are not ordinary shares
- Disclaimer must explain that REs give right to participate in rights issue or renounce before closure
- Warning that unsubscribed/unrenounced REs will lapse after issue closing date

## Important Dates

- Trading commencement: August 6, 2025
- Renunciation period: August 6, 2025 to August 14, 2025
- Trading suspension: August 18, 2025
- Circular effective: August 6, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact limited to Inox Wind shareholders and investors interested in the rights issue. Provides liquidity for rights entitlements during the renunciation period, allowing investors to trade their rights before the issue closes.
