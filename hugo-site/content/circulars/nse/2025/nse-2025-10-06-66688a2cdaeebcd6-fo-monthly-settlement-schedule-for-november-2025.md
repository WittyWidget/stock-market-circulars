## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued the settlement schedule for the Futures & Options segment for November 2025. The schedule covers 19 trading days from November 3 to November 28, 2025, with settlements occurring on T+1 basis (next trading day). This is a routine operational circular providing settlement dates for F&O trades.

## Key Points

- Settlement schedule covers November 2025 F&O trades
- 19 trading days scheduled from November 3-28, 2025
- Standard T+1 settlement cycle maintained throughout the month
- First trade date: November 3, 2025 (settlement: November 4, 2025)
- Last trade date: November 28, 2025 (settlement: December 1, 2025)
- Circular reference: NCL/CMPT/70626, No. 132/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard operational schedule announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

All F&O segment members must adhere to the published settlement schedule for November 2025 trades. Members should ensure adequate preparation for settlement obligations on the specified dates.

## Important Dates

- **First Trading Day**: November 3, 2025 (Settlement: November 4, 2025)
- **Last Trading Day**: November 28, 2025 (Settlement: December 1, 2025)
- **Trading Holidays**: November 5, 2025 (no trade/settlement scheduled)
- **Month-end Settlement**: November 28 trades settle on December 1, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. This is a routine operational schedule that enables F&O market participants to plan their settlement obligations. The schedule follows standard T+1 settlement protocol with one holiday observed during the month. No changes to settlement procedures or timing.
