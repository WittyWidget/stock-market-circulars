## Summary

NSE announces the permanent discontinuation of login facility on the NMF Platform effective September 15, 2025. This follows the successful migration of the platform to NSE MF Invest on August 22, 2025, with all critical data including client data, active mandates, and systematic transactions successfully migrated.

## Key Points

- Login facility on NSE NMF Platform will be permanently discontinued from September 15, 2025
- Platform was successfully migrated to NSE MF Invest on August 22, 2025
- Client Data, Active Mandates, and Active Systematic Transactions have been migrated
- Historical data must be downloaded before September 15, 2025
- Platform will cease to be accessible after the discontinuation date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is an operational system upgrade and migration notice.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must download any required historical data on or before September 15, 2025
- Transition operations to NSE MF Invest platform
- Contact support team for clarifications via toll-free number 18002100940 or support@nseinvest.com

## Important Dates

- **September 15, 2025**: Permanent discontinuation of NMF Platform login facility
- **August 22, 2025**: Successful migration to NSE MF Invest platform completed

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact on mutual fund operations as members need to complete transition to new platform. The successful data migration minimizes operational disruption, but members must ensure they download any required historical data before the cutoff date to avoid data loss.
