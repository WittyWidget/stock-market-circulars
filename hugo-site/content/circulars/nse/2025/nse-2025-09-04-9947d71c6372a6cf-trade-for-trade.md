## Summary

NSE has announced changes to the Trade for Trade segment affecting 16 securities, effective September 09, 2025. Eight securities from the main board (EQ series) and eight from SME (SM series) will be shifted to Trade for Trade segment with 5% price band due to surveillance review and market safety considerations.

## Key Points

- 16 securities will be moved to Trade for Trade segment from Rolling segment
- All affected securities will have 5% price band
- Settlement will be on trade-to-trade basis with no netting off allowed
- Movement is purely for market surveillance and not an adverse action against companies
- Some securities will also be shifted back from Trade for Trade to Rolling segment

## Regulatory Changes

- Securities moved from Rolling segment (EQ/SM series) to Trade for Trade segment (BE/ST series)
- Price band of 5% or lower applicable to all Trade for Trade securities
- Trade-to-trade settlement mandatory with no netting off facility

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must take adequate precaution while trading in affected securities
- Members should refer to NSE website for criteria governing movement between segments
- Queries should be directed to surveillance@nse.co.in

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 09, 2025 (Tuesday)
- **Circular Date**: September 04, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Main Board Securities (EQ to BE):**
- CL EDUCATE LIMITED (CLEDUCATE)
- COFFEE DAY ENTERPRISES LIMITED (COFFEEDAY) 
- JUBILANT AGRI AND CONSUMER PRODUCTS LIMITED (JUBLCPL)
- KINGFA SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY (INDIA) LIMITED (KINGFA)
- OLA ELECTRIC MOBILITY LIMITED (OLAELEC)
- PRAJAY ENGINEERS SYNDICATE LIMITED (PRAENG)
- RISHABH INSTRUMENTS LIMITED (RISHABH)
- SILGO RETAIL LIMITED (SILGO)

**SME Securities (SM to ST):**
- AGNI GREEN POWER LIMITED (AGNI)
- BALAJI PHOSPHATES LIMITED (BALAJIPHOS)
- C2C ADVANCED SYSTEMS LIMITED (C2C)
- FLYSBS AVIATION LIMITED (FLYSBS)
- GRAND CONTINENT HOTELS LIMITED (GCHOTELS)
- MANGLAM INFRA & ENGINEERING LIMITED (MIEL)
- TAC INFOSEC LIMITED (TAC)
- TAPI FRUIT PROCESSING LIMITED (TAPIFRUIT)

The movement to Trade for Trade segment will impact liquidity and require immediate settlement, affecting trading strategies for these securities.
