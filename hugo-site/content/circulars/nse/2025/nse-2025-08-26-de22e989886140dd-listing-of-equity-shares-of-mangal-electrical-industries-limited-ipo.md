## Summary

NSE announces the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Mangal Electrical Industries Limited following their Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol MEIL with ISIN INE0PKD01011.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Mangal Electrical Industries Limited
- Trading Symbol: MEIL
- ISIN: INE0PKD01011
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Standard IPO listing procedure

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - standard listing procedure following IPO completion.

## Compliance Requirements

Exchange members are informed about the new listing. Standard trading and settlement procedures will apply once listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: August 26, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine new listing announcement. Addition of one new equity share to NSE trading platform. Members should prepare systems for new symbol MEIL trading once listing date is confirmed.
