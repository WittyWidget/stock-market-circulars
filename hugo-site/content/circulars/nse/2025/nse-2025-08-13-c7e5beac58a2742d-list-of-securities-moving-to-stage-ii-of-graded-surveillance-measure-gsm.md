## Summary

NSE has moved YAARI Digital Integrated Services Limited (ISIN: INE126M01010) to Stage II of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework effective August 14, 2025. This action implements enhanced surveillance measures including trade-for-trade settlement and additional surveillance deposit requirements.

## Key Points

- YAARI Digital Integrated Services Limited moved to GSM Stage II
- Effective date: August 14, 2025
- Trading restricted to Trade for Trade basis with no netting off allowed
- Price band limited to 5% or lower as applicable
- Additional Surveillance Deposit (ASD) of 50% of trade value required from buyers
- Security added as per BSE coordination

## Regulatory Changes

Implementation of Stage II GSM surveillance measures:
- Mandatory trade-for-trade settlement
- Price band restriction of 5% or lower
- Additional Surveillance Deposit requirement of 50% of trade value
- No netting off of positions allowed

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must take adequate precautions while trading in the security
- Buyers must deposit ASD of 50% of trade value
- Settlement will be done on trade-to-trade basis only
- No netting off of positions permitted

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 14, 2025 - GSM Stage II measures come into effect

## Impact Assessment

High impact on trading activity for YAARI shares due to:
- Significant reduction in trading flexibility with trade-for-trade requirement
- Increased cost burden on buyers due to 50% ASD requirement
- Limited price movement with 5% price band
- Enhanced settlement risk due to no netting off provision
- Potential reduction in liquidity and trading volumes
