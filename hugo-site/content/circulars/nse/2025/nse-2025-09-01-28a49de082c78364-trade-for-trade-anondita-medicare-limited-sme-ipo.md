## Summary

Anondita Medicare Limited will be transferred from the trade for trade segment (series: ST) to the rolling segment (Series: SM) effective September 15, 2025, following SEBI guidelines.

## Key Points

- Transfer from trade for trade segment to rolling segment
- Effective date: September 15, 2025
- Single security affected: Anondita Medicare Limited (Symbol: ANONDITA)
- Based on SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012

## Regulatory Changes

The security will move from restrictive trade for trade trading (ST series) to normal rolling settlement (SM series), allowing more flexible trading arrangements.

## Compliance Requirements

Members should note the segment change and adjust their trading systems accordingly for the affected security.

## Important Dates

- **September 15, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this affects only one SME stock. The transfer to rolling segment typically improves liquidity and trading flexibility for the security.
