## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India Limited has announced the delisting of equity shares of Manpasand Beverages Limited effective August 05, 2025. This action follows the compulsory delisting of the company by BSE Limited under Rule 21(2)(b) of the Securities Contracts (Regulations) Rules 1957.

## Key Points

- Manpasand Beverages Limited (Symbol: MANPASAND) will be delisted from NSE effective August 05, 2025
- The delisting is in accordance with Rule 21(2)(b) of the Securities Contracts (Regulations) Rules 1957
- This action follows the compulsory delisting by BSE Limited
- The delisting involves withdrawal of admission to dealings in equity shares

## Regulatory Changes

The delisting is implemented under the Securities Contracts (Regulations) Rules 1957, specifically Rule 21(2)(b), which provides the regulatory framework for delisting securities from stock exchanges.

## Compliance Requirements

- All members are notified of the delisting decision
- Trading in MANPASAND equity shares will cease on NSE from August 05, 2025
- Members must ensure compliance with delisting procedures and settlement of outstanding positions

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 05, 2025 - Delisting of Manpasand Beverages Limited equity shares from NSE
- **Circular Date**: August 04, 2025 - Notification issued to all members

## Impact Assessment

The delisting will result in complete cessation of trading in Manpasand Beverages Limited equity shares on NSE. This has significant implications for existing shareholders who will lose the ability to trade these shares on the exchange. The compulsory nature of the delisting indicates serious regulatory or compliance issues with the company, potentially affecting investor confidence and liquidity for shareholders.
