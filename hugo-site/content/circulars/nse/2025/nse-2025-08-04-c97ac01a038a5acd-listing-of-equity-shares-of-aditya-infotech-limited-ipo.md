## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of Aditya Infotech Limited's equity shares on the Capital Market segment with trading symbol CPPLUS, effective August 05, 2025. The IPO consists of 117,221,884 equity shares of Re. 1/- each at an issue price of Rs. 675/-.

## Key Points

- Trading Symbol: CPPLUS
- ISIN: INE819V01029
- Face Value: Re. 1/- per share
- Issue Price: Rs. 675/- per share
- Total Shares: 117,221,884 equity shares
- Market Lot: 1 share
- Trading starts: August 05, 2025
- Will be part of pre-open session for IPO category

## Company Details

- **Registered Office**: F-28, Okhla Industrial Area, Phase-1, New Delhi – 110020
- **Corporate Office**: A-12, Sector 4, Noida – 201301, Uttar Pradesh
- **Website**: www.adityagroup.com
- **Company Secretary**: Ms. Roshni Tandon
- **Financial Year**: April 2025-March 2026

## Lock-in Details

- Various lock-in periods ranging from August 30, 2025 to February 03, 2027
- Majority of shares (67,075,651) locked until February 03, 2026
- Some shares are free for trading immediately

## Registrar Information

- **Registrar**: MUFG Intime India Private Limited
- **Address**: C-101, 247 Park, 1st Floor, L.B.S. Marg, Vikhroli (West), Mumbai 400083
- **Contact**: (+91) 8108114949
- **Email**: adityainfotech.ipo@in.mpms.mufg.com

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 05, 2025
- **Various Lock-in Expiry Dates**: August 30, 2025; October 29, 2025; February 03, 2026; February 03, 2027

## Impact Assessment

This listing adds a new security to NSE's Capital Market segment, providing trading opportunities for members and investors. The significant lock-in provisions indicate substantial promoter holding with staggered release schedule.
