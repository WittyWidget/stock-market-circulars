## Summary

NSE announces the launch of HDFC Diversified Equity All Cap Active Fund of Funds (FOF) on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The scheme will be available for investment from September 10, 2025 to September 24, 2025 until 11:59 PM, with allotment scheduled for September 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Six variants of the fund launched: Direct Growth, Direct IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, Regular Growth, Regular IDCW Payout, and Regular IDCW Reinvestment
- All schemes categorized under Equity category
- Each variant has unique ISIN codes ranging from INF179KC1JR0 to INF179KC1JW0
- Investment window: September 10-24, 2025
- Common allotment date: September 29, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard new fund offering launch announcement.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received in designated Clearing Corporation account on or before NFO closing date
- Standard KYC and investment procedures apply for retail investors

## Important Dates

- **Investment Period**: September 10, 2025 to September 24, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: September 29, 2025
- **Circular Date**: September 09, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine new fund offering. The launch expands investment options for mutual fund investors seeking diversified equity exposure through a fund of funds structure. Expected to attract investors looking for professional fund selection and management within the equity space.
