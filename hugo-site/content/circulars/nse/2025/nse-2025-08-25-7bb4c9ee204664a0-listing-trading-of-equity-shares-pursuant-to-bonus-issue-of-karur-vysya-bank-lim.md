## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and trading of equity shares pursuant to the bonus issue of Karur Vysya Bank Limited. The bonus shares will be available for trading on NSE's Capital Market segment with effect from August 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Karur Vysya Bank Limited
- Symbol: KARURVYSYA
- ISIN: INE036D01028
- Number of bonus securities: 16,10,35,439 shares
- Distinctive number range: 805329457 to 966364895
- Trading will commence on NSE Capital Market segment

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes specified in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities will be identified by designated codes for trading purposes
- Trading members must use the specified symbol KARURVYSYA for transactions

## Important Dates

- Record Date: August 26, 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: August 28, 2025
- Trading Commencement: August 29, 2025
- Circular Effective Date: August 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The bonus issue will increase the number of tradeable shares of Karur Vysya Bank in the market. Existing shareholders will receive additional shares without any cash payment, potentially improving liquidity and making shares more accessible to retail investors due to proportional price adjustment.
