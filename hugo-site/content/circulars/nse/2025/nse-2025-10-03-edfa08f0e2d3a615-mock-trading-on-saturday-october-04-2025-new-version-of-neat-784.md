## Summary

NSE will conduct a mock trading session on Saturday, October 04, 2025 in the Capital Market Segment to test the new NEAT+ version 7.8.4. The new version will be available for download from October 03, 2025 at 18:00 hours. Login using the older NEAT+ version 7.8.3 will be discontinued from November 01, 2025. The mock session will include testing of both primary site and disaster recovery (DR) site operations.

## Key Points

- New NEAT+ version 7.8.4 release for Capital Market Segment
- Mock trading scheduled for Saturday, October 04, 2025
- Software available for download from October 03, 2025 at 18:00 hours on NSE Extranet path /common/NEATPlus784
- NEAT+ 7.8.3 login will be discontinued from November 01, 2025 (mock)
- Mock session includes two trading sessions: Session-1 from Primary Site and Session-2 from DR Site
- All outstanding orders will be cleared before each session
- No financial obligations from mock trading trades
- UCC validation will be skipped during contingency session

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a routine system upgrade and testing exercise.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must download and install NEAT+ version 7.8.4 from NSE Extranet
- Members must transition from NEAT+ 7.8.3 before November 01, 2025
- Members using NNF software must manually clear orders in their systems
- Only valid and compliant UCC/PAN uploaded and approved before cutoff will be allowed
- Members should refer to circular NSE/MSD/46441 and SEBI circular SEBI/HO/MRD1/DSAP/CIR/P/2020/234 for software testing requirements
- Members may use either Mock Trading or Simulated Environment to meet regulatory requirements

## Important Dates

- **October 03, 2025, 18:00 hours**: NEAT+ 7.8.4 available for download
- **October 04, 2025**: Mock trading session
  - Trading Session-1 (Primary Site): 10:45 - 14:00
  - Trading Session-2 (DR Site): 14:45 - 16:30
  - Live Re-login: 18:00 - 18:30
- **November 01, 2025**: NEAT+ 7.8.3 login discontinued (from mock)

## Impact Assessment

**Operational Impact**: Medium - All trading members must upgrade their systems and participate in mock trading to ensure smooth transition. The mock session provides comprehensive testing of both primary and DR site operations.

**Market Impact**: Low - This is a scheduled mock trading session with no real financial obligations. Trading schedules include pre-open, normal market, T+0 market, block deal windows, auction sessions, and contingency operations.

**Technical Impact**: Medium - Members need to coordinate system upgrades, test connectivity, and ensure compatibility with the new NEAT+ version. The mock session tests various trading modes including special pre-open sessions, call auction for illiquid securities, and DR site switchover procedures.

**Timeline**: Members have approximately one month (until November 01, 2025) to complete the transition from NEAT+ 7.8.3 to 7.8.4.
