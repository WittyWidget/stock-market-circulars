## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the launch of Qsif Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund NFO by Quant Mutual Fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be available for transactions from October 24, 2025, to November 07, 2025, until 11:59 pm. The fund offers six plan variants across Direct and Regular plans with IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, and Growth options.

## Key Points

- NFO period: October 24, 2025 to November 07, 2025 (till 11:59 pm)
- Fund house: Quant Mutual Fund
- Fund category: Equity
- Six scheme variants available with unique ISINs
- Allotment date: November 12, 2025
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Scheme Details

**Direct Plans:**
- Qsif Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund Direct Plan IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF966L30167)
- Qsif Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund Direct Plan IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF966L30175)
- Qsif Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund Direct Plan Growth (ISIN: INF966L30159)

**Regular Plans:**
- Qsif Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund Regular Plan IDCW Payout (ISIN: INF966L30191)
- Qsif Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund Regular Plan IDCW Reinvestment (ISIN: INF966L30209)
- Qsif Equity Ex-Top 100 Long-Short Fund Regular Plan Growth (ISIN: INF966L30183)

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before November 12, 2025
- All transactions to be conducted through NSE MF Invest Platform during the specified NFO period

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date:** October 24, 2025
- **NFO End Date:** November 07, 2025 (11:59 pm)
- **Allotment Date:** November 12, 2025
- **Payment Deadline:** November 12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch with minimal market impact. The fund is a specialized equity long-short strategy focusing on stocks outside the top 100, targeting sophisticated investors. The availability on NSE MF Invest Platform provides members with access to a new investment product from Quant Mutual Fund. No direct impact on stock trading or market operations is expected.
