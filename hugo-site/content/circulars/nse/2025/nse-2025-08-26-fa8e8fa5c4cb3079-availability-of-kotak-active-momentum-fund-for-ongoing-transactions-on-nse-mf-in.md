## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has announced that Kotak Active Momentum Fund will be reopened for ongoing transactions on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The fund will be available for subscriptions, redemptions, and various investment facilities starting August 28, 2025.

## Key Points

- Kotak Active Momentum Fund reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Six scheme variants available (Direct and Regular options with Growth and IDCW plans)
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be available
- All schemes categorized under Equity category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are informed of the fund availability for client transactions. No specific compliance actions required.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 28, 2025 - Fund reopens for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions

## Impact Assessment

Positive impact for investors seeking exposure to Kotak Active Momentum Fund. Provides additional investment options on NSE MF Invest Platform with systematic investment and withdrawal facilities. Limited broader market impact as this is a single fund reopening announcement.
