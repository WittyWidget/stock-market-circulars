## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of Glottis Limited's equity shares on the Capital Market segment effective October 07, 2025. The company issued 92,403,000 equity shares of Rs. 2/- face value at an issue price of Rs. 129/-. The security will trade under symbol "GLOTTIS" with ISIN INE0TQE01026 and will participate in pre-open session for IPO category scrips.

## Key Points

- **Trading Symbol**: GLOTTIS
- **ISIN**: INE0TQE01026
- **Face Value**: Rs. 2/- per share
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 129/- per share
- **Total Shares**: 92,403,000 equity shares
- **Market Lot**: 1 share
- **Series**: EQ (Equity)
- Pre-open session applicable as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012

## Lock-in Details

- **18,942,700 shares**: Locked until October 06, 2028 (3-year lock-in)
- **48,845,660 shares**: Locked until October 06, 2026 (1-year lock-in)
- **816,000 shares**: Locked until April 06, 2026
- **2,141,877 shares**: Locked until November 01, 2025
- **2,141,878 shares**: Locked until December 31, 2025
- **19,514,885 shares**: Free for trading

- Lock-in provisions must be observed for respective share categories

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine IPO listing with no market-wide impact. The listing adds a new logistics company to NSE's equity segment. With approximately 21% of shares free for immediate trading, liquidity may be limited initially. The staggered lock-in structure with major portions locked until 2026 and 2028 indicates significant promoter/anchor investor holdings.
