## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced a change in the ISIN code for equity shares of Agi Infra Limited (AGIIL). The new ISIN code INE976R01033 will be effective from October 10, 2025, following a share sub-division from Rs. 5/- to Re. 1/-.

## Key Points

- Company: Agi Infra Limited (Symbol: AGIIL)
- New ISIN: INE976R01033
- Reason: Sub-division of shares from Rs. 5/- per share to Re. 1/- per share
- The new ISIN will be effective for all trades executed on and from the ex-date
- NSE Circular Reference: 1945/2025
- NSE Download Reference: NSE/CML/70653

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard corporate action circular notifying members of an ISIN code change resulting from share subdivision.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their systems to reflect the new ISIN code INE976R01033 for AGIIL
- All trades executed on or after October 10, 2025 must use the new ISIN code
- Members should ensure proper mapping and transition to the new ISIN in their internal systems

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: October 07, 2025
- **Ex-Date**: October 10, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 10, 2025 (for all trades)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium. The share subdivision increases the number of outstanding shares by a factor of 5, making shares more affordable for retail investors while maintaining the same market capitalization.

**Operational Impact**: Medium. Exchange members and market infrastructure providers need to update their systems to accommodate the new ISIN code. The subdivision will also require adjustments to market lot sizes and any existing orders or positions.

**Investor Impact**: Positive. Lower face value shares typically improve liquidity and accessibility for retail investors. Existing shareholders will receive 5 shares for every 1 share held.
