## Summary

NSE announces the upcoming listing of equity shares of Laxmi India Finance Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol LAXMIINDIA with ISIN INE06WU01026.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Laxmi India Finance Limited
- Trading Symbol: LAXMIINDIA
- ISIN: INE06WU01026
- Listing Type: IPO (Initial Public Offering)
- Exchange: National Stock Exchange of India

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are informed about the forthcoming listing for awareness and preparation purposes.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: August 04, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This is a standard IPO listing announcement that will introduce a new equity share to the NSE trading platform. The actual listing date will be communicated separately, allowing market participants to prepare for trading the new security.
