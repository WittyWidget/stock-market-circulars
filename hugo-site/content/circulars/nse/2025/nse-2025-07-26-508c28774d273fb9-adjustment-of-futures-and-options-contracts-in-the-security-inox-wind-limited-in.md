## Summary

NSE has issued a circular regarding the adjustment of futures and options contracts for INOX WIND LIMITED (INOXWIND) following a corporate action. This adjustment ensures continuity of derivative contracts while reflecting the underlying stock's corporate action impact.

## Key Points

- Contract adjustments will be applied to all existing INOXWIND futures and options positions
- New contract specifications will be effective from the specified ex-date
- Existing open positions will be automatically adjusted by the exchange
- Members must inform clients about the contract modifications

## Regulatory Changes

- Modified strike prices and lot sizes for INOXWIND options contracts
- Updated contract specifications for futures contracts
- Revised margin requirements reflecting new contract terms
- Adjusted position limits based on new contract specifications

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must update their systems with new contract specifications
- Mandatory client communication about position adjustments
- Risk management systems must incorporate revised contract terms
- Clearing members must ensure adequate margin coverage for adjusted positions

## Important Dates

- Circular Issue Date: July 25, 2025
- Ex-Date: As specified in the corporate action announcement
- Last Trading Date: Final trading day under old contract specifications
- Settlement Date: Settlement of adjusted contracts

## Impact Assessment

The contract adjustment is a routine procedure following corporate actions and ensures that derivative instruments continue to reflect the underlying security's value accurately. Existing position holders will see automatic adjustments to their positions, maintaining economic equivalence while updating contract specifications. Market participants should review their positions and adjust risk parameters accordingly.
