## Summary

NSE announces the transfer of Current Infraprojects Limited from the trade for trade segment (series: ST) to the rolling segment (series: SM) effective September 17, 2025, following SEBI guidelines.

## Key Points

- Current Infraprojects Limited (symbol: CURRENT) will move from trade for trade to rolling segment
- Transfer effective from September 17, 2025
- Change from series ST to series SM
- Follows previous exchange circular NSE/CML/69976 dated September 02, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

Transfer from trade for trade segment to rolling segment as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Compliance Requirements

Members should note the segment change and adjust trading practices accordingly for Current Infraprojects Limited shares.

## Important Dates

- **September 17, 2025**: Effective date for segment transfer

## Impact Assessment

The transfer to rolling segment will provide increased trading flexibility for Current Infraprojects Limited shares, allowing for T+1 settlement instead of immediate delivery requirements of trade for trade segment.
