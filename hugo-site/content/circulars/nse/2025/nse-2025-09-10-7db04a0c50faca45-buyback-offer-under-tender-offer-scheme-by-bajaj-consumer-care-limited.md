## Summary

National Stock Exchange announces the availability of its web-based bidding platform for Bajaj Consumer Care Limited's buyback offer under the Tender Offer scheme. The offer allows buyback of up to 64,34,482 equity shares at a fixed price of Rs. 290 per share.

## Key Points

- Buyback offer period: September 11-17, 2025
- Bidding time: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM daily
- Custodial confirmation: 9:15 AM to 4:00 PM on issue closure
- Live bidding URL: https://eipo.nseindia.com
- All Capital Market segment trading members eligible to participate
- Existing IPO bidding platform users can use same credentials

## Regulatory Changes

Offer conducted in accordance with Tender Offer Scheme as per Exchange circular NSE/CMTR/52776 dated June 29, 2022.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members without system access must use admin terminal
- Admin login format: Adminxxxxx (where xxxxx is Member Code)
- Members must create branches and users through admin terminal for bidding participation
- Eight investor categories defined with specific status codes (FII, MF, IC, FI, OTH, CO, IND, NOH)

## Important Dates

- Issue Period: September 11-17, 2025
- Daily bidding hours: 9:15 AM - 3:30 PM
- Custodial confirmation deadline: 4:00 PM on final day

## Impact Assessment

Medium impact corporate action affecting BAJAJCON shareholders with specific participation requirements. Provides structured buyback mechanism through NSE's web-based platform with defined investor categories and operational parameters.
