## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has notified the listing and admission to trading of equity shares issued pursuant to a bonus issue by Jakharia Fabric Limited. The 81,27,660 bonus shares with ISIN INE00N401018 will be available for trading on the Capital Market segment from October 10, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Jakharia Fabric Limited (Symbol: JAKHARIA)
- Number of bonus shares: 81,27,660
- ISIN: INE00N401018
- Distinctive Number Range: 4063831 to 12191490
- Securities admitted under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

Members must use the designated security codes (Symbol: JAKHARIA, ISIN: INE00N401018) for trading purposes on the NSE system from the effective date.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: October 07, 2025
- Record Date: October 08, 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: October 09, 2025
- Trading Effective Date: October 10, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a company-specific corporate action with limited market-wide impact. The bonus issue increases the outstanding share capital of Jakharia Fabric Limited, providing additional liquidity for existing shareholders. No impact on other market participants or trading operations beyond the specific security.
