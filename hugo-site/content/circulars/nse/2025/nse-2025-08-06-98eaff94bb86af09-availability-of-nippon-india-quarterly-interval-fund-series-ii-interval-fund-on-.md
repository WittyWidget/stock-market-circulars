## Summary

NSE announces the availability of Nippon India Quarterly Interval Fund - Series II (Interval Fund) on the NSE MF Invest Platform for a specified transaction period from August 07, 2025, to August 08, 2025. The fund offers 6 different plan options across IDCW and Growth categories.

## Key Points

- Transaction window: August 07-08, 2025
- Available during normal business hours
- Both subscription and redemption requests accepted
- NAV applicable based on cut-off timing and fund confirmation
- 6 scheme variants available (IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, Growth, and Direct versions)
- All schemes categorized as Debt funds

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must follow prescribed cut-off timings for transactions
- Fund confirmation must be uploaded/received for NAV applicability
- Transaction requests accepted only during specified business hours

## Important Dates

- **Transaction Period**: August 07, 2025 to August 08, 2025
- **Business Hours**: Normal business hours during the transaction period

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine notification for mutual fund availability. Affects only investors interested in this specific interval fund during the limited transaction window.
