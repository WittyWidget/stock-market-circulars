## Summary

NSE has announced the listing of further securities for 23 companies on the Capital Market segment, effective September 08, 2025. The circular covers equity shares allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP) and other further issues for companies including Aarti Industries, Computer Age Management Services, Chalet Hotels, Cholamandalam Investment and Finance Company, Dr. Reddy's Laboratories, and many others.

## Key Points

- 23 companies will have additional securities listed on NSE Capital Market segment
- Most allotments are equity shares issued under ESOP schemes
- All securities will be identified by designated codes for trading purposes
- Securities will be pari passu with existing shares
- No lock-in restrictions apply to these allotments

## Regulatory Changes

None - this is a routine listing notification under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for these securities
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes as mentioned in the annexure
- Market lot size of 1 applies to all listed securities

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 08, 2025
- **Circular Date**: September 05, 2025
- Various allotment dates ranging from August 12, 2025 to September 02, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Routine operational impact with increased share count for the listed companies. The ESOP allotments represent normal corporate actions and employee benefit exercises. No significant market disruption expected as these are additional shares of existing listed companies with established trading patterns.
