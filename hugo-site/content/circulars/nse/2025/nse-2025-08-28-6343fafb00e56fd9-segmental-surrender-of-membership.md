## Summary

NSE has approved the request of ACML Capital Markets Limited to surrender its membership in the Currency Segment, effective August 26, 2025. This is an administrative notification informing all members of the approved segmental surrender.

## Key Points

- ACML Capital Markets Limited (SEBI Registration: INZ000260134) has surrendered Currency Segment membership
- Surrender became effective on August 26, 2025
- All NSE members are notified to take note of this change

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is an administrative approval of a member's voluntary surrender request.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members are requested to take note of the membership surrender
- No specific compliance actions required from other members

## Important Dates

- **August 26, 2025**: Effective date of Currency Segment membership surrender
- **August 28, 2025**: Circular notification date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves only one member's voluntary surrender of Currency Segment membership. No operational disruptions or regulatory implications for other market participants.
