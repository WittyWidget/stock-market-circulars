## Summary

NSE has announced the launch of Bajaj Finserv Equity Savings Fund NFO on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The fund will be available for transactions from July 28, 2025, until 11:59 PM on August 11, 2025. Six different plan variants are being offered across Direct and Regular plans with IDCW Payout, IDCW Reinvestment, and Growth options.

## Key Points

- Bajaj Finserv Equity Savings Fund NFO available on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Transaction window: July 28, 2025 to August 11, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- Six scheme variants offered with different plan types and dividend options
- All schemes categorized under Equity category
- Allotment date scheduled for August 19, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard NFO launch notification for mutual fund platform operations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received in designated Clearing Corporation account on or before NFO closing date
- Funds can be received through any approved payment mode
- All transactions must be completed within the specified NFO window period

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: July 28, 2025
- **NFO End Date**: August 11, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: August 19, 2025
- **Fund Settlement**: On or before NFO closing date

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch with minimal market impact. The circular primarily serves as an informational notice to NSE members about the availability of a new mutual fund scheme on the platform. No changes to existing trading mechanisms or compliance requirements beyond standard NFO procedures.
