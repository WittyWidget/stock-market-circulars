- Company Name: Brigade Hotel Ventures Limited
- Trading Symbol: BRIGHOTEL
- ISIN: INE03NU01014
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Circular reference: NSE/CML/69368, dated July 30, 2025

Medium impact for market participants interested in hotel and hospitality sector investments. The listing will provide a new investment opportunity in Brigade Hotel Ventures Limited, expanding the available equity options on NSE.
