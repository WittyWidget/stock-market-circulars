## Summary

NSE has announced that Sah Polymers Limited will undergo a name and symbol change effective August 22, 2025. The company name will change to Aeroflex Neu Limited and the trading symbol will change from SAH to AERONEU.

## Key Points

- Company name changing from Sah Polymers Limited to Aeroflex Neu Limited
- Trading symbol changing from SAH to AERONEU
- Changes effective from August 22, 2025
- All exchange members have been notified

## Regulatory Changes

This is a corporate action involving name and symbol change, following standard NSE procedures for such changes.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their systems to reflect the new company name and symbol
- Trading in the new symbol AERONEU will commence from August 22, 2025

## Important Dates

- **August 18, 2025**: Circular issued
- **August 22, 2025**: Name and symbol change becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

The change affects trading identification and requires system updates by brokers and market participants. Shareholders and investors need to be aware of the new company name and trading symbol for future transactions.
