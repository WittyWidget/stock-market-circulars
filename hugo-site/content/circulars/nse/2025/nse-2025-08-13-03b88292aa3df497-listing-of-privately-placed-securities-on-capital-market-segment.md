## Summary

NSE has admitted two privately placed debt securities to trading on the capital market segment effective August 14, 2025. The securities include Alpha Alternatives Financial Services' market-linked instrument maturing in 2029 and NHPC's 6.40% coupon bond maturing in 2027.

## Key Points

- Two new debt securities admitted for trading on NSE capital market segment
- Both securities will trade with market lot size of 1
- Trading identification through designated security codes only
- Effective date: August 14, 2025

## Securities Details

**Alpha Alternatives Financial Services Private Limited (AAFS29C)**
- Issue: AAFS Market Linked 2029
- Coupon: Market Linked
- Maturity: November 8, 2029
- ISIN: INE0L6807237

**NHPC Limited (640NHPCL27)**
- Issue: NHPC 6.40% 2027
- Coupon: 6.4%
- Maturity: August 12, 2027
- ISIN: INE848E08375

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for system identification
- All trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes
- Securities subject to Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 14, 2025
- **AAFS Maturity**: November 8, 2029
- **NHPC Maturity**: August 12, 2027

## Impact Assessment

Routine addition of two debt securities to the trading platform. Limited market impact as these are privately placed instruments with standard lot sizes. Provides additional investment options for institutional and qualified investors in the debt market segment.
