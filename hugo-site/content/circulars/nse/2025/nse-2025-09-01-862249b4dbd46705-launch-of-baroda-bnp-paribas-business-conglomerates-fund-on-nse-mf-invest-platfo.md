## Summary

Baroda BNP Paribas Mutual Fund is launching the Business Conglomerates Fund on NSE MF Invest Platform. The fund will be available for subscription from September 2, 2025 to September 15, 2025 till 11:59 PM, with six different plan options across Regular and Direct categories.

## Key Points

- New equity fund launch by Baroda BNP Paribas Mutual Fund
- Six scheme variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes categorized under Equity category
- Allotment date scheduled for September 22, 2025
- Clear funds must be received in designated Clearing Corporation account on or before NFO closing date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before NFO closing date
- Standard mutual fund transaction compliance applies

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: September 2, 2025
- **NFO End Date**: September 15, 2025 (till 11:59 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: September 22, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this is a routine mutual fund launch. The fund focuses on business conglomerates segment and provides investment options for retail and institutional investors through NSE's mutual fund platform. No immediate effect on stock prices or market operations expected.
