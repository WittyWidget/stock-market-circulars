## Summary

NSE has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of All Time Plastics Limited following their Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol 'ALLTIME' with ISIN INE0GV601021. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: All Time Plastics Limited
- Trading Symbol: ALLTIME
- ISIN: INE0GV601021
- Security Type: Equity Shares (IPO)
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are informed about the upcoming listing for their awareness and preparation.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: August 13, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This represents a new equity listing on NSE, expanding the exchange's listed securities portfolio. The impact is considered medium as it introduces a new trading instrument for market participants in the plastics sector.
