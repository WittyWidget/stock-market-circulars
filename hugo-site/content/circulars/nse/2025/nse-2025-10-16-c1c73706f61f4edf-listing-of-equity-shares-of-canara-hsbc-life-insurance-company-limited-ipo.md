## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has admitted Canara HSBC Life Insurance Company Limited's equity shares to trading on the Capital Market segment effective October 17, 2025. The IPO comprises 950 million equity shares of Rs. 10 face value each, issued at Rs. 106 per share. The security will trade under the symbol CANHLIFE (ISIN: INE01TY01017) with a market lot of 1 share and will be part of the pre-open session for IPO category scrips.

## Key Points

- **Symbol:** CANHLIFE
- **ISIN:** INE01TY01017
- **Total Shares:** 950,000,000 equity shares
- **Face Value:** Rs. 10 per share
- **Issue Price:** Rs. 106 per share
- **Market Lot:** 1 share
- **Series:** EQ (Equity)
- **Listing Date:** October 17, 2025
- **Pre-open Session:** Applicable as per SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012

## Impact Assessment

This IPO represents a significant listing in the insurance sector, with Canara HSBC Life Insurance Company Limited bringing 950 million shares to the market at a premium of Rs. 96 over face value (issue price Rs. 106 vs face value Rs. 10). The staggered lock-in periods indicate a mix of promoter holdings, pre-IPO investors, and freely tradable shares. Approximately 17.5% of shares (166.715 million) will be immediately available for trading without lock-in restrictions. The listing expands investor options in the life insurance sector and adds substantial market capitalization to the exchange.
