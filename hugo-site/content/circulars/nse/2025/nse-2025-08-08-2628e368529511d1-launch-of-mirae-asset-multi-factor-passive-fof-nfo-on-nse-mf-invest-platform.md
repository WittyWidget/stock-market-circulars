## Summary

NSE announces the launch of Mirae Asset Multi Factor Passive Fund of Funds (FOF) New Fund Offer on NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO will be available for subscription from August 11, 2025 to August 25, 2025 until 11:59 PM, with allotment scheduled for August 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- Six scheme variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes categorized under Equity category
- Subscription period: August 11-25, 2025 (until 11:59 PM)
- Allotment date: August 29, 2025
- Clear funds must be received in designated Clearing Corporation account by NFO closing date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received through any payment mode in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before NFO closing date
- Standard NFO compliance procedures apply

## Important Dates

- **NFO Start Date**: August 11, 2025
- **NFO End Date**: August 25, 2025 (11:59 PM)
- **Allotment Date**: August 29, 2025
- **Fund Clearance Deadline**: On or before NFO closing date

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine NFO launch. Affects only NSE MF Invest Platform users interested in passive fund of funds investment options. No direct impact on equity markets or trading operations.
