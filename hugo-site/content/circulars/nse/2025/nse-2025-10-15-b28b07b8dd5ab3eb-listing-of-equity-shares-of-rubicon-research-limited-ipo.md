## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has informed all members about the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Rubicon Research Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will be listed with the symbol RUBICON and ISIN INE506V01022. The exact date of listing will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Rubicon Research Limited
- Trading Symbol: RUBICON
- ISIN: INE506V01022
- Listing type: IPO (Initial Public Offering)
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes associated with this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements. This is an informational circular for exchange members regarding the upcoming listing.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: October 15, 2025
- Listing Date: To be informed through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine listing notification with minimal market impact. The circular serves to inform exchange members about a new security that will be available for trading. The actual listing date announcement will be more significant for trading purposes.
