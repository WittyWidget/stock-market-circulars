## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of privately placed commercial paper securities on its debt market segment effective October 09, 2025. The circular includes 8 commercial paper listings (5 fresh issues and 3 re-issues) from companies including Godrej Properties Limited, Godrej Industries Limited, Tata Projects Limited, JM Financial Services Limited, L&T Finance Limited, and Kotak Securities Limited. All securities have been admitted to dealings under Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Key Points

- 5 fresh issue commercial papers listed from Godrej Properties, Godrej Industries, Tata Projects, and JM Financial Services
- 3 re-issue commercial papers listed from L&T Finance and Kotak Securities
- Total quantity ranges from 1,500 to 5,000 securities per issue
- Face value of all securities is Rs. 5,00,000
- Redemption periods range from 21 days (October 29, 2025) to 11 months (September 10, 2026)
- Allotment dates are October 08-09, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine notification under existing Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements for market participants. The circular is informational, notifying members of newly listed debt securities available for trading.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date of Listing**: October 09, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: October 08-09, 2025
- **Redemption Dates**: Range from October 29, 2025 to September 10, 2026 depending on the security

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. This is a routine administrative notification of debt securities listing. The commercial papers provide short to medium-term funding instruments for the listed companies and investment options for institutional investors in the debt market.

**Operational Impact**: No operational changes required for trading members. The securities are now available for trading on the debt market segment with designated security codes and ISINs as specified in the annexure.

**Investor Impact**: Provides additional commercial paper investment opportunities for institutional investors with varying maturity profiles (21 days to 11 months) from established corporate issuers across real estate, industrial, and financial services sectors.
