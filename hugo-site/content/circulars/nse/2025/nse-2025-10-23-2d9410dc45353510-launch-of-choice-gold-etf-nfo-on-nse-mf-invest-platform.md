## Summary

National Stock Exchange has announced the launch of Choice Gold ETF New Fund Offer (NFO) from Choice Mutual Fund on the NSE MF Invest Platform. The NFO subscription period will be open from October 24, 2025 to October 31, 2025 till 11:59 pm, with allotment scheduled for November 4, 2025.

## Key Points

- Choice Gold ETF - Direct Growth (ISIN: INF2KCX01012) will be available for subscription
- The scheme is categorized as Debt category
- NFO opens on October 24, 2025 and closes on October 31, 2025 at 11:59 pm
- Allotment date is scheduled for November 4, 2025
- Members must ensure clear funds are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before the allotment date

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must ensure clear funds through any payment mode are received in the designated Clearing Corporation account on or before November 04, 2025
- Members need to facilitate customer transactions during the NFO subscription period

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** October 23, 2025
- **NFO Start Date:** October 24, 2025
- **NFO End Date:** October 31, 2025 (till 11:59 pm)
- **Allotment Date:** November 4, 2025
- **Funds Settlement Deadline:** November 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine NFO launch announcement with minimal market impact. The circular provides operational information for exchange members to facilitate customer subscriptions in the Choice Gold ETF through the NSE MF Invest Platform. The launch adds another gold ETF investment option for investors but does not affect stock trading operations or broader market dynamics. Members should update their systems and customer service teams to handle NFO subscription requests during the specified period.
