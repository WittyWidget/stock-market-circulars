## Summary

NSE announces the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Gem Aromatics Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol GEMAROMA with ISIN INE06XZ01023. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Gem Aromatics Limited
- Trading Symbol: GEMAROMA
- ISIN: INE06XZ01023
- Listing follows completion of IPO process
- Exact listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification following IPO completion.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members are informed about the upcoming listing
- Members should prepare systems for trading the new security
- Standard listing compliance procedures apply

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: August 25, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds a new equity option for investors in the aromatics/chemicals sector. Impact is moderate as it expands market choices without affecting existing trading operations or market structure.
