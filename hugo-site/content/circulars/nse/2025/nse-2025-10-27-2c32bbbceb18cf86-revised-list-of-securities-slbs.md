## Summary

NSE Clearing Limited has issued the revised list of securities eligible for transactions under the Securities Lending & Borrowing Scheme (SLBS) for November 2025. This circular, issued under reference NCL/CMPT/70984 on October 27, 2025, provides the updated list in accordance with SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/18/2013 dated May 30, 2013. The list contains over 175 securities spanning various sectors including banking, pharmaceuticals, automotive, energy, and technology.

## Key Points

- Circular Reference: NCL/CMPT/70984, Circular No. 116/2025
- Effective Period: November 2025
- Reference to SEBI circular CIR/MRD/DP/18/2013 dated May 30, 2013
- Reference to NSE Clearing consolidated circular NCL/CMPT/67763 dated April 30, 2025
- The list includes major stocks like Adani Group companies, Axis Bank, Bajaj Group companies, BPCL, Asian Paints, Apollo Hospitals, and numerous ETFs
- Applies to all participants and custodians in the SLBS scheme

## Regulatory Changes

This is a routine monthly update with no new regulatory changes. The circular maintains compliance with existing SEBI regulations governing securities lending and borrowing schemes.

## Compliance Requirements

- All SLBS participants and custodians must refer to the updated list for eligible securities
- Only securities listed in Annexure-I are available for SLBS transactions during November 2025
- Participants must ensure their transactions comply with the revised list

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: October 27, 2025
- Applicable Period: November 2025

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine operational update with medium impact on market participants actively involved in securities lending and borrowing. The comprehensive list of over 175 securities provides substantial options for SLBS transactions. Participants should review the list to adjust their lending/borrowing strategies for November.
