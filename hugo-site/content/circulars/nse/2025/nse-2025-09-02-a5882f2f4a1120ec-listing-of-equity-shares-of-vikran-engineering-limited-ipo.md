## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced the forthcoming listing of equity shares of Vikran Engineering Limited following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will be listed under the symbol VIKRAN with ISIN INE01R501028.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Vikran Engineering Limited
- Trading Symbol: VIKRAN
- ISIN: INE01R501028
- Listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes announced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are informed about the upcoming listing and should prepare for trading in the new security once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: September 02, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

Routine IPO listing with standard market impact. Trading members should prepare systems for the new security. The actual market impact will depend on the IPO subscription levels and initial trading performance.
