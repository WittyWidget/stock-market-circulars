## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the suspension of trading in equity shares of Ind-Swift Limited (Symbol: INDSWFTLTD) effective from August 14, 2025. The suspension is implemented due to a Scheme of Arrangement and will take effect from the closing hours of trading on August 13, 2025.

## Key Points

- Company: Ind-Swift Limited
- Stock Symbol: INDSWFTLTD
- Series: EQ (Equity)
- Suspension effective from: August 14, 2025
- Reason: Scheme of Arrangement
- Regulatory basis: Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

The suspension is implemented under Regulation 3.1.2 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A, which provides the framework for suspending securities from trading under specific circumstances.

## Compliance Requirements

- All NSE members must cease trading in INDSWFTLTD equity shares from August 14, 2025
- Trading systems will reflect the suspension status
- Members should inform their clients about the trading suspension

## Important Dates

- **August 8, 2025**: Circular issued
- **August 13, 2025**: Last day of trading (closing hours)
- **August 14, 2025**: Trading suspension becomes effective

## Impact Assessment

The trading suspension will completely halt secondary market trading in Ind-Swift Limited equity shares on NSE. This represents a significant development for existing shareholders who will be unable to trade the stock during the suspension period. The Scheme of Arrangement typically indicates corporate restructuring activities that may affect shareholder rights and company structure.
