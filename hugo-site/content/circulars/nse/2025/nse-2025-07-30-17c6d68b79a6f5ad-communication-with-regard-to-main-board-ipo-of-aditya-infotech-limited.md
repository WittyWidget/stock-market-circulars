## Summary

NSE has issued a clarification regarding the Main Board IPO of Aditya Infotech Limited, emphasizing that the company is not affiliated with the Aditya Birla Group despite the similarity in names. The clarification comes from the company's merchant bankers to prevent potential investor confusion.

## Key Points

- Aditya Infotech Limited IPO is open for subscription from July 29-31, 2025
- The company has no affiliation with Aditya Birla Group
- Clarification issued by merchant bankers to avoid name-based confusion
- Investors advised to refer to Red Herring Prospectus dated July 23, 2025
- Communication targeted at all IPO applicants

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a clarification circular.

## Compliance Requirements

- Investors must refer to the Red Herring Prospectus dated July 23, 2025 before making investment decisions
- Due diligence required to understand company details and avoid confusion with similarly named entities

## Important Dates

- IPO Subscription Period: July 29, 2025 to July 31, 2025
- Red Herring Prospectus Date: July 23, 2025
- Circular Date: July 30, 2025

## Impact Assessment

This clarification helps maintain market integrity by preventing investor confusion that could arise from name similarity with the well-known Aditya Birla Group. It ensures informed investment decisions during the IPO subscription period and protects both the issuing company and investors from potential misunderstandings.
