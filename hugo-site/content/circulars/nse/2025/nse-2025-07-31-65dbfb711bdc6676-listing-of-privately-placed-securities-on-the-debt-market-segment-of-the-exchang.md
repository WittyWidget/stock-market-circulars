## Summary

NSE has admitted multiple privately placed securities to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective July 31, 2025. The listing includes commercial papers from Angel One Limited, Godrej Industries Limited, and Welspun Corp Limited, along with debt securities from Godrej Finance Limited and Godrej Seeds & Genetics Limited.

## Key Points

- Multiple commercial papers and debt securities admitted to NSE debt market trading
- Securities from companies including Angel One, Godrej group companies, and Welspun Corp
- Commercial papers with tenures ranging from 2-6 months
- Debt securities with longer tenures up to 5 years
- Face values of ₹5,00,000 for commercial papers and ₹1,00,000 for debt securities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced - this is a routine listing notification under existing NSE Debt Market Trading Regulations 3.1.1.

## Compliance Requirements

No new compliance requirements for market participants. Standard debt market trading rules apply to these newly listed securities.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: July 31, 2025 - Securities admitted to debt market trading
- **Allotment Dates**: July 29-30, 2025 for all listed securities
- **Redemption Dates**: Range from September 2025 to July 2030 depending on security

## Impact Assessment

Positive impact on debt market liquidity and provides additional investment options for institutional investors. Companies gain access to organized debt market trading for their privately placed securities, potentially improving secondary market liquidity and pricing discovery.
