## Summary

NSE has admitted three privately placed debt securities to trading on the capital market segment effective August 19, 2025. The securities include bonds from ESAF Small Finance Bank Limited, L&T Finance Limited, and Sammaan Capital Limited with varying coupon rates and maturity dates.

## Key Points

- Three new debt securities admitted to NSE capital market segment
- All securities have market lot size of 1
- Trading to commence August 19, 2025
- Securities identified by designated codes on trading system

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - routine listing process under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for identification
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes
- Compliance with NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 19, 2025
- **ESFB Bond Maturity**: May 14, 2031
- **LTFL Bond Maturity**: March 15, 2028
- **SCL Bond Maturity**: August 14, 2035

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this involves routine listing of privately placed debt securities. Provides additional investment options for institutional investors in the debt market segment.
