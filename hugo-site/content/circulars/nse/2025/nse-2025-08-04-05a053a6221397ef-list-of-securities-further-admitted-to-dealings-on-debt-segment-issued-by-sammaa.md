## Summary

NSE has admitted 24 new debt securities issued by Sammaan Capital Limited to dealings on the Debt Segment. These securities include both fixed coupon and zero coupon debentures with maturity periods ranging from 2027 to 2035.

## Key Points

- 24 new debt securities admitted to NSE Debt Segment
- All securities issued by Sammaan Capital Limited
- Maturity dates range from August 1, 2027 to August 1, 2035
- Mix of fixed coupon (periodic payment) and zero coupon (payment on maturity) instruments
- Coupon rates range from 8.42% to 9.95% for fixed coupon securities
- All securities have CRISIL AA & ICRA AA credit ratings
- Tick size of 0.01 for all instruments
- All are publicly issued securities

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine admission of securities to the debt segment.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members can commence trading in these securities on the Debt Segment
- Standard debt segment trading rules and procedures apply
- Securities will trade under their respective symbols (RDNGDB SCL series and RDNGDC SCL series)

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: August 4, 2025
- Issue Date: August 1, 2025 for all securities
- Maturity Dates: Range from August 1, 2027 to August 1, 2035

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of debt securities. Provides additional investment options for debt market participants seeking AA-rated corporate bonds with various tenor options. The securities offer competitive coupon rates in the current interest rate environment.
