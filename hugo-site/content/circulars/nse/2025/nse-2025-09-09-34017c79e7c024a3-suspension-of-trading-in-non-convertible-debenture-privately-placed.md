## Summary

NSE has announced the suspension of trading in Non-Convertible Debenture (Privately Placed) of TMF Holdings Limited effective September 10, 2025. The suspension is due to the redemption of the debenture.

## Key Points

- Company: TMF Holdings Limited
- ISIN: INE909H08451
- Suspension effective from September 10, 2025
- Reason: Redemption of the debenture
- Action taken under Regulation 3.1.2 of NSE Debt Market (Trading) Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No new regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine suspension under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must cease trading in the specified NCD from the suspension date
- No new positions can be created in this instrument
- Existing positions will be settled as per normal settlement cycle

## Important Dates

- **September 10, 2025**: Effective date of trading suspension
- **September 9, 2025**: Circular issuance date

## Impact Assessment

Limited market impact as this affects only a single privately placed NCD. The suspension is routine due to redemption and will not affect broader market operations. Investors holding this specific debenture should coordinate with their brokers for settlement procedures.
