## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of two privately placed debt securities of Piramal Finance Limited on the capital market segment, effective July 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Two debt securities of Piramal Finance Limited admitted to NSE trading
- PFL 9.00% 2027 bond with ISIN INE202B07JT0, symbol 9PFL27
- PFL 9.10% JUN 2030 bond with ISIN INE202B07JU8, symbol 91PFL30
- Both securities have market lot size of 1
- Trading identification by designated security codes only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - routine listing notification under existing regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading must use designated security codes (9PFL27 and 91PFL30)
- Securities must be traded in specified lot sizes (1 unit each)
- Compliance with NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: July 30, 2025
- **Maturity Dates**: 
  - 9PFL27: June 28, 2027
  - 91PFL30: June 28, 2030

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of debt securities. Provides additional investment options for institutional investors in Piramal Finance debt instruments with attractive coupon rates of 9.00% and 9.10%.
