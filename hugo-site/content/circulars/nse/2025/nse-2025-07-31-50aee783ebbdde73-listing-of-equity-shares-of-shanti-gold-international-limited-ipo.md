## Summary

NSE announces the listing of equity shares of Shanti Gold International Limited following its IPO, effective from August 01, 2025. The company will trade under symbol SHANTIGOLD with ISIN INE06ZD01017, face value of Rs. 10/- and IPO issue price of Rs. 199/-.

## Key Points

- Company Symbol: SHANTIGOLD
- ISIN: INE06ZD01017
- Face Value: Rs. 10/- per share
- IPO Issue Price: Rs. 199/- per share
- Total shares: 72,096,000 equity shares
- Market lot size: 1
- Series: EQ (Equity)
- Trading effective from: August 01, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

The security will be part of pre-open session for IPO and Other category of scrips as per SEBI circular no. CIR/MRD/DP/01/2012 & CIR/MRD/DP/02/2012 dated January 20, 2012.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use designated security codes for trading
- Trading must be conducted in specified lot sizes
- Security identification only by designated codes on the system

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 01, 2025
- **Lock-in periods**: Various dates ranging from August 28, 2025 to August 05, 2028 for different share categories
- **Financial Year**: April 2025 - March 2026

## Impact Assessment

The listing adds a new equity security to NSE's Capital Market segment, providing investors access to Shanti Gold International Limited shares. The company operates in the gold sector with registered office in Mumbai. The detailed lock-in schedule indicates structured release of promoter and other restricted holdings over time.
