## Summary

NSE has moved Noida Toll Bridge Company Limited (Symbol: NOIDATOLL, ISIN: INE781B01015) to Stage II of the Graded Surveillance Measure (GSM) framework. This represents an escalation in surveillance measures for the security.

## Key Points

- Security moved to Stage II of GSM framework
- Company: Noida Toll Bridge Company Limited
- Trading Symbol: NOIDATOLL
- ISIN: INE781B01015
- Enhanced monitoring and surveillance measures now applicable

## Regulatory Changes

The security is now subject to Stage II GSM provisions which typically include:
- Enhanced disclosure requirements
- Increased monitoring by exchange
- Additional compliance obligations

## Compliance Requirements

- Company must comply with Stage II GSM requirements
- Enhanced reporting and disclosure obligations
- Adherence to specific trading and surveillance parameters

## Important Dates

- Effective Date: As notified by NSE
- Ongoing compliance required under Stage II framework

## Impact Assessment

Movement to Stage II GSM indicates the exchange has identified concerns requiring enhanced surveillance. This may affect trading volumes and investor confidence. Investors should exercise increased caution and monitor company disclosures closely.
