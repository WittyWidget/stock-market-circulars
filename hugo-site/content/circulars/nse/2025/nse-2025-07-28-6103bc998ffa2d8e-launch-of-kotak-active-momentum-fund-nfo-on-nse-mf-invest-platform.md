## Summary

Kotak Mutual Fund is launching the Kotak Active Momentum Fund NFO on NSE MF Invest Platform. The fund will be available for all transactions from July 29, 2025, to August 12, 2025, with an allotment date of August 20, 2025.

## Key Points

- Six scheme variants available: Regular and Direct plans with Growth, IDCW Payout, and IDCW Reinvestment options
- All schemes categorized as Equity funds
- Transaction period: July 29, 2025 to August 12, 2025
- Allotment date: August 20, 2025
- Each scheme has unique ISIN codes for identification

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must ensure clear funds are received in designated Clearing Corporation account on or before NFO closing date
- All payment modes accepted for fund transfers

## Important Dates

- NFO Start Date: July 29, 2025
- NFO Closing Date: August 12, 2025
- Allotment Date: August 20, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine NFO launch. Provides additional investment options for investors through NSE MF Invest Platform. No operational disruptions expected.
