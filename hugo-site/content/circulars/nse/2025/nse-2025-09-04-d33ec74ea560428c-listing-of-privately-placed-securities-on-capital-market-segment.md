## Summary

NSE has admitted SMFG India Credit Company Limited's privately placed debt securities to trading on the capital market segment. The 7.45% coupon rate senior securities with September 2030 maturity will commence trading from September 5, 2025.

## Key Points

- SMFG India Credit Company Limited debt securities admitted to NSE capital market trading
- Security symbol: 745SICCL30, Series: N0
- Market lot size: 1 unit
- Coupon rate: 7.45% per annum
- ISIN Code: INE535H07CR9

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - standard listing admission process under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use designated security codes for system trading
- Securities must be traded in specified lot sizes only
- Standard capital market segment trading rules apply

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 5, 2025 - Trading commencement
- **Maturity Date**: September 3, 2030 - Security maturity

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine listing of a single corporate debt security. Provides additional investment option for debt market participants seeking medium-term corporate exposure with 7.45% coupon yield.
