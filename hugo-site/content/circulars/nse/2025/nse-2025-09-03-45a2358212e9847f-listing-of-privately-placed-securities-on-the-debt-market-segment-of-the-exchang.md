## Summary

NSE has admitted privately placed commercial papers from 8 companies to dealings on the Debt Market Segment effective September 3, 2025. The securities include commercial papers with various maturity dates ranging from September 25, 2025 to January 27, 2026.

## Key Points

- Total of 10 commercial paper issues from 8 different companies
- All securities have face value of ₹5,00,000
- Allotment dates are September 2-3, 2025
- Issue prices range from ₹486,767.5 to ₹498,076
- Maturity periods vary from 22 days to 147 days

## Companies and Securities Listed

- **Godrej Agrovet Limited**: 1,000 units maturing November 28, 2025
- **ICICI Securities Limited**: 8,000 units maturing September 29, 2025
- **Cholamandalam Investment and Finance Company Limited**: 14,000 units maturing January 27, 2026
- **Godrej Industries Limited**: 1,500 units maturing December 2, 2025
- **Aditya Birla Capital Limited**: 1,000 units maturing December 2, 2025
- **Indian Oil Corporation Limited**: 25,000 units maturing December 1, 2025
- **L&T Finance Limited**: Three separate issues totaling 5,000 units with various September 2025 maturities
- **Kotak Securities Limited**: 5,000 units maturing December 3, 2025

## Regulatory Framework

- Listed under Regulation 3.1.1 of National Stock Exchange Debt Market (Trading) Regulations
- Each security assigned unique ISIN codes for trading

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 3, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: September 2-3, 2025
- **Maturity Range**: September 25, 2025 to January 27, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Routine debt market listing with minimal market impact. Provides additional short-term investment options for institutional investors in commercial paper segment. No compliance actions required from market participants.
