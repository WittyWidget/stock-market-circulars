## Summary

NSE has approved the total surrender of membership for Power Bulls Stock Broker Private Limited (SEBI Registration Number: INZ000282337). The broker's last trade date was July 12, 2023, and the surrender was approved on August 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- Power Bulls Stock Broker Private Limited surrenders NSE membership
- SEBI Registration Number: INZ000282337
- Last trading activity occurred on July 12, 2023
- Surrender approval granted on August 22, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a standard membership surrender process.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are required to take note of this membership surrender.

## Important Dates

- **Last Trade Date**: July 12, 2023
- **Surrender Approval Date**: August 22, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as the broker ceased trading activities in July 2023. This is an administrative closure of an inactive membership.
