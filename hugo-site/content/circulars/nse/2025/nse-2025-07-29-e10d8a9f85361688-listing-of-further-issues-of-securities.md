## Summary

NSE announces the listing of further equity shares for Globe International Carriers Limited (GICL) and Transteel Seating Technologies Limited (TRANSTEEL) on the SME Emerge platform, effective July 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Globe International Carriers Limited: 3,069,147 equity shares at Rs. 95.9 per share from preferential issue
- Transteel Seating Technologies Limited: 1,267,500 equity shares at Rs. 80 per share from warrant conversion
- Both listings effective from July 30, 2025 on NSE SME Emerge platform
- Securities will have designated market lot sizes for trading

## Company Details

**Globe International Carriers Limited (GICL)**
- ISIN: INE947T01014
- Face Value: Rs. 10
- Issue Price: Rs. 95.9
- Market Lot: 1500 shares
- Lock-in Expiry: February 10, 2027
- Allotment Date: June 19, 2025

**Transteel Seating Technologies Limited (TRANSTEEL)**
- ISIN: INE0NVI01020
- Face Value: Rs. 10
- Issue Price: Rs. 80
- Market Lot: 2000 shares
- Lock-in Expiry: January 31, 2026
- Allotment Date: April 18, 2025

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: July 30, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry (GICL)**: February 10, 2027
- **Lock-in Expiry (TRANSTEEL)**: January 31, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Routine listing of additional equity shares for SME companies. Limited market impact as these are further issues of existing listed companies on the SME platform with appropriate lock-in periods.
