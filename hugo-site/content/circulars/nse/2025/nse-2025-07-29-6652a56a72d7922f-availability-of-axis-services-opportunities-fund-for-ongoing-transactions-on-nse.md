## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has announced that the Axis Services Opportunities Fund of Axis Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions along with SIP, STP, and SWP facilities on the NSE MF Invest Platform effective July 30, 2025.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening effective July 30, 2025
- All 6 variants of Axis Services Opportunities Fund will be available
- Includes both Direct and Regular plans with IDCW Reinvestment, IDCW Payout, and Growth options
- SIP, STP, and SWP facilities will be available
- All schemes fall under Equity category

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is a platform availability notification.

## Compliance Requirements

No specific compliance requirements mentioned for members.

## Important Dates

- **July 30, 2025**: Effective date for fund availability on NSE MF Invest Platform

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine fund reopening on the investment platform. Provides additional investment options for investors using NSE MF Invest Platform.
