## Summary

360 ONE Multi Asset Allocation Fund of 360 ONE Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing subscriptions and redemptions along with SIP, STP, SWP facility on NSE MF Invest Platform effective August 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- Fund reopening for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform
- Available services include subscriptions, redemptions, SIP, STP, and SWP facilities
- Six scheme variants available covering Regular and Direct plans with Growth and IDCW options
- All schemes categorized as Debt funds
- ISIN codes provided for all six scheme variants

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned - this is a routine fund availability notification.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are informed of the fund availability for client transactions through the NSE MF Invest Platform.

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 22, 2025 - Fund available for ongoing transactions

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine reopening of a mutual fund for ongoing transactions. Provides additional investment options for investors seeking debt fund exposure through the NSE platform.
