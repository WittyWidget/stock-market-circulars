## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has admitted DSP Nifty500 Flexicap Quality 30 ETF units to trading on the Capital Market segment effective October 16, 2025. The ETF, managed by DSP Asset Managers Private Limited, issued 6,564,592 units with a face value of Rs. 10/- and an issue price of Rs. 10.56. Nine market makers have been designated to provide liquidity for the ETF.

## Key Points

- **Symbol**: FLEXIADD
- **ISIN**: INF740KA1WU9
- **Number of Units Issued**: 6,564,592
- **Face Value**: Rs. 10/- per unit
- **Paid Up Value**: Rs. 10/- per unit
- **Issue Price**: Rs. 10.56 per unit
- **Market Lot**: 1 unit
- **Date of Allotment**: October 10, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Series**: EQ (Equity)

## Market Makers

The following trading members have been designated as market makers:

- Kanjalochana Finserve Pvt Ltd. (TM code: 90246)
- Parwati Capital Market Pvt Ltd. (TM code: 11890)
- Share India Securities Ltd. (TM code: 10798)
- East India Securities Ltd. (TM code: 8134)
- Kotak Securities Limited (TM code: 8081)
- Achintya Securities Pvt Ltd. (TM code: 13302)
- Choice Equity Broking Private Limited (TM code: 13773)
- Irage Broking Services LLP (TM code: 90068)
- Vaibhav Stock Broking Pvt Ltd. and Derivatives (TM code: 12151)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard listing notification under Regulation 3.1.1 and 2.5.5 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must use the designated security code (FLEXIADD) and ISIN (INF740KA1WU9) for all trades
- Trading must be conducted in the specified market lot size of 1 unit
- Market makers listed must provide liquidity as per NSE regulations

## Important Dates

- **Date of Allotment**: October 10, 2025
- **Circular Date**: October 15, 2025
- **Effective Trading Date**: October 16, 2025
