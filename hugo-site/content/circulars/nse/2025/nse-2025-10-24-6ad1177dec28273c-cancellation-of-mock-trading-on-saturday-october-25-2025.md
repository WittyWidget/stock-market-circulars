## Summary

The National Stock Exchange of India has cancelled the mock trading session for the Commodity Derivatives Segment (COM) that was previously scheduled for Saturday, October 25, 2025. This cancellation supersedes the earlier announcement made in Exchange Circular NSE/COM/70936 dated October 23, 2025. The existing NEAT trading platform versions 1.5.1 and 1.5.2 will continue to remain operational until further notice.

## Key Points

- Mock trading session for Commodity Derivatives Segment scheduled for October 25, 2025 has been cancelled
- This cancellation is in reference to previous circular NSE/COM/70936 dated October 23, 2025
- NEAT versions 1.5.1 and 1.5.2 shall continue to be available till further notice
- Circular issued by Department: Commodity Derivatives
- Circular Reference: 57/2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update cancelling a previously scheduled mock trading session.

## Compliance Requirements

No compliance requirements. Members are simply notified of the cancellation of the mock trading session. No action is required from trading members.

## Important Dates

- **October 24, 2025**: Date of circular issuance
- **October 25, 2025**: Originally scheduled mock trading session date (now cancelled)
- **October 23, 2025**: Date of previous circular NSE/COM/70936 that scheduled the mock trading

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Nil. This is a cancellation of a test/mock trading session and does not affect actual trading operations.

**Operational Impact**: Minimal. Members who had planned to participate in the mock trading session on October 25, 2025 will not need to allocate resources for testing. The cancellation has no impact on live trading operations.

**Technology Impact**: NEAT trading platform versions 1.5.1 and 1.5.2 remain operational without any changes, providing continuity for all trading members.

**Member Impact**: No impact on trading members' business operations as this relates only to a mock/test session and not actual trading activities.
