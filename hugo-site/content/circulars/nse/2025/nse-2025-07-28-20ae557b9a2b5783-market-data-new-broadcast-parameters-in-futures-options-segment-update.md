## Summary

NSE has issued an update to its July 18, 2025 circular regarding new broadcast parameters for market data in the Futures & Options segment. The exchange is introducing new transcodes for Bhavcopy broadcast and additional broadcast parameters to enable streamwise dissemination, building upon previously announced enhanced data dissemination capabilities.

## Key Points

- Partial modification to Exchange circular NSE/MSD/69181 dated July 18, 2025
- New transcode for Bhavcopy broadcast being introduced
- New broadcast parameter for streamwise dissemination of Bhavcopy broadcast
- Enhanced data dissemination for Ticker, Market Index, Market Watch Update & Underlying Open Interest
- Price volume data with 5 depth 1 second refresh frequency
- New transcodes: 17201, 17202, 17130 & 11833 replacing existing 7201, 7202, 7130 & 1833
- Interim coexistence period provided for smooth transition

## Regulatory Changes

- Introduction of new broadcast parameters for F&O segment market data
- Implementation of new transcodes for improved data dissemination
- Enhanced streaming capabilities for market data broadcasts
- Updated NNF API document (Version 9.43) with encryption protocol details

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must refer to updated annexures for new parameter details
- Implementation of new broadcast parameters required
- Migration from existing to new transcodes mandatory
- Compliance with updated NNF API documentation
- Adherence to coexistence and migration timeline

## Important Dates

- **August 02, 2025**: Mock testing begins, release of new additional details
- **August 04, 2025**: Live environment implementation
- **September 06, 2025**: Discontinuation of existing market data broadcast parameters for NNF users

## Impact Assessment

**Technical Impact**: Medium - requires system updates and migration planning by members
**Operational Impact**: Medium - affects market data infrastructure and requires coordination during transition period
**Market Impact**: Low to Medium - improved data dissemination capabilities may enhance market efficiency
**Member Impact**: Medium - requires technical implementation and staff training on new parameters and transcodes
