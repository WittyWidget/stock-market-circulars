## Summary

NSE has announced the upcoming listing of BlueStone Jewellery and Lifestyle Limited's equity shares following their IPO. The company will trade under the symbol BLUESTONE with ISIN INE304W01038. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: BlueStone Jewellery and Lifestyle Limited
- Trading Symbol: BLUESTONE
- ISIN: INE304W01038
- Listing type: IPO (Initial Public Offering)
- Exact listing date to be announced separately

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes mentioned in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are informed about the forthcoming listing and should prepare for trading operations once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: August 18, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

The listing will add a new equity share in the jewelry and lifestyle sector to NSE, providing investors with additional investment opportunities in this segment. Market participants should prepare for potential trading activity once the listing becomes effective.
