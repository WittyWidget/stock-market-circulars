## Summary

NSE has issued a circular listing 9 Investment Companies (ICs) and Investment Holding Companies (IHCs) that are eligible for a special call-auction mechanism for price discovery, as mandated by SEBI Circular No. SEBI/HO/MRD/MRD-PoD 3/P/CIR/2024/86 dated June 20, 2024. The special call auction is scheduled to be held on October 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- 9 companies identified as eligible for special call-auction mechanism
- All listed companies have high proportion of assets (51.91% to 99.68%) invested in other listed companies
- If call auction is unsuccessful on day 1, it will continue on subsequent days until price is discovered
- Companies range from GFL Limited with 51.91% investment proportion to Welspun Investments with 99.68%
- Last traded prices vary significantly from Rs. 59.99 (GFLLIMITED) to Rs. 17,228.00 (TVSHLTD)
- None of the companies have recent buy-back or delisting offers

## Eligible Companies

The following 9 securities are eligible for special call-auction:

1. **GFL Limited (GFLLIMITED)** - 51.91% assets in listed companies, last traded at Rs. 59.99
2. **Kalyani Investment Company Limited (KICL)** - 87.22% assets, last traded at Rs. 4,890.00
3. **Maharashtra Scooters Limited (MAHSCOOTER)** - 98.98% assets, last traded at Rs. 17,228.00
4. **Nalwa Sons Investments Limited (NSIL)** - 58.09% assets, last traded at Rs. 7,650.00
5. **Pilani Investment and Industries Corporation Limited (PILANIINVS)** - 89.28% assets, last traded at Rs. 5,285.00
6. **SIL Investments Limited (SILINV)** - 81.88% assets, last traded at Rs. 617.85
7. **TVS Holdings Limited (TVSHLTD)** - 2090.39% book value ratio, last traded at Rs. 13,500.00
8. **Vardhman Holdings Limited (VHL)** - 374.42% book value ratio, last traded at Rs. 3,833.90
9. **Welspun Investments and Commercials Limited (WELINV)** - 99.68% assets, last traded at Rs. 1,076.20

## Regulatory Changes

This circular implements the special call-auction mechanism mandated by SEBI for ICs and IHCs that have significant investments in other listed companies. The mechanism is designed to facilitate better price discovery for securities where a substantial portion of company assets are invested in other listed entities.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must note that special call-auctions will be held for these 9 securities
- Members should be aware that if the call auction is not successful on October 29, 2025, it will continue on subsequent days
- The auction process will repeat daily until price is successfully discovered for each security

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date:** October 15, 2025
- **Special Call Auction Date:** October 29, 2025
- **Continuation:** Daily thereafter until price discovery is achieved (if needed)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact:** Medium - This affects trading in 9 specific securities representing investment companies and holding companies. The special call-auction mechanism may lead to improved price discovery for these securities, which have significant investments in other listed companies.

**Operational Impact:** Trading members need to be prepared for the special call-auction mechanism and understand that the process may extend beyond a single day if price discovery is not achieved initially.

**Investor Impact:** Investors holding or interested in these 9 securities should be aware of the special trading mechanism and potential price volatility during the price discovery process.
