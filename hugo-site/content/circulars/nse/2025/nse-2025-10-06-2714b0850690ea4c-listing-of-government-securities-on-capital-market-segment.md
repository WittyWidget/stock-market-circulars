## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India Limited has notified the listing of Government of India 6.48% 2035 security on the capital market segment. The security will be available for trading with effect from October 08, 2025. This listing is pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Key Points

- Security Symbol: 648GS2035
- Series: GS (Government Securities)
- Coupon Rate: 6.48%
- Maturity Date: October 06, 2035
- ISIN Code: IN0020250091
- Lot Size: 1
- Market: Capital Market Segment
- Trading identification by designated codes only

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a routine listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security code (648GS2035) for trading purposes
- Trading must be conducted in the specified lot size of 1 unit
- Compliance with Regulation 3.1.1 and Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: October 06, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 08, 2025
- **Maturity Date**: October 06, 2035

## Impact Assessment

This is a routine administrative listing of a government security on the capital market segment. The impact is limited to debt market participants and government securities traders. There is no direct impact on equity markets or listed companies. The 10-year government security provides an additional investment avenue for institutional and retail investors seeking fixed-income instruments with sovereign credit quality.
