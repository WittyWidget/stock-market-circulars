## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of SG Mart Limited's equity shares under the symbol SGMART, effective September 12, 2025. The company will have 126,000,000 equity shares of Re. 1/- each fully paid up trading in the Normal Market segment with compulsory demat requirements.

## Key Points

- Symbol: SGMART
- ISIN: INE385F01024
- Face Value: Re. 1/- per share
- Total shares: 126,000,000
- Market lot: 1 share
- Trading segment: Normal Market (Rolling Settlement)
- Compulsory demat for all investors

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard new listing following existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading must be conducted in compulsory demat mode
- Securities identified only by designated codes
- Trading in specified lot sizes as per annexure
- Lock-in provisions apply as detailed in Annexure-1

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: September 10, 2025
- Trading Effective Date: September 12, 2025
- Lock-in expiry dates vary: Free shares, December 10, 2025, and February 8, 2026

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is a routine new listing. Provides new investment opportunity for retail and institutional investors. The company operates in retail/mart sector with registered office in Delhi and corporate office in Noida.
