## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further equity shares for 19 companies allotted under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP), effective August 19, 2025. These shares will be admitted to trading on the Capital Market segment with designated security codes and specified lot sizes.

## Key Points

- 19 companies getting additional equity shares listed under ESOP allotments
- Effective date: August 19, 2025
- All shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Market lot size is 1 for all listed securities
- No lock-in restrictions applicable

## Companies and Share Details

**Major Listings Include:**
- **Avalon Technologies**: 131,620 shares of Rs. 2/- each (allotted Aug 5, 2025)
- **DRC Systems India**: 387,000 shares of Re. 1/- each (allotted Aug 12, 2025) 
- **Dhani Services**: 160,000 shares of Rs. 2/- each (allotted Aug 7, 2025)
- **CARE Ratings**: 16,066 shares of Rs. 10/- each (allotted Aug 5, 2025)
- **5Paisa Capital**: 6,000 shares of Rs. 10/- each (allotted Aug 8, 2025)

## Regulatory Framework

- Issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Lot sizes specified under Regulation 2.5.5 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A
- Securities identified by designated codes for trading purposes

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: August 18, 2025
- **Effective Date**: August 19, 2025
- **Various Allotment Dates**: August 5-12, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as these are routine ESOP share listings. The additional shares increase the free float marginally for each company but represent standard employee compensation practices. No immediate trading restrictions apply, providing liquidity to ESOP beneficiaries.
