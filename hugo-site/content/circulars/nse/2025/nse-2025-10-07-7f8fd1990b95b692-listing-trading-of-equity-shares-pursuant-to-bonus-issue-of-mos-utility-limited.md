## Summary

National Stock Exchange of India has announced the listing and trading of equity shares of MOS Utility Limited pursuant to a bonus issue. The new shares will be admitted to dealings on the Capital Market segment effective from October 16, 2025. A total of 12,87,20,205 equity shares with a new ISIN INE0N7S01028 will be listed.

## Key Points

- Company: MOS Utility Limited (Symbol: MOS)
- Total new securities being listed: 12,87,20,205 equity shares
- New ISIN: INE0N7S01028
- Distinctive Number Range: 128720206 to 257440410
- Securities will be identified by designated codes for trading purposes
- Circular issued under Regulation 3.1.1 of NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. This is a standard listing notification for bonus issue shares in accordance with existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must use the designated security codes for trading the new bonus shares
- Trading systems must be updated to recognize the new ISIN and distinctive number range
- All trading participants should note the effective date for commencement of trading

## Important Dates

- **Circular Date**: October 07, 2025
- **Record Date**: October 14, 2025
- **Deemed Date of Allotment**: October 15, 2025
- **Effective Date for Trading**: October 16, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Medium - The bonus issue significantly increases the number of shares outstanding (12.87 crore shares), which will increase liquidity in MOS Utility Limited stock. The share price will adjust proportionately based on the bonus ratio.

**Shareholder Impact**: Positive for existing shareholders who will receive additional shares based on their holdings as of the record date, increasing their total shareholding without additional cost.

**Trading Impact**: Trading participants need to ensure their systems are updated with the new ISIN and distinctive number range before October 16, 2025 to enable seamless trading of the bonus shares.
