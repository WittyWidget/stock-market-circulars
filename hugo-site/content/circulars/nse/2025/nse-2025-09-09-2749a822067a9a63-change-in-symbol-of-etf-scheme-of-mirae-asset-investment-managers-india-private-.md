## Summary

NSE announces a symbol change for Mirae Asset Silver ETF from SILVRETF to SILVERAG, effective September 15, 2025. This is a routine administrative change affecting only the trading symbol identification.

## Key Points

- Mirae Asset Silver ETF (ISIN: INF769K01KG6) will have its symbol changed
- Current symbol: SILVRETF
- New symbol: SILVERAG
- Change applies to Exchange Traded Funds scheme of Mirae Asset Investment Managers (India) Private Limited

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is an administrative symbol update only.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must update their systems to reflect the new symbol
- Trading platforms should be updated before the effective date
- Risk management systems should incorporate the symbol change

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 15, 2025
- **Circular Date**: September 9, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a routine symbol change that will not affect the underlying ETF operations, ISIN, or trading mechanisms. Members need to update their systems to ensure continuity of trading and operations.
