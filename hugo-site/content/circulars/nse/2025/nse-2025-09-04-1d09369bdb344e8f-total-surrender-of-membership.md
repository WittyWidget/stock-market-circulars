## Summary

NSE has approved the total surrender of membership for SIDDHARTHA CAPITAL MARKET LIMITED (SEBI registration: INZ000251937). The member's last trade date was February 24, 2025, and the surrender was approved on August 22, 2025.

## Key Points

- Trading member: SIDDHARTHA CAPITAL MARKET LIMITED
- SEBI registration number: INZ000251937
- Last trade date: February 24, 2025
- Surrender approval date: August 22, 2025
- All members notified to take note of this change

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. Standard membership surrender process completed.

## Compliance Requirements

All NSE members are required to take note of the membership surrender.

## Important Dates

- **Last Trade Date**: February 24, 2025
- **Surrender Approval Date**: August 22, 2025
- **Circular Date**: September 4, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact as this is an administrative action involving a single trading member. The member had already ceased trading activities in February 2025 before the formal surrender approval.
