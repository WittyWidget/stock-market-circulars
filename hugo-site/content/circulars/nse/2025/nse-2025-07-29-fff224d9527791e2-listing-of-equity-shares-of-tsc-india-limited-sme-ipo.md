## Summary

TSC India Limited's equity shares have been listed on the NSE SME platform following their IPO. The company has filed its shareholding pattern under Regulation 31(1)(a) with ISIN INE16VK01010 and NSE symbol TSC.

## Key Points

- Company Name: TSC India Limited
- NSE Symbol: TSC
- ISIN: INE16VK01010
- Security Type: Equity Shares
- Listing Date: July 29, 2025
- Platform: NSE SME
- Promoter & Promoter Group: 6 shareholders holding 9,391,250 shares
- Public shareholders: 433 shareholders holding 4,656 shares

## Regulatory Changes

No specific regulatory changes announced. Standard SME listing procedures and disclosure requirements apply.

## Compliance Requirements

- Quarterly shareholding pattern filing under SEBI regulations
- Compliance with SME listing requirements
- Regular disclosure obligations as per NSE SME guidelines
- Maintenance of minimum public shareholding as applicable

## Important Dates

- Listing Date: July 29, 2025
- Shareholding Pattern Filing Date: July 29, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The listing provides new investment opportunity in the SME segment. Impact is moderate as SME listings have restricted investor base and lower liquidity compared to main board listings. The company's shareholding structure shows concentrated promoter holding which is typical for SME companies.
