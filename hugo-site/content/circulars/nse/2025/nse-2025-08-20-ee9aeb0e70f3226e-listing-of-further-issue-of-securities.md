## Summary

NSE has approved the listing of 817,600 equity shares of Le Merite Exports Limited (Symbol: LEMERITE) on the SME EMERGE platform. These shares result from the conversion of warrants allotted on preferential basis and will commence trading on August 21, 2025.

## Key Points

- 817,600 equity shares of Rs. 10/- each to be listed
- Issue price: Rs. 146.5 per share
- Trading symbol: LEMERITE
- Market lot size: 400 shares
- ISIN: INE0G1L01017
- Securities are pari passu with existing shares

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing procedure under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Securities identified by designated codes only
- Trading must be in specified lot sizes of 400 shares
- Lock-in restrictions apply as per Annexure A

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 21, 2025
- **Allotment Date**: March 18, 2025
- **Lock-in Expiry**: February 28, 2026 (for all tranches)

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as this is a routine listing of a relatively small number of shares from warrant conversion. The securities carry lock-in restrictions until February 2026, limiting immediate trading volume.
