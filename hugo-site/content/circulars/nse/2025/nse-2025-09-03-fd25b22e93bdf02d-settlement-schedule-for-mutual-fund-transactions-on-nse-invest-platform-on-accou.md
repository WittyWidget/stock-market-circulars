## Summary

NSE has announced revised settlement schedules for mutual fund transactions on the NSE Invest Platform due to the clearing holiday (Id-E-Milad) on September 5, 2025. The changes affect settlement dates for subscription and redemption orders placed around the holiday period.

## Key Points

- Liquid and debt schemes subscription funds will be settled on September 8, 2025 (next business day)
- Other schemes subscription funds received within cut-off time will be settled on September 5, 2025
- Subscription units pay-in/pay-out for orders placed on September 4-5 will be on September 8, 2025
- Redemption units pay-in/pay-out for September 5 orders will be on the same day
- No redemption funds pay-out on September 5, 2025
- No liquid subscription orders or settlements on September 5, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No permanent regulatory changes - temporary operational adjustments for the holiday period only.

## Compliance Requirements

- Participants must note the revised settlement schedule
- Check scheme category in scheme master for redemption settlement timelines
- Follow applicable NAV rules for delayed settlements
- Adhere to specified cut-off times for fund settlements

## Important Dates

- **September 3, 2025**: Circular issued
- **September 4, 2025**: S 2025168 settlement, K 2025168 liquid settlement
- **September 5, 2025**: Id-E-Milad clearing holiday, U 2025169 redemption settlement
- **September 8, 2025**: Delayed settlements for liquid/debt schemes and subscription units

## Impact Assessment

Medium operational impact on mutual fund participants and investors. Settlement delays may affect liquidity planning for liquid and debt scheme investments. Participants need to adjust their operational processes and inform clients about revised settlement timelines during the holiday period.
