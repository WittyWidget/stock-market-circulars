## Summary

NSE has announced the forthcoming listing of Pace Digitek Limited equity shares on the Exchange following its Initial Public Offering (IPO). The company will trade under the symbol PACEDIGITK with ISIN INE0S3G01027. The exact listing date will be communicated through a separate circular.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Pace Digitek Limited
- Trading Symbol: PACEDIGITK
- ISIN: INE0S3G01027
- Listing date to be announced separately
- Circular issued to all NSE members

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced in this circular.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are informed about the upcoming listing and should prepare their systems accordingly once the listing date is announced.

## Important Dates

- Circular Date: October 03, 2025
- Listing Date: To be announced through separate circular

## Impact Assessment

Minimal immediate impact. This is a standard listing notification for a new IPO. Members and market participants will need to update their systems with the new security details once the listing date is confirmed. The actual market impact will depend on the company's business profile and IPO subscription details.
