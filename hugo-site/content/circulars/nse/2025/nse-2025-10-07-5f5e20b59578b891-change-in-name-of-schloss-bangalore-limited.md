## Summary

The National Stock Exchange has announced that Schloss Bangalore Limited will undergo a corporate name change to Leela Palaces Hotels & Resorts Limited, effective October 13, 2025. The company's trading symbol THELEELA will remain unchanged.

## Key Points

- Company name changes from Schloss Bangalore Limited to Leela Palaces Hotels & Resorts Limited
- Trading symbol THELEELA remains unchanged
- Change effective from October 13, 2025
- Circular reference number: 1950/2025
- NSE reference: NSE/CML/70658

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes are introduced by this circular. This is a standard administrative update for a corporate name change.

## Compliance Requirements

Members of the exchange are required to:
- Note the name change in their systems
- Update company records effective October 13, 2025
- Continue using the existing trading symbol THELEELA

## Important Dates

- **Circular Issue Date**: October 07, 2025
- **Effective Date**: October 13, 2025

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Minimal. Name changes are administrative in nature and do not affect trading operations or investor holdings.

**Operational Impact**: Low. The trading symbol remains unchanged, minimizing the need for system updates by market participants.

**Investor Impact**: Negligible. Shareholders' holdings remain unaffected, and the familiar trading symbol THELEELA continues to be used for all transactions.
