## Summary

Bajaj Finserv Equity Savings Fund of Bajaj Mutual Fund will be reopened for ongoing transactions on NSE MF Invest Platform effective August 21, 2025. The fund will offer subscriptions, redemptions, and systematic investment facilities including SIP, STP, and SWP.

## Key Points

- Bajaj Finserv Equity Savings Fund reopening for ongoing transactions
- Available on NSE MF Invest Platform from August 21, 2025
- Includes subscription and redemption facilities
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), STP (Systematic Transfer Plan), and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) facilities available
- Six scheme variants available across Direct and Regular plans with different dividend options

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is an operational update regarding fund availability.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are informed of the fund availability for client transactions. No specific compliance actions required beyond standard mutual fund transaction procedures.

## Important Dates

- **August 21, 2025**: Effective date for fund reopening and availability of all transaction facilities

## Impact Assessment

Low impact operational update. Provides additional investment option for investors seeking equity savings funds through NSE platform. Enhances product availability but does not affect market structure or trading operations.
