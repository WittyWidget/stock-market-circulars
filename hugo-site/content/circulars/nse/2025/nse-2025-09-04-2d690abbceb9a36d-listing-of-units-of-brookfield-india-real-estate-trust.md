## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 32,258,065 units of Brookfield India Real Estate Trust (BIRET) allotted on preferential basis. The units will be available for trading from September 05, 2025 under symbol BIRET with ISIN INE0FDU25010.

## Key Points

- 32,258,065 units allotted on preferential basis on September 02, 2025
- Trading symbol: BIRET
- ISIN: INE0FDU25010 (permanent)
- Distinctive number range: 607752449 to 640010513
- Units are pari passu with existing units
- Currently credited under temporary ISIN pending activation

## Regulatory Changes

Units admitted to dealings on NSE Capital Market segment under existing trading regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading participants must use designated security codes (BIRET)
- Units must be traded in specified lot sizes as per annexure
- Compliance with NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations Part A

## Important Dates

- **Allotment Date**: September 02, 2025
- **Trading Commencement**: September 05, 2025
- **Lock-in Period Ends**: September 06, 2026
- **Circular Effective Date**: September 05, 2025

## Impact Assessment

The preferential allotment increases the tradeable units of Brookfield India Real Estate Trust. The one-year lock-in period until September 2026 means these specific units cannot be traded immediately, providing stability to the trust's unit price. This is a routine listing procedure for REIT preferential allotments with limited immediate market impact.
