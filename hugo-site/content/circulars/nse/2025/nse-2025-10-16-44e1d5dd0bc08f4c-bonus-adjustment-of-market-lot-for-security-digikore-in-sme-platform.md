## Summary

NSE has announced the adjustment of market lot for DIGIKORE STUDIOS LIMITED (Symbol: DIGIKORE) trading on the SME Platform due to a 1:1 bonus issue. The market lot will be adjusted to 400 shares (from the previous 200 shares) with an adjustment factor of 2, effective October 24, 2025.

## Key Points

- Security Symbol: DIGIKORE
- Company Name: DIGIKORE STUDIOS LIMITED
- Corporate Action Type: Bonus Issue
- Bonus Ratio: 1:1 (one bonus share for every one share held)
- Adjustment Factor: 2
- Adjusted Market Lot: 400 shares
- Ex-date/Effective Date: October 24, 2025
- Record Date: October 24, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This is a standard corporate action notification for market lot adjustment following a bonus issue.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must load updated security.gz/nnf_security.gz/MII - Security File.gz file in their trading applications before trading on October 24, 2025
- Updated files can be obtained from the directory common/NTNEAT on the Extranet server
- MII - Security File.gz is also available on NSE website at https://www.nseindia.com/all-reports

## Important Dates

- **October 16, 2025**: Circular issue date
- **October 24, 2025**: Ex-date/Effective date for bonus issue
- **October 24, 2025**: Record date for determining bonus eligibility

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: Limited to DIGIKORE stock traders on SME platform. The 1:1 bonus will double the number of shares outstanding and adjust the market lot accordingly to 400 shares.

**Operational Impact**: Trading members must update their systems with new security files before the effective date to ensure proper trading lot sizes. Failure to update may result in order rejection or trading errors.

**Investor Impact**: Shareholders on record date will receive one bonus share for each share held. The stock price will adjust proportionately, and minimum trading quantity will change to 400 shares from the effective date.
