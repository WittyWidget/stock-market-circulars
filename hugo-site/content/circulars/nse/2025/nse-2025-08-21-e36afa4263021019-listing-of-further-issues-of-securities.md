## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of further equity shares for 11 companies under Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOP), effective August 22, 2025. The companies include major banks like HDFC Bank and ICICI Bank, along with other listed entities across various sectors.

## Key Points

- 11 companies are listing additional equity shares under ESOP
- All shares are pari passu with existing equity shares
- Market lot size is 1 for all securities
- No lock-in period applicable
- Trading to commence August 22, 2025

## Companies and Share Details

- **APTUS (Aptus Value Housing Finance)**: 283,472 shares of Rs. 2 each
- **GENESYS (Genesys International Corporation)**: 42,050 shares of Rs. 5 each
- **HDFCBANK (HDFC Bank)**: 2,102,087 shares of Re. 1 each
- **ICICIBANK (ICICI Bank)**: 626,130 shares of Rs. 2 each
- **ICICIPRULI (ICICI Prudential Life Insurance)**: 65,115 shares of Rs. 10 each
- **JBCHEPHARM (JB Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals)**: Details partially provided

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 22, 2025
- **Allotment Dates**: Range from August 13-19, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected as these are routine ESOP allotments. The shares will trade normally without any restrictions and will not affect the overall market capitalization significantly given their nature as employee compensation.
