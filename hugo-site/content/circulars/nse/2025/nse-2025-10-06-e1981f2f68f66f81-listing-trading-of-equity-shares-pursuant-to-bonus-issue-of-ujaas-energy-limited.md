## Summary

NSE has notified the listing and commencement of trading of bonus equity shares issued by Ujaas Energy Limited. A total of 2,22,65,184 bonus shares will be listed on the Capital Market segment with effect from October 14, 2025. The shares will trade under the symbol 'UEL' with ISIN INE899L01030.

## Key Points

- Company Name: Ujaas Energy Limited
- Trading Symbol: UEL
- ISIN: INE899L01030
- Number of Bonus Shares: 2,22,65,184
- Distinctive Number Range: 111132631 to 133397814
- Record Date: October 10, 2025
- Deemed Date of Allotment: October 13, 2025
- Trading Commencement Date: October 14, 2025
- Circular Reference: NSE/CML/70636 (Circular No. 1928/2025)

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes. This circular notifies the admission of additional securities to trading under existing regulations pursuant to Regulation 3.1.1 of the National Stock Exchange (Capital Market) Trading Regulations Part A.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members must identify the security by its designated codes (Symbol: UEL, ISIN: INE899L01030) for trading on the system
- No additional compliance requirements for members beyond standard trading protocols

## Important Dates

- **October 06, 2025**: Circular issued
- **October 10, 2025**: Record Date for bonus issue
- **October 13, 2025**: Deemed Date of Allotment
- **October 14, 2025**: Trading commencement date (effective date)

## Impact Assessment

**Market Impact**: The bonus issue will increase the outstanding share capital of Ujaas Energy Limited by 2,22,65,184 shares, which will impact the stock's liquidity and market capitalization proportionally. Existing shareholders will receive bonus shares as per the entitlement ratio (not specified in circular).

**Shareholder Impact**: Positive for existing shareholders who will receive additional shares without any cost. The share price will adjust downward proportionally to reflect the increased share count.

**Trading Impact**: Enhanced liquidity expected due to increased float. Price adjustment will occur automatically on the ex-bonus date, making shares more affordable for retail investors.
