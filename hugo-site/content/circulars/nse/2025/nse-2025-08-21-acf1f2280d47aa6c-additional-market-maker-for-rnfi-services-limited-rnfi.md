## Summary

NSE has appointed NNM Securities Private Limited as an additional market maker for RNFI Services Limited, effective August 22, 2025. This is in addition to the existing market maker Choice Equity Broking Private Limited.

## Key Points

- Company: RNFI Services Limited (Symbol: RNFI)
- Current Market Maker: Choice Equity Broking Private Limited (Member Code: 13773)
- Additional Market Maker: NNM Securities Private Limited (Member Code: 10446)
- Effective Date: August 22, 2025

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes - this is an operational update regarding market maker appointments.

## Compliance Requirements

Members are advised to note the additional market maker appointment for RNFI Services Limited.

## Important Dates

- **August 22, 2025**: Effective date for additional market maker appointment

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact. The addition of another market maker may improve liquidity and trading efficiency for RNFI Services Limited shares on the SME platform.
