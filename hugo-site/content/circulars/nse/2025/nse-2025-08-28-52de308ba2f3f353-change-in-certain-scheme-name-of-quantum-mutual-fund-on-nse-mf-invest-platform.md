## Summary

Quantum Mutual Fund is changing the names of two of its schemes on the NSE MF Invest platform effective August 29, 2025. This is an administrative change that updates scheme naming conventions.

## Key Points

- Two Quantum Mutual Fund schemes are being renamed
- Changes effective from August 29, 2025
- Applied to NSE MF Invest platform
- Notice cum addendum issued by Quantum Mutual Fund attached

## Scheme Name Changes

**Quantum Multi Asset Fund of Funds** → **Quantum Multi Asset Active FOF**

**Quantum Equity Fund of Funds** → **Quantum Diversified Equity All Cap active FOF**

## Compliance Requirements

- NSE members are informed of the name changes
- Platform will reflect new names from effective date
- Reference documentation updated accordingly

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: August 29, 2025
- **Circular Date**: August 28, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal operational impact as this is purely an administrative name change. The underlying fund structures and operations remain unchanged. Members and investors should note the new names for future reference and transactions.
