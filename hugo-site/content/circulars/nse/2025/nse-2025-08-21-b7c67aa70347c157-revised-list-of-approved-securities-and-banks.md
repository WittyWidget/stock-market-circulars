## Summary

NSE has revised the list of approved equity shares that can form part of the non-cash component of liquid assets for margin purposes. The list contains 40 securities with VAR-based haircuts, where members can deposit each security subject to a maximum of 25% of total margins.

## Key Points

- 40 equity shares approved as non-cash component of liquid assets
- VAR-based haircut applicable on all listed securities
- Maximum 25% limit per security of total margins for each member
- Overall permitted quantities specified for each security across all segments
- No memberwise permitted quantity specified for CM segment (marked as N.A)

## Regulatory Changes

- Updated list replaces previous approved securities for margin purposes
- Maintained VAR-based haircut methodology for risk management
- Continued application of 25% concentration limit per security

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members must adhere to 25% maximum limit per security
- Securities to be valued with applicable VAR haircuts
- Compliance with overall permitted quantities across all segments

## Important Dates

- Circular date: August 21, 2025
- Implementation: Immediate upon circular issuance

## Impact Assessment

- Provides clarity on acceptable securities for margin requirements
- Helps members plan their margin funding strategies
- Ensures diversification through concentration limits
- Maintains risk management through VAR-based haircuts
