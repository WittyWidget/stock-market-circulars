## Summary

NSE announces the addition of a new Point of Presence (POP) at Rabale, Navi Mumbai to enhance member connectivity options. This is a partial modification to earlier circular NSE/MSD/67754 dated April 29, 2025.

## Key Points

- New POP location: Rabale DC Tower 1, Level 1, Plot No. R-847, 1/2, T.T.C. Industrial Area, MIDC Industrial Area, Sector 2, Rabale, Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra 400701
- Connectivity available in both Managed and Self Service modes
- Both conventional wired and Cross Connect last mile connectivity options available
- Applications can be submitted via ENIT platform starting August 11, 2025
- Same service providers and modalities as per previous circular NSE/MSD/67754

## Regulatory Changes

Expansion of NSE's connectivity infrastructure with addition of Rabale POP to the existing list of Points of Presence.

## Compliance Requirements

- Members intending to use the new Rabale POP must apply through ENIT platform
- Path for application: ENIT-NEW-TRADE > Membership > TCP IP Scenario > Activation
- Members remain responsible for procuring and maintaining their network architecture and connectivity

## Important Dates

- **August 11, 2025**: Applications for Rabale POP connectivity can be submitted via ENIT

## Impact Assessment

Positive infrastructure development that provides members with additional connectivity options in the Mumbai region. Enhanced redundancy and choice for trading system access, particularly beneficial for members operating in Navi Mumbai area. SIFY Technologies Limited continues as the Telecom Network Integrator (TNI) for end-to-end connectivity services.
