## Summary

NSE has notified the listing of 1,979 equity shares of Grasim Industries Limited resulting from conversion of partly paid-up equity shares to fully paid-up equity shares, effective August 06, 2025.

## Key Points

- 1,979 equity shares of Grasim Industries Limited admitted to NSE Capital Market segment
- Conversion from partly paid-up to fully paid-up equity shares
- Face value and paid-up value: Rs. 2/- each
- Market lot size: 1 share
- Trading symbol: GRASIM
- ISIN: INE047A01021
- Pari passu with existing shares
- No lock-in restrictions

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory changes introduced. Standard listing notification under existing NSE Capital Market Trading Regulations.

## Compliance Requirements

- Trading members to use designated security codes for system trading
- Securities identified by designated codes only
- Trading in specified lot sizes as per annexure

## Important Dates

- Circular date: August 05, 2025
- Effective date: August 06, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact due to small number of shares (1,979) and routine conversion process. No effect on existing shareholders' rights as shares are pari passu. Temporary ISIN arrangement in place for dematerialized shares as per SEBI guidelines.
