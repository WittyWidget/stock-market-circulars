## Summary

NSE has announced that Ganesh Housing Corporation Limited will undergo a name and symbol change effective September 15, 2025. The company's trading symbol will change from GANESHHOUC to GANESHHOU, and the company name will change from "Ganesh Housing Corporation Limited" to "GANESH HOUSING LIMITED".

## Key Points

- Current symbol GANESHHOUC will change to GANESHHOU
- Company name changing from "Ganesh Housing Corporation Limited" to "GANESH HOUSING LIMITED"
- Changes effective from September 15, 2025
- Circular issued by NSE Listing Department

## Regulatory Changes

No regulatory framework changes. This is a routine corporate administrative action requiring exchange notification.

## Compliance Requirements

- Exchange members must update their systems to reflect new symbol and name
- Trading participants should ensure proper identification of the security post-change
- Market data vendors need to update reference data

## Important Dates

- **Effective Date**: September 15, 2025
- **Circular Date**: September 09, 2025

## Impact Assessment

Minimal market impact expected. This is a routine administrative change that does not affect the underlying business operations or financial position of the company. Trading will continue normally under the new symbol from the effective date.
